'''Macil''' is a [[chromium]] [[Misting]] (aka a [[Leecher]]) and one of the women captured by the [[Set]] on [[Scadrial]].{{book ref|mb7|60}}

== History ==
Macil was kidnapped by the [[Vanishers]] and delivered to the [[Set]] to be a part of the [[Community]]. The Set told her that she had been chosen by random lottery to be saved from a catastrophic [[ashmount]] eruption.{{book ref|mb7|56}}

After [[Armal]] Rioted [[Gave Entrone]]'s shame. Macil Leeched his [[Allomantic]] reserves so that Armal may tie him up.{{book ref|mb7|60}} Marasi and the Allomancers of [[Wayfarer]] drained the [[perpendicularity]] just before [[Autonomy]]'s forces could enter Scadrial through it.{{book ref|mb7|64}} After resting, Marasi led Macil and the other Allomancers out of the caverns and into [[Bilming]].{{book ref|mb7|68}}
