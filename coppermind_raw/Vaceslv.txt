'''Vaceslv''' is a member of the [[Kholinar Wall Guard]] in [[Alethkar]] on [[Roshar]]. He is a part of [[Noro]]'s squad, Platoon Seven, Squad Two.{{book ref|sa3|73}}

== Appearance and Personality ==
Kaladin describes Vaceslv as a friendly man; [[Alethi]], but with obvious [[Thaylen]] heritage. Vaceslv is of the tenth [[dahn]], the lowest rank of [[lighteyes]], and views 'middlers'&mdash;lighteyes who are of at least the eighth dahn but not quite highlords&mdash;as useless. During a patrol, when Vaceslv's group passes [[Adolin]], who is disguised as a middler, Vaceslv mocks the stereotypical middler desire for fancy clothing.{{book ref|sa3|73}}

== Abilities ==
Vaceslv is trained in using a side sword, truncheon, and small round shield while on patrol. He is also trained in the use of a tower shield and pike in a defensive formation.{{book ref|sa3|73}} 

== History ==
Vaceslv came to know Kaladin as a fellow soldier in his squad. Vaceslv and another member named [[Alaward]] tried multiple times to get Kaladin to play cards with them; Kaladin declined, thinking of his experiences with [[Sah]] and the other [[Voidbringers]].{{book ref|sa3|73}}

After a raid by the [[Fused]], Vaceslv was noted as having taken a gash to the chest that required assistance from surgeons,{{book ref|sa3|73}} but he was able to recover. Noro's squad, likely including Vaceslv, was present when Highmarshal [[Azure]] revealed how she supplied food for the army.{{book ref|sa3|81}}

Vaceslv followed Kaladin into [[Kholinar Palace]] with his squad and fought in the [[Siege_of_Kholinar#Battle_of_Kholinar_Palace|Battle of Kholinar Palace]]. Most of his squadmates, including Noro and [[Beard]], were killed in the fighting.{{book ref|sa3|84}} There were a few survivors, but Vaceslv is not mentioned as being one of them, and may have died in the battle.
