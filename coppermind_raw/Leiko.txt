'''Leiko''' (Callsign: '''Lizard''') is a member of [[Nightmare Flight]] in the [[DDF]]. Lizard has bright blue eyes and waist-length black hair.{{book ref|sf1|1}} Lizard participates in a large battle against the Krell when Spensa returns. Lizard is swarmed by several Krell, and though her squadmates try to help her she is killed.{{book ref|sf1|2}}
