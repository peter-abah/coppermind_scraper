'''Tenny''' is a stuffed [[kandra]] owned by [[Maxillium Ladrian]] on [[Scadrial]].{{book ref|mb7|5}} Tenny is a [[Soonie pup]], a stuffed animal representing [[TenSoon|TenSoon]] the kandra's pre-Catacendre wolfhound form.{{file ref|The_Two_Seasons_broadsheet.jpg|text=''The Two Seasons'' broadsheet}} "Tenny" is likely a childlike play on the name "TenSoon" that Maxillium created. Maxillium often hugs Tenny when he is sad.{{book ref|mb7|5}}
