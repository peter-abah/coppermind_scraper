The '''''Jagged Star gunship''''' is a type of gunship in the '''Jada''' class of [[Malwish]] military spacecraft. The Jagged Star serves as a long range cruiser,{{book ref|iote|53}} meant for both firing at other capital ships and supporting military operations on the ground. The front of the gunship is outfitted with a large cannon, which glows with a red and harsh light before firing.{{book ref|iote|60}} This cannon isn't its only weapon however.{{book ref|iote|35}} As it’s also meant to support military operations on the ground, it has the ability to serve as a full base of operations; it carries pods to serve as living quarters or research stations{{book ref|iote|36}} and has an amazingly powerful engine capable of supplying enough power to use a large weapon like the [[Intensifier]].{{book ref|iote|38}} The gunship's pods are also useful because due to them, it won't have to take its scientists and engineers with it into battle.{{book ref|iote|42}} 

As a capital ship, it dwarfs many other ships, including starfighters and even large cargo ships like the [[Dynamic]].{{book ref|iote|48}} The Jagged Star gunship has multiple bridges, each capable of flying the ship, and a layer of [[silver]] in its hull, a standard precaution. The ship does take a long time to reach its top speed.{{book ref|iote|59}} The [[Malwish Planetary Guard]] had one stationed in the [[Drominad system]] as part of a mission to gain the use of a new [[perpendicularity]] there.{{book ref|iote|35}}
