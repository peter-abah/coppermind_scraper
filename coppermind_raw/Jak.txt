'''Jak''' also known as "Allomancer Jak" and "Gentleman Jak", is a [[Scadrian]] nobleman and an acclaimed explorer.{{book ref|eltania}}

== Appearance & Personality ==
{{for|/Gallery|more images}}
Jak is depicted in all his broadsheets as being a well groomed man around his early thirties. He is always shown sporting a large mustache which is complimented by long hair and glasses. Although his outfit varies from time to time, it almost always bears a resemblance to a suit of some kind with a [[mistcloak]] added. The mistcloak seemingly serves no purpose, as Jak is not a [[Coinshot]] or [[Lurcher]]. When in less formal settings and outside exploring, he wears a cowboy hat. Jak frequently wears buttoned up shirts, which have a tendency to break open as his stories go on. Underneath, he has a well-muscled physique.{{file ref|Elendel Daily broadsheet.png|''The Elendel Daily'' Broadsheet}}{{book ref|eltania}} Sometime before his arrival in New Seran, Jak received a finely woven shirt from the daughter of the owner of [[Gilles & Gilles]]. He values this shirt greatly. He also has a large number of bullet scars from various adventures of his.{{file ref|House Record broadsheet.jpg|''The House Record'' Broadsheet}}{{file ref|The Two Seasons broadsheet.jpg|''The Two Seasons'' Broadsheet}}

It can be difficult to understand what Jak's personality is like; his depictions in his adventures diverge greatly from the opinions of those around him. Jak depicts himself as a crafty and courageous individual who displays little to no fear at the variety of threats that face him. He is shown to be competent and capable of escaping a wide array of dangerous scenarios using his smarts alone. Being both well-cultured and well-traveled, he also has few issues getting along with those whose paths he crosses during his adventures.{{file ref|Elendel Daily broadsheet.png|''The Elendel Daily'' Broadsheet}}{{book ref|eltania}}{{file ref|House Record broadsheet.jpg|''The House Record'' Broadsheet}} He himself believes that honesty is his greatest virtue.{{book ref|eltania}}

{{sidequote|These lands of adventure and danger are my natural habitat, and I thrive upon the challenge they provide. If I were to spend too long in the safe and mundane environment of milky Elendel, I fear I would wilt away.|Jak about the Roughs in one of his stories{{book ref|eltania}}|side=right|size=300px}}

In contrast to this, however, his closest peers give a different account of both his personality and adventures. According to his editor and [[Terris]] steward, [[Handerwym]], Jak is in fact quite ignorant about the events he recounts. Handerwym attests that Jak does little in the way of true research or double-checking his accounts for accuracy, not even for grammar mistakes or continuity. Most of these errors are rectified by Handerwym before the publication of the book editions of his journeys, with biting commentary given in the form of footnotes added to provide a more accurate account of events. Although he refrains from outright stating that Jak lies in his adventures, Handerwym displays a large amount of doubt about his more outlandish claims. From these footnotes, a different image of Jak is painted, in which he appears more idiotic than brave during his adventures. It seems a common occurrence for him to completely misunderstand what is happening and instead consistently escalate the situation.{{book ref|eltania}} Despite this, and the many improbable claims that Jak makes, he himself does not actually think he is lying whilst recounting his stories.{{wob ref|12661}} 

Jak believes that the reason that he travels the Roughs is, for at least a small part, so that the people of the Basin may learn of the Roughs and its fascinating people.{{file ref|Elendel Daily broadsheet.png|''The Elendel Daily'' Broadsheet}} He also doesn’t appear to have a very high opinion of the Outer Cities. His favorite wine is [[Chamblis Montreau]], and he often smokes [[Doxonar]] brand cigars.{{file ref|House Record broadsheet.jpg|''The House Record'' Broadsheet}} He is blissfully unaware of modern research about Koloss life and culture, and this causes him to view them as savages and uncultured beasts. He is also in love with Elizandra.{{book ref|eltania}}

== Attributes and Abilities ==
Jak is a [[tin]] [[Misting]] (a [[Tineye]]). He considers himself skilled at using his abilities to seek out important information. While some Allomancers fill their vials of metal with whiskey, Jak does not, believing it to be important that a gentleman remains clear of mind at all times. He doesn’t otherwise seem to strictly follow this rule however. He also carries a pouch of tin inside a hidden compartment in one of his boots. This system was designed by [[Ranette]], and Jak is happy with it, though it has a tendency to open unexpectedly.{{book ref|eltania}} He is also proficient with [[Glint]], his sidearm.{{file ref|Elendel Daily broadsheet.png|''The Elendel Daily'' Broadsheet}} Jak claims to have skill in animal handling, having apparently managed to domesticate a "long necked" animal from the [[Roughs]].{{file ref|House Record broadsheet.jpg|''The House Record'' Broadsheet}} The animal in question is either a [[Wikipedia: llama| llama]] or a [[Wikipedia: giraffe| giraffe]].{{wob ref|4362}} 

Stories about Jak also claim that he can fly, eat rocks and talk to birds,{{book ref|mb7|prologue}} although this is obviously not true. He has also been in a number of fistfights. He also has a company, Jak also claims to have a faceless Immortal as a guide, called [[Lyndip]]. She appears to him during moments of great danger or difficulty. She has the appearance of a talking crow. Her existence has not been confirmed by anyone other than Jak himself however.{{book ref|eltania}}

== History ==
{{image|AU Allomancer Jak.jpeg|side=right|width=250px|[[Elizandra]] finding Jak}}

=== Early Adventuring ===
Jak isn’t exactly sure of his parentage, although what this means isn’t completely clear.{{book ref|eltania}} He began his adventuring career sometime around the year 319 PC. His stories initially received great success and acclaim, with stories about him being told around both the Roughs and the Basin. He was noted due to the claim that he had never killed a criminal yet and instead arrested them, although this is known to have been false.{{book ref|mb7|prologue}}{{file ref|House Record broadsheet.jpg|''The House Record'' Broadsheet}} One story told about him claims that the first men he killed were a terrible criminal called [[Blatant Barm]] and his lackeys.{{book ref|mb7|prologue}} He at some point met Handerwym after pulling him from a lake and saving him from drowning.{{file ref|Elendel Daily broadsheet.png|''The Elendel Daily'' Broadsheet}}

Over the years, Jak would eventually leave the Roughs and begin exploring more distant and dangerous locations. He was thrown into the ocean in ''Allomancer Jak and the Mask of Ages'' while tied to a bust of the [[Survivor]] (or the [[Spook|Lord Mistborn]]). He claims to have swum through river rapids in ''Allomancer Jak and the Waters of Dread'', although this may not have actually happened.{{book ref|eltania}} He also claims to have, at some stage, climbed the Ashmounts and domesticated a long-necked animal of the [[Plains of Kaermeron]].{{file ref|House Record broadsheet.jpg|''The House Record'' Broadsheet}} During this time, Jak was captured and hit on the head at least eighteen times.{{book ref|eltania}}

=== The Pits of Eltania ===
During a later adventure Jak ventured to the [[Pits of Eltania]] in search of the "Survivor's treasure". He at some point split from his caravan, and was captured by koloss. He at one stage believed he was to be executed for some unknown transgression against the local koloss tribe. Before being executed, Jak was allowed to say goodbye to his acquaintances. Jak managed to recover his gun from [[Handerwym]] while embracing him. Jak used the gun to escape his bonds and kill his executioner. Jak then resolved to be accepted by the koloss tribe as one of their own; he also came to believe that he was now king of the tribe.{{file ref|Elendel Daily broadsheet.png|''The Elendel Daily'' Broadsheet}}

Jak was challenged by a koloss, and killed them with his gun. This was part of his plan to be accepted by the koloss tribe, but Handerwym felt it was not a good one. Jak later woke up alone in a cave within a canyon, and believed himself trapped there. Concerned for his companion, Miss [[Elizandra Dramali]], who he feared was in danger, Jak decided that he needed to escape quickly. Realising that he was in an area known for possessing a great amount of minerals, Jak began searching the walls of the cave for metals. He soon found a vein of tin, and used his tongue to swallow a bit of tin. After burning it, he noticed two things. The first was the tinkling of water, and the second was the presence of his Faceless Immortal guide, Lyndip. They talked, and she told him that Elizandra was to undergo a horrible thing. Jak, set on finding and saving her, saw a large raincloud heading his way, and realised that a small river near him would soon swell up to many times its original size. After waiting for the perfect moment, Jak jumped and dove straight into the river.{{book ref|Eltania}}

{{sidequote|“You’re amazing,” I whispered.
“Well damn, Jak,” she whispered back. “You’ve finally gone and said something smart, for once.|Jak and Elizandra{{book ref|eltania}}|side=right|size=300px}}

Jak managed to avoid any rocks and stay afloat until he reached a wider and less treacherous area of the river, where he managed to climb onto shore. He was immediately taken captive by Koloss again, and without gun or metals he couldn’t resist them. They took him to an oasis, which Jak believed was a holy place for them. After having been put down by the Koloss, Jak spoke to the elders, who served as leaders of the tribe. They told him that he was to be killed and made into a Koloss, as per the will of the daughter of the one he had killed. He asked after her, questioning the elders about her identity until it was revealed that the daughter of this Koloss warrior was the very same person as his companion Elizandra. Shocked to learn this, he questioned her, it didn’t take him long to forgive her however, and she informed him of the plan to make him into a Koloss. She revealed that, despite what the Koloss had told him, it wasn’t her will, but that they wouldn’t listen to her, and actually planned to do the same to her.{{book ref|Eltania}}

Improvising, Jak proclaimed to the Koloss that he already ''was'' a koloss and that he would prove it by recovering the Survivor's Treasure from the bottom of a lake. Before he went swimming, Elizandra kissed him, and passed him some tin powder. Afterwards, he dove down and managed to retrieve the treasure. They opened the box, and found a number of [[Hemalurgy|spikes]] inside. After leaving the Koloss, with no one attempting to stop them, Jak discovered that the box was made of [[aluminum]]. They soon returned upon realizing that they had forgotten Handerwym and left him behind.{{book ref|eltania}}

=== New Seran ===
{{image|Jak and Elizandra by Shuravf.jpg|side=right|width=200px|With [[Elizandra]]}}
During a later adventure, Jak found himself in New Seran, where Handerwym caught news that [[Cobbleguild criminals were going to replace the buttons of [[Spook]]'s favorite smoking jacket with forgeries. Deciding to put a stop to this, Jak and Handerwym went to an auction for the buttons being held by [[Lady Lavont]]. Handerwym was left to keep an eye on the buttons and make sure they were not swapped out with fakes while Jak went off to talk to Lady Lavont and her circle of friends. While doing so, a man pulled a gun on Jak, claiming he had killed his brother and that he wanted revenge. With little to no Tin left due to an impromptu wine drinking contest earlier in the evening, Jak, in an effort to buy time, proclaimed that he was unarmed and spun around in a circle to prove his point; then, realizing he did not recall killing the man's brother, he informed him that he was innocent. The man realized that he had mistaken Jak for [[Waxillium Ladrian]]. Jak laughed off the mistake and offered to buy the man a drink. Before he got the chance, however, Handerwym arrived and signaled to Jak that the buttons had been stolen, after which Jak went after the thieves to get them back.{{file ref|House Record broadsheet.jpg|''The House Record'' Broadsheet}}

At a later time, Jak made the acquaintance of [[Nicelle Sauvage]], who then started publishing under the Allomancer Jak brand.{{file ref|New Ascendancy broadsheet.jpg|''The New Ascendancy'' Broadsheet}} She would later serve as his assistant during his ventures, a time she considered among the strangest in her life.{{file ref|The Two Seasons broadsheet.jpg|''The Two Seasons'' Broadsheet}}

=== Breakup with Handerwym ===
In the years following the discovery of the [[Bands of Mourning]], Jak continued to publish his stories, and by happenstance discovered a large number of Survivor artifacts.{{book ref|mb7|32}} While expanding his business, Jak decided to invest in new forms of media entertainment. In order to fund these ventures, Jak took a stake out of Handerwym's ownership in the company. This led to a public falling-out between the two, with the judge ultimately siding with Handerwym. Jak continued to be published in ''[[Sentinel of Truth|The Sentinel of Truth]]'', while Handerwym kept exclusive access to ''[[The Two Seasons]]'' as well as other papers. Nicelle Sauvage continued under Handerwym's banner. Jak later spoke to the public, stating that, after teaching him the ways of greatness, he had always planned to cut Handerwym loose and watch him either sink or float.{{file ref|The Two Seasons broadsheet.jpg|''The Two Seasons'' Broadsheet}}

== Relationships ==
=== Waxillium Ladrian ===
[[Wax]], much like Jak, began his career in the Roughs working to collect money from the various bounties around the region. Jak was his elder in this profession by about six years; Wax began this career sometime around {{date|325|PC}}.{{book ref|mb5|prologue}} Despite this common connection, however, the two never got along during the early days. Wax himself states that he finds Jak's stories ridiculous and considers him a 'fop' rather than a true lawman,{{book ref|bom|11}} while Jak shows open disdain for Wax, stating that he would gladly look forward to shooting him if given the chance.{{file ref|House Record broadsheet.jpg|''The House Record'' Broadsheet}} The reason for this animosity is unclear; the pair likely never actually worked together, as neither ever mention working with the other; notably, Wax does not list Jak among the lawmen with whom he worked in the Roughs.

Later on, however, their relationship would slowly improve over time, at least on Jak's end. Following the discovery of the [[Bands of Mourning]] and the start of Wax's political career, Jak warmed considerably to the man and began mentioning him much more frequently in his writings, insisting that the pair were friends and comrades during past adventures. Wax himself denies this, and grows quite frustrated when the connection is mentioned; however, he willingly plays along with the story when needed to comfort someone or gain information.{{book ref|mb7|32}}

=== Handerwym ===
{{sidequote|I once mentioned to Jak that my people, the Terris, were at one time considered savages—at least according to the records given us by Harmony. He put a hand on my shoulder and said, “It is all right. I am proud to count a savage as my friend.” He was so sincere, I dared not explain just how insulting he was being.|Handerwym{{book ref|eltania}}|side=right|size=300px}}

Jak met Handerwym in the Roughs, after saving him from drowning in a lake.{{file ref|Elendel Daily broadsheet.png|''The Elendel Daily'' Broadsheet}} Afterwards, Handerwym decided to join Jak in his adventures, and would take on the role of his assistant and companion. He reads Jack’s writings and corrects mistakes in them, as well as writing humorous and biting annotations. He is at times annoyed by the fact that Jak does little in the way of true research or double-checking his accounts for accuracy. Handerwym also displays a large amount of doubt about his more outlandish claims. Despite this, he is fond of Jak, and Handerwym has saved his life many times.{{book ref|Eltania}}

Jak himself is also fond of Handerwym. He considers Handerwym to be his faithful servant. He can be somewhat forgetful of Handerwym however, such as forgetting and leaving him at the Pits of Eltania. He is also a little ignorant of Handerwym’s Terris heritage, and has at times insulted him because of this.{{book ref|eltania}} The two had a falling out after Jak used some of Handerwym’s stakes in the company to invest. The two went to court, after which a judge ruled in favour of Handerwym. This had a large impact on Jak’s publishing, although he later stated that it was always his plan to cut Handerwym loose and watch him either fail or become successful.{{file ref|The Two Seasons broadsheet.jpg|''The Two Seasons'' Broadsheet}}

=== Elizandra ===
Jak also has a girlfriend, named Elizandra Dramali. He originally believed her to be a beautiful and witty noblewoman, but eventually discovered that she was koloss-blooded. He forgave her for lying to him however, confessing that he still loved her. They have traveled together for a long time, and been on many adventures together.{{book ref|eltania}}

== Publishing ==
{{image|Allomancer Jak presents.png|side=right|width=250px}}
Jak's sensationalized adventures are published in ''[[The Elendel Daily]]''{{file ref|Elendel Daily broadsheet.png|''The Elendel Daily'' Broadsheet}} and ''[[The House Record]]''.{{file ref|House Record broadsheet.jpg|''The House Record'' Broadsheet}} In the House Record, he insults other newspapers and claims the House Record is superior.{{file ref|House Record broadsheet.jpg|''The House Record'' Broadsheet}} These versions are unedited by Handerwym, and as a result are terribly written. Handerwym then takes several episodes and combines them into one volume that he edits and annotates. The annotations generally consist of humorous insults and corrections.{{book ref|eltania}} A story about [[Nicelle Sauvage]] in ''[[The New Ascendancy]]'' is published under an "Allomancer Jak presents" logo,{{file ref|New Ascendancy broadsheet.jpg|''The New Ascendancy'' Broadsheet}} suggesting that Jak may help publish the stories of other adventurers.

Jak has published various series, notably ''Allomancer Jak and the Mask of Ages'',{{book ref|eltania}} ''Allomancer Jak and the Waters of Dread'',{{book ref|eltania}} ''Allomancer Jak and the Pits of Eltania'',{{file ref|Elendel Daily broadsheet.png|''The Elendel Daily'' Broadsheet}}{{book ref|eltania}} and ''Gentleman Jak in the City of Fountains''.{{file ref|House Record broadsheet.jpg|''The House Record'' Broadsheet}} He often references previous adventures and assumes the reader is familiar with them, implying that he has published other unnamed series.{{file ref|House Record broadsheet.jpg|''The House Record'' Broadsheet}} After the discovery of the Bands of Mourning, and with Survivor fervor at a high point, Jak spent years discovering Survivor-related artifacts. During this time he also released the ''Survivor's Last Testament'' arc of his adventures in the ''Sentinel of Truth'' broadsheet.{{book ref|mb7|32}}

''Gentleman Jak in the City of Fountains'' is further known to have named episodes, with ''Part 5: A Sport of Spirits'' and ''Part 6: The Sinister Soiree''{{file ref|House Record broadsheet.jpg|''The House Record'' Broadsheet}} After leaving Handerwym, Jak announced to the public that he would soon be releasing vizbooks.{{file ref|The Two Seasons broadsheet.jpg|''The Two Seasons'' Broadsheet}} Many people in the Roughs also tell stories about Jak and his adventures.{{book ref|mb7|prologue}}

== Trivia ==
* Jak appears to be a [[Survivorist]], as he references the [[Survivor]] providing for him and regards the Survivor's teachings as true. However, he thinks his alleged spirit guide [[Lyndip]] may be a [[Faceless Immortal]] (which are part of [[Pathian]] doctrine as noted by [[Handerwym]]), so his theological depth appears to be limited.{{book ref|eltania}}
* [[Wayne's mother]] and [[Wayne]] himself both claim to be fans of Allomancer Jak and his stories, the latter claiming to have read all of his published adventures.{{book ref|mb7|prologue}}{{book ref|mb7|32}}
