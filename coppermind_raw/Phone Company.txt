The '''Phone Company''', commonly referred to as the '''PC''', was a corporate entity responsible for developing human [[cytonics]].{{book ref|Elysium}}

== History ==
In the year 2071 a failing phone company called Northern Bell Incorporated invested in developing [[cytonics|Cyto]], a cybernetic-based telepathic linking. The few signals that they managed to get through the system attracted the attention of the [[tenasi]]. When the United Governments military accidentally shot down the tenasi ambassadorial ship, the PC brokered the [[Tenasi Agreement]] in exchange for complete autonomy and a monopoly on FTL technology.{{book ref|Elysium}}
 
After negotiating the Tenasi Agreement, they were the only group of humans with cytonic capabilities or technology, and the de facto controllers of access to it. Additionally, aliens refused to deal with humanity except through the Phone Company.{{book ref|Elysium}}

Though the [[Superiority]] keeps humans contained and [[Earth (Skyward)|Earth]] has vanished,{{book ref|sky2|10}}{{book ref|sky2|18}} they managed to maintain their existence through the [[Human wars|Human Wars]].{{wob ref|11991}}

== Members ==
As of 2211, their chief operative was [[Jason Write]] and the head of their Central Operations was [[Lanna Write]]. Other members included [[Aaron]], [[Doran]], and [[Riely]].{{book ref|Elysium}}
