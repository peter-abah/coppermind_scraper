'''Deti''' is a [[Order of Windrunners|Windrunner]] [[squire]] to [[Sigzil]] on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa5|56}}

{{quote|It comes! The Night of Sorrows! I stand on the precipice of dawn and watch it advance, consuming all light, all life, all hope! IT COMES!|[[Death rattle]] spoken by Deti}}
Deti fought in the [[Second Battle of Narak]] alongside Sigzil. He complimented Sigzil's fighting skills and attributed them to [[Kaladin]]'s training.{{book ref|sa5|56}} He died on the battlefield, killed by a [[Heavenly One]] stabbing him with a [[Raysium]] lance. Sigzil desperately attempted to heal Deti, but was out of [[Stormlight]]. The death rattle he spoke revealed the presence of [[Moelach]] on the battlefield to Sigzil. Sigzil saw it as a failure, and realized the feeling he felt was exactly what Kaladin felt trying to save everyone on the [[bridge crew]]s.{{book ref|sa5|74}}
