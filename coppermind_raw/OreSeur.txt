'''OreSeur''' is a [[kandra]] from [[Scadrial]].{{book ref|mb1|5}}

His [[contract]] is with [[Kelsier]] at first. He mainly imitates Lord [[Renoux]] and keeps an eye on [[Vin]] while she acts as Valette Renoux. Upon Kelsier's death, his contract is passed to [[Vin]]. She is angry with him because he knew of Kelsier's plan to die and inspire the [[skaa]] to fight, but did not stop it, though OreSeur only acted in accordance with his contract. Eventually, Vin’s disgust at the idea of a kandra eating humans drives her to order OreSeur to ingest the bones of a wolfhound instead. Mid-transformation, he is secretly killed and replaced by [[TenSoon]].{{book ref|mb2|20}}

His contract is assigned to [[Kelsier]] by [[KanPaar]] even though it would be to act against the [[Lord Ruler]], due to the high price Kelsier was willing to pay for it.{{book ref|mb2|11}} Being a rather rebellious member of the Third Generation, OreSeur is one of the few kandra willing to accept this job, and he leaves the [[Kandra Homeland]] after a long period of retirement to do it.{{wob ref|7514}}

Like [[TenSoon]], he is of the third generation of Kandra and was thus over 700 years old at the time of his death.{{book ref|mb2|11}}

== Trivia ==
*Brandon wanted to write a short story from OreSeur's perspective to explore the kandra's motives for taking Kelsier's contract, as well as his feelings after [[Rashek]] was killed.{{wob ref|7514}}
