The '''Bay of Elibath''' is a body of water north of [[Alethkar]] on [[Roshar]].{{map ref|Alethkar}}

The Bay of Elibath separates Alethkar from the northwest peninsula of [[Herdaz]] and the [[Reshi]] island of [[Akak]].{{map ref|Alethkar}} It borders the princedoms of [[Sadeas princedom|Sadeas]], [[Aladar princedom|Aladar]], and [[Roion princedom|Roion]]. The bay feeds into the [[Steamwater Ocean]].{{map ref|Roshar}} The Alethi port of [[Dalilak]] sits on the Bay of Elibath.{{map ref|Alethkar}}

During the [[Heraldic Epochs]], the bay bordered both [[Rishir]] and [[Alethela]].{{map ref|Silver Kingdoms}}
