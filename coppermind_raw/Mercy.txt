'''Mercy''' is a [[Shard]] of [[Adonalsium]] that has not been [[Splinter#Formation|Splintered]] and whose Vessel is unknown.{{epigraph ref|sa4|25}}

== History ==
Mercy was created at the [[Shattering of Adonalsium]] and picked up by an unknown Vessel.{{msh ref|3|2}} It is not known what Mercy did directly after their Ascension, or where they settled, if they settled at all.

Mercy was involved in a battle between [[Odium]] and [[Ambition]], which resulted in a wound upon the [[Spiritual Realm]] and ultimately in Ambition's Splintering. This clash had profound effects on [[Threnody]].{{epigraph ref|sa4|27}} 

Some time around the [[True Desolation]], [[Harmony]] reached out to Mercy about the threat posed by Odium, at [[Hoid]]'s request, but became troubled by Mercy as a result of their exchange.{{epigraph ref|sa4|25}}{{epigraph ref|sa4|22}}

== Trivia ==
* Before [[Aona]]'s Shard was known (and well before it was known that Mercy is a Shard), fans speculated that its Intent might be Mercy.{{wob ref|4228}} It was later revealed Aona's Shard is Devotion.{{wob ref|10084}}{{wob ref|783}}
