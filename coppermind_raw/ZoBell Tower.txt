'''ZoBell Tower''' is a skyscraper in the Fourth Octant of [[Elendel]] on [[Scadrial]].{{book ref|mb5|8}}  

The tower is around twenty stories tall and has decorative stonework and balconies. Like many sky scrapers in the city, it narrows as it rises and is built with steel girders. It has a coach portico at the entrance.{{book ref|mb5|8}} There is also a green in front of the tower.{{book ref|mb5|12}}

The entire top floor is an open penthouse ringed with windows, at least some of which have balconies. It is lit by crystal chandeliers and has red carpeting. During [[Lady ZoBell's party]] in 342 [[Catacendre|PC]], it is decorated with small display stands with glass boxes containing impressive specimens of [[Allomantic]] metals.{{book ref|mb5|9}} [[Waxillium Ladrian]] tests out [[Ranette]]'s grappling hook [[Ranette#Notable Inventions|invention]] while ascending the tower prior to the party.{{book ref|mb5|8}}
