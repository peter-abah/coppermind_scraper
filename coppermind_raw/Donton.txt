'''Donton''' is the minister of public affairs in [[Elendel]] and a member of Governor [[Replar Innate]]'s executive staff.{{book ref|mb5|18}} 

In a meeting attended by [[Marasi]], Donton suggested that [[House Lekal]] could have been responsible for the assassination attempt on Innate, but the sanitation director and secretary of education quickly dismissed this theory.{{book ref|mb5|18}}
