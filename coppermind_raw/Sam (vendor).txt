'''Sam''' is a hotdog vendor who owns a hotdog stand in [[Newcago]]. After [[Steelheart (Epic)|Steelheart]]'s defeat, Sam opens up his stand again, though he doesn't get much business. He serves his hot dogs classic Chicago style, loaded with every topping except for ketchup. [[David]] and [[Abraham]] visit his stand and order hot dogs, and Sam gives them half off because of their service to the city.{{book ref|mitosis|1}}
