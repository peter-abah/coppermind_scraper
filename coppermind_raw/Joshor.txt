'''Joshor''' is the head of King [[Taravangian]]'s [[Silent Gatherer]]s on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa1|endnote}}

==Attributes and Abilities==
Joshor seems to be a relatively high-ranking member of Taravangian's team; he oversees research that is thought to be very important to as a supplement to the [[Diagram (literature)|Diagram]].{{book ref|sa2|i|14}} He also exhibits direct knowledge of Taravangian's variable intelligence.{{book ref|sa1|endnote}}

He is knowledgeable regarding literature and mythology that would help him interpret the typical contents of Death Rattles, including [[ketek]]s and other forms of [[Vorin]] poetry.{{book ref|sa1|endnote}}

==History==
As head of the Silent Gatherers, Joshor's primary focus was on collecting and analyzing [[Death Rattle]]s.{{book ref|sa1|endnote}} He likely had oversight of other members of the [[Diagram (group)|Diagram]] that were working on the same task, such as [[Dova]].

Taravangian attributed Death Rattles to the presence of [[Moelach]]. After Death Rattles suddenly became very rare near Kharbranth, he assumed that Moelach had moved westward.{{book ref|sa2|i|14}} [[Adrotagia]] later reported that Moelach was thought to have settled in the [[Horneater Peaks]], and Joshor was sent to investigate.{{book ref|sa3|107}}
