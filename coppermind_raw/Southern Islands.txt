The '''Southern Islands''' are a series of isles between the [[Sea of Yomend]] and [[Sea of Lennes]], once known as the [[Southern Sea]].{{map ref|Final Empire 1021|the Final Empire}}{{map ref|Scadrial}} Very little is known about them.

During the time of the Final Empire, it had several settlements on it,{{map ref|Final Empire 1021|the Final Empire}} and at least one nation existed on it during Classical Scadrial.{{epigraph ref|mb1|12}}

The [[Bennet]] used to live on the Southern Islands. They were a highly developed people and were famous for being brave seafarers and brilliant cartographers. Some of the maps the Final Empire used were developed by Bennet explorers. Their belief was the [[Truths of the Bennet]].{{book ref|mb1|29}}

They have an unnamed mountain range running down their length.{{map ref|Final Empire 1021|the Final Empire}}{{map ref|Scadrial}}
