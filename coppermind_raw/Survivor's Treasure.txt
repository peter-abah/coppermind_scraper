The '''Survivor's Treasure''' is a cache of [[iron]] spikes in the [[Pits of Eltania]] on [[Scadrial]].{{book ref|eltania}}

== Legend ==
The treasure was said to have been placed by the Survivor ([[Kelsier]]) when he stopped for a drink after being stabbed by the [[Lord Ruler]]. The treasure is "hidden opposite the sky" and can be raised only by life itself. The legends also state that the location is marked by the symbol of a spearhead and a set of bloody footprints left by the wounded Survivor.{{book ref|eltania}} Even [[Allomancer Jak]] admits that these stories must be partially invented.{{book ref|eltania}}

== History ==
During his adventures in the [[Roughs]], Jak sought the treasure for many months, noting that its discovery was the dying wish of his friend [[Mikaff]]. After being taken captive by [[koloss]], he scared away their chief, killed their champion, and briefly became their "king".{{file ref|Elendel Daily broadsheet.png|''The Elendel Daily'' broadsheet}}{{book ref|eltania}} However, the koloss did not want a human as their leader; after a few days, he was placed in small natural cave with no obvious means of exit. He was able to escape by jumping into an adjacent river, but was quickly recaptured. He was then taken to a small, hidden natural spring in an oasis not marked on any maps. The area was a holy site for the koloss, and several respected elders were present. The oasis held an extremely deep pool with stone walls, shaped like an arrowhead. Jak also noticed that water dripping onto the nearby rocks resembled bloody footprints. All of the signs aligned with the legends surrounding the Pits of Eltania.{{book ref|eltania}}

At this point, [[Elizandra]] revealed that she was koloss-blooded and that Jak had unwittingly passed all of the tests that the koloss had presented to him. They intended to use [[Hemalurgy]] to transform both Jak and Elizandra into full koloss. Jak suddenly claimed he was already koloss, and would prove it by retrieving the Survivor's Treasure. Using [[tin]] that Elizandra passed to him through a kiss, he recovered a strongbox located 18.3 fathoms (110 feet or 33.5 meters) deep in the waters of the pool. According to his account, the box had a latch on it that released what appeared to be a bladder, into which he blew air from his lungs, lifting the treasure to the surface ("raising it with life", per the legends). The box was opened with a key that Jak had found in [[Maelstrom's mine]], and it contained enough iron spikes to create dozens of koloss. The koloss were so excited by this discovery that Jak and Elizandra were able to slip away with the empty box, soon realizing that it was worth a small fortune since it was made of pure [[aluminum]].{{book ref|eltania}}

== Authenticity ==
Since Kelsier died centuries before Jak's discovery, the actual provenance of the treasure is unclear. It is unknown who placed it or how the legends surrounding the Survivor began. The story of its discovery is also obfuscated by Jak's fanciful writing style. However, [[Handerwym]] confirms most of the salient points of Jak's narrative, including his interactions with the koloss, the location and shape of the pool, and the recovery and nature of the "treasure". The fact that the koloss revered the area and kept it secret also lends credence to the legends being rooted in actual events.{{book ref|eltania}} 

== Speculation ==
{{theory}}
It is likely that whomever created the box used aluminum in order to hide the spikes from those who would otherwise able to sense them, such as some Allomancers and [[Ruin]].

The attribution of the treasure to Kelsier is reasonable, although it is not confirmed. He is one of perhaps a few individuals in the history of Scadrial who could have had access to a large number of Hemalurgic spikes, plus the motivation and resources (including a large amount of aluminum) to keep them hidden. He is known to have influenced events on Scadrial as a [[Cognitive Shadow]] after his death.{{msh ref|epilogue}} He is aware that koloss have value in battle,{{book ref|mb1|6}} and he also knows how koloss are made.{{msh ref|6|2}} He has a Hemalurgic spike through his own eye, and he was able to return to Scadrial in the [[Physical Realm]].{{book ref|mb6|epilogue}} Although it is unlikely that Kelsier placed the box in the pool immediately after his fight with the Lord Ruler, it is possible that he placed it there at some other point.
