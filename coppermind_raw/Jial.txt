'''Jial''' is a [[singer]] on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa4|74}}

She was part of the group of singers led by [[Venli]] who hoped to escape the [[Fused]]. She was present in [[Urithiru]] during the singer occupation. After Venli got a map of the tunnels underneath the tower from [[Lirin]], she gave it to Jial.{{book ref|sa4|74}} After Urithiru was liberated, Jial presumably traveled to the [[Shattered Plains]] with the rest of Venli's group.{{book ref|sa4|114}}
