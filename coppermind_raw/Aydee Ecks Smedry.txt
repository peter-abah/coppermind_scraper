'''Aydee Ecks Smedry''' is a [[Mokia|Mokian]] [[House Smedry|Smedry]] whose [[Smedry Talent|Talent]] is that she is very bad at math.{{book ref|a4|42}} She is the younger sister to [[Sing Sing Smedry|Sing]] and [[Australia Smedry|Australia]] Smedry.{{book ref|a4|4 1/2}}

== Appearance and Personality ==
Aydee is an eight-year-old Mokian girl with short dark hair and brown skin. She wears a colorful dress and a flower in her hair.{{book ref|a4|4 1/2}}

Like her older sister Australia, Aydee is cheerful, bubbly, and somewhat naive. However, she is shown to be cleverer than she initially appears, having figured out how to manipulate her Talent to her advantage with a degree of control not shown in many older Smedrys.{{book ref|a4|∞}} She also displays something of a mischievous streak.

== Attributes and Abilities ==
Aydee has the Talent of being really bad at math, which changes reality to match her perception of how many things she thinks there are of whatever she is adding.{{book ref|a4|42}} 

Later, Alcatraz uses her Talent to increase the number of [[teddy bear]] grenades they have in their arsenal.{{book ref|a4|70706}} Though she knows that the equation he gives to tell her how many bears they have is fake, she feigns ignorance so that her Talent will work.{{book ref|a4|∞}}

Aydee is also a capable pilot, like her sister Australia.{{book ref|a4|4 1/2}}

== History ==
Aydee was sent away from the [[Siege of Mokia]] to deliver a piece of [[Communicator's Glass]] to the [[Nalhalla]]n embassy.{{book ref|a4|42}} When she arrived, she was conscripted by [[Kazan Smedry]] to fly Kaz, [[Alcatraz]], and [[Bastille]] back to Mokia.{{book ref|a4|4 1/2}} Her Talent proved essential to Mokia's defense and the eventual defeat of the [[Librarian]]s there.
