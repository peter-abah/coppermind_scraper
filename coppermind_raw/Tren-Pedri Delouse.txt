Lady '''Tren-Pedri Delouse''' is a [[noble]] in [[Luthadel]].{{book ref|mb1|28}}

Her father wished to form an alliance with [[House Aime]] by marrying her off to one of the head of House Aime's sons. [[Vin]] spread rumors through [[Kliss]] that Tren-Pedri's hesitance was because of Aime's ties to [[House Hasting]], and with Hasting's fictitious withdrawal from [[Luthadel]], Tren-Pedri wouldn't want to associate with them.{{book ref|mb1|28}}
