'''Ker Kedasha''' is the capital city of [[Kerzta]] on [[Taldain]].{{map ref|Dayside}}

It's located in the south western portion of the [[Dayside]] continent, to the west of the [[Lossand]]in capital of [[Kezare]].{{map ref|Dayside}} It's the seat of power of the king of Kerzta, who is elected there every fifty years by a gathering of Kerztian [[Daikeen]] nobility, the [[Kli]]n.{{book ref|ws|3}}

The current king is of the Merchant Daikeen, who has held the monarchy for the last 200 years.{{book ref|ws|3}} The [[A'kar]], the Kerztian high priest, might also live there.
