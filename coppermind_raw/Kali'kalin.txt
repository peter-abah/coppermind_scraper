'''Kali'kalin''' is an [[Unkalaki]] person or deity on [[Roshar]]. The Horneaters believe that he has a ghost. The Unkalaki sometimes curse with the name of this ghost, simply referring to it as Kali'kalin's ghost.{{book ref|SA1|55}} This is likely associated with a similar Horneater expression, Kali'kalin'da.{{book ref|sa3|37}}
