'''Moshe Feder''' was [[Brandon Sanderson]]'s editor at [[Tor]] for most of his books. He retired sometime after the publication of ''[[Oathbringer]]''. ''[[Rhythm of War]]'' is the first [[cosmere]] novel Brandon has published that was not edited by Moshe.{{book ref|row|preface}}
