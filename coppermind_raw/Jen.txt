'''Jen''' is a medieval historian and [[Runian]]'s former partner.{{book ref|frugal|34}}

== History ==
Jen, John, and [[Ryan Chu]] were friends at least as early as high school. During that time, Jen quickly found an interest in history.{{book ref|frugal|27}}

At some point, Jen began dating John. Two weeks later, she started secretly cheating on John with Ryan.{{book ref|frugal|34}} Jen and John often argued during their relationship.{{book ref|frugal|30}}

Eventually, Jen infiltrated [[Ulric Stromfin]]'s organization, using her knowledge as a medievalist to involve herself with his dimension projects. During her infiltration, she pretended be on a trip in Europe{{book ref|frugal|11}} and let John think she had died in an accident.{{book ref|frugal|34}}

When the [[hordamen]] began their attack on [[Maelport]], Jen escaped with Ryan through the dimensional portal to the police department headquarters.{{book ref|frugal|34}}
