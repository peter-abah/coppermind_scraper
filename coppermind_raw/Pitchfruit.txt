'''Pitchfruit''' is a type of fruit from [[ReDawn (planet)|ReDawn]]. [[Alanik]] compares a fruit eaten on [[Detritus]] to it, noting that both have a satisfying crush, though the fruit from Detritus has more of a brisk taste and is nowhere near as sweet as pitchfruit.{{book ref|sf2|5}}
