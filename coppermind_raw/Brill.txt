'''Brill''' is a construction worker in [[Elendel]] on [[Scadrial]]. He works as a line riveter and is a member of the [[United Tradeworkers Union]]. When he learned of [[Elors Durnsed]]'s apparent abandonment of his duties as union chief, he and many of his colleagues became extremely angry, nearly to the point of violence.{{file ref|Elendel_Daily_broadsheet.png|''The Elendel Daily'' broadsheet}}
