'''Dorgo''' is a member of the groundcrew at [[Alta Base]].{{book ref|skyward|32}}{{book ref|skyward|49}}{{book ref|skyward|50}} He is a kind person{{book ref|sky1|49}} and well knowledgeable about the workings of the [[DDF]]'s aircrafts.{{book ref|sky1|32}}

He is familiar with the pilots of Alta Base, knowing [[Spensa]] by sight, and knowing detail about Callsign: [[Vigor]]'s history in the DDF.{{book ref|sky1|32}} He often works with [[Skyward Flight]], and with Spensa's [[Poco]] in particular.{{book ref|sky1|49}} He's part of the team responsible for dismantling Skyward Five, [[Arturo]]'s ship, after it was severely damaged.{{book ref|sky1|49}}

Dorgo has some level of authority, giving the go ahead to allow Spensa to take off in Arturo's damaged ship during the [[Second Battle of Alta]], despite no official orders from [[Ironsides]]. Before she takes off, Dorgo hands Spensa the helmet with her callsign, indicating that someone in the ground crew had taken the time to find her specific helmet, despite the fact that she had been dismissed from the DDF.{{book ref|sky1|49}}
