'''Abrobadar''' is a [[Shardbearer]] in [[Shattered Plains|Aladar's warcamp]] on the Shattered Plains. He is one of the more skilled duelists in the warcamps.{{book ref|sa2|56}}

He is present during the meeting when [[Shallan]] arrives on the Plains. He is amongst the task force standing guard in case [[Szeth]] attacks, along with [[General Khal]], [[Jakamav]], [[Resi]], and [[Relis]].{{book ref|sa2|37}}

He is also one of the four opponents against [[Adolin]], and later [[Renarin]]. He distracts Renarin when he is distracted for a while and is distracted for a time by [[Pattern]]. He rejoined the main fight after [[Kaladin]] begins to turn the tide of the duel. He is defeated by Adolin while Kaladin is fighting [[Jakamav]] and [[Relis]].{{book ref|sa2|56}}{{book ref|sa2|57}}

His plate is orange, and is later given to [[Moash]] by Adolin at the request of [[Kaladin]].{{book ref|sa2|66}}
