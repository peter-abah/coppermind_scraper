'''Udal nuts''' are a type of food common on [[ReDawn (planet)|ReDawn]]. They grow on bushy plants that live in the crevices of the branches of ReDawn’s giant trees. They taste quite good, and are used in food bars, which last a long time without spoiling.{{book ref|sf2|15}} They are not toxic to humans for the most part, though eating too many can cause flatulence.{{book ref|sf2|17}} [[Nedd]] enjoys them greatly{{book ref|sf2|15}}, though [[Kimmalyn]] prefers to dip them in custard.{{book ref|Sf2|16}}
