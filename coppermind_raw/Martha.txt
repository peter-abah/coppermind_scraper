'''Martha''' is an employee of [[Havendark Factory]] in [[Newcago]]. She oversees children used for labor to build weapons for [[Enforcement]] at the Factory, including [[David Charleston]] and [[Roy]].

==History==
David recalls Martha as being a benevolent person, despite the fact that she was charged with supervising child labor with some kids being as young as nine years old.{{book ref|steelheart|8}}{{book ref|steelheart|1}} She ensured that the children had their basic needs met and did not allow violence between them;{{book ref|steelheart|8}} she had a system of assigning older kids to be "adjuncts" who would help protect younger or weaker workers.{{book ref|steelheart|10}} She saved the wages that they earned through their work and gave them to each child when they turned eighteen and were forced to leave Factory work.{{book ref|steelheart|8}}{{book ref|steelheart|9}} She even offered counsel to the kids, and was among the people who warned David that his desire for vengeance on [[Steelheart]] was not healthy.{{book ref|steelheart|20}}

David noted that Martha allowed the children to learn how to use the guns they were making, even though it was a violation of Factory policy. Several of the kids who worked for Martha used this knowledge to later join Enforcement, while David's knowledge of guns helped him as a member of the [[Reckoners]].{{book ref|steelheart|8}}
