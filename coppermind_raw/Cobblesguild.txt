The '''Cobblesguild''' is a gang in [[New Seran]] on the planet [[Scadrial]]. Members of the Cobblesguild are called Cobblesguilders. The leader of the Cobblesguild tried to steal the [[Tin]] buttons on the [[Spook|Lord Mistborn]]'s favorite smoking jacket during an auction held by [[Lady Lavont]]. He succeeded in doing this, although he was pursued by Gentlemen Jak and his [[Handerwym|assistant]].{{file ref|House Record broadsheet.jpg|''The House Record'' Broadsheet}}
