'''Dai-Gonarthis''' is one of the [[Unmade]],{{book ref|sa5|i|3}} an ancient and terrible [[spren]] of [[Odium]].{{book ref|sa3|62}} She is also known as the '''Black Fisher'''.{{book ref|sa5|i|3}}

== Appearance and Personality ==
Dai-Gonarthis wishes to break and burn the world.{{book ref|sa5|67}} Even Odium considers her dangerous to unleash and, at the time of the [[True Desolation]], the spren thought he would never do so again.{{book ref|sa5|i|3}}{{book ref|sa5|67}}

== Attributes and Abilities ==
Dai-Gonarthis can utilize [[Connection]] and the Surge of Transportation to create [[Surgebinding#Transportation|Elsegate]]s, though only between two locations that have a high concentration of [[Investiture]].{{rpg ref|slwg|97}} At least prior to the [[Night of Sorrows]], most places controlled by the [[coalition of monarchs]] were protected from her touch, though the lands of the former [[Silver Kingdom]] of [[Natanatan]] were not.{{book ref|sa5|i|3}} Her Elsegates manifest as a circle of violet light that extends into the ground like a pit or tunnel. Those traveling through one of her Elsegates appear to jump out of the exit, often with enough momentum that they need someone to help stabilize them when they land.{{book ref|sa5|67}} Using this ability of hers has a cost, though not one that needs to be paid immediately.{{book ref|sa5|i|3}}

Like many of the Unmade, Dai-Gonarthis also appears to have some ability to cause emotional pain and suffering, as [[Jezrien]] wonders if she was responsible for the pain [[Dalinar Kholin]] felt after he burned [[Rathalas]] and killed [[Evi Kholin]].{{book ref|sa3|88}} One [[Death Rattle]] implies that she has the ability to take a person's sorrow and consume it,{{epigraph ref|sa1|67}} which may work similarly to how Odium took [[Moash]]'s emotions.

== History ==
[[Hessi]] posits in ''[[Mythica]]'' that Dai-Gonarthis may have been responsible for the [[scouring of Aimia]], although her sources are limited and she is unsure whether Dai-Gonarthis is one of the Unmade at all.{{epigraph ref|sa3|113}}

{{quote
|So outside of reason that it was barely worth consideration
|[[Vienta]] on Odium's decision to empower Dai-Gonarthis again{{book ref|sa5|67}}

Shortly after his Ascension to Odium, [[Taravangian]] met with the [[Fused]] [[El]] and asked him to develop a plan for bringing more of Odium's forces to the [[Shattered Plains]]. After confirming that Odium was willing to pay a significant price to do so, El responded that, without an allied [[Order of Elsecallers|Elsecaller]] or [[Battar]]'s [[Honorblade]], an Elsegate from Dai-Gonarthis was the only way to get more forces to the Shattered Plains in time.{{book ref|sa5|i|3}} Odium then instructed El to travel to the [[Horneater Peaks]], where Dai-Gonarthis would be able to Connect [[Cultivation's Perpendicularity]] to the [[Well of Control]].{{book ref|sa5|i|3}}{{book ref|sa5|90}} El then led an army of hundreds of [[Regal]]s through the Elsegate to join the attack on [[Narak]].{{book ref|sa5|67}}
