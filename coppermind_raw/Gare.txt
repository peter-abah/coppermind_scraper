'''Gare''' is a squadleader in Highmarshal [[Meridas Amaram]]'s army on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa1|47}}

== Appearance & Personality ==
Gare is a beefy man, with a thick black beard.{{book ref|sa1|47}} He fights with an axe rather than a spear, and is missing a section of his scalp on the right side of his head, presumably lost in combat. Kaladin considered Gare so incompetent that he might as well be fighting for the other army.{{book ref|sa1|1}}

Gare is a stubborn and belligerent man.{{book ref|sa1|47}} He does not enjoy taking Kaladin's bribes because he does not like how everyone listens to Kaladin all the time. However, it seems that his greed is stronger than his anger, as he eventually accepts the bribe.

== History ==
At some point Gare became a squadleader in Amaram's army. He appointed [[Nalem]] and [[Korabet]] to be his sergeants, and they often accompanied him around.{{book ref|sa1|47}}

In 1172, [[Cenn]] was assigned to Gare's squad when he joined Amaram's army as a new recruit.{{book ref|sa1|47}}{{book ref|sa1|1}} [[Kaladin]] found Gare and bribed him with some [[spheres]] to transfer Cenn to his squad.
