'''Diren''' is an [[Elantrian]] on [[Sel]].{{book ref|Elantris|18}}

Prior to his [[Shaod]] he worked on [[Aor Plantation]]. After about eight months he is brought to [[Hrathen]] to be questioned so he can learn about Elantris. After this he is taken by [[Dilaf]], who cuts his face deeply several times, leading to Diren losing his sanity.{{book ref|Elantris|18}}

He is nearly executed by [[Dilaf]] during a congregation, which is stopped by [[Hrathen]] to ensure that the crowd does not enter an uncontrollable zealous fervor. Diren is then put out of his misery by [[Hrathen]] personally.{{book ref|Elantris|18}}
