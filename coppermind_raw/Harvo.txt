'''Harvo''' is a [[singer]] on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa4|114}}

Harvo is one of the [[listener]]s and survived at least most of the [[War of Reckoning]]. When [[Venli]] left [[Urithiru]] to find to the listeners, [[Rlain]] asked her to give his regards to Harvo and [[Thude]] if they were still alive.{{book ref|sa4|114}}
