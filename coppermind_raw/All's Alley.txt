'''All's Alley''' is a dive bar in [[Urithiru]] owned by [[Sullik]].{{book ref|sa3|18}} A number of [[lighteyed]] thugs, including [[Betha]] and [[Ur (character)|Ur]], provide protection for the bar.{{book ref|sa3|18}} It is frequented by disreputable soldiers and craftsmen.{{book ref|sa3|18}}

== Description ==
{{quote
|Both richer than the other tavern and lower at the same time.
|A description of All's Alley{{book ref|sa3|18}}
}}
Located in the [[Breakaway]], All's Alley is a big set of five tents sewn together, each a different color.{{book ref|sa3|18}} The tents are sewn to create partitioned-off areas and darkened nooks with a few containing tables and chairs instead of boxes.{{book ref|sa3|18}} According to [[Navani]]'s original layout for the Breakaway, the bar should be located in section two, in the central corridor near the lifts.{{book ref|sa3|2}} The tavern is not set in an alley.{{book ref|sa3|18}}

The “bar” is a plank set across some boxes, with a draped cloth over the middle.{{book ref|sa3|18}} The bar stocks both single barrel [[Veden saph]] and [[Horneater white]].{{book ref|sa3|18}} The Horneater white is kept as a joke and not intended to be sold to customers.{{book ref|sa3|18}} The pub smells worse than other establishments and while oil lamps are used for light, the bar is still quite dim.{{book ref|sa3|18}}

A bouncer guards the entrance to the bar, however, does not stop patrons from entering.{{book ref|sa3|18}} Hired thugs also provide protection for the bar and deal with issues when they arise.{{book ref|sa3|18}} Sullik is the bartender and has little tolerance for people who are likely to cause trouble in his bar.{{book ref|sa3|18}}

The murders that occurred near the bar do not seem to have affected overall turnover with the pub being packed with people when visited by [[Shallan]].{{book ref|sa3|18}}

== History ==
It is unknown whether All's Alley had existed in the warcamps before they were evacuated by a large majority of the [[Alethi]] army. After moving to Urithiru following the [[Everstorm]], Sullik set up All's Alley in the Breakaway. Not long after doing so [[Ned]], a drunk from Sadeas's camp, strangled his wife [[Rem]] just outside the bar. The next day one of the barmaids was also killed in the same way. Ur one of the hired protectors of All's Alley dropped [[Ned]] off the plateau for the death of the barmaid.{{book ref|sa3|18}} 

Shallan learned of the murder while visiting bars in the Breakaway, trying to find out information that may relate to the death of Sadeas. Sullik  refused to tell Shallan details of the murders, however, pointed her towards his hired thugs, who were drinking in another part of the bar. After speaking with the thugs, and injuring Ur, Shallan learned of Rem's death, as well as the second murder. It is during this encounter that Shallan first meets [[Ishnah]], who trails Shallan later in an attempt to join the [[Ghostbloods]].{{book ref|sa3|18}}{{book ref|sa3|25}}{{book ref|sa3|40}} Shallan later realized that the barmaid had been killed by the [[Unmade]] [[Re-Shephir]], who had copied the initial murder of Rem.{{book ref|sa3|32}} 

Later, [[Rock]] was attacked by Re-Shephir, who mimicked Veil's attack on Ur, several streets away from the bar.{{book ref|sa3|25}}
