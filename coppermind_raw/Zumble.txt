A '''zumble''' is a type of large herbivorous crustacean living in the east of [[Roshar]]. They can easily reach a height of twenty feet, and they have bulbous black carapace. Some zumble live on or around the [[Shattered Plains]], and they are at times hunted by [[chasmfiend]]s. In {{rosharan date|1175}}, the [[listener]]s at the Shattered Plains used zumble as a food source when hunted by their chasmfiends.{{book ref|sa5|55}}
