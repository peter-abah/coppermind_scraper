'''Hannerdam''' is a [[Malwish Empire|Malwish]] city on [[Scadrial]]. Just outside this "great city" stands the Hannerdam Bridge. The bridge was a bold engineering project that was supposed to greatly speed up traffic in and out of the city. However, not long after its completion, the bridge collapsed. This disaster is considered to be the engineers' fault, as they didn’t bind the nuts tightly enough, forgot the washers, and didn’t double check their work. Additionally, the lead engineer was fired halfway through the project for spending too much.{{book ref|iote|38}}
