'''Rembrinor''' is a swordmaster from [[Alethkar]] on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa3|16}}

[[Dalinar Kholin]] asked [[Kadash]] if he had ever told him the story of his first sword trainer.{{book ref|sa3|16}} Kadash guessed that it was Rembrinor, but it was actually [[Harth]].{{book ref|sa3|16}} Rembrinor may be an [[ardent]], as Dalinar alludes to additional sword training that he later received at monasteries on Kholin land.{{book ref|sa3|16}}
