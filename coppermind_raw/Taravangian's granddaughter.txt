'''Taravangian's granddaughter''' is a member of the [[Kharbranthian]] royal family on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa1|5}}

She is quite young, below the age that a modesty sleeve is required for the sake of propriety and is attended by several nursemaids.{{book ref|sa1|5}} 

== History ==
Following a [[highstorm]] in {{Rosharan date|1174}} Taravangian's granddaughter, along with her nursemaids and several palace servants, became trapped after a rockslide cut off the entrance to the room they were in inside the [[Conclave]]. The young girl was rescued by [[Jasnah Kholin]] after [[Taravangian|King Taravangian]] enlisted the [[Alethi]] princess to [[Soulcast]] the rock that was blocking the doorway to the room.{{book ref|sa1|5}} 
 
Following the incident, the young girl recovered well, though she developed a tendency to avoid the narrower passages in the Conclave.{{book ref|sa1|29}} 

Later during the year, she was gifted a portrait of the king by her grandfather, drawn by [[Shallan Davar]].{{book ref|sa1|29}}

== Relationships ==
=== Taravangian ===
In the hopes that he could witness Jasnah using her Soulcaster, Taravangian deliberately had his granddaughter trapped via a cave-in following the highstorm.{{wob ref|8070}} Despite his ulterior motives, he was concerned for his granddaughter's wellbeing and did not want her to be harmed, even though he put her in a dangerous situation.{{wob ref|3242}}

== Trivia ==
* [[Gvori]], [[Karavangia]], and [[Ruli]] are different grandchildren to the one who Taravangian had trapped by the rockfall.{{wob ref|11637}}
* It is unknown if this grandchild is a sibling or cousin of Taravangian's other grandchildren.{{wob ref|11590}}
