'''Vespan''' is an [[honorspren]] on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa4|111}}

Dalinar found his corpse in a tent used by [[Ishar]] to conduct experiments on bringing spren to the [[Physical Realm]]. The [[Stormfather]] stated to Dalinar that he had known Vespan.{{book ref|sa4|111}}

Ishar noted that Vespan lived nearly fifteen minutes after the transfer, much longer than other spren, and believed this was because honorspren have "the most humanlike essences." Ishar's notes indicated he intended to capture more of them,{{book ref|sa4|111}} which would explain the attempted capture of [[Notum]] by [[Tukari caravaneers]] in [[Shadesmar]].{{book ref|sa4|35}}
