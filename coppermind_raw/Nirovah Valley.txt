The '''Nirovah Valley''' is located in southeastern [[Shinovar]] on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa5|40}}

It contains the town of [[Clearmount]], along with other scattered towns and homesteads. There are prairies for grazing sheep in the region.{{book ref|sa5|24}} The area also has plains with wild horses, as well as simple earthen buildings.{{book ref|sa1|i|6}} The [[Stoneward]] monastery gives a perspective over the valley.{{book ref|sa5|40}}
