The '''Battle of Lakeprivy''' is a battle which took place on [[Lumar]]. [[Dirk]] led troops in this battle, though it is unknown whether or not he personally fought in it.{{book ref|tress|5}}

Ten thousand of his men died in the battle, leading [[Charlie's father|the duke]] of [[Diggen's Point]] to declare Dirk a "war hero".{{book ref|tress|5}}
