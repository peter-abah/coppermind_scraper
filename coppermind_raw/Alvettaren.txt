'''Alvettaren''' is an [[honorspren]] guard in [[Lasting Integrity]] on [[Roshar]]. She chooses to manifest a breastplate and a steel cap over close-cropped hair, all formed from her substance.{{book ref|sa4|75}}

When [[Adolin Kholin]] offered to stand trial for humanity in an attempt to convince the honorspren to [[Nahel bond|bond]] with humans and create new [[Windrunners]], Alvettaren was assigned to guard him. She did not usually say anything, preferring to let [[Vaiu]] or the others converse with Adolin.{{book ref|sa4|75}}
