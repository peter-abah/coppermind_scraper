'''Sarqqin''' is an [[Azish]] blacksmith and a member of the [[Unoathed]] on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa5|60}}{{book ref|sa5|147}}
== Appearance and personality ==
Sarqqin was a Azish person assigned female at birth who had filled out the papers needed to live as a man. He has a larger frame and is heavily muscled.{{book ref|sa5|60}}

== Attributes and Abilities ==
As a member of the Unoathed, Sarqqin has access to dead [[Shardplate]] and a [[deadeye]] [[Shardblade]] that have recovered from the [[Recreance]]. As an Unoathed, his plate glows with a fiery light.{{book ref|sa5|136}} Due to the refounding of the [[Oathpact]], the Unoathed are able to summon and dismiss their Blades and Plate like Radiants, even after the start of the [[Everstorm]]’s permanent arrival.{{book ref|sa5|147}}

== History ==
Just before the [[Battle of Azimir]], he had just been named a master blacksmith after seven years of apprenticing. On the third day of the defense of Azimir, he tried out to join the military where he met [[Adolin|Adolin Kholin]].{{book ref|sa5|60}} On the day of the [[Contest of champions]], he was one of the soldiers who had made it to the bunker with Adolin and [[Yanagawn]] and did a count of their supplies when the news of the [[Shattered Plains]] falling and [[Thaylenah]] making a deal with [[Odium]] came in.{{book ref|sa5|126}} Sarqqin would join in the retaking of the Azish throne and entered the palace through the smuggler hole. He acted as a rearguard as the team moved towards the throne.{{book ref|sa5|127}}{{book ref|sa5|127}}{{book ref|sa5|129}} When [[Mayalaran]] returned with a group of dead Shardblades and Shardplate, nine of sets of them appeared in the [[Physical Realm]] to be used by the rest of the strike team including Sarqqin.{{book ref|sa5|136}} Sarqqin would survive the defense of Azimir.{{book ref|sa5|147}}
