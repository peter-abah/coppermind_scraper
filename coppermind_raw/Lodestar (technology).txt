A '''lodestar''' is a device used by the various peoples of the [[cosmere]] to assist them in using unfamiliar technological systems.{{book ref|iote|22}}

== Appearence and Use ==
A lodestar is a rectangular handheld device commonly around ten inches wide and six inches tall with thumbsticks, triggers, buttons, and sometimes small foldout screens or keypads made to a specific culture or individual’s preference.{{book ref|iote|22}} Lodestars owned by [[arcanist]]s are often bigger and bulkier, with twice the number of buttons as anyone else’s.{{book ref|iote|25}} Most lodestars can be pulled apart into two pieces, both of which can be plugged into sockets on a device to interface with it.{{book ref|iote|22}} These halves snap together for storage and are often carried in cases or holsters on the hips. {{book ref|iote|30}}

The lodestar ‘translates’ the user's inputs to fit the device’s system as well as providing localized readouts. A lodestar is incredibly useful as there are many different technological strains in the cosmere (sometimes several within a single ship) and a lodestar allows for the use of all of them with a single personalized control scheme. However, a person who grows familiar with a system rarely uses their lodestar for everything.{{book ref|iote|22}} 

Many people who regularly use their lodestar will customize it both functionally and aesthetically. The casing of [[Illistandrista|Starling]]'s lodestar is made to replicate the feel of stonework to make it feel ancient, like her people. [[Aditil]]'s, by contrast, is more modern and reminiscent of a game controller with vibrant gaudy colors, smooth grips, and a tiny readout. [[ZeetZi]]'s device has a shiny metallic outer shell with almost no markings on its various buttons and triggers.{{book ref|iote|22}} [[Crow]]'s lodestar has an old-school nautical feel to it to match her clothing while [[Leonore]]'s is made of a variety of different kinds of metal that are spiraled and fused together and [[Eddlin|Ed]]'s is larger and bulkier with many more buttons than usual as is typical of an arcanist.{{book ref|iote|25}}

Though they are all trained in their use, [[Scadrian]] soldiers generally do not carry lodestars as their tech is almost exclusively of Scadrian origin. However, they are still used on occasion and all Scadrian military units are expected to have some available in case they are needed.{{book ref|iote|36}}

== History ==
Lodestars were amongst the technology offered to the people on [[First of the Sun]] by [[Xisisrefliel|Xisis]] in exchange for the right of first negotiation for reduced tariffs.{{book ref|iote|epilogue}}
