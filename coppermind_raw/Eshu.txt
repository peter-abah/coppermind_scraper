'''Eshu''' is one of the [[ten fools]],{{book ref|sa2|71}} which are a part of the teachings of [[Vorinism]].{{wob ref|4157}}

Eshu speaks of things he does not understand in front of those who do.{{book ref|sa2|71}}

In late 1173, [[Kaladin]] gets himself and Brightness [[Shallan Davar]] lost in the chasms of the [[Shattered Plains]], which makes him feel like Eshu.{{book ref|sa2|71}}
