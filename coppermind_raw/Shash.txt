'''Shash''' can be three different things:
* The first letter of the [[Hallandren]] alphabet on [[Nalthis]].
* On [[Roshar]], shash is the name of two different [[glyph]]s:{{wob ref|14019}}
** See [[Glyphs/Index#Shash (Lightweaver)]] for the glyph used for the [[Herald]] [[Shalash]], the [[Order of Lightweavers]], and the number six. As the number six, shash is also used as the name for the sixth month of the [[Roshar#Calendar|Rosharan calendar]] as well as the name of the sixth gas giant from the sun of the [[Rosharan system]].
** See [[Glyphs/Index#Shash (dangerous)]] for the glyph meaning "dangerous" that was branded onto [[Kaladin]]'s forehead.
* A gas giant in the [[Rosharan System]]
