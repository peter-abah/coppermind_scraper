'''Rothan''' is a [[singer]] on [[Roshar]] who wears [[stormform]] and leads Lady [[Leshwi]]'s soldiers during the [[True Desolation]].{{book ref|sa4|31}}

== Appearance and Personality ==
While in stormform, Rothan is powerfully built. He seems to crackle with energy, and his red eyes occasionally flash with sparks. He does not typically crave conversation; he simply speaks his mind and expects that others understand him. He cares about [[Venli]] because of Leshwi's regard for her.{{book ref|sa4|31}}

== Attributes and Abilities ==
Rothan's use of stormform indicates that he is a [[Regal]] and that he is bonded to a [[stormspren]].{{book ref|sa4|86}} Stormform grants several supernatural powers, including enhanced strength and agility and the ability to shoot red lightning.{{book ref|sa2|i|8}}{{book ref|sa4|111}} He is a loyal and well-trained soldier.{{book ref|sa4|107}}

== History ==
Rothan, as head of her soldiers, accompanied [[Leshwi]] and her entourage when she joined [[Raboniel]] in capturing [[Urithiru]].{{book ref|sa4|14}}{{book ref|sa4|37}} Leshwi sent Rothan to be part of the group that made the first contact with humans in the tunnels under Urithiru while infiltrating the tower.{{book ref|sa4|37}}

When the [[Pursuer]]'s soldiers began attacking the humans in an effort to get to the infirmary and slaughter the unconscious [[Knights Radiant]], Rothan's temperament and training held him and the rest of Leshwi's soldiers back from joining the fray,{{book ref|sa4|107}} presumably until Leshwi herself began fighting.{{book ref|sa4|109}}
