'''Tabaar''' is a [[kandra]] from Scadrial and an ally of [[Nicki Savage]]. He is close to another Kandra, [[KeSun]] and the three of them have shared many adventures together.{{file ref|The Two Seasons broadsheet.jpg|''The Two Seasons'' Broadsheet}}

== Attributes and Abilities ==
As all Kandra, Tabaar is able to shape his flesh at will, allowing him to impersonate a wide variety of people. He also seem to have experience with (aluminum) [[True Body|True Bodies]]. He is also said to be able to ‘merge’ with other Kandra, such as KeSun. This process takes about ten minutes, and it’s said to be somewhat nasty, with both Tabaar and KeSun preferring to do it unseen. Unlike his companion and friend KeSun, Tabaar doesn’t have any experience impersonating birds, and he needs KeSun to help him change his form to become a great [[Ornisaur]]. He is very good at impersonating people however.{{file ref|The Two Seasons broadsheet.jpg|''The Two Seasons'' Broadsheet}}

== History ==
At some point during the third century PC, Tabaar encountered a woman named [[Nicelle Sauvage]]. They took to travelling together, and, by 348 PC, they were longtime companions. At some point during their travels, Tabaar impersonated an actor playing [[Human]] in [[A Hero for All Ages]]. They also visited the [[Ornisaur Quarry]] together, where the Kandra found an aluminum skeleton of an [[Ornisaur]]. Afterwards, Nicki took them to find the [[Compass of Spirits]].{{file ref|The Two Seasons broadsheet.jpg|''The Two Seasons'' Broadsheet}}

After finding and retrieving it, one of Nicelle's rivals, [[Vila Mecant]], stole the compass. She didn’t manage to take the key with her however, and Nicelle moved to hand the key to the device to Nazh so that he could keep it away from Vila while Nicelle persuaded her two companions to merge together into an Ornisaur, using the bones that they had found. With this done, they gave chase to Vila and Nicelle fought her off, managing to retrieve the compass in the process. After Nicelle fell off the great bird, Tabaar and KeSun managed to save her, and put her down on the ground safely.{{file ref|The Two Seasons broadsheet.jpg|''The Two Seasons'' Broadsheet}}

Afterwards, she spoke to Nazh who held her and then moved in for a kiss before stealing the compass from Nicelle. He apologized before disappearing to the ghostly dimension. Realizing the betrayal, Nicelle was hurt and panicked, realizing that there was no way for them to locate Nazh, concluding their quest was lost. KeSun and Tabaar, however, stopped Nicelle and told her that there was another way to enter the ghostly dimension that they had never told her about.{{file ref|The Two Seasons broadsheet.jpg|''The Two Seasons'' Broadsheet}}
