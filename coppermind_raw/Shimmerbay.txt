'''Shimmerbay''' is a port city in the [[Emerald Sea]] on [[Lumar]], and is located far from [[Kingsport]].{{book ref|tress|21}}

[[Crow]] decided to sail to Shimmerbay so [[Tress]] could "escape" when she tricked Crow into thinking that she--if she had the opportunity--would tell the [[Verdant King|king's]] men that the ''[[Crow's Song]]'' crew killed everyone on the ''[[Oot's Dream]]'', and thus are [[deadrunner]]s.{{book ref|totes|20}} At Shimmerbay, Tress decided to become a pirate and joined the ''Crow's Song'' as their [[sprouter]].{{book ref|totes|21}}
