'''Garthwood''' is a city in the [[Final Empire]] on [[Scadrial]].{{book ref|mb3|1}} It is in the [[Southern Dominance]], southeast of [[Vetitan]].{{17s ref|topic|59143|10th Anniversary Final Empire Map|date=May 9, 2017}}

Three months before [[koloss]] attacked [[Vetitan]], koloss attacked Garthwood.{{book ref|mb3|1}} Garthwood was larger than Vetitan--which meant that it had many more than Vetitan's seven thousand residents--but less than a hundred survivors eventually made it to Vetitan.
