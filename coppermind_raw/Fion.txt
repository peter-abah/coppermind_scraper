'''Fion''' is a member of the [[Set]] on [[Scadrial]].{{book ref|mb7|47}}

In {{date|348|PC}}, they assisted in carrying out [[hemalurgic]] experiments on political dissidents, such as [[Pielle Fromed]] and [[Kyndlip Ternavyl]], taken from [[Bilming]]. However, when [[Autonomy]]'s [[men of gold and red|army]] began arriving, [[Gave Entrone]] realized he was out of time and demanded to see the results of the experimentation. Dissatisfied with the extent of their discoveries, he placed his hope on the [[Community]] project and told Fion's team to kill the captives. The leader of the team instructed Fion to announce to their loyalists a priority-one evacuation order to the caverns under Bilming.{{book ref|mb7|47}}
