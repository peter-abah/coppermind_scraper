'''Tusa''' is a [[listener]] on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa4|81}} They are one of [[Venli]]'s team of scholars in [[Narak]] who research new [[Singer#Forms|forms]] and try to bring about the [[Everstorm]]. Their gender is not specified in the text.

About two and a half years into the [[War of Reckoning]], they are involved in a war strategy meeting attended by [[Eshonai]], [[Thude]], [[Bila]], [[Rlain]], and [[Harvo]]. They are considered as a potential spy to infiltrate the warcamps, but are deemed too essential to research. When Rlain volunteers, Tusa declares him to be perfect for the role.{{book ref|sa5|79}}

Venli and [[Ulim]] select Tusa as one of the few they can trust to be first to take a [[Voidspren]] into their [[gemheart]].{{book ref|sa4|81}}
