Lady '''Dorise Chevalle''' is a resident of [[Elendel]] on [[Scadrial]].{{file ref|House_Record_broadsheet.jpg|''The House Record'' broadsheet}}

In {{date|342|PC}}, [[Waxillium Ladrian]] caused Dorise's motorcar to veer off towards the canal while he was chasing the [[Marksman]]. Wax was able to save the lives of Dorise and the other passengers by crushing the front of the car to the ground, but the car was destroyed.{{book ref|mb5|2}}

After the incident Dorise hired a solicitor to seek compensation for her losses.{{file ref|House_Record_broadsheet.jpg|''The House Record'' broadsheet}}
