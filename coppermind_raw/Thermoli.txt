'''Thermoli''' is an outer village in the [[Elendel Basin]]. It is west of [[New Seran]], at the edge of the mountains that form that border between the Basin and the Southern [[Roughs]].{{ref|text=The village is only labelled in an annotation by [[Nazh]] on the version of the [[:File:{{map|Elendel Basin}}|Elendel Basin map]] in ''[[Shadows of Self]]''.}}{{book ref|mb5|9}}

The village is the source of the Thermolian accent, known for its distinctive "v" sounds.{{book ref|mb5|9}}

Professor [[Hanlanaze]] grew up in Thermoli.{{book ref|mb5|9}}
