'''Gavil's Carriages''' is a taxiing company in [[Elendel]].{{book ref|mb4|12}}

All the drivers for the carriage company wore matching hats, made of stiff felt in a conical shape, with a flat top and a feather on top.{{book ref|mb4|12}}

[[Wax]], [[Wayne]] and [[Marasi]] rode in a carriage to the [[Outer Estates]] to visit the scene of a [[Vanishers]] robbery.{{book ref|mb4|12}} Wayne asked for and received the driver's hat, since Wax had paid him so well.
