'''Caeb''' was a historical resident of [[Roshar]]. He fought alongside the [[Knights Radiant]] during the [[Heraldic Epochs]].{{book ref|sa2|4}}

==History== 
Caeb was seen in one of [[Dalinar Kholin]]'s visions.{{book ref|sa2|4}} He and Dalinar were among a group of around twelve soldiers that accompanied an unnamed female Radiant who wore red-accented [[Shardplate]].{{book ref|sa2|4}} The soldiers were likely Radiant [[squire]]s, as Dalinar was surprised to see many of them glowing faintly with [[Stormlight]].{{book ref|sa2|4}}

Caeb's Radiant team joined several others to search the [[Purelake]] for a [[spren]] corrupted by [[Sja-anat]]. Caeb was the first person to catch a glimpse of the spren.{{book ref|sa2|4}} Dalinar subsequently found the red-eyed spren in the water, and a Radiant told Caeb to run back to the checkpoint.{{book ref|sa2|4}}
