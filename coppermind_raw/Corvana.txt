'''Corvana''' is a scholar on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|twok|60}}

Corvana wrote the ''[[Analectics]]'', a book that contained at least some quotes from the songs of the [[Vanrial]] that were thought to be in [[Dawnchant]].{{book ref|twok|60}} A copy of her book was owned by Princess [[Jasnah Kholin]] and later used by Brightlady [[Navani Kholin]] in {{Rosharan date|1173}} to confirm that Highprince [[Dalinar Kholin]] was speaking in the Dawnchant during his visions.{{book ref|twok|60}}
