'''Khornaks''' are predators native to shallow coastal waters, like the [[Longbrow's Straits]], on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa2|1}} Khornaks thrive in packs, and they have thick, webbed limbs protected by chunks of carapace, along with eight beady eyes, a jagged tail, and a toothy maw that sailors wisely fear. Khornaks bond with an unknown type of [[spren]], allowing their hefty frames to skim through the water in search of prey, barely disturbing the surface despite their thick carapace jutting out.{{rpg ref|slwg|225}}

A khornak is most dangerous in close range, where they unveil a massive jaw lined with many rows of jagged teeth—perfect for gouging prey, latching onto skin, and dragging victims into the water where the khornak pack can descend. Should a khornak not have a pack, they often simply drown their prey.{{rpg ref|slwg|225}} Captain [[Tozbek]] was worried that the waters were shallow enough for khornaks when [[Shallan Davar]] wanted to be lowered into the water in order to get a better look at a [[santhid]].{{book ref|sa2|1}}
