'''Mistborn: Birthright''' is a cancelled computer game set in the [[Final Empire]] on [[Scadrial]]. It was originally expected to be released in Fall of 2013, but was pushed back to a tentative release date of 2015 and moved to the new console generation.{{wob ref|11176}} It was then planned to be released in Fall 2016.{{17s ref|news|202|Birthright release date|date=2014-12-23}}

It would have featured an original storyline created by Brandon Sanderson set several hundreds of years before the first Mistborn novel. The game focused on a unique combat system that put Allomancy into the hands of gamers, as players follow Fendin "Fiddle" Fathvell, an arrogant young nobleman who must quickly master his newfound Allomantic abilities before forces at work can destroy his entire family.{{url ref|url=https://www.tor.com/2012/03/26/mistborn-birthright-is-coming-in-2013/|text=Tor announcement|site=Tor.com|date=2012-03-26}}

A young [[Terris|Terriswoman]] was featured in the story.{{tweet ref|user=PeterAhlstrom|469600416800329729|Comment on Peter's Twitter|date=2014-05-22}}

As of late 2016, Brandon has announced that the game is not going to be happening.{{wob ref|2029}}
