'''Sefawynn''' is a [[Weswaran]] [[skop]].{{book ref|frugal|5}}

== Appearance and Personality ==

Sefawynn bears the Night Marks, a birthmark of three dark blue spots which is seen as a sign of being chosen or cursed by Woden.{{book ref|frugal|24}}

Sefawynn doubts her own abilities as a skop; while she can see [[wight]]s invisible to other people,{{book ref|frugal|20}} her boasts do not seem to take effect, so she considers herself essentially a grifter.{{book ref|frugal|18}}

==History==

Sefawynn met [[John West]] when she and her brother captured John shortly after his arrival. She decided he must be a charlatan rather than an aelv, despite his appearance. She comes to respect him as they travel together, attempting to rescue her brother [[Wyrm]].{{expand}}

As they continued to travel together, Sefawynn and John fell in love. When the [[Hordamen]] launched a major invasion of [[Maelport]], she defied [[Woden]] and her boasts inspired the wights to attack the Hordamen.{{book ref|frugal|35}} After the battle, [[Logna]] told John that his other-dimensional nature would protect Sefawynn from Woden's retaliation,{{book ref|frugal|37}} and they eventually married.{{book ref|frugal|epilogue}}
