'''Pattywagon Smedry''' is the daughter of [[Leavenworth Smedry]] and the sister of [[Attica]] Smedry and [[Kazan Smedry]]. Her [[Smedry Talent]] is the ability to say inappropriate things at awkward moments.{{book ref|a3|4}}.

== Appearance and Personality ==
Pattywagon has short blond hair and a full figure. She is full of enthusiasm and affectionate but usually embarrassing and often overwhelming. Her Talent is enhanced by a voice that carries well and cuts through general surrounding noise.{{book ref|a3|4}}

== History ==
Alcatraz meets Pattywagon at a meeting of the [[Council of Kings]] when she interrupts the proceedings to boisterously greet Alcatraz and her father Leavenworth.{{book ref|a3|4}} She acts as a guide for Alcatraz to help him find her son [[Folsom Smedry|Folsom]]. On their journey, she tells Alcatraz about Transporter's Glass and critiques some of [[Attica]]'s theories.{{book ref|a3|5}} 

Once they find Folsom at the ice cream parlor, Pattywagon does her best to fend off Alcatraz's hoards of fans, much to his chagrin. Once she gets them settled, Pattywagon leaves them to return to the court discussion.{{book ref|a3|6}}

During the battle that ensues after the potential treaty is rejected, Pattywagon provides a distraction that gives Alcatraz and Grandpa Smedry time to get to King Dartmoor to defend him.{{book ref|a3|21}}

== Trivia ==
* Her name is a reference to the nickname "[[wikipedia:Police_van|paddy wagon]]" for a police vehicle used to transport prisoners.{{wob ref|13678}}
