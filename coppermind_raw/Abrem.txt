'''Abrem''' is a man kidnapped by the [[Set]] on [[Scadrial]].{{book ref|mb7|56}} The Set brought him to the [[Wayfarer]] and made him believe that the [[Ashmounts]] had erupted and the outside world was a wasteland. He had been keeping notes in the Wayfarer. {{book ref|mb7|56}} After [[Marasi Colms]] arrived in the Wayfarer, [[Armal]] told [[Fialia]] who is another resident of the Wayfarer, to fetch him. After Marasi revealed that the Set had been lying to the people of Wayfarer, it is unknown what happened to him. {{book ref|mb7|56}}
