'''Voidhawk''' is the name of the longship belonging to [[Varion Crestmar]] in the empire of [[Firstborn]]{{book ref|Firstborn}}. Despite it being a relatively small ship, it was used by [[Varion Crestmar]] as his flagship during his campaign. The Empire have implanted several bugs aboard it, including the one that allows capture and limited alteration of FTL communications.{{book ref|Firstborn}}
