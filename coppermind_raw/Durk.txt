'''Durk''' is a [[Rosharan]]--probably serving in Brightlord [[Meridas Amaram]]'s army--and likely one of [[Kaladin]]'s mentor figures or friends prior to his arrival on the [[Shattered Plains]].{{book ref|sa1|46}}

== Appearance and Personality ==
Nothing is known of Durk's appearance. He is a fairly superstitious man, and wears a pair of 'lucky boots' into every battle.{{book ref|sa1|49}} Durk believes that one should not hedge when living ones life; he should either commit to something fully or not at all.{{book ref|sa1|46}}

== History ==
It is unknown when Durk and Kaladin met. At some point before Kaladin was enslaved, Durk told Kaladin that he was a natural born soldier and an artist with the spear.{{book ref|sa1|43}} He also gave Kaladin advice, telling him not to live his life hedging bets.{{book ref|sa1|46}} Kaladin often recalls Durk and his advice later on in his life.{{book ref|sa1|46}}{{book ref|sa1|49}}
