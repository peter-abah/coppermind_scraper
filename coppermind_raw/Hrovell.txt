'''Hrovell''' is a nation under the domain of the [[Fjordell Empire]] located on the continent of [[Opelon]]{{map ref|Opelon}} on [[Sel]] during the [[Late Era]]. It is located in the rural southeast of the Fjordell Empire, so they have very little knowledge of what is happening in the world outside their nation.{{glossary ref|Elantris|H}} They speak a dialect of Derethi that has evolved so much that many Fjordell have difficulty understanding them.{{book ref|Elantris|18}}

Like all the nations in the Fjordell Empire, Hrovell had [[Shu-Dereth]] as their state religion. However, due to the nation's isolation, some places do not see a Derethi priest for months,{{book ref|Elantris|61}} many other religions were still practiced in secret, such as the [[Jeskeri Mysteries]].{{book ref|Elantris|18}} Priests of Shu-Dereth considered their souls to be in great danger.

Hrovell had a standing army which was at the [[Wyrn (title)|Wyrn]]'s disposal.{{book ref|Elantris|15}}

The Hroven were an oppressed people who lived in fear,{{book ref|Elantris|23}} likely of the Fjordell Empire.
