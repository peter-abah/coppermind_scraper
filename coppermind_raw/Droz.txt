'''Droz''' is the helmsman of the ship ''[[First Dreams]]'' on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa3|i|4}}

== Appearance and Personality ==
Droz is [[Liaforan]]. He is lanky and has dark brown eyes and skin. He is curious about [[Kaza]]'s royal lineage and her [[Soulcasting]] [[savant]]hood, and strikes up a conversation with her before she scares him away.{{book ref|sa3|i|4}} 

== Attributes and Abilities ==
He is a skilled helmsman, as he successfully steers the ''First Dreams'' through a raging storm.{{book ref|sa3|i|4}}

== History ==
Droz accompanied his captain [[Vazrmeb]] in pursuit of rumored hidden treasure on the island of [[Akinah]] in [[Aimia]]. He was eager to make the dangerous journey as he believed rumors that "generations" of [[greatshell]]s had left their [[gemheart]]s on the island.{{book ref|sa3|i|4}}

''First Dreams'' sailed north from [[Liafor]] with a mercenary crew and encountered an intense storm on the [[Aimian Sea]] that abruptly ended near Akinah.{{book ref|sa3|i|4}} Droz managed to keep the ship afloat, although some of the crew were swept overboard.{{book ref|sa3|i|4}} He boarded one of the ship's dinghies in an attempt to reach the island, but he was poisoned and killed along with the rest of the crew by the ship's cook, a [[Dysian Aimian]] posing as a human.{{book ref|sa3|i|4}}
