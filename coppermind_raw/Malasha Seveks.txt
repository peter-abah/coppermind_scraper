'''Malasha Seveks''' is a [[lighteyes|lighteyed]] woman on [[Roshar]] who was courted by [[Adolin Kholin]].{{book ref|sa1|24}}

==Appearance and Personality==
Malasha is likely [[Alethi]] since she was present in the warcamps in the [[Shattered Plains]]. She wears her hair up in braids and is considered to be very nice looking.{{book ref|sa1|26}}

She is curious about war and asks Adolin if she can accompany him to the battlefield; she becomes annoyed when he will not let her come. Dalinar considers a woman desiring to watch the fighting to be "unnatural".{{book ref|sa1|26}} Malasha's interest in war is indeed unusual given Adolin's description of her as "rather religious",{{book ref|sa1|24}} since the [[Vorin]] work ''[[Arts and Majesty]]'' expressly defines combat as a masculine art.{{book ref|sa1|26}}

==History==
Adolin started courting Malasha in {{Rosharan date|1173}} shortly after [[Janala Lustow]] grew bored of him. Malasha was religious, and Adolin liked her enough to spend time listening to readings from ''[[The Arguments]]''.{{book ref|sa1|24}} However, Malasha started to grow cold towards Adolin after he refused to satisfy her curiosity by taking her with him on a plateau run.{{book ref|sa1|26}}{{book ref|sa1|28}} Adolin still seemed interested, but also became intrigued by [[Danlan Morakotha]]. Malasha and Adolin broke up a short time later.{{book ref|sa1|28}}
