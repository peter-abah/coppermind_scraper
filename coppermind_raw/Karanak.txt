'''Karanak''' is a city in southern [[Alethkar]] in [[Bethab princedom]]. It sits near [[Mevan Bay]] and the delta where the [[Deathbend River]] meets the [[Tarat Sea]].{{map ref|Alethkar}} As a major port, Karanak is of great strategic importance.{{rpg ref|slwg|152}}

In the year following the [[Battle of Thaylen Field]], [[Coalition of monarchs|coalition]] forces took control of southwestern Alethkar along the coast of the Tarat Sea.{{book ref|sa4|17}} Karanak was under coalition control at the time of [[Taravangian]]'s betrayal and coalition forces in southern Alethkar successfully regrouped at the city with minimal casualties.{{book ref|sa4|50}} Not long after, the Fused closed in on the city, laying siege to it. During the siege, the defenders often clashed with a violent singer who calls himself “Brightlord” [[Hathalash]].{{rpg ref|slwg|152}}
