'''Dwookim''' is a [[Torish]] [[yoki-hijo]] who is very active in the [[reform movement]] prior to her retirement.{{book ref|yumi|22}}

As a reformed yoki-hijo, she often eats, bathes, and makes other decisions for herself. Later in her career, Dwookim works only about half the days of any given week, taking the other days off for rest or other personal reasons. When working, she summons an average of roughly ten [[hijo]] in a session. She eventually retires, presumably at or by the age of seventy. [[Hwanji]] works as one of Dwookim's attendants for several years.{{book ref|yumi|22}}
