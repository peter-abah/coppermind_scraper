'''Barringtons''' are a type of vehicle in [[Elendel]] named after [[Lord Barrington]].

They are a four-wheeled coach with a completely enclosed passenger compartment and room at the top front for a driver. They vary widely in paint-jobs but are fairly consistently shaped.{{book ref|mb5|16}}

[[House Ladrian]]'s coaches are Barringtons as are many of the coaches at [[Ashweather Carriage and Coach]].{{book ref|mb5|14}}
