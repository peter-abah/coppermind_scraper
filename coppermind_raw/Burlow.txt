Old '''Burlow''' is a man who lives in [[Weathering]] in the [[Roughs]].{{book ref|mb4|13}}

Old Burlow had a daughter who was murdered by [[Pars the Deadman]].{{book ref|mb4|13}} Her corpse was left in the center of Weathering to taunt [[Waxillium Ladrian]], who was hunting Pars.
