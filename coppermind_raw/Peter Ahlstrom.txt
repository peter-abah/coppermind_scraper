'''Peter Ahlstrom''' is Vice President of Editorial for [[Dragonsteel Entertainment]]{{book ref|mb7|part=ack}}.

[[Brandon Sanderson]] met Peter while attending Brigham Young University. Peter was an editor for numerous anime comics, including several for TOKYOPOP, before being hired as Brandon's assistant around 2007. Peter was promoted to editor and then editorial director by at least March, 2020.{{wob ref|13972}}

Peter is married to continuity editor [[Karen Ahlstrom]], with whom he has three children.

== Cameos ==
Peter has several cameos in Brandon's work. In the [[Mistborn (series)|Mistborn]] series there's "Ahlstrom Square" in Luthadel,{{map ref|Luthadel}} the "Ahlstrom Tower" in Elendel,{{book ref|mb5|12}}, [[Peterus|Lord Peterus]] in ''[[Alloy of Law]]'', and [[Lieutenant Ahlstrom]] in ''[[Shadows of Self]]''.

Both Peter and his wife Karen cameo in [[The Stormlight Archive]] as [[Peet]], a member of [[Bridge Four]],{{wob ref|11086}} and Peet's fiancée [[Ka]].{{wob ref|12956}}

In the [[Skyward_(series)|Skyward]] series, the commonly used "[[:File:Turning Methods.jpeg|Ahlstrom Loop]]" flight maneuver is named after Peter.

In the ''[[Alcatraz Versus the Evil Librarians (series)|Alcatraz]]'' series, the green jacket Alcatraz wears is actually Peter's green jacket.{{cite}}

== Acknowledgements ==
Peter's name can also be found after many unique and often nonsensical adjectives beginning with "I" in the acknowledgements sections of Brandon's books, as part of an in-joke that has persisted ever since he referred to Peter as "Incalculable" in the first Mistborn novel.{{wob ref|7485}}{{book ref|mb1|part=ack}}

Other adjectives used so far  include "Indivisible",{{book ref|mb3|part=ack}} "Insoluble",{{book ref|wb|part=ack}} "Incandescent", "Inevitable",{{book ref|sa1|part=ack}} "Immaculate", "Inseparable",{{book ref|mb4|part=ack}} "Interpolated", "Instant", "Incumbent",{{book ref|sa2|part=ack}} "Inciting",{{book ref|au|part=ack}} "Insistent",{{book ref|sa3|part=ack}} "Inconsecutive",{{book ref|sky1|part=ack}} "Institutional",{{book ref|sky2|part=ack}} Indefatigable",{{book ref|sa3.5|part=ack}} "Ineffable",{{book ref|sa4|part=ack}}, "In-Demand",{{book ref|sky3|part=ack}} "Insatiable",{{book ref|mb7|part=ack}} "Inland",{{book ref|sp2|part=ack}} "Installed",{{book ref|sp3|part=ack}} "Instructional",{{book ref|sp4|part=ack}} "Industry-Standard",{{book ref|sky4|part=ack}} and "Inviting".{{book ref|sa5|part=ack}}

Janci Patterson appears to have contributed, possibly unintentionally, by referring to Peter as an "incredible" editor.{{book ref|a6|part=ack}}
