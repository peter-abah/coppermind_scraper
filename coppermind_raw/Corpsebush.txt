The '''Corpsebush''' is a dangerous plant species found on the island [[Patji (island)|Patji]] on [[First of the Sun]]. A Corpsebush has a sharp scent, like aged meat. They survive by coaxing insects into its gullet with false promises of carrion.{{book ref|Iote|43}}
