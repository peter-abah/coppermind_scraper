'''Dymab''' is a malen ''[[Makay-im]]'' [[Fused]] on [[Roshar]]. Dymab has made a mission of eliminating the anti-Fused resistance in [[Rall Elorim]], where he is one of the three singer monarchs.{{rpg ref|slwg|155}} He managed to drive them out of the [[Dirgehollow]], although they escaped his grasp and immediately went searching for a new safehouse.{{rpg ref|slwg|156}}
