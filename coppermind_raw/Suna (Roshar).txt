'''Suna''' is a slave in [[Tvlakv]]'s caravan.{{book ref|sa2|24}}

He's a timid, quiet man, wearing a set of tunic and trousers given to [[Shallan]] by merchants she'd rescued. Rather than being branded, he is, like the rest of Tvlakv's group, tattooed, indicated he's either a child of a slave or someone who willingly entered servitude, perhaps to pay off a debt.{{book ref|sa2|24}}

He joins Shallan during her journey towards the [[Shattered Plains]] warcamps.{{book ref|sa2|24}} He presumably reaches them with her, as slaves are mentioned carrying her trunk into the [[House Kholin|Kholin]] camp.{{book ref|sa2|36}} If so, he would later become a member of [[Sebarial]]'s manor staff.{{book ref|sa2|42}}
