'''Zoral''', also known as the '''[[Voidbringer]]''', is the ruler of [[Odium's empire]] on [[Ashyn]], whose conflict with [[Makibak]] led to the ruination of the planet.{{book ref|sa5|107}}

== History ==
Zoral led [[Odium]]'s [[Odium's empire|empire]] on Ashyn, then known as Alaswha, and Odium imbued his chosen people with [[Surges]]. He led the empire, slowly swallowing up city-states or forcing their kings, like [[Jezrien]], to pledge allegiance. He was opposed by [[Makibak]], and his nephew, [[Nale]].{{book ref|sa5|104}}{{book ref|sa5|107}} He died in the collapse of Odium's empire during the destruction of Ashyn, and was succeeded by Jezrien, who led humanity to Roshar.{{book ref|sa5|107}}
