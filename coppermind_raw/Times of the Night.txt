'''''Times of the Night''''' is a serial [[hion viewer]] program on [[Komashi]], with new episodes broadcast on [[Samday]] evenings. Characters in the show include a woman, depicted with blue [[hion]] lines, a man, depicted with violet, and the man's brother, Lee. The main character is a man who wakes up one day without his memories. He is the only one who knows the location of an incredible treasure, and so many people try to convince him that they are his friends. The story is about the man trying to figure out his past and discern which people were truly his friends and who is trying to deceive him. Each episode ends with a cliffhanger.{{book ref|yumi|11}}

[[Painter]] is a regular viewer of the program, and shows it to [[Yumi]] while explaining both the show and the technology used to display it.{{book ref|yumi|11}}
