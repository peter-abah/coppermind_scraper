'''Jom''' is a cobbler in [[Kholinar]].{{book ref|sa3|72}}

He broke his arm before the [[Siege of Kholinar]].{{book ref|sa3|72}} 

[[Grund]] knew of Jom, and suggested to [[Shallan Davar|Veil]] that Jom would be a good candidate to give extra food to, along with many other candidates.{{book ref|sa3|72}} However, the [[Grips]] killed all of Grund's friends for the food and terrorized anyone who received food,{{book ref|sa3|80}} so it is possible Jom is now dead.
