'''The Scribbler''' is the name given to the person responsible for the disappearance of several [[Rithmatics|Rithmatic]] students from [[Armedius Academy]].{{book ref|rithmatist|16}}

Their victims describe them as wearing a bowler hat and a cape and holding what appears to be a long cane. They attack with creatures that seem similar to wild [[chalkling]]s.{{book ref|rithmatist|16}}{{book ref|rithmatist|prologue}}
