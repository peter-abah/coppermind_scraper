'''Kaminah''' is the oldest [[ward]] of [[May Aladar]].{{book ref|sa5|39}} She serves as [[Adolin Kholin]]s aide-de-camp during the [[Battle of Azimir]].{{book ref|sa5|39}}

== Appearance & Personality ==
Kaminah is a young Alethi woman with curls of light brown hair, pointing to some partial foreign ancestry.{{book ref|sa5|39}} She is atleast seventeen years old at the time of the Battle of Azimir.{{book ref|sa5|50}}{{book ref|sa5|39}}
Although initially somewhat nervous about her promotion to aide-de-camp due to her complete lack of experience, she rapidly takes to the role with eagerness, showing initiative and looking for ways to be helpful.{{book ref|sa5|39}}{{book ref|sa5|47}}

== History ==
Kaminah arrived at Azimir together with her Teacher May Aladar who was going to assist Adolin Kholin as his head scribe.{{book ref|sa5|37}} When Adolin assigned May to command his archers he promoted Kaminah to his new aide-de-camp.{{book ref|sa5|39}} Kaminah rapidly took to her new role and and relayed Adolins orders and messages during the siege. She also saw to that he got enough food{{book ref|sa5|52}} and sleep, directly ordering him to go to bed on atleast one occassion.{{book ref|sa5|47}} When asked about what it meant to control Azimir by Adolin, she offered to write to Urithiru to try and find out more.{{book ref|sa5|47}}

When the Oathgate defense finally completly broke during the seventh day of the siege, Kaminah, along with Mays other wards, made it to the Emperors hidden saferoom where she received the news of Naraks fall and [[Thaylenah]]s treaty via [[spanreed]].{{book ref|sa5|126}}
