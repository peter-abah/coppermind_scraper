'''Melur''' is an [[Alethi]] singer working as a surgeon for the [[Kholinar]] resistance. She is reserved, calculating and compassionate, wanting to save as many lives as possible. Melur has deep red hairstrands, thick limbs and a broad face.{{rpg ref|ss|21}}

== History ==
Melur learned her trade by observing human surgeons at work, and after the original surgeons at her hospital were killed, she joined the resistance. She quickly became a high-value member of the resistance, and [[Eltharon]], the Fused tasked with destroying the resistance, recognised that he needed to capture or kill her. Eltharon captured [[Zoyah]], another member of the resistance, and used her to send a fake extraction request to Melur. The surgeon assembled a party to rescue Zoyah, and, while Melur remained behind to organise the resistance’s retreat and gather more sympathisers, they slipped into a warehouse and freed the spy.{{rpg ref|ss|21}} In the meantime, Eltharon attacked the hospital and its occupants, including Melur. It is unclear what happened afterwards, though the rescue party, including Zoyah, returned while Eltharon was still inside the building and a fight is known to have ensued.{{rpg ref|ss|23}}
