Lady '''Carmine Feltry''' is a resident of [[Elendel]] on [[Scadrial]].{{file ref|House_Record_broadsheet.jpg|''The House Record'' broadsheet}}

She is planning on opening a racing circuit exclusively for motorcars on the old fairgrounds near the [[Irongate River]].{{file ref|House_Record_broadsheet.jpg|''The House Record'' broadsheet}}

==Trivia==
*Her last name is similar to that of Elendel politician [[Alloran Feltri]], but there is no confirmed connection between them.
