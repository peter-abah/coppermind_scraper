'''Eliah Matal''' is a [[Lighteyes|lighteyed]] [[Alethi]] woman from the [[Sadeas princedom]] on [[Roshar]]. She was part of a failed expedition to study a number of recently uncovered ruins on the [[Shattered Plains]].{{rpg ref|b9|8}}

== Appearance and Personality ==
Eliah Matal has bright purple eyes, marking her as a lighteyes. She keeps her black hair braided, as is Alethi fashion. By the time of her failed expedition with [[Bridge Nine]], she was thirty two years old.{{rpg ref|b9|9}} She often wears a formal green havah, although she pairs this with more practical boots while on an expedition.{{rpg ref|b9|31}}

Eliah has been sheltered her entire life, ignorant of the real world. She is an earnest person,{{rpg ref|b9|9}} and works hard as a historian. She is generally very concerned with propriety.{{rpg ref|b9|14}} Eliah is also very eager to prove herself in the field of Alethi scholarship. She is the favorite niece of [[Avarak Matal|Avarak]] and [[Hashal]] Matal. However, she differs quite a lot from them; she isn’t cruel at all, although she is used to being obeyed. She is also mentioned as being bright and inquisitive.{{rpg ref|b9|31}} Eliah has at least one ward, named [[Zvynda]],{{rpg ref|b9|38}} and speaks three languages; Alethi, Veden and Azish.{{rpg ref|b9|31}}

== History ==
At some point during the [[War of Reckoning]], Eliah Matal traveled to the Shattered Plains. Here, she studied history. At some point during the war, she heard of a number of recently uncovered ruins close to the warcamps. She organised an expedition, taking a number of her scholars, soldiers and a Bridge crew with her. During their journey, very close to their destination, the crew was attacked by a [[Chasmfiend]], which toppled their bridge, with Eliah still on it, into the chasm. The beast itself retreated, hurt from the fall and being hit by the bridge, leaving Eliah and a few of her surviving companions alone.{{rpg ref|b9|8}}
