'''Brainstorm''' is an [[Epic]] whose powers were stolen by [[Larcener]]. She possesses conjuration powers, and could summon anything she wanted that smaller than a size of a couch and up to a certain mass limit (although it is not said what that mass limit is). These conjurations could also be within three miles of Brainstorm and still exist.{{book ref|calamity|24}}
