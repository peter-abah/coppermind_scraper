'''Hal Davis''' is the son of [[Anthony Davis]].{{book ref|Snapshot|1}} Hal is visited by his dad in the Snapshot at the park on Ingred and Ninth. In real life, Hal hasn't been seen his dad for six months because of Anthony's and [[Molly (Snapshot)|Molly's]] divorce.{{book ref|Snapshot|7}}

== Appearance & Personality ==
Hal is an eight-year-old boy who has a mop of black hair and is small for his age. He loves playing his made-up games, visiting the zoo, and collecting coins like his dad. {{book ref|Snapshot|7}}
