The '''Plank Boys''' are a criminal group or gang in the [[Roughs]] on [[Scadrial]].{{book ref|mb7|58}}

[[Wayne]] joined the Plank Boys for a period of time following his [[Wayne's mother|mother's]] death and considered some of the other members his friends.{{book ref|mb7|58}} They recognized Wayne's growing potential and talked him into branching out into robbery with guns; the ensuing incident led to [[Durkel]]'s death and indirectly brought about Wayne's association with [[Waxillium Ladrian]].{{book ref|mb4|10}} Waxillium later brought up the Plank Boys to illustrate to Wayne how creating a world in which fewer children are left to grow up alone could lead to fewer criminals.{{book ref|mb7|58}}
