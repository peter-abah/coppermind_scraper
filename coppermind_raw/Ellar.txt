'''Ellar''' is an [[Alethi]] sergeant on [[Roshar]]. He keeps his hair closely shaved, and people consider him to be belligerent, intolerant and self-important. Despite this, he wants to be a respected figure. Ellar generally views strangers with disdain, until they prove themselves useful or capable.{{rpg ref|slsw|21}}

== History ==
Shortly before the [[Everstorm]]’s arrival, Ellar lead a large [[House Kholin|Kholin]] caravan consisting of ten wagons and a company of one hundred soldiers. This caravan also carried [[Bordin]], the madman [[Talenel]] and his [[Honorblade]]. Two days before they reached the [[Covenant Stone Crossroads]], the caravan was attacked by bandits. A few men were injured, but no one was killed.{{rpg ref|slsw|21}} Shortly after arriving here, while preparing for a [[Highstorm]], Ellar noticed a [[Shin]] [[Taszo-son-Clutio|stranger]] standing next to the wagon carrying their prisoner, Taln. He ordered the man to move away.{{rpg ref|slsw|23}} 

Shortly afterwards, the caravan was ambushed by bandits. The soldiers fought them off, but one person died, and, unbeknownst to the Alethi, the Honorblade was stolen.{{rpg ref|slsw|29}} The rest of the journey was uneventful.{{rpg ref|slsw|34}} After their arrival at the [[Shattered Plains]], Ellar ordered his men to bring the prisoner to the Highprince.{{rpg ref|slsw|36}} Later, Ellar, having heard of a report regarding some potential enemy movement not far from the warcamps, ordered an expedition to find and destroy these [[listener]]s.{{rpg ref|slsw|39}}
