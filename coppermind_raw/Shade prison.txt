A '''shade prison''' is a device developed by the [[Malwish Empire|Malwish]] during the Space Age of the [[Cosmere]]. The device takes the form of a metal box,{{book ref|iote|38}} small enough to be held with one hand,{{book ref|iote|60}} with equipment inside. It takes a lot of juice to engage a [[shade]] prison, although a single [[Jagged Star gunship]] is capable of powering one. The device transforms the targeted shade into glowing white smoke; a process that seems to cause the shade pain. The [[Malwish Planetary Guard]] has access to a number of shade prisons, they are considered to be less effective then the [[Roshar]]an device with a similar function, leaning more heavily on electronics rather then [[Investiture]].{{book ref|iote|38}}
