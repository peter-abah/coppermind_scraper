'''Paclo''', sometimes referred to as '''Paclo the Dusty''', was a deputy lawman under [[Waxillium Ladrian]] in the town of [[Weathering]] on [[Scadrial]].{{book ref|aol|15}} 

Paclo was Wax's deputy before Wax met [[Wayne]].{{book ref|aol|15}} He was a very jumpy man, easily startled into shouting by something as quiet as a softly closed door. However, he was a very brave man, who did not hesitate to run into danger. Wax spoke of Paclo in a past tense, implying that Paclo was dead.
