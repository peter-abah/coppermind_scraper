'''Push''' is the pseudonym of a member of the [[Set]] on [[Scadrial]]. He is a [[Coinshot]] that also works with the [[Vanishers]].{{book ref|mb4|17}}

==Appearance and Personality==
Push wears a black suit.{{book ref|mb4|18}} He has a serious demeanor and speaks in a near-monotone.{{book ref|mb4|17}}

==Attributes and Abilities==
{{quote
|He's good. Dangerous. We’ll never take out Miles unless that Allomancer is dead.
|Wax, on Push{{book ref|mb4|19}}
}}
Push is a [[steel]] [[Misting]] also known as a [[Coinshot]]. He is a skilled [[Allomancer]] and gunman, to the point that he was able to injure and nearly kill [[Waxillium Ladrian]] during a fight. He can fly through the air gracefully and easily Push incoming bullets away from himself.{{book ref|mb4|18}}

== History ==
He was employed by the criminal organization known as the [[Set]], and was partnered with a [[Lurcher]] nicknamed [[Pull]]. The pair dressed similarly and worked together during heists and other jobs.{{book ref|mb4|17}}

In {{date|341|PC}}, the Set hired the [[Vanishers]] to perform robberies and kidnappings,{{book ref|mb4|epilogue}} and [[Mister Suit]] assigned Push and Pull to support [[Miles Dagouter]] in these efforts. They acted as bodyguards for Suit, and were also present to defend against potential interference by [[Waxillium Ladrian]].{{book ref|mb4|17}}

Wax indeed stowed away on the [[Breaknaught]] knowing that the Vanishers would steal it. Miles anticipated this and warned Push and Pull that Wax was likely present before the train car was opened in the Vanishers workshop; Push was angry that Miles had not warned them sooner.{{book ref|mb4|17}} Wax evaded an ambush waiting outside the Breaknaught by creating a diversion with dynamite, and Push and Pull pursued him up the [[Ironspine Building]]. Push was able to Steelpush [[Vindication]] out of Wax's hands, but was lucky to evade an [[aluminum]] bullet from Wax after he retrieved the gun. The Allomancers continued to duel; Push shot the lawman in the arm, but Wax used a clever strategy that caused Push to go flying away from the building.{{book ref|mb4|18}}

[[Marasi]] then killed Pull with a rifle that she had gotten from [[Ranette]]. As Push returned to the fight, he nearly killed Wax and Marasi, and then angrily pursued Marasi seeking vengeance. Marasi was forced to drop the rifle as a decoy, and Push retrieved it. He attempted to shoot [[Wayne]], but was unsuccessful. He confirmed to Miles that Pull had been killed.{{book ref|mb4|18}}

Wax and Wayne knew Push was dangerous, but were not sure how to take him out. Although he had dropped Vindication, Wax realized that he could use a [[Hazekiller#Hazekiller_Rounds|hazekiller round]] intended for Coinshots by "firing" it with a Steelpush. The round worked as intended, and the ceramic tip of the bullet hit an unsuspecting Push in the eye, killing him.{{book ref|mb4|19}}
