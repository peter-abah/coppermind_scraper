'''Cob''' is a coachman in the employ of [[House Ladrian]] in [[Elendel]].

==Appearance and Personality==
He is a small man. He performs his job very earnestly, as he knows that [[Wax]] does not actually need to travel by coach since he is a [[Coinshot]].{{book ref|mb6|3}}

He loves broadsheets and horse racing.{{book ref|mb6|3}}

==History==
He became Wax's new coachman in {{date|341|PC}},{{book ref|mb6|3}} likely replacing [[Hoid]].{{book ref|mb5|7}} He was charged with caring for the coach and its horses, along with his driving duties.{{book ref|mb6|3}}

He picked up Wax and [[Wayne]] from the crime scene after the raid on [[Migs]]'s gang on the morning of Wax's wedding.{{book ref|mb6|1}} After delivering them to [[Ladrian mansion]], Cob was dismissed early and given a broadsheet, and Wax presumed that he would spend the day at the races.{{book ref|mb6|3}}
