The '''Hawkwind''' was a flying vessel made from glass shaped like a bird.{{book ref|a3|1}} It ran on [[Silimatic]] technology, using brightsand. It was the vehicle that [[Leavenworth Smedry]], [[Alcatraz]], [[Bastille]], [[Draulin]], and others used to get from the [[Library of Alexandria]] to the Free Kingdoms.{{book ref|a3|1}} It crash landed on a tower of Keep Smedry after a bomb planted inside exploded.{{book ref|a3|2}}
