The '''Ralinsa''' is the main roadway of [[Kharbranth]].{{book ref|sa1|36}} 

The Ralinsa consists of switchbacks that crisscross the steep [[lait]] that contains Kharbranth. It connects the port at sea level to the [[Conclave]] at the top of the city.{{book ref|sa1|36}} Because Kharbranth is so steep and crowded, merchants sometimes use porters instead of [[chull]] carts to transport their goods through streets and walkways.{{book ref|sa1|71}} Wealthy [[lighteyes]] often travel the Ralinsa in palanquins.{{book ref|sa1|36}}

While the Ralinsa itself is safe, it has turnoffs to less desirable areas frequented by thieves and footpads.{{book ref|sa1|36}}{{book ref|sa1|70}}
