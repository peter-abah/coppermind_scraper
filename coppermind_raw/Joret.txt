'''Joret''' is a [[Skybreaker]] squire under the Skybreaker masters [[Ki]] and [[Warren]].{{book ref|sa3|98}}

He competes against [[Szeth]] in a martial competence test over the [[Purelake]], to test their ability with the [[Surge]] of Gravitation. The squires have to throw bags of colored powder at each other and avoid being hit. Joret's tactic was to hover in one place defending a pole of bags. It didn't work well for him, as remaining still made him a target.{{book ref|sa3|98}}
