The '''''MW-232''''' is a class of [[Malwish]] military spacecraft. The MW-232s serve as dogfighters to the Malwish, and although they are built for combat in space, they are capable of fighting in Shadesmar as well.{{book ref|iote|35}} The Malwish often claim that the MW-232 is the best fighter in the entire [[cosmere]], with both a regular [[aether]] engine for propulsion, and the ability to [[Steel|Push]] and [[Iron|Pull]] on starship anchors in a proper [[Scadrian]] [[steelfield]], making them capable of outmaneuvering any ordinary ship.{{book ref|iote|38}} The MW-232 is capable of vaporizing a [[Sleepless]], and each fighter also has a sort of [[Sleepless interference machine]].{{book ref|iote|46}}

The MW-232 only makes use of Scadrian technology, and this, along with the pilots' training, has led to the fact that they don't rely on [[Lodestar_(technology)|lodestar]]s to pilot their craft.{{book ref|iote|36}} The Malwish Colonel [[Dajer]] had six MW-232s with him while trying to find a [[perpendicularity]] in the [[Drominad system]], he eventually lost one of them.{{book ref|iote|39}} Additionally, [[Leonore]] flew an MW-232 before she deserted.{{book ref|iote|35}}

MW-232s -- and all starfighters -- are expensive, but recently, their price has risen more.{{book ref|iote|39}}
