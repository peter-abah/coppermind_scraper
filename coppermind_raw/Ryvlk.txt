'''Ryvlk''' is a [[Thaylen]] person on [[Roshar]]. He owns a menagerie, which he brought to the [[Shattered Plains]] at some point during the [[War of Reckoning]].{{rpg ref|slsw|44}}

== Appearance and Personality ==
Ryvlk has dyed his long eyebrows and beard blue, and he wears a colorful motley vest.{{rpg ref|slsw|44}} Like the vest, the rest of his clothes are also colorful. He is flamboyant, fast-talking and somewhat reckless. Above all else, he wants to outdo his rivals, especially [[Volikk]], to be the most magniﬁcent menagerie in the warcamps. As far as Ryvlk is concerned, an exciting lie is worth far more than a boring truth.{{rpg ref|slsw|45}}

{{sidequote|Come see the weird and wonderful fauna from every corner of Roshar! From tiny cremling to lurching greatshell!|Ryvlk calling out to passersby|side=right|size=300px}}

He talks loudly, spins tall tales, and drapes himself and his wares in as many colors as he can. His curiosity for the world’s wonders is matched only by his tendency to take great risks. Ryvlk is particularly keen to one-up an [[Azish]] menagerie keeper named Volikk. who is pitched in the Outer Market. Ryvlk considers Volikk’s [[whitespine]] “dull, boring, and a travesty against entertainment.”{{rpg ref|slsw|45}} He is a performer and a charlatan, most of his display pieces are partially or wholly fake.{{rpg ref|slsw|44}} He is the babsk of an unnamed Thaylen woman.{{rpg ref|slsw|46}}

== History ==
At some point during the [[War of Reckoning]], he brought his menagerie to the [[Shattered Plains]]. He set up camp outside the crater wall of the [[Ruthar princedom|Ruthar]] warcamp, as he had previously made enemies among some of the merchants residing in the Outer Market. His menagerie is made up of several painted wagons, forming a half-circle. It has a dozen strange [[Shin]] chickens, which are actually just regular chickens with dyed feathers, rare ﬁsh from the [[Purelake]] and several fearsome whitespines.{{rpg ref|slsw|44}} Ryvlk also has a tiny red-breasted songbird from Shinovar and a goose from the [[Horneater Peaks]]. He won the songbird gambling with a Shin traveler in the [[Aladar princedom|Aladar]] warcamp.{{rpg ref|slsw|45}} At some point after acquiring two new whitespines, an incident occurred. The two whitespines escaped and rampaged through the menagerie. It is unknown what happened to them, though Ryvlk was fine.{{rpg ref|slsw|46}}
