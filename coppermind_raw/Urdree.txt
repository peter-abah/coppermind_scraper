'''Urdree''' was a philosopher in the [[Final Empire]] on [[Scadrial]].{{book ref|mb3|32}}

When [[Aradan Yomen]] and [[Elend Venture]] discussed religion, Elend claimed that Urdree was more poet than philosopher, who had ignored convention and never gave proper attributions. Elend thought that if Yomen had quoted [[Hardren]], it would have given him a much better foundation.
