The '''Perpetual''' is a small cruiser and a command vessel within the fleet of the [[Stormwind]]. It is under the sub-command of [[Dennison Crestmar|Dennison]] during the battle between [[Varion Crestmar|Varion]]'s forces and those of [[Kern|Admiral Kern]]. Part of the ships retinue  contains 20 fighters, and a support vessel known as the [[Windless]] - a long-range gunship.{{book ref|firstborn}}
