'''Fialia''' is a woman kidnapped by the [[Set]] on [[Scadrial]]. She is an [[iron]] [[Misting]] (aka a [[Lurcher]]).{{book ref|mb7|56}} After her kidnapping, she was a part of the [[Community]] project.

== History ==
She was kidnapped by the [[Vanishers]], who were employed by the [[Set]], sometime during {{date|341|PC}}. After she woke up, Fialia was told that the world would soon end, with the [[Ashmounts]] erupting and [[Hemalurgic chimera|metal mutants]] rampaging. She was also told that she was part of a group chosen to carry on human civilization and that her 'rescuers' had to stage a kidnapping to prevent mass panic. She spent the first two years being moved around several cavern complexes, before arriving at [[Wayfarer]], where she would stay for the next five years. During these years, she became completely convinced that what the Set had told her had really happened.{{book ref|mb7|56}}{{book ref|mb7|57}} During {{date|348|PC}}, she, [[Armal]], and [[Drenya]] spotted an [[Marasi Colms|outsider]] walking through Wayfarer. They immediately dragged her inside, planning to hide her from the [[Gave Entrone|mayor]], and Armal sent Fialia to fetch multiple friends of theirs.{{book ref|mb7|56}} After returning, Marasi told everyone that they'd been lied to and that the world hadn't ended.{{book ref|mb7|57}} Marasi decided that she needed to find Gave Entrone, and the women, including Fialia, took her to Entrone's mansion.{{book ref|mb7|59}} After saving Marasi from Gave Entrone, the Allomancers, under directions from Marasi, burned up all the [[Investiture]] in [[Autonomy]]'s nearby [[perpendicularity]].{{book ref|mb7|64}} Afterwards, Fialia and the other captives were taken to the surface by Marasi.{{book ref|mb7|68}} She was possibly part of the group Marasi escorted to [[Blantach]]'s constabulary offices to give evidence against Entrone.{{book ref|mb7|71}}{{book ref|mb7|epilogue|1}}
