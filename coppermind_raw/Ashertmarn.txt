'''Ashertmarn''', the '''Heart of the Revel''', is a mindless [[Unmade]] on [[Roshar]].{{epigraph ref|sa3|106}} During the [[True Desolation]], his residence in [[Kholinar]] and his influence on [[Aesudan]] undermine the [[Alethi]] rule in the city and leave it open to [[singer]] domination during the [[Siege of Kholinar]].{{book ref|sa3|84}}

== Appearance and Personality ==
Ashertmarn takes the form of a giant, black, inhuman heart that beats to an irregular rhythm. Thick black veins sprout from the heart, melding with the ground nearby.{{book ref|sa3|78}} Ashertmarn is described as mindless.{{epigraph ref|sa3|104}}

== Attributes and Abilities ==
Ashertmarn leads people to indulge in excess. He speaks to the people in his area, urging them to abandon their lives and responsibilities. The closer one is to him, the harder it is to resist Ashertmarn's tempting plea.{{book ref|sa3|78}} A few rare revelers give themselves over so utterly to the Unmade that they become conduits of his power whenever he’s near, compelling others to join the debauchery.{{rpg ref|aa|7}}

== History ==
{{image|Ashertmarn by Eccos.jpg|side=left|width=250px|Facing off with [[Shallan|Veil]] in [[Kholinar]]}}
{{for|/Gallery|more images}}
Ashertmarn awakened/arrived in [[Kholinar]] when the [[Everstorm]] first passed over the city.{{book ref|sa3|62}}{{wob ref|12054}}

During the mission to aid Kholinar, [[Shallan Davar]] discovered the presence of two [[Unmade]], including Ashertmarn.{{book ref|sa3|77}} In order to investigate the revel, Shallan created a reputation by delivering food to the poor. Once she had been heard of, she took a large offering of food to the revel and was accepted onto the [[Oathgate]], Ashertmarn's location. The Oathgate was divided into three sections. In the outer section, the people delighted in an abundance of delicious foods, mindlessly sating their hunger for the revel. In the next ring inward, the occupants crawled from place to place, too focused on the excess to even stand. The food there was rotten and moldy, infested with [[decayspren]]. In the center ring was the Unmade himself, completely covering the Oathgate's control building. Shallan would not have been able to extract herself from the revel if not for the interruption of [[Sja-anat]], who appeared to Shallan, breaking her focus on the black heart.{{book ref|sa3|78}}

Later, when Shallan had to clear the Oathgate platform as an escape route for the people of Kholinar, she tried to banish it the same way she did with [[Re-Shephir]]. After trying to convince Shallan to indulge mindlessly, Ashertmarn retreated for reasons unknown.{{book ref|sa3|84}} It's possible he did so so that the Oathgate, whose spren had been corrupted by Sja-anat, could kill Shallan, [[Kaladin]], [[Adolin]], and [[Azure]].
