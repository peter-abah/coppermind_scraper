'''Alezarv''' was a person existing in the time prior to the [[Recreance]] on [[Roshar]]. They may have been a member of the [[Knights Radiant]].{{epigraph ref|sa1|39}} 

At some point, Alezarv used an [[Oathgate]]. This was recorded in the text ''Among the Darkeyed'' (suggesting perhaps Alezarv was darkeyed) by [[Calinam]] and [[Jasnah Kholin]] included it in her [[Jasnah's notebook|notebook]].{{epigraph ref|sa1|39}}
