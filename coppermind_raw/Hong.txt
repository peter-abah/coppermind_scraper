Mrs. '''Hong''' is a resident of [[Igneous Cavern]]. She lives in an apartment near [[Spensa]]'s home.{{book ref|sky1|45}}

Mrs. Hong is an older women, who has treated Spensa unkindly in the past. When Spensa returns to Igneous to talk with her mother and [[Becca Nightshade|grandmother]], she noted that Mrs. Hong no longer scowled at her, and instead avoided her, retreating into her apartment when Spensa went past.{{book ref|sky1|45}}
