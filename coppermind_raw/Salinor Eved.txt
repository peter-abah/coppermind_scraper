[[Brightlord]] '''Salinor Eved''' is a [[Shardbearer]] from the princedom of [[Thanadal]] on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa2|14}}

==Attributes and Abilities==
Salinor is a duelist who fights in [[Flamestance]].{{book ref|sa2|14}} He has a [[Shardblade]], but no [[Shardplate]]. While Salinor is skilled, he often boasts that he is a better duelist than the rankings show.{{book ref|sa2|14}}

==History==
[[Adolin]] and his father [[Dalinar]] hoped to embarrass [[Torol Sadeas]] and the others in his faction by gaining all of their Shards through duels.{{book ref|sa2|5}} Adolin's friend [[Jakamav]] suggested Salinor as a target, due to his boasting about his skill. In order to get Salinor to agree to the duel, Adolin had to wager both his Blade and his Plate for Salinor's Blade alone, a proposal that demeaned Adolin.{{book ref|sa2|14}}

When Adolin and Salinor fought in their duel, Salinor borrowed a set of the King's Plate. Adolin abandoned his traditional [[Windstance]] to fight in [[Ironstance]] in a brutal display of strength and skill. While Adolin broke tradition in his conduct during the duel, he did not violate any rules, so Highlady [[Istow]] was forced to award the victory to Adolin. Salinor initially refused to give up his Blade, but after Adolin threatened him, he broke the bond and gave it to Adolin. Adolin bestowed the Blade upon his brother, [[Renarin Kholin]], so that he could have a complete set of Shards.{{book ref|sa2|14}}

Adolin thought that beating Salinor would make Shardbearers more inclined to duel him, which turned out to not be the case.{{book ref|sa2|22}}
