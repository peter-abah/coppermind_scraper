The '''Acumidian delver''' is a [[delver]] that crossed into the universe nearly two hundred years before the [[Battle of Alta Second]].{{book ref|sky2|21}} 

It destroyed the planet of [[Farhaven]] by vaporizing its population. This event was captured on video, and was later used in a Superiority training program at [[Starsight (station)|Starsight]] to fight against the delvers.{{book ref|sky2|21}}
