'''Caelith''' is a [[Order of Lightweavers|Lightweaver]] on [[Roshar]].{{rpg ref|ss|24}} While he was undercover in [[Marabethia]], Caelith met a [[Fused]] named [[Talenai]]. The two of them slowly fell in love, and after Caelith was discovered by the enemy, Talenai helped him evade capture. In response, [[Odium]] removed Talenai’s powers; when Talenai fled, [[Order of Skybreakers|Skybreakers]] captured and sentenced them to death by Marabethian custom. Afterwards, Caelith recruited a group of people to rescue Talenai.{{rpg ref|ss|25}}
