'''Nightmare Flight''' is a group of [[DDF]] pilots. Callsign: Nose is flight leader of [[Nightmare Flight]].{{book ref|Skyward|46}} The Flight graduated in the class prior to Skyward Flight.{{book ref|sky1|9}} At least one of the Flight members flies a Poco.{{book ref|sky1|47}} 

== History ==

Nightmare Flight were on rest and relaxation down in the deep caverns when Skyward Flight was first called into duty, they later relieved Skyward Flight after they acted as decoy reinforcements.{{book ref|sky1|9}}

As part of their training, [[Skyward Flight]] was paired with Nightmare Flight for a routine debris tagging mission. During that time, Spensa and FM were offered positions on Nightmare Flight by Nose after he witnessed their skill with light-lances.{{book ref|sky1|46}} Afterwards, the group was attacked by the Krell and meant to receive reinforcement from Riptide Flight. Before those arrived, however, Spensa was shot down, forcing her to eject and avoid crashing.{{book ref|Skyward|46}}{{book ref|Skyward|47}}

Later, Nightmare Flight participated in the [[Second Battle of Alta]].{{book ref|sky1|48}}
