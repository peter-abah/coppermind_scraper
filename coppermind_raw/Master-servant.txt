A '''master-servant''' is a [[Vorin]] profession that can be held by [[darkeyed]] citizens of the second [[nahn]]. They are hired to work for well-off [[lighteyes]] and to attend to their needs. They perform a number of duties including, but not limited to, fetching refreshments and meals, delivering messages, staffing events and institutions, and even act as guides. A variety of hand signals can be used to indicate to the master-servant what task is required, or simply to request assistance.{{book ref|sa1|3}}{{book ref|sa2|prologue}}{{book ref|sa1|54}}{{book ref|sa1|7}}{{book ref|sa1|8}}{{book ref|sa4|prologue}}

The standard uniform for a master-servant includes a crisp white shirt with an emblem over the breast and either black pants or a black skirt. {{book ref|sa1|7}}{{book ref|sa1|8}} They also wear white gloves and sometimes tuck them in their belts.{{book ref|sa4|43}} Those with long hair will often wear it bound.{{book ref|sa1|7}} Master-servants in training have an all black uniform and there is no emblem on their shirts.{{book ref|Sa1|8}} The refined manner in which they act is a point of pride for most master-servants, though this attitude is mocked by those of other classes.{{book ref|sa1|3}}

Master-servants staff the [[Kharbranth]]ian [[Conclave]] and the [[Palanaeum]].{{book ref|sa1|7}}{{book ref|sa1|39}}

[[Szeth]] posed as a master-servant when he broke into the Conclave in Kharbranth to kill [[Taravangian]].{{book ref|sa1|71}}

During the invasion of [[Urithiru]] master-servants often took double duty as messengers.{{book ref|sa4|43}}

In Urithiru, master-servants have quarters of their own, where they contain maps of the lower floors.{{book ref|sa4|74}}
