'''Emberdark Channel''' is a channel in the [[Sea of Lost Lights]].{{map ref|Sea of Lost Lights}} It connects a river leading to [[Celebrant]], and the [[North Hallen Channel|North]] and [[South Hallen Channel|South Hallen Channels]] to [[Emberdark (settlement)|Emberdark]], and thus towards the landmass that contains the [[Burning Gardens]].{{map ref|Sea of Lost Lights}}
