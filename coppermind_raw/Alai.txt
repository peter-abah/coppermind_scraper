'''Alai''' is a scholar who lives on [[Roshar]] during the [[Era of Solitude]] and studies [[spren]].{{book ref|sa2|6}}

Alai claims that spren are fragments of the [[Shard|powers of creation]], which is a common opinion among scholars.{{book ref|sa2|3}} She also studies spren that have gained sentience, such as the [[Nightwatcher]]. Alai claims that the Nightwatcher is one of these, and that she is definitely female, contrary to [[Alethi]] folktales.{{book ref|sa2|6}}

[[Shallan Davar]] reads some of Alai's work while studying sentient spren.{{book ref|sa2|6}}{{book ref|sa2|3}}
