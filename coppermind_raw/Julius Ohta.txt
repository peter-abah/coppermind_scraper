'''Julius Ohta''' is one of the artists of the graphic novel adaptation of [[Brandon Sanderson]]'s ''[[White Sand (prose)|White Sand]]''. He is credited as the artist of chapter 6 of ''[[White Sand|White Sand Volume 2]]''. His other work for Dynamite Comics includes ''Bettie Page'' and ''Sherlock Holmes: The Vanishing Man''.{{url ref|url=https://comicvine.gamespot.com/julius-ohta/4040-70404/|text=Comic Vine profile|site=Comic Vine|date=date unknown}}

He was brought on to the ''White Sand'' project to complete volume 2 due to timeliness issues with the original artist, [[Julius Gopez]].{{wob ref|12257}} Brandon hoped to have Julius Ohta do the art for ''[[White Sand|White Sand Volume 3]]'',{{wob ref|12257}} but he was ultimately unavailable.{{wob ref|9468}}

==Trivia==
*Some versions of ''White Sand Volume 2'' misspell his name as "Julius Otha".
