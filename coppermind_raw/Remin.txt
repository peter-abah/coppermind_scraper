'''Lady Remin''' is a noblewoman on [[Scadrial]]. She attended [[Lady ZoBell's party]] in [[Elendel]].{{book ref|mb5|9}} 

She was affected by [[Wax]]'s [[steel]] bubble that he unconsciously kept up around him at almost all times. Wax unintentionally steelpushed her ring into her drink as he walked by, which [[Steris]] found amusing.{{book ref|mb5|9}}
