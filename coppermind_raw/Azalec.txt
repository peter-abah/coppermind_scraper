'''Azalec''' is a wealthy person in the [[Rose Empire]] on [[Sel]].{{tes ref|97}}

He is known only from a letter forged by [[Shai]] that was intended to turn her [[Striker]] guards against [[Weedfingers]], the [[Bloodsealer]] who helped imprison her in the [[Rose Palace]]. The letter referred to Azalec as well-known in [[Dzhamar]] for earning a large amount of money in the Southern Provinces of the empire.{{tes ref|97}} Although the letter was forged, Shai went to great lengths to ensure that it seemed authentically written by Weedfingers, and it is likely that the information about Azalec was accurate.
