'''Brittels''' are a form of plant on [[Roshar]].

[[Lift]] sees some in [[Yeddaw]].{{book ref|Edgedancer|11}}

They are shaped like flat plates for protection from weather, but have stems that grow around the sides and up toward the sunlight.
