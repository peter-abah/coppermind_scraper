'''Fuego''' (Spanish for '''Fire''') is an [[Epic]] in [[Prof]]'s squad. Fuego presumably has some variety of fire abilities.{{book ref|Calamity|28}}

[[Prof]] had Fuego use a dowser to find [[Larcener]], and wanted Fuego to make a big show of where they were looking.{{book ref|Calamity|28}}

It is unknown as to Fuego's fate. It is also unknown if they have any relationship to the Epic the [[Reckoners]] killed, [[Puños de Fuego]], or if Fuego simply is another fire-related Epic.{{book ref|Firefight|31}}
