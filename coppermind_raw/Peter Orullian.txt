'''Peter Orullian''' is a novelist and musician. He is the coauthor of [[Brandon Sanderson]]'s planned novel ''[[Songs of the Dead]]''.{{sots ref|2019}}

In 2010, Brandon mentioned wanting to read the first book in Peter's ''Vault of Heaven'' epic fantasy series, titled ''The Unremembered''.{{wob ref|10240}} He also read the second book in the series, ''Trial of Intentions'', after it was published in 2015.{{wob ref|6828}}

==Collaboration==
Brandon had been sitting on an idea for a necromancer novel for many years. He changed the protagonist from a pizza delivery man to a heavy metal singer and decided to bring in a coauthor, and he immediately thought of Peter, who is also a heavy metal musician and has toured internationally.{{sots ref|2018}} Brandon completed the outline and worldbuilding, while Peter wrote the first draft and developed the characters.{{wob ref|12209}} The coauthored novel, which is tentatively titled ''Songs of the Dead'', is in the revision process as of the end of 2019.{{sots ref|2019}}
