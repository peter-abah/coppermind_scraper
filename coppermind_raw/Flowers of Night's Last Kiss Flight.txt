'''Flowers of Night's Last Kiss''' is the name [[Hesho]] gives to [[Spensa]]'s flight on [[Starsight (station)|Starsight]] after he and Spensa agree that '''Flight Fifteen''', the name assigned to them by the [[Superiority]] command, is too boring. Although the most of the members of the flight pick callsigns, in the end they don't actually use them and instead just go with their actual names.{{book ref|sky2|21}}
