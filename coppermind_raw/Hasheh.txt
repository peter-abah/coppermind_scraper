Brightness '''Hasheh''' is a [[lighteyes|lighteyed]] scholar and tutor on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa2|45}}

Hasheh worked as [[Shallan Davar]]'s tutor at the Davar estates in [[Jah Keved]] around the year {{Rosharan date|1170}}. She was an expert in languages and taught Shallan some [[Azish]]. Shallan also noted that Hasheh taught her etiquette, and would chide her for having poor posture.{{book ref|sa2|45}}

Hasheh abruptly left her position after one of [[Lin Davar]]'s unhinged attacks on [[Malise Gevelmar]].{{book ref|sa2|45}} Hasheh was one of a series of Shallan's tutors who quit their jobs due to Lin's instability,{{book ref|sa2|65}} and Shallan generally remembered them as "stern matrons".{{book ref|sa1|5}}
