The '''Well of Control''' is a [[perpendicularity]] associated with [[Odium]], and later [[Retribution]].

== Location and Appearance ==
{{quote
|We must travel to the Well of Control, within the shroud of the fragments of the dead moon. There, we will find our destiny. We cannot stop him from destroying us. It is time.
|[[Sleepless]]{{epigraph ref|sa5|41}}{{epigraph ref|sa5|42}}
}}

The Well of Control is located at the [[Shattered Plains]], hidden beneath a rocky cavern with no exits.{{book ref|sa5|90}}

The Well appears as a pool, roughly thirty yards wide, full of thick liquid.{{book ref|sa5|90}} As Odium's perpendicularity, it is a shimmering golden color, while as Retribution's it is a brilliant blue-black.{{book ref|sa5|90}}{{book ref|sa5|146}} It pulses with a [[rhythm]] that is difficult to hear, as it is located within the fragmentary remnants of Roshar's fourth moon, which can block even a Shard's sight.{{book ref|sa5|90}}{{book ref|sa5|120}} Being [[Connection|Connected]] to both [[Honor]] and [[Odium]] may help a person sense its rhythm.{{book ref|sa5|90}}

== Purpose ==
Those who use the Well of Control are able to gain the powers of Odium.{{book ref|sa5|120}}{{book ref|sa5|121}} This allows the user to [[Connection|Connect]] to the [[singer]]s and [[spren]] of [[Roshar]], Connect to certain lands of Roshar, or create their own version of [[Fused]].{{book ref|sa5|128}}{{book ref|sa5|120}}{{book ref|sa5|121}}

== History and Mythology ==
During the [[False Desolation]], [[Ba-Ado-Mishram]] found the Well of Control and used its powers to Connect to the singers on Roshar.{{book ref|sa5|121}} [[Ishar]] would later find and take up some of the powers of Odium from the Well.{{book ref|sa5|120}}{{book ref|sa5|128}}

After Odium and Honor combined to make [[Retribution]], the Well of Control drained, before refilling with Retribution's [[Investiture]].{{book ref|sa5|146}}
