'''Thomas York''' is the principal of [[Armedius Academy]].{{book ref|Rithmatist|25}}

Despite being in charge of one of the eight Rithmatic Academies of the [[United Isles of America]], he is notably not a [[Rithmatics|Rithmatist]].{{cite}} 

He had a close relationship with [[Trent Saxon]], [[Joel Saxon|Joel's]] father.{{cite}} After Trent died in a [[springrail]] accident, he awarded Joel a scholarship for free tuition so he could afford to attend Armedius.{{book ref|Rithmatist|6}}
