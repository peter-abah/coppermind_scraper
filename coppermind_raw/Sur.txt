'''Sur''' is a kingdom on [[Roshar]] during the [[Desolation]]s.{{book ref|sa1|60}} [[Nohadon]] said to [[Dalinar]] in one of his [[Dalinar's visions|visions]] that Sur had almost certainly vanished from the war.

Dalinar had never heard of the place.
