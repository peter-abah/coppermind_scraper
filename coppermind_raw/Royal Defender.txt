'''Royal Defender''' is a title traditionally given to a full Shardbearer of [[Thanadal princedom]] in [[Alethkar]] on [[Roshar]]. The Royal Defender's [[Shardplate]] is painted yellow.{{book ref|sa1|58}}

The tradition of the Royal Defender lasted for centuries. Even though Thanadal had other Shards, only the Royal Defender was given both Blade and Plate. In {{Rosharan date|1173}}, Brightlord [[Resi]] was the holder of the Shards and the title.{{book ref|sa1|58}}
