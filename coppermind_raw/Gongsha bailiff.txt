The '''bailiff''' of is a resident of [[Gongsha Town]].

When [[Yumi]] declared she would be destroying the scholars' [[machine]], [[Liyun]] sent [[Hwanji]] to get the bailiff in order to help execute the [[yoki-hijo]]'s decision. The bailiff, along with a dozen men carrying hammers, accompanied [[Nikaro]] and Yumi's retinue to confront the scholars. The bailiff turns into a nightmare along with the others before they can accomplish their task.{{book ref|yumi|34}} Later, when Yumi searches for the machine, she finds the scholars have hidden it in the bailiff's home.{{book ref|yumi|38}}
