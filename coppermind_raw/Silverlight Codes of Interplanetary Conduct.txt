The '''Silverlight Codes of Interplanetary Conduct''' are a set of laws governing interactions between [[worldhopper]]s.{{book ref|tsm|43}}

== Bills ==
The Codes are a collection of bills, only one of which is currently known. The '''Refugee and Lost Expatriate Bill''' allows individuals to request asylum in interplanetary facilities, and likely from other entities as well, and seems to force such entities into giving asylum under unspecified circumstances.{{book ref|tsm|43}}
