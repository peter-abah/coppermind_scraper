The '''secabird''' is a bird with bright plumage that is native to the lowlands of [[Duladel]] on [[Sel]].{{glossary ref|Elantris|S}}

[[Raoden]] compared the colors of the clothing worn by the inhabitants of [[New Elantris]], made from cloth supplied by [[Sarene]] as part of her [[Widow's Trial]], to those of secabirds.{{book ref|Elantris|41}}
