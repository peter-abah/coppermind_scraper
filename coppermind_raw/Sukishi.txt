'''Sukishi''' is an old [[nightmare]] [[painter]] foreman in [[Kilahito]] on [[Komashi]]. He has a small office in the [[Nightmare Division]] headquarters.{{book ref|yumi|14}}

== Appearance & Personality ==
Sukishi is a large man with white hair and the complexion of an elderly person. He wears thick trousers and a button-up shirt.{{book ref|yumi|11}}

== History ==
[[Nikaro]] visited Sukishi's office earlier on the night that the painter first spotted [[Liyun|a stable nightmare]], to turn in two paintings of [[nightmare]]s.{{book ref|yumi|5}} After Nikaro did not report for work the following day, the foreman stopped by Nikaro's apartment, where he met [[Yumi]] and asked her to have the painter report in.{{book ref|yumi|11}} Yumi later visited Sukishi's office to report that Nikaro was sick, and he did not believe the story. When Yumi then claimed that Nikaro faced a stable [[nightmare]], Sukishi assumed it to be a complete fabrication on Nikaro's part and did not report the sighting to the [[Dreamwatch]].{{book ref|yumi|14}} 

Sukishi eventually had other painters cover Nikaro's territory, after he stopped showing up for work.{{book ref|yumi|25}} Later, when the stable nightmare was encountered once more, [[Tojin]] reported to Sukishi, who summoned the Dreamwatch after seeing the damage caused by the creature firsthand.{{book ref|yumi|30}}

== Relationships ==
=== Nikaro ===
Sukishi is aware of the way Nikaro lied about joining the Dreamwatch and skipped his classes, giving him an extremely negative and mistrusting opinion of the painter.{{book ref|yumi|14}} He also dislikes the way Nikaro repeatedly uses bamboo to paint nightmares, considering the technique to be lazy.{{book ref|yumi|5}} He was surprised by the idea of Nikaro having a woman in his apartment, after meeting Yumi there.{{book ref|yumi|14}} Nikaro can sense the man's contempt for him.{{book ref|yumi|14}}
