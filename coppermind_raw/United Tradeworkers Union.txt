'''United Tradeworkers Union''' is a union in [[Elendel]] on [[Scadrial]] made up of Line Riveters and Shovelmen.{{file ref|Elendel_Daily_broadsheet.png|''The Elendel Daily'' broadsheet}}

==History==
The United Tradeworkers Union was in negotiations with the Noble Houses Collective about fair pay and rights of their employees, until Erols Durnsed the Union’s leader announced that he no longer supported the continuation of negotiations. This sparked rumors of secret meeting between Durnsed and the Noble houses. Members of the Union reacted with outrage toward Erols and some members him with violence but were covered by union representatives saying that they were speaking in metaphor.{{file ref|Elendel_Daily_broadsheet.png|''The Elendel Daily'' broadsheet}}
