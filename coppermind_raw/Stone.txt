The '''Stone''' is an ancient [[spren]] on [[Roshar]] who was created by [[Adonalsium]] alongside the planet, the [[Wind]], and the [[Night]].{{book ref|sa5|4}}{{book ref|sa5|52}}

The Stone does not have a mortal standard of reference of passage of time, describing multiple events as being not very long ago. It remembers the days singers could mold it on their own without being [[Knights Radiant|Radiant]]. It sings joyously of the past, as heard in [[Urithiru]] by [[Venli]]. The Stone doesn’t care about the future, considering that to be the work of the Wind.{{book ref|sa5|55}}

== Attributes and Abilities ==
=== Embodiment of Stone ===
Being the embodiment of Roshar's stone, the Stone is nearly omnipresent. It watches much of what goes on throughout the world, and remembers all that happens within it.{{book ref|sa5|58}} It remembers when Singers were able to mold it with ease using Stoneshaping.{{book ref|sa5|60}} It is able to interact with Stoneshaping in order to manipulate what images are formed as to communicate with others.{{book ref|sa5|58}}{{book ref|sa5|60}}

=== Nahel Bond (Historical) === 
{{for|Order of Bondsmiths}}
Prior to the creation of the [[Sibling]] from the Stone, they were one of three Bondsmith spren.{{book ref|sa5|21}} As such, it would follow that forming a Nahel bond with them may have granted a Bondsmith power over [[Connection]] and other Spiritual attributes, but the full scope of its effects are unknown.

The Stone has been able to speak to the inhabitants of Roshar since its creation, but for an unknown reason, possibly related to Odium, chose not to do so for a long time.{{book ref|sa5|33}}

== History ==
=== Creation and Pre-Desolation Roshar ===
The Stone was created by Adonalsium along with Roshar prior to the [[Shattering]], over ten thousand years before the [[Night of Sorrows]].{{book ref|sa5|4}}{{book ref|sa5|52}}{{book ref|sa5|100}} It is said to be an echo of it and to possess its voice.{{book ref|sa5|100}} It was given instructions to protect, shroud, and nurture Roshar and its inhabitants.{{book ref|sa5|100}} It is the Stone of the very planet itself, and it remembers when the Singers used to mold it with ease, and [[Connection|Connect]] to it using Stoneshaping.{{book ref|sa5|60}}

Prior to the arrival of [[Odium]], the Stone, along with the Wind, was worshipped by the [[Singers]].{{book ref|sa5|43}}{{book ref|sa5|55}}

=== The Heraldic Epochs ===
In time, the primeval spren, including the Stone, went silent, with the Night entirely leaving the planet.{{cite}} Part of the essence of the Stone was used to create the [[Sibling]].{{book ref|sa5|21}}

=== The True Desolation ===
Eventually, in {{Rosharan date|1175}} in the days leading up to the [[Night of Sorrows]], the Stone began to stir once more and communicate with people. It particularly began to communicate with a [[Listener]] named [[Venli]], granting her information about Roshar's history and leading her towards the [[Well of Control]].{{cite}}
