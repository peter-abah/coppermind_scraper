'''Marple''' is, likely, a fictitious character created by [[Tress]] on [[Lumar]].{{book ref|tress|20}}

Tress mentioned Marple, [[Kaplan]], and [[Mallory]] when she was lying to [[Crow]] about having family on the ''[[Oot's Dream]]'' because she wanted to run away from the ''[[Crow's Song]]''. She wanted Crow to believe that she had cause to tell people that the crew of the ''Crow's Song'' had killed all the people on the ''Oot's Dream'' and thus were [[deadrunner]]s.{{book ref|totes|20}} Crow gave her the opportunity to leave at [[Shimmerbay]] because of this lie, but Tress ultimately opted to stay.{{book ref|tress|21}}
