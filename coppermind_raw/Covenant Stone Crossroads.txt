The '''Covenant Stone Crossroads''' is a crossroads in the [[Unclaimed Hills]] on [[Roshar]]. It is named for the '''Covenant Stone''', a large cliff protecting the crossroads. The cliff wall stands five hundred feet high and twice as long. It provides a natural barrier against [[highstorm]]s, making it a popular waypoint for caravans.{{rpg ref|slsw|19}} The cliﬀ wall, a well-known landmark where promises are made, is covered in burned and painted [[glyph]]s. Since the [[War of Reckoning]] started, a number of business deals, minor truces, and oaths of fealty were conducted at the crossroads.{{rpg ref|slsw|20}} Shortly before the arrival of the [[Everstorm]], bandits attacked two caravans resting at the Covenant Stone.{{rpg ref|slsw|29}}
