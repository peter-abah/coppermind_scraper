Brightlady '''Jayla Ruthar''' is an [[Alethkar|Alethi]] noblewoman on [[Roshar]] during the [[Era of Solitude]].{{book ref|sa2|38}}

==Appearance and Personality==
[[Jasnah Kholin]] believes that Jayla and her husband are thieving and dangerous. They are usually political allies of [[Torol Sadeas|Torol]] and [[Ialai Sadeas]],{{book ref|sa2|38}}{{book ref|sa3|107}} who use them for their ambition.{{book ref|sa2|29}}

==History==
At some point in her youth, Jayla wed Highprince [[Ruthar]];{{book ref|sa2|38}} by {{Rosharan date|1145}} she had given birth to their eldest son [[Relis Ruthar|Relis]]{{book ref|sa2|37}} and later had at least one more child, a son named [[Ivanar Ruthar|Ivanar]].{{book ref|sa4|50}} She is the aunt of [[Elit Ruthar]].{{book ref|sa2|56}}

During the [[War of Reckoning]], she lived on the [[Shattered Plains]], presumably in the Ruthar warcamp. In late {{Rosharan date|1173}}, she attended a meeting in the [[Pinnacle]] with other members of the Alethi nobility to discuss the threat of the [[Assassin in White]].{{book ref|sa2|38}}

Jayla's fate during the [[True Desolation]] is unclear. Highprince Ruthar moved to [[Urithiru]] after the [[Battle of Narak]];{{book ref|sa3|107}} he was later stripped of his title and exiled to [[Azimir]], and Relis became the new highprince.{{book ref|sa4|50}}{{book ref|sa4|54}} Jayla is not mentioned in connection with these events.
