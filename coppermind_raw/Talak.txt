'''Talak''' was a [[Veden]] man, who served [[Liss]], the Weeper, an assassin on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa2|prologue}}

Talak had a brutish look to him, and although he was a Veden, he had a speckle of red on his beard, hinting at [[Unkalaki]] heritage.{{book ref|sa2|prologue}}

Talak replaced [[Szeth]] as Liss's servant; Liss had found Szeth creepy.{{book ref|sa2|prologue}} Talak guarded the door when [[Jasnah Kholin]] met with Liss about the surveillance and the potential assassination of Queen [[Aesudan Kholin]], Jasnah's sister-in-law.
