'''Fedik''' was a [[Scadrian]] who lived during the [[Classical Scadrial|classical era]].

Fedik is known only from [[Alendi's journal]]. He was a member of the party that traveled with [[Alendi]] to the [[Well of Ascension]] shortly before the [[Ascension of the Lord Ruler]].{{book ref|mb1|29}} He was suspicious of the party's [[Terris]]men guides, fearing that they would abandon the group in the mountains.{{book ref|mb1|29}} This implies that Fedik was from [[Khlennium]] or one of the other nations that supported Alendi's quest.{{epigraph ref|mb1|10}}

During the journey, Fedik discovered [[Ruin's Perpendicularity]]. He was curious about it, but Alendi did not allow him to take a sample of the waters.{{book ref|mb1|33}} Fedik was subsequently stabbed in the chest by a mist spirit wielding an "invisible knife" that had been following the group.{{wob ref|6358}}{{book ref|mb1|29}}{{book ref|mb2|16}} Alendi wondered if the spirit had been angered by Fedik's interest in the Perpendicularity.{{book ref|mb1|33}} Fedik survived the stabbing and continued on the expedition, but he was traumatized by both his wound and the sight of the spirit;{{book ref|mb2|16}} Alendi feared that he would accidentally wander off of a cliffside.{{book ref|mb1|29}}

Alendi was the only other person that saw the mist spirit stab Fedik. [[Rashek]] spread rumors that Alendi was the actual perpetrator, causing Alendi to question his own sanity.{{book ref|mb2|16}}

==Trivia==
*The identity of the mist spirit that stabbed Fedik is unclear. Brandon has [[RAFO]]'d most questions about it, such as whether it was [[Preservation]] or [[Ruin]]{{wob ref|4417}} and the possibility of the knife being a [[Hemalurgy|Hemalurgic spike]].{{wob ref|9958}} It is not the same mist spirit later seen by [[Vin]] and [[Elend]].{{wob ref|6358}}
