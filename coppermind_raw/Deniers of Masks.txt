The '''Deniers of Masks''' are a group of people from [[Southern Scadrian|southern]] [[Scadrial]].{{book ref|mb6|21}} They are likely the Maskless mentioned on the Scadrial map.{{map ref|Scadrial}}

They are implied to avoid wearing the masks commonly worn by the people who live in the South.{{book ref|mb6|21}} The Deniers are dangerous for some unknown reason, and [[Allik Neverfar]] believes that the [[Bands of Mourning]] could be used to fight them. This term does not refer to the inhabitants of the [[Elendel Basin]] and the surrounding areas, as the Southern Scadrians simply refer to them as barbarians.{{book ref|mb6|21}}
