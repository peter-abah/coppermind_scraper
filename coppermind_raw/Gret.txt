'''Gret''' is a citizen of [[Diggen's Point]] on [[Lumar]]. She assists [[Tress]] in escaping the Rock.{{book ref|tress|8}}

== Appearance and Personality ==
Gret is a young, raven-haired woman. She is slight and small enough to fit comfortably in an ale barrel, and still have room to insulate the inside with feathers.{{book ref|tress|7}}

== History ==
=== Diggen's Point and Tress' Escape ===
Gret, like most residents of [[Diggen's Point]], lived on the Rock for most of her life. Similar to those that lived there with her, she was not allowed to leave.{{book ref|tress|1}}

She took part in the plan to help [[Tress]] escape aboard the ''[[Oot's Dream]]'' by hiding herself in an ale barrel full of feathers to muffle her breathing and subsequently allowing herself to be discovered by the [[inspector]]. The inspector loudly declared that Gret's betrayal would be reported to the [[Verdant King]].{{book ref|tress|7}} However, she; along with [[Brick]], [[Gremmy]], and her father [[Sor]], knew this to be a ruse, for the inspector was actually Tress in disguise. By the time Tress had boarded the ''Oot's Dream'' and sailed off, Gret had quietly slipped away. The only people to know of her involvement were Tress, Gremmy, Brick, and her father.{{book ref|tress|8}}

=== Tress' Return ===
Tress returned to Diggen's Point as captain of the ''[[Two Cups]]'', but only for long enough to deliver the king's new instructions, along with several other letters, and retrieve her family. During her return, she took special care to thank Gremmy, Brick, Gret, and Sor for their assistance in helping her initially escape the Rock.{{book ref|tress|epilogue}}
