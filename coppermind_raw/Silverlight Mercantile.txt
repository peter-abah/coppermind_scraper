'''Silverlight Mercantile''' is an enterprise based out of [[Silverlight]] whose business include an interplanetary postal service, which operates through mysterious [[Messengers|messengers]].{{book ref|mb7|epilogue|6}}

[[Harmony]] used them in order to send [[MeLaan]] a message concerning [[Wayne]]'s passing.{{book ref|mb7|epilogue|6}} They manufacture clocks with replaceable faces which are capable of being swapped out to account for different planetary local times.{{book ref|sa5|22}}  

== Trivia ==
* Silverlight Mercantile doubles as the name of Dragonsteel LLC's real life mercantile business.
