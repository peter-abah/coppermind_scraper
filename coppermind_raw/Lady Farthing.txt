'''Lady Farthing''' is a noblewoman from [[New Seran]] on [[Scadrial]]. She owns [[Farthing Mansion]]. Some of Lady Farthing's jewelry was stolen by the [[Set]] in {{date|342|PC}} during a robbery of her mansion, and she subsequently coordinated with the Uptown Precinct to offer a reward for its return. These details were published in an edition of ''[[The New Ascendancy]]''.{{file ref|New_Ascendancy_broadsheet.jpg|''The New Ascendancy'' broadsheet}}{{book ref|mb6|10}}
