Commander '''Spears''' is [[M-Bot]]'s previous pilot, and a [[cytonics|cytonic]]. He has the ability to extend his life using cytonics, and likely had other abilities as well. However, he cannot hyperjump.{{book ref|sky3|39}}

== Appearance and Personality ==
In [[M-Bot]]'s projection, Spears appears as a clean-cut, young-looking man with tan skin, and wears a crisp uniform [[Spensa]] doesn't recognize.{{book ref|Skyward|17}}

Spears was likely acutely aware of the dangers associated with artificial intelligence, as M-Bot assumes Spears to be the one who severely limited his functions and capabilities.{{book ref|sky2|27}}

== History ==
One hundred and seventy two years before [[M-Bot]] is found by [[Spensa]], Commander Spears traveled to [[Detritus]] in search of taynix slugs where,{{book ref|sky2|34}} for some reason, he deactivated M-Bot.{{book ref|sky1|17}} He disappeared into the [[nowhere]] at some point after. He lived in the nowhere for hundreds of years, using his cytonic abilities to extend his life. He tried to escape through the lightburst, but he was unpracticed with his powers and the [[delver]]s destroyed him. Afterward, the delver known as [[Chet]] made a body based off of his form to disguise himself as a human.{{book ref|sky3|39}}

== Relationships ==
=== M-Bot ===
Due to his damaged memory banks, [[M-Bot]] remembers nothing of Spears or their history together, besides his name, appearance, and final command.{{book ref|sky1|17}} As [[M-Bot]] has no knowledge of the [[KRELL|Krell]], it seems likely that neither did Spears,{{book ref|sky1|17}} however, Spears was likely aware of the [[Superiority]].
