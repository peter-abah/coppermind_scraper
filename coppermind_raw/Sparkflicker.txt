A '''sparkflicker''' (sometimes '''spark-flicker''') is a [[Herdaz]]ian fire striker that can also be worn as an ornament.{{book ref|sa1|26}}

It is similar in use to flint-and-steel, albeit it lacks the flint. Rather, a Herdazian wearing a sparkflicker uses their own nails (which are crystalline, owing to their [[singer]] ancestry) to start the fire. In addition, it is sometimes used as an ornament or a decorative article of clothing. It has no martial application.{{wob ref|12826}}{{wob ref|3080}} They are similar to wide rings, worn over the top of a finger and fashioned from steel. Receiving a sparkflicker from one’s parents is an important coming-of-age experience for the children of ranchers, as the gift signifies they can be trusted with starting fires.{{rpg ref|slwg|44}}

In the [[Shattered Plains]] warcamps, a Herdazian is seen using a sparkflicker and oil to make bursts of flames in the air.{{book ref|sa2|43}} The [[Dieno|Mink]] carries a sparkflicker with him to the [[coalition of monarchs|coalition's]] conflict in [[Emul]] and is seen absently creating sparks while he speaks with [[Jasnah Kholin]] following a battle.{{book ref|sa4|64}} Some [[Alethi]] believe that all Herdazians wear sparkflickers, though [[Havarah]], an [[Alethi]] with Herdazian ancestry and fingernails, doesn't carry one.{{book ref|sa1|26}} Some Herdazians carry sparkflickers engraved with religious symbols, though it’s considered tacky to flaunt them.{{rpg ref|slwg|46}}
