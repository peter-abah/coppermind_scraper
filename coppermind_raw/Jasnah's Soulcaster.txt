'''Jasnah's Soulcaster''' is a fake [[Soulcasting]] [[fabrial]]. [[Jasnah Kholin]] claims to have several fakes, as she uses them to disguise her innate ability to Soulcast using the [[Surge]] of Transformation.{{book ref|sa1|70}}

== Appearance ==
The Soulcaster consists of two rings and a bracelet, with chains connecting them.{{book ref|sa1|5}} Three extremely large oval-cut [[gemstone]]s&ndash;a smokestone, a diamond, and a ruby&ndash;are set in a triangle that lays across the back of the hand.{{book ref|sa1|5}} The gems glow brightly with [[Stormlight]] when Jasnah is Soulcasting.{{book ref|sa1|5}} Jasnah sometimes wears a fingerless glove over the Soulcaster to block the light from the gemstones.{{book ref|sa1|8}}

== History ==
Jasnah's use of the Soulcaster exacerbated her conflict with the [[Vorin]] Church, as they maintain a monopoly on the use and control over Soulcasters, and her Soulcaster was to all appearances, the only Soulcaster outside their control. This monopoly is likely in order to protect the secrets of the Soulcaster - including the toll it takes on the user. The Church also believes the Soulcasters to be holy objects and gifts of the Almighty, which is one justification they use to try to take the Soulcaster away from Jasnah. {{book ref|sa1|42}} Many members of the church have attempted to remove the Soulcaster from her possession - either through bribery, coercion, or theft. {{book ref|sa1|70}}

Jasnah's open and notorious use of the Soulcaster made her the only viable target for [[Shallan Davar]], who needed to steal a functional Soulcaster to return to the [[Ghostbloods]] as a replacement for the broken [[Davar Soulcaster]].{{book ref|sa1|5}} Shallan steals the Soulcaster{{book ref|sa1|36}} before later finding out that it is a fake.{{book ref|sa1|70}}

== Abilities ==
Jasnah's Soulcaster is thought to be a very powerful version of the fabrial.{{book ref|sa1|5}} However, it could not actually Soulcast any of the essences, as it was essentially a hollow shell. The gems within were used by [[Jasnah]] as a repository of Stormlight that fueled her own Soulcasting.
