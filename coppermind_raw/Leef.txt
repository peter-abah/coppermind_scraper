'''Leef''' is a member of the wall guard at [[Feverstone Keep]] in ancient [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa1|52}} During one of [[Dalinar's visions]], he experiences the [[Day of Recreance]] from Leef's point of view.{{book ref|sa1|52}} 

==Appearance and Personality==
Leef is of low rank, and he wears a simple uniform consisting of a cloth skirt, a leather jerkin, a cloak, and a bronze cap. He wields a spear with a high-quality steel spearhead. Like several of his fellow guardsmen, he is not known for staying alert. He is disliked by his colleagues.{{book ref|sa1|52}}
