'''"Icy" Ben Oldson''' is a criminal caught by [[Waxillium Ladrian]] in the [[Roughs]] of [[Scadrial]]. Wax had a deputy named [[Blinker]] at the time.{{book ref|mb7|58}}

Just after catching Oldson, [[Wayne]] smacked some beer bottles on a table, so when Wax removed the caps with [[Allomancy]], the beer sprayed all over him. In {{date|348|PC}}, Wayne recounted the incident while sharing a beer with Wax in [[Bilming]]; it was the first of many times he pranked Wax with the same trick. Wax believes it was the first time he really saw Wayne smile.{{book ref|mb7|58}}
