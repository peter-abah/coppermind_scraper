'''Ingo''' is a type of tea on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa3|107}}

[[Taravangian]] drinks it with mint and its smell reminds him of his home, [[Kharbranth]]. [[Maben]] served him a hot cup of the tea while they were in [[Urithiru]] discussing [[Dalinar]]'s recovery; on this occasion, the reminder of home prompted Taravangian to ponder his mortality.{{book ref|sa3|107}}
