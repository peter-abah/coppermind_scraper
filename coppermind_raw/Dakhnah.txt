'''Dakhnah''' is a [[lighteyed]] scribe living in [[Urithiru]] on [[Roshar]]. She is the daughter of a general and once courted [[Adolin]]; Adolin considers her as a potential date for [[Kaladin]].{{book ref|sa4|12}}

Dakhnah is a young woman with a curvy figure and dark skin. She wears lip paint and a tight [[havah]], and she doesn't consider it out of place to visit a primarily [[darkeyed]] bar such as [[Jez's Duty]], which she and a friend visit on the day of [[Jor]] and [[Kryst]]'s wedding party. Having scribed for her father during war meetings since the age of fourteen, she loves to talk war strategy.{{book ref|sa4|12}}
