'''Dashe''' is an [[Elantrian]] from [[Sel]].

He is [[Karata]]'s second-in-command, helping to keep the [[Roost]] safe. After [[Raoden]]'s arrival he forms a team to raid [[Aanden]] against the behest of both Karata and Raoden.{{book ref|Elantris|16}}

He brings Raoden the news that [[Hrathen]] has been 'healed' after being through the [[Shaod]].{{book ref|Elantris|34}} He also is present when [[Sarene]] is struck by the Shaod.{{book ref|Elantris|40}}

He helps [[Matisse]] keep the children of [[Elantris (city)#New Elantris|New Elantris]] safe during the [[invasion of Arelon]].{{book ref|thoe}}

He is awarded with two [[seon]]s, one of whom, [[Aeo]], he Passes to [[Matisse]].{{book ref|thoe}}
