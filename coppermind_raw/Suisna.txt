'''Suisna''' is a small city a day away from [[Luthadel]],{{book ref|mb2|42}} in the [[Central Dominance]] of the [[Final Empire]].{{book ref|mb2|34}}

It is pillaged by the [[koloss]] army during the [[Siege of Luthadel]].{{book ref|mb2|34}} After being attacked, the people fled towards Luthadel, and [[Straff Venture]] sent them towards the besieged city to stretch their already strained resources further.
