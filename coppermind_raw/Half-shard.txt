A '''half-shard''' is a type of [[Fabrial#Augmenter|augmenter fabrial]] developed by [[Veden]] [[Artifabrian]]s late during the [[War of Reckoning]].{{book ref|sa1|22}}

They are the only known objects able to block a [[Shardblade]] apart from [[Shardplate]], other Shardblades, and [[aluminum]]. Despite not granting any of the other benefits of Shardplate, such as enhanced strength and agility,{{book ref|sa1|22}} they represent a significant breakthrough in fabrial science. Their bulky size and shape often leads to their users being hampered in ways Shardbearers are not.{{book ref|sa1|i|9}} While inspired by Shardplate, half-shards work according to a different design principle.{{book ref|sa1|60}} 

Their design consists of a diamond-shaped metal plate with an infused gem attached to its back to increase its durability.{{book ref|sa1|i|9}}

Half-shards were used by King [[Hanavanar]] of Jah Keved in his failed attempt to kill [[Szeth]].{{book ref|sa1|i|9}}

When [[Dalinar]] travels to [[Azimir]], [[Navani]] sends along detailed schematics of the new half-shards provided by [[Taravangian]] in an effort to generate good will with the viziers.{{book ref|sa3|65}} The Azish artifabrians, however, were never able to create half-shards from those schematics.{{book ref|sa5|63}}

Some of Odium's forces in the [[Battle of Azimir]] were equipped with half-shards.{{book ref|sa5|40}}

==Design==
Half-shards are diamond-shaped kite shields, large enough to cover a person's entire side.{{book ref|sa3|100}} The back of the shield contains a steel box with a gemstone inside of it.{{book ref|sa3|100}}

It is currently unknown which spren is used in the creation of half-shards, but Taravangian says in a conversation with Dalinar that the spren entrapped in the half-shard "might have blessed a Knight Radiant"{{book ref|sa3|100}}. However, he may have been lying or trying to mislead Dalinar. He also could have simply been wrong, his understanding incomplete. Alternatively, he could be referring to an Order's Platespren. He doesn't name the type of spren but he does say it "gives things substance, the kind that holds the world together". This might refer to bindspren.

During the Battle of Azimir, [[Neziham]] speculates that Taravangian's schematics were a trick and that half-shards are just ordinary shields made with aluminum.{{book ref|sa5|63}}
