'''Dandos Heraldin''', also known as '''Dandos the Oilsworn''', was a master artist with pencils on [[Roshar]]. He was also knowledgeable about mathematics and created the study of ratios in art.{{book ref|sa3|44}}

[[Shallan Davar]] learned how to draw by reading one of his books,{{book ref|sa2|27}} though Dandos himself had died three hundred years previously.{{book ref|sa1|7}} In his teachings he advised that aspiring artists should paint self-portraits.{{book ref|sa3|29}}

[[Mraize]] used some of Oilsworn's art to bait [[Shalash]] into meeting him; she found a picture she wanted to destroy in his laundry room, and he cornered her after she slashed it.{{book ref|sa3|i|8}}

== Trivia ==
* He is a cameo for [[Dan dos Santos]], one of the people that create art for [[Brandon]]'s works.
