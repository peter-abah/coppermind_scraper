'''Gys''' was a nation on [[Nalthis]] that fought in the [[Manywar]].{{book ref|wb|32}} When [[Peacegiver]] seized control of [[Hallandren]], his own country, Gys was brought out of the conflict as the Manywar ended.

It is possible that Gys was part of the [[Pahn Unity]], the coalition of nations that fought against [[Hallandren]] during the [[Manywar]].{{book ref|wb|26}}
