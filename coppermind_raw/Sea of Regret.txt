The '''Sea of Regret''' is a large sea on Roshar's [[subastral]] of the [[Cognitive Realm]].{{map ref|Shadesmar}} It is bordered by the [[Nexus of Truth]], the [[Nexus of Transition]], and the [[Sea of Souls]].{{map ref|Shadesmar}} In the [[Physical Realm]], this sea corresponds to the western part of the continent of [[Roshar]], including [[Shinovar]] and [[Iri]].{{map ref|Roshar}}

The reason this part of Shadesmar is called the Sea of Regret is unclear.
