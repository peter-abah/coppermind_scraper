'''Au-lam''' is a man who lives in a small port village on the eastern edge of [[Roshar]] that contains the lighthouse [[Defiance]]. The village is in an area that used to be part of the kingdom of [[Natanatan]], and Au-lam is ethnically [[Natan]].{{book ref|sa3|i|1}}

He lost his barn when the [[Everstorm]] hit the village. [[Puuli]] helped him clean up and took some of the destroyed boards to use as fuel for the lighthouse.{{book ref|sa3|i|1}}
