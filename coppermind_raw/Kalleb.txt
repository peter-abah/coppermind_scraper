'''Kalleb''' is a [[Order of Windrunners|Windrunner]] [[squire]] to [[Sigzil]] on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa5|81}}

He has a short, dark beard and the typical abilities of a squire, including healing and [[Lashing]]s.{{book ref|sa5|81}}

== History ==
Kalleb was present for the Windrunner clash with enemy [[Skybreaker]]s at Narak Prime during the [[Second Battle of Narak]]. He and [[Fent]] checked on Sigzil during the battle. Kalleb observed that the ploy to divert the opposing forces to Narak Three was working.{{book ref|sa5|81}}

He and other squires subsequently accompanied Sigzil to a platform where [[Moash]] had laid a trap for them. All of the squires were forced to retreat due to Moash's [[suppression fabrial]] that prevented [[Surgebinding]].{{book ref|sa5|81}} Kalleb presumably lost his powers after Sigzil renounced his [[Knights Radiant|Radiant]] oaths.{{book ref|sa5|118}}
