The '''Caller of Springs''' is a ''[[makay-im]]'' [[Fused]] on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa4|42}}

As a ''[[makay-im]]'', or Deepest One, the Caller of Springs can use [[Voidlight]] to manipulate the [[Surge]] of [[Cohesion]] to meld with and move through stone.{{book ref|sa4|31}}

Deepest Ones are commonly deployed as scouts{{book ref|sa4|37}} and the Caller of Springs was tasked with searching for [[Navani Kholin]] during the invasion of [[Urithiru]]. They discovered Navani shortly after she and her scholars destroyed the first node protecting the [[Sibling]]. They summoned a security force to take her into custody and deliver her to [[Raboniel]].{{book ref|sa4|42}}
