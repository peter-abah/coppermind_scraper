'''Jess''' is an [[arcanist]] working at the [[Silverlight]] university.{{book ref|iote|30}}

Ed asks Starling's permission to show Jess, [[Argent]], and [[Huio]] the fourty-seventh count tapestry from the [[Iriali]] Long Trail record, that documents a map to [[First of the Sun]]'s [[perpendicularity]] to verify its authenticity. It is possible she knows Middle-old Iriali and/or Old Iriali.{{book ref|iote|30}}

== Trivia ==
* Jess is a tuckerization of Jessie Lake (LadyLameness), one of Brandon's beta readers, a Coppermind Keeper, and 17th Shard Staff Member.
