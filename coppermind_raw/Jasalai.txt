Brightness '''Jasalai''' is the leader of the Kholin warcamp at the [[Shattered Plains]] on [[Roshar]] after [[Dalinar Kholin]] moves to [[Urithiru]].{{book ref|sa3|50}}

She is described as tall and stately. She leads Dalinar and [[Navani]] on a tour of the warcamp before Dalinar talks to [[Kadash]]. Dalinar is pleased that the warcamp seems to be in good shape despite the coming of the [[Everstorm]].{{book ref|sa3|50}}
