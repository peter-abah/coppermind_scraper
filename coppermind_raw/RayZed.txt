'''RayZed''' is a young pirate in the [[belt]] of the [[nowhere]], and a member of the [[Broadsider Faction]].{{book ref|sky3|23}}

== History ==
RayZed worked at the [[Superiority]] base of [[Surehold]], and defected alongside [[Peg]] and several others. She became a pilot, flying in one of the flights.{{book ref|cytonic|23}} RayZed went to the arena duel between [[Spensa Nightshade|Spensa]] and [[Hesho|Darkshadow]]{{book ref|sky3|29}}, and afterward joined in the celebrations back at the Broadsider base. She ate some real food during the celebration, an old memory of life outside of the nowhere.{{book ref|cytonic|30}} Before the joint pirate attack on [[Surehold]], RayZed took Chet out flying for some g-force training, building up his endurance so he could fly with Spensa into battle.{{book ref|cytonic|32}} When a delver-controlled fragment flew on a collision course to strike the Broadsider Base, RayZed was curious about the anomaly, asking questions of Peg to try and find out more.{{book ref|cytonic|33}} Afterward, RayZed presumably participated in the assault on Surehold, and moved into the facility with the rest of the faction.{{book ref|sky3|36}}
