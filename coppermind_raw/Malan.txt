'''Malan''' is a [[lighteyed]] [[Alethi]] military officer on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa1|68}}

== Appearance and personality ==
He is described as tall and stately.{{book ref|sa1|68}} He wears a matching steel helm and breastplate, with a dark blue uniform that identifies him as a ranking officer in the [[House Kholin|Kholin]] army.{{book ref|sa1|68}} He is dutiful and appears to faithfully adhere to military procedures, as he is seen saluting "crisply" in the midst of a deadly battle.{{book ref|sa1|68}} He is trusted by Dalinar.{{book ref|sa3|9}}

== History ==
He is a captainlord of the [[Cobalt Guard]] assigned to protect [[Adolin Kholin]].{{book ref|sa1|68}} He participates in the [[Battle of the Tower]] and encounters [[Kaladin]], who tells him to retreat.{{book ref|sa1|68}} However, he refuses to leave [[Adolin]]. Instead, he leads the Cobalt Guard as they follow Kaladin to rescue [[Dalinar]] from the [[Parshendi]] [[Shardbearer]], [[Eshonai]].{{book ref|sa1|68}}

After the [[Battle of Narak]], Dalinar mentions that he would have assigned Malan to investigate the murders of [[Torol Sadeas]] and [[Vedekar Perel]] in [[Urithiru]] if he were still alive.{{book ref|sa3|9}} It is not clear when exactly he was killed.
