'''Valentine''', often called '''Val''', was the broody leader of the [[Reckoners]] team based in [[Babilar]].{{book ref|Firefight|9}} She worked with [[Exel]], [[Sam (Reckoner)|Sam]], and [[Mizzy]] on many missions to kill [[Epic]]s.

==Appearance and Personality==
She is tall, with angular features. She is in her early thirties. She is Hispanic and has black hair, with a lock in the front dyed purple.{{book ref|Firefight|9}}

== History ==
[[David]] mentioned that she once killed an Epic by the name of [[Puños de Fuego]] using the same tank he'd thrown at her.

When David arrived at the outskirts of Babilar with Tia and Prof, he meets Val in one of the Reckoner's hideout. She is immediately described as gloomy and suspicious.

She, along with [[Exel]], was crushed to death by [[Jonathan Phaedrus]]' forcefields.{{book ref|firefight|50}}
