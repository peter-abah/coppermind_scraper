'''Torio City''' is the capital of the kingdom of [[Torio]] on [[Komashi]], in the era before the activation of the [[father machine]]. 

The queen of Torio resides in the city.{{book ref|yumi|2}} Torio City is also the home of a renowned university (including the [[Institute of Mechanical Solutions]]) which is known around the countryside of Torio for the aid they offer to the villages.{{book ref|yumi|20}} The city holds an annual festival called the [[Festival of Reveals]], which is focused at the exhibition hall and courtyard in the center of the city.{{book ref|yumi|40}} Most structures within the city are made of stone and have red roofs. The city is also full of trees, fountains, and sculptures of [[dragon (cosmere)|dragons]].{{book ref|yumi|40}}

After the father machine is activated and the people of Torio were consumed, Torio City fell into disrepair.{{book ref|yumi|40}} The ruins the city are located in the vicinity of Kilahito, to the west, and take up a round area of comparable size.{{book ref|yumi|33}}{{book ref|yumi|34}} The people of [[Nagadan]] located plants in the ruins of Torio City which were useful in creating new strains of crops that could grow on the planet following the loss of the [[shroud]].{{book ref|yumi|part=anotherepilogue}}
