'''Harmon''' is a burglar in [[Camon's crew]] in [[Luthadel]] on [[Scadrial]]. He is an older man with a scraggly beard.{{book ref|mb1|4}}

Harmon and the rest of [[Camon]]'s group, then reporting to [[Milev]], told the story of the Survivor of Hathsin to [[Vin]] just before [[Kelsier's crew]] arrived at their safe house. Harmon dismissed [[Hrud]]'s notion that [[Kelsier]] was something other than a man but posited that he was a [[allomancy|mistborn]]. He and Milev's crew left, leaving Vin behind to attend the meeting with Kelsier's crew.{{book ref|mb1|4}}
