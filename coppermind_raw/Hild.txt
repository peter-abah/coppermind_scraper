'''Hild''' is a resident of [[Stenford]]. She has the appearance of a peasant, and she wears an archaic, long, white, long-sleeved dress with a shorter, sleeveless, brown dress over the top.{{book ref|frugal|1}}

Hild, along with [[Ealstan]] and one other man, were the first people to investigate the burn marks in a field just outside of Stenford, created by [[John West]]'s arrival to the dimension. Not knowing the cause of the fire, she suggested to the men that it may have been caused by [[Logna]]'s flames, noting the burned outline of a person, and the [[The Frugal Wizard's Handbook for Surviving Medieval England (in-world)|burned pages]] that had been scattered around. Ealstan asked her if [[Sefawynn]] had left Stenford yet, to which Hild answered that she had. Ealstan then ordered Hild to send a boy to beg Sefawynn to return, as they would be in need of her [[Skop|services]]. Hild and the two men then returned to Stenford on foot.{{book ref|fwh|1}}
