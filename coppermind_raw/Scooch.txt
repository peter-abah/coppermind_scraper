'''Scooch''' is a sand fox owned by [[Kenton]]'s family in [[Lossand]] on [[Taldain]].{{book ref|ws|prologue}} Little is known about sand foxes, but they are presumably a relatively small species of domesticated [[sandling]].

Scooch served as a guard fox, but she was never called into action and spent most of her time napping and eating table scraps, eventually becoming fat and lazy.{{book ref|ws|prologue}}{{book ref|ws|2}} At some point in Kenton's youth, an a'keldar (sand wolf) wandered into the city and tore Scooch apart in an alley, where Kenton later found her corpse.{{book ref|ws|prologue}}

Kenton recalls Scooch's death when the [[Diem]] is attacked by [[Kerzta|Kerztians]], comparing the complacency of the [[sand mastery|sand masters]] to that of his former pet.{{book ref|ws|2}}
