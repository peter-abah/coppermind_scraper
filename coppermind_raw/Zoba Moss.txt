'''Zoba Moss''' is a [[checkout]] and a member of [[ICON]]. They are Latinx, and have a streak of purple in their hair. Zoba’s family was displaced by a flood. Zoba is part of the fire team that kidnaps [[Holly Winseed (Provisional Replica)|Holly provisional replica]], and argues in favor of keeping her alive. They are a primary recruiter for ICON, and are described as very charismatic.{{book ref|og|10}} 

When Holly is captured, she calls Zoba “outside man” in her head.
