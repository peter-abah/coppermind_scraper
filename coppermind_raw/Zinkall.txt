A '''zinkall''', plural '''zinkallin''', is a ranged weapon wielded by [[Dayside|Daysiders]] on [[Taldain]]. It utilizes pneumatics to fire small arrows at high speed.{{book ref|ws|part=ars}}

==Characteristics==
A zinkall looks like a set of thin reed-like pipes held together with rope. It is mounted on the forearm, near the wrist. There are usually four or five tubes, although some zinkallin may have many more.{{book ref|ws|2}} Each tube contains a front-loaded arrow that can be launched by the wielder by flicking their wrist to release compressed air, pushing the arrow out of the tube at high speed. Each firing chamber has its own compressed air, and the user must use a levered pump on the top of the zinkall to recharge for the next shot. The arrows are small and are sometimes referred to as darts.{{book ref|ws|part=ars}} The arrowheads can be carved from sandling carapace, making them [[terken]],{{book ref|ws|part=ars}} and the arrows usually have fletching on the back of their shafts.{{book ref|ws|4}} Extra arrows can be carried in a small quiver attached to a belt or sling.{{book ref|ws|11}} 

Zinkallin are common weapons on Dayside, being the preferred weapon for duels, battles, and self-defense.{{book ref|ws|part=ars}} They are seen used by [[Kerzta|Kerztian]] warrior-priests, [[Lossand]]in [[trackt]]s, and members of [[Sharezan]]'s gang.{{book ref|ws|4}} They appear to be quite accurate, although proficiency may take some practice.{{book ref|ws|12}} Zinkallin can be used in very close quarters and the arrows can be fired in rapid succession.{{book ref|ws|4}}{{book ref|ws|8}} They are very convenient and portable, but lack the power of bows and crossbows. Their exact range is unknown, but they are typically used at short range to wound an opponent before engaging them. Most users wield a single zinkall to allow the use of a melee weapon in their other hand, usually a sword.{{book ref|ws|part=ars}} In some nations of [[Darkside]], they have been made largely obsolete by the discovery of gunpowder.{{book ref|ws|4}}{{book ref|ws|part=ars}}

==History==
Zinkallin are seen many times during the events that led to [[Kenton]] becoming [[Lord Mastrell]]. They were used by the Kerztians in their attack on the [[Diem]],{{book ref|ws|2}} by both [[Lokmlen]] and [[Ais]] during their fight in the market,{{book ref|ws|4}} and by several assassins who tried to kill Kenton.{{book ref|ws|8}}{{book ref|ws|11}}

[[Khrissalla]] found the weapons interesting and noted that they operated on the same basic principles as a pistol.{{book ref|ws|4}} [[Jon Acron]] was surprised that the Daysiders had pneumatic technology.{{book ref|ws|1}}

[[Tain]] gave Ais a defective zinkall before luring her into a trap.{{book ref|ws|17}}
