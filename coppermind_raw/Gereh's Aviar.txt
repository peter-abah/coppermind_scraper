'''Gereh's Aviar''' is an [[Aviar]] on [[Roshar]]. Formerly owned by [[Gereh]], it is rescued by [[Lift]] after his death and currently lives with her.{{book ref|sa4|116}}

== Appearance and Personality ==
Gereh's Aviar is bright red on its head and body; its wings are a bright blue that fades to red toward the tips. It stands about a foot tall.{{book ref|sa4|i|5}}

The Aviar is extremely loyal to Gereh and insists on returning to him. It quickly discerns that Lift's intentions are friendly and accepts her help calmly and readily.{{book ref|sa4|i|5}}

== Attributes and Abilities ==
Gereh's Aviar has mind abilities.{{book ref|iote|2}}{{wob ref|15828}} However, this talent is unknown, and it is possible that it has been affected in some way by its travel to Roshar. The bird is highly intelligent and has a sense of the location of the person to whom it is bonded. It can to a certain extent both understand and communicate with humans.{{book ref|sa4|i|5}}

== History ==
{{image|Lift_and_Chicken_by_clarinking.jpg|side=right|height=200px|With [[Lift]]}}

Gereh's Aviar originated from [[First of the Sun]].{{wob ref|8900}} [[Gereh]] either had not obtained it yet or did not keep it with him commonly during his duties as a steward in [[Kholinar]];{{book ref|sa4|prologue}} however, by {{Rosharan date|1175}} he did have it with him at [[Urithiru]].{{book ref|sa4|i|5}} During the pair's stay there, [[Mraize]] murdered Gereh for unknown reasons. Mraize's own Aviar then attempted to kill Gereh's Aviar, badly wounding it. [[Lift]] defended Gereh's Aviar and healed it with [[Lifelight]], saving its life. The Aviar then guided her through Urithiru down to Gereh's corpse. It mourned over its loss until Mraize and his Aviar entered the room, at which point Lift fled with Gereh's Aviar.{{book ref|sa4|i|5}} During Lift's capture and imprisonment, she lost track of the Aviar; she began looking for it again immediately upon her release.{{book ref|sa4|83}} Some days later, with the help of the [[Sibling]], she finds the Aviar and [[Zahel]] have both have been kidnapped by [[Axindweth]]; she defeats the Feruchemist and liberates the captives.{{book ref|sa5|i|13}}

== Trivia ==
* The status of the Aviar after the liberation of Urithiru was subject to a continuity change. In earlier editions of ''[[Rhythm of War]]'', Dalinar claims to have seen Lift running around with her "red chicken." In more recent printings, he now says she's running around asking everyone if they've seen a red chicken.{{reddit ref|Cosmere|1govkx1|lwmvu4v|text=Peter Ahlstrom confirms the continuity change|date=2024-11-11}}
