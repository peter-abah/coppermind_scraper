The '''Roughs''' are the frontier regions just outside the [[Elendel Basin]] on [[Scadrial]]. They are separated from the fertile basin by mountain ranges, and are divided into the '''Northern Roughs''' and the '''Southern Roughs''' (with the [[Irongate River]] as the approximate dividing line).{{map ref|Scadrial}} 

The ideal conditions in the Basin meant that there was little pressure to expand following the founding of [[Elendel]].{{wob ref|697}} As a result of this, the Roughs were sparsely populated and less advanced than the urban centers even generations later.

A political movement once began to make the Roughs a province within the Elendel Basin with political representation. Others within the Roughs instead favored the idea of being their own nation.{{book ref|mb7|epilogue|7}} The many railroad and mining companies in the Roughs often post bounties on criminals, and some people make a living from these bounties.{{book ref|mb5|prologue}} While the Roughs are technologically behind compared to the Basin, it is quickly modernising.{{book ref|mb7|2}}

== Geography and Ecology ==
The Roughs consist of vast dry and dirty plains.{{book ref|mb5|prologue}} The Roughs experience harsh sunlight.{{book ref|mb4|prologue}} Trees here are scraggly and short,{{book ref|mb4|7}} as are many plants. Many plants are also prickly.{{book ref|mb5|prologue}} The Roughs contain little large fauna, but they do have lions, giraffes and gazelles.{{book ref|mb5|prologue}}{{book ref|mb6|6}}{{file ref|House Record broadsheet.jpg|''The House Record'' Broadsheet}}

== Culture ==
The men and women living in the Roughs are very different from those in the Basin, although many of them lived in the Basin at some point. It is likely that the people of the Roughs speak Skaa,{{book ref|mb6|12}} although they have a Roughs accent.{{book ref|mb5|5}} They are not terribly hygienic. The people have a type of fatalism to them, they distrust authority and often shun lawmen, even if that means letting criminals go about their business. They also smoke many cigars.{{book ref|mb5|prologue}} The poor people mostly eat beans and oatmeal, and the Roughs is home to a kind of fruit that has a tough shell, but is all gooey inside. People in the Roughs have stories about dragons.{{book ref|mb7|prologue}} The Roughs are also home to multiple Koloss clans.

{{sidequote|If you want to know a man, dig in his firepit| A Roughs saying, likely [[Koloss]] in origin.
{{book ref|mb5|14}}|side=right|size=300px}}

Men and women often wear dusters, which may be decorated with patterns of colors, such as a white duster reddened at the bottom. Hats from the Roughs, worn by both men and women, are often broad-brimmed.{{book ref|mb5|5}} They also wear thick Roughs trousers and buttoned shirts.{{book ref|mb4|14}} It seems that the Roughs’ people follow the same religions as the Basin’s, including the Path and the church of the survivor. Weddings are often hosted in a simple wooden chapel.{{book ref|mb6|1}} Theater shows are often dirty and not proper at all.{{book ref|mb6|14}}
