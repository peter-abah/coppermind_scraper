'''Beamlin Dorset''' is an [[Alethi]] soldier and a former guard for [[Jasnah Kholin]] on [[Roshar]]. Beam and [[Talig]] are a matched pair; they are always near each other on the battlefield. Beamlin is handy with a bow, and his sister is a Radiant. Due to this, and having been one of Jasnah’s guards, Beamlin is comfortable taking orders from a woman. This made Adolin suggest that during the [[Battle of Azimir]], [[Colot]] make Beam [[May Aladar|May]]’s second, with Talig as head sergeant. He told Colot to put them on the interior balcony with the [[Azish]] archers.{{book ref|sa5|39}} It is unclear if he survived the battle.
