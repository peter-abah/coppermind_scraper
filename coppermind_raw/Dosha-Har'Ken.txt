'''Dosha-Har'Ken''' is a port city in [[Kerzta]] on the northwest shore of [[Taldain]]'s [[Dayside]].{{book ref|ws|prologue}}{{map ref|dayside|Taldain Dayside}}

Duchess [[Khrissalla]] and her party arrive in Dosha-Har'Ken after transiting the [[Border Ocean]] from [[Darkside]]. They encounter a Kerztian merchant there with signage in [[Dynastic]], who also speaks that Darkside language. Despite the Dynasty heavily restricting travel, the merchant claims that one or two ships from Darkside dock at the port in Dosha-Har'Ken each week.{{book ref|ws|prologue}}
