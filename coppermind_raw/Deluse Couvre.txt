'''Deluse Couvre''' was an author during the [[Final Empire]] on [[Scadrial]].{{book ref|mb1|19}}

He wrote numerous books, including ''[[Book of the False Dawn]]''{{book ref|mb1|19}}--a book that any [[Keeper]] could quote, as it damned the [[Lord Ruler]]'s leadership blunders over the centuries.{{book ref|mb1|18}}

Deluse went on to write even more damning books about the [[Final Empire]], and though he never blasphemed against [[Allomancy]], the [[Steel Ministry]] made an exception and executed him anyway.{{book ref|mb1|19}}
