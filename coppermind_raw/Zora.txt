'''Zora''' is an [[Azish]] member of the [[Ghostbloods]] on [[Roshar]].

When [[Mraize]] left the [[Physical Realm]] during the events of the ten days before the [[contest of champions]], he placed Zora temporarily in charge of the Rosharan chapter of the Ghostbloods, but it is uncertain whether he maintained this position after Mraize's death.{{book ref|sa5|25}}{{book ref|sa5|147}}
