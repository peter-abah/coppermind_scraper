'''Riotings''' are a type of gun on [[Scadrial]]. Riotings have a barrel .22 inches wide and can carry six bullets. [[Waxillium Ladrian]] had a couple of Riotings, but he rarely used them in combat. In {{date|342|PC}}, at a party in [[New Seran]], [[Steris Harms]], Ladrian’s betrothed, strapped a Rioting to her thigh, in case they would have to go to the party unarmed.{{book ref|mb6|14}} [[Kelesina Shores]] was killed by a bullet from this Rioting, although Wax wasn’t the one to shoot her.{{book ref|mb6|16}}
