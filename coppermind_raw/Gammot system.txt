The '''Gammot system''' is a region of space near the edge of the [[Reaches]] of the [[High Empire]].{{book ref|firstborn}} Towards the end of his time aboard the ''[[Stormwind]]'', [[Dennison Crestmar]] is informed that they are in the Gammot system and is surprised to realize that [[Varion Crestmar]] has already gotten so far, let alone [[High Admiral]] [[Kern]]'s rearguard force.{{book ref|firstborn}}
