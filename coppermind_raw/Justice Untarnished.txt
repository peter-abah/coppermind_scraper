'''Justice Untarnished''' is a settlement in the [[Sea of Souls]], part of the [[Shadesmar|Rosharan subastral]] of the [[Cognitive Realm]]. It is located on a peninsula east of the [[Oathbound Spires]], and north of the [[Astral Banks]] and [[Lasting Integrity]].{{map ref|Sea of Souls}} In the [[Physical Realm]], the peninsula Justice Untarnished exists on is a river that separates [[Emul]] and [[Tukar]].{{map ref|Roshar}}

Justice Untarnished's name and proximity to [[Lasting Integrity]] may suggest the place is under [[honorspren]] control.{{map ref|Sea of Souls}}
