'''Condwish''' is one of [[Mraize]]'s servants on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa3|i|8}}

Condwish has at least some authority over Mraize's finances and, after [[Mem]] gets the [[aether]] out of Mraize's [[Azir|Azish]] cavalrylord's suit, is the one who pays out her three-[[firemark]] bonus.{{book ref|sa3|i|8}}
