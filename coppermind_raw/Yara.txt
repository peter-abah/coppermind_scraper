Sister '''Yara''' is an [[ardent]] on [[Roshar]]. She is in charge of visitation requests at the [[Devotary of Mercy]].{{book ref|sa4|25}}

When [[Kaladin]] and [[Teft]] came looking for [[Noril]], the [[Kuno|ardent]] at the door told them that unless they were [[lighteyes]] of at least the third [[dahn]] they would have to submit a form to Yara before he could let them see Noril.{{book ref|sa4|25}}
