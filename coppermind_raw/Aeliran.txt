'''Aeliran''' is an [[honorspren]] on [[Roshar]].{{rpg ref|b9|36}}

== Appearance and Personality ==
Aeliran is an intense honorspren with an especially strong sense of right and wrong. In the [[Physical Realm]], he appears most often like a [[windspren]]; a ribbon of blue light. He can also appear as a stern man with an [[ardent]]’s square beard, but he only shows that form to people ready to swear the [[Immortal Words|First Ideal]].{{rpg ref|b9|36}}

A person who embodies protection gains Aeliran’s approval. That includes defending the weak and risking serious harm to protect someone vulnerable. Aeliran can be overzealous in his interpretation of honorable behavior, and he holds any potential Radiant to high standards of dignity. Aeliran sees himself a seasoned veteran and is intent on finding a worthy student. He does not engage in conversation with potential students, but may choose one to follow. During a particularly crucial moment, he might briefly appear, and they might hear a terse “No” or “That’s it!” from a voice like wind whispering in their ear.{{rpg ref|b9|36}}

== History ==
Aeliran was created sometime after the [[Recreance]]. As he felt the [[True Desolation]] coming, he left the honorspren to find a worthy student. He accompanied a research party onto the Shattered Plains, and when their mission went horribly wrong, with them getting attacked by a chasmfiend and thrown into the chasms, Aeliran took this as an excellent opportunity to see whether any of them were worth his time and effort.{{rpg ref|b9|36}}
