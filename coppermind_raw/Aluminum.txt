'''Aluminum''' is the internal enhancement pulling metal. An [[Allomancy|Allomancer]] burning aluminum will make all of their metal reserves vanish.{{book ref|mb2|5}} [[Feruchemist]]s can use aluminum to store [[Identity]], and when used as a [[Hemalurgic]] spike, aluminum removes all powers. It is known as '''Ralkalest''' in the [[Rose Empire]] on [[Sel]] and among [[singers]],{{wob ref|6364}}{{book ref|sa4|37}} as '''the unForgeable metal''' in the Rose Empire,{{wob ref|6364}} and historically as '''starmetal''' in [[Azir]].{{book ref|sa5|76}}

Aluminum has been confirmed to have strange effects to all magic systems, is magically inert, and will act as an [[Investiture]] block.{{wob ref|7942}}

The paired alloy of aluminum used in the Metallic Arts is [[duralumin]].

== Allomantic Use ==
An aluminum [[Misting]] is known as an '''Aluminum Gnat'''. Burning aluminum causes the body to instantly metabolize its metal reserves—including the aluminum itself—without any other effect.{{wob ref|2461}} As such, an Aluminum Gnat gains no useable powers from their Allomancy,{{book ref|mb4|part=ars}} however, it can be used to negate effects of other Investiture, such as the withering effect of [[Shade]]s on [[Threnody]].{{wob ref|6374}} Only Allomantically relevant metals can be burned away.{{wob ref|12563}} Allomancy does not affect aluminum even though the metal itself is Allomantically viable.{{book ref|mb1|36}}

=== Savantism ===
If someone were able to burn the amount of aluminum needed to become an Allomantic [[savant]] with the metal, it may be possible to cleanse their spirit of unwanted effects of Investiture.{{wob ref|1247}}

== Feruchemical Use ==
An aluminum [[Ferring]] is known as a '''Trueself'''. Aluminum is used to store "spiritual sense of [[Identity]]." This ability is not fully understood, and is rarely discussed outside of Terris communities.{{book ref|mb4|part=ars}} A Trueself who fills an aluminummind to the point where they have no Identity and then fills another metalmind is able to produce a metalmind that any Feruchemist with the relevant power can tap. In a similar manner, a Feruchemist may be able to tap another person's metalminds by filling their own aluminummind completely.{{book ref|mb6|3}} Trueselfs can heal damage done to them in the [[Cognitive Realm]] by storing Identity, but it takes a roundabout method.{{wob ref|7836}} Storing Identity makes the Feruchemist more susceptible to many things, such as [[Forgery]].{{wob ref|3547}}

It is unclear why Identity can be stored in and withdrawn from an aluminummind when other Invested Arts cannot affect aluminum.

== Hemalurgic Use ==
When used as a Hemalurgic spike, aluminum removes all powers,{{file ref|Hemalurgy table.jpg|Hemalurgy Table}} such as Allomancy or [[sand mastery]].{{wob ref|14896}}

== Weaponry ==
On Scadrial, guns and bullets made from aluminum are highly effective (albeit expensive) because they cannot be [[steel|Pushed]], [[iron|Pulled]], or even detected using the sight granted by steel or iron Allomancy. Aluminum bullets are actually composed of an Allomantically inert alloy of aluminum since the ballistics of a pure aluminum bullet would not work.{{book ref|mb4|7}}

== Investiture-inert Properties ==
While aluminum doesn't always directly negate all magic systems, it does have very weird, interesting effects in all places.{{wob ref|11798}} A person having aluminum in their body would interfere with many magic systems in some way.{{wob ref|11937}} Alloys of aluminum get some of the Investiture-inert properties of aluminum.{{wob ref|15745}} This list is non-exhaustive and only contains known phenomena; there are likely other ways that known (and unknown) arcana on various worlds would have unique interactions with aluminum.

=== Scadrial ===
* Allomantic [[Iron]]/[[Steel]]: Pushing or Pulling on aluminum will do nothing.{{book ref|mb7|part=ars}}
* Allomantic [[Pewter]]: If an aluminum object is stuck inside a body, it is impossible to use [[Pewter#Allomantic Use|pewter Allomancy]], making it impossible for wounds to heal around the aluminum until it is removed from the body, however the effect would not be too severe on the Thug, as they still have strength. There is a similar and more severe effect on [[Gold#Feruchemical Use|gold Feruchemists]].{{wob ref|6365}}
* Allomantic [[Zinc]]/[[Brass]]: Aluminum can shield people from emotional Allomancy.{{book ref|mb7|part=ars}}{{wob ref|15745}} Because aluminum can shield against emotional Allomancy, those who can afford to often wear hats with an aluminum lining.{{book ref|mb5|1}}
* Allomantic [[Bronze]]: Aluminum hats can be used to block Seekers from using [[bronze]] on them.{{wob ref|8897}}
* Allomantic [[Bendalloy]]/[[Cadmium]]: An object made out of aluminum can create dead zones within [[cadmium#Allomantic use|cadmium]] and [[bendalloy#Allomantic Use|bendalloy]] bubbles, where the shielded area is not included within the time bubble.{{wob ref|4297}} 
* Allomantic [[Atium]]: Objects made out of aluminum generate no Atium shadows.{{wob ref|6870}}
* [[Hemalurgy|Hemalurgic]] spikes: It is possible that encasing Hemalurgic spikes in aluminum may prevent Hemalurgic decay.{{wob ref|5060}}
* [[Primer cube]]: If used in a primer cube, aluminum would block the use of Invested Arts within a certain range.{{wob ref|14829}}

=== Sel ===
* [[Forgery]]: Aluminum cannot be altered at all via Forgery.{{tes ref|2}}

=== Roshar ===
* [[Surgebinding]]: Aluminum cannot be affected by the [[Surge]]s [[Adhesion]] or [[Gravitation]].{{wob ref|8423}} It interferes with most Radiant powers.{{epigraph ref|sa4|17}} Aluminum can be produced with [[Soulcasting]].{{book ref|sa2|48}}{{book ref|sa5|63}} However, aluminum resists attempts to Soulcast it into other [[Ten Essences|Essences]].{{wob ref|8306}} It can prevent Soulcasting from being detected by others.{{book ref|sa3|81}}
* [[Shardblade]]: Shardblades are unable to cut aluminum through magical means but would be able to cut it physically, like a conventional blade, if the aluminum were thin enough.{{wob ref|1442}}{{epigraph ref|sa4|17}}{{book ref|sa3|118}} A room lined with aluminum can also stop a Shardblade from being summoned, but only if the entire room is covered.{{book ref|sa5|i|13}}{{book ref|sa5|129}} This will also prevent the Shardbearer and their Blade from sensing each other.{{book ref|sa5|130}}{{book ref|sa5|135}}
* [[Fabrial]]: Aluminum can block [[spanreed]] communication.{{book ref|sa3|81}} It cannot prevent [[Light]] leakage, as it is leaking directly into the Spiritual Realm.{{wob ref|16759}} Aluminum interferes with the spren's ability to sense thoughts.{{book ref|sa3.5|7}} By placing aluminum foil in a fabrial, each axis of motion and rotation could be blocked separately by proper application. This allows certain, but not all, functions to be blocked.{{book ref|sa3.5|9}}
* [[Spren]]: A room lined with aluminum will stop spren from entering.{{book ref|sa5|130}} Boxes lined with aluminum allow spren to move between planets in the [[Rosharan system]] if transported via [[Shadesmar]].{{book ref|sa5|prologue}}
* [[Light]]: Boxes lined with aluminum allow Light to move between planets in the Rosharan system if transported via Shadesmar.{{book ref|sa5|prologue}}

=== Nalthis ===
* [[Awakening]]: Aluminum cannot be Awakened.{{wob ref|6364}}
* [[Returned]]: It is possible for aluminum to prevent someone from Returning, but not simply by stabbing the body: aluminum is inert and blocks Investiture, but does not actively absorb it.{{wob ref|6364}} 
* [[Heightening]]: Aluminum can block an Awakener's life sense.{{wob ref|8897}}

=== Threnody ===
* [[Shade]]: Shades are unable to move through aluminum.{{book ref|iote|23}}
* [[Evil]]: Aluminum can block the Evil from sensing Investiture.{{book ref|iote|26}}

=== Lumar ===
* [[Silver]]: Aluminum is said to "block" the effect that silver has on [[Aether spores]].{{book ref|tress|38}}

=== Cosmere ===
* [[Shard]]: Wearing aluminum can help a person resist the influence of a Shard.{{wob ref|14926}}
* [[Perpendicularity]]: Aluminum can be used to take liquid from a Perpendicularity, and it will stay Invested.{{wob ref|9233}}

== History ==
=== Rose Empire ===
Aluminum is known as ralkalest in the [[Rose Empire]].{{tes ref|2}} The cells for Forgers in the [[Rose Palace]] have ralkalest grates in their ceilings for entrance and a thin plate of ralkalest behind the stone walls.{{tes ref|2}}

=== Pre Catacendre ===
The [[Lord Ruler]] kept aluminum and its alloy [[duralumin]] from public knowledge to give the [[Inquisitors]] an advantage.{{wob ref|8037}} During his rule, aluminum was harvested from the [[Ashmounts]].{{book ref|mb6|3}}

=== Nalthis ===
[[Nightblood]]'s sheath is made of aluminum.{{wob ref|8162}}

=== Roshar ===
{{quote
|There are legends of metal that can block a Shardblade. A metal that falls from the sky. Silver, but somehow lighter.
|[[Taravangian]] on aluminum{{book ref|sa3|100}}
}}
The Knights Radiant once lined tunnels deep beneath [[Urithiru]] with ralkalest, likely to protect against intrusion into the tunnels from the foothills below via Surgebinding. It is unknown whether the term ralkalest is of Selish origin alone, or if Raboniel learned of it from another source.{{book ref|sa4|37}} [[Azir]] obtained aluminum from stars that fell from space, and historically called it starmetal.{{book ref|sa5|76}}

The [[Unkalaki]] people are able to obtain aluminum by trading with the spren world, and are aware that it has an effect on spren.{{book ref|sa3.5|7}}

[[Vasher]] and [[Gavilar]] discovered that boxes lined with aluminum could allow [[Light]] and spren to move between planets in the [[Rosharan system]] if transported via [[Shadesmar]].{{book ref|sa5|prologue}}

Highmarshal Azure was given metal sheets by [[Wit]] during her time in Kholinar that were possibly aluminum. When lining a room, they allowed for the use of Soulcasting [[fabrial]]s without detection by the [[Voidspren]].{{book ref|sa3|81}} A [[Fused]] used Nightblood's sheath to block an attack from [[Szeth]], who was wielding Nightblood.{{book ref|sa3|118}}

An aluminum box was used to secure the gemstone containing [[Nergaoul]], which was then sunk to the bottom of the ocean, to prevent the Unmade from being released again.{{book ref|sa4|16}}

During the expedition to Aimia, [[Rushu]] is tasked by [[Navani]] to experiment with aluminum and discover the details of its impact on fabrials. They first note that the thickness of the metal may impact its effect on fabrial operation (e.g. block vs modify).{{book ref|sa3.5|7}} After tinkering with a spanreed and aluminum foil, [[Huio]] theorizes that the spren react to aluminum "almost like prey react to a predator" and that it interferes with the spren's ability to sense thoughts.{{book ref|sa3.5|7}} [[Rushu]] further theorizes that each axis of motion and rotation could be blocked separately by proper application of aluminum. It is likely that other types of fabrials (i.e. heating fabrials) could be modified similarly.

This experimentation accidentally resulted in a series of modified fabrials in which the mechanism contained bits of aluminum foil that blocked certain functions, but not all. Among these were a spanreed that transferred lateral motion, but not vertical; a lift to permit [[Rysn]] to move between decks of the ''[[Wandersail (1174 ship)|Wandersail]]'' and a number of "wheelchair" fabrials which levitated and permitted [[Rysn]] to maneuver while seated using a partially aluminum-blocked counterweight.{{book ref|sa3.5|9}} These are particularly useful due to a known drawback of motion-transferring fabrials caused by a moving frame of reference (e.g. a moving ship at sea communicating by spanreed to shore).

Odium's forces later lined their weapons in aluminum to protect against Shardblades.{{book ref|sa5|63}}{{book ref|sa5|41}} Before the True Desolation, during the Alethi [[War of Reckoning]], an aluminum arrowhead was worth about three hundred marks.{{rpg ref|b9|23}}

=== Post Catacendre ===
A significant percentage of the aluminum in the [[Elendel Basin|Basin]] is produced by the [[Ghostbloods]]. [[Dean]], the Scadrian branch's chemist, believes that the Scadrians would one day discover how to create it via electrolysis, and that the value would plummet to being cheaper than [[tin]]. The Ghostbloods have been using their aluminum to fund their operations for the past two decades.{{book ref|mb7|40}} During {{date|342|PC}} and {{date|348|PC}}, due to advances in aluminum production technology and processes, Scadrian aluminum dropped in price, though it still remained expensive and unaffordable to most people.{{book ref|mb7|25}}

=== Lumar ===
Aluminum lined rooms and boxes are used to protect [[Aether spores]] from [[silver]].{{book ref|tress|22}}{{book ref|tress|23}}

=== Space Age ===
Some smugglers use aluminum smuggler's boxes, which are undetectable even to modern scans.{{book ref|iote|35}} Aluminum boxes are also used to prevent communication devices from overhearing conversations, though this isn't usually a problem because [[Silverlight]]'s communication hubs are impartial.{{book ref|iote|epilogue}}

== Trivia ==
* Brandon thought of having [[Vin]] burn [[silver]] rather than aluminum when captured in [[Kredik Shaw]] but decided that aluminum would be less abundant.{{wob ref|7983}}
* Brandon chose aluminum for the role it serves because it is rare pre-industrial age, then becomes incredibly common, providing a way for technology to counter Allomancy.{{wob ref|9281}}
