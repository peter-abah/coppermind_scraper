'''Kneespike''' is a city in [[Bavland]].

It is the residence of the lord who owns the land around [[Bornwater]] and [[Staplind]]. [[Makkek]] plans to move there after outgrowning Bornwater, and [[Szeth]] knew that would mean he'd be busy helping establish Makkek by killing all the criminals that didn't bow to his rule.{{book ref|sa1|i|6}}
