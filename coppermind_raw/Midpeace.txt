'''Midpeace''' is a period that marks the middle of the [[Roshar]]an year.{{book ref|sa2|45}}

The Rosharan year is 500 days long{{book ref|sa2|46}} and ends with a four week period of rain known as the [[Weeping]].{{book ref|sa2|76}} Midpeace occurs at the midpoint between Weepings.{{book ref|sa2|45}} [[Highstorm]]s occur more frequently as Midpeace approaches and after it ends,{{wob ref|10231}} but they do not occur at all during Midpeace.{{book ref|sa2|45}} Although the exact duration of Midpeace is not mentioned, it is the longest sustained period on Roshar without rain; since "natural" storms that are not highstorms or the [[Everstorm]] are rare,{{wob ref|5701}} many rivers typically dry up by the end of Midpeace.{{book ref|sa3|58}} 

Residents of Roshar use the fair weather of Midpeace to celebrate and travel.{{book ref|sa1|44}} [[Alethkar]], [[Jah Keved]], and possibly other nations hold outdoor festivals known as [[Middlefest Fair]]s.{{book ref|sa2|45}} [[Yokska]] mentions traveling to [[Liafor]] during Midpeace to see the latest fashion trends.{{book ref|sa3|69}}
