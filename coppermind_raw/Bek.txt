'''Queen Bek the Fifteenth''' is a queen on [[Lumar]] and a known [[spore eater]]. She is reportedly the only person in recorded history on Lumar to be cured from her condition, and [[Xisis]] was allegedly involved.{{book ref|tress|31}} 

As Xisis claims he can only temporarily subdue the gestation, and not completely remove it, Bek may not have been entirely cured as histories claim.{{book ref|tress|52}} However, this could have been a lie to prevent [[Crow]] from trying to escape.{{book ref|tress|52}}
