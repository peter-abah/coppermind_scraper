'''Fenweed''' is a poisonous plant that grows in the [[Forests of Hell]] on [[Threnody]].{{book ref|sfs}}

The primary property of fenweed is to induce extreme headaches, drowsiness, dizziness, sleepiness, and a cloudy-head.{{book ref|sfs}} Fenweed has a very light taste, so it can easily be masked by the flavor of food or beer.

Fenweed has to be mixed into a paste to be used.{{book ref|sfs}} A full dose of fenweed in a man's meal or drink is enough to put him straight to sleep. A half dose will leave him mostly unaffected for a while, but after a time he will fall asleep, although not so deeply that he could not be roused.

The name of fenweed implies that it can be found in [[Wikipedia:Fen|swamps]].

[[Silence Montane]] has her daughter, [[William Ann]], give [[Chesterton Divide]] and the four men of his gang a half dose of fenweed in their beer, so that she can later kill them in their sleep.{{book ref|sfs}}
