Old '''Jed''' is a resident of a small village of the [[Eastern Dominance]].

He worked hard, even after the local nobility fled after the [[Collapse]]. He worked through the mists during the day right up until he was struck down by the [[Deepness]]. His corpse was twisted and the ground around him disturbed as he struggled in pain.{{book ref|mb2|4}}
