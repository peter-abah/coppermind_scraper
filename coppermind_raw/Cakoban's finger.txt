'''Cakoban's fingers'''{{ref|group=fn|text=Called Patji's fingers in the ''[[Sixth of the Dusk (novella)|Sixth of the Dusk]]'' novella}} are a type of tree endemic to [[Patji (island)|Patji]] in the [[Pantheon]] on [[First of the Sun]].{{book ref|iote|14}}

== Appearance ==
{{image|IotE ch icon fig 45.png|side=left|width=200px|Cakoban&#39;s finger's fruit}}
Cakoban's fingers are massive trees, with large drooping fronds. Each frond has a large blossom on the end, as long as two hands. After the flowers blossom, the tree produces fruit that falls to the ground.{{book ref|iote|14}}

== Ecology ==
The flowers of Cakoban's fingers possess a unique trait. When they bloom, the flowers mimic the minds of an injured and fearful animal. This draws in predators that can sense minds, such as [[tuskrun]], hoping to find easy prey. The predators congregate around the tree and kill one another instead, fertilizing the ground beneath the tree with their corpses. These events are usually easy to avoid, but if a [[trapper]] happens by a tree that has just bloomed, they may be caught by surprise.{{book ref|iote|14}}

The trees also play another crucial role in the ecosystem. A large grove of a dozen of them cluster around [[Patji's Eye]], where all juvenile [[Aviar]] visit. The birds eat the fruit that fall to the ground; in doing so, they also consume a parasite in the fruit that grants them their abilities.{{book ref|iote|18}} The birds have evolved alongside the parasites in Patji's unique ecosystem, and their gut flora facilitates a symbiotic relationship.{{wob ref|10841}} Humans are not part of this ecosystem and cannot gain abilities by eating these fruits, although some have tried.{{wob ref|8768}}{{wob ref|10841}}

== History ==
The trapper [[First of the Sky]] happened upon one of Cakoban's fingers that had just bloomed and was killed by a tuskrun pack.{{book ref|iote|14}}

When [[Vathi]] and [[Sixth of the Dusk]] were hiding from [[nightmaw]]s near Patji's Eye, they tied one of the blossoms to a spear from Vathi's spear gun and fired it away from them. The nightmaws were confused by the thoughts of the blossom and followed the spear, allowing Vathi and Dusk to escape.{{book ref|iote|18}}
