Lord '''Rene''' is a [[noble]] in [[Luthadel]] on [[Scadrial]].{{book ref|mb1|23}}

==Appearance and Personality==
He is described as having an "ample brow". He is somewhat oblivious to the intricacies of house politics.{{book ref|mb1|23}}

==History==
He attended a ball at [[Keep Hasting]] as the date of Lady [[Kliss]]. He had difficulty following Kliss's theory about why [[House Geffenry]] may have killed [[Charrs Entrone]], and Kliss spoke to him patronizingly on the subject.{{book ref|mb1|23}}

Rene asked [[Milen Davenpleu]] to join him in a game of [[shelldry]] with Lord [[Tyden]] the day after the ball even though Milen had failed to show up to a previous game. Milen had an [[obligator]] witness his promise to join them this time.{{book ref|mb1|23}}
