'''Jendellah''' is the author of '''''City Management in the Modern Era'''''.{{book ref|mb2|13}}

His work mentions how hard it is to police [[Luthadel]] because of it's large population of [[skaa]] slums. It suggests using groups of officers to patrol the streets, which [[Elend]] suggests might work to defend the wall during the [[Siege of Luthadel]].
