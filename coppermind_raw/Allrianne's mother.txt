'''Allrianne's mother''' is a noblewoman from the [[Western Dominance]] of [[Scadrial|Scadrial's]] [[Final Empire]].{{book ref|mb2|25}} 

She was married to [[Ashweather Cett]] and had two children, [[Allrianne]] and [[Gneorndin Cett|Gneorndin]]. She trained [[Allrianne]] in the ways of the [[Luthadel]] court despite residing in [[Fadrex City]].{{book ref|mb2|25}}{{book ref|mb2|part=ars}} Allrianne believed that her mother was grooming her to be an attractive bride with the intent to use her as a tool to further Ashweather's ambitions.{{book ref|mb2|25}}
