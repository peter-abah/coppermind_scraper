A '''black leech''' is a dangerous creature native to the [[Pantheon]] on [[First of the Sun]]. Black leeches are known to affix themselves to their prey and drive them mad by sending thoughts into the minds of their prey. [[Trapper]]s usually have an [[Aviar]] with the ability to shield their mind with them, as such a bird can prevent a black leech from sending them thoughts.{{book ref|iote|46}}
