'''Bitterleaf''' is a plant on [[Roshar]] used as a medicine for epilepsy.{{book ref|sa2|41}}

Chewing bitterleaf is believed to reduce the jerking and uncontrollable motion associated with epileptic seizures. It is unknown if it is helpful in treating other sorts of seizures. It can be steeped as a daily tea, but it's most efficacious when chewed{{rpg ref|slwg|12}}.

[[Kaladin]] was familiar with the medicinal properties of bitterleaf. Brightlord [[Renarin Kholin]] was prescribed bitterleaf to chew for his seizures, but he did not believe that it helped much in treating them.{{book ref|sa2|41}}
