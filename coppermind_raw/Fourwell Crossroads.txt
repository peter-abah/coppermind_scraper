'''Fourwell Crossroads''' is an intersection in [[Luthadel]] on [[Scadrial]]. Its name comes from its four wells, one in each of its corners. It also has a copper fountain in its center, which depicts the [[Lord Ruler]] with the [[Deepness]] defeated and lying at his feet.{{book ref|mb1|11}}

Many people pass though Fourwell Crossroads, which makes it a popular spot for beggars to set up, though they have to fight to maintain their place there. After [[Kelsier]] had him made a beggar, [[Camon]] did not get a spot at Fourwell Crossroads, and had to set up at a smaller and less popular square three streets to the west.{{book ref|mb1|11}}
