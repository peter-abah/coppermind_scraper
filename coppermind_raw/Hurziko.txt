'''Hurziko''' is a village in [[Herdaz]] on [[Roshar]]. It has a small market square,{{rpg ref|ss|9}} a single winehouse{{rpg ref|ss|11}} and many farms. The pastoral village sits on a key trade route and is the home of a prize-winning hog named [[Highprince Sadeas (hog)|Highprince Sadeas]].{{rpg ref|ss|9}} The villagers are known to farm [[rockbud]]s, melons, [[longroot]]s and [[chull]]s, such as [[Tessie]].{{rpg ref|ss|10}} During the [[True Desolation]], the village was conquered by [[singer]]s. Afterwards, a [[Fused]] named [[Tashgarn]] was sent to manage it. At some point, Tashgarn declared that he was going to eat the village’s prized hog.{{rpg ref|ss|9}} A small party of people tried to rescue the hog, but its not clear if they succeeded. During that same time period, there was at least one member of the resistance in Hurziko.{{rpg ref|ss|11}}
