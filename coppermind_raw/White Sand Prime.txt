'''''White Sand'' Prime''' is the first novel written by [[Brandon Sanderson]]. It is followed by ''[[Lord Mastrell]]''. Brandon rewrote it from scratch, resulting in [[White Sand (prose)|''White Sand'' (prose)]].{{wob ref|7825}} The graphic novel ''[[White Sand]]'' is the novel's final incarnation.

He considers it to be a Dune rip-off.{{wob ref|5132}}
