'''Guillian''' is a felon apprehended in [[Elendel]] in 342.{{book ref|mb6|prologue}}

Guillian joined [[Migs]] and eight other men to commit some sort of crime, but they were found out and trapped in a building for the entire night.{{book ref|mb6|prologue}} Around dawn, [[Wax]] and [[Wayne]] helped the constables capture the men. Wayne sneaked into the room, posing as a robber before knocking out Guillian and taking his ivory-handled knife, Guillian's favorite.
