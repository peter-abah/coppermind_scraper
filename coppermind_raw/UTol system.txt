The '''UTol system''' is the planetary system that contains the planets [[UTol]] and [[Komashi]], which form a binary system orbiting their sun.{{book ref|yumi|part=anotherepilogue}} The sun itself is distinctly red-orange in color. It is larger than Roshar's sun, and comparatively closer to its inhabited planets as well.{{book ref|yumi|2}} 

[[Virtuosity]] [[Splinter]]ed herself in the vicinity of the UTol system, leaving Splinters on Komashi and perhaps elsewhere.{{book ref|yumi|1}}

The [[Iron Seven Waystation]] may also be located in the vicinity of this planetary system.{{book ref|yumi|part=anotherepilogue}}

After fleeing [[Canticle]], [[Sigzil|Zellion]]  likely skips to the UTol System, specifically the planet UTol.{{book ref|tsm|epilogue}}

== Planets ==
;[[UTol]]: UTol is a world dominated by oceans, with very few land masses. It is inhabited by [[Sho Del]].{{book ref|yumi|30}}

;[[Komashi]]: Komashi is the home of the [[Torish]] and [[Nagadan]] people, as well as the [[hijo]] which make life on the planet possible. The invention of a certain [[machine]] caused the entire planet to be engulfed by a black [[shroud]] and produced the world's [[hion]] lines. The machine was destroyed centuries later by [[Yumi]], which also caused the dissipation of the shroud.{{book ref|yumi|part=anotherepilogue}}
