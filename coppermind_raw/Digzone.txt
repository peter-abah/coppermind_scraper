'''Digzone''', previously known as '''Jacob Pham''', is a transference [[Epic]] able to excavate efficiently. Tunnels created with his powers are rectangular.{{book ref|Calamity|20}} He gifts his powers to people who become known as '''Diggers'''.{{book ref|Steelheart|25}}

== History ==
Jacob Pham became an Epic while living in the city of St. Joseph.{{book ref|calamity|20}} He granted his powers to Diggers there, who created an extensive set of caverns throughout the countryside that later became the site of [[David Charleston]] and [[Megan Tarash]]'s confrontation with [[Jonathan Phaedrus]].{{book ref|calamity|38}}

Later, Digzone moved to [[Newcago]] to work with [[Steelheart (Epic)|Steelheart]]. With his powers, Diggers there created the underground portion of the city, complete with drainage and air shafts.{{book ref|steelheart|2}} They were given the whole of the city under which to work, with the exception of the area under the [[First Union Bank (Newcago)|First Union Bank]].{{book ref|steelheart|25}} The original plan was for the city to stretch down several levels, and the Diggers carved out multiple layers with tunnels and rooms. These places became home to many of the lower class people, and during the time when the Diggers were active, people poured into the city for work.{{book ref|steelheart|1}} However, soon the Diggers began to go mad due to the power gifted them.{{book ref|steelheart|25}} The tunnels began to change size, and the Diggers left unfinished rooms behind.{{book ref|steelheart|5}} Loose wiring hung down in various places, and the [[Reckoners]] in Newcago even found rooms carved out with no way to enter but with lights shining inside.{{book ref|steelheart|11}} Digzone's fate since the Diggers' madness is unknown.
