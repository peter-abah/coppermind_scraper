The '''Misted Mountains''' are a mountain range located on the eastern border of [[Shinovar]] on [[Roshar]].{{file ref|Words of Radiance endpaper.jpg|Map of Roshar}}{{book ref|twok|i|4}}

== Geography ==
The Misted Mountains border several nations during the [[Era of Solitude]], including Shinovar, [[Alm]], [[Yezier]], [[Desh]], [[Babatharnam]], [[Rira (country)|Rira]], and [[Iri]].{{file ref|Words of Radiance endpaper.jpg|Map of Roshar}}

They serve as a windbreak for the whole nation of Shinovar, weakening the [[highstorm]]s enough to protect the country and its inhabitants from the destructive power of the storms.{{book ref|twok|i|4}} Due to this effect, the flora and fauna of Shinovar are not defined by surviving the highstorms, so they are vastly different from the rest of Roshar. The eastern slopes of the Misted mountains are home to a particularly rugged kind of rockbud, with a thicker shell and shorter vines than those common in the east of the continent.{{book ref|sa5|43}}

== Trivia ==
*The Misted Mountains, much like Robert Jordan's Mountains of Mist, are an homage to J. R. R. Tolkien's Misty Mountains.{{wob ref|10394}}
