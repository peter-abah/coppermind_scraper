The '''Synod''' is the ruling council of the [[Keeper]]s of [[Terris]].{{book ref|mb2|30}} They were present both before and during the [[Final Empire]], as well as after the [[Catacendre]].

== The Final Empire ==
The Synod charged themselves with keeping knowledge of the world before the [[Lord Ruler]]'s Ascension. However, they did not openly rebel against the Final Empire, instead staying as a secret organization based in [[Tathingdwen]], in the Terris dominance. The Synod opposed [[Sazed]]'s decision to assist the [[skaa rebellion]] and removed him from his position among them after he refused to defend his actions.{{book ref|mb2|37}}

After the Lord Ruler's death, the Synod spread the knowledge collected from before the Ascension. [[Tindwyl]], a Keeper of the Synod, traveled to [[Luthadel]] to assist [[Elend Venture]] in securing his kingdom's borders.{{book ref|mb2|14}}

After the fall of the Lord Ruler, [[Steel Inquisitors]] attacked Tathingdwen while under the influence of [[Ruin]], killing all of the Synod in the city.{{book ref|mb2|56}} This left Sazed as the only known living Keeper.

== Post-Catacendre ==
During [[Wax]]'s lifetime, the Synod rule from within [[the Village]], a Terris enclave in the city of [[Elendel]].{{book ref|mb5|5}}
