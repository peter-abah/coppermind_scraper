'''Shmone''' is a [[Singer]] leader under [[Ba-Ado-Mishram]] during the [[Roshar#False Desolation|False Desolation]]. She falls in love with [[Garith]], a [[Knights Radiant|Radiant]]. She is present at the capture of Ba-Ado-Mishram and has her [[Singer#form|form]] stripped from her.

==Appearance==
Shmone holds [[Direform]], and as such she is covered in carapace barbs. Her skin pattern is made up of large patches of black and red. She has an intense stare, and is taller than Garith.{{book ref|sa5|85}}

==History==
Shmone met [[Garith]] on the battlefield at an unspecified time years before the capture of [[Ba-Ado-Mishram]]. On their first meeting, she stabbed him, but he was understanding about it. They began meeting in secret, as although they were enemies, they shared a desire for peace.{{book ref|sa5|87}}

Shmone became a leader of her people under Ba-Ado-Mishram, and she argued fiercely against Garith in the peace talks between her people and the [[Knights Radiant|Radiant]]s. She demanded more farmland, but Garith refused to fight human kings to obtain land to give them. When Garith said what they have was always enough before, she and her contingent left in their anger.{{book ref|sa5|85}}

Shmone and three other singers met with Garith and some of his [[Windrunners]] later that same day in a glade between rock formations. While interacting with the humans, Shmone exaggerated her mannerisms for their sake, smiling more obviously than a Singer usually would. She and Garith shared their story with the other Windrunners. When one of them expressed distrust of Shmone as the enemy, Garith soothed them. The two share that they told their story to prove to the Windrunners that peace is possible.{{book ref|sa5|87}}

Shmone had convinced Ba-Ado-Mishram to meet with the Windrunners as a show of good faith, and the [[Unmade]] showed herself. She said she agreed with their push for peace, as Garith's sincerity had proven to her that singers and humans could find peace. She was the singer's god, and she wanted them to be safe. She noticed [[Melishi]] and his strike team hidden in the bushes, and they sprung into action at the command of [[Honor]]. Melishi captured Mishram in a perfect gemstone, and for three seconds the tones of Roshar fell still before the Shmone and the other Singers began to writhe. Garith held Shmone as her form was traumatically ripped from her, leaving them both covered in her blood. He hummed to her the [[Rhythm]] of Joy, trying to get through to her, but she had lost some of herself along with her form.{{book ref|sa5|87}}


Shmone appeared in [[Dalinar Kholin]]'s visions in the [[Spiritual Realm]] millennia later. Her story may have been part of what convinces [[Renarin Kholin]] and [[Rlain]] to release Mishram from her imprisonment.{{book ref|sa5|85}}{{book ref|sa5|87}}{{book ref|sa5|135}}

==Relationships==
===Garith===
Shmone has known Garith for years. They keep their relationship quiet, going so far as to pretend to be extremely antagonistic at peace talks between their people. Their love is likely part of the reason she wishes for peace, as she knows that humans are not so different from Singers. Shmone was likely the one that taught Garith to hum to the Rhythm of Joy.
