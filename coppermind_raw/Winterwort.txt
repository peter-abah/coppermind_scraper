'''Winterwort''' is a type of plant native to [[Roshar]]. It is used by surgeons as a soporific anesthetic.{{book ref|sa1|10}}

Winterwort can be used to prepare a patient for surgery. It is ingestible after being crushed to a powder and mixed with oil, although it has a bitter taste. At one spoonful per ten brickweight of the patient, it will keep a person unconscious for around five hours.{{book ref|sa1|10}}

[[Kaladin]] and [[Lirin]] used winterwort on [[Sani]] when they attempted to repair her crushed hand.{{book ref|sa1|10}}
