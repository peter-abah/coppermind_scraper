'''Zendid''' is a member of [[Shallan Davar]]'s guard on [[Roshar]].

He was in the [[Sebarial]] barracks in [[Urithiru]] while some of Shallan's other guards (including [[Gaz]], [[Red (Roshar)|Red]], and [[Vathah]]) were playing [[pieces]]. He was drinking at a table nearby and interjected in a conversation between [[Shallan#Veil|Veil]] and Gaz about being useful.{{book ref|sa3|40}}
