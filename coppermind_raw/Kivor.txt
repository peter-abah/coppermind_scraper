'''Kivor''' is a malen [[listener]] on [[Roshar]]. He is a member of the new [[Council of Five]] created after the first [[Battle of Narak]]. During the True Desolation, he held warform. Due to this, it is likely that he fought in the [[War of Reckoning]]. He was also among the listeners who refused Stormform and fled into the chasms. As a member of the Five, he was among the listeners who went on an expedition to the center of the Plains, where the [[Second Battle of Narak]] was being fought.{{book ref|sa5|89}}

As the expeditionary group neared the Plains, Kivor suggested that they vote on whether or not to continue forward, stating that Odium was crafty and might be playing them. He himself did vote to continue however.{{book ref|sa5|89}} Later, after having been taken captive by Odium’s forces and given an ultimatum, either help them conquer Narak or face destruction, Kivor admitted that he was relieved to finally face this question. They didn’t choose either though, as Venli suggested a cunning plan.{{book ref|sa5|101}} He later served as a member of the first Council of Five of the new listener nation.{{book ref|sa5|146}}
