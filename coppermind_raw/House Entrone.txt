'''House Entrone''' is a [[noble]] house on [[Scadrial]] during the [[Final Empire]] and after the [[Catacendre]].{{book ref|mb1|19}}{{book ref|mb5|1}} 

==History==
===Final Empire===
During the [[Final Empire]], House Entrone was not one of the great houses of [[Luthadel]]. However, it had enough clout to be considered a major political ally of [[House Tekiel]].{{book ref|mb1|19}} When [[Kelsier]] was sowing chaos among nobles as a prelude to the [[skaa rebellion]], he murdered [[Charrs Entrone]] and dumped the corpse in [[Keep Tekiel]]'s gardens.{{book ref|mb1|19}}
===Post-Catacendre===
The house survived through the [[Final Ascension]] and had a presence in [[Elendel]].{{book ref|mb5|1}} [[Hughes Entrone]] was among [[Bleeder]]'s victims at [[Winsting Innate's auction]]{{book ref|mb5|1}} and [[Valette Entrone]] from Elendel once courted [[Waxillium Ladrian]].{{book ref|mb4|2}}{{book ref|mb6|12}} Her cousin, a noble named [[Gave Entrone]], lived in [[New Seran]] and worked in the textile industry.{{book ref|mb6|12}}
