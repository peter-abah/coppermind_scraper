'''Yol Chay''' is the owner of the tech company [[Innovation Information Incorporated]].{{book ref|skin deep|2}} He hires [[Stephen Leeds]] to recover the corpse of [[Panos Maheras]].{{book ref|skin deep|3}}

Yol Chay is a Korean billionaire, with an estimated net worth of 1.2 billion dollars. He's also an amateur rapper and has released at least five albums, all of which were spectacular flops.{{book ref|skin deep|2}} Yol Chay is known to be one of the few people to play along with Stephen's aspects, although he isn't very good at it.{{book ref|legion2|4}} However, he appears to have a certain animosity for [[J.C.]]; on one occasion, he refers to J.C. as a "spook" and donates a substantial amount of money to the [[wikipedia:Brady Campaign|Brady Campaign to Prevent Gun Violence]] in J.C.'s name. Yol Chay also dresses very extravagantly, which includes sunglasses with diamonds on them.{{book ref|legion2|2}}
