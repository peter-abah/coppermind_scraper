'''Cecil G. Bagsworth III''' is the author of several books about interdimensional travel, as well as the editor of [[Alcatraz Smedry]]'s autobiographies.{{book ref|frugal|1}}{{book ref|a6|epilogue}}{{book ref|frugal|part=postscript}} In addition, he is the editor of [[bws:a-night-of-blacker-darkness|''A Night of Blacker Darkness'']].{{wob ref|15487}}

== Appearance and Personality ==
Bagsworth loves truth and enjoys his adventures through the multiverse, but he also finds the breadth of knowledge isolating.{{frugal iw ref|The Wizard's Burden}}

== History ==
At some point, Bagsworth participated in the [[Micronization War]]. In 2085, the year after the invention of interdimensional travel, Bagsworth was recruited for a government-sponsored Middle Ages expedition. There, he won a jousting tournament, introduced electricity, and earned the trust of Henry II.{{frugal iw ref|You Are a Wizard}}{{frugal iw ref|How to Be a Wizard}} Eventually, Bagsworth founded [[Frugal Wizard Inc.]] and wrote several books about interdimensional wizardry.
