'''Relador''' is the [[Order of Windrunners|Windrunner]] that was [[Nahel bond|bonded]] to [[Sylphrena]] before the [[Recreance]].{{book ref|sa4|55}} 

==History==
Relador was a kindly, elderly man. Syl bonded him soon after she was "born" as one of the first [[honorspren]] created by the [[Stormfather]].{{book ref|sa3|95}} Due to his advanced age, Syl believed that he should not have been fighting in the [[Desolation]]s, and he was indeed killed in his first battle.{{book ref|sa4|i|1}}{{book ref|sa4|55}} Upon his death, his bond with Syl was broken and she experienced significant trauma, including pain and memory loss.{{book ref|sa4|i|1}} Because of her relative youth, she was not able to weather the loss of her knight; she instead felt so alone that she lost herself and began to drift until she went to "sleep".{{book ref|sa3|95}}{{book ref|sa4|55}}

==Legacy==
Due to Relador's death, Syl was not bonded when the other [[Knights Radiant|knights]] and [[spren]] soon decided to break their bonds. She was therefore the only [[honorspren]] who did not become a [[deadeye]] on the Day of Recreance.{{book ref|sa4|87}} Her slumber lasted around one thousand years, until she was found by the [[Stormfather]].{{book ref|sa3|95}} 

Although Syl actively tried to forget the pain of losing Relador, her bond with [[Kaladin]] helped restore her memories,{{book ref|sa3|5}} and she was able to recall bits and pieces of her former knight, including being with him in [[Urithiru]].{{book ref|sa4|i|1}} She realized that she could draw on her difficult experience to try and help Kaladin with his depression.{{book ref|sa4|i|1}}
