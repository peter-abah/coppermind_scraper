Brightlord '''Tumul''' is a noble on the [[Shattered Plains]] on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa1|22}}

He is mocked by [[Wit]] along with Brightlord [[Habatab]], Lord [[Yonatan]] and Lady [[Meirav]]. Wit says that it is so easy to insult Habatab, his mind will be as dull as Tumul's, and Tumul happens to be right there. Wit says Tumul's stupidity is spectacular and worthy of praise.{{book ref|sa1|22}}
