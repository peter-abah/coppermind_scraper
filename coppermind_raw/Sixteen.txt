'''Sixteen''' is a [[worldhopper]] originally from [[Scadrial]]{{wob ref|14812}}{{wob ref|15984}} who lives in [[Lasting Integrity]] in the [[Rosharan subastral]], also known as Shadesmar.{{book ref|sa4|75}} His name is likely an alias inspired by the significance of the number sixteen within the [[cosmere]], particularly on Scadrial.{{epigraph ref|mb3|71}}{{wob ref|14017}}

== Appearance and Personality ==
He is a short, completely bald man with pallid skin and eyes that make him appear [[Shin]] to Rosharans, likely indicating a lack of [[Wikipedia:Epicanthic fold|epicanthic folds]]. He walks with a hunched posture and wears a hooded cloak to hide his appearance.{{book ref|sa4|75}}

== Attributes and Abilities ==
Sixteen lives in a small box near the statue garden in Lasting Integrity. He leaves his home every sixteen days, a practice that amuses the [[honorspren]] due to its consistency. His motivation for this routine is unknown, but when he leaves his house he brings no food or waste out of or into it, and the spren do not believe that he ever bathes.{{book ref|sa4|75}}

== History ==
Sixteen is from Scadrial,{{wob ref|15984}} but it is unclear how or why he came to live in Lasting Integrity. The local [[spren]] find him odd, but they do not mind having him around; he seems to have very little interaction with anyone.{{book ref|sa4|75}}

When [[Shallan Davar]] came to Lasting Integrity hunting [[Restares]], she suspected that Sixteen was the man she was looking for. When the day came for Sixteen to leave his house, Shallan sat outside waiting for him to emerge. When he left, he moved in a furtive, hurried fashion through the statue park near his home. When he stopped to look at some notes, Shallan accosted him and his hood fell. Shallan quickly realized that Sixteen's appearance did not match [[Mraize]]'s description of Restares. He shouted, then spoke to Shallan in a language that she did not recognize before he fled back into his house.{{book ref|sa4|75}} Shallan later briefed Mraize on the encounter; he believed it very unlikely that Sixteen was Restares in disguise, but he did not completely rule it out.{{book ref|sa4|78}}
