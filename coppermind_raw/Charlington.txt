'''Charlington''', or '''Chuck''', is a student at [[Armedius Academy]]. Although he has been a classmate of [[Joel Saxon]]'s for three years, he often forgets Joel's name. He has a reputation for only paying attention to girls.{{book ref|Rithmatist|4}}

Charlie was angry that he couldn't take his history final early due to the disappearance of [[Lilly Whiting]], as he wanted to go join his family in Europe. After explaining the situation to Joel, he was unmollified by Joel's attempt to placate him and moved off to complain to a different group of students instead.{{book ref|rithmatist|4}}
