'''Liquidyne''' is an [[Epic]] from West Virginia. He has the power to melt any substance into a puddle without using heat, including wood, metal, and cloth. He is dead, and [[Parik]] uses his severed left hand in an experiment to give Liquidyne's power to [[Lifeforce]].{{book ref|lux|25}}
