The '''Stormfalls''' are a number of waterfalls located in the [[Iriali]] city of [[Rall Elorim]] on [[Roshar]]. Freshwater cascades down the mountainous overhang of Rall Elorim, collecting in a large reservoir below. The Stormfalls have a large impact on the ecology of the region nearby Rall Elorim, promoting the growth of vines and algae.{{rpg ref|slwg|154}}

Climbing the crest of the Stormfalls is said to be notoriously dangerous, especially with the numerous unpredictable storm surges. However, the climb is generally worth the effort, as the top is full of groves of rare plants that can be refined into expensive spices and medicines. Weary [[tidereader]]s and [[Order of Truthwatchers|Truthwatchers]] have also been known to seek peaceful meditation at the brink of the roaring waterfalls.{{rpg ref|slwg|155}}
