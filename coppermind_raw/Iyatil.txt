'''Iyatil''' is a mysterious member of the [[Ghostbloods]], a secret organization across multiple planets across the cosmere, of which she is part of the [[Roshar]]an division. She is referred to as [[Mraize]]'s [[babsk]], and is a high-ranking member of the organization.{{book ref|sa2|63}}{{book ref|sa2|64}} Though she currently lives on Roshar, she is a [[worldhopper]] and is originally from [[Silverlight]].{{wob ref|190}}{{rpg ref|slwg|141}} She has [[Southern Scadrian]] ancestry{{wob ref|4879}}{{wob ref|12046}} and always wears a distinctive mask.{{book ref|sa2|43}}

== Appearance and Personality ==
{{image|Iyatil by Marie Seeberger.jpg|side=right|width=220px}}
Iyatil usually wears a simple outfit of a dark brown shirt and trousers, along with an intricate belt that appears to be studded with carapace.{{book ref|sa2|63}}{{book ref|sa2|43}} She does not cover her [[safehand]], and [[Shallan]] mistook her for a male the first time they met.{{book ref|sa2|43}} She usually wears gloves and carries a dagger at her side.{{book ref|sa2|63}} When she is trailing someone, she dons a black cloak.{{book ref|sa2|43}} She has tan skin; she is also described as short or small,{{book ref|sa2|43}} although this is common for worldhoppers on [[Roshar]] since the humans there are unusually tall.{{wob ref|15034}} She is described as middle-aged.{{book ref|sa5|133}}

She has dark, beady eyes and rarely seems to blink.{{book ref|sa2|43}}{{book ref|sa2|54}} The visible part of her face is impassive; her presence is generally unsettling.{{book ref|sa2|43}}{{book ref|sa2|63}} She does not reveal more information than necessary, often simply refusing to answer questions; this keeps her motivations concealed.{{book ref|sa2|63}}{{book ref|sa2|64}}{{book ref|sa4|9}} She exploits other's weaknesses to her advantage.{{book ref|sa5|32}}{{book ref|sa5|49}} She is a collector and keeps a trophy case filled with various items from across the [[Cosmere]].{{book ref|sa5|26}}

===Southern Scadrian Mask===
{{quote
|My mask reserves my self. Besides, it gives me the ability to adapt.
|Iyatil{{book ref|sa2|63}}
}}
Iyatil's most distinguishing feature is a red-orange carapace mask that is made up of pieces, like a mosaic. The mask covers the area around her eyes; it has an angry appearance, and seems to be growing into her skin.{{book ref|sa2|54}} The mask, which is at least partially painted,{{book ref|sa2|54}} is not [[Investiture|Invested]].{{wob ref|717}} Her mask is similar to those worn by the [[Southern Scadrian#The Hunters|Hunters nation]] on Scadrial,{{book ref|mb6|22}} and that culture is part of Iyatil's heritage.{{wob ref|13475}} She considers her mask an asset{{book ref|sa2|63}} and never removes it; she will kill someone if they try to do so.{{book ref|sa2|63}}

== Attributes and Abilities ==
{{image|Iyatil with masks.jpeg|side=left|width=200px}}
Iyatil is a [[worldhopper]]. She hails from [[Silverlight]] in the [[Cognitive Realm]] but lives on [[Roshar]] in the [[Physical Realm]].{{rpg ref|slwg|141}}{{wob ref|12733}} She has also been to other unidentified planets.{{wob ref|1394}} Her personal background along with her membership in both the [[Seventeenth Shard]]{{wob ref|8134}} and the [[Ghostbloods]] presumably give her a very high level of [[cosmere]] awareness.

===Role in the Ghostbloods===
The Ghostbloods have a clear hierarchy{{book ref|sa4|30}} and Iyatil appears to be one of the highest-ranking members on Roshar. She serves as a teacher and mentor for [[Mraize]]; he refers to her as his '[[babsk]]' in local parlance, and she has supervised him on several trips off-world.{{wob ref|1394}} She is aware of people and organizations such as King [[Gavilar Kholin]] and the [[Sons of Honor]] that are tampering with magic that could have larger cosmere implications, and works to investigate them.{{book ref|sa2|63}} She investigated [[Talenel]]{{book ref|sa2|63}} and sought to speak with [[Shalash]].{{book ref|sa3|i|8}} She is empowered to negotiate on behalf of the master of the Ghostbloods, [[Thaidakar]],{{book ref|sa4|60}} and keeps him apprised of the situation on Roshar since he cannot visit himself.{{book ref|sa4|115}} Although Thaidakar is Iyatil's master, she leads independently; Thaidakar's remote location means that he does not always have detailed knowledge of the Ghostbloods' activities.{{wob ref|15689}}{{wob ref|15014}}

===Spy and Assassin===
Iyatil is extremely stealthy{{book ref|sa2|63}} and can seem to appear out of nowhere in a room.{{book ref|sa2|54}} She stays well-informed regarding the security, schedules, and geography of the area that she is in, such as the [[Alethi]] [[warcamp]]s. She is observant, skilled at tracking, and able to conceal herself, making her an effective spy and assassin.{{book ref|sa2|63}}{{book ref|sa2|64}}{{book ref|sa2|88}} She is trained in the use of several different weapons,{{book ref|sa2|63}}{{book ref|sa2|88}}{{book ref|sa5|32}} and she considers escaping from an Alethi monastery without assistance to be a trivial task.{{book ref|sa2|64}} She is also able to move "too quickly" to be chased.{{book ref|sa2|88}}

===Invested Arts===
Her [[Scadrian]] heritage could theoretically enable her to use one or more of the [[Metallic Arts]],{{wob ref|6148}}{{wob ref|12046}} but there is no confirmation that she can do so.{{wob ref|12046}}{{wob ref|9325}} Additionally, a side effect of the [[Catacendre]] was the need for people of Southern Scadrian heritage to use [[Feruchemy#Unsealed_Metalminds|Feruchemical medallions]] created with [[Excisor]]s in order to survive in cool temperatures;{{book ref|mb6|21}} it is unclear if Iyatil possesses a medallion or if she even needs one.{{wob ref|2531}}

Iyatil is bonded to an [[Enlightened]] [[inkspren]], giving her access to the [[Surge]]s of Transformation and Transportation. Because her [[spren]] is Enlightened, it is unclear if her abilities differ from a typical [[Elsecaller]], but she is able to create an Elsegate.{{book ref|sa5|28}}

== History ==
{{image|Iyatil by EccoS.jpg|side=left|width=200px}}
Iyatil was born in [[Silverlight]],{{wob ref|12733}} a city of cosmere-aware people in the [[Cognitive Realm]], supposedly alongside her brother, [[Dlavil]].{{book ref|mb7|epilogue|1}}{{wob ref|2520}} She is of [[Southern Scadrian]] heritage,{{wob ref|4879}}{{wob ref|12046}} specifically from the [[Southern Scadrian#The Hunters|Hunters nation]].{{wob ref|13475}} It is not known how her family or her ancestors came to live in Silverlight, but their history there is relatively recent.{{book ref|mb6|22}} As a people, the Hunters are known to be stern former warriors that now seek answers and secrets about the [[Catacendre]].{{book ref|mb6|22}}

At some point, Iyatil was a member of the [[Seventeenth Shard]], a mysterious organization that includes various worldhoppers. She left for unknown reasons,{{wob ref|8134}} although it is possible that she was directly recruited by the [[Ghostbloods]].{{wob ref|11599}}

Iyatil came to live on [[Roshar]] at some point prior to {{Rosharan date|1173}}. Her early activities on the planet are unknown, other than her training of [[Mraize]].{{wob ref|1394}} She was present at [[Shallan Davar]]'s first meeting with the Ghostbloods and followed Shallan after the meeting, trying to track her back to [[Tyn]].{{book ref|sa2|43}} She was also present when Mraize learned of Shallan's drawing abilities and decided to give her a new mission.{{book ref|sa2|54}} Iyatil then decided to surveil Shallan, but she was discovered by [[Pattern]] despite her skill in spycraft. Shallan then tricked her into revealing herself with the use of [[Surgebinding#Illumination|Lightweaving]]. Iyatil was stunned, but quickly recovered and explained to Shallan that she would be investigating the recent arrival of a madman to the [[Alethi]] [[warcamp]]s. She then helped Shallan infiltrate the monastery where [[Talenel]] was being held.{{book ref|sa2|63}} 

After the [[Battle of Narak]], she attempted to assassinate Highprince [[Meridas Amaram]] with poisoned darts and a blowgun. Her motive is unclear, but it was likely related to Amaram's activities with [[Restares]] and the [[Sons of Honor]] and his knowledge about the Ghostbloods.{{book ref|sa1|51}} She nearly succeeded in killing Amaram, but she was foiled by Talenel. Amaram summoned his [[Shardblade]], but Iyatil easily escaped.{{book ref|sa2|88}}

During the [[True Desolation]], Iyatil continued to work behind the scenes. Her master, [[Thaidakar]], wanted to be able to collect and transport [[Stormlight]]. To help reach this goal, he sought control of the [[Oathgate]]s.{{book ref|sa4|13}} He was unhappy when Iyatil informed him that this was impossible, and she and Mraize pivoted to simply gaining access to the gates. Mraize attempted to convince [[Raboniel]] to negotiate with Iyatil by giving the [[Fused]] a valuable [[Silver#Threnodite_Chains|Threnodite chain]].{{book ref|sa4|60}}

Sometime before the [[contest of champions]], Iyatil brings [[Aleen]] and [[Lieke]] to Roshar from off-world to reinforce the Ghostblood cell.{{book ref|sa5|16}} She and Mraize hold a meeting of the cell informing the other Ghostbloods of their plan to infiltrate the [[Spiritual Realm]] and find the prison of [[Ba-Ado-Mishram]].{{book ref|sa5|25}} Iyatil, along with Mraize and Lieke, infiltrate the [[Windrunner]] guard and attack Shallan, [[Renarin]], and [[Rlain]] to gain access to the Spiritual Realm.{{book ref|sa5|31}} During the fight, Iyatil injures Shallan with a blowgun before the group is pulled into the collapsing perpendicularity.{{book ref|sa5|32}} Inside the Spiritual Realm, Iyatil pretends to be [[Formless]], appearing to Shallan inside multiple visions.{{book ref|sa5|49}}{{book ref|sa5|134}} In this disguise, she instructs Shallan to kill both Mraize and Dalinar.{{book ref|sa5|65}} After realizing that Formless is a disguise, Shallan attacks her with an [[anti-Stormlight]] knife. Iyatil attempts to heal with [[Stormlight]] which triggers an explosion, killing her.{{book ref|sa5|134}}

== Relationships ==

=== Mraize ===
{{image|Iyatil face.jpg|side=right|width=150px}}
{{quote
|A group of hunters working together could turn upon one another far too easily. And so, I respect my babsk, and you respect me. We do not strike against our own, and we do not negotiate upward.
|Mraize, to Shallan{{book ref|sa4|30}}
}}
Iyatil is Mraize's master; he uses the [[Thaylen]] term '[[babsk]]' to describe their relationship.{{book ref|sa4|9}} Iyatil calls Mraize her student{{book ref|sa2|63}} and she closely supervised him during his training.{{wob ref|1394}} She believes that Mraize is too focused on himself, often losing sight of the larger purpose of the organization.{{book ref|sa5|26}} At one point, Iyatil had Mraize followed, seemingly a tradition within the [[Ghostbloods]] since the person doing the following can step in and help if needed.{{book ref|sa4|13}} She advises Mraize on his activities, including his recruitment of [[Shallan Davar#Veil|Veil]],{{book ref|sa2|88}} and Mraize is often empowered to negotiate on Iyatil's behalf.{{book ref|sa4|13}} At the same time, Mraize is not bound to follow Iyatil's instructions, as the Ghostblood hierarchy allows its members to act independently.{{wob ref|14637}} However, she has some level of direct control over his activities, forbidding him from engaging in [[worldhopping|interplanetary travel]]. While he respects her capability, he resents the restrictions she imposes.{{book ref|sa5|134}}

=== Thaidakar ===
{{quote
|Master Thaidakar will see eventually. He is smarter than you give him credit for.
|Iyatil to Shallan{{book ref|sa5|26}}
}}

Thaidakar is the master of the Ghostbloods and Iyatil reports to him using a [[seon]].{{book ref|sa4|13}}{{book ref|sa5|5}}  While Thaidakar is very interested in the events on [[Roshar]], there is a degree of separation between him and the local branch of the Ghostbloods.{{wob ref|15014}} Iyatil works to accomplish his goals and provides updates on the situation on Roshar.{{book ref|sa4|13}} However, Thaidakar's remote location means that he may not be aware of the specific activities of Iyatil and the other Ghostbloods.{{wob ref|15689}} Despite this unawareness, Iyatil believes that Thaidakar will accept the course of action pursuing the prison of Ba-Ado-Mishram.{{book ref|sa5|26}}

=== Dlavil ===
Dlavil is her brother.{{book ref|mb7|epilogue|1}} Thaidakar believes he will eventually seek revenge for her death.{{book ref|sa5|147}}

=== Sja-anat ===
Iyatil receives an enlightened inkspren from Sja-anat.{{book ref|sa5|138}} They communicate about the Spiritual Realm and the prison of Ba-Ado-Mishram.{{book ref|sa5|26}}
