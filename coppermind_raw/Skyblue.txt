'''Skyblue''' is a [[chasmfiend]] on [[Roshar]] who allied with the [[listeners]] after the [[War of Reckoning]].{{book ref|sa5|118}}

== Appearance and Personality ==
{{for|Chasmfiend#Appearance_and_Anatomy|more information|Chasmfiend}}
Very little is known about Skyblue’s appearance beyond what is typical for a chasmfiend--she is a massive crustacean-like [[greatshell]] with a sturdy carapace.{{book ref|sa5|118}}{{book ref|sa1|13}} She shows enough intelligence to follow specific instructions from the listeners during combat, and is singled out as the chasmfiend who understands not to destroy the humans’ bridge.{{book ref|sa5|118}}

== History ==
Skyblue is part of the group of chasmfiends led by [[Thundercloud]] who decided to make peace with the listeners after the War of Reckoning.{{book ref|sa5|70}}{{book ref|sa5|58}} She joins the listeners in their deception at the [[Second Battle of Narak]], where she and the other chasmfiends launch a mock assault on the human fortifications.{{book ref|sa5|118}} During the battle, Skyblue is specifically tasked with destroying the gate between two plateaus, while carefully leaving the humans’ bridge intact, so that the humans could flee using the Oathgate platform.{{book ref|sa5|118}}
