'''Iald''' is a port city in [[Arelon]] on the planet of [[Sel]].{{book ref|Elantris|11}} It is located in the [[Arelon#Plantations|Ial Plantation]], and is adjacent to the Nae Plantation.{{map ref|Arelon}}

During [[Iadon]]'s reign, Iald is the second wealthiest city in the country, behind only [[Kae]]. [[Roial]] is the governor of Iald before the [[Reod]], and after the catastrophe, Roial's holdings include the city, making him fabulously wealthy.{{book ref|Elantris|11}}
