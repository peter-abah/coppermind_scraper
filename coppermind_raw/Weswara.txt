'''Weswara''' is the homeland of the '''Weswarans'''.{{book ref|frugal|6}} 

== Geography and Ecology == 

It is on the island equivalent to Britain, along with the [[Waelish]] kingdom of the [[Black Bear]]. [[Hordaland]] lies to the east, across the ocean.
  
Settlements in Weswara include [[Stenford]], [[Wellbury]], and [[Maelport]].

Weswara is fairly thinly populated. The earldom centered at [[Maelport]] includes ten villages as well as the city, and is about a five-day walk - perhaps a hundred miles - across{{book ref|frugal|12}}.

== History ==


== Politics ==
Weswara is governed by thegns (who oversee a village and given the title of Little Father), reeves (an intermediate authority and given the title of Midfather), and earls (controlling large areas and given the title of Highfather). The title of [[bretwalda]] is roughly equivalent to king, but it appears to be currently vacant.

Weswara is threatened from both directions, between the Black Bear's kingdom and the Hordamen from the sea.{{book ref|fwh|15}}

== Culture ==
Life there is generally harsh, and people are focused on the needs of survival.{{book ref|frugal|15}}
