'''Kuma'tiki''' is [[Lunamor]]'s second daughter. She is named after a type of shell found in the [[Horneater Peaks]].{{book ref|sa3|37}}

Kuma'tiki arrived at the [[Shattered Plains]] with her family in {{Rosharan date|1174|1}} after traveling for months to meet with Lunamor, who sent them a message shortly after his arrival. Kuma'tiki's caravan was attacked by [[Voidbringer]]s and her family defended themselves behind a pile of furniture. During the attack a member of her family shot a Voidbringer with an [[Unkalaki]] arrow. They were found by [[Bridge Four]] during a patrol and taken to [[Urithiru]]. After settling in Urithiru, she helped her father serve food and drink to Bridge Four.{{book ref|sa3|37}}{{book ref|sa3|55}}

Shortly after the evacuation of the residents of [[Hearthstone]], Kuma'tiki traveled back to the Peaks with most of her family, with just [[Cord]] and [[Gift]] staying behind.{{book ref|sa4|12}}
