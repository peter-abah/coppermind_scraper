'''Mara''' is a female pilot on [[Detritus]].  She is a wise woman who is good at consoling people during times of loss and grief.{{book ref|sky1|20}}

[[Cobb]] refers to her as 'Old Mara' -- it is unclear if she was an older woman, if this was a nickname, or if he is referring to the amount of time that has passed since he knew her.{{book ref|sky1|20}}

She was Cobb's instructor in his youth, and at some point she passed away.{{book ref|sky1|20}}  Mara's role in teaching Cobb implies that she was a member of the [[DDF]].
