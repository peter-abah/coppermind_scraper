'''Tremredare''' is a large city in the [[Western Dominance]] of the [[Final Empire]]. It is one of [[Scadrial]]'s major cultural centers before the [[Collapse]].{{book ref|mb3|25}}

It is located in the southwest portion of the Dominance, west of [[Morag]] and near the base of a mountain range.{{map ref|Final Empire}} A river or canal connects Tremredare to [[Lake Tyrian]] via [[Chardees]] and [[Fadrex City]]. Another arm of the same waterway connects to the [[River Channerel]].{{map ref|final empire 1021|the Final Empire (1021)}}

Tremredare served as the capital of the Western Dominance until [[Ashweather Cett]] seized power in the area and moved the capital to Fadrex City since it was smaller and more defensible.{{book ref|mb3|25}}

It is unclear if anything remains in Tremredare's former location after the [[Catacendre]].{{map ref|Scadrial}}
