'''Stilla''' is one of [[Holly Winseed (Original)|Holly]]’s good friends. She owns a citrus fruit stand in the [[Riverside Marketplace]], and sells real fruit there. Her citrus stand is the place where Holly first met [[Jonathan Gentry|Jonathan]]. Stilla has a mop of curly hair that falls over her eyes. She wears a homespun vest over a white shirt and trousers. {{book ref|og|4}}
