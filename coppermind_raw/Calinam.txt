'''Calinam''' is a scholar on [[Roshar]].{{epigraph ref|sa1|39}}

== History ==
Calinam wrote the book ''Among the Darkeyed'', which appears to be a compilation of darkeye folktales.{{epigraph ref|sa1|39}} During the course of her research, Princess [[Jasnah Kholin]] noted that this book contained many mentions of [[Oathgate]]s and instantaneous travel. It seems likely that this served as a piece of evidence for Jasnah that [[Stormseat]] might still have a functioning Oathgate connected to [[Urithiru]].{{book ref|sa2|6}}

After the loss of the ''[[Wind's Pleasure]]'', Brightlady [[Shallan Davar]] discovered the mentions of Oathgates from ''Among the Darkeyed'' in Jasnah's notes.{{book ref|sa2|47}} This helped her to realize that Jasnah had likely been looking for an Oathgate on the [[Shattered Plains]].
