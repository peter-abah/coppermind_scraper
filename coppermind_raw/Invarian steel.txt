'''Invarian steel''' is a metal alloy on [[Scadrial]]. It is silvery in color. [[Ranette]] uses it to make [[Vindication]], her [[Allomancer]] specific handgun. It is stronger and lighter than ordinary steel. This allows her to increase the number of chambers in the cylinder without making the pistol too large.{{book ref|mb4|14}}
