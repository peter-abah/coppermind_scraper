'''Second of Saplings''' is an [[Eelakin]] general on [[First of the Sun]] present at the talks with the [[Ones Above]] as well as the [[Rosharan]] envoy. He has a grey and brown [[Aviar]] and carries a repeating pistol in a holster at his side.{{book ref|iote|7}}

Saplings' primary concern during the talks is the aliens using their vast military superiority to destroy the [[homeisles]] or their infrastructure. When presented with the Rosharan envoy's offer of keeping one out of a hundred Aviar, he becomes agitated and reaches for his sidearm causing the Rosharan to threaten him with a [[Shardblade#Radiant Shardblade|Shardgun]].{{book ref|iote|7}}
