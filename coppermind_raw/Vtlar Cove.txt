'''Vtlar Cove''' is a sheltered area along the coast of [[Thaylenah]] on [[Roshar]].

The [[Azish]] fleet, including [[Yanagawn]] and other high-ranking officials, sheltered there from an [[Everstorm]] as they attempted to sail away from the [[Battle of Thaylen Field]].{{book ref|sa3|116}}
