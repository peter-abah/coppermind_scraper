'''Jon Deadfinger''' is a lawman from [[Far Dorest]] in the [[Roughs]] on [[Scadrial]].{{book ref|mb4|11}}

== Appearance Personality ==
Deadfinger is  a hard man, willing to beat a confession out of his suspects.{{book ref|mb4|10}}

== History ==
Jon Deadfinger worked as a lawman before [[Waxillium Ladrian]] went to the Roughs. However, as a lone lawman in a town with a high population, his influence did not extend far beyond the borders of Far Dorest.{{book ref|mb4|4}} Deadfinger was too busy handling problems in his own town to do much work outside of it. When Wax was first learning to be a lawman, Jon Deadfinger mentored him and taught him the basics.{{book ref|mb5|6}}

Deadfinger was the lawman who easily caught a sixteen year-old [[Wayne]]{{book ref|mb5|6}} after Wayne inadvertently killed the innocent man he had been trying to rob.{{book ref|mb4|10}} Deadfinger and Wax found him hiding underneath a crawlspace of a house. Jon dragged Wayne out of the crawl space while Wayne complained of a sound of gunfire only he could hear.{{book ref|mb5|6}}

For a few years after Wax departed to be the lawman of [[Weathering]], Deadfinger, Wax, and [[Miles Dagouter]] had a sort of lawkeeper's alliance, but this eventually seems to have fallen apart.{{book ref|aol|11}}
