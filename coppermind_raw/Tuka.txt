'''Tuka''' is the director of the park that holds flora and fauna from the [[Pantheon]] in the [[homeisles]] capital on [[First of the Sun]]. She has never been to [[Patji (island)|Patji]] (or likely any of the Pantheon) and while she respects [[Dusk]] and is glad to have him give presentations at the park, she does not understand the [[trapper]] way of life or the true dangers of the wildlife on the Pantheon.{{book ref|iote|1}}

== Appearance and Personality ==
Tuka is a stout, boisterous woman with long black hair. She wears orange clothing practically religiously which makes her look like a fruit.{{book ref|iote|1}}
