The '''Worldspire''' is a massive glass spike located in the Pacific Ocean and marks the exact center of the world according to the people of the [[Free Kingdoms]].{{book ref|a2|5}}

== Structure ==
It runs from the upper atmosphere down into the center of the planet, which is made of glass. It is a recreational activity to skydive off of it.{{book ref|a4|3}} It is located somewhere around the Kalmarian Wilds. There is a city built at the base of the Worldspire and wooden scaffolding going all the way to the top.

== Magical Effects ==
What happens to the spire is transferred to a random number of people, with different people each time.{{book ref|a5|Lilliana}} This can happen by tapping it, which causes a ringing sensation in the ears of some people, wind blowing on it, which can cause a cold chill, or striking the spire, which causes headaches. 

== History ==
The events involving the Worldspire were hard to ignore, even in the [[Hushlands]]. [[Attica Smedry]] planned to use the Worldspire's properties to give the [[Smedry Talent]]s to the everyone in the world. However, [[Biblioden]] stole Attica's notes and learned how to use the Worldspire's properties to vaporize the people of the [[Free Kingdoms]] and end the war in the [[Librarian]]s' favor.{{book ref|a6|4}}
