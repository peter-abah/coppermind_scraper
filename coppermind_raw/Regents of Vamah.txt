The '''Regents of Vamah''' are a group of [[lighteyes]] based in [[Revolar]]. After the occupation of Revolar by the [[Fused]], the Regents were cast down from their leadership positions. However, after the conquest of [[Kholinar]], they seized back control over a few of Revolar’s districts, and the few Fused present in Revolar let them.{{rpg ref|slwg|152}}
