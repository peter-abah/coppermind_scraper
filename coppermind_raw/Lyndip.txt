'''Lyndip''' is [[Allomancer]] [[Jak]]'s spirit guide on [[Scadrial]]. Jak claims that Lyndip appears to him during moments of great danger or difficulty. She has the appearance of a talking crow, but Jak suspects that she is one of the [[kandra|Faceless Immortals]]. She purports to follow and speak for the [[Survivor]], and Jak regards her as sent by him.{{book ref|eltania}}

[[Handerwym]], Jak's [[Terris]]man steward, has never seen Lyndip and cannot confirm her existence. He suspects that at least one instance of Lyndip's appearances was a hallucination. He has also pointed out that the Faceless Immortals are a feature of [[Pathism]], not [[Survivorism]], but Jak does not seem to care.{{book ref|eltania}}

==Trivia==
*[[Brandon]] has not confirmed whether Lyndip is a [[kandra]], an [[Aviar]], or whether she even actually exists.{{wob ref|12661}}
