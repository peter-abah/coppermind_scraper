'''Vif Sparkle Tonic''' is a brand of drink on [[Scadrial]].{{file ref|House_Record_broadsheet.jpg|''The House Record'' Broadsheet}}

It was made by Doctors [[Fronks Vif|Fronks]] and [[Selvest Vif]], who claimed that it relieved drowsiness and irritability caused by ulcers, animalcules, and poor nutrition.{{file ref|House_Record_broadsheet.jpg|''The House Record'' Broadsheet}} By {{date|348|PC}}, other drinks claiming to have similar benefits were on the market, and Vif Sparkle Co. ran an ad in ''[[The Two Seasons]]'' broadsheet of [[Bilming]] touting their secret formula and exhorting customers always to buy the Vif name brand.{{file ref|The_Two_Seasons_broadsheet.jpg|''The Two Seasons'' broadsheet}}
