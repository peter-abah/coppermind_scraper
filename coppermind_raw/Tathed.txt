'''Tathed''' is a [[Terrisman]] who lives in [[The Village]] on [[Scadrial]].{{book ref|bom|prologue}}

== History ==
When a constable came to the Village to speak to Elder [[Vwafendal]] about the recent arson in the area, [[Idashwy]] overheard Tathed asking about the situation.{{book ref|bom|prologue}} She later told [[Waxillium Ladrian]] what she had heard.
