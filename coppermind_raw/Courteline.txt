'''Courteline''' is a settlement near [[Luthadel]], presumably in the Central Dominance.{{book ref|mb1|6}}

It is the location of a failed [[skaa]] rebellion that occurred half a century prior to the [[Collapse]]. The entire [[Luthadel]] Garrison was sent in retaliation.{{book ref|mb1|6}} It was the only time that [[Hammond]] knew that the entire Garrison left Luthadel.

== Trivia ==
*This town is possibly named after [[Wikipedia: Georges Courteline| Georges Courteline]]
