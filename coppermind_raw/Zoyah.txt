'''Zoyah''' is a [[Order of Windrunners|Windrunner]] and spy from [[Kholinar]] on [[Roshar]]. She is known to be passionate and honorable. After being captured by [[Eltharon]], Zoyah became somewhat haunted, and wanted revenge above all.{{rpg ref|ss|23}} This thirst for revenge, along with her revealing Melur’s membership of the resistance, disrupted Zoyah’s bond with her spren.{{rpg ref|ss|22}} She is said to look like a statuesque Alethi human with light blue eyes.{{rpg ref|ss|23}}

== History ==
A Kholinar native, Zoyah joined the resistance after she became a Windrunner.{{rpg ref|ss|23}} At some point in time, [[Eltharon]] sent a fake spanreed message to Zoyah, which lead her to reveal her location to him in an unknown way. Because of this, he managed to take her captive. In the following weeks, he tortured her until she revealed [[Melur]]’s membership of the resistance.{{rpg ref|ss|22}} The other members of the resistance assumed that Eltharon had murdered her. This changed when Melur received a spanreed communication, supposedly from Zoyah, that asked her for help escaping. The surgeon-spy assembled a party to rescue Zoyah,{{rpg ref|ss|21}} which it successfully did. After encountering Zoyah, who was surprised to see them, the party realised that it was a trap, and swiftly returned to a hospital, which was the nearest base. There, they encountered Eltharon and a fight ensued. It is unclear how the fight ended and what happened afterwards.{{rpg ref|ss|23}}
