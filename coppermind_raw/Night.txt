The '''Night''' is an ancient [[spren]] on [[Roshar]] who was created by [[Adonalsium]] alongside the planet, the [[Wind]], and the [[Stone]].{{book ref|sa5|4}}{{book ref|sa5|52}} Her role was to shroud or possibly to nurture the singers.{{book ref|sa5|100}}

== Attributes and Abilities ==
=== Nahel Bond (Historical) ===
{{for|Order of Bondsmiths}}
Prior to the creation of the [[Nightwatcher]] from the Night, she was one of three Bondsmith spren.{{book ref|sa5|21}} As such, it would follow that forming a Nahel bond with her may have granted a Bondsmith power over [[Connection]] and other Spiritual attributes, but the full scope of its effects are unknown.

== History ==
When [[Cultivation]] arrived on Roshar, she found the Night's song to be particularly beautiful, which helped convince her to stay on Roshar despite the fact that she and [[Honor]] had originally planned to find an uninhabited planet to settle on.{{book ref|sa5|100}}

Even in the earliest days of Roshar, few people loved or even spoke of the Night.{{book ref|sa5|21}} Instead, the [[singer]]s claimed her siblings Wind and Stone, as well as the spren, as their gods.{{book ref|sa5|43}}{{epigraph ref|sa3|116}} As a result, the Night eventually left Roshar, though how this affected the planet or where she went are unclear. As a replacement, Cultivation created the [[Nightwatcher]] from some of the same essence.{{book ref|sa5|21}}

When in a vision of the [[Recreance]], [[Dalinar Kholin]] seemed to feel the presence of the Night,{{book ref|sa5|85}} though she would have already left Roshar by that time.{{book ref|sa5|21}}
