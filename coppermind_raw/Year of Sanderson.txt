'''''[[Year of Sanderson]]''''' was a Kickstarter campaign by [[Dragonsteel Entertainment]] that released in 2023.{{url ref|url=https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/dragonsteel/surprise-four-secret-novels-by-brandon-sanderson|text=Kickstarter Campaign|site=Kickstarter|name=KickstarterCampaign}}

The Kickstarter campaign was announced and went live on March 1, 2022.{{url ref|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6a-k6eaT-jQ|text=Brandon's Announcement Video|site=Youtube|name=AnnouncementVideo}} The campaign's goal was set at one million dollars, and it surpassed that amount in 35 minutes. Within 24 hours, it had raised $15.4 million, the single most successful day of any Kickstarter campaign.{{url ref|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2022/03/03/books/brandon-sanderson-kickstarter.html|text="Fantasy Author Raises $15.4 Million in 24 Hours to Self-Publish"|site=nytimes.com|date=2022-02-03|name=nytimes}} After three days, the campaign broke the previous Kickstarter funding record of $20,338,986 held by the [[wikipedia:Pebble Time|Pebble Time]] smartwatch.{{url ref|url=https://www.theverge.com/2022/3/4/22960365/brandon-sanderson-fantasy-novels-kickstarter-cosmere|text=Brandon Sanderson's surprise novels overtake Pebble watch as most successful Kickstarter ever|date=2022-03-04|site=theverge.com|name=Smartwatch}} The project ended on March 31, 2022, with 185,341 backers and $41,754,153 in funding, attracting widespread media attention due to its sheer size and groundbreaking nature within the publishing industry.{{url ref|url=https://www.kickstarter.com/discover/most-funded|text=Most Funded Kickstarters|site=Kickstarter|name=MostFunded}}

==Rewards==
The campaign gave rewards every month, with four new books, called Secret Projects, being released quarterly. Swag boxes, each based around a theme of Brandon's books, were released in the remaining eight months. There was also a free download on Brandon's website, matching the theme of each month.{{ref|name=Rewards}} Extra boxes were sold on Brandon's website after the Kickstarter ended.{{url ref|url=https://www.dragonsteelbooks.com/collections/the-year-of-sanderson|text=Year of Sanderson|site=dragonsteelbooks.com|name=YearofSanderson}}

The Cosmere Character Pin were designed by [[Coppermind:Artists/Sandara Tang|Sandara Tang]].{{url ref|url=https://www.brandonsanderson.com/secret-novels/|text=Secret Novels|site=brandonsanderson.com|name=Rewards}}

===January===
''[[Tress of the Emerald Sea]]'' (Secret Project 1) released on January 1, 2023. It would later have a standard publishing which was released on April 4, 2023.{{url ref|url=https://www.amazon.com/Winter-2023-Tor-Title-Announced/dp/1250899656/|site=Amazon.com|text=''Tress of the Emerald Sea'' on Amazon|name=AmazonTress}} On March 3, 2022, Brandon released the first five chapters along with some brief commentary, which can be read [[bws:first-look-at-secret-project-1|on his website]] and listened to [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bBUVXcIE0E0 on his YouTube channel]. He also did a full spoiler Q&A about these preview chapters, which can be listened to [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wvXvV3rWsqg on his YouTube channel].

The box also included:
* [[Vin]] character pin (Cosmere Character Pin series 1, pin #1)

The free download for January was a Teacup Color Sheet.{{ref|name=Rewards}}
