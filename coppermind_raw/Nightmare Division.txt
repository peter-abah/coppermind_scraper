The '''Nightmare Division''' is the government agency in [[Nagadan]] responsible for defending the general population from [[nightmare]]s, through [[painter]]s and the [[Dreamwatch]].{{book ref|yumi|14}} They organize painters into departments, which are each assigned a specific shift.{{book ref|yumi|21}} A foreman is tasked with managing the painters in their locale, with responsibilities that include documenting encountered nightmares, disciplining staff who neglect their duties, and summoning the Dreamwatch.{{book ref|yumi|12}}{{book ref|yumi|14}}

The Nightmare Division has a headquarters in [[Kilahito]] in which Foreman [[Sukishi]]'s office is located.{{book ref|yumi|14}} The agency employs hundreds of painters in Kilahito alone.{{book ref|yumi|1}}
