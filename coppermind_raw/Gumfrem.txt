'''Gumfrems''' are domesticated animals on [[Roshar]]. They are described as pasture animals that are similar to [[chull]]s, although gumfrems are relatively uncommon. They are raised in [[Alethkar]] for their [[gemheart]]s, presumably heliodors, which are small but can be used to [[Soulcast]] meat.{{book ref|sa3|10}} 

Gumfrem farms existed in the [[Akanny]] region of the [[Sadeas princedom]].{{book ref|sa3|10}} Enough gumfrems to clog multiple rooms were taken to [[Urithiru]].{{book ref|sa3|10}}
