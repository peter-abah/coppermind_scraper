'''Star''' is [[Lunamor]] and [[Tuaka]]'s third son. Per his cultural traditions, he has the honor of being a craftsman.{{book ref|sa1|49}}{{book ref|sa3|37}}

Star arrived at the [[Shattered Plains]] with his family in {{Rosharan date|1174|1}} after traveling for months to meet with Lunamor, who sent them a message shortly after his arrival. Star's caravan was attacked by [[Voidbringer]]s and his family defended themselves behind a pile of furniture. During the attack a member of his family shot a Voidbringer with an [[Unkalaki]] arrow. They were found by [[Bridge Four]] during a patrol and taken to [[Urithiru]]. After settling in Urithiru, Star helped his father serve food and drink to Bridge Four.{{book ref|sa3|37}}{{book ref|sa3|55}}

Shortly after the evacuation of the residents of [[Hearthstone]], Star traveled back to the Peaks with most of his family, with just [[Cord]] and [[Gift]] staying behind.{{book ref|sa4|12}}
