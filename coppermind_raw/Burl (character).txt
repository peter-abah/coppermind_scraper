'''Burl''' is a [[bronze]] [[Misting]] (aka a [[Seeker]]) from [[Scadrial]] in the employ of [[Kelesina Shores]].{{book ref|mb6|15}} 

==History==
Burl was at or near Kelesina Shores's mansion during [[Kelesina Shores' party|her party]] in [[New Seran]] and was tasked with keeping watch for the use of metals.{{book ref|mb6|15}} During the party, [[Wax]] used a coin to [[Steel]]push his way onto a balcony in order to covertly follow Kelesina and eavesdrop on her.{{book ref|mb6|14}}  [[Kelesina Shores's steward|Kelesina's Terriswoman steward]] soon received a report from Burl that someone had used metals in the area.{{book ref|mb6|15}} Although Burl worked for Kelesina, it is not clear if he was aware of her association with the [[Set]].
