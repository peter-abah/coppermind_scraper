The '''Honored Dead''' are a collection of posthumously accoladed historical figures on [[Roshar]] who have contributed great things to society.{{book ref|sa2|i|2}} The term is only used by [[Ym]], and it is unclear if it is specific to the [[Iriali]] culture.

Ym believes that it is an important duty for every being in the [[cosmere]] to experience different parts of life. He therefore finds spectacles to be wondrous, as they allow him to see and experience things properly. Accordingly, he submitted a proposal that the [[Azish]] creator of spectacles be counted among the Honored Dead.{{book ref|sa2|i|2}}
