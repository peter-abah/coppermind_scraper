'''Kyndlip Ternavyl''' is a resident of [[Bilming]] on [[Scadrial]]. She is the owner and editor of ''[[The Two Seasons]]'' broadsheet.{{file ref|The Two Seasons broadsheet.jpg|''The Two Seasons'' broadsheet}}{{book ref|mb7|50}} She is of [[Terris]] heritage, giving her darker skin and kinky hair.{{book ref|mb7|50}}

At some point, Kyndlip married and had children.{{file ref|The Two Seasons broadsheet.jpg|''The Two Seasons'' broadsheet}} She either started ''The Two Seasons'' or became its proprietor some years prior to the [[Set]]'s endeavor to impress [[Autonomy]]; being sympathetic to [[Elendel]] interests, she sent staff to Elendel on at least one occasion and interviewed [[Marasi Colms]].{{book ref|mb7|50}} In {{date|348|PC}}, Kyndlip made a comment unfavorably comparing Bilming's mayor [[Gave Entrone]] to a boar.{{file ref|The Two Seasons broadsheet.jpg|''The Two Seasons'' broadsheet}} She was subsequently kidnapped by the Set, taken to the caverns underneath Bilming, and placed with other political dissidents.{{book ref|mb7|50}} This prompted her family to search for her, appealing to the mayor himself in their efforts, and to publish a notice about her disappearance in the ''Seasons''.{{file ref|The Two Seasons broadsheet.jpg|''The Two Seasons'' broadsheet}} While in captivity, Kyndlip took notes on the things she saw and heard. Giving her notebook to Marasi when she, [[Moonlight]], and [[TwinSoul]] discovered and freed the group of captives, Kyndlip urgently warned them about the existence of an army put together by the Set and directed them to find the [[Community]].{{book ref|mb7|50}} TwinSoul took the group of former captives and guided them back to Bilming.{{book ref|mb7|51}} It is likely that Kyndlip later gave evidence against Gave Entrone.{{book ref|mb7|epilogue|1}}
