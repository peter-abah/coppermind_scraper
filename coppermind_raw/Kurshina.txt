'''Kurshina''' is the former Empress of the [[Rose Empire]] on [[Sel]].{{tes ref|98}}

==Appearance and Personality==
Kurshina has red hair.{{tes ref|85}} Little else is known about her, but she is likely close in age to her husband, Emperor Ashravan, who is forty.{{tes ref|2}}

==History==
During an assassination attempt against Ashravan by the [[Glory Faction]], Kurshina was killed and Ashravan sustained a brain injury that put him in a vegetative state.{{tes ref|2}} Kurshina's death allowed the [[arbiter]]s from the [[Heritage Faction]] to keep Ashravan's condition secret from the empire, since tradition allowed him to have a mourning period of one hundred days.{{tes ref|2}} The arbiters publicly claimed that Ashravan had sequestered himself while he mourned, enlisting [[Shai]] to try to make an [[Essence Mark]] and fix Ashravan's mind before the end of the mourning period.{{tes ref|2}}

During Shai's detailed research into Ashravan's life, she discovered that Ashravan's first love from a storybook had red hair; Shai believed that this subconsciously drew him to Kurshina.{{tes ref|85}} She also discovered that Ashravan once nearly had an affair.{{tes ref|85}}

After Ashravan was stamped with Shai's Essence Mark and woke up, he remembered Kurshina's death. Shai could see that this caused him intense grief, although he hid it quickly.{{tes ref|98}} He later vowed to punish his wife's murderers.{{tes ref|101}}
