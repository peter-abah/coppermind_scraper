'''Kelin''' is a guard assigned to the commander of the [[Defiant Defense Force]]. She'd been assigned to guard Admiral [[Matthew Cobb|Cobb]] since his appointment. She is assigned to guard the door to Vice Admiral [[Stoff]]'s room. Late in the evening, [[Jorgen Weight|Jorgen]], [[Freyja Marten|FM]], and [[Rodge McCaffery|Rig]] wanted to meet with Stoff, and Kelin woke Cobb at their request. Jorgen outranked her, so she deferred to him. When Jorgen introduced the nature of their discussion, Stoff ordered Kelin to leave them, and she left down the hall.{{book ref|sf3|13}}
