'''Hawin''' is a young woman in [[Urithiru]] on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa4|23}} [[Kaladin]] refers to her as a "town girl"; she is likely one of the people that was evacuated from [[Hearthstone]] during the [[singer]] occupation. 

She worked in [[Lirin]]'s clinic in the tower and sometimes acted as Kaladin's assistant. She was able to read medical texts to Kaladin and perform other tasks such as fetching patients from the waiting room.{{book ref|sa4|23}}
