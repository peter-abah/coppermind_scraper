'''Slink''' is one of the eight members of [[Migs]]' gang in [[Elendel]].{{book ref|mb6|prologue}}

He is captured in a raid on the gang's hideout, the morning of [[Wax]]'s wedding. After the constabulary unsuccessfully attempted to convince the gang to surrender, Wax stormed the old pub while [[Wayne]] disguised himself as a fellow gang member, knocking out several thugs, including Slink.{{book ref|mb6|prologue}}

It is unknown whether Slink was executed for his crimes, though Waxillium advised the gang leader that those captured wouldn't be hung if he got his way.{{book ref|mb6|prologue}}
