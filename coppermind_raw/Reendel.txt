Lestrell '''Reendel''' is a [[sand mastery|sand master]] on [[Taldain]]. 

Reendel was present during the advancement ceremony prior to the [[Kerztian]] surprise attack, where he was advanced to the rank of lestrell. He is the son of the craftsman [[Keshdel]].{{book ref|ws|2|review=yes}} His survival following the attack by the Kerztians is unknown.
