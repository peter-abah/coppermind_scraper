'''Talinar''' is a town somewhere in [[Alethkar]] on [[Roshar]]. [[Teft]] grew up there.{{book ref|sa4|91}} 

Little is known about Talinar's location or geography. The town is small, too little to be called a city; it smelled of flowers back when Teft lived there.{{book ref|sa4|91}} There are cliffs in the area tall enough to kill someone who would fall off them.{{book ref|sa2|71}}

== History ==
Around the year 1129, a group of [[Envisagers]] visited the town and shared their ideas of gaining the powers of a [[Knight Radiant]] through exposing oneself to mortal danger with the locals. Soon, an Envisager cult formed in Talinar, dedicated to resurrecting the Radiant Orders.{{book ref|sa4|91}} Members of the cult would attempt suicide through leaping off cliffs, letting boulders fall on them and more, all in attempts to summon Radiant abilities. Eventually, following the suicide of his mother, [[Teft]] informed Talinar's citylord about the activities of the Envisagers. As a result, all surviving members of the cult were hanged.{{book ref|sa2|71}}

Teft later left Talinar to join the army, never to return. As such, its current state is unknown.{{book ref|sa2|71}}
