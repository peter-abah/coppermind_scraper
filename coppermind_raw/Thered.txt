'''Thered''' is a [[Derethi]] arteth on [[Sel]].{{book ref|elantris|21}}

==Appearance and Personality==

He is Fjorden, and had a strong and tall frame, with dark hair, along with squarish features. He is a muscular man from his training in the monastery.{{book ref|elantris|21}}

==History==
Thered was an arteth in [[Kae]] prior to the arrival of [[Hrathen]]. Hrathen was impressed with Thered's service, and offered him the position of head [[arteth]] in the chapel, but he refused the position. Hrathen was shocked, thinking no Derethi priest would refuse a position of such power, and demanded to know why Thered refused. Thered offered no explanation. Hrathen worried that [[Fjon]] had weakened the priests in Kae, but tossed that idea aside, instead suspecting that Thered refused the position out of respect for [[Dilaf]]'s growing influence within the chapel.{{book ref|elantris|21}}
