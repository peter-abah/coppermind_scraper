'''Galan''' is a soldier in Highmarshal [[Meridas Amaram]]'s army on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa1|47}}

In 1172, Galan told [[Kaladin]] about a new recruit who had joined the army named [[Cenn]].{{book ref|sa1|47}} This led Kaladin to bribe [[Gare]] so he could recruit Cenn into his squad and protect the young man.
