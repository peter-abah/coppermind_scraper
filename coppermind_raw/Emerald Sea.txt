The '''Emerald Sea''', also known as the '''Verdant Sea''', is an ocean on [[Lumar]].{{book ref|tress|5}}{{wob ref|15450}} The sea is made up of [[Aether spores#Types|Verdant spores]], which drop from the Verdant [[Lunagree]].{{book ref|tress|1}} Like all seas on Lumar, it is pentagonal in shape.{{book ref|tress|42}}

It borders the [[Crimson Sea]] and [[Rose Sea]],{{book ref|tress|10}}{{book ref|tress|37}} and three other unknown seas. It might border the [[Sapphire Sea]], as the Sapphire Sea is supposedly easy to access from the Emerald Sea, though this hasn't been confirmed.{{book ref|tress|23}}
== Government ==
It is ruled over, at least partially, by a figure known as the [[Verdant King]].{{book ref|tress|57}}

They have a royal navy which tracks down criminals such as [[deadrunners]].{{book ref|tress|14}} The government also has tax collectors and inspectors who have the authority to control the coming and going of ships and cargo through the sea.{{book ref|tress|2}}{{book ref|tress|6}}

Their government is known to recognize the [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Felony_murder_rule Felony murder rule], wherein, if somebody dies in the committing or furtherance of a dangerous or enumerated crime, the offender and their accomplices are held guilty of murder.{{book ref|tress|14}}
