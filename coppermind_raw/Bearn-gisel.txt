{{quote|Your people have a tradition, I believe. Bearn-gisel? Did I say that right?|Ulric to Ealstan{{book ref|fwh|7}}}}
'''Bearn-gisel''' is a tradition, likely across all of [[Weswara]], but at least within [[Stenford]]. It involves the taking of a hostage, in order to keep a local lord under control. If the lord stays in line, they may be allowed to visit the hostage.{{book ref|fwh|7}}

[[Ulric Stromfin]] refers to the tradition when talking with [[Ealstan]], just prior to kidnapping [[Wyrm]]. Ulric mistook Wyrm to be Ealstan's son, and took him [[Wellbury]] to be kept captive.{{book ref|frugal|7}}
