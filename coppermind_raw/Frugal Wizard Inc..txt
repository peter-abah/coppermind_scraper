'''Frugal Wizard Inc.''' is a company that sells access to alternate dimensions.{{frugal iw ref|Your Own Dimension}}

== History ==

Interdimensional travel was first discovered in 2084, with [[Cecil G. Bagsworth III]] as an early participant. Eventually, the technology was deregulated, which resulted in a new industry of dimensional tourism. Frugal Wizard Inc. obtained a band of medieval-type dimensions and began selling dimensions from that band to interested customers.{{frugal iw ref|Your Own Dimension}} Bagsworth wrote various customer-facing resources used by Frugal Wizard Inc., including ''[[The Frugal Wizard's Handbook for Surviving Medieval England (in-world)|The Frugal Wizard's Handbook for Surviving Medieval England]]''.
