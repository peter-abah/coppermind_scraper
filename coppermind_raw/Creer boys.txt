The '''Creer Boys''', '''Hanjah''' and '''the Mad Pen''', are twin [[Epic]]s who are members of the [[Coven]], along with [[Larcener]]. They also have matching powers, which are air pressure control, pain manipulation, and precognition. They aren't the only pair of twin Epics to have existed, but they are the only pair where one hasn't killed the other.{{book ref|Calamity|10}} Their weaknesses are unknown.

==Trivia==
* The Creer boys are likely cameos of [[Jeff Creer]]--a roommate of Brandon's who made art for his website and the original [[Elantris (book)|Elantris]] maps--and potentially Greg Creer, who was the first person who read Brandon's writing that was not himself.
