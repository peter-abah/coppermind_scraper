The '''gloomdancer''' is a mythological creature in [[Thaylen]]ah on [[Roshar]]. Modern Thaylens and Rosharan scholars generally consider them to be fictitious, alongside [[sea hag]]s and [[Sleepless|living heaps of cremlings]].{{book ref|sa3.5|9}} 

During the [[True Desolation]], there were increased reports of unexplainable events, causing [[Jasnah Kholin]] to reconsider the authenticity of some legends and folk tales. [[Vstim]] mentioned Jasnah's thoughts on [[Aimia]]ns and the gloomdancer to [[Rysn]] during a discussion of the unusual events that were occurring on the ''[[Wandersail (1174 ship)|Wandersail]]'' during the [[expedition to Akinah]].{{book ref|sa3.5|9}}
