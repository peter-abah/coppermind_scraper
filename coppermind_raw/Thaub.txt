The '''Thaub''' is a minor [[Epic]] who was killed in [[Ildithia]] during [[Prof]]'s fight with [[Tavi]]. The Thaub and [[Inshallah]] were both Epics from Bahrain that had linguistic abilities. The Thaub was either able to speak in any made-up language that anyone anywhere had ever imagined, or to force people to speak in rhyme.{{book ref|calamity|36}}

== Trivia ==
*A [[Wikipedia:thawb|thawb]] is a type of garment worn in some Arab countries, including Bahrain.
