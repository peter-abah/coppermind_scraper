The '''Stingray Clan''' is one of the "families" or gangs in [[Ildithia]].{{book ref|Calamity|21}}

[[Carla]] is the leader of the clan. She allows [[Tia]] to hide with them until [[Jonathan Phaedrus|Prof]] traces her to them.

They have sufficient clout to live in downtown Ildithia and receive invitations to the parties in Sharp Tower.{{book ref|Calamity|26}} They also provide two cupcake decorators to the party.
