A '''Sleepless interference machine''' is a device created by [[Malwish Empire|Malwish]] scholars. It is used to protect an area from [[Sleepless]]. It does this by creating an interference cloud, which makes a Sleepless' consciousness fall apart.{{book ref|iote|53}} It doesn't directly harm them however, and a Sleepless will be able to regain consciousness as soon as the cloud is turned off.{{book ref|iote|60}} It is said to work somewhat like a mindshield created by an [[Aviar]], albeit far more potent.{{book ref|iote|46}}

Additionally, an interference cloud has a number of other effects; like blocking a [[Dragon (cosmere)|dragon]]'s ability to contact others of their kind and interfering with all kinds of systems, such as FTL communication.{{book ref|iote|56}}{{book ref|iote|53}} A Sleepless interference machine generally assumes the form of a large bulb, with disks on its front.{{book ref|iote|53}} These machines are said to be fairly new as of the discovery of [[Patji's Eye]] by the Malwish.{{book ref|iote|55}}
