'''Hem''' is a barmaid at [[Jez's Duty]] in the [[Ten Rings]] section of [[Urithiru]] on [[Roshar]].

She is young, tall, and has unusually light hair.{{book ref|sa4|12}}

[[Shallan|Veil]] and [[Adolin]] thought Hem was attractive despite her unflattering work uniform. They pointed her out as a possible romantic fling for [[Kaladin]], who did not seem interested.{{book ref|sa4|12}}
