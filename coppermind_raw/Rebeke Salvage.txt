'''Rebeke Salvage''' is a [[Charred]] on [[Canticle]].{{book ref|tsm|7}} She is now a leader of the people of [[Beacon]].{{book ref|tsm|epilogue}}

== Appearance and Personality ==
Rebeke is a woman in her twenties-- while [[Nomad]] initially estimates her age as being in her early twenties,{{book ref|tsm|7}} she claims she is about a decade or so younger than Nomad,{{book ref|tsm|40}} who was thirty-eight [[Rosharan]] years old when he stopped aging, but he looks younger.{{book ref|tsm|11}}

She has black hair, which she wears in a braid, that has silver in it.{{ref|text=Either has some kind of silver material braided into it, as her hair is described as "woven with silver,"{{book ref|tsm|7}}, but it also said she looks similar to Elegy who's described as having "black hair, streaked with silver"{{book ref|tsm|3}} so one of these might be a metaphor)}}
She has light green eyes and pale skin, features she shares with [[Elegy]].{{book ref|tsm|7}}
Rebeke has a [[cinderheart]], but unlike other Charred, Rebeke's sits embedded at the surface of the skin, leaving her chest structurally intact, though with areas of ashen skin around it.{{book ref|tsm|50}}
{{expand}}

==Attributes and Abilities ==
Rebeke is a somewhat skilled pilot, able to pilot the [[Dawnchaser]] through the great maelstrom beyond sunset with just the instruments.{{book ref|tsm|34}}

Due to her cinderheart being siphoned off before fully implanting, she is in possession of a set of abilities unlike other Charred. She also does not have the cognitive effects of the cinderheart, meaning that she does not feel the rage, and maintains her memories.{{book ref|tsm|50}} Like the [[Cinder King]], Rebeke has the ability to control still corrupted Charred through their cinderhearts, though if there is another will pushing against hers, her control is limited.{{book ref|tsm|50}}{{book ref|tsm|epilogue}} It is unclear whether she has enhanced strength like other Charred, though the Cinder King does have enhanced strength from being [[Investiture|Invested]],{{book ref|tsm|2}} it is possible that Rebeke does as well.

==History==
Shortly before the arrival of Nomad, Rebeke embarked on a mission along with [[Divinity]] to rescue Elegy. Although Divinity died in the attempt, she was able to rescue Elegy and [[Thomos]], with the help of Nomad.{{book ref|tsm|7}} After returning to Beacon, she is assigned to be Nomad's babysitter by the [[Greater Good]].{{book ref|tsm|12}}. In her babysitting duty, she and Nomad, run across a scout from [[Union]], whom they run down{{book ref|tsm|14}}, though not before the scout got a call out to the Cinder King's forces. Despite his promise of no trickery, the Cinder King takes Rebeke hostage in an attempt to manipulate Nomad.{{book ref|tsm|16}} After being freed, more of the Cinder Kings forces attack Beacon, during which Rebeke pilots the ship that Nomad is on and helps him rescue the Greater Good.{{book ref|tsm|18}}{{expand}}
