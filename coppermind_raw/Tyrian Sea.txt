The '''Tyrian Sea''' is a body of water north of the [[Elendel Basin]] on [[Scadrial]].{{map ref|Elendel Basin}}

It is located in the Northern [[Roughs]], north of [[Covingtar]].{{map ref|Elendel Basin}} Its location is about where the [[Ashmount]] Tyrian stood during the [[Final Empire]] period of [[Scadrial]].{{map ref|Final Empire}} It is presumably the same body of water as [[Lake Tyrian]].
