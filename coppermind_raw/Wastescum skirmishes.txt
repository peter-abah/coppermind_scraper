The '''wastescum skirmishes''' are raids by [[Reshi]] on the northern coast of [[Alethkar]] that occurred early in the reign of [[Gavilar Kholin]].{{book ref|sa1|16}}

[[Jost]]'s father fought in them, and claims to have won a [[Shardblade]] though another man took the credit, and the Blade. As the Reshi are said to have never possessed Shardblades, this is likely a false claim.{{book ref|sa1|16}}
