'''Salavashi Trench''' is a body of water in the [[Sea of Lost Lights]] and the [[Sea of Oracles]].{{map ref|Sea of Lost Lights}}

The trench is the reflection of the [[Sunmaker Mountains]] in the [[Physical Realm]], hence, no rivers in the Sea of Lost Lights move past the Trench.{{map ref|Alethkar}} It appears to be named after the town [[Savalashi]] just south of the Sunmaker Mountains.{{map ref|Alethkar}}
