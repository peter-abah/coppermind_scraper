'''Hoid's Travails''' is a series of novels by [[Brandon Sanderson]]. Set in the [[cosmere]], each book recounts one of [[Hoid]]'s misadventures on lesser-known planets, narrated in his own voice.{{sots ref|2025}}

== Development ==
Hoid's stories were not originally conceived as a series. However, Brandon enjoyed writing both ''[[Tress of the Emerald Sea]]'' and ''[[Yumi and the Nightmare Painter]]'', and he realized that there was a lot of potential in recounting the terrible things that have happened to Hoid. When he was writing ''[[The Fires of December]]'', he decided to make "Hoid's Travails" into a series.{{sots ref|2025}}

==Books==
Brandon has noted that the books in this series are not numbered and can be read in any order.{{sots ref|2025}}
