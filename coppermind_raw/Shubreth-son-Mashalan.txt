'''Shubreth-son-Mashalan''' is a historical conqueror on [[Roshar]].

A young [[Gavilar]] references his rule along with the [[Sunmaker]] and the [[Hierocracy]] as examples of overstretching resources leading to collapse of empires soon after they're built.{{book ref|sa3|19}} Shubreth was likely a leader of [[Shinovar]] during the Shin invasions.{{book ref|sa3|2}}
