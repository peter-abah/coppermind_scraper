'''Flend''' is a [[Thaylen]] sailor on the ''[[Wandersail (1174_ship)|Wandersail]]''.{{book ref|sa3.5|4}}

He was a member of the [[expedition to Akinah]] and had the day shift in the [[Skyeel#Cultural References|eel's nest]]. [[Rysn]] encouraged [[Nikli]] to ask Flend about [[Spren#Sailorspren|sailorspren]] in order to fit in better with the crew.{{book ref|sa3.5|4}}
