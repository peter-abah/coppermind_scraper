'''Lamio''' is an ancient nation on [[Sel]].

Their artistic skill is lauded by the [[Heritage Faction]]; the corridors of the Imperial Wing of the [[Rose Palace]] are worked to appear as Lamio stonework, covered in reliefs, and are adorned with Lamio pottery.{{tes ref|2}} The [[Ashravan|Emperor]]'s bed is worked in an ancient Lamio style, carved in the shape of a rising phoenix.{{tes ref|2}}
