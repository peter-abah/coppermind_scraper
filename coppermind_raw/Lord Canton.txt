'''Lord Canton''' is a commander of unknown rank aboard the [[Stormwind]]. He assumed control of the ship after [[Kern|High Admiral Kern]] was killed when the Stormwind's main bridge was blown up. He was killed shortly after due to unknown circumstances, with the only evidence of his death being a shout over a communication device.{{book ref|Firstborn}}

[[Lord Haltep]] assumed command of the ship after he was killed.{{book ref|Firstborn}}
