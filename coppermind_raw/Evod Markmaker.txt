'''Evod Markmaker''' was a historical figure on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa1|51}}{{book ref|sa2|44}}

Evod was a [[darkeyes]] of legend that was known for killing a [[Shardbearer]] and claiming his [[Shardblade|Blade]].{{book ref|sa2|42}} There are many stories and songs about his deed. Evod is revered by darkeyes across Roshar, including [[Kaladin Stormblessed]] when he was younger.{{book ref|sa1|51}}{{book ref|sa2|44}}

Either Evod or [[Lanacin]] actually poisoned a Shardbearer to obtain their Shards, although this isn't well known.{{wob ref|15880}}
