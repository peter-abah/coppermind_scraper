'''Wzmal''' is a man mentioned in one of the records in the [[Urithiru gem archive]].{{epigraph ref|sa3|59}} It is likely due to his naming structure that he is [[Thaylen]].

His wife was planning to depart [[Urithiru]] and was given the opportunity to leave a message. She chose to leave a record of her family. A ruby was used to record the message, so she was likely a [[Dustbringer]].{{epigraph ref|sa3|59}}

She felt Wzmal was as good a man as any woman could dream of loving.{{epigraph ref|sa3|59}}
