'''Hazzel''' is a town in the mountains separating western [[Azir]] and western [[Yulay]] on [[Roshar]], and is north of the Azish settlement of [[Mazzu]]. It is unclear if Hazzel is under Azir's or Yulay's control, or is politically independent.{{map ref|Eastern Makabak}}
