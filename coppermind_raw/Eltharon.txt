'''Eltharon''' is a ''[[Metacha-im]]'' [[Fused]] on [[Roshar]]. He is a sadistic Fused; a self-styled “sculptor” whose favored materials are the body parts of [[Knight Radiant|Radiant]]s.{{rpg ref|ss|21}} He keeps these “sculptures” in a gallery inside a stormshelter in [[Kholinar]].{{rpg ref|ss|22}} He adorns himself with trinkets from his various kills.{{rpg ref|ss|23}} Eltharon also has many rivals among the Fused.{{rpg ref|ss|22}}

== History ==
During the [[True Desolation]], Eltharon was ordered to dismantle the resistance network in occupied [[Kholinar]]. He managed to capture [[Zoyah]], a [[Order of Windrunners|Windrunner]] spy,{{rpg ref|ss|21}} with a fake spanreed message. He then tortured her until she revealed [[Melur]]’s membership in the resistance.{{rpg ref|ss|22}} He used Zoyah’s code phrase to send a fake extraction request to Melur. Eltharon planned to let his targets gather at a hospital so he could kill them all at once. In the four weeks after capturing Zoyah, he caught many other resistance members.{{rpg ref|ss|21}} After Zoyah was rescued, she, along with the party that had rescued her, rushed back to the hospital. There, they found and fought Eltharon. It is unclear what happened afterwards.{{rpg ref|ss|22}}
