'''Marri''' is a lady's maid serving [[Shallan Davar]].{{book ref|sa2|77}}

She follows Shallan onto the [[Shattered Plains]] on the expedition that culminates in the [[Battle of Narak]].{{book ref|sa2|77}} Shallan would not allow Marri to ride in her coach because she did not want to explain [[Pattern]] to the girl. Whilst in [[Urithiru]], Shallan keeps sending Marri away to keep the secret of Veil.{{book ref|sa3|21}}
