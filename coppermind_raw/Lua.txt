'''Lua''' is one of [[Stephen Leeds]]' [[aspect]]s.

He is a Samoan survivalist who is described as a beefy man with a big smile. He weighs about three hundred pounds. In the [[White Room]] he would start conversations with whoever was closest.{{book ref|skin deep|8}}

Lua builds a "stone age" house in his spare time, using only technology he could build by himself. When [[Jenny]] manifests and Stephen begins losing aspects, he is working on the hearth of the house.{{book ref|legion3|2}} He also runs or helps run a Cub Scout troop.{{book ref|legion3|4}}

While Stephen is chasing after [[Sandra]], Lua left the house to grab something from his survival hut, but he disappeared.{{book ref|legion3|9}} He then showed up to help Stephen escape the survival-island simulation. Claiming that he had broken too many rules by teleporting and constructing a catamaran instantaneously, he vanishes without becoming a nightmare.{{book ref|legion3|12}}
