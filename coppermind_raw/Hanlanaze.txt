'''Professor Hanlanaze''' is a well known mathematician and scientist from [[New Seran]] on [[Scadrial]].

He grew up in the outer village of [[Thermoli]], located west of New Seran at the southern edge of the [[Elendel Basin]],{{ref|group=fn|text=The village is only labelled in an annotation by [[Nazh]] on the version of the [[:File:{{map|Elendel Basin}}|Elendel Basin map]] in ''[[Shadows of Self]]''.}} and his accent includes the distinctive "v" sounds common in that village. He has a beard, is fairly heavyset, and is at least part [[Terris]].{{book ref|mb5|9}}

He is known for contributing to several new discoveries related to combustion technology. He maintains correspondence with other scientists, including [[Rame Maldor]], and is familiar with recent research in the field of electromagnetism.{{book ref|mb5|9}} [[Sophi Tarcsel]] claims that Hanlanaze stole [[Remmingtel Tarcsel|her father's]] research on incandescent lights and falsely took credit for the designs that gained widespread use.{{book ref|mb5|10}}

In {{date|342|PC}}, he was invited to the governor's spring dinner and policy speech at [[Lady ZoBell]]'s penthouse in [[Elendel]], but was a known recluse and replied that he might not make it to the party. Despite a note on the name manifest not to let anyone else in using his ticket, [[Wayne]] successfully impersonated him to sneak into the party.{{book ref|mb5|9}}
