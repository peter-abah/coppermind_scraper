'''Dilisteni''' was an author during the [[Final Empire]] on [[Scadrial]].{{book ref|mb1|12}}

He wrote the book ''Trials of Monument'', which [[Elend Venture]] read at the ball when he first met [[Vin]].{{book ref|mb1|12}} The book was quite famous in the Final Empire, and there was a copy in the Keep Orielle's library in [[Fadrex City]], which Elend borrowed when he and Vin attended a ball at the keep while they had the city under siege.{{book ref|mb3|32}}
