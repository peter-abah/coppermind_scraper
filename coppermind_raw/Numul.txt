The '''numul''' is a type of crustacean on [[Roshar]]. Although less commonly seen than the larger [[chull]]s, they have been used by humans for millenia.{{book ref|sa5|62}} 

==Appearance and Anatomy==
They are described as midsized shellbeasts. They have cloudy, inch-long [[gemheart]]s that are covered in sinew. The gemheart quality is considered inferior to those of some other crustaceans.{{book ref|sa5|62}}

==Behavior==
They live in herds, occasionally migrating. In ancient Roshar large herds could be found near present-day [[Azir]]. In modern Roshar the numul's range is unclear, but they can be found in the west of the continent. [[Dalinar Kholin]] has only "occasionally" seen them in the wild, implying that they are relatively uncommon.{{book ref|sa5|62}} 

==Use==
===Food===
Numul meat is edible. Early Rosharan humans hunted numul,{{book ref|sa5|62}} although their meat is not mentioned as a food source in modern Roshar.

===Domestication===
Like the [[chull]], the numul can be domesticated and raised as livestock. The [[Azish]] ranch them in great quantities, as numul gemhearts have value (despite their low quality) since they can be used to [[Soulcast]] [[bronze]].{{book ref|sa5|62}}

==History==
During [[Dalinar]]'s journey in the [[Spiritual Realm]], he visited a vision that took place in a warcamp on the day of the [[Oathpact]]. He observed men butchering a large numul, and speculated that early armies provisioned themselves by following the herds. He was surprised to see the men ignoring the gemheart, and realized that they did not know what it was.{{book ref|sa5|62}}
