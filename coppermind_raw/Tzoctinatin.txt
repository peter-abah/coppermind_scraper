'''Tzoctinatin''' is a [[Dragon (Alcatraz)|Dragon]] living in [[Nalhalla]].{{book ref|a3|3}}

== Appearance and Personality ==
Tzoctinatin is a large scaly creature. He resents his sentence but does not speak much. His eyes have a deep vastness to them, with many colors and folds.{{book ref|a3|3}}

Due to his sentence he has had his wings clipped and travels around by climbing buildings instead of flying.{{book ref|a3|3}}

He carries a box on his back for passengers made from [[Orientation Glass]] which makes the occupants' gravity oriented in the same direction, towards the dragon's feet, at all times. It is well furnished and has comfortable couches.{{book ref|a3|3}}

== History ==
He was charged with first-degree maiden munching, had his wings clipped, and was sentenced to taxi service for 700 years.{{book ref|a3|3}}{{book ref|a4|4 ½}}

When he gave [[Alcatraz Smedry|Alcatraz]] and his [[Leavenworth Smedry|grandfather]] a ride he had 300 years of his sentence remaining.{{book ref|a3|3}}
