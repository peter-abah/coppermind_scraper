The '''Machineborn''' are the native sentient life in the [[State]]s of the [[Wode]]. They are artificial intelligence "[[Wikipedia:Non-player character|NPCs]]" used to fill out the population of each [[Liveborn]]'s state. They cannot leave their home state.

All Machineborn have a '''Concept''', the central premise that describes their character, which can be hacked by both the Wode and Liveborn.{{book ref|ps|1}} Examples of Concepts include chancellor [[Besk]]'s cleverness, [[Shale]]'s insistence on wearing armor,{{book ref|ps|1}} and Chief [[Letmere]]'s pragmatism.{{book ref|ps|4}}
