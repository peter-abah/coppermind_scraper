'''Palafruit''' are a type of fruit found on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa1|27}} They have pits, and they are eaten by biting into them.{{book ref|sa1|27}}{{book ref|sa5|21}} Palafruit are juicy and can stain clothes.{{book ref|sa4|22}}{{book ref|sa5|21}} At least some of them are small and green. A dozen palafruit is considered a large quantity to eat at once.{{book ref|sa5|21}}

Pits from the fruit were occasionally thrown at bridgemen by soldiers from the [[Alethi]] army during the [[War of Reckoning]].{{book ref|sa1|27}} [[Adolin Kholin]] fed [[Gallant]] a palafruit before departing on the expedition to [[Lasting Integrity]], and he gave him another one later in the journey.{{book ref|sa4|21}}{{book ref|sa4|22}} [[Wit]] brought palafruit with him when he joined [[Dalinar]], [[Navani]], [[Fen]], and [[Jasnah]] in their discussion about Dalinar seeking the power of [[Honor]] prior to the [[contest of champions]]. Once [[Lift]] was discovered eavesdropping, she helped herself to the snacks and ended up eating at least a dozen palafruit.{{book ref|sa5|21}}
