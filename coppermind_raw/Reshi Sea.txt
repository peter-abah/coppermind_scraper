The '''Reshi Sea''' is a body of water in northern [[Roshar]]. It is the largest known sea on Roshar and it covers a significant portion of the middle of the continent. The southern end of the sea is separated from the [[Purelake]] by [[Marabethia]], while the [[Reshi Isles]] archipelago lies at the northern end of the sea, adjacent to the [[Steamwater Ocean]]. Other countries along the sea's coastline include [[Iri]], [[Rira (country)|Rira]], [[Babatharnam]], [[Tu Bayla]], [[Jah Keved]], and [[Herdaz]].{{map ref|Roshar}} During the [[Heraldic Epochs]], the sea was in the territory of [[Rishir]].{{map ref|Silver Kingdoms}}

In addition to the various islands in the Reshi Sea, it is also home to massive inhabited [[greatshell]]s known as [[Tai-na]]. Although the sea is deeper than the Purelake, it is shallow enough to allow the Tai-na to walk along the seafloor.{{book ref|sa2|i|3}} At times, the mist on the Reshi Sea grows so dense, it’s becomes difficult to see anything.{{rpg ref|slwg|64}}

The area has a hot and humid tropical climate, and it does not snow.{{book ref|sa1|i|8}}{{book ref|sa3.5|3}}{{book ref|sa4|50}} The waters of the sea are usually quite calm,{{book ref|sa2|i|3}} although the region is presumably in the path of [[highstorm]]s. The seawater is quite warm most of the time.{{book ref|sa1|i|8}}{{book ref|sa2|i|3}}

== Inhabitants ==
{{for|Reshi Isles}}
Many of the islands in the Reshi Sea are known to be inhabited by the native people, known as the Reshi.{{book ref|sa2|i|3}} Various Tai-na contain self-contained kingdoms that also attract foreigners seeking a simple life.{{book ref|sa2|i|3}} The [[ardent]]s [[Ashir]] and [[Geranid]] live on a small island where they can focus on scientific experiments.{{book ref|sa1|i|8}} The [[Herdaz]]ian coastline is known to be inhabited,{{book ref|sa3.5|3}} and it is [[Toh]]'s last known location.{{book ref|sa3|66}}
