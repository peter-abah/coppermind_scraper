'''Dyel''' is an Iriali teenager alive during the migration to the [[Iriali#History|fifth land]]. She helps her [[Dyel's mother|mother]], a [[Knights Radiant|Radiant]], run their shop, where they take in visitors, several of them [[worldhoppers]].{{book ref|sa5|i|11}}

==Appearance and Personality==
Dyel has the golden skin and hair typical of Iriali. She wears her hair long, considering it part of her heritage. In Iriali fashion, she usually wears a bandeau for a top.{{book ref|sa5|i|11}}

==History==
A year and a month before the migration to the fifth land, respectively, Dyel and her mother met [[Hoid]] and a strange woman with [[feruchemy|many rings]]. {{book ref|sa5|i|11}}

Just before the migration, [[Demoux]], [[Baon]], and [[Galladon]] were visitors to her shop. When they asked her if she had seen a [[mistspren]], she assumed Baon was the herald [[Nale]], returned to kill her mother as he had killed her grandfather [[Ym]], another budding radiant. After her mother returned, her spren [[Uma]] cleared up the miscommunication. The family delivered a note from Hoid to the three men. Immiediately after they left, [[Cusicesh|Cusicesh the Protector]] rose out of the bay and announced the migration to the fifth land. Dyel, her mother, and Uma made their way into [[Shadesmar]] to begin their journey with the rest of the Iriali.{{book ref|sa5|i|11}}

==Relationships==
===Ym===
Dyel loves and misses her grandfather Ym. She expresses that she believes in the religion of the One because it brings her closer to him.{{book ref|sa5|i|11}}
