'''Wights''' (sometimes also called ''landswights'') are unseen magical entities in medieval England. To most people, the wights are simply invisible, but [[skop]]s notice them from shadows in the corners of their vision.{{book ref|frugal|24}} A wight will serve the people if bound to a [[runestone]] and given offerings, but otherwise it may become a destructive bog.{{book ref|frugal|13}}
