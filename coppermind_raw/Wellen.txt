'''Wellen''', also known as '''Wells''',{{book ref|mb3|part=ars}} is a soldier on [[Scadrial]].

He was a member of [[Ashweather Cett]]'s army that attacked [[Luthadel]].{{book ref|mb2|43}} He was on ''mist duty'' with [[Jarloux]] when [[Vin]] and [[Zane Venture]] attacked [[House Hasting#Keep|Keep Hasting]]. He was the sole survivor of his squad of forty men.{{book ref|mb2|43}}

After Cett swore allegiance to Emperor [[Elend Venture]], he was reemployed as a guard in Luthadel.{{wob ref|7803}} He and [[Rittle]] met with [[TenSoon]] playing [[Kelsier]], and told him of the madness afflicting King [[Ferson Penrod]]. When TenSoon asks after the whereabouts of Elend and Vin, Wellen instead relays the location of Lord [[Breeze]], in [[Urteau]]. TenSoon leaves them with the advice to find shelter from the mists in the [[Arguois caverns]].{{book ref|mb3|52}} 
Elend questions him and Rittle in the refugee camp after having led the people out of Luthadel.{{book ref|mb3|80}}

Wellen survives the [[Catacendre]] and has children.{{wob ref|16110}}

== Personality ==
Wellen is superstitious of the mists, though he keeps his opinions to himself while on mist duty to avoid ridicule from the other guards.{{book ref|mb2|42}}

== Trivia ==
* Wellen is based on [[Dan Wells]], a friend of Brandon's who is also an author.{{wob ref|7803}}
