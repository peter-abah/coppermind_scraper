'''Hathan''' is a [[master-servant]] and [[Dalinar Kholin]]'s room steward in [[Kholinar]] on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa3|36}}

==Appearance and Personality==
He is short.{{book ref|sa3|36}} As a master-servant, he is presumably [[darkeyes|darkeyed]] and wears a uniform.

==History==
Hathan served as Dalinar and [[Evi]]'s room steward a few years after their marriage, when Evi was pregnant with [[Adolin]]. In {{Rosharan date|1150|missing-date=y}}, when Gavilar came to confront Dalinar about whether he had killed the younger [[Tanalan]], Hathan answered the door and announced Gavilar's arrival to Dalinar.{{book ref|sa3|36}}
