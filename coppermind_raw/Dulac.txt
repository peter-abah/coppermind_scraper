'''Dulac''' is a [[Scadrian]] [[worldhop]]per bonded to the [[cryptic]] [[Cluster]].{{rpg ref|cfm1|14-15}}

==Attributes and Abilities==
Having bonded a cryptic, Dulac has access to the [[surges]] of [[Illumination]] and [[Transformation]]. They use these to good effect in their line of work to sneak into restricted areas and keep themselves well provisioned while traveling through [[Shadesmar]].

== History ==
Dulac was commissioned by anonymous patrons to produce a travelogue about [[Roshar]]. They arrived a few years before [[Vorin|1174]]{{rpg ref|cfm1|14-15}} and during their time visited [[Azimir]],{{rpg ref|slwg|38-39}} [[Herdaz]],{{rpg ref|slwg|45}} [[Vedenar]],{{rpg ref|slwg|54-55}} [[Thaylen City]],{{rpg ref|slwg|72-73}} occupied [[Kholinar]],{{rpg ref|slwg|153}} [[Rall Elorim]],{{rpg ref|slwg|156-157}} occupied [[Narak]],{{rpg ref|slwg|159}} the [[Alethi]] [[warcamp]]s,{{rpg ref|slwg|167}} and [[Celebrant]].{{rpg ref|slwg|174}} By [[Catacendre|349 PC]] they were back on Scadrial.{{rpg ref|cfm2|28-29}}
