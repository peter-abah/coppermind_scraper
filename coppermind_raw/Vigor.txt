Callsign '''Vigor''' is a former cadet in the [[DDF]] [[Flight School]] who was kicked out before graduation, after she was shot down and ejected from her aircraft.{{book ref|Skyward|32}}

She is in her early twenties, with a prominent scar across her forehead.{{book ref|Skyward|32}}

She was placed in the reserve, and was drafted to fly in [[Spensa]]'s place during the battle in which [[Hurl]] died. Spensa arrived before she could take off, and reluctantly, Vigor relinquished the [[Poco]] to Spensa.{{book ref|sky1|32}}
