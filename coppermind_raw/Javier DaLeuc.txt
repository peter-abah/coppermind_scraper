'''Javier DaLeuc''' is an actor on [[Scadrial]].{{file ref|New Ascendancy broadsheet.jpg|''The New Ascendancy'' broadsheet}}

In Cladence and the following month of {{date|342|PC}}, he starred in a run of ''[[The Den of the Survivor]]'', a play about the overthrow of the [[Final Empire]] that was being shown at the [[Uptown Trio Theater]] in [[New Seran]]. Along with his co-star, [[Penelope Portreau]], he was mentioned in an advertisement for the performances in ''[[The New Ascendancy]]''.{{file ref|New Ascendancy broadsheet.jpg|''The New Ascendancy'' broadsheet}}
