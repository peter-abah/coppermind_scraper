'''Ruen''' is an [[Eelakin]] scientist on [[First of the Sun]].{{book ref|iote|17}} He is head of scientific research on the island of [[Patji (island)|Patji]]. Ruen takes the appearence of a short and bald man, with a pointed nose. Like other [[homeisles|homeislers]], Ruen talks a lot, at least compared to the solitary [[trapper]]s. Despite having spent a lot of time near the [[Patji's Eye|Drominad perpendicularity]], Ruen still manages to get a mouthful of water every time he transfers. He seems to find this funny however.{{book ref|iote|15}}

He has attempted to recreate [[Sak]]’s talent, feeding [[glowing worm]]s to other ravens, but came to the conclusion that the bird was unique.{{book ref|iote|15}} Ruen also thinks that [[Cognitive Realm|Shadesmar]] must be the endless darkness mentioned in the old stories, and has done plenty of research in this realm.{{book ref|iote|17}} Ruen is bonded to a tall [[Aviar]], who has a large crest upon its head.{{book ref|iote|15}}
