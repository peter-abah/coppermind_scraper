'''Kelathar''' is a city in [[Alethkar]] in the [[Ruthar princedom]] on [[Roshar]].{{map ref|Alethkar}}

It is located near the source of a major tributary of the [[Windrunner River]], probably in the northern foothills of the [[Sunmaker Mountains]].{{map ref|Alethkar}}
