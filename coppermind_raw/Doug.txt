'''Doug''' is a crew member of the ''[[Crow's Song]]'' on [[Lumar]].{{book ref|tress|12}} Unlike other minor crew members that Hoid names 'Doug' for simplicity, Doug is her actual name.

At the time the ''Crow's Song'' attacks the ''Oot's Dream'', Doug is sewing a pirate flag for the ship.{{book ref|tress|12}} She later raises the flag at [[Captain Crow]]'s command when the ''Crow's Song'' attacks a merchant ship, resulting in their surrender.{{book ref|tress|25}}
