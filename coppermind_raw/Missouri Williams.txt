'''Missouri Williams''', known as '''Mizzy''', is a member of the [[Reckoners]].

She is based with [[Valentine]] and [[Exel]] in [[Babilar]].{{book ref|Firefight|9}} She is dark-skinned and slight of build, and wears her hair back in a way that [[David]] describes as an Afro mushroom cloud.{{book ref|Calamity|9}} Mizzy has a bubbly and optimistic view on life, and is very trusting, but still desperately wants to kill [[Megan Tarash|Megan]], who had previously killed [[Sam (Reckoner)|Sam]], Mizzy's mentor.{{book ref|Firefight|20}} However, David helps her focus this thirst for vengeance towards [[Calamity (Epic) |Calamity]], the true source of the problem.{{book ref|Calamity|11}} Mizzy is the sniper for her team, and has experience with explosives, equipment, and repairs, but wants to be point.{{book ref|Firefight|9}}{{book ref|Firefight|15}} It is stated by Megan and David that after Calamity is defeated, Mizzy becomes an [[Epic]], although they are not certain how she will take to being one.{{book ref|calamity|epilogue}}
