Mistress '''Halex''' is a [[brass]] [[Misting]] (aka a [[Soother]]) and the proprietor of a [[Soothing parlor]] on Decan street in [[Elendel]] on [[Scadrial]].{{book ref|mb5|14}}{{book ref|mb5|15}}{{file ref|Elendel Daily broadsheet.png|''The Elendel Daily'' Broadsheet}}

She wears a matronly gown and a hat that is likely lined with [[aluminum]]. She is unruffled by the presence of a constable in her establishment.{{book ref|mb5|15}}

Mistress Halex opened her Soothing parlor in 341 [[catacendre|PC]].{{file ref|Elendel Daily broadsheet.png|''The Elendel Daily'' Broadsheet}} In 342, [[Chapaou]] fled to the parlor after his disturbing encounter with [[Bleeder]] and paid for a private session.{{book ref|mb5|15}} The [[Coinshot]] that also worked for [[Ashweather Carriage and Coach]] knew where he was and directed [[Waxillium Ladrian]] to the parlor.{{book ref|mb5|14}} Mistress Halex greeted Wax and, after seeing his credentials, took him back to Chapaou's room. She urged Wax to take Chapaou with him, as he was upsetting the other customers.{{book ref|mb5|15}}
