The '''''Oot's Dream''''' is a boat that travels the twelve seas on [[Lumar]]. It is a vessel used by smugglers pretending to be merchants, who forged writs from the king to avoid paying taxes.{{book ref|tress|9}} 

== Appearance ==
The ''Oot's Dream'' is a small smuggling vessel disguised as a merchant ship. It has a single, small cannon on the foredeck.{{book ref|tress|11}} The ship has two masts, and is described best as being similar to a [[Wikipedia:brigantine|brigantine]].{{book ref|tress|12}}

== History ==
[[Tress]] snuck aboard the ''Oot's Dream'' while imitating a Royal Inspector, and consequently spooked the crew and captain, who believed she was onto them and poisoned her so they could capture her.{{book ref|tress|8}} She awoke and met [[Huck]] the rat who was also held captive. Soon after, the ship sank after being attacked by the ''[[Crow's Song]]'', which used cannonballs with sabotaged timers, designed to sink instead of incapacitating the ship. [[Tress]] and [[Huck]] were the only survivors.{{book ref|tress|12}}{{book ref|tress|19}} Although the captain of the ''Crow's Song'' left another man alive on a lifeboat, he died before he could fulfill Captain [[Crow]]'s plan of making known which ship sank the ''Oot's Dream''.{{book ref|tress|19}}
