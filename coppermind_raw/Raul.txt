Raul is a [[Liveborn]] that plays in the game of paintball on [[Maltese]]. He and some other men from his faction within the game have set up a trap for [[Jasmine]] and another woman from her faction. After shooting Jasmine's ally, they are interrupted by [[Kairominas|Kai]], who is trying to rescue the women. Raul's hand gets broken by Kai attacking him with a metal rod.

Raul attempts to convince the [[Wode]] to count Kai's kill on Jasmine as a hit for his faction.{{book ref|ps|2}}
