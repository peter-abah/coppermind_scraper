'''Navigation''' is a manifestation of [[Investiture]] on [[First of the Sun]] in the [[Drominad System]]. A '''Navigator''' is a person who has the ability to sense the [[Current]] of the [[Grand Knell]] and use it to navigate the [[Cognitive Realm]].{{book ref|iote|33}}

== Prerequisites ==
{{quote
|Anyone? Do I bless anyone? Only those who are tested. And who survive.
|[[Patji (being)|Patji]] and [[Dusk]]{{book ref|iote|33}}
}}
Navigation is unique to the people of [[First of the Sun]].{{book ref|iote|52}}{{book ref|iote|epilogue}} The [[Initiation]] for this ability is not fully understood, but it is only known to affect [[trapper]]s who have survived working in the [[Pantheon]] and spent time near [[Patji's Eye]].{{book ref|iote|33}}{{book ref|iote|57}}{{book ref|iote|epilogue}} [[Patji (being)|Patji]] seems to have an active role in determining whether someone should be granted abilities.{{book ref|iote|33}} Some trappers cannot gain the ability to Navigate, and [[Sixth of the Dusk]] wonders if they could instead gain another unknown ability.{{book ref|iote|epilogue}} 

Navigators need to hold [[Investiture]] in order to Navigate; this is typically accomplished by ingesting [[Luminist]].{{book ref|iote|50}}{{book ref|iote|52}} The more Investiture the user has, the more powerful their sensing is.{{book ref|iote|33}} The ability to Navigate is constant until the user's Investiture runs out.{{book ref|iote|45}} 

It is unknown if Navigation is a manifestation of Patji's own native Investiture or a manifestation of [[Autonomy]] through Patji.

== Abilities ==
Navigators are able to sense pulses in the Cognitive Realm from the Grand Knell.{{book ref|iote|45}} Unlike a [[Seeker]], who can only get a bearing to the Grand Knell, a Navigator can sense subastral obstructions to the Current. Navigators can locate these disturbances from a great distance away, and can innately understand what is causing them.{{book ref|iote|45}}{{book ref|iote|57}} This is similar to how wayfinders use waves to navigate in the [[Physical Realm]], but with an exact opposite reaction, as disturbances add to the strength of the pulse rather than subtract.{{book ref|iote|33}}{{book ref|iote|34}} Navigators are able to sense things both in the unsea (the "surface" of the [[Cognitive_Realm#Emberdark|emberdark]]) and in the air.{{book ref|iote|34}}{{book ref|iote|52}} They are able to release a sound similar to a tuning fork by hitting their chest, which acts like a sonar.{{book ref|iote|52}} 

Navigators can use their abilities to chart an accurate course through the emberdark, in a way that no other known magic or technology can replicate.{{book ref|iote|57}} They can also share their ability to hear the pulses with a bonded [[Aviar]]. The bird does not need to hold its own Investiture.{{book ref|iote|33}}
