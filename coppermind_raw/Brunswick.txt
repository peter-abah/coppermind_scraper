'''Brunswick''' is the steward for the [[duke]] of [[Diggen's Point]] on [[Lumar]].{{book ref|tress|5}}

== History ==

Three days after finding out that [[Charlie]] had been captured by the [[Sorceress]], [[Tress]] went to Brunswick with a plea for more information.{{book ref|tress|5}} Because Brunswick liked Tress's pies, he told her that a ransom letter had been received from the sorceress, but that the duke believed this to be a trick and the king had officially declared Charlie as dead.{{book ref|tress|5}}
