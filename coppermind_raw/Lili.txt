'''Lili''' is a servant of the dragon [[Xisisrefliel]] at the bottom of the [[Crimson Sea]] on [[Lumar]]. They used to be the floor scrubber for Xisisrefliel; however, they were promoted from the position shortly before [[Crow]]'s arrival, and Crow succeeded them in the position.{{book ref|totes|52}}
