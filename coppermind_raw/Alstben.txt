'''Alstben''' is a [[Thaylen]] sailor on the ''[[Wandersail (1174_ship)|Wandersail]]''. He is a tall man and likes to spike his eyebrows.{{book ref|sa3.5|8}}

He was a crew member of the ''Wandersail'' for the [[expedition to Akinah]]. When the ship encountered what appeared to be a dead [[santhid]], Alstben took it as a bad omen and wanted to abort the mission.{{book ref|sa3.5|8}}
