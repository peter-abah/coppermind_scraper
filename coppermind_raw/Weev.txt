'''Weev''' is an officer on the ''[[Crow's Song]]'', a ship that sails the planet [[Lumar]]. He serves as the ship's [[sprouter]] before being murdered by [[Crow]] for opposing her desire to make the crew pirates.{{book ref|totes|16}}

== Appearance and Personality ==

A crewmember ([[Crow's Song#Dougs|Doug]]) from the ''Crow's Song'' describes Weev as a nice guy who was terrible at bluffing and playing cards.{{book ref|totes|16}} He was the only one to argue against piracy, and didn't deserve to get murdered.{{book ref|totes|16}} [[Hoid]] acknowledges that Weev was a cunning fellow up until he argued with Crow.{{book ref|totes|22}}

Weev is a sprouter and often uses and experiments with [[aether spores]]. All sprouters are viewed as crazy, but Weev is crazier than the rest.{{book ref|totes|25}}

Before he died, Weev likely did the majority of the cooking for the crew of the ''Crow's Song''.{{book ref|totes|37}}

== History ==

Weev had a room on the ''Crow's Song'' with a bed, mirror, and running water, but no silver to allow for experiments with spores. Though it was larger than other officer's rooms,{{book ref|totes|45}} it remained unclaimed until [[Tress]] took his job as a sprouter.{{book ref|totes|22}} While he did use the running water for spore experiments, it was also an excuse to take baths at his leisure.{{book ref|totes|22}}

He collected several different types of spores, including zephyr, rose, verdant, sunlight, and midnight spores.{{book ref|totes|22}}{{book ref|totes|25}} He acquired the midnight spores from Hoid.{{book ref|totes|28}} In his profession, Weev was considered a purist and preferred classical tools over modern ones.{{book ref|totes|26}} His tools for patching holes in the ship included a small iron plate and a steel trowel.{{book ref|totes|26}} Over the course of his experiments, he compiled a considerable collection of notes detailing his results and knowledge, but they were not very organized.{{book ref|totes|29}}{{book ref|totes|37}} He was a mental hoarder, and had a tendency to write down random thoughts and half-finished ideas.{{book ref|totes|37}} Crow wanted him to figure out how to make cannonballs himself to save money, but he never succeeded.{{book ref|totes|37}}

During Crow's quest to heal herself, she had secret meetings with Weev to help her locate [[Xisisrefliel]].{{book ref|totes|31}} According to Crow's notes, these ended when he turned to blackmail and Crow threw him off the ship.{{book ref|totes|31}}
