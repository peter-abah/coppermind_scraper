'''Screech''' is a pet [[skyeel]] kept by the crew of the ''[[Wandersail (1174_ship)|Wandersail]]''.{{book ref|sa3.5|10}} Screech was well-liked by the crew and hunted rats onboard for them. During the voyage to [[Akinah]], Screech was killed by [[Nikliasorm|Nikli]] in one of his attempts to frighten the crew into turning back by creating bad omens.{{book ref|sa3.5|4}}{{book ref|sa3.5|6}}{{book ref|sa3.5|16}} Screech's death indeed gave the crew pause due to their superstitions stemming from the [[Passions]], but they pressed on since their orders came directly from Queen [[Fen]].{{book ref|sa3.5|4}} 

Nikli used [[blackbane]] to kill Screech. He later tried to frame [[Cord]] for the skyeel's death, but Rysn figured out that he was the true culprit.{{book ref|sa3.5|10}}
