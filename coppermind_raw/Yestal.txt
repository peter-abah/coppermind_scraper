Lord '''Yestal''' is a [[noble]] in [[Luthadel]] on [[Scadrial]].{{book ref|mb1|30}}

He regularly attended balls held by the nobility in Luthadel. He initially welcomed [[Valette Renoux]], and danced with her on at least one occasion.

He was among the nobles that suddenly became cold towards Valette during a ball at [[Keep Venture]], ignoring her when she approached his group before excusing himself and [[Triss]] for their meal. Valette suspected that the change in attitude may have been related to her confrontation with [[Shan Elariel]]. However, [[Sazed]] pointed out that the ball was likely to be the last one for a long while, and nobles such as Yestal did not want to waste time politicking with neutral parties such as Valette.{{book ref|mb1|30}}
