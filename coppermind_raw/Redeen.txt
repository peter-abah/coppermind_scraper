'''Lord Redeen''' is a nobleman on [[Sel]].

He attends a party at Duke [[Roial]]'s mansion in [[Arelon]], and falls into a pond due to excess drink.{{book ref|Elantris|32}} Roial asks [[Opa]] to ensure that Redeen is provided with some towels and a ride home.{{book ref|Elantris|32}}

== Trivia ==
* Redeen was known as "Redeem" in the original published version of ''Elantris'', but his name was changed in the tenth anniversary edition.
