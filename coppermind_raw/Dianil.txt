'''Dianil''' is a femalen [[listener]] on [[Roshar]] who is one of the first to successfully adopt [[warform]].{{book ref|sa4|68}}

== Appearance and Personality ==
As a result of adopting warform, Dianil's voice becomes deeper. She has a distinctive curl of black marbling along her brow.{{book ref|sa4|68}}

== History ==
Dianil was one of the volunteers to whom [[Venli]] provided gemstones in order to test out adopting the new warform. She was one of the dozen who were successful in doing so. When Dianil stood in the [[highstorm]] for the experiment, she was overwhelmed by the need to bask in the tone of warform. Immediately afterwards, the group of new warforms heard the battle drums that meant that another [[Listener#Families|family]] had come to raid their city. They met up with [[Sharefel]] and went with him to confront them. After [[Eshonai]] intimidated the family and then asked them join forces, the group of warforms went to train.{{book ref|sa4|68}}
