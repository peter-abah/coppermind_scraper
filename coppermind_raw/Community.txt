'''The Community''' is a project designed by members of the [[Set]]. The project was meant to be a way to create a supply of Allomancers for the Set's [[Hemalurgy|experiments]].{{book ref|mb7|57}} Aside from the captured people, a number of Set tranquility officers and scientists were part of the Community.{{book ref|mb7|56}}{{book ref|mb7|47}}

== History ==
The Community was a project devised by the [[Set]] sometime before {{date|341|PC}}.{{book ref|mb4|epilogue}} The project was initially led by [[Edwarn Ladrian]], who orchestrated the capture of various men and women with a strong [[Allomancy|Allomantic]] presence in their heritage.{{book ref|mb7|56}} These men and women, from all over the Basin and the Roughs,{{book ref|mb7|29}} were captured by various criminals, under the guise of kidnappings or hostage takings.{{book ref|mb4|4}} During the first two years of the project, the captured people were moved between multiple caverns. For the five years after this, they stayed in an underground town named [[Wayfarer]], which was located underneath [[Bilming]]. They were told that the world had ended, with the Ashmounts erupting and metal mutants rampaging. The individuals kidnapped were also told that they were chosen to carry on human civilization and that their 'rescuers' had to stage a kidnapping to prevent mass panic.{{book ref|mb7|56}}{{book ref|mb7|57}} Additionally, they were told that there were multiple cavern complexes, such as ones beneath [[Elendel]]. The Set did its best to convince the people, hiring actors to play survivors and even letting a ‘metal mutant’ into the town once.{{book ref|mb7|56}}

At some point, likely after Edwarn’s death, Gave Entrone took over as leader of the project. He, together with Telsin Ladrian, also planned to use the town of Wayfarer, where the project took place, as a bunker in case [[Autonomy]] invaded. During {{date|348|PC}}, [[Marasi Colms]] broke into the facility, where she would convince several people that what they’d been told about the world above wasn’t true. After confronting Gave, and defeating him with the help of several of the captured people,{{book ref|mb7|60}} Marasi led them out onto the surface, ending the Community.{{book ref|mb7|68}}
