Brightlord '''Taselin''' was an [[Alethi]] noble on the [[Shattered Plains]] of [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa1|22}}

Taselin attended one of King [[Elhokar Kholin]]'s feasts, where his intelligence was mocked by [[Wit]].{{book ref|sa1|22}}
