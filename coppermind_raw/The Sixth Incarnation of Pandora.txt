'''''The Sixth Incarnation of Pandora''''' is a far-future cyberpunk sci-fi novel about immortal warriors who have been turned into superweapons with nanites.{{wob ref|6096}}{{wob ref|14248}}  It is the fifth novel [[Brandon Sanderson]] wrote, originally completed in 1999.{{wob ref|7825}} It is unlikely that it will ever be published.{{wob ref|7818}}

The story is about an immortal warrior named Zellion who has lived too long and lost connection to the world around him because he has lost his connection to people.{{book ref|tsm|part=postscript}} It features a man who used nanotechnology to make himself immortal, but was also driven insane by the process and the consequences thereof. Brandon has described it as the "most un-Brandon-like" book he has ever written as well as it being dark, grisly, and violent.{{wob ref|1985}}

==Trivia==
*The name and some aspects of the character of Zellion were later [[Zellion|repurposed]] for use in [[The Sunlit Man]]{{book ref|tsm|part=postscript}}{{wob ref|16104}}
*Sanderson considers The Sixth Incarnation of Pandora to potentially be his worst title.{{book ref|tsm|part=postscript}}
