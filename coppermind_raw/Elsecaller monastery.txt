The '''Elsecaller monastery''' is a [[Monastery of Truth]] located in southern [[Shinovar]] on [[Roshar]].{{file ref|Map_of_Shin_Monasteries.jpg}} While [[Pozen-son-Nash]] is the resident [[Honorbearer]], it is sometimes referred to as '''Pozen's monastery'''. It is dedicated to the worship of the [[Herald]] [[Battar|Batlah]] and associated with the [[Order of Elsecallers]].

== Location ==
The monastery is located in the center of the city of [[Mokdown]]. It and the city are on an island that lies between two rivers.{{book ref|sa5|73}}

== Structure ==
Much like the other monasteries, the Elsecaller monastery has a great hall, presumably with a mural of its patron Herald on the floor. A hallway with thin-slitted windows links the great hall with the acolytes' quarters. These rooms are purposely designed to make their occupants accustom themselves to touching metal as they have metal door handles and stone floors. There are at least seven of these bunk-filled rooms. In the seventh room down, Szeth's doll of Molli the sheep is hidden in the wall until Szeth reveals its location and [[Sylphrena|Syl]] takes it and gives it to [[Kaladin]].{{book ref|sa5|66}}

== History ==
Pozen-son-Nash was the Elsecaller Honorbearer for some time before the [[True Desolation]]. In {{Rosharan date|1157}}, he visited the [[Stoneward monastery]] to discipline [[Szeth-son-Neturo]] for acting rashly. However, the voice of [[Ishar]] directed Pozen to recruit Szeth as an acolyte, which he did. Szeth was taken to the Elsecaller monastery to train under an active Honorbearer.{{book ref|sa5|68}}

[[Gonda-son-Darias]] held the position of swordsmaster around {{Rosharan date|1159}}. He was handily beaten in a sparring match by Szeth, which conferred the title of swordsmaster to Szeth. Between Szeth's weakness with [[Transportation|Elsecalling]] and the need to replace [[Tuko-son-Tuko]], the current [[Order of Windrunners|Windrunner]] Honorbearer, it was decided that Szeth would undertake a pilgrimage to become the Windrunner Honorbearer, rather than work towards the position of Elsecaller Honorbearer.{{book ref|sa5|73}}

Szeth visits the Elsecaller monastery during his quest to [[Cleansing of Shinovar|cleanse Shinovar]] in 1175. He and Kaladin find the monastery empty, and Szeth goes into his old room, retrieves the doll, and weeps over it. Kaladin has a conversation with him about his desire to stop killing and its conflict with his quest to cleanse of Shinovar. After this, Syl gives the sheep doll to Kaladin, and they leave the monastery with Szeth, only to find [[Nale]] waiting outside, accompanied by his [[highspren]] [[121]], as well as Szeth's [[spren]] [[Auxiliary|12124]].

== Politics ==
The monastery has a position of swordsmaster, who is favored to become the next Honorbearer.{{book ref|sa5|73}}

The monastery  has jurisdiction over the city of Mokdown. The Elsecaller Honorbearer appoints some positions within the Mokdown bureaucracy, such as the finance minister.{{book ref|sa5|73}}
