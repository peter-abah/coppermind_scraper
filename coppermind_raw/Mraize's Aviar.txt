'''Mraize's Aviar''' is an [[Aviar]] on [[Roshar]] owned by [[Mraize]], {{wob ref|8807}} and later [[Axindweth]].

== Appearance and Personality ==
Mraize's Aviar has a large hooked, knifelike beak and bright green feathers.{{book ref|sa3|22}}{{book ref|sa4|i|5}} It has a sleek body.{{book ref|sa3|22}}

The Aviar often rides on Mraize's shoulder.{{book ref|sa3|22}} Its chirps sound like words.{{book ref|sa4|13}} It appears to dislike the cold.{{book ref|sa4|13}} The Aviar likes being in charge of confrontations and will flee when things go unexpectedly for it.{{book ref|sa4|i|5}}

== Attributes and Abilities ==
Mraize's Aviar's talent is unknown.{{wob ref|8176}} It is trained to return to him with a whistle and is carnivorous.{{book ref|sa4|13}}{{wob ref|14718}} It is highly intelligent.{{book ref|sa4|60}}{{book ref|sa4|13}}

== History ==
Mraize's Aviar originated from [[First of the Sun]].{{wob ref|8900}}{{wob ref|15208}} Mraize obtained it sometime prior to arriving at [[Urithiru]], by the Rosharan year {{Rosharan date|1174}}.{{book ref|sa3|22}} It would often ride on his shoulder, as he liked flaunting the bird to others, even while disguised.{{book ref|sa3|22}}

During the year {{Rosharan date|1175}}, Mraize murdered [[Gereh]] for unknown reasons. Mraize's own Aviar then attempted to kill [[Gereh's Aviar]], badly wounding it. [[Lift]] defended Gereh's Aviar and healed it with [[Lifelight]], saving its life. The Aviar then guided her through Urithiru down to Gereh's corpse. It mourned over its loss until Mraize and his Aviar entered the room, at which point Lift fled with Gereh's Aviar.{{book ref|sa4|i|5}}

After Mraize enters the [[Spiritual Realm]] to search for [[Ba-Ado-Mishram]]'s prison, the Aviar sometimes accompanies [[Axindweth]] when she tortures [[Vasher]],{{book ref|sa5|i|9}} but is not present when she is defeated by Lift.{{book ref|sa5|i|13}}
