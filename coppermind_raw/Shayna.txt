'''Lady Shayna''' is a noblewoman on [[Scadrial]] who attends [[Lady ZoBell's party]] in [[Elendel]] in {{date|342|PC}}. 

She has a reputation for being long-winded and boastful. She was a staunch supporter of Governor [[Replar Innate|Innate]], both politically and financially, and spoke to him at the party.{{book ref|mb5|9}}
