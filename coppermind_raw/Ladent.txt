'''Ladent''' is an ardent in [[Dalinar]]'s camp.

He is bald but wears a beard. He wore thick robes with long sleeves.{{book ref|sa1|28}}

Dalinar orders him to dispatch a messenger from the bridge building yards to tell [[Havrom]] of their imminent inspection.{{book ref|sa1|28}}

He also refused to marry Dalinar and Navani.{{book ref|sa3|4}}
