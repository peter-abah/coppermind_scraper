'''Rokke''' is an [[Aviar]] on [[First of the Sun]] bonded with [[Sixth of the Dusk (character)|Sixth of the Dusk]].{{book ref|iote|31}}

== Appearance and Personality ==
Rokke is a small black and white bird with a scarlet head, belonging to one of the five breeds of Aviar the [[Eelakin]] use to shield their minds.{{book ref|iote|15}}{{book ref|iote|21}} Upon first meeting Dusk, She was initially quite skittish and shy but gained confidence and bravery through Dusk's verbal encouragement and their adventures together.{{book ref|iote|19}}{{book ref|iote|24}}{{book ref|iote|29}}{{book ref|iote|48}}{{book ref|iote|59}}

== Attributes and Abilities ==
{{for|Aviar}}
Rokke has very similar abilities to that of [[Kokerlii]], being able to form a [[Nahel bond]] with humans and grant a "talent" that cloaks from mental attacks and intrusions, similar to [[Allomancy|Allomantic]] [[Copper]]. Rokke gained this ability by visiting [[Patji's Eye]] when she was young.{{book ref|iote|16}} It is shown that Rokke can prevent other entities such as the [[Sleepless]] from reading thoughts and mentally speaking to the bearer of her talent.{{book ref|iote|46}}{{book ref|iote|50}}
In addition to the abilities she bestows on her bonded partner, Rokke also has the ability to sense the [[Current|Grand Knell]] through her bond with Dusk.{{book ref|iote|33}}

== History ==
{{image|IotE_ch_icon_fig_101.png|side=left|height=250px|Sak and Rokke}}
Rokke was initially kept by a [[trapper]] on one of the [[Pantheon]] islands. After his death, the bird was found in the trapper's [[safecamp]] and eventually ended up in the care of [[Vathi]].{{book ref|iote|15}} Likely in an effort to help him after the loss of Kokerlii,{{book ref|iote|15}}{{book ref|iote|24}} Vathi presented Rokke to Dusk just before they all passed through [[Patji's Eye]].{{book ref|iote|15}}{{book ref|iote|17}}{{book ref|iote|19}}.
Rokke accompanied Dusk and [[Sak]] on their expedition through the [[Drominad]] [[Cognitive Realm#Subastrals|subastral]].{{book ref|iote|24}} She used her ability to veil the group's mental presence to protect them from predatory [[Skullsnake|skullsnakes]] and occasionally lure the creatures by dropping the veil so Dusk could hunt them.{{book ref|iote|29}}{{book ref|iote|31}}

Upon reaching the [[Dakwara's island]] and being apprehended by the [[Scadrial|Scadrians]],{{book ref|iote|34}}{{book ref|iote|37}} Rokke accompanied Dusk to [[Dajer|Dajer's]] office and then to their own quarters where Dusk set up some toys and food bowls for the Aviar.{{book ref|iote|39}}{{book ref|iote|41}} She also accompanied him on his many trips down the island's tunnel,{{book ref|iote|43}}{{book ref|iote|48}}{{book ref|iote|57}}{{book ref|iote|59}} helped him shield his mind from the [[Sleepless]] [[Chrysalis]],{{book ref|iote|46}}{{book ref|iote|50}} and went with him across the island's lake to the central island.{{book ref|iote|59}}
