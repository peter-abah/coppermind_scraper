The '''Nor'Tallon''' river is a major river on the [[Dayside]] of [[Taldain]].

It flows west from the headwaters in the mountains in [[Tallon]], through the [[Deep sand]] until it reaches the [[Ry'DoAli River]] in [[Lossand]].{{map ref|Dayside}} 

[[Nor'Tallon]], the capital of Tallon, resides on the river near the headwaters in the mountains.{{book ref|ws|part=ars}} [[Kezare]], the capital of Lossand, is located at the confluence of the two rivers.{{map ref|Dayside}}
