'''Gibsey''' is a pirate fighter pilot in the [[belt]] of the [[nowhere]]. He is part of the [[Broadsider Faction]], and is a member of [[Flintlock Flight]].{{book ref|sky3|26}}

== History ==
When members of the [[Jolly Roger Faction]] raided the Broadsider base, Gibsey was one of the pilots who came out to fight. He picked up two enemy tails, but [[Spensa Nightshade]] dealt with them. Later during the battle, [[Peg]] and [[Maksim]] aided Gibsey, who picked up another two tails.{{book ref|sky3|26}} 

Gibsey was presumably present at the duel to determine the new pirate champion along with the rest of the faction{{book ref|cytonic|29}}, as well as the battle to take control of [[Surehold]].{{book ref|cytonic|35}}
