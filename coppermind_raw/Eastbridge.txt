'''Eastbridge''' is a large suspension bridge in [[Elendel]] spanning the [[Irongate River]].{{book ref|mb5|18}}

The bridge is on the outermost ring-road of Elendel.{{map ref|Elendel}}{{book ref|mb5|18}} 

The bridge itself has both a roadway and railway running over it.{{book ref|mb5|24}}

[[Waxillium Ladrian]] stopped to think about things on the bridge after his meeting with [[Edwarn]]. He looks back into the city and observes that the city's electric lighting doesn't extend into the outskirts of Elendel past the bridge.{{book ref|mb5|18}}

Wax then chases [[Bleeder]] onto the bridge for their final confrontation on and around the bridge.{{book ref|mb5|24}}
