'''Balsas''' is a resident of [[Hearthstone]] on [[Roshar]] during the [[Era of Solitude]].{{book ref|sa1|31}}

In {{Rosharan date|1167}}, Balsas, [[Horl]], [[Luten]] and others tried to rob [[Lirin]] of the [[sphere]]s he got from Brightlord [[Wistiow]].{{book ref|sa1|31}} Brightlord [[Roshone]] had convinced them that Lirin had stolen the spheres. Balsas and the others were caught by Lirin outside his home, where he confronted them, shaming them for their behavior. Despite the black mask that Balsas was wearing, [[Kaladin]] recognized him by the nice new coat he was wearing. Once they were caught by Lirin, Balsas and the rest left Lirin's home, ashamed.
