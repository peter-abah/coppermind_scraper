'''House Davar''' is a small [[Veden]] family living in [[Valam princedom]] on [[Roshar]].

It gives Highprince [[Valam]] its fealty, but his health was ailing him and he no longer provided the protection for the Davars that he once did.{{book ref|sa1|3}}

== History ==
{{image|Davar family by zoethatcher art.jpg|side=left|width=300px}}

The Davar family live on an estate in Valam princedom and are known for mining. After their father's death, the Davar children learned that their father had been creating new quarries using a [[Soulcaster]] until the [[fabrial]] broke.{{book ref|sa1|7}}

[[Lin Davar]] and [[Shallan's mother|Lady Davar]] had five children; [[Helaran]], [[Balat]], [[Jushu]], [[Wikim]] and [[Shallan]].

<!-- this needs years and timeline stuff -->At the age of eleven Shallan begins manifesting [[Surgebinding]] abilities and her mother attempts to kill her, leading to her own death which Lin takes the blame for.{{book ref|sa1|10}} Shallan blanks out the experience and blames her father with everyone else. This strain on Lin causes him to become harsh and abusive towards his children. He eventually exiles Helaran, and then kills his second wife [[Malise Gevelmar]].{{book ref|sa2|73}} After he threatens to kill Balat, Shallan poisons and then throttles Lin.{{book ref|sa2|73}} During this time [[Helaran]] engages in a battle against [[Meridas Amaram]] and is killed by [[Kaladin]].{{book ref|sa1|47}}

After the [[Battle of Vedenar]] and the death of Highprince Valam, the surviving brothers travel to [[Urithiru]] to meet [[Shallan]]. They arrive in the days after the [[Battle of Thaylen Field]], the day of Shallan's wedding to [[Adolin Kholin]].{{book ref|sa3|122}}
