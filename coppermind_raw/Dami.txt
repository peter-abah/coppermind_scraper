'''Dami''' is a [[Riran]] [[Stoneward]] of the Fourth Ideal on [[Roshar]] bonded to an unknown [[peakspren]].{{book ref|sa5|6}} He fought during the [[True Desolation]], at the [[Emuli offensive]] and at the [[Shattered Plains]], and is legendary among soldiers and Radiants alike.

== Appearance and Personality ==
Dami is unusually tall in his [[Shardplate]], standing a head taller than other more conventional [[Shardbearer]]s. His Plate is golden and is outlined in glowing orange. He prefers using his [[Shardblade]] in the form of a tower shield.{{book ref|sa5|63}}

Dami is fiercely loyal and resolute, revering [[Dalinar]] as a military genius{{book ref|sa5|90}}. He is unwilling to retreat, though he follows orders. He upholds the philosophy of the Stonewards, "I will be there where I am needed." He is well liked by others.{{book ref|sa5|6}}
== Abilities and Attributes ==
Dami is a mighty warrior, famous for his prowess, capable of holding his own against Fused, helping to turn the tide of battles.{{book ref|sa5|63}}

=== Surgebinding ===
As a [[Stoneward]] of the Fourth Ideal, Dami can use the [[Surges]] of [[Tension]] and [[Cohesion]] through his [[Nahel bond]]. The Surge of Cohesion gives him the ability to reshape stone, whereas Tension gives him the ability to control the hardness of an object. He utilizes both of these surges to great effect in combat, with a strategy of his being using ribbons in combat as a kind of whip.{{book ref|sa5|63}}

== History ==
Dami swore up to the Third Ideal of the Stonewards sometime during the lead-up to the [[True Desolation]]. Dami fought at the Emuli Offensive, swearing his Fourth Ideal right afterward in a moment considered quite impressive by spectators, gaining fame for it.{{book ref|sa5|6}}{{book ref|sa5|63}} He was nominated to the line of succession for the throne of [[Urithiru]] by [[Dalinar]].{{book ref|sa5|6}}

=== Second Battle of Narak ===
Dami arrived at the [[Shattered Plains]] via [[Windrunner]], coming in a pivotal moment and helping push the enemy back.{{book ref|sa5|63}} He became part of the leadership there for the [[coalition of monarchs|coalition]].{{book ref|sa5|90}} He continued to fight, defending the bridge during the retreat from [[Narak]] Four and helping [[Sigzil]] fight against the [[Fused]].{{book ref|sa5|74}}{{book ref|sa5|103}}.

When the second Fused army arrived, Sigzil ordered a retreat, which Dami resisted initially, not liking to give ground to the enemy. However, he was persuaded by the fact that Dalinar had made Sigzil his commanding officer.{{book ref|sa5|118}}
