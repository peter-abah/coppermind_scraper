A '''Dulo''' is a two seat [[starfighter]] used by the [[DDF]] on [[Detritus]]. It has two co-pilot seats side by side in the cockpit. The cockpit is narrow, with a fuselage only a little wider than that of a [[Largo]] class. A Dulo is able to travel at a speed of at least Mag-9.{{book ref|sf1|9}} 

[[Rodge McCaffery|Rig]] and [[Freyja Marten|FM]] fly together in a Dulo while experimenting with the [[taynix]].{{book ref|sf1|9}} During the mission to [[Evershore (planet)|Evershore]], [[Cuna]] flies with FM in a Dulo.{{book ref|sf3|5}}
