**Hana** is a [[Jah Keved|Veden]] teenager on [[Roshar]]. Shortly before the arrival of the [[Everstorm]], Hana and her [[Tet Rebin|father]] were travelling to the [[Shattered Plains]] to sell wine at the warcamps. Close to the [[Covenant Stone Crossroads]], their wagon got stuck in a pool of wet [[crem]]. After trying and being unable to pull the wagon free, her father sent her to ask for help at the crossroads. While they were trying to get it out of the crem, the group got ambushed by bandits. Hana survived the encounter, although it is unclear whether or not her father survived.
