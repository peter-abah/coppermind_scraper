**Callins** is a farmer in [[Hearthstone]] on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa1|44}}

When [[Meridas Amaram]] comes to Hearthstone, Amaram says he was visiting with his cousin, so he does not need to delegate his task. Callins asks what task he is referring to, and Amaram says that it is for recruitment for his army.{{book ref|sa1|44}}

Callins's fate upon the arrival of the [[True Desolation]] is unknown.
