**Shansan** is a young ardent on [[Roshar]]. During the [[War of Reckoning]], they resided in the [[House Kholin|Kholin]] warcamp on the [[Shattered Plains]]. There, they were the local expert on esoteric [[glyph]]s. As an ardent, Shansan keeps their head shaved and wears gray robes. They are said to be enthusiastic{{rpg ref|slsw|43}} meticulous, well-read and dramatic. They also wear spectacles, and have an eye for detail. They tend to babble and ﬁdget, and their excited exclamations are often accompanied by [[sincerityspren]]. Shansan loves to quote obscure texts and deliver dramatic readings to a captive audience.{{rpg ref|slsw|44}} 

At some point during the War of Reckoning, Shansan heard stories about a caravan under the command of sergeant [[Ellar]] being ambushed by bandits with strange glyphs painted on their heads. Shortly afterwards, they met with a few people from this caravan and discussed the glyphs.{{rpg ref|slsw|43}}
