**Rahul** is one of [[Stephen Leeds]]' [[aspect]]s. He is an Indian man with a moustache and a round face. He speaks with an Indian accent and knows a lot about photography and computers, making him unusual in his ability to have more than one specialization. He is married to [[Kalyani]].

Rahul was unusual in that Stephen didn't intentionally manifest him, but instead he spontaneously appeared via his relationship with Kalyani. He first appeared to Stephen while Stephen was on a video call with Kalyani, when he promised to be very helpful.

## Trivia
* Rahul is named after [[Brandon]]'s good friend Rahul Pantula, who is a beta reader and assists Brandon with aspects of the [[cosmere]] that connect to Indian mythology.
