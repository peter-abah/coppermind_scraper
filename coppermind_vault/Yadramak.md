**Yadramak** is a [[Malwish Empire|Malwish]] politician. He eventually rose to the position of prime minister and became the head of his party.{{book ref|iote|35}} Two years before the discovery of the [[Patji's Eye|Drominad perpendicularity]], he engaged the services of a [[Chrysalis|Sleepless spy]]. While meeting with her however, he was ambushed and killed by partisans.{{book ref|iote|48}} Some of the nodes belonging to the spy were killed as well, and when the body was found, everyone assumed the assassination to have been the work of the Sleepless.{{book ref|iote|55}}
