**Yadramak** is a [[Malwish Empire|Malwish]] politician. He eventually rose to the position of prime minister and became the head of his party. Two years before the discovery of the [[Patji's Eye|Drominad perpendicularity]], he engaged the services of a [[Chrysalis|Sleepless spy]]. While meeting with her however, he was ambushed and killed by partisans. Some of the nodes belonging to the spy were killed as well, and when the body was found, everyone assumed the assassination to have been the work of the Sleepless.
