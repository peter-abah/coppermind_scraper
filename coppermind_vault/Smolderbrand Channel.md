**Smolderbrand Channel** is a channel in the [[Sea of Lost Lights]].{{map ref|Sea of Lost Lights}} One can travel downstream towards the [[North Hallen Channel]] and eventually reach [[Celebrant]].

[[Nazh]] notes on [[:File:Sea of Lost Lights.jpg|a map of the Sea of Lost Lights]] that he met [[Smolderbrand]], the namesake of the channel, and stole said map from her.{{map ref|Sea of Lost Lights}}
