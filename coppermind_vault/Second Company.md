The **Second Company** is a mercantile business and political party on [[First of the Sun]]. During a meeting of the most important [[Eelakin]] officials and politicians, it was suggested that the Second Company fake a coup as a way of forcing the [[Malwish Empire|Ones Above]] into dealing with a new organization, thereby resetting the talks.
