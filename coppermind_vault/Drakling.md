> I've ordered the hunting range filled with wild draklings, Your Majesty. The most vicious we could find. Perhaps destroying them will put you in a more fond mood.
> — [[Besk]], to [[Kairominas]]

**Draklings** are species of animal native to [[Alornia]] that [[Kairominas]] hunts for sport. They can be quite vicious. 

Due to their name, they may be related to dragons.
