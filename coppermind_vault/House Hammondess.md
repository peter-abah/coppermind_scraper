**House Hammondess ** is a noble house on [[Scadrial]] founded by [[Hammond]] after the [[Catacendre]]. Hammond had two children and a wife, it can be assumed that at least one of his children, the eldest boy, ruled House Hammondess himself.{{book ref|mb3|epilogue}}

House Hammondess holds a hereditary seat on the Elendel Senate.{{book ref|mb7|31}} In {{date|348|PC}} this seat was held by [[Mari Hammondess]].{{book ref|mb7|33}} Governer [[Replar Innate]] appointed a Hammondess scion to a judge’s seat in {{date|342|PC}}.{{book ref|mb7|18}}
