Brightlady **Jayla Ruthar** is an [[Alethkar|Alethi]] noblewoman on [[Roshar]] during the [[Era of Solitude]].

==Appearance and Personality==
[[Jasnah Kholin]] believes that Jayla and her husband are thieving and dangerous. They are usually political allies of [[Torol Sadeas|Torol]] and [[Ialai Sadeas]], who use them for their ambition.

==History==
At some point in her youth, Jayla wed Highprince [[Ruthar]]; by  she had given birth to their eldest son [[Relis Ruthar|Relis]] and later had at least one more child, a son named [[Ivanar Ruthar|Ivanar]]. She is the aunt of [[Elit Ruthar]].

During the [[War of Reckoning]], she lived on the [[Shattered Plains]], presumably in the Ruthar warcamp. In late , she attended a meeting in the [[Pinnacle]] with other members of the Alethi nobility to discuss the threat of the [[Assassin in White]].

Jayla's fate during the [[True Desolation]] is unclear. Highprince Ruthar moved to [[Urithiru]] after the [[Battle of Narak]]; he was later stripped of his title and exiled to [[Azimir]], and Relis became the new highprince. Jayla is not mentioned in connection with these events.
