The **Burning Gardens** is a settlement in the [[Sea of Lost Lights]] in [[Shadesmar]], the [[subastral]] of [[Roshar]]. It is near [[Ravizadth]], and is adjacent to the [[Sea of Oracles]]. The provenance of the name "Burning Gardens" is not known, but a map stolen from [[Smolderbrand]] by [[Nazh]] indicates that it is not simply a garden, as there are some large buildings located there.

In the [[Physical Realm]], the location of the Burning Gardens corresponds to the northeastern portion of the [[Tarat Sea]], near [[Mevan Bay]].
