**Ivans** is one of [[Stephen Leeds]]' [[aspect]]s.

They were some form of mechanic, and Stephen regrets not taking them to Jerusalem. He, along with [[Chin]], managed to disassemble and reassemble a complex computer system during the [[Longway Incident]], preventing a catastrophic detonation.
