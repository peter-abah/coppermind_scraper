> The landswights corrupted, and the barghests came forth out of shadow and flame to his call.
> — [[Sefawynn]] tells the story of the [[battle of Badon]]

A **barghest** is a type of creature of darkness in the [[Earth (Frugal Wizard)|alternate medieval dimension]]. The barghests come from shadow and flame, and they served the [[Black Bear]] in his war against the [[Weswarans]] during the [[battle of Badon]]. 

As [[Sefawynn]] told [[John West]] the story of the battle of Badon, she explained how the Black Bear had turned to the darkness, and how the barghests answered to his call in the war. The battle ultimately culminated in the Black Bear seeking out [[Fenris]] the Wolf, and Fenris consuming [[Friag]].

## Trivia
*In real-world Northern English folklore, a barghest is a type of monstrous, ghostly or supernatural dog. The barghests may therefore be the same creatures as the Black Bear's hounds that haunt the forests.
