The ***Red Panther*** was a sailing vessel on [[Nalthis]].{{book ref|wb|57}}

A man from [[Hallandren]] named [[Llarimar]] had a hobby of sailing. His brother [[Stennimar]] often joined him, and even gained some sailing skills through accompanying him.{{book ref|wb|57}} Llarimar borrowed the *Red Panther* and Stennimar joined him on a pleasure cruise. They were accompanied by some deckhands and Llarimar's family, including his daughter [[Tatara]]. A violent storm suddenly hit, and Tatara was swept overboard. Stennimar dove in after her and tied a rope around her leg so that she could be pulled up. Tatara was saved, but Stennimar was pulled further underwater. He was unable to get back to the surface and drowned.{{book ref|wb|57}} This led to Stennimar becoming a [[Returned]] and Llarimar becoming his priest.{{book ref|wb|54}}

After Stennimar was resurrected as [[Lightsong]] the Bold, he did not remember his former life. He was surprised when he realized that he knew a number of sailing terms.{{book ref|wb|30}} He often saw ships and storms in his dreams or in abstract paintings, but he did not know why.{{book ref|wb|3}} He also had recurring nightmares about a red panther and a young woman's face fading into darkness.{{book ref|wb|26}}{{book ref|wb|38}} He even used "red panther" as a [[Lifeless]] [[Awakening#Awakening Commands|Command]] phrase.{{book ref|wb|47}} He eventually regained his memory regarding the ill-fated ship.{{book ref|wb|57}}
