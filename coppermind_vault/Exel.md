**Exel** is a member of the [[Reckoners]] cell based in [[Babilar]]. Exel gathers information from the inhabitants of [[Babilar]], and is recognized by people in the city. Much of this skill in this can be assumed to come from his experience as a pre-Calamity government agent. As a former mortician, Exel visits funerals and often makes morbid (but well-natured) jokes about how someone would look as a corpse. He is described as an extremely large man by [[David]].

After [[Prof]]'s prevention of Babilar's destruction and subsequent corruption from overuse of his [[Epic]] abilities, Exel was brutally crushed to death, along with [[Valentine|Val]], by their former leader's forcefield abilities.
