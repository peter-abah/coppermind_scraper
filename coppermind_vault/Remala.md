**Remala** is a [[listener]] on [[Roshar]]. When [[Rlain]] and [[Venli]] reunited in [[Urithiru]], Rlain asked about Remala following the [[Battle of Narak]] and the return of the [[Fused]]. Venli responded that no other listeners were left. The specifics of Remala's fate are unknown.
