**Miss Coussaint** is [[Allriandre]]'s landlady in [[Elendel]] on [[Scadrial]].

She sometimes watches over [[Ruri]], Allriandre's daughter, when Allriandre is out. However, she shouts at Ruri when she draws on the walls using toothpaste, causing the little girl to cry.

She takes her rent on the first of the month, and plans to do renovations.

She has a lot of chins, with one way bigger than the rest.
