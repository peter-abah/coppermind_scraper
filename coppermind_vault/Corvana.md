**Corvana** is a scholar on [[Roshar]].

Corvana wrote the *[[Analectics]]*, a book that contained at least some quotes from the songs of the [[Vanrial]] that were thought to be in [[Dawnchant]]. A copy of her book was owned by Princess [[Jasnah Kholin]] and later used by Brightlady [[Navani Kholin]] in  to confirm that Highprince [[Dalinar Kholin]] was speaking in the Dawnchant during his visions.
