The **Basin of the Veiled Sun** is a body of water in the [[Sea of Souls]], part of the [[Shadesmar|Rosharan subastral]] of the [[Cognitive Realm]].{{map ref|Sea of Souls}} 

It is located west of [[Urithiru]], north of [[Perpetual Sobriety]], and north of the [[Azimir]] [[Oathgate]]. North of that Oathgate, [[Nazh]] notes that something is weird in the basin, and intends to report back to [[Khriss]] in person regarding it.{{map ref|Sea of Souls}}

In the [[Physical Realm]], it corresponds to territory in [[Azir]].{{map ref|Roshar}}
