**Gavri** is a military officer in the [[coalition of monarchs]] on [[Roshar]]. 

Gavri's soldiers were stationed on the upper floors of [[Urithiru]] when [[Raboniel]] compromised the tower's defenses and the [[singer]] forces invaded. He was one of the few officers present in the command center when Queen [[Navani]] and Battalionlord [[Teofil]] were deciding on the best course of action. After Navani decided to focus on an incursion into the crystal pillar room, Teofil directed Gavri and [[Shuanor]] to withdraw their troops to safer locations while [[Radathavian]] led a harrying force to cover their retreat.
