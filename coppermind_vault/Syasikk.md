**Syasikk** is a philosopher or scholar on [[Roshar]]. Nothing of their works is known.{{book ref|sa1|5}}

In 1173, when [[Shallan Davar]] requests to be [[Jasnah Kholin]]'s [[ward]] in [[Kharbranth]], [[Jasnah]] asks [[Shallan]] whether she had read a number of works, including Syasikk, and Shallan had not read their work.{{book ref|sa1|5}}
