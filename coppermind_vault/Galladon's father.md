> Growing up in Elantris with a bitter father made me a poor Dula.
> — [[Galladon]]

**Galladon's father** is an [[Elantrian]] on [[Sel]]. 

## Appearance and Personality
Like other Elantrians, his skin is silvery and glows faintly.

Although he loves his homeland and his family, he has a bitter edge to his demeanor after being taken by the [[Shaod]] and eventually suffers from severe depression. 

## Attributes and Abilities
As an Elantrian, he is gifted with long life and access to [[AonDor]]. He is also exceptionally knowledgeable about agriculture and farming techniques. 

## History

> They said that Elantris was the most blessed of cities, but my father was never happy here. I guess even in paradise there are those who don't fit in.
> — Galladon, on his father

Galladon's father was a farmer in [[Duladel]], but he moved to [[Elantris (city)|Elantris]] with his wife and [[Galladon|son]] soon after the Shaod took him.

He was never happy in Elantris, and wished just to be a simple farmer. Because his homeland never left his mind, he became a scholar and continued to study agriculture even though the topics were useless to him in Elantris. He knew that Elantrians were not welcome in other lands so he could never follow his dream of returning back to [[Duladel]].

One morning he woke up to find his wife dying next to him, suffering from an unexpected sickness. The sickness had come on suddenly and he was unable to heal her with AonDor. He had never been happy, but after this event, he became deeply depressed. Galladon felt that his father's pain taught him that Elantrians were not truly gods.
 
Galladon's father told his son to move back to Duladel and become a farmer once he was old enough to live on his own. Shortly afterwards, he contracted a disease known as heart-death; however, instead of going to the healers, he shut himself up in his small underground study. His Elantrian physiology extended his life, but he eventually died about a year after Galladon moved away.

## Legacy
Although Galladon loved being a farmer, he felt like an outcast in Duladel since his demeanor had been affected by growing up in Elantris with his father, who did not want to be there.

Since Galladon grew up in Elantris, he came to be very familiar with the layout of the city. After he was taken by the Shaod, he was able to move around efficiently and find many obscure routes and hiding spots. He found his father's old study and cleaned it of slime, and was very protective of the study and the books it contained. [[Raoden]] was the first person to whom he showed the study, and they found some answers to their questions about AonDor in Galladon's father's old books.
