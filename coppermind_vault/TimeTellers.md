The **TimeTellers** are a [[Scadrian]] political movement, likely from the [[Malwish Empire]].

They claim neutrality in interplanetary conflicts. They are made up of scientists who wish to understand the mysteries of the cosmere.{{book ref|tsm|44}} [[Zellion]] suspects they are secretly an arm of the Malwish military who work to develop tech in order to stay ahead in the accelerating interplanetary arms race.{{book ref|tsm|44}}

The TimeTellers wear small triangular metal ornaments decorated with red enamel on the sides of their faces.{{book ref|tsm|44}}

## History
The TimeTellers likely developed at some point after {{date|348|FA}}, They were most active during Scadrial’s early Space Age.{{book ref|tsm|44}}

A group of TimeTellers landed on [[Canticle]] in order to study its sun and core, and how they interact with one another.{{book ref|tsm|44}}

## Discoveries 
While on Canticle they made the following discoveries. 
* the Investiture in the star gets ‘sucked’ down into the planet’s core.
* how to recharge [[Sunheart|Sunhearts]].
* how to create [[Sunheart#Cinderhearts|Cinderhearts]].
* various other technologies involving the planet's native investiture, like guns strong enough to destroy ships
