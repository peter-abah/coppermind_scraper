The **Channerel Range** is a mountain range in the [[Elendel Basin]]. It is northwest of [[Elendel]] and is one of the mountain ranges that separates the basin from the [[Roughs]]. Its location is about where the [[River Channerel]] was during the [[Final Empire]] period of Scadrial.
