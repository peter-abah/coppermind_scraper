**Destroyer** is a horse owned by a young [[Waxillium Ladrian]] during his initial adventures in the [[Roughs]] of [[Scadrial]].{{book ref|mb5|prologue}}

Wax gave her the name "Destroyer" in an attempt to inspire her, as she had a placid nature. He tried to train her to come when he whistled, but she tended to ignore him.{{book ref|mb5|prologue}}
