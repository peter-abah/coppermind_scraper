> Karkun's time was without number. Forever and never at all. It set the [[Narrative]] in motion.
> — [[Rastik]] to [[Paul Tanasin]]

**Karkun** is the first [[Dark One (title)|Dark One]] on [[Mirandus]], and set the Narrative in motion according to Rastik. They created the [[Dark Keep]] in [[Pitch]] where the [[Well of Sorrows]] is contained.

The [[Warfields of Karkun]] and/ [[Uls Karkun]] were likely named after Karkun.
