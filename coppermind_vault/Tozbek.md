**Tozbek** is a [[Thaylen]] ship captain on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|twok|3}}

==Appearance and Personality==

Tozbek is a short, portly man with a bald head and has long white [[Thaylen]] eyebrows, a foot long each, that curve up into a stiff, fanned shape above his eyes.{{book ref|twok|3}}{{book ref|sa2|1}} He has a scar on his jaw caused by loose tackle during rough weather. He is a cautious, calm man and an excellent sailor.{{book ref|twok|3}}

==History==
Tozbek captains the ship *[[Wind's Pleasure]]* and is married with [[Ashlv]], who sails with him. He transported [[Shallan Davar]] from [[Jah Keved]] to [[Kharbranth]] in her pursuit of [[Jasnah Kholin]] at a generously discounted price, because of [[House Davar]]'s investments in his shipping business.{{book ref|twok|3}}

{{quote
|Of course we'll give you passage and return to your estates. It would be my pleasure to have you aboard. We are Davar men, we are. Indebted to your family.
|Captain Tozbek {{book ref|twok|42}}
}}

Jasnah selected the *Wind's Pleasure* with Tozbek and his crew for her and Shallan's voyage from Kharbranth to the [[Shattered Plains]].{{book ref|sa2|1}} Following the sinking of the *Wind's Pleasure*, Tozbek's fate is unknown.{{book ref|sa2|7}}
