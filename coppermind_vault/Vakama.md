**Vakama** are traditional [[Veden]] warrior garments, similar to the [[Alethi]] [[takama]], worn on [[Roshar]]. The main difference is that the vakama is pleated, while the takama is straight.{{book ref|sa4|20}}

[[Shallan]] wears a vakama while overseeing training of the [[Unseen Court]].{{book ref|sa4|20}}
