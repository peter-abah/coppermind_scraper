> Retreat? But the enemy!
> — Kellen to [[Marasi]]

**Kellen** is a member of the [[Elendel]] constabulary on [[Scadrial]].

In , Kellen was part of a team which accompanied [[Marasi]] to [[Bilming]] in order to conduct a sting operation on the [[Set]]. Finding themselves drawn into a firefight, Marasi ordered Kellen to help organize a retreat. Kellen and Captain [[Blantach]] waited for Marasi's signal to act, but [[Dumad]] upset the plan by [[steel|Pushing]] the trucks they were using as cover away. In the ensuing chaos, it is unknown how Kellen fared.

## Trivia
* She is likely a cameo for Kellyn Neumann, a member of the [[Dragonsteel Entertainment]] team.
