**Masher wasps** are a dangerous type of wasp found on the island of [[Patji (island)|Patji]] on [[First of the Sun]]. They are one of the few creatures that can only be found on Patji, rather then the entire [[Pantheon]]. They generally live inside trees, making small holes in the bark to enter and exit their nest. [[Trapper]]s know to look for wasp paper as an indicator for the presence of such a nest. After coming across a nest, they put a stick inside the hole, so that no wasp can exit the nest. After that, the only danger is the presence of workers already outside the nest.{{book ref|iote|43}}

The sting of a Masher wasp is extremely deadly, and after killing their victim, masher wasps will lay eggs in their corpse. After stinging their prey, the wasp will immediately die. Masher wasps may also steal kills from other nearby predators, such as [[Memist]].{{book ref|iote|43}}
