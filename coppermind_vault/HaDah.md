**HaDah** is a religion from [[Classical Scadrial]], and has been extinct for a thousand years.

HaDah is a religion in the south which worships an agricultural god. [[Sazed]] gives  [[Jed]] a HaDah funeral after learning from [[Teur]] that he was killed by the [[Deepness]].

The HaDah funeral ceremony involves taking a branch and driving it into the bottom of the grave next to the corpse's head, such that once the grave is full, only the tip of the branch breaches the surface.
