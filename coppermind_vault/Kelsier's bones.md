**Kelsier's bones** are the physical remains of [[Kelsier]]. They were utilized by the [[kandra]] [[OreSeur]] and [[TenSoon]] on at least two separate occasions. 

On the first occasion, OreSeur consumes them in order to inspire the [[skaa]] to rebel against the [[Lord Ruler]] as the conclusion of Kelsier's plan to bring down the [[Final Empire]].{{book ref|mb1|35}}

Later, they are used by TenSoon to save remnants of the population in [[Luthadel]] from a [[Ruin]]-influenced [[Penrod]].{{book ref|mb3|52}} The bones were eventually brought to the [[Kandra Homeland]].{{wob ref|7980}}

The remains survived [[Harmony]]'s [[Final Ascension|Ascension]].{{wob ref|5951}}
