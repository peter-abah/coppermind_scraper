**Earth**, sometimes called **Old Earth**, is the home planet of humans in the [[Cytoverse]].

During the early days of humanity's contact with aliens, Earth was ruled by a single institution called the [[United Governments]], aided by the [[United Intelligence Bureau]]. The [[Phone Company]], the telecom that served as the liaisons between humanity and aliens, was also headquartered there. Over time, however, the planet grew largely irrelevant. Nonetheless, both astrophysical data and numerous historical records of Old Earth remain within humanity's and [[Superiority]]'s data banks. 

At some point during the last of the [[Human Wars]], the alien coalition gathered to launch an all-out attack on Earth. However, when they arrived they found that the planet had disappeared completely, leaving only its moon, Luna, behind. The Superiority established the [[Earth Disappearance Station]] on Luna to determine what might have happened to the planet, but despite the combined efforts of its best scientists and licensed [[cytonics]] they could not find any trace of Earth.
