**Earth**, sometimes called **Old Earth**, is the home planet of humans in the [[Cytoverse]].{{book ref|sky1|1}}

During the early days of humanity's contact with aliens, Earth was ruled by a single institution called the [[United Governments]], aided by the [[United Intelligence Bureau]]. The [[Phone Company]], the telecom that served as the liaisons between humanity and aliens, was also headquartered there.{{book ref|elysium}} Over time, however, the planet grew largely irrelevant.{{book ref|sky1|45}} Nonetheless, both astrophysical data and numerous historical records of Old Earth remain within humanity's and [[Superiority]]'s data banks.{{book ref|sky1|37}}{{book ref|sky2|18}} 

At some point during the last of the [[Human Wars]], the alien coalition gathered to launch an all-out attack on Earth. However, when they arrived they found that the planet had disappeared completely, leaving only its moon, Luna, behind. The Superiority established the [[Earth Disappearance Station]] on Luna to determine what might have happened to the planet, but despite the combined efforts of its best scientists and licensed [[cytonics]] they could not find any trace of Earth.{{book ref|sky4|6}}
