**Sebasinar** is a scholar on [[Roshar]]. They are a member of [[Navani Kholin]]'s team in [[Urithiru]] and a colleague of [[Rushu]]'s.

During the [[singer]] occupation of the tower, Sebasinar was one of the abstract thinkers tasked with devising a way to identify hidden [[Fused]].
