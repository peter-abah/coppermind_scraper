**Acis** is an [[Alethi]] soldier in Highmarshal [[Meridas Amaram]]'s army.{{book ref|sa1|47}}

Acis was a member of [[Kaladin's squad]], fighting with them in border skirmishes against Brightlord [[Hallaw]].{{book ref|sa1|47}} He was killed during the attempt on Amaram's life by [[Helaran Davar]], along with [[Toorim]], [[Hamel]], [[Raksha]], [[Navar]] and eleven other members of [[Kaladin]]'s squad.
