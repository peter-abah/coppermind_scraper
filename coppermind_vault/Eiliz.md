**Eiliz** was a kingdom on [[Roshar]] during the [[Desolation]]s of the [[Heraldic Epochs]].{{book ref|sa1|60}} 

In one of Highprince [[Dalinar Kholin]]'s [[Dalinar's visions|visions]], King [[Nohadon]] spoke of Eiliz to Dalinar, who had taken the appearance of [[Karm]]. Nohadon believed that Eiliz, like [[Tarma]], would likely not survive the aftermath of the Desolation as too many of its people had perished.{{book ref|sa1|60}} 

Eiliz was not well known by the [[Era of Solitude]]; Dalinar had never heard of the place.{{book ref|sa1|60}}
