**Lani** is a relation of a grain merchant on [[Roshar]] during the [[Heraldic Epochs]].{{book ref|sa3|103}} They are a contemporary of [[Nohadon]] and are mentioned in a strange vision or nightmare experienced by [[Dalinar Kholin]].{{book ref|sa3|103}}

Nohadon believed that he was overpaying for some exotic grains (although the merchant thought otherwise). Nohadon told the merchant to buy something nice for Lani with the extra [[spheres]].{{book ref|sa3|103}}
