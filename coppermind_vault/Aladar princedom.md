**Aladar princedom** is one of the ten princedoms that form [[Alethkar]].{{map ref|Alethkar}} Their colors are black and maroon.{{book ref|sa3|64}} <!--black with maroon stripes{{wob ref|10274}}/white and dark green{{book ref|sa2|76}}{{disputed|book vs wob}}--> Their symbol is a [[chull]].{{book ref|sa3|40}}

## Geography
It is one of the most northerly princedoms, sitting on the [[Bay of Elibath]]. It is bordered by the princedoms of [[Roion princedom|Roion]] to the east, [[Sadeas princedom|Sadeas]] to the west, [[Vamah princedom|Vamah]], [[Ruthar  princedom|Ruthar]], and [[Kholin princedom|Kholin]] to the south. The [[Windrunner River]] forms the west and south borders. [[Dalilak]] lies on the coast of Aladar.{{map ref|Alethkar}}

## History
After the advent of the [[Everstorm]], [[Kaladin]] left the [[Shattered Plains]] flying toward his family in [[Hearthstone]], but he ran out of [[Stormlight]] somewhere in Aladar princedom and was forced to travel the final ninety miles by foot.{{book ref|sa3|5}} During the initial months of the [[True Desolation]], the princedom was occupied by [[singer]] forces.{{book ref|sa3|121}}
