> Eleven years of war, and nine out of ten people I once ruled are dead. Do we even have kingdoms to lead any longer? Sur is gone, I'm sure of it.
> — [[Nohadon]] to [[Dalinar]]

**Sur** is a kingdom on [[Roshar]] during the [[Desolation]]s. [[Nohadon]] said to [[Dalinar]] in one of his [[Dalinar's visions|visions]] that Sur had almost certainly vanished from the war.

Dalinar had never heard of the place.
