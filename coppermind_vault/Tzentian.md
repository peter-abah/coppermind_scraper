**Tzentian** is a tall officer of Aztek descent.

He is present at the scene of the [[Scribbler]] attack on [[Charles Calloway]], reporting the preliminary assessment of the scene to [[Harding|Inspector Harding]]. He also warns Harding of a [[Rithmatics#Line of Forbiddance|Line of Forbiddance]], which was not removed per Harding's orders, as Harding did not want officers to breach the scene of the crime.
