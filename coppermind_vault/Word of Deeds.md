The **Word of Deeds** is a [[Fused]] on [[Roshar]].

After the fourth and last of the [[Sibling]]'s nodes in [[Urithiru]] was destroyed, [[Raboniel]] sent a guard to tell the Word of Deeds and the [[Night Known]] to come to her. She presumably sought their help in completing the Sibling's corruption.
