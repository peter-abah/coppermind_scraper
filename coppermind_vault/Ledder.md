**Ledder** is a soldier on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa4|22}}

He accompanied [[Adolin]] and [[Shallan]] on their expedition to [[Shadesmar]] on behalf of the [[coalition of monarchs]] in {{Rosharan date|1175}}. When Adolin introduced [[Mayalaran]] to his team of soldiers, Ledder complimented her on her appearance as a [[Shardblade|Blade]].{{book ref|sa4|22}}
