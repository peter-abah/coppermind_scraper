**Waber** is the gardener at the citylord's manor in [[Hearthstone]] on [[Roshar]].

## History
In , Waber was tasked by [[Roshone]] to gather the residents for a full town meeting when [[Amaram]] arrived on his recruitment drive. He and his assistants erected a canopy in the town square.

During the evacuation of Hearthstone to [[Urithiru]] on the *[[Fourth Bridge]]* in , Waber was part of a group that was attacked by [[Lezian]] and a few of his soldiers in order to draw the attention of the [[Windrunners]]. When [[Kaladin]] arrived, the [[singers]] retreated into the manor with [[Godeke]], but they allowed Waber and the others to go free.
