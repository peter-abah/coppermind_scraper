The **lawnarks** are a sapient species originating on the [[Grand Apparatus]]. People generally avoid lawnarks because of their terrible foreign policy and their association with the [[Sleepless]].

## Biology and Appearance
Lawnarks resemble humans, having incredibly similar features. However, instead of hair, lawnarks have something akin to feathers. Their heads are mostly bald, with a single crest of feathers on the very top. Lawnarks have also evolved to grow tiny feathers, almost invisible to the naked eye, on their arms. At least some lawnarks have dark brown skin, like a cool earthy tone. It is unclear if the colors of their feathers differ between lawnarks, but at least one lawnark is known to have yellow and white feathers. When they are worried, a lawnark's feathers often lay flat against their head.

Arcanists have determined that lawnarks didn’t evolve from birds, as many people apparently think. Rather, they were humans who were at some point isolated, and whose hair had evolved into something more akin to feathers. They are unable to fly.
