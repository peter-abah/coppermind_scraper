The **Dragonaut** was a flying vessel made from glass. It is sleek and snake-like, with six wings, six legs, and a long tail.{{book ref|a2|2}} It ran on [[Silimatic]] technology. It crashed after being attacked by an evil [[Librarian]] and then broken by [[Alcatraz]].{{book ref|a2|5}}

Alcatraz and [[Bastille]] later discovered the crashed aircraft on an island near the [[Worldspire]].{{book ref|a6|7}} Alcatraz was able to power it with his [[Oculator|Oculatory Powers]], and escape a hostile [[Gak]] that lived on the island.{{book ref|a6|10}} They ended up crashing the Dragonaut a second time into the [[Worldspire]].{{book ref|a6|11}}
