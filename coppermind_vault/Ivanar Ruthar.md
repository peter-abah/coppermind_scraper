**Ivanar Ruthar** is the son of the former [[Highprince]] [[Ruthar]] on [[Roshar]].

His father was abusive and broke his arm about a month before the [[coalition of monarchs]] recaptured [[Emul]]. Ivanar confided in [[Wit]] about this. Wit later used that information to get Ruthar angry enough to demand trial by combat, enabling [[Jasnah Kholin]] to rid her rule of the last opposing highprince.
