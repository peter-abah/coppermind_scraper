**Stardragons** are a potentially fictitious species known to the peoples of [[Detritus]].

[[Spensa]] believed she had the "heart of a stardragon", an idiom of courage, and Spensa and [[Rig]] often played together as children, pretending to slay stardragons.

There is a [[Stardragon Flight]] in the [[DDF]], and two of the pilots were present in the dorms when Spensa sneaks in.
