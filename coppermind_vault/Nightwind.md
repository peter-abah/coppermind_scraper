**Nightwind** is a fungus on [[First of the Sun]]. It grows on trees on the island of [[Patji (island)|Patji]] and possible other islands of the [[Pantheon]].

When touched, it releases deadly spores as a defense mechanism. This can be stopped by smothering the fungus with water, preventing the spores from escaping. These spores are sometimes cultivated by [[trapper]]s to protect their resources.

[[Dusk]] cultivated a patch of the fungus for years on Patji. While [[Vathi]] made her way towards Dusk's safecamp, she recognized and poured water over it, successfully neutralizing the threat of its spores.

The cave on the [[Dakwara's island]] included nightwind fungus near the entrance, which helped Dusk recognize the pattern to the threats in the cave.
