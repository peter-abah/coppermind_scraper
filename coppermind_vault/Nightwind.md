**Nightwind** is a fungus on [[First of the Sun]]. It grows on trees on the island of [[Patji (island)|Patji]] and possible other islands of the [[Pantheon]].{{book ref|iote|6}}{{book ref|iote|39}}

When touched, it releases deadly spores as a defense mechanism.{{book ref|iote|39}} This can be stopped by smothering the fungus with water, preventing the spores from escaping. These spores are sometimes cultivated by [[trapper]]s to protect their resources.{{book ref|iote|6}}

[[Dusk]] cultivated a patch of the fungus for years on Patji. While [[Vathi]] made her way towards Dusk's safecamp, she recognized and poured water over it, successfully neutralizing the threat of its spores.{{book ref|iote|6}}

The cave on the [[Dakwara's island]] included nightwind fungus near the entrance,{{book ref|iote|39}}{{book ref|iote|43}} which helped Dusk recognize the pattern to the threats in the cave.{{book ref|iote|44}}
