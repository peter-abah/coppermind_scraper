**Ruin's Perpendicularity** was a [[perpendicularity]]  of a reflective black liquid metal{{bws ref|mistborn-2-alternate-ending-part-two|*Well of Ascension* alternate ending part 2|date=Sep 2, 2013}} that belonged to the [[Shard]] [[Ruin]].{{wob ref|5786}}

Ruin's Perpendicularity was a manifestation of his gathered consciousness, much like the darker [[mist]]s.{{wob ref|5786}}

## History
When [[Alendi]] and [[Rashek]] were climbing the [[Terris]] mountains, to the [[Well of Ascension]], they encountered Ruin's Perpendicularity.{{book ref|tfe|29}} Alendi's companion, Fedik showed interest in the pool but Alendi refused to let him take samples.{{epigraph ref|mb1|33}} The [[Leras|mist spirit]] stabbed Fedik shortly afterward, possibly due to his interest in the perpendicularity.

When Rashek took the power at the Well, he moved Ruin's Perpendicularity underground, where it remained at least up until the [[Catacendre]].{{wob ref|5786}} The perpendicularity was moved to the [[Pits of Hathsin]], which is why the crystals produced [[atium]].{{wob ref|5525}} The perpendicularity was used by [[worldhopper]]s in an interplanetary trade system, which was upended when [[Kelsier]] destroyed the atium geodes, to which [[Hoid]] remarked "was fun to watch".{{msh ref|2|1}} The pools underneath the pits no longer exist following the Catacendre,{{wob ref|5600}}, as Sazed moved his Perpendicularity to Malwish land.{{book ref|TLM|50}}

## Trivia
* [[Keep Lekal]] had stained glass windows that depicted Ruin's Perpendicularity.{{book ref|mb1|28}}
* Before [[Brandon]] changed the location of the Well of Ascension to [[Luthadel]], Vin and Elend discovered Ruin's Perpendicularity near the Well.{{bws ref|mistborn-2-alternate-ending-part-two|*Well of Ascension* alternate ending part 2|date=Sep 2, 2013}}
