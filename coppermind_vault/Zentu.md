**Zentu** is a [[dione]] philosopher and scientist and is known for their work on the properties of life.

Their theory claims that for something to be considered *alive* it must have the ability to change over time, the ability to respond to stimuli, and the ability to reproduce. Information on their work can be found in the [[Starsight (station)|Starsight]] information archive.

[[M-Bot]] accesses information on Zentu while debating his own existence with [[Spensa]].

## Trivia
* Currently, there is no clear consensus on the definition of [[wikipedia:Life#Biology|life]]. Change, response, and reproduction are three of the common physiological functions used in biology to describe life, with other common categorizes include; homeostasis, organization, metabolism, and growth.
