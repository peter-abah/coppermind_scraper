**Moelach** is one of the [[Unmade]],{{epigraph ref|sa3|101}} an ancient and terrible [[spren]] of [[Odium]].{{book ref|sa3|62}} Moelach is the source of the [[Death Rattle]]s, visions of the future experienced by people on [[Roshar]] during their final moments of life.{{book ref|sa2|i|14}}{{epigraph ref|sa3|103}} Most recently, Moelach has taken up residence in the [[Horneater Peaks]].{{book ref|sa3|107}}

## Appearance and Personality
Moelach is one of the great Mindless Unmade, like [[Ashertmarn]] and [[Nergaoul]],{{epigraph ref|sa3|104}} more a force or a creature of instinct than a conscious being.{{epigraph ref|sa3|98}} Ominous sounds such as scratching, scraping, and heavy breathing are thought to be signs Moelach is near.{{rpg ref|slwg|97}}

## Attributes and Abilities
{{quote
|Moelach was said to grant visions of the future at different times—but most commonly at the transition point between realms. When a soul was nearing the [[Tranquiline Halls]].
|From [[Hessi]]'s [[Mythica]], page 144{{epigraph ref|sa3|102}}
}}
*See also: [[Death Rattle]]*

Moelach is strongly associated with precognition; he is said to grant visions of the future at various times.{{epigraph ref|sa3|101}}{{epigraph ref|sa3|102}} The most typical of these visions are known as the Death Rattles, wherein people on their deathbed are granted a brief vision of the future,{{epigraph ref|sa3|102}} often speaking aloud in their final moments to communicate what they are seeing.{{book ref|sa1|71}} Moelach's touch seeps into a person's soul as they separate from their body, powering the vision with their death.{{epigraph ref|sa2|82}} This effect is directly associated with proximity to Moelach, the Death Rattles occur only when he is in the region,{{epigraph ref|sa3|103}} although it is uncertain how far this effect extends. Traditionally, these Death Rattles have been ascribed to the [[Almighty]], although some such as [[Hessi]] have figured out their true origin. It has been indicated that he may cause precognition to occur at other times,{{epigraph ref|sa3|102}} but these are as of yet unknown.

## History
{{quote
|Moelach is close. I can hear his wheezing, his scratching, his scraping at time like a rat breaking through walls.
|[[Jezrien]] on Moelach{{book ref|sa3|88}}
}}
Moelach's origin is unknown, it is uncertain when he came into being. He was involved in the past [[Desolation]]s,{{book ref|sa3|77}} although it is uncertain what if any role he played in them. After [[Aharietiam]], Moelach seems to have remained active on Roshar, roving from place to place. It seems that over the centuries Moelach has slumbered at times, during which the Death Rattles cease.{{book ref|sa2|i|14}} They were noted to have returned around around the year 1166.{{book ref|sa1|71}} By that time Moelach was somewhere near [[Kholinar]], as [[Jezrien]] sensed his proximity.{{book ref|sa3|88}} 

By 1173 or so, Moelach was in the vicinity of [[Kharbranth]], where King [[Taravangian]] was systemically killing ill and insignificant people and collecting their Death Rattles.{{book ref|sa1|71}} By late that year, Moelach had begun to move westward, drastically reducing the number of Death Rattles Taravangian could collect.{{book ref|sa2|i|14}} It had settled in the Horneater Peaks by early 1174,{{book ref|sa3|107}} where it possibly has had some deleterious effects on the [[Unkalaki]].{{book ref|sa3|37}} [[Joshor]], one of Taravangian's [[Silent Gatherer]]s,{{book ref|sa1|endnote}} was pursuing it, attempting to gain access to the Death Rattles.{{book ref|sa3|107}}

Moelach was present at the [[Second Battle of Narak]].{{book ref|sa5|74}}{{book ref|sa5|81}}

## Trivia
* As with several other [[Unmade]], Moelach's name is derived from a real-life deity of ancient Canaanite pantheon. In his case, the inspiration behind his name is most likely [[wikipedia:Moloch|Moloch]], a god commonly associated with child sacrifice.{{url ref|url=https://www.17thshard.com/forum/topic/62476-ob-unmade-name-linguistic-origins/|text=Unmade Name Linguistic Origins|site=17th Shard}}
