**Toravi** is a farmer from [[Hearthstone]] in [[Alethkar]] on [[Roshar]].

In Kaladin's childhood, he thought of Toravi as being enormous and thick-muscled. Upon meeting Toravi again in {{Rosharan date|1174}}, Kaladin realized that his youthful perception had been distorted and Toravi was not a particularly large man. Like most of Hearthstone's residents, Toravi did not seem to recognize Kaladin as an adult.{{book ref|sa3|6}}
