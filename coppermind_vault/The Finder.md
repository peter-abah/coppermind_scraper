**The Finder** is a *[[makay-im]]* [[Fused]] on [[Roshar]]. He is curious, menacing and patient. He has long limbs and fingernails that he drags along surfaces to intimidate his prey. He is known to smile wickedly. The Finder was ordered to explore Urithiru to lay the groundwork for [[Occupation of Urithiru|an invasion]]. It seems like he only knows [[Dawnchant]]. While exploring, he stumbled upon a party of [[coalition of monarchs|coalition]] explorers. After using the surge of [[Cohesion]] to block their exit and chasing them for a while, the Finder engaged in combat with them. It is unclear what happened during and after their fight.
