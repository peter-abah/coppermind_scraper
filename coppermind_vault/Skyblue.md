**Skyblue** is a [[chasmfiend]] on [[Roshar]] who allied with the [[listeners]] after the [[War of Reckoning]].

## Appearance and Personality

Very little is known about Skyblue’s appearance beyond what is typical for a chasmfiend--she is a massive crustacean-like [[greatshell]] with a sturdy carapace. She shows enough intelligence to follow specific instructions from the listeners during combat, and is singled out as the chasmfiend who understands not to destroy the humans’ bridge.

## History
Skyblue is part of the group of chasmfiends led by [[Thundercloud]] who decided to make peace with the listeners after the War of Reckoning. She joins the listeners in their deception at the [[Second Battle of Narak]], where she and the other chasmfiends launch a mock assault on the human fortifications. During the battle, Skyblue is specifically tasked with destroying the gate between two plateaus, while carefully leaving the humans’ bridge intact, so that the humans could flee using the Oathgate platform.
