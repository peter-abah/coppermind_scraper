> Oh, Brightlord Tumul! How unexpected it is to see you standing there! I didn't mean to insult your stupidity. Really, it's quite spectacular and worthy of much praise.
> — Wit insulting Tumul

Brightlord **Tumul** is a noble on the [[Shattered Plains]] on [[Roshar]].

He is mocked by [[Wit]] along with Brightlord [[Habatab]], Lord [[Yonatan]] and Lady [[Meirav]]. Wit says that it is so easy to insult Habatab, his mind will be as dull as Tumul's, and Tumul happens to be right there. Wit says Tumul's stupidity is spectacular and worthy of praise.
