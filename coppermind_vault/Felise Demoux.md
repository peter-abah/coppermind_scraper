Lady **Felise Demoux** is a noblewoman living in [[New Seran]] on [[Scadrial]].{{book ref|mb6|12}}

## History
Lady Demoux was presumably a descendant of Captain [[Demoux]], a member of the [[skaa rebellion]] in the [[Final Empire]].{{book ref|mb1|21}} Her only known family was a nephew.{{book ref|mb6|12}}

Lady Demoux kept several prize-winning hounds.{{book ref|mb6|12}}

Lady Demoux and her nephew attended a party held by Lady [[Kelesina Shores]] in the year 342.{{book ref|mb6|12}} Lord [[Waxillium Ladrian]] requested to dance with her by giving an introduction card to her nephew. Before they could begin dancing, [[Khriss]] cut in between them, dancing with Wax to ask him about his abilities. After Khriss left, Waxillium found Lady Demoux and apologized to her profusely. Lady Demoux allowed him the next dance with her, and spent the whole time telling him about her hounds.
