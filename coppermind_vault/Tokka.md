**Tokka** are a dangerous type of carnivorous plant found on the [[Pantheon]] islands on [[First of the Sun]]. They have teeth as long as a human arm, and they hunt by building a facade for themselves using mucus, which they match to the colorings of their surroundings. After a creature walks into their range, which can be up to four feet away, they extend their jaws and bite it, holding on and waiting for the creature to die. After each bite, they take about an hour to reset.{{book ref|iote|44}}

[[Trapper]]s know to look out for bare sections of ground or stone with mounds rising on it, as this indicates the presence of a Tokka.{{book ref|iote|44}} Some places have entire fields full of Tokka.{{book ref|iote|12}}
