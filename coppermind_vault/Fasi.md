**Fasi** is an [[Azish]] historian on [[Roshar]]. She has umber skin and ties her hair up with a golden ribbon. She is said to laugh often.{{rpg ref|ss|7}} Sometime during the [[True Desolation]], Fasi paid fifty marks and boarded a ship called the *[[Dalewillow (ship)|Dalewillow]]*.{{rpg ref|ss|8}} During the voyage, she tried to surprise her lover, [[Nenal]], with a proposal using a stack of heartfelt letters. Nenal in turn also attempted to surprise Fasi with a proposal.{{rpg ref|ss|7}} It is unclear if Nenal’s proposal succeeded, but if it didn’t, Fasi proposed to her lover. The two are known to have been engaged by midnight.{{rpg ref|ss|8}}
