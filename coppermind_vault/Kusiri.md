**Kusiri** is a woman from [[Roshar]]. She is presumably from the same town as [[Cenn]] in [[Alethkar]].

Cenn compared [[Kaladin]]'s seething hatred towards [[lighteyes]] to his mother's anger at Kusiri after she ran off with the cobbler's son. It is likely that Kusiri was in Cenn's family or otherwise close to his mother, since the mere mention of her name evoked a strong reaction.
