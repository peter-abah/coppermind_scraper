**Kusiri** is a woman from [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa1|1}} She is presumably from the same town as [[Cenn]] in [[Alethkar]].{{book ref|sa1|1}}

Cenn compared [[Kaladin]]'s seething hatred towards [[lighteyes]] to his mother's anger at Kusiri after she ran off with the cobbler's son.{{book ref|sa1|1}} It is likely that Kusiri was in Cenn's family or otherwise close to his mother, since the mere mention of her name evoked a strong reaction.
