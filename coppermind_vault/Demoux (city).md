**Demoux** is a city in the [[Elendel Basin]]. It is in the south of the Basin, about twenty-five miles from the [[Sea of Lennes]].{{map ref|Elendel Basin}}{{ref|text=It is only noted in the map of the Basin in *[[The Lost Metal]]*, and can be presumed to have reached a certain threshold during the seven years between *[[The Bands of Mourning]]* and *The Lost Metal* -- enough to be featured on the map.}} Wax reads an article about it in a broadsheet when he is searching for the location of the [[Set]]'s [[harmonium]] bomb tests around the month of [[Scadrial#Calendar|Marewill]], {{date|348|PC}}.{{book ref|mb7|19}}{{file ref|The_Two_Seasons_broadsheet.jpg|*The Two Seasons* broadsheet}}

It is named after the [[Originators|Originator]] [[Demoux]].

## History
In {{date|348|PC}}, the town suffered a series of broken windows, most likely caused by a small tornado.{{book ref|mb7|19}}
