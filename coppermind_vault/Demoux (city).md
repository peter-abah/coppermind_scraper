**Demoux** is a city in the [[Elendel Basin]]. It is in the south of the Basin, about twenty-five miles from the [[Sea of Lennes]]. Wax reads an article about it in a broadsheet when he is searching for the location of the [[Set]]'s [[harmonium]] bomb tests around the month of [[Scadrial#Calendar|Marewill]], .

It is named after the [[Originators|Originator]] [[Demoux]].

## History
In , the town suffered a series of broken windows, most likely caused by a small tornado.
