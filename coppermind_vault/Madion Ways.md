**Madion Ways** is the richest area in the First Octant of [[Elendel]]. [[House Tekiel]] owned much of the land there.{{book ref|mb4|16}}

[[Wayne]] once imitated an accent from Madion Ways to escape from a guard, when he tried to get information on the [[Breaknaught]].{{book ref|mb4|16}}
