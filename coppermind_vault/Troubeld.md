**Troubeld** is the author of *Arts of Scholarship* in the [[Final Empire]] on [[Scadrial]].

His work was discussed by [[Elend]] during the [[Siege of Luthadel]]. He told [[Vin]] that he read all three volumes during their trip escaping the city.{{book ref|mb2|50}}
