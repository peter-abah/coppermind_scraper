**Mrs. Emuishere** is on the staff of [[Armedius Academy]].

She is of Egyptian descent. She tells [[Joel]] about the disappearance of [[Charles Calloway]] and the deaths of his servants. She speculates with him that the deaths were caused by wild [[chalklings]].
