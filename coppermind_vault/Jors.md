**Jors** is a guard in [[Igneous Cavern]].{{book ref|Skyward|45}}

It is likely that he guards the entrance which [[Spensa]] uses to get between Igneous and the caverns where she hunts rats, along with Aluko.{{book ref|sky1|1}} Neither he nor [[Aluko]] recognize her when she returns in her pilot's uniform, and they salute her as she passes.{{book ref|Skyward|45}}
