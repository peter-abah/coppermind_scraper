The **mainland** is a landmass (presumably a continent) on [[First of the Sun]], a planet in the [[Drominad System]].

## Geography and Ecology
The mainland lies somewhere to the south of the [[homeisles]], but within sailing distance. It is said to be a frozen wasteland. The mainland has animal species that differ from those found in the homeisles or [[Pantheon]]; [[Vathi]] is able to immediately identify [[Sak]] (a raven) as a mainlander bird, noting physical characteristics such as her black color, sharp beak, and sleek feathers. It is not clear how birds survive in the harsh environment of the mainland, if they still live there.

## History
Little is known about the history of the mainland. There are [[Eelakin]] stories of vast cities and wars that some claim must have occurred on the mainland, since the Eelakin are not thought to be native to the homeisles and the mainland is the nearest large landmass. However, the Eelakin have explored at least some of the mainland, and they have discovered no archaeological evidence to support this theory. Scientists such as [[Ruen]] believe that the mainland has never been inhabited, and that the Eelakin originated elsewhere.

## Trivia
*In the original standalone version of *[[Sixth of the Dusk (novella)|Sixth of the Dusk]]*, the mainland was inhabited, and the people there had regular contact with the Eelakin. The nature of the continent was changed significantly for *Isles of the Emberdark*, and it no longer seems hospitable to flora and fauna. This caused some fans to question the origin of bird species such as Sak's, but Brandon has confirmed that they are native to the mainland.
