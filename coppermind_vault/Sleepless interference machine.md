A **Sleepless interference machine** is a device created by [[Malwish Empire|Malwish]] scholars. It is used to protect an area from [[Sleepless]]. It does this by creating an interference cloud, which makes a Sleepless' consciousness fall apart. It doesn't directly harm them however, and a Sleepless will be able to regain consciousness as soon as the cloud is turned off. It is said to work somewhat like a mindshield created by an [[Aviar]], albeit far more potent.

Additionally, an interference cloud has a number of other effects; like blocking a [[Dragon (cosmere)|dragon]]'s ability to contact others of their kind and interfering with all kinds of systems, such as FTL communication. A Sleepless interference machine generally assumes the form of a large bulb, with disks on its front. These machines are said to be fairly new as of the discovery of [[Patji's Eye]] by the Malwish.
