> The sun hadn't quite set, but in the darkening sky, stars had begun to appear around Taln’s Scar. The Tear hung just above the horizon, a star much brighter than the others, named for the single tear that Reya was said to have shed.
> — [[Kaladin]]'s thoughts while looking at the sky

The **Tear** is a particularly bright star seen from [[Roshar]]. It is named for the single tear shed by [[Reya]]. In early [[Roshar#Calendar|Ishi]], it can be seen around sunset from the [[Shattered Plains]], where it appears just above the horizon. It may also be visible from [[Scadrial]].
