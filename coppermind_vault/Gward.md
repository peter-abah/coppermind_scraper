**Gward** is the leader of a faction of pirates in the [[belt]] of the [[nowhere]].{{book ref|sky3|29}}

## History
Gward attended the duel to determine the pirate champion, along with the other faction leaders. After the duel, when [[Peg]] proposed a unified assault on the [[Superiority]] base of [[Surehold]], Gward was initially skeptical, not wanting to have the same discussion again. When [[Semm]] and [[Gremm]] eventually agreed to join the assault, Gward reluctantly did as well. Her faction needed the reality ashes that could be obtained at Surehold, and she confirmed that the factions would split the spoils evenly.{{book ref|sky3|29}} With the help of Gward and his faction, the assault succeeded, and they took control of Surehold.{{book ref|cytonic|35}}
