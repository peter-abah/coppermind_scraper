**Mandras** are a type of [[spren]] on [[Roshar]]. In the [[Physical Realm]] they are called **luckspren**,{{ref|name=Mandras}}{{ref|name=Skyeel}}{{book ref|sa3|99}} **chasmspren**{{book ref|sa4|45}} or **apaliki'tokoa'a**.{{book ref|sa3.5|9}} In [[Shadesmar]], they are used for transportation.{{book ref|sa3|99}}

==Appearance==
{{image|Mandras.jpg|Sketches of Mandras from [[Shallan's sketchbook]]|side=left|height=300px}}
{{for|/Gallery|more images}}
===Shadesmar===
Mandras have triangular shaped heads,{{book ref|sa3|97}} which are the exact same shape as those that are found near [[greatshell]]s in the Physical Realm.{{ref|name=Mandras}} They come in a variety of sizes;{{ref|name=Mandras}} the ones used for pulling ships in Shadesmar are much larger than luckspren that appear in the Physical Realm.{{book ref|sa3|99}} Their bodies are long, sinuous{{book ref|sa3|97}}{{book ref|sa3|99}} with multiple sets of legs{{ref|name=Mandras}} and multiple sets of undulating wings along their bodies.{{book ref|sa3|97}} The wings move at a regular rhythm.{{ref|name=Mandras}}

===Physical Realm===
Luckspren have the appearance of tiny, blue, arrowhead-like fish that dart around the creature they're swarming in the general direction of its travel.{{book ref|sa2|70}} The shape of their heads correspond to the triangular heads of the Mandras.{{book ref|sa3|99}} Luckspren are often found near greatshells, [[chasmfiend]]s, [[skyeel]]s, anything that is lighter than it appears to be.{{book ref|sa3|99}} They appear in swarms around Skyeels.{{ref|name=Skyeel}} When they disappear from the physical realm it is like a small plume of smoke rising into the air.{{book ref|sa2|70}}

==Abilities==
Mandras are the spren who bond with greatshells, chasmfiends, and [[larkin]], preventing them from being crushed under their own weight. This was discovered by [[Bavamar]] with mathematical calculations.{{book ref|sa3|99}} Mandras from different locations can be different sizes and strengths, and a greatshell may need to return multiple times during their lifecycle to bond additional mandras so they can continue growing.{{book ref|sa3.5|19}} Mandras seem to avoid the rules of physics{{ref|name=Mandras}} and their wings are not the source of their locomotion.{{ref|name=Mandras}} They can be pushed to exhaustion.{{book ref|sa3|110}}

{{image|Chasmfiend Connor Chamberlain.jpg|Mandras flying around a [[chasmfiend]]|side=right|width=300px}}

Mandras are seen to be used in Shadesmar to pull ships by Reachers,{{book ref|sa3|99}} [[honorspren]],{{book ref|sa3|102}}{{book ref|sa3|108}} and most likely any other ship crew as every ship in Celebrant has them.{{book ref|sa3|101}}{{book ref|sa3|102}} They are harnessed with a set of pulleys on either side of their body. To steer them in either direction the sailors shorten one of the pulleys and tie it off on cleats connected to the sides of the ship.{{ref|name=Mandras}} The Mandras hooked to the honorspren ship have golden harnesses{{book ref|sa3|102}} and they employ more than just two, having Mandras attached to wings on the side of their ship.{{book ref|sa3|108}}

Mandras have a tendency to vanish, called "dropping". This occurs when they get pulled into the Physical Realm.{{book ref|sa3|99}} They cannot be removed from human population centres for long as they begin to waste away and die. Why this happens is unknown to the [[Reacher]]s who use them to pull their ships.{{book ref|sa3|99}}

Because of Mandras' tendency to "drop" the sailors always employ at least two Mandras{{book ref|sa3|101}} so they do not end up stranded.{{book ref|sa3|99}} Once the ships have docked the Mandras are detached from the ship using long hooks and lead away.{{book ref|sa3|101}} [[Adolin]] wonders if they are attached to the stern of the ship to pull them from the docks{{book ref|sa3|101}} but given that Adolin is [[Alethi]], a people who are infamous for knowing little about sailing,{{book ref|sa3|12}} this is to be taken with a grain of salt.

Mandras do not seem to like fire. Their sailors try to control and move them away when [[Ico]]'s ship is burning.{{book ref|sa3|102}}

Additionally, mandras may also be the same as gravitationspren and may form [[Skybreaker]] [[Shardplate]].{{ref|group=fn|name=MandrasGravitationspren|text=In 2023, Brandon said that Skybreaker Plate is made of gravitationspren,{{wob ref|16262}} but the spren playing cards (which include the canonical armor spren for other orders) depict mandras for Skybreaker Plate.{{wob ref|16808}} It is unclear whether one of these sources is inaccurate or if these are simply different names for the same type of spren.}}

==Mythology==
Legends tell of luckspren leading to treasure.{{book ref|sa3.5|19}}
