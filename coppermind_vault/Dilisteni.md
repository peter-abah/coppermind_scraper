**Dilisteni** was an author during the [[Final Empire]] on [[Scadrial]].

He wrote the book *Trials of Monument*, which [[Elend Venture]] read at the ball when he first met [[Vin]]. The book was quite famous in the Final Empire, and there was a copy in the Keep Orielle's library in [[Fadrex City]], which Elend borrowed when he and Vin attended a ball at the keep while they had the city under siege.
