**Dia Vmeer** is a student in [[Spensa]]'s class and the daughter of Mrs. [[Vmeer]].{{book ref|Skyward|2}}

She has dark hair and is seventeen.{{book ref|Skyward|2}}

Dia is a bully who often picks on [[Spensa]]. Dia makes a habit of trying to goad the other girl, often by taking jabs at Spensa's [[Zeen Nightshade|father]], and can be quite vicious with her remarks. Spensa intensely dislikes Dia, to the point of wishing to punch her.{{book ref|Skyward|2}}

Dia is aware that the children of [[First Citizen]]'s automatically pass the entrance exam to [[Flight School]].{{book ref|Skyward|2}} 

Dia joined the Administration Corp once she finished school, a job secured for her by her father.{{book ref|Skyward|2}}
