The **Roughs** are the frontier regions just outside the [[Elendel Basin]] on [[Scadrial]]. They are separated from the fertile basin by mountain ranges, and are divided into the **Northern Roughs** and the **Southern Roughs** (with the [[Irongate River]] as the approximate dividing line). 

The ideal conditions in the Basin meant that there was little pressure to expand following the founding of [[Elendel]]. As a result of this, the Roughs were sparsely populated and less advanced than the urban centers even generations later.

A political movement once began to make the Roughs a province within the Elendel Basin with political representation. Others within the Roughs instead favored the idea of being their own nation. The many railroad and mining companies in the Roughs often post bounties on criminals, and some people make a living from these bounties. While the Roughs are technologically behind compared to the Basin, it is quickly modernising.

## Geography and Ecology
The Roughs consist of vast dry and dirty plains. The Roughs experience harsh sunlight. Trees here are scraggly and short, as are many plants. Many plants are also prickly. The Roughs contain little large fauna, but they do have lions, giraffes and gazelles.

## Culture
The men and women living in the Roughs are very different from those in the Basin, although many of them lived in the Basin at some point. It is likely that the people of the Roughs speak Skaa, although they have a Roughs accent. They are not terribly hygienic. The people have a type of fatalism to them, they distrust authority and often shun lawmen, even if that means letting criminals go about their business. They also smoke many cigars. The poor people mostly eat beans and oatmeal, and the Roughs is home to a kind of fruit that has a tough shell, but is all gooey inside. People in the Roughs have stories about dragons. The Roughs are also home to multiple Koloss clans.

> If you want to know a man, dig in his firepit
> — A Roughs saying, likely [[Koloss]] in origin.

Men and women often wear dusters, which may be decorated with patterns of colors, such as a white duster reddened at the bottom. Hats from the Roughs, worn by both men and women, are often broad-brimmed. They also wear thick Roughs trousers and buttoned shirts. It seems that the Roughs’ people follow the same religions as the Basin’s, including the Path and the church of the survivor. Weddings are often hosted in a simple wooden chapel. Theater shows are often dirty and not proper at all.
