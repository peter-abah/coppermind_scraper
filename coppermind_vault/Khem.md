**Khem** is a resident of [[Hornhollow]] in [[Alethkar]] on [[Roshar]].

He was in town when [[Kaladin]] visited the area after the coming of the [[Everstorm]]. Khem was hurt when a group of around fifty [[singer]]s in [[warform]] raided Hornhollow's grain stores, and he later complained about a ringing in his ear that wouldn't go away. He reported to a local [[ardent]] that he got a good view at one of the singers before being struck down, and that their eyes did not appear to be glowing red.
