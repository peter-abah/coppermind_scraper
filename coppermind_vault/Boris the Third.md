**Boris the Third** is a resident of the [[Roughs]] on [[Scadrial]]. He is not very clever. His brother is [[Boris Junior]].{{book ref|mb5|2}}

[[Waxillium Ladrian]] shot Boris' father twice, after which Boris swore he would eat Wax's toes. Lady [[Steris Harms]] found record of the incident in the public records regarding Wax's exploits and contemplated inviting the Boris brothers to their wedding ceremony. She was compiling a list of his old enemies with the intent of inviting one to the ceremony so that she could prepare for the disturbance.{{book ref|mb5|2}}
