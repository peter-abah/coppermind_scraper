**Den** is a member of the underworld in [[Urithiru]] on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa4|26}}

He was part of a group of old acquaintances who approached [[Ishnah]] asking for money once she began drawing a stipend as a [[Order of Lightweavers|Lightweaver]], supposedly to help get them out of the criminal life. Instead, they used her funds to buy gambling dens, hire goons, and set up a protection racket. Den threatened an unknown man in the course of their schemes.{{book ref|sa4|26}}
