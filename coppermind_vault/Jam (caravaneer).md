**Jam** is a caravaneer in [[Alethkar]] on [[Roshar]].

[[Guff]] says that [[Moash]] was brought to the [[Shattered Plains]] by Jam's caravan.

It is not clear if this is the same as the person named [[Jam (Hearthstone)|Jam]] from [[Hearthstone]].
