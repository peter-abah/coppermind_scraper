**Iald** is a port city in [[Arelon]] on the planet of [[Sel]]. It is located in the [[Arelon#Plantations|Ial Plantation]], and is adjacent to the Nae Plantation.

During [[Iadon]]'s reign, Iald is the second wealthiest city in the country, behind only [[Kae]]. [[Roial]] is the governor of Iald before the [[Reod]], and after the catastrophe, Roial's holdings include the city, making him fabulously wealthy.
