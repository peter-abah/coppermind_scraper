> Adolin was confronted by a figure in glistening black Shardplate, a large hammer strapped to its back. The helm had stylized eyebrows like knives sweeping backward, and the Plate was skirted with a triangular pattern of interlocking scales.
> — [[Adolin]]'s impression of Cvaderln.

**Cvaderln** is a set of [[Shardplate]] held by the kingdom of [[Thaylenah]].

It is [[Thaylen]] for "shell of Cva" and is painted gloss black. The helmet has Thaylen eyebrows which are stylized into backwards-pointing knives, and the legs are covered by a scale armor skirt. 

During the [[Battle of Thaylen Field]], [[Hrdalm]] wears Cvaderln, armed with a large warhammer. He fought the [[thunderclast]] [[Kai-garnis]] and some [[Fused]] along with support from [[Adolin]], who lent him [[Maya]] for a time. At one point his helm cracked but he continues to fight, and helps [[Renarin]] remove all of Kai-garnis' limbs before [[Renarin]] forces her to leave.
