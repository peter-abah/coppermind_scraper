The **17th Shard** is the official [[Brandon Sanderson]] fansite dedicated to all of the works of fantasy author Brandon Sanderson. 

Founded by Eric Lake, Josh and Mi'chelle Walker as **Hoid's Compendium**, the site reopened in the summer of 2010 as the 17th Shard. The name was recommended by Brandon upon his discovery and blessing of the site.

## Sites
;[https://www.17thshard.com/forum/ Forums]: For discussing and theorizing Brandon's works
;[[Project:About|The Coppermind]]: A one-stop comprehensive resource for Brandon, his books, and the [[cosmere]]
;[[Arcanum]]: An archive of Q&As, interviews, and other Words of Brandon.
;[https://www.17thshard.com/news/shardcast/ Shardcast]: A podcast hosted by 17th Shard
;[https://discord.gg/17thShard 17th Shard Discord]: A Discord chatroom for general discussion of things relating to Brandon Sanderson (note: the [https://discord.gg/zyJMB2d Coppermind Discord] has a separate server)
;[https://twg.17thshard.com TWG]: Archive of the [[Timewasters Guide]] forum
