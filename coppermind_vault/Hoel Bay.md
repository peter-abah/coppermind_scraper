**Hoel Bay** is a body of water north of [[Alethkar]] on [[Roshar]].{{map ref|Alethkar}}

Hoel Bay borders the Alethi princedom of [[Roion princedom|Roion]], as well as the [[Unclaimed Hills]].{{map ref|Alethkar}} The Alethi city of [[Varikev]] sits on a peninsula on the southern coast of the bay. Hoel Bay feeds into the [[Nightstream Sea]].
