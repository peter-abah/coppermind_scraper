**Hoel Bay** is a body of water north of [[Alethkar]] on [[Roshar]].

Hoel Bay borders the Alethi princedom of [[Roion princedom|Roion]], as well as the [[Unclaimed Hills]]. The Alethi city of [[Varikev]] sits on a peninsula on the southern coast of the bay. Hoel Bay feeds into the [[Nightstream Sea]].
