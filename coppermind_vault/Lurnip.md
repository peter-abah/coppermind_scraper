**Lurnips** are a type of plant native to [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa1|29}}

Lurnips are green and cultivated as a vegetable.{{book ref|sa1|29}} They may be related to the similarly named [[curnip]]s, an edible species of [[rockbud]].{{book ref|sa1|19}}

==History==
[[Jasnah]] ate a lurnip as part of a women's vegetable stew during a lunch meeting with [[Taravangian]] in [[Kharbranth]].{{book ref|sa1|31}}

In [[Emul]], there was a lurnip famine a few decades before the year {{Rosharan date|1173}}, implying that they are a staple food in the region. [[Av]]'s father went to the [[Nightwatcher]] because his family was starving due to the famine.{{book ref|sa1|i|7}}
