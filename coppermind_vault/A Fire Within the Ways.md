***A Fire Within the Ways*** is a deleted sequence of scenes from *[[A Memory of Light]]*, the final book in [[The Wheel of Time]] series. It was published in the *[[Wikipedia: Unfettered (anthology)|Unfettered III]]* anthology. Unlike *[[River of Souls]]*, *A Fire Within the Ways* is considered non-canon.
