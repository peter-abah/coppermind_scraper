The **God Beyond** or the **Unknown God** is a god that is worshipped on multiple [[Cosmere#Planets|planets]] in the [[cosmere]]. It is a belief in a greater God of the universe beyond entities like Shards, and presumably Adonalsium itself. It is unknown what relationship the God Beyond has to [[Adonalsium]] or to the [[Beyond]], although it is presumably held to be greater than Adonalsium.

## Mentions of the religion
* There were no mentions of the God Beyond prior to the Shattering.

* On [[Scadrial]], [[Wayne]] and [[Lessie]] mention the God Beyond in passing. Wayne believes the God Beyond to have directly made the world and the people in it.

* On [[Roshar]], Hoid refers to the God Beyond in a parable while talking with Shallan. [[Dalinar]] also casually mentions the God Beyond while talking to [[Kaladin]]. [[Nomad]] states that he believes in the God Beyond while on [[Canticle]], he believes it to be separate from Adonalsium.

* On [[Sel]], it is referred to as the Unknown God by the [[MaiPon]], although this is indeed the same as the God Beyond. [[Wan ShaiLu]] prays to the Unknown God, and her people relate it to the rocks that fell from the sky. Additionally, while attempting to become an [[Elantrian]] at [[Devotion's Perpendicularity]], [[Hoid]] thinks that the lights of the restored city of [[Elantris (city)|Elantris]] seem to ascend "to the Unknown God's domain itself."

* On [[Threnody]], [[Silence Montane]] and her daughter [[William Ann]] swear to the God Beyond.

## Trivia
* The relation between the [[Beyond]] and the God Beyond, like most questions about the Beyond, is currently [[RAFO]]'d.
* Brandon Sanderson has compared the God Beyond to various [[wikipedia:Gnosticism|Gnostic]] interpretations of God, likely referring to the [[wikipedia:Monad (Gnosticism)|Monad]]. 
* According to Silence Montane, the symbol for the religion worshipping the God Beyond is a silver dagger.
