**Doral** is a bridgeman on [[Roshar]]. At some point during the [[War of Reckoning]], Doral was assigned to [[Bridge Nine]]. Somehow, Doral survived for over a year, making him the oldest bridgeman he knows. Sometime while he was fifty-six, he was part of an expedition to research a group of recently uncovered ruins. The expedition was surprised by a [[chasmfiend]] while crossing a bridge, and Doral was thrown into the chasm when the bridge broke. He survived the fall though, leaving only with a broken leg.{{rpg ref|b9|9}} The group of survivors quickly decided that they should leave the wreckage, lest they’d get killed by the chasmfiend after it returned. Captain [[Selinar]] drew his sword and moved towards Doral, telling everyone that they’d leave the old bridgeman as bait. It is not known if Doral survived the encounter.{{rpg ref|b9|12}}
