**Brigs Wennington** is a business in [[Elendel]] that sells electric lights.{{file ref|Elendel_Daily_broadsheet.png|*The Elendel Daily* broadsheet}}

They are located at 4567 Cunning Place in the fifth Octant of Elendel. They advertise in *[[The Elendel Daily]]* telling potential customers to send them a runner with the number of rooms they want lit for an estimate.{{file ref|Elendel_Daily_broadsheet.png|*The Elendel Daily* broadsheet}}
