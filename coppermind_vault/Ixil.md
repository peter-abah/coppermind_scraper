Brother **Ixil** is a an [[ardent]] in [[Kharbranth]] on [[Roshar]].

He is implied to be a high-ranking ardent as he and king [[Taravangian]] are mentioned as overseeing the ardentia together. He believes [[Kabsal]] to be on cataloging duty when in fact he is having a picnic with [[Shallan]]. When [[Jasnah]] investigates Kabsal's assassination attempt, none of the ardents actually remember where he came from.
