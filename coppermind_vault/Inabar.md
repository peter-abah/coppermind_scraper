**Inabar** is an [[artifabrian]] on [[Roshar]].

She worked at [[Urithiru]] under [[Navani Kholin]]'s direction, but was in the room with the [[Sibling]]'s pillar when [[Raboniel]]'s forces invaded the tower and was killed.
