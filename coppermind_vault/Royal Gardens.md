The **Royal Gardens** is a public park in the capitol of [[Nalhalla]], across the street from the palace. [[Folsom Smedry]] and [[Himalaya Smedry|Himalaya Rockies]] have a picnic there a short time before they're married by [[Alcatraz]]. It is a large, open field of grass dotted with beds of flowers. [[Sing Sing Smedry|Sing]] says that it is "not a park," although he also says "nevermind" afterward. It seems to be a common place to go for picnics.
