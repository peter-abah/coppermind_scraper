**Shiversting** is an [[Epic]]. She had the ability to shatter metal objects, and possibly other things. The power leaves a slight black discoloration on the shattered pieces. With [[Parik]]'s help, [[Lifeforce]] uses Shiversting's DNA to steal her power. Lifeforce later uses Shiversting's power to shatter [[Jax (Reckoners)|Jax]]'s [[Shiloh|sword]].{{book ref|lux|25}} The power caused black veins to spread throughout the sword before it shattered.{{book ref|lux|22}}
