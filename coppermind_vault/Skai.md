**Skai** was the original [[Vessel]] of the [[Shard]] [[Dominion]]. Sometime after the [[Shattering]], she and [[Aona]] took up residence on [[Sel]]. [[Edgli]] believes that, by doing so, they violated the original Vessels' pact to not settle on the same world as another Shard, though the exact terms of that agreement are somewhat unclear.

## Abilities and Attributes
Skai was a [[Shard]] of [[Adonalsium]], making her one of the most powerful entities in the [[cosmere]]. Prior to being splintered she was immortal, unaging, and incorporeal. The [[Dor]] is her and Aona's Shards pushed into the Cognitive Realm by Odium; it fuels the magics [[AonDor]], [[Dakhor]], [[ChayShan]], [[Forgery]], and [[Bloodsealing]].

## History
Nothing is known about Skai's origins and life prior to the [[Shattering]], other than that she came from [[Yolen]]. For unknown reason, she and a group of others broke the [[cosmere]]'s creator deity, [[Adonalsium]], into sixteen pieces. Of those sixteen, she took up [[Dominion]], and departed alongside [[Devotion]] for [[Sel]].

Odium manipulated her into conflict with Aona and finished her off while she was weakened from it.

## Trivia
* Although Skai was often assumed to be male in the fandom, Brandon currently plans for Skai's pronouns to be she/her, though that is subject to change until canonized in a book.
