**Skai** was the original [[Vessel]] of the [[Shard]] [[Dominion]].{{wob ref|783}} Sometime after the [[Shattering]], she and [[Aona]] took up residence on [[Sel]].{{epigraph ref|sa1|22}} [[Edgli]] believes that, by doing so, they violated the original Vessels' pact to not settle on the same world as another Shard,{{epigraph ref|sa3|37}}{{epigraph ref|sa3|39}} though the exact terms of that agreement are somewhat unclear.{{wob ref|10835}}

## Abilities and Attributes
Skai was a [[Shard]] of [[Adonalsium]], making her one of the most powerful entities in the [[cosmere]]. Prior to being splintered she was immortal, unaging, and incorporeal. The [[Dor]] is her and Aona's Shards pushed into the Cognitive Realm by Odium; it fuels the magics [[AonDor]], [[Dakhor]], [[ChayShan]], [[Forgery]], and [[Bloodsealing]].{{wob ref|9830}}{{wob ref|4090}}

## History
Nothing is known about Skai's origins and life prior to the [[Shattering]], other than that she came from [[Yolen]].{{wob ref|7939}} For unknown reason, she and a group of others broke the [[cosmere]]'s creator deity, [[Adonalsium]], into sixteen pieces.{{msh ref|3|2}} Of those sixteen, she took up [[Dominion]], and departed alongside [[Devotion]] for [[Sel]].{{au ref|Sel}}

Odium manipulated her into conflict with Aona and finished her off while she was weakened from it.{{book ref|sa5|113}}
