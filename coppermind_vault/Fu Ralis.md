**Fu Ralis** is a [[Purelake]] settlement on [[Roshar]] during the [[Era of Solitude]].

The buildings of the village were likely constructed so that their floors were submerged by several inches of water.

In 1173, [[Ishikk]] searched for [[Hoid]] in Fu Ralis for several members of the [[Seventeenth Shard]].
