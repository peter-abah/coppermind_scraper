**Huisi** is an orphaned urchin on [[Roshar]]. She lives in the [[Stump]]'s care at [[Tashi's Light Orphanage]] in [[Yeddaw]].

She has trouble speaking. [[Lift]] implies that she once blamed Huisi for some missing cookies because Huisi has a tendency to snore.
