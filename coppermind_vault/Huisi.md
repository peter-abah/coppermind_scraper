**Huisi** is an orphaned urchin on [[Roshar]]. She lives in the [[Stump]]'s care at [[Tashi's Light Orphanage]] in [[Yeddaw]].{{book ref|Edgedancer|20}}

She has trouble speaking.{{book ref|Edgedancer|20}} [[Lift]] implies that she once blamed Huisi for some missing cookies because Huisi has a tendency to snore.{{book ref|Edgedancer|20}}
