**Holmes** is a citizen of [[Diggen's Point]] on [[Lumar]]. He is a baker and is a kind man.

Holmes used to save the ends of loaves of bread to sell very cheaply to [[Tress]]. He invited Tress and her [[Ulba|mother]] up onto the steps of his house so that they could have a good view of the return of the [[Duke]] the day that he introduced [[Dirk]] and the [[Princess of Dormancy]].
