**Cecil G. Bagsworth III** is the author of several books about interdimensional travel, as well as the editor of [[Alcatraz Smedry]]'s autobiographies. In addition, he is the editor of [[bws:a-night-of-blacker-darkness|*A Night of Blacker Darkness*]].

## Appearance and Personality
Bagsworth loves truth and enjoys his adventures through the multiverse, but he also finds the breadth of knowledge isolating.

## History
At some point, Bagsworth participated in the [[Micronization War]]. In 2085, the year after the invention of interdimensional travel, Bagsworth was recruited for a government-sponsored Middle Ages expedition. There, he won a jousting tournament, introduced electricity, and earned the trust of Henry II. Eventually, Bagsworth founded [[Frugal Wizard Inc.]] and wrote several books about interdimensional wizardry.
