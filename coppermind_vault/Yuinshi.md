**Yuinshi** is a [[nightmare]] [[painter]] in [[Kilahito]] on [[Komashi]]. When [[Nikaro]] was looking for help in the anticipated [[battle of Kilahito]], [[Tojin]] proposed asking Yuinshi to join them. Tojin figured that Yuinshi would get a laugh out of the situation, and that he could invite some others.{{book ref|yumi|38}}
