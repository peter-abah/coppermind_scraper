Old **Jell** was a [[skaa]] in [[Urbene]]. He was said to be a bull-headed one.{{book ref|mb2|15}}

After the [[Collapse]] and mists grew stronger, the [[mist]] killed Jell when he tried to leave the village, since he would starve there because of the growing [[Deepness]].{{book ref|mb2|15}}
