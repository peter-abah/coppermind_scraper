**Hathan** is a [[master-servant]] and [[Dalinar Kholin]]'s room steward in [[Kholinar]] on [[Roshar]].

==Appearance and Personality==
He is short. As a master-servant, he is presumably [[darkeyes|darkeyed]] and wears a uniform.

==History==
Hathan served as Dalinar and [[Evi]]'s room steward a few years after their marriage, when Evi was pregnant with [[Adolin]]. In , when Gavilar came to confront Dalinar about whether he had killed the younger [[Tanalan]], Hathan answered the door and announced Gavilar's arrival to Dalinar.
