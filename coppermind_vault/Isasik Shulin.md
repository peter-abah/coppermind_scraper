**Isasik Shulin** is the [[Alethi]] royal cartographer on [[Roshar]].

He acted as the Royal High Cartographer to Kings [[Gavilar]] and [[Elhokar]] and likely Queen [[Jasnah Kholin]]. In his capacity as royal cartographer he made smaller copies of the kings maps for Highprince [[Dalinar Kholin]]

It is possible he hails from the city of [[Shulin]].

He was present on the [[Shattered Plains]], and accompanied the trip to [[Narak]] and onward to [[Urithiru]].

He couldn't join the expedition to [[Hearthstone]] on the *[[Fourth Bridge]]* as he was out mapping the eastern part of the [[Shattered Plains]].

He believes [[Shadesmar]] is incredible and tried to explain to [[Highprince]] [[Adolin Kholin]] why three times, but Adolin never understood.

## Trivia
* He was named after [[Isaac Stewart]], one of the interior artists for [[The Way of Kings]].
