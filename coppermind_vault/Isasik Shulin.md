**Isasik Shulin** is the [[Alethi]] royal cartographer on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa1|26}}

He acted as the Royal High Cartographer to Kings [[Gavilar]] and [[Elhokar]] and likely Queen [[Jasnah Kholin]]. In his capacity as royal cartographer he made smaller copies of the kings maps for Highprince [[Dalinar Kholin]]{{book ref|sa1|26}}{{book ref|sa4|3}}

It is possible he hails from the city of [[Shulin]].

He was present on the [[Shattered Plains]], and accompanied the trip to [[Narak]] and onward to [[Urithiru]].{{book ref|sa2|76}}{{book ref|sa2|81}}

He couldn't join the expedition to [[Hearthstone]] on the *[[Fourth Bridge]]* as he was out mapping the eastern part of the [[Shattered Plains]].{{book ref|sa4|3}}

He believes [[Shadesmar]] is incredible and tried to explain to [[Highprince]] [[Adolin Kholin]] why three times, but Adolin never understood.{{book ref|sa4|22}}

## Trivia
* He was named after [[Isaac Stewart]], one of the interior artists for [[The Way of Kings]].
