**Lady ZoBell** is a noble in [[Elendel]] on [[Scadrial]].

==Attributes and Abilities==
She is presumably wealthy, as she owns [[ZoBell Tower]], a twenty-story skyscraper in the Fourth Octant of Elendel.{{book ref|mb5|8}}{{book ref|mb6|4}} The penthouse of the tower, which spans the entire top floor, contains an elegant ballroom.{{book ref|mb5|9}} She is also well-connected among the Elendel elite.{{book ref|mb5|9}}

==History==
In {{date|342|PC}}, she hosted a [[Lady ZoBell's party|red carpet party]] in her penthouse attended by many nobles from Elendel and other cities in the [[Elendel Basin|Basin]].{{book ref|mb5|9}} Governor [[Replar Innate]] was scheduled to give his spring dinner and policy speech at the party.{{book ref|mb5|5}} Security was increased at the tower due to a recent assassination attempt on Innate, leading to some guests being turned away.{{book ref|mb5|9}} Although [[Waxillium Ladrian]] and his crew encountered [[Bleeder]] at the party,{{book ref|mb5|9}} there was no indication that Lady ZoBell had been aware of Bleeder's presence.

==Trivia==
*She is named after [[Kaylynn ZoBell]], from Brandon's writing group.
