**Kaylor Vosian** is a [[Skybreaker]] on [[Roshar]]. By the [[True Desolation]], he had sworn the Fourth Ideal. He is generally calm, authoritative and unyielding. He is also broad and strong, with shimmering Shardplate and a dark grey cape accentuating his rigid posture. At some point after the [[Iri|Iriali]] conquest of Marabethia, Vosian and three other Skybreakers captured the [[Fused]] traitor [[Talenai]] and, according to Marabethian law, hung them from a cliffside, waiting for them to get eaten by [[Yu-nerig]]. Shortly after, Vosian interrupted a rescue party trying to rescue Talenai. It is unknown what happened afterwards.
