**Kalofruit** are a type of fruit found on the island of [[Patji (island)|Patji]], and likely the entire [[Pantheon]], on [[First of the Sun]]. Kalofruit are long, thin, and orange, and they have a rind. Some [[trapper]]s eat these fruits. Nothing is known about the plants that grow kalofruit.
