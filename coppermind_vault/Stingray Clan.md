The **Stingray Clan** is one of the "families" or gangs in [[Ildithia]].

[[Carla]] is the leader of the clan. She allows [[Tia]] to hide with them until [[Jonathan Phaedrus|Prof]] traces her to them.

They have sufficient clout to live in downtown Ildithia and receive invitations to the parties in Sharp Tower. They also provide two cupcake decorators to the party.
