**Evod Markmaker** was a historical figure on [[Roshar]].

Evod was a [[darkeyes]] of legend that was known for killing a [[Shardbearer]] and claiming his [[Shardblade|Blade]]. There are many stories and songs about his deed. Evod is revered by darkeyes across Roshar, including [[Kaladin Stormblessed]] when he was younger.

Either Evod or [[Lanacin]] actually poisoned a Shardbearer to obtain their Shards, although this isn't well known.
