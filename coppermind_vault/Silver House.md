The **Silver House** is a mansion in [[Bilming]] on [[Scadrial]]. It is the residence of the sitting lord mayor.{{book ref|mb7|37}}

## Design
The building is far from the tallest in Bilming, but its design and silvery-white paint give it a majestic quality.{{book ref|mb7|37}} It has a "springy" grass lawn.{{book ref|mb7|41}}

## History
In {{date|348|PC}}, the Silver House was home to mayor [[Gave Entrone]],{{book ref|mb7|37}} although he owned another mansion at the edge of town.{{book ref|mb7|57}} 

During [[Wax]] and [[Wayne]]'s investigation of [[Telsin]] and the [[Set]], they became suspicious of Gave. They traveled to Bilming and broke into his office in the Silver House, where Wax discovered a calendar with various references to a "lab", a safe that only a [[Allomancy|Mistborn]] or [[Hemalurgy|Hemalurgist]] could open, and a trapdoor.{{book ref|mb7|41}} The trapdoor led to a small bunker with access to a storm drain, such that someone could leave the building undetected.{{book ref|mb7|46}} Wayne, who was tasked with creating a distraction, set a fire that quickly raged out of control until the building was fully ablaze.{{book ref|mb7|46}}

The Set had not anticipated Wax breaking into the building, and the information he found there helped him piece together Gave's true intentions.{{book ref|mb7|52}}{{book ref|mb7|66}}
