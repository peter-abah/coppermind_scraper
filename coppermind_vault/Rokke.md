**Rokke** is an [[Aviar]] on [[First of the Sun]] bonded with [[Sixth of the Dusk (character)|Sixth of the Dusk]].

## Appearance and Personality
Rokke is a small black and white bird with a scarlet head, belonging to one of the five breeds of Aviar the [[Eelakin]] use to shield their minds. Upon first meeting Dusk, She was initially quite skittish and shy but gained confidence and bravery through Dusk's verbal encouragement and their adventures together.

## Attributes and Abilities

Rokke has very similar abilities to that of [[Kokerlii]], being able to form a [[Nahel bond]] with humans and grant a "talent" that cloaks from mental attacks and intrusions, similar to [[Allomancy|Allomantic]] [[Copper]]. Rokke gained this ability by visiting [[Patji's Eye]] when she was young. It is shown that Rokke can prevent other entities such as the [[Sleepless]] from reading thoughts and mentally speaking to the bearer of her talent.
In addition to the abilities she bestows on her bonded partner, Rokke also has the ability to sense the [[Current|Grand Knell]] through her bond with Dusk.

## History

Rokke was initially kept by a [[trapper]] on one of the [[Pantheon]] islands. After his death, the bird was found in the trapper's [[safecamp]] and eventually ended up in the care of [[Vathi]]. Likely in an effort to help him after the loss of Kokerlii, Vathi presented Rokke to Dusk just before they all passed through [[Patji's Eye]]..
Rokke accompanied Dusk and [[Sak]] on their expedition through the [[Drominad]] [[Cognitive Realm#Subastrals|subastral]]. She used her ability to veil the group's mental presence to protect them from predatory [[Skullsnake|skullsnakes]] and occasionally lure the creatures by dropping the veil so Dusk could hunt them.

Upon reaching the [[Dakwara's island]] and being apprehended by the [[Scadrial|Scadrians]], Rokke accompanied Dusk to [[Dajer|Dajer's]] office and then to their own quarters where Dusk set up some toys and food bowls for the Aviar. She also accompanied him on his many trips down the island's tunnel, helped him shield his mind from the [[Sleepless]] [[Chrysalis]], and went with him across the island's lake to the central island.
