**Dreder** is a [[Skybreaker]] on [[Roshar]] who follows the [[Herald]] [[Nale]].{{book ref|sa5|93}} He is an associate of [[Shallan's mother]].{{book ref|sa2|45}}

## Appearance and Personality
Dreder wears a Skybreaker symbol on his sleeve. He is described as a "foreigner" by [[Shallan]], indicating that he is not ethnically [[Veden]]. He does not relish killing children, but will not hesitate to do so if it is necessary under the law.{{book ref|sa5|93}} 

## Attributes and Abilities
Dreder presumably has the typical abilities of a Skybreaker. He has attained at least the [[Order_of_Skybreakers#The_Second_Ideal|Second Ideal]], as he can heal himself by drawing in [[Stormlight]]. He appears to be a competent fighter, and is able immobilize an enraged [[Lin Davar]].{{book ref|sa2|88}}{{book ref|sa5|93}} He has some knowledge of the [[Oathpact]] and the various Orders of the [[Knights Radiant]].{{book ref|sa5|93}}

## History

|[[Nale]]{{book ref|sa5|93}}

Dreder was one of the Skybreakers that chose to follow Nale near the end of the [[Era of Solitude]], incorrectly believing that the Herald's sanity remained fully intact.{{book ref|sa3|40}}{{book ref|sa5|93}} He was one of Nale's minions that was dispatched to kill anyone who was close to bonding a [[spren]]. This was in support of Nale's mission -- set into motion by [[Ishar]]{{book ref|sa2.5|9}} -- to prevent a [[Desolation]] that would supposedly follow the return of other Radiants.{{book ref|sa3|40}} Dreder was presumably one of Nale's most trusted acolytes; he was aware of [[Chana|Shallan's mother's true identity]] and was sent to potentially kill Shallan himself if necessary. He communicated with Nale through a [[seon]].{{book ref|sa5|93}}

Shallan's memories of Dreder are hazy, but she did not recognize him when he appeared at [[Davar manor]] in {{Rosharan date|1167|10|8}}. She assumed that he was a friend of her mother's.{{book ref|sa2|88}} In fact, he was there to ensure Shallan's death on Nale's orders.{{book ref|sa5|93}}

After speaking with Nale through a seon, Dreder told Shallan's mother that her daughter needed to die. He realized that she was becoming mentally unstable, but he still intended to see Nale's orders followed.{{book ref|sa5|93}} Seeking Shallan, Dreder and Chana got into an argument with Lin. Lin slashed Dreder's arm with a knife, spilling blood onto the carpet,{{book ref|sa2|88}}{{book ref|sa5|93}} but Dreder quickly healed his injuries and restrained Lin.{{book ref|sa2|88}} Chana took the knife and attacked Shallan, but she hesitated. Shallan manifested [[Testament]] as a [[Shardblade]] and killed Chana; Dreder shouted and reached towards them, but Shallan killed him as well.{{book ref|sa5|93}} 

## Aftermath
Dreder's association with her mother is one of the things that Shallan wishes never happened.{{book ref|sa2|45}} She suppressed the memories of this event for most of her life, only recalling brief flashes of Dreder, including the blood from his arm wound staining the carpet and his corpse lying on the floor next to her mother's.{{book ref|sa2|10}}{{book ref|sa2|88}} 

In an attempt to protect Shallan, Lin maintained that Dreder and Shallan's mother were lovers and that Dreder had murdered her and then killed himself,{{book ref|sa2|39}} while other rumors claimed that Lin had killed both of them in a rage.{{book ref|sa2|19}}{{book ref|sa2|88}}

## Relationships
The nature of Dreder's relationship with Shallan's mother is unclear. Mraize claims that they had "intimate" contact.{{book ref|sa3|40}} Although they do seem to have a level of familiarity with one another, anything beyond that is not evident in their interactions.{{book ref|sa5|93}} It is possible that Mraize's account may have been influenced by the rumors that spread after Dreder's death, or that the full extent of their history has not been revealed.
