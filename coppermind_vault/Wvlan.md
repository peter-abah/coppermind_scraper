**Wvlan** is a [[Thaylen]] sailor on the *[[Wandersail (1174 ship)|Wandersail]]*.{{book ref|sa3.5|11}}

He was a member of the [[expedition to Akinah]]. When the *Wandersail* passed through the [[Akinah#Protection_Storm|storm surrounding the island]], he lost his footing and was nearly swept overboard, but [[Lopen]] was able to save him.{{book ref|sa3.5|11}}
