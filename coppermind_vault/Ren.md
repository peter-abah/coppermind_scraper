**Ren** is a coachman for [[House Davar]].{{book ref|sa2|45}}

He is one of the coachmen who accompany the household to the [[Middlefest]] fair. [[Shallan]] overheard him discussing a rotation with the other coachmen so as to allow them each to see the fair without leaving the coaches unattended.{{book ref|sa2|45}}
