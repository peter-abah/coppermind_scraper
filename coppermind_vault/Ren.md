**Ren** is a coachman for [[House Davar]].

He is one of the coachmen who accompany the household to the [[Middlefest]] fair. [[Shallan]] overheard him discussing a rotation with the other coachmen so as to allow them each to see the fair without leaving the coaches unattended.
