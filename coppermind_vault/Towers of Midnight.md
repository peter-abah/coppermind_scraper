***Towers of Midnight*** is the thirteenth volume of [[The Wheel of Time]], which [[Brandon Sanderson]] was chosen to complete after Robert Jordan's death. It is the second part of what was to be one book, *[[A Memory of Light]]*, but which grew too large and had to be split into three parts.
