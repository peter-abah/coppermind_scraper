**Gerrig** is an engineer in the [[Defiant Defense Force]]. When [[Rodge McCaffery|Rig]] hyperjumps [[Detritus]] to the planet [[Evershore (planet)|Evershore]] during a [[Superiority]] attack, Gerrig is in the control room with him. At Rig’s request, Gerrig helps enter coordinates for individual platforms to go, so that they could protect different islands from Superiority ships.
