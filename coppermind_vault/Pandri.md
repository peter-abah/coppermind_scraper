**Pandri** is an [[Azish]] woman on [[Roshar]]. 

She is among a pool of applicants (made up of viziers, scribes, and arbiters) to become the [[Prime Aqasix]] of [[Azir]]. However, the [[Assassin in White]] has killed the last two Primes in less than a week, and no one actually wants the job. [[Lift]] infiltrates the [[Bronze Palace]] with [[Huqin]]'s crew and she hears a conclave discussing the succession. Pandri is mentioned as having written something insulting across the top of her application in an attempt to disqualify herself.
