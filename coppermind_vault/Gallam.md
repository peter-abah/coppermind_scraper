**Gallam** is an [[Alethi]] [[Shardbearer]] allied with [[Kalanor]]. He fought [[Gavilar Kholin]]'s army in 1145.{{book ref|sa3|26}}

Highprince [[Ruthar]] killed Gallam, and won both [[Shardblade]] and [[Shardplate]] for his son, [[Relis Ruthar|Relis]].{{book ref|sa3|26}}{{book ref|sa2|37}}
