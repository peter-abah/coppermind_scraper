**Temadre** is a scholar and political theorist in the [[Final Empire]] on [[Scadrial]].

==Political Theories==
Temadre believed that the [[skaa]] would eventually rebel against the [[Lord Ruler]], and that they would slaughter every [[noble]] that they could find. He predicted that such a rebellion would be short but brutal, with tens of thousands of deaths. Despite Temadre's belief that an initial rebellion would be quashed, he was one of several authors who theorized that the Final Empire would someday fall despite the power of the Lord Ruler. The Lord Ruler typically executed anyone who published similar political treatises that he considered treasonous, but it is not known if Temadre suffered this fate.

[[Elend Venture]] read Temadre's writings, and considered his theories when deciding to defect to the [[skaa rebellion]].
