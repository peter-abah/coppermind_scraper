**Terushal** is a [[thunderclast]] on [[Roshar]]. They were formerly a [[singer]], but were made into a thunderclast when they died. They fought, among others, at the battle of [[Battle of Azimir|Azimir]].

## Appearance and Personality
Terushal is described as "walking with a hunched-over gait" and having "arms so long that they would scrape the ground if it didn't curl them up while walking." They are 30 feet tall, with glowing red eyes and "a face that evoked the lean arrowhead danger of an [[axehound]]." [[Adolin]] considers them to look more feral than [[Kai-garnis]], the thunderclast he fought at the [[Battle of Thaylen Field]].
