**Professor Irich** is a scientist and a member of the [[Set]] on [[Scadrial]]. Within the Set, he holds the rank of Array.{{book ref|mb6|18}} He is a [[chromium]] [[Misting]] (aka a [[Leecher]]).

## Appearance and Personality 
Irich is thin, approximately six feet tall, and has a narrow face and dark hair.{{book ref|mb6|9}} He has a degenerative disease that weakens his muscles and makes breathing difficult.{{book ref|mb6|18}} His physician has told him that the disease will eventually kill him, and he can tell that his condition is worsening daily.{{book ref|mb6|8}} He carries a cane to assist his walking, and his gait has been changing every few months due to the rapid degeneration of his muscles.{{book ref|mb6|8}} The cane and his stooped posture can make him appear to be older than he is.{{book ref|mb6|8}} Irich doesn't care about the power and authority he holds as an Array, as neither can prolong his life.{{book ref|mb6|18}}

## History
### Activities with The Set
He was involved in the attack on the train that carried [[Waxillium Ladrian]] and his companions to [[New Seran]]. Upon encountering [[Marasi Colms]], Irich fled. In his haste, he dropped the [[primer cube]] he was carrying, which [[Marasi Colms]] recovered{{book ref|mb6|8}} and eventually discovered could be used as an [[allomancy|allomantic]] grenade.{{book ref|mb6|17}}

He is in charge of studying the wrecked [[Southern Scadrian]] vessel-- the *[[Brunstell]]* --recovered by the Set in [[Dulsing]].{{book ref|mb6|18}} He is intrigued by its many mysteries but is focused on creating a weapon for the [[Telsin|Sequence]] and [[Mister Suit]].
