**"Sleep My Baby Dear"** is a lullaby on [[Roshar]]. 

[[Lin Davar]] sang the lullaby to [[Shallan]] when she was a child, especially when she was frightened or woke up screaming in the night.{{book ref|sa2|10}}{{book ref|sa2|73}} He held her and sang the lullaby on the night she was forced to kill [[Shallan's mother|her mother]].{{book ref|sa2|10}} Shallan then sang it to her father while she strangled him several years later.{{book ref|sa2|73}} The lullaby is prominent in Shallan's traumatic memories of these events.{{book ref|sa2|10}}{{book ref|sa3|82}}
