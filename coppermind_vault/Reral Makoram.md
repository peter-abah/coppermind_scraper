**Reral Makoram** is a [[lighteyed]] member of the sixth battalion in Sadeas's army on the [[Shattered Plains]], likely its commanding officer.

## History
[[Adolin Kholin]] was to meet with Reral, but was sidetracked when he protected a prostitute from being beaten by one of Sadeas's officers. Adolin abandoned his meeting with Reral in order to escort the prostitute home. Instead, he gave [[Kaladin]] an [[Spheres|emerald chip]] to tell Reral that he could no longer make the meeting and will reschedule for another time. Kaladin never deliverd the message because he felt that Adolin was talking down to him.
