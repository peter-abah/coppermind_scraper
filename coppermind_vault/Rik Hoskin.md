**Rik Hoskin** is an author that adapted [[Brandon Sanderson]]'s *[[White Sand (prose)|White Sand]]* into a three-volume [[White Sand|graphic novel]].

Rik is based in London and has written for DC Comics, Disney, and Dynamite.{{url ref|url=https://www.bleedingcool.com/2015/10/16/rik-hoskin-talks-dynamites-cage-hero/|text=Rik Hoskin Talks Dynamite’s Cage Hero|site=*Bleeding Cool*|date=2015-10-16}} He has also written a number of works under the collective pseudonym "James Axler".{{wp ref|James Axler}}

Brandon has praised Rik's work in condensing his prose to fit into a different medium.{{sots ref|2015}}{{wob ref|6449}} Rik worked with Brandon and his team{{wob ref|9259}} to help solve some nagging issues with the plot of *White Sand*, and Brandon enjoyed working with him.{{book ref|ws1|part=ack}}
