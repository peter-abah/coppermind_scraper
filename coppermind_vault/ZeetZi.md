**ZeetZi** is a [[lawnark]], originally from the [[Grand Apparatus]]. He serves as technician on the [[Dynamic]].

## Appearance & Personality

As a lawnark, ZeetZi is humanoid in appearance except for white and yellow feathers that grow on his head, eyebrows, and arms. He is mostly bald and has dark brown skin.

He wears trousers, suspenders, and rolls up the sleeves on his shirt. His [[Lodestar_(technology)|lodestar]] is shiny with few markings.

ZeetZi maintains a calm demeanor and speaks formally.

## Attributes & Abilities

ZeetZi is able to interface with [[Scadrial|Scadrian technology]] without a lodestar using electricity that runs into his body. He believes he is not an adequate navigator. However, he is highly skilled with other aspects of the ship. He can cook and make poisonous mixtures. He is able to fire a rifle.

ZeetZi cannot fly.

He considers himself of [[Invention]].

## History

ZeetZi lived on the Grand Apparatus which involved living from a trunk while moving from room to room.

At some point in the past, he visited [[Yolen]].

ZeetZi is serving as the technician and navigator on the Dynamic when the crew encounters an [[Evil|entity]] while attempting to answer a distress signal. ZeetZi participates in the rescue in defiance of [[Captain Crow]]. After this rescue, the ship returns to [[Silverlight]], and ZeetZi obtains landing clearance in the city.

He accompanies the Dynamic on their mission to find a perpendicularity in the [[Drominad]] subastral. He attempts to prevent the Malwish guards from executing [[Leonore]]. In captivity, ZeetZi is able to hack the Scadrian technology to learn about their surveillance systems. He agrees to work with [[Chrysalis]] to spoof the recording feeds to allow the crew to speak freely. He discusses plans with the crew. Later while Starling travels the [[Cave of Death]], ZeetZi discusses further escape plans with the crew including one where he poisons food he cooks for the Scadrians. He hopes to make use of Chrysalis as a distraction or pretend a crew member is sick, but Starling rejects these plans.

ZeetZi participates in further discussions with the crew, and notes that the [[Intensifier]] draws a substantial amount of electricity and that the crew is held with magnetic locks. After the frenzy of the [[Dakwara]], ZeetZi informs the rest of the crew that Chrysalis has been captured by the Scadrians. He discusses with [[Ed]] what a disrupted Sleepless feels like. He and the crew make use of the Intensifier causing a power outage to escape from their captivity. ZeetZi helps Starling onboard as the crew flies away. However, Starling realizes that Chrysalis has been left behind and the crew, including ZeetZi, agrees to return to save their crew member. He provides cover fire while Starling rescues the Sleepless and is injured in the process. He is on the Dynamic when [[Dusk]] uses the Dakwara to take down a Scadrian gunship, saving the crew.

At the end of their six month visit to [[First of the Sun]], ZeetZi announces the crew has decided to help Starling escape her manacles and is present when Dusk joins the crew as their [[Navigator]].

## Relationships

### Starling

ZeetZi is comforted by Starling's presence and trusts her decisions. He is the first to call her captain after the mutiny.

She encourages him to see Chrysalis as an individual rather than a representative of the [[Sleepless]]. 

### Chrysalis

ZeetZi distrusts Chrysalis because she is a Sleepless, the ones who enslaved and experimented on the lawnarks in the Grand Apparatus.

He is terrified on seeing one of her horde. He believes that Sleepless do not understand people.

He does not trust her, but does consider her a fellow crew member.

### Leonore

Leonore and ZeetZi playfully banter about his vocabulary.
