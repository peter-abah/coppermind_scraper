**ZeetZi** is a [[lawnark]], originally from the [[Grand Apparatus]].{{book ref|iote|22}}{{book ref|iote|25}} He serves as technician on the [[Dynamic]].{{book ref|iote|22}}

## Appearance & Personality

As a lawnark, ZeetZi is humanoid in appearance except for white and yellow feathers that grow on his head, eyebrows, and arms. He is mostly bald and has dark brown skin.{{book ref|iote|22}}

He wears trousers, suspenders, and rolls up the sleeves on his shirt. His [[Lodestar_(technology)|lodestar]] is shiny with few markings.{{book ref|iote|22}}

ZeetZi maintains a calm demeanor and speaks formally.{{book ref|iote|25}}{{book ref|iote|38}}{{book ref|iote|42}}

## Attributes & Abilities

ZeetZi is able to interface with [[Scadrial|Scadrian technology]] without a lodestar using electricity that runs into his body.{{book ref|iote|25}}{{book ref|iote|42}} He believes he is not an adequate navigator.{{book ref|iote|25}} However, he is highly skilled with other aspects of the ship.{{book ref|iote|26}}{{book ref|iote|51}} He can cook and make poisonous mixtures.{{book ref|iote|46}} He is able to fire a rifle.{{book ref|iote|60}}

ZeetZi cannot fly.{{book ref|iote|25}}

He considers himself of [[Invention]].{{book ref|iote|42}}

## History

ZeetZi lived on the Grand Apparatus which involved living from a trunk while moving from room to room.{{book ref|iote|25}}

At some point in the past, he visited [[Yolen]].{{book ref|iote|25}}

ZeetZi is serving as the technician and navigator on the Dynamic when the crew encounters an [[Evil|entity]] while attempting to answer a distress signal.{{book ref|iote|25}} ZeetZi participates in the rescue in defiance of [[Captain Crow]].{{book ref|iote|26}}{{book ref|iote|28}} After this rescue, the ship returns to [[Silverlight]], and ZeetZi obtains landing clearance in the city.{{book ref|iote|30}}

He accompanies the Dynamic on their mission to find a perpendicularity in the [[Drominad]] subastral.{{book ref|iote|35}} He attempts to prevent the Malwish guards from executing [[Leonore]].{{book ref|iote|38}} In captivity, ZeetZi is able to hack the Scadrian technology to learn about their surveillance systems. He agrees to work with [[Chrysalis]] to spoof the recording feeds to allow the crew to speak freely. He discusses plans with the crew.{{book ref|iote|42}} Later while Starling travels the [[Cave of Death]], ZeetZi discusses further escape plans with the crew including one where he poisons food he cooks for the Scadrians. He hopes to make use of Chrysalis as a distraction or pretend a crew member is sick, but Starling rejects these plans.{{book ref|iote|46}}

ZeetZi participates in further discussions with the crew, and notes that the [[Intensifier]] draws a substantial amount of electricity and that the crew is held with magnetic locks.{{book ref|iote|53}} After the frenzy of the [[Dakwara]], ZeetZi informs the rest of the crew that Chrysalis has been captured by the Scadrians. He discusses with [[Ed]] what a disrupted Sleepless feels like.{{book ref|iote|55}} He and the crew make use of the Intensifier causing a power outage to escape from their captivity. ZeetZi helps Starling onboard as the crew flies away.{{book ref|iote|58}}{{book ref|iote|59}}{{book ref|iote|60}} However, Starling realizes that Chrysalis has been left behind and the crew, including ZeetZi, agrees to return to save their crew member. He provides cover fire while Starling rescues the Sleepless and is injured in the process. He is on the Dynamic when [[Dusk]] uses the Dakwara to take down a Scadrian gunship, saving the crew.{{book ref|iote|60}}

At the end of their six month visit to [[First of the Sun]], ZeetZi announces the crew has decided to help Starling escape her manacles and is present when Dusk joins the crew as their [[Navigator]].{{book ref|iote|epilogue}}

## Relationships

### Starling

ZeetZi is comforted by Starling's presence and trusts her decisions.{{book ref|iote|25}}{{book ref|iote|30}} He is the first to call her captain after the mutiny.{{book ref|iote|30}}{{book ref|iote|42}}

She encourages him to see Chrysalis as an individual rather than a representative of the [[Sleepless]].{{book ref|iote|42}} 

### Chrysalis

ZeetZi distrusts Chrysalis because she is a Sleepless, the ones who enslaved and experimented on the lawnarks in the Grand Apparatus.{{book ref|iote|22}}{{book ref|iote|23}}

He is terrified on seeing one of her horde. He believes that Sleepless do not understand people.{{book ref|iote|42}}

He does not trust her, but does consider her a fellow crew member.{{book ref|iote|60}}

### Leonore

Leonore and ZeetZi playfully banter about his vocabulary.{{book ref|iote|25}}{{book ref|iote|46}}
