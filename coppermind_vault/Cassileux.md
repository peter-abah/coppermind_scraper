**Cassileux** is a member of the [[Elendel]] constabulary on [[Scadrial]]. She died in a raid on the [[Nomad Gang]] about sixteen months before [[Marasi]]'s operation against the [[Set]] in [[Bilming]] in . Her bones were recovered by the [[kandra]] [[LeeMar]], who used them to infiltrate the constabulary and keep an eye on [[Marasi]].
