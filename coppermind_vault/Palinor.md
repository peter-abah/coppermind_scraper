**Palinor** is a [[Darkeyes|darkeyed]] [[Alethi]] man on [[Roshar]]. Born a peasant, Palinor has worked his way up the ranks of [[Torol Sadeas|Sadeas]]’ military, aiming to become a sergeant. He is inspiring, earnest and self righteous, as well as a capable combatant. He is especially skilled at fighting defensively, and often bristles against the command of his superior officer, captain [[Selinar]].
