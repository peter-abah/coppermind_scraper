**Oswald** is [[Ealstan]]'s nephew and a guard in [[Stenford]].{{book ref|frugal|12}}{{book ref|Frugal|2}} He is a very good-natured, cheerful, and respectful young man.{{book ref|frugal|2}}{{book ref|frugal|6}}{{book ref|frugal|7}}

Oswald manned a wooden watchtower in [[Stenford]] and came down to close the gate after [[Ulric Stromfin]] and [[Quinn Jericho]] arrived.{{book ref|frugal|2}} He then took an axe and shield and went to guard the door to the manor.{{book ref|frugal|6}} He was killed when Ulric shot him with a modern gun in order to prove to Ealstan that he could kill whomever he chose.{{book ref|frugal|7}} [[Rowena]] arranged for Oswald's burial and spoke with his mother and with [[Hairud]] to tell them of his passing and of his heroism.{{book ref|frugal|8}}
