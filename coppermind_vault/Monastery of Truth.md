The **Monasteries of Truth** are a group of ten [[Shinovar|Shin]] monasteries dedicated to the [[Herald]]s. They are the seats of the Shin government and the religion of [[Stone Shamanism]]. The [[Honorbearer]]s, head shamans tasked with the custody of the [[Honorblade]]s, reside within them.

## Structure
The monasteries are built to function as watchtowers and fortresses, being tall stone buildings designed to be capable of withstanding an attack.{{book ref|sa5|40}}{{book ref|sa5|45}} They have slit-shaped windows.{{book ref|sa5|90}}

Each monastery features a great hall accessed by a large gate.{{book ref|sa5|40}}{{book ref|sa5|45}}{{book ref|sa5|66}}{{book ref|sa5|90}} The great hall’s floor has a large mural, depicting the Herald to which it is dedicated.{{book ref|sa5|40}}{{book ref|sa5|71}}{{book ref|sa5|83}} The great hall is generally lit with gemstones,{{book ref|sa5|40}}{{book ref|sa5|45}}{{book ref|sa5|90}} being one of the few places in Shinovar where gemstone lighting is deemed appropriate.{{book ref|sa1|i|6}}

At least some of the monasteries feature a rock garden for meditation,{{book ref|sa5|75}} as well as a ceremonial stone on an altar near the front of the great hall.{{book ref|sa5|45}} They feature rooms for the acolytes to sleep in.{{book ref|sa5|66}}

The [[Bondsmith monastery]] is an exception to all of this, instead being a natural rock alcove.{{book ref|sa5|94}}
