The **Monasteries of Truth** are a group of ten [[Shinovar|Shin]] monasteries dedicated to the [[Herald]]s. They are the seats of the Shin government and the religion of [[Stone Shamanism]]. The [[Honorbearer]]s, head shamans tasked with the custody of the [[Honorblade]]s, reside within them.

## Structure
The monasteries are built to function as watchtowers and fortresses, being tall stone buildings designed to be capable of withstanding an attack. They have slit-shaped windows.

Each monastery features a great hall accessed by a large gate. The great hall’s floor has a large mural, depicting the Herald to which it is dedicated. The great hall is generally lit with gemstones, being one of the few places in Shinovar where gemstone lighting is deemed appropriate.

At least some of the monasteries feature a rock garden for meditation, as well as a ceremonial stone on an altar near the front of the great hall. They feature rooms for the acolytes to sleep in.

The [[Bondsmith monastery]] is an exception to all of this, instead being a natural rock alcove.
