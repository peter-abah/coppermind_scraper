**Koen** is a [[darkeyes|darkeyed]]{{book ref|sa2|16}} soldier from [[Alethkar]] on [[Roshar]]. A former member of the [[Cobalt Guard]], he joined [[Bridge Four]] after the [[Battle of the Tower]] and was one of the first non-bridgemen to become a [[Windrunner]] [[squire]].{{book ref|sa3|46}}

As a former squire to Kaladin, he bonded an [[honorspren]] and swore at least the [[Immortal Words|Second Ideal]] of the Windrunners, assuming that he was alive in early {{Rosharan date|1175}}.{{book ref|sa4|12}}
