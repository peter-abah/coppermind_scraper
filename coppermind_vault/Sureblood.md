**Sureblood** is [[Adolin Kholin]]'s [[Ryshadium]] stallion.{{book ref|sa1|13}}

## Appearance and Personality
{{image|Musicspren by Eccos.jpg|side=left|width=250px|With [[Adolin]]}}
Sureblood is a white [[Ryshadium]] stallion{{book ref|sa1|26}} with dark colored eyes;{{book ref|sa2|81}} [[Shallan]] notes that he is actually a pure white color, not a dusty grey.{{book ref|sa2|77}} He is quite tall and will tower over a gelding.{{book ref|sa2|76}} He isn't as powerfully built as [[Gallant]], who is much bulkier and squatter than Sureblood.{{book ref|sa2|51}} Unlike Gallant, he does not make mirthful clicking noises with his mouth.{{book ref|sa4|22}}

Sureblood is not prone to panic, remaining calm under archery fire even when warhorses would not,{{book ref|sa1|13}} and hardly needs handling by grooms.{{book ref|sa1|26}} Sureblood and his master share a close bond. He is very aware of [[Adolin]]'s emotions and will often prance while Adolin is riding him in an attempt to improve his rider's mood;{{book ref|sa2|26}} Adolin feels like he is aware of Sureblood's emotions as well.{{book ref|sa3|10}} Simply being near Sureblood is enough to make Adolin feel better,{{book ref|sa2|26}} and during Sureblood's life, Adolin has felt morose to be riding a horse other than Sureblood.{{book ref|sa2|51}}

## History
[[Adolin]] was more nervous on the day he met Sureblood than on the day he won his [[Mayalaran|Shardblade]] because [[Ryshadium]] choose their own rider. He was young when they bonded; his exact age is not known.{{book ref|sa2|81}} Sureblood and Adolin had a close bond already by {{Rosharan date|1173}}, when they were living in the warcamps on the [[Shattered Plains]] and King [[Elhokar]] wanted to go on a [[chasmfiend]] hunt. When the chasmfiend unexpectedly appeared on their plateau, Adolin ran for Sureblood and rode him into the conflict, cutting into the creature's legs as he rode by.{{book ref|sa1|13}} Adolin also rode Sureblood out to skirmishes with the [[Parshendi]] on the Shattered Plains as the [[Alethi]] attempted to win [[gemheart]]s, although he typically led the charge on foot so he could leap the chasms.{{book ref|sa1|26}}

{{image|Narak Charge by EccoS.jpg|side=right|width=250px|At the [[Battle of Narak]]}}

Sureblood carried Adolin to the [[Battle of the Tower]], where [[Torol Sadeas]] abandoned the Kholin army to die.{{book ref|sa1|64}} Although the close-quartered fighting wasn't conducive to mounted attacks, Sureblood served usefully as a ride for a few of the wounded until [[Bridge Four]] gave them an avenue of retreat;{{book ref|sa1|68}} on the way back to the warcamps, he carried Adolin once more.{{book ref|sa1|69}}

Adolin continued to ride Sureblood, though he had less opportunity to ride or to groom the Ryshadium the more [[Dalinar]] focused on uniting the [[highprince]]s instead of riding out to capture gemhearts.{{book ref|sa2|26}}{{book ref|sa3|10}} He took Sureblood out to a skirmish that only [[Roion]] and [[Ruthar]] were scheduled to go on in order to lend [[Jakamav]], the only other [[Shardbearer]], a hand. When Adolin left his conversation with Jakamav stewing, Sureblood attempted to ease his mood.{{book ref|sa2|26}}

Sureblood also took Adolin on the expedition to [[Narak]] despite [[Shallan]]'s envy over Adolin having the taller mount.{{book ref|sa2|76}}{{book ref|sa2|77}} As the fighting commenced, Sureblood was unsettled by the song the Parshendi were singing to summon the [[Everstorm]]. He charged into [[Battle of Narak|battle]] with Adolin, but a flash of red lightning struck him, killing him and flinging Adolin off of his back. When Adolin came across Sureblood's body, he screamed in grief.{{book ref|sa2|81}}

Adolin, realizing he had taken Sureblood for granted toward the end of his life, began visiting [[Gallant]] in the stables.{{book ref|sa3|10}} This budding relationship in the wake of Sureblood's demise eventually led to Gallant's loyal help in [[Shadesmar]] on the trip to [[Lasting Integrity]].{{book ref|sa4|35}}

In a trip to Shadesmar, it was noted that there was a musicspren (which likely bond Ryshadium) that seemed to intimately know Adolin. It is possible that this was Sureblood's spren before his death.{{book ref|sa5|11}}
