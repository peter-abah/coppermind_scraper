The **Fallen Tower** is a tavern on [[Roshar]] located in one of the suburb towns of [[Kholinar]].

It was one of [[Moash]]'s preferred stops from his days as a caravaneer, as it had been welcoming. He passed it on his way to the lumberyard while he was serving the [[singers]] during the [[Siege of Kholinar]].
