**Wells** is a constable of the [[Elendel]] constabulary on [[Scadrial]].

When [[Marasi]] and [[Javies]] approached a senior captain about watching Governor [[Replar Innate]]'s speech so that they could report back to [[Aradel]], the captain told Marasi that Wells could admit her to an area reserved for the press.

## Trivia
*Wells may be a cameo for Brandon's friend [[Dan Wells]], similar to [[Wellen]].
