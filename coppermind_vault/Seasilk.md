**Seasilk** is a type of fabric used on [[Roshar]].

## Properties and Characteristics
Seasilk is produced from undersea plants that grow along the Rosharan coasts. It seems likely that it is an export of [[Rall Elorim]] in [[Iri]]. When laundering seasilk it must be put in a warm brine to relax its fibers and then rinsed and brushed with the grain to restore its softness.

Seasilk is used in all manner of [[lighteyes|lighteyed]] garments, such as dresses and suits.

## Trivia
* Despite the fact that seasilk is produced from undersea plants, it would look very similar to silk from Earth. As Roshar's insect cocoons typically have stone in them or melt in water, they cannot be used to make material.
* [[wikipedia: sea silk|Sea silk]] is also a real-world phenomenon, but is different from Rosharan seasilk in one respect: real sea silk is harvested from certain species of clams, rather than from plants, and is also spelled as two words instead of one.
