**Adelle Choi** was a [[Rithmatics|Rithmatist]] who was one of the final two participants in the [[Melee#1888 Crew-Choi duel|1888 Melee]].  She was a definite underdog as she was the only member of her team to make it past the first few minutes.  She was able to defeat [[Paul Crew]] when she bounced a Line of Vigor off of several lines left by previously eliminated Rithmatists.{{book ref|Rithmatist|1}}
