> Lord Evenstrom is excitable, sometimes. I know how it goes; this isn't the first time he’s sent me to help someone who didn’t need it.
> — [[Wayne]] to a [[House Tekiel]] guard

Lord **Evenstrom Tekiel** may be a member of [[House Tekiel]] on [[Scadrial]].

When helping [[Waxillium Ladrian]] sneak into the [[Breaknaught]], [[Wayne]] disguised himself as a Tekiel guard and claimed that Evenstrom Tekiel had sent him to help. It is unclear whether Evenstrom Tekiel is a real person or a name Wayne made up.
