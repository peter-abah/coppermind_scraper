**Fiddlepox** is a disease on [[Roshar]].

It is one of numerous diseases that [[Lirin]] taught [[Kaladin Stormblessed]] to be able to identify during his training as a surgeon.

## Symptoms
Someone infected with fiddlepox would be easily noticeable due to their excess of nervous energy. They might also often have trouble sleeping, a powerful thirst, and swelling on the undersides of the arms.
