**Drenya** is a woman kidnapped by the [[Set]] on [[Scadrial]]. She would end up living in the [[Community]] project. When [[Marasi]] arrives, Drenya is one of the people to gather at [[Armal]]’s house to greet her. She was likely freed with the others after the draining of the [[perpendicularity]].
