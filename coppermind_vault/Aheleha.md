**Aheleha** is a settlement in the [[Cosmere]]. The [[Malwish Empire]] fought a [[Battle of Aheleha|large and destructive engagement]] at Aheleha. It is not known who won this battle, nor what happened to the settlement afterwards.{{book ref|iote|38}} As its name follows [[Vorin]] rules of symmetry, it was likely founded by Rosharans and may even be on [[Roshar]] itself.
