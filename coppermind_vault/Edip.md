**Edip** is someone who has lived in the [[Elendel]] slums on [[Scadrial]]. 

When [[Wayne]] is pursuing the [[Marksman]] in , a resident of the slums claims to recognize him as Edip's son; Wayne uses this to his advantage to try and fit in. A different person later identifies Wayne as Edip's son while Wayne is near the slums looking for [[Ranette]].
