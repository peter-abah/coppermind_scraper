**Silverlight Mercantile** is an enterprise based out of [[Silverlight]] whose business include an interplanetary postal service, which operates through mysterious [[Messengers|messengers]].

[[Harmony]] used them in order to send [[MeLaan]] a message concerning [[Wayne]]'s passing. They manufacture clocks with replaceable faces which are capable of being swapped out to account for different planetary local times.  

## Trivia
* Silverlight Mercantile doubles as the name of Dragonsteel LLC's real life mercantile business.
