**Rick Stranger** was a criminal which High Lord [[Waxillium Ladrian]] pursued during his time as a lawman in the [[Roughs]] of [[Scadrial]]. 

Lady [[Steris Harms]] compiled biographies of many of Wax's old enemies, including Rick Stranger, in order to prepare for their wedding. She hoped to invite one of them to the wedding ceremony so that she could plan for a disturbance. Upon seeing Rick Stranger's name, Wax remarked that he had almost forgotten about him.

## Trivia
*Rick Stranger is a cameo for one of [[Brandon Sanderson]]'s friends and alpha readers Ben Olsen, whose role-playing characters' names often appear both as in-world characters, and as faux beta readers as a joke between them.
