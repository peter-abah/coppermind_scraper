The **Machineborn** are the native sentient life in the [[State]]s of the [[Wode]]. They are artificial intelligence "[[Wikipedia:Non-player character|NPCs]]" used to fill out the population of each [[Liveborn]]'s state. They cannot leave their home state.

All Machineborn have a **Concept**, the central premise that describes their character, which can be hacked by both the Wode and Liveborn. Examples of Concepts include chancellor [[Besk]]'s cleverness, [[Shale]]'s insistence on wearing armor, and Chief [[Letmere]]'s pragmatism.
