> — Ishal to [[Dalinar]] on [[Adolin]]'s name

**Ishal** is an [[Alethi]] [[ardent]] from [[Kholinar]] in the [[Era of Solitude]] on [[Roshar]].

At some point in her life, Ishal became an ardent in Kholinar, joining the [[Devotary of Purity]]. Late in her life, in the year , Ishal was present for the birth of Prince [[Adolin Kholin]]. She asked Highprince [[Dalinar Kholin]] to name Adolin, so she could burn the appropriate [[glyphward]]s.
