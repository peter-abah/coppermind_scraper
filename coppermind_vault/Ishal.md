**Ishal** is an [[Alethi]] [[ardent]] from [[Kholinar]] in the [[Era of Solitude]] on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa3|49}}

At some point in her life, Ishal became an ardent in Kholinar, joining the [[Devotary of Purity]].{{book ref|sa3|49}} Late in her life, in the year {{Rosharan date|1150}}, Ishal was present for the birth of Prince [[Adolin Kholin]].{{book ref|sa3|49}} She asked Highprince [[Dalinar Kholin]] to name Adolin, so she could burn the appropriate [[glyphward]]s.{{book ref|sa3|49}}
