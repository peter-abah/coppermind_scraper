**Crews Geffenry** is a [[noble]] [[Allomancer#Mistborn|Mistborn]] on [[Scadrial]].

He is implicated by [[Kelsier]] in the murder of [[Charrs Entrone]]. He is known to be a competent knife fighter. Prior to Charrs' murder, Crews had been gambling with him. Additionally he had been petitioning [[House Tekiel]] for a stronger alliance. His cousins [[Ardous Geffenry|Ardous]] & [[Callins Geffenry|Callins]] are killed in retaliation.
