> Progress is not worth the blood of my people.
> — Vathi

**Vathi** is a woman from the [[Eelakin Islands]] on [[First of the Sun]]. She is president of the [[First Company]] and leader of the Eelakin people.

## Appearance and Personality

> This woman spoke. A lot. People did that back on the Eelakin Islands. Her skin was dark, like his, and she had the sound of his people.
> — Dusk, on Vathi

She has dark skin and dark, curly hair, and is seen wearing a tight vest and green linen skirts. In the jungle, she carries a pack and her white and green [[Aviar]], [[Mirris]]. After her journey back to her camp, Dusk notes that she wears a new dress and "smells of flowers", although her hair still has snarls. Later on, she tends to wear clothes that imitate the modern style of the Ones Above, while blending these styles with Eelakin tradition. 

She is born in the homeisles but her name does not follow the Eelakin tradition, an example of the growing influence of the [[mainland]]. Growing up, she was often told the story of [[Yaalani]] the Brave, supposedly the only female [[trapper]] ever to have lived. As an adult, she questions the authenticity of this story, noting that most cultures have similar myths of female role reversal that are actually used to reinforce gender stereotypes.

She has an Eelakin accent, but with a polish that indicates that she is educated, and she mentions having read books about trappers and the [[Pantheon]]. She is intellectually curious, and she irritates [[Sixth of the Dusk (character)|Sixth of the Dusk]] with her many questions about his life and the flora and fauna found on [[Patji (island)|Patji]]. She is courageous and unflappable, and is able to react quickly in dangerous situations. She is persuasive, determined and uses these qualities effectively in her political career. 

The independence of her people is very important to her, though if she had seen no other way out, she would have submitted to the terms of the Ones Above. 

## Attributes and Abilities

> She was not a trapper. A scholar should not have been able to do what she did.
> — Dusk, on Vathi

Vathi is knowledgeable, confident, and resourceful. With the help of her cognitive shroud Aviar, she is able to move through the jungle on Patji without being killed. Dusk notes that she is fast and agile, and even believes that he is tracking a young trapper when he first comes across her path. She appears to a very high level of academic knowledge about the Pantheon, and is limited only by her lack of real-life experience on the islands. She is able to figure out several key pieces of information even when she only has a few clues to work with.

There are some indications that Eelakin society is patriarchal, and Vathi seems to defy convention. Dusk assumes she is a scribe when he first meets her, and she surprises him with her fortitude and skill on multiple occasions. Even after their trip through the jungle, Dusk is still perplexed to see that Vathi holds a position of some authority in the Company and is addressed as "Lady Vathi". Vathi makes note of her disdain for the tale of Yaalani the Brave and also mentions that Captain Eusto sees her as a threat to his authority.

She is a good negotiator, and succeeds in balancing pressures from within her own government and from the Ones Above. She has a "tough as bark" reputation among the public, and works to maintain this image. Even though information on the rest of the [[cosmere]] is scarce on First of the Sun, Vathi figures out enough to juggle relations with the [[Roshar|Rosharans]], [[Malwish Empire]] and eventually [[Xisisrefliel]] and the greater cosmere.

## History
### The Company
Vathi was employed by the [[Northern Interests Trading Company]] on the mainland. It is not clear how long she worked there, but she referred to a number of past meetings with the [[Ones Above]]. She had also previously met Dusk on one of his rare visits to the company to acquire technology.

She joined the [[Patji expedition]] captained by [[Eusto]], although her interests were more scientific than financial. After the Company established a small fortress on the island, Vathi joined a scout team seeking a site for a second fortress. The scout team stayed too close to the beach and was attacked and annihilated by a [[deepwalker]]. Vathi managed to fire a spear cannon at the beast; it had little effect, but she escaped into the jungle. Mirris was injured in the process.

Dusk came across the remains of the scout team and tracked the survivors into the jungle, eventually following Vathi's path which led towards one of his [[safecamp]]s; unbeknownst to Dusk, Vathi had used a handheld Aviar locator to detect the location of the safecamp, as she hoped to find a trapper and request assistance. She was able to successfully navigate the extreme dangers of the jungle, but she was not skilled enough to avoid being tracked by Dusk and eventually got caught in one of his traps.

### Meeting Sixth of the Dusk

Dusk was not pleased to find Vathi, but he was impressed by her skill and her treatment of her injured Aviar. Vathi believed that the Pantheon would eventually be tamed and revealed the true scope of the Company's expedition to Dusk, which angered him. Something suddenly agitated all of the Aviar on the island, and [[Sak]] gave Dusk visions of imminent death. Dusk grabbed Vathi and demanded that she tell him more about what her colleagues were doing. Although Dusk's actions disturbed her, Vathi discussed her interactions with the spacefaring [[Ones Above]], including the fact that they were very interested in Aviar but seemed to have rules about trading with less advanced civilizations. She informed him of the large Aviar tracking device held on one of the Company's ships and she assumed that it was the source of the disturbance.

### Through the Jungle
Although traveling in the jungle at night usually led to certain death, Dusk demanded that Vathi immediately leave with him to stop the Company from activating the device again. She declined, and Dusk allowed Sak to show Vathi visions of her death, revealing that Sak granted a talent even though she was not a known species of Aviar. Vathi quickly realized that Sak was a bird from the mainland and had somehow gained a talent on Patji. She also learned that [[Cakoban's finger]]s was a type of tree that had blossoms with a mental signature. She was shocked by these revelations and wanted to learn more. Leaving Mirris behind to heal, she agreed to travel to the Company fortress.

At one point Vathi saw a mating plume from an Aviar and grabbed it, setting off a trap, but she and Dusk avoided serious injury. After he admonished her, he realized that he was treating her like an apprentice and opened up to her a bit more, even telling her the true meaning of his name. A [[nightmaw]] attacked, and Dusk was dumbfounded when Vathi was able to kill it with her spear cannon. However, the noise attracted other nightmaws, and the pair recklessly fled through the jungle, ending up at [[Patji's Eye]]. Vathi was incredulous when she saw hundreds of Aviar around the lake, and she deduced that the birds gained their talents from eating the fruit of the Cakoban's fingers that grew nearby; Dusk clarified that the key was parasitic worms that lived in the fruit. Dusk nearly sacrificed himself to try and allow Vathi to escape, but they realized at the last moment that they could use one of the blossoms and the spear cannon to lead the nightmaws away from the area.

### Return to the Company

> We'll lose it all. We can’t fight them. They’ll find an excuse, they’ll seize the Aviar. It makes perfect sense. The Aviar use the worms. We use the Aviar. The Ones Above use us. It’s inevitable, isn’t it?
> — Vathi

Vathi and Dusk made it back to the fortress, and she quickly told [[Winds]] not to activate the machine again, only to learn that it was never turned on - only opened. After considering some suspicious actions by the Ones Above, Dusk and Vathi realized that the technology was essentially a trap. The Ones Above wanted to rapidly advance society on First of the Sun to the point that they would not violate their "rules" by taking the Aviar. Dusk agreed to help Vathi foil this plan.

### Presidency
Following the events on Patji, Vathi was elected president of the First Company, formerly known as the Northern Interests Trading Company. The company then made a bid for the leadership of the Eelakin people as a whole, and succeeded. As president, Vathi regularly consulted Dusk on matters regarding the Pantheon and the Aviar, but he was often aggressive to other senators, and she had to restrict his visits. The primary political concern of the government at this time was negotiation with the Ones Above ([[Scadrial|Scadrians]]). Vathi oversaw projects such as the industrialization of Patji and early exploration of the [[Cognitive Realm#Drominad subastral|Drominad subastral]].

> This is our fate. Caught between the ocean wave and the unbroken stone.
> — Vathi

Five years after meeting Dusk, Vathi agreed provisionally to the terms of the Ones Above. However, she and her senators had no intention of sticking to this, and were also secretly in discussions with the [[Roshar|Rosharans]]. Both the Scadrians and the Rosharans wanted the Aviar, but the Rosharans were also seeking the [[Perpendicularity]]. The severity of the situation was highlighted when Sak showed Dusk a vision of Vathi's corpse, dressed in a Scadrian uniform. In a last-ditch attempt to preserve their independence, Vathi agreed to send Dusk on an expedition into the [[Cognitive Realm]], through Patji's Eye. She worried about Dusk, as the two had grown close over the years, but understood that the risk was worth it for the sake of the planet. She gave [[Rokke]] to him as a protective measure.

[[Admiral Rattu]] began proceedings to remove Vathi from office, leaving her with a political catastrophe to manage while Dusk was away. Despite this, she managed to keep stalling the Ones Above from taking control of First of the Sun. Three votes of no confidence were held during this period, and all failed to dissolve the government. Immediately after the third vote, Vathi was introduced to [[Illistandrista|Starling]] by Dusk. Dusk showed Vathi the [[Dakwara]] and explained all the events surrounding the race for the perpendicularity.

Six months later, Vathi leveraged the newly trained [[Navigator|Navigators]] to broker a deal with the dragon [[Xisisrefliel]]. This magnified political power allowed the Eelakin to maintain their independence, as the other planetary powers now needed their cooperation. Dusk passed over command of the Dakwara to Vathi. The Dakwara will be under the control of Vathi, until the next president takes office and control passes over.

## Relationships
### Dusk

> Thank you for putting up with me all these years. I know I've been quite the pain at times.
> — Vathi to Dusk

Vathi and Dusk are close friends, despite their unconventional dynamic. Dusk is somewhat of a troublemaker, and Vathi has to keep him away from other senators unless his advice is urgently needed. She often has to remind Dusk to answer people when they speak to him, and gets called if he is in trouble with the police. Dusk does try to heed Vathi's advice.

Vathi greatly admires Dusk and values his advice. She worried a lot after he went out into the [[Cognitive Realm#Emberdark|emberdark]], fearing that he would die. She even put her position as president at stake in order to wait for Dusk's return, instead of giving in to public pressure. She makes a great effort always to help him integrate into modern society, and he in turn mentors her on matters relating to Aviar and the Pantheon. Some people, such as Starling, assume their relationship to be of a romantic nature, but it is not. 

The two have matching medallions of [[Cakoban]], as reminders of each other. Dusk's Aviar Kokerlii was especially fond of Vathi.
