The **Steamwater Ocean** is a body of water to the northeast of the main continent of [[Roshar]].{{map ref|Roshar}}

[[Dalinar Kholin]] once proposed to [[Evi]] that they and [[Adolin]] take a vacation to the Steamwater Ocean, as Evi had grown tired of the wind and cold in [[Alethkar]].{{book ref|sa3|66}} There is no indication that they ever took this trip.

The *[[Fourth Bridge]]* spent several weeks flying over the Steamwater Ocean, taking refuge in [[lait]]s and coves during [[highstorm]]s, in order to test the airship's endurance over a longer period of time.{{book ref|sa4|3}}
