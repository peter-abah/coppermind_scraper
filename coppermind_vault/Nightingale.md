> — [[Ironsides]] when asked by [[Cobb]] if she wished someone else had survived

**Nightingale** is a pilot in the [[DDF]] on [[Detritus]]. It is likely that this is this pilots callsign, however, it is not clear.

Nightingale fought against the [[varvax|Krell]] in the [[Battle of Alta]] and did not survive the battle, however, if they did, they would be considered a [[First Citizen]]. It is unclear which flight Nightingale belonged to or how they died, however, it is possible that they were a part of [[Hope Flight]].

[[Ironsides]] may have greatly disliked Nightingale, however, it is unclear.
