{{spoilers|iote}}

**Nol** is an [[Iriali]] man in the [[cosmere]], likely from [[Zidorna]].{{book ref|iote|28}}{{book ref|iote|30}} He is married to [[Crux]], another Iriali, with whom he has a son. He speaks [[Yolen#Ethnicity and Language|Yolish]] and has the golden hair typical of his people.{{book ref|iote|25}}{{book ref|iote|27}}

==History==
Following a map passed down in his family depicting a portion of the [[Iriali#History|Long Trail]] record, Nol and his family sold all of their belongings to purchase a ship to sail through the [[Cognitive_Realm#Emberdark|emberdark]] in the hopes of being the ones to rediscover a hidden [[perpendicularity]] shown thereon. However, due to a character shift in Middle-old Iriali they misinterpreted the coordinates and were searching the wrong part of the emberdark when an electrical fire started in the ship's cockpit.{{book ref|iote|27}}{{book ref|iote|28}}{{book ref|iote|30}} They deployed [[Investiture|Invested]] floats so as to not sink into the [[Cognitive_Realm#Emberdark|unsea]] and turned on an Invested distress beacon before moving on top of the diminutive ship to get out of the smoke and fire inside.{{book ref|iote|25}}{{book ref|iote|27}} While the distress beacon was eventually picked up by the crew of the *[[Dynamic]]*, the Investited signal also attracted a [[Evil|Type 1-6 Threnodite entity]].{{book ref|iote|25}}

[[Illistandrista|Starling]] and the crew of the *Dynamic* were able to distract the [[Evil|Eidolith]] long enough to mount a rescue and brought Nol and his family on board. When asked by [[ZeetZi]] why they were so far outside the regular lanes of travel, Nol lied in an attempt to disguise their true motivation.{{book ref|iote|28}} The family was then brought to [[Silverlight]] where they had relatives at the university.{{book ref|iote|28}}{{book ref|iote|30}} Upon reaching Silverlight, Nol admitted to Starling the true reason they had ended up so far into the emberdark and gave her his map both in thanks for her help and to keep him from being tempted to try something so foolish again.{{book ref|iote|30}}
