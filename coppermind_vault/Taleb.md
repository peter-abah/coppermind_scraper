**Taleb** is a resident of [[Hearthstone]] on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa1|44}}

In {{Rosharan date|1168|missing-date=y}}, when Highmarshal [[Meridas Amaram]] visited to Hearthstone to recruit men for his army, Citylord [[Roshone]] chooses Taleb's son [[Caull]] to be one of the men conscripted, along with [[Agil]], [[Habrin]], [[Jorna]] and [[Tien]].
