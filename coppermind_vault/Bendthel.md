**Bendthel** is a [[Rosharan]] scholar who studied the [[Dawnchant]] and collected works created in the language.

The transcriptions from Bendthel's collected Dawnchant fill at least three codices. [[Ellista]] works from these codices while working on translations at the [[Jokasha Monastery]]. Ardent [[Urv]] comes to retrieve the copy from Ellista and ask her progress on deciphering the works. He thinks her reasoning might be applicable to the [[Covad Fragment]] he is studying.
