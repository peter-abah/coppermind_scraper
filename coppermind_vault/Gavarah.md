**Gavarah** is scholar on [[Roshar]] during the [[Era of Solitude]].{{book ref|twok|29}}

At age twenty, Gavarah proposed the theory of the three realms.{{book ref|twok|29}} This is likely a version of [[Realmatic Theory]]. Princess [[Jasnah Kholin]] used Gavarah as an example for Brightness [[Shallan Davar]] to show that young people can change the world.{{book ref|twok|29}}
