**[[Lady ZoBell]]'s Party** is an event that takes place in [[ZoBell Tower]] in [[Elendel]].{{book ref|mb5|8}} 

This is the first party held in the penthouse of [[ZoBell Tower]] and was planned around the governor’s spring dinner and policy speech.{{book ref|mb5|5}}{{book ref|mb5|8}} Invitations were sent to the upper crust of the society, both in Elendel and beyond.{{book ref|mb5|9}}

As [[Steris]] predicts, the coach attendants at the entrance of the tower were not properly prepared to handle all of the traffic and it backs up significantly.{{book ref|mb5|8}} This is complicated by increased security due to the assassination attempt on the governor earlier that day. As a result they are turning away anyone who is not specifically on the guest list, even if they have procured an invitation.{{book ref|mb5|9}}  

[[Wayne]] and [[Marasi]] both end up on the list of people who are not to be let in under any circumstances. Wayne uses a time bubble to sneakily examine the guest list and determines that he should impersonate Professor [[Hanlanaze]]. Marasi aids him in this disguise.{{book ref|mb5|9}}

While Wax and Steris are talking to Governor Innate, Bleeder begins to talk mentally to Wax. The conversation includes a threat to Wax's father, who Wax takes to mean [[Jackstom Harms|Steris's father]]. After carefully studying the movements of everyone else in the room, Wax concludes that Bleeder is impersonating a particular waiter. There is a scuffle in which Wayne tackles the waiter and is dog-piled by the governor's guards. The waiter escapes in the confusion and Wax spots him near a window. Bleeder tries to distract Wax by mentioning Lessie, but Wax shoots at the waiter anyway and misses, hitting a window instead. The waiter smashes the window and leaps out. Wax follows.{{book ref|mb5|10}}
