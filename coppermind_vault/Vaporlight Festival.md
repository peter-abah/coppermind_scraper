The **Vaporlight Festival** is an [[Iriali]] holiday celebrated in, at the very least, [[Rall Elorim]]. The festival occurs during the first days of the Weeping, when rainbows spill across the [[Stormfalls]] most brightly. The citizens take rafts onto the [[Rall Elorim#Structure|reservoir]] to celebrate, singing and dancing together. They also don elaborate [[Spren#vaporspren|vaporspren]] costumes in myriad colors. During the [[True Desolation]] however, many Iriali worried that this festival would become a flashpoint for tension between the Fused and the Iriali [[Rall Elorim#Culture|resistance]].{{rpg ref|slwg|155}}
