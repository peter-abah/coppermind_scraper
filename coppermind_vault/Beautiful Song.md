**Beautiful Song** is an [[Unkalaki]] on [[Roshar]]. She is [[Lunamor]] and [[Tuaka]]'s youngest daughter. Because her mother's nickname is Song, she is sometimes referred to as "Little Song". Beautiful Song is presumably a nickname as well; her full name is unknown.

Beautiful Song hails from the [[Horneater Peaks]]. She arrives at the [[Shattered Plains]] with her family in {{Rosharan date|1174}} after traveling for months to meet with [[Lunamor]], who had sent them a message shortly after his arrival. During the trip, Beautiful Song's caravan was attacked by [[Voidbringer]]s and her family defended themselves behind a pile of furniture; one of them managed to shoot a [[singer]] with an [[Unkalaki]] arrow. They were found by [[Bridge Four]] during a patrol and taken to [[Urithiru]].{{book ref|sa3|37}}

Beautiful Song was only four years old when the family reunited with Lunamor, and she did not remember him after he had been away for so long, breaking his heart.{{book ref|sa3|37}}

In {{Rosharan date|1175}}, Lunamor takes Beautiful Song and the rest of his family (except for [[Cord]] and [[Gift]]) back to the Horneater Peaks.{{book ref|sa4|12}} They do not plan to return.{{book ref|sa4|114}}
