**Beautiful Song** is an [[Unkalaki]] on [[Roshar]]. She is [[Lunamor]] and [[Tuaka]]'s youngest daughter. Because her mother's nickname is Song, she is sometimes referred to as "Little Song". Beautiful Song is presumably a nickname as well; her full name is unknown.

Beautiful Song hails from the [[Horneater Peaks]]. She arrives at the [[Shattered Plains]] with her family in  after traveling for months to meet with [[Lunamor]], who had sent them a message shortly after his arrival. During the trip, Beautiful Song's caravan was attacked by [[Voidbringer]]s and her family defended themselves behind a pile of furniture; one of them managed to shoot a [[singer]] with an [[Unkalaki]] arrow. They were found by [[Bridge Four]] during a patrol and taken to [[Urithiru]].

Beautiful Song was only four years old when the family reunited with Lunamor, and she did not remember him after he had been away for so long, breaking his heart.

In , Lunamor takes Beautiful Song and the rest of his family (except for [[Cord]] and [[Gift]]) back to the Horneater Peaks. They do not plan to return.
