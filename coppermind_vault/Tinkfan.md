**Tinkfans** are a sweet pastry from [[Pahn Kahl]] on [[Nalthis]]. They are considered a delicacy, eaten by [[Returned]] in the [[Court of Gods]].{{book ref|wb|36}} 

One is supposed to eat tinkfans in small bites and eat a piece of bread in between bites to clear away the taste.{{book ref|wb|36}}
