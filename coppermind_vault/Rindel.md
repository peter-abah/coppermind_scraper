**Rindel** was a young [[Terrisman]] on [[Scadrial]] during the end of the [[Final Empire]].

[[Tindwyl]] commented on his hot-headed nature. He is likely to have died during the genocide of [[Terris]] by the [[Steel Inquisitor]]s.
