The **Lichfather** was a being in [[Alornia]] who had a great hoard of tomes and manuscripts that came to be in [[Kairominas]]'s possession. The books from this hoard include visions of the other [[State]]s and have valuable information on abilities that may function in a [[State]] other than one's own. {{book ref|ps|2}}
