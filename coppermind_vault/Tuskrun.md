**Tuskrun** is a predatory species on [[First of the Sun]].{{book ref|iote|14}}

They are native to the isles of the [[Pantheon]], including [[Patji (island)|Patji]].{{book ref|iote|14}} Tuskrun are nearly the size of humans and hunt in packs, primarily during the day. They follow predictable movement patterns unless they are attracted by the blooming of one of [[Cakoban's finger]]s. It is possible that they are omnivorous, as tuskrun make mounds while rooting around in the ground.{{book ref|iote|14}}

The trapper [[First of the Sky]] was killed by a tuskrun pack on Patji that had been attracted by blooming Cakoban's fingers.{{book ref|iote|14}}
