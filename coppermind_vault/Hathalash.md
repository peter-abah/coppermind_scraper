**Hathalash** is an ambitious and violent Regal fighting in [[Alethkar]] during the [[True Desolation]]. He strives to impress the Fused with his passion, which has lead him to clash frequently with [[Karanak]]’s besieged defenders. He tends to call himself *Brightlord* Hathalash, although he isn’t a true brightlord.{{rpg ref|slwg|152}}
