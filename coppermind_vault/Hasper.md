**Haspers** are a form of mollusk on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa1|64}} A **stranna hasper** refers to a variety of the mollusk that can be cooked and eaten.{{book ref|sa3|13}}

## Appearance and Behavior
Haspers are comprised of two shells{{book ref|sa1|44}} that enclose a slimy interior.{{book ref|sa2|55}} These two shells open and close in rhythm with one another.{{book ref|sa1|64}} Due to this motion, haspers are often compared to tiny mouths that are breathing. At least some varieties of haspers have pearly shells.{{book ref|sa1|64}} They may be related to tiny snails.{{book ref|sa1|44}}

Haspers are creatures that appear to spend most, if not all, of their life totally stationary.{{book ref|sa1|44}}{{book ref|sa1|63}} They grow in clusters,{{book ref|sa1|44}} attached to the side of objects made of stone,{{book ref|sa2|69}} wood,{{book ref|sa1|3}} or on [[shalebark]].{{book ref|sa1|64}} A cluster of haspers can support the weight of a few waterskins.{{book ref|sa1|63}}

Haspers can be found in a wide variety of places across eastern Roshar, including the docks of [[Kharbranth]],{{book ref|sa1|3}} [[Hearthstone]],{{book ref|sa1|44}} and the chasms of the [[Shattered Plains]].{{book ref|sa1|55}} They reproduce during the [[Weeping]], with clusters seeming to grow out of nowhere.{{book ref|sa1|44}} It is unknown what haspers feed on.{{book ref|sa1|3}}

==Culinary Use==
[[Adolin]] included steamed stranna haspers in a platter of food that he brought to [[Shallan]]'s quarters in [[Urithiru]] after training her in the use of a [[Shardblade]].{{book ref|sa3|13}}

[[Eshonai]] ate a meal of steamed haspers just before discovering the humans.{{book ref|sa4|45}}
