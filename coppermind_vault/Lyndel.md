**Lyndel** was a soldier in [[Amaram]]'s army on [[Roshar]].

He was a member of [[Kaladin]]'s squad, and was killed by a [[Helaran Davar|Shardbearer]] who attacked. Lyndel was trampled, and had his face smashed in before dying.
