**Allahn** was a poet on [[Roshar]].

> Radiant / of birthplace / the announcer comes / to come announce / the birthplace of Radiants
> — A [[ketek]] by Allahn referring to [[Urithiru]]

[[Jasnah Kholin]] believed Allahn, along with others, mistook the home of the [[Knights Radiant]]--Urithiru--as their birthplace.
