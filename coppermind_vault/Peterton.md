> "You see Peterton there? Shouldn't he be on the 3:15 back to Georgiabama?"
> — [[Davis]] to [[Joel Saxon]]

**Peterton** is a student at [[Armedius Academy]].

When Inspector [[Harding]] arrived on campus, he was in a crowd outside Principal [[Thomas York|York's]] office, trying to peak through the window. [[Davis]] recognized him from a distance and thought the was supposed to have taken the 3:15 [[springrail]] train back to [[United Isles of America#The Sixty Islands|Georgiabama]].
