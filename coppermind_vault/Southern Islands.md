The **Southern Islands** are a series of isles between the [[Sea of Yomend]] and [[Sea of Lennes]], once known as the [[Southern Sea]]. Very little is known about them.

During the time of the Final Empire, it had several settlements on it, and at least one nation existed on it during Classical Scadrial.

The [[Bennet]] used to live on the Southern Islands. They were a highly developed people and were famous for being brave seafarers and brilliant cartographers. Some of the maps the Final Empire used were developed by Bennet explorers. Their belief was the [[Truths of the Bennet]].

They have an unnamed mountain range running down their length.
