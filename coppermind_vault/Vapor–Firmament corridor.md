{{spoiler|iote}}
The **Vapor-Firmament corridor** is a location near the [[Cognitive Realm#Drominad subastral|Drominad subastral]] in the [[Cognitive Realm]]. The [[Malwish Empire|Malwish]] have patrols along the Vapor–Firmament corridor to prevent people from searching and finding the [[perpendicularity]] on the planet [[First of the Sun]]. The Malwish have also made declarations, stating that the area is off-limits, and under their exploratory jurisdiction.{{book ref|iote|36}}
