**Silajana** is one of the primal [[aether]]s from [[Dhatri]], being one of the roseite aethers.{{book ref|mb7|39}} Like all aethers he predates the [[Shattering]], though the aethers' religious followers argue they even predate [[Adonalsium]].{{book ref|mb7|42}} He was eager to assist the [[Ghostbloods]] in their mission to prevent [[Autonomy]] from taking over [[Scadrial]],{{book ref|mb7|39}}{{book ref|mb7|40}} but also seemed reluctant to simply kill soldiers of the [[Set]] and asked his aetherbound to offer them surrender.{{book ref|mb7|51}}
