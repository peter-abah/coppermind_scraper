**Silajana** is one of the primal [[aether]]s from [[Dhatri]], being one of the roseite aethers. Like all aethers he predates the [[Shattering]], though the aethers' religious followers argue they even predate [[Adonalsium]]. He was eager to assist the [[Ghostbloods]] in their mission to prevent [[Autonomy]] from taking over [[Scadrial]], but also seemed reluctant to simply kill soldiers of the [[Set]] and asked his aetherbound to offer them surrender.
