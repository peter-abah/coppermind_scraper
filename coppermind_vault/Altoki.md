**Altoki** is a [[listener]] who becomes one of the [[Fused]].

[[Ulim]] selected [[Venli]], [[Demid]], [[Melu]], [[Mrun]], Altoki and five others to open themselves to "new power" during the [[Everstorm]]. The group was not aware that they were volunteering their bodies as vessels for the Fused, which would effectively destroy their original identities. [[Odium]] prevented Venli from being chosen by a Fused, but all of the other singers were either possessed or killed. Altoki was possessed by an unnamed Fused that seemed predatory and deranged.
