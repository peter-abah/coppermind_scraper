**House Entrone** is a [[noble]] house on [[Scadrial]] during the [[Final Empire]] and after the [[Catacendre]]. 

==History==
===Final Empire===
During the [[Final Empire]], House Entrone was not one of the great houses of [[Luthadel]]. However, it had enough clout to be considered a major political ally of [[House Tekiel]]. When [[Kelsier]] was sowing chaos among nobles as a prelude to the [[skaa rebellion]], he murdered [[Charrs Entrone]] and dumped the corpse in [[Keep Tekiel]]'s gardens.
===Post-Catacendre===
The house survived through the [[Final Ascension]] and had a presence in [[Elendel]]. [[Hughes Entrone]] was among [[Bleeder]]'s victims at [[Winsting Innate's auction]] and [[Valette Entrone]] from Elendel once courted [[Waxillium Ladrian]]. Her cousin, a noble named [[Gave Entrone]], lived in [[New Seran]] and worked in the textile industry.
