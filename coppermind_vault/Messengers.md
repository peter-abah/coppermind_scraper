**Messengers** are mysterious [[Investiture|Invested]] beings that are able to deliver messages across the [[Cognitive Realm]]; they are capable of reading [[Connection]] in order to find anyone, wherever they are. They are known to work for [[Silverlight Mercantile]]. 

[[Harmony]] used one to send a message to [[MeLaan]] about [[Wayne]]'s death.
