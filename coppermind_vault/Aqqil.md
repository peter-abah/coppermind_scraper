**Aqqil** is the heroine of a famous [[Azish]] folk tale on [[Roshar]]. In the story, she dresses up as a boy (eluding the Azish ban on military service for women) and goes to war to save her brother. At least one modern rendition of her story has been published. 

In , Aqqil inspired [[Zabra]] when she attempted to join the [[coalition of monarchs|coalition]] army at the [[Battle of Azimir]]. [[Challa]] was familiar with the story, but [[Adolin]] was not.
