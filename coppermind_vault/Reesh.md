**Reesh** is a soldier in [[Amaram]]'s army in [[Alethkar]] on [[Roshar]].

He fights in the battle against [[Hallaw]] in which [[Helaran Davar]] is killed by [[Kaladin]]. Just prior to Kaladin attacking Helaran, Reesh yells at Kaladin to stop, and is unheeded.{{book ref|sa1|47}} He is executed by [[Amaram]]--along with [[Hab]], [[Alabet]], and [[Coreb]]--after Kaladin gives Coreb Helaran's [[Shardblade]].{{book ref|sa1|51}}
