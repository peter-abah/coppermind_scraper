**Reep** is a soldier during the [[True Desolation]] on [[Roshar]]. He fought in the [[Battle of Azimir]], defending the city from invaders during the days leading up to the [[contest of champions]]. During this period, he is [[Adolin Kholin]]'s second armor standby, behind [[Moore]].

Reep is presumably [[Alethi]]; Adolin considers him to be an excellent soldier and well trained in the use of [[Shardplate]].

## Trivia
* Reep is likely a cameo for Ally Reep and Logan Reep, members of the [[Dragonsteel Entertainment]] merchandising team that are mentioned in the acknowledgements of several other works.
