**Perez** is a former detective who was killed during a shootout that occurred, some many years prior. [[Anthony Davis|Davis]] had the opportunity to shoot the guy that ended up killing her, something that traumatized Davis and was part of the reason he was moved to the [[Snapshot (event)|Snapshot]] division.
