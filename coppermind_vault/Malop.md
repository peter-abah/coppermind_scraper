**Malop** is a member of [[Bridge Four]] on [[Roshar]].

==Appearance and Personality==
He has thick, black hair and a bushy beard. His hands are both tattooed with a [[glyphward]]. He isn't considered very smart, and he has limited knowledge of the world beyond [[Alethkar]].

==History==
During [[Kaladin]]'s first run leading Bridge Four, Malop was one of the last men to get into position.

He was killed by the [[Parshendi]] during the [[Battle of the Tower]]. He suffered a severe axe wound to the arm and subsequently lost too much blood. Kaladin regretted being unable to save Malop, [[Jaks]], and [[Narm]] during the battle.

Malop was one of the many people that Kaladin remembered being unable to protect while he was trying to swear the [[Immortal Words|Fourth Ideal]] of the [[Windrunners]].
