***The Sixth Incarnation of Pandora*** is a far-future cyberpunk sci-fi novel about immortal warriors who have been turned into superweapons with nanites.  It is the fifth novel [[Brandon Sanderson]] wrote, originally completed in 1999. It is unlikely that it will ever be published.

The story is about an immortal warrior named Zellion who has lived too long and lost connection to the world around him because he has lost his connection to people. It features a man who used nanotechnology to make himself immortal, but was also driven insane by the process and the consequences thereof. Brandon has described it as the "most un-Brandon-like" book he has ever written as well as it being dark, grisly, and violent.

==Trivia==
*The name and some aspects of the character of Zellion were later [[Zellion|repurposed]] for use in [[The Sunlit Man]]
*Sanderson considers The Sixth Incarnation of Pandora to potentially be his worst title.
