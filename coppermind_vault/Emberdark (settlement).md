**Emberdark** is a settlement in the [[Sea of Lost Lights]] in [[Shadesmar]], the [[subastral]] of [[Roshar]]. It is south of the [[Nor Channel]] and is adjacent to the [[Emberdark Channel]]. It is along the [[Glasswater Deep]].

In the [[Physical Realm]], Emberdark seems to lie along the [[Deathbend River]].
