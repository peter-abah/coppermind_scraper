**Kamo** is a [[Kerztian]] herb found on [[Taldain]]. It can be used as a drug to heighten emotions.

[[Sharezan]] used kamo as part of a torture designed to frighten [[Raagent]] to death. The herb likely needs to be consumed to have a psychoactive effect, as [[Ais]] was able to see traces of kamo on Raagent's lips.
