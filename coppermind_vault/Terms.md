**Terms** is an [[Epic]] with time-manipulation powers. 

## History
She ruled a group of nomadic traders called the UTC. The UTC sent a few shipments of wooden crates to the city of [[Ildithia]], specifically to the [[Stingray Clan]].{{book ref|calamity|13}}{{book ref|Calamity|18}} It is unknown if Terms was in attendance with these shipments, as [[David]] thought it would be wise to not look for her. 

## Attributes and Abilities
{{theory}}
Little is known about her powers other than that they involve time manipulation. It is likely that she is a "High Epic", since most time manipulation powers allow the user to escape conventional means of death (slowing or stopping time and dodging a bullet, going back in time to escape an unfortunate circumstance, etc.).
