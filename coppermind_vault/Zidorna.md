**Zidorna** is a location in the [[cosmere]]. Not much is known about Zidorna, other than that it presumably has a few [[Iriali]] occupants. [[Crux]] and [[Nol]] falsely claimed that they were attempting to return to Zidorna when they had to be rescued in the [[Cognitive Realm#Emberdark|emberdark]] by the crew of the *[[Dynamic Storyteller's Incredible Conveyance|Dynamic]]*.{{book ref|iote|28}}
