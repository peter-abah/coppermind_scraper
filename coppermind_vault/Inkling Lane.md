**Inkling Lane** is a street in the city of [[Elendel]] on Scadrial. Wayne owns 662 Inkling Lane, and [[Call and Son and Daughters Accounting and Estate]] recognizes it as his home. 662 Inkling Lane presumably passed to [[Allriandre Durkel]] upon Wayne's death.
