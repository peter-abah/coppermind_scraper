**Dip** is a member of the [[Set]] in [[Bilming]] on [[Scadrial]]. He’s not terribly important within the Set. He usually wears work clothing; suspenders, trousers and buttoned shirts.{{book ref|mb7|21}} 

## History
During {{date|348|PC}}, he was operating out of a Set safehouse in Bilming, waiting for a shipment of goods to be brought by other Set members. However, the goods had been apprehended by the Elendel constables a short while before, and the constabulary planned to go along with the original meeting and carry out a raid against the Set. After the trucks carrying their goods arrived, Dip and another man named [[Embrier]] walked out to meet the [[Set#organisation|Cycle]] heading the convoy. They talked with him, and Dip became increasingly suspicious of him.{{book ref|mb7|21}}

After [[Wayne]], disguised as [[Franis]], took Embrier aside to talk to him, the Cycle, who was actually [[VenDell]] in disguise, talked to Dip about their goods. Embrier, convinced by Wayne that they were actually Set members, told VenDell to begin unloading his trucks, and he and Dip went to get the [[Set#Organisation|Sequence]]. While they were gone, their warehouse was raided by the constabulary, although these weren’t Elendel but instead Bilming constables. It’s likely Dip was either killed or taken captive during the aftermath of this sting.{{book ref|mb7|21}}
