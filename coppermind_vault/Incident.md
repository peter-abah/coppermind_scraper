The **Incident** is the term used by the [[father machine]]'s scholars to refer to the event on [[Komashi]] in which some [[hijo|spirits]] were able to escape the machine and make contact with the residents of [[Futinoro]]. [[Honam]] believes the Incident occurred because the father machine was experiencing a power shortage.

The Incident resulted in the destruction of Futinoro.
