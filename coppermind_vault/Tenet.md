The **Tenets** are a [[lighteyes|lighteyed]] family in [[Alethkar]] on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa3|77}}

The Tenet family owns a small palace called the [[Mausoleum]] in [[Kholinar]]. It is possible to buy floorplans of the family's home, likely through illegal means.{{book ref|sa3|77}} They have a tradition of [[Soulcasting]] their favorite servants into statues post-mortem, decorating the mansion's grand hall with it, which is where its name came from. In addition, they are said to be extremely wealthy, with numerous riches hidden within their home.{{book ref|sa3|77}} 

While in Kholinar, [[Shallan]] and her group steal their food to gain access to the [[Cult of Moments]]' revel.{{book ref|sa3|77}}

## Trivia
Their name is similar to *tanat*, the [[Rosharan]] word for number nine.{{book ref|sa3|part=ars}}
