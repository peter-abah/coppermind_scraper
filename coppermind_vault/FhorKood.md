**FhorKood** is a [[kandra]] of the Fifth Generation on [[Scadrial]].{{book ref|mb3|78}} He is one of the Fifths that supported [[KanPaar]] and the Second Generation during their attempt to overthrow the First Generation. He serves as one of the guards of the imprisoned Firsts.{{book ref|mb3|78}}

## Appearance and Personality
He displays contempt for TenSoon, calling him a "dirty Third", referring to the older kandra's generation. He is actually terrified of TenSoon's abilities.{{book ref|mb3|78}}

## Attributes and Abilities
He has the typical abilities of a kandra, and uses a [[True Body]] made of granite.{{book ref|mb3|78}} He presumably has the [[Blessing of Potency]].{{book ref|mb3|75}}{{ref|group=fn|text=FhorKood is not expressly stated to have the Blessing of Potency, but he is very likely among the group of Fifths with this Blessing who physically overpower the Firsts{{book ref|mb3|75}} and he participates in the fight against TenSoon.{{book ref|mb3|78}}}} 

## History
When [[KanPaar]] and the Second Generation initiated their coup, FhorKood and some other members of the Fifth Generation supported them. Fifth Generation kandra with rock bones and Blessings of Potency assisted the Seconds in physically overpowering and imprisoning [[Haddek]] and the rest of the Firsts, along with Sazed.{{book ref|mb3|75}} 

[[TenSoon]], in his wolfhound body, broke into the [[kandra Homeland]] and freed Sazed with the help of [[MeLaan]] and other kandra revolutionaries. However, TenSoon had been seen entering the Homeland, and FhorKood and other Fifths soon came to subdue him. TenSoon was forced to retreat, but FhorKood and the other Fifths did not expect Sazed's [[Feruchemy]]. Using [[iron]] and [[steel]], Sazed was able to incapacitate most of the Fifths by breaking their bones.{{book ref|mb3|78}}

TenSoon held FhorKood down and demanded to know what he and his compatriots had done. FhorKood refused to speak except to insult TenSoon. After Sazed realized that some of the Firsts were still alive, TenSoon threatened FhorKood and demanded he turn over his True Body; a panicked FhorKood relinquished the bones and slinked away. This allowed TenSoon to impersonate FhorKood so he could tell the Second Generation that the traitor, TenSoon, had been captured. This bought time so that the rescued First Generation could reform their bodies.{{book ref|mb3|78}} FhorKood's fate after this point is unknown.

TenSoon was still wearing FhorKood's former body when [[Ruin]] forced him to attack Sazed.{{book ref|mb3|79}}
