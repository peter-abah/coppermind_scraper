**Kallib** is a soldier in the [[Malwish Empire#military|Malwish]] military. He was present on the mission to discover a new [[perpendicularity]] in the [[Cognitive Realm#Drominad subastral|Drominad subastral]]. [[Dajer|His colonel]], trying to impress a local named [[Sixth of the Dusk]], asked him to go on a test flight, which Kallib was already planning to do. During his flight, he flew an [[MW-232]].{{book ref|iote|38}}
