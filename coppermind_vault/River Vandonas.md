The **River Vandonas** is a river that flowed by the [[Alethi]] warcamps.

The artist [[Vandonas]] named it after himself when he visited the warcamps, but that is likely not its true name, or what everyone calls it.

The River Vandonas is possibly a tributary of the [[Deathbend River]], as it flows past where the [[Parshendi]] lived before the [[War of Reckoning]], and [[Gavilar]] and [[Dalinar]] discovered the Parshendi while exploring a tributary of the Deathbend.
