The **River Vandonas** is a river that flowed by the [[Alethi]] warcamps.{{map ref|warcamps|Alethi Warcamps}}

The artist [[Vandonas]] named it after himself when he visited the warcamps, but that is likely not its true name, or what everyone calls it.{{wob ref|10074}}

The River Vandonas is possibly a tributary of the [[Deathbend River]], as it flows past where the [[Parshendi]] lived before the [[War of Reckoning]], and [[Gavilar]] and [[Dalinar]] discovered the Parshendi while exploring a tributary of the Deathbend.{{book ref|sa1|28}}
