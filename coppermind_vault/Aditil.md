**Aditil** is an [[aetherbound]] and the engineer of the [[Dynamic]]. Her [[aether]] is dead, which is considered by Dhatrians to be terrible luck.

## Appearance and Personality
### Appearance
Aditil has brown skin with light tan—almost golden—undertones. She has black hair.  In her right hand, the center of the palm is replaced with a transparent aether the color of the sky.

## Attributes and Abilities
### Aetherbound
She completed the apprenticeship required to become a full [[Aetherbound]] but at an unknown point in time, her aether broke leaving her unable to perform the normal abilities of an aetherbound. However, she is still able to talk to spores. This lets her vitalize them as well as put them to sleep.

### Engineering
She is the engineer aboard the [[Dynamic]].

#### Lodestar
Her [[Lodestar_(technology)|lodestar]] is around ten inches wide and six inches tall. It can be pulled apart into two pieces. Hers is modern with smooth grips and felt like a controller to a game system.

## History
Her parents didn't want her to leave, saying she was too young even though she had completed the full training to become an [[Aetherbound]]. But after her aether bud cracked, she couldn't stand to stay on [[Dhatri]] and made a deal to work for passage off-world.

### Onboard the Dynamic
When she starts working as the engineer for the dynamic, she is frequently homesick and falls down on her job.

During the rescue of some [[Iriali]], she was skilled, pre-empting [[ZeetZi]]'s request and keeping the ship running at top speed . Afterwards, she commends the crew for their mutiny against [[Crow]].  

She is a key part of the first plan for the Dynamic to escape from [[Dajer]], using her ability to talk to [[aether]] to put the Scadrian's engines to sleep.  

She then comes up with a key part of their new escape plan using [[Nazh]]  and is the one to actually set him free.

## Relationships
### Starling
While initially somewhat standoffish from [[Starling]], when Starling asks about Aditil's family she shows her, opening up even more when Starling gives her a ticket to [[Dhatri]].

After the mutiny, she decides to stay with Starling and the Dynamic for longer before she goes home.

After escaping, she commits to help Starling get her manacles off, even giving back the ticket she was given.

### Family
Her family with seven children of which she is the oldest. She keeps 4 photos of them all smiling with her that she looks at when homesick.
