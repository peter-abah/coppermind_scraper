**Talnah** is an engineer and [[artifabrian]] on [[Roshar]].

==Appearance and Personality==
She is short, and her hair reaches past her ears. She wears a glove on her [[safehand]]. She is intelligent and naturally curious. She is friendly with her fellow scholars, including [[Nem]] and [[Rushu]].

==Attributes and Abilities==
Talnah is one of Queen [[Navani]]'s most versatile and trusted scholars. She worked as a jeweler before turning to optical lenses, and is an expert on a variety of subjects including [[polestone]]s, lensmaking, weather readings, and astronomy.

==History==
During the [[True Desolation]], Talnah was a member of [[Navani]]'s team of [[artifabrian]]s and scholars in [[Urithiru]]. In , she and [[Nem]] were tasked by [[Navani]] with studying the [[black sphere]] that [[Gavilar]] had given to [[Szeth]]. At the time, [[anti-Voidlight]] was not well-understood; as Talnah and Nem experimented with the sphere, they inadvertently caused an explosion that destroyed their lab, killing them both. Navani knew that she could not have reasonably expected a sphere to explode, but she still blamed herself for their deaths. She asked [[Rushu]] to perform a detailed forensic analysis on the lab.
