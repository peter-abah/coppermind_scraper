**Krent** is [[House Ladrian]]'s coachman in [[Elendel]].

He was present after [[Tillaume]] caused an explosion in an attempt to kill High Lord [[Waxillium Ladrian]], [[Wayne]] and Lady [[Marasi Colms]]. He stood outside Ladrian Mansion in a state of shock, hands on his head, eyes wide, watching the mansion burn. Wax left him there, hoping that Krent would discover the unconscious [[Limmi]] and Miss [[Grimes]] in the back garden.
