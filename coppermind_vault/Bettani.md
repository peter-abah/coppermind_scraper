**Bettani** is a scribe in the [[House Kholin|Kholin]] warcamp on the planet [[Roshar]]. She is described as being industrious, scattered and proud. Bettani is a known people-watcher and coordinates messaging between the warcamps. She has black hair and is known to wear a teal havah. At some point during the [[War of Reckoning]], she had a dishevelled look; the sleeve of her safehand frayed from overuse and her black hair falling out of its pins. As a member of the eighth [[dahn]], Bettani is accustomed to working for a living, and she enthusiastically throws herself at her profession. She loves reading and takes pride in the artistry of her writing. She is prone to expressive language, loves to talk details, and is susceptible to ﬂattery. She has the glyph to enter the [[Sebarial]] warcamp.

At some point during the war, she needed to purchase things from several trading posts in the Sebarial warcamp. However, her spanreed broke, and the messages needed to be sent quickly before rival camps could snap up the best deals. She enlisted a couple strangers to either fix her spanreed or deliver the messages to the Sebarial warcamp. It is unknown if she managed to purchase her goods. Shortly after this, she headed to the [[Thanadal]] princedom on urgent business.
