**Syles** is a desirable brand of brandy on [[Scadrial]].

[[Marasi]] offered a small bottle of Syles to [[Eriola]] in exchange for his discretion when she was performing an investigation in [[New Seran]]. [[Wayne]] was thirsty, so he 'swapped' the bottle for Eriola's own letter opener (that he had recently taken from his desk) and quickly downed the brandy. Wayne later swapped the empty bottle for Marasi's credentials, telling her she'd need to fill the bottle back up if she wanted to get her credentials back.
