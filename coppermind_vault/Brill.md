> [[Elors Durnsed|Mr. Durnsed]] should bloody well keep his head clear of these parts if he knows what's what.
> — Brill, addressing a reporter for *[[The Elendel Daily]]*.

**Brill** is a construction worker in [[Elendel]] on [[Scadrial]]. He works as a line riveter and is a member of the [[United Tradeworkers Union]]. When he learned of [[Elors Durnsed]]'s apparent abandonment of his duties as union chief, he and many of his colleagues became extremely angry, nearly to the point of violence.
