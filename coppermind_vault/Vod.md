**Vod** is a [[singer]] living in [[Kholinar]] on [[Roshar]].

He is one of [[Leshwi]]'s servants. When the servants were preparing for Leshwi's return from the battle at [[Hearthstone]], [[Venli]] straightened his shirt and sash to ensure that he would not earn the [[Fused]]'s anger.
