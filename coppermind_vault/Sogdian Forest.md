The **Sogdian Forest** is a forest just outside of the [[Imperial Seat]] of the [[Rose Empire]] on [[Sel]].{{tes ref|101}} 

It is a deep and dense forest, thick enough to hide someone for a long time. [[Shai]] lay low there for a few months after her escape from the [[Rose Palace]].{{tes ref|101}}
