**Fran** is an unarmed guard at [[Mercystar]]'s palace on [[Nalthis]]. He wears yellow and gold to identify himself as part of Mercystar's staff, and he is tasked with keeping people out during the evening.{{book ref|wb|21}}

He and [[Gagaril]] were on duty when [[Vasher]] broke in. They were unconcerned to see a seemingly mad man wander in, and they offered to take Vasher to see if they could find a shelter to take him for the night. Despite their kindness, Vasher took them by surprise and knocked Fran out by sending him to the floor and stepping on his neck.{{book ref|wb|21}} When [[Lightsong]] later interviewed him and Gagaril about the disturbance, Fran had developed bruises on his stomach and neck from Vasher's attack. He was able to describe both Vasher's and [[Nightblood]]'s appearance to Lightsong when questioned.{{book ref|wb|26}}
