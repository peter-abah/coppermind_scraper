> Come on, friend. Let's get you back to the gates and see if there's a shelter that's still taking people in for the night.
> — Fran to [[Vasher]]

**Fran** is an unarmed guard at [[Mercystar]]'s palace on [[Nalthis]]. He wears yellow and gold to identify himself as part of Mercystar's staff, and he is tasked with keeping people out during the evening.

He and [[Gagaril]] were on duty when [[Vasher]] broke in. They were unconcerned to see a seemingly mad man wander in, and they offered to take Vasher to see if they could find a shelter to take him for the night. Despite their kindness, Vasher took them by surprise and knocked Fran out by sending him to the floor and stepping on his neck. When [[Lightsong]] later interviewed him and Gagaril about the disturbance, Fran had developed bruises on his stomach and neck from Vasher's attack. He was able to describe both Vasher's and [[Nightblood]]'s appearance to Lightsong when questioned.
