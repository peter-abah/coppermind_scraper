**Ehen** is a well-known [[Aonic]] poet on [[Sel]]. Their name is derived from [[Aon#Ehe|Aon Ehe]], which literally means "fire", but is also associated with danger. Poets and artists such as Ehen sometimes take a new name based on Ehe upon reaching maturity as an artist, to signify the inherent danger of artistry and to distance themselves from their older works.{{wob ref|7592}}
