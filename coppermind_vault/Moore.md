**Moore** is a soldier during the [[True Desolation]] on Roshar. He fought in the [[Battle of Azimir]], acting as [[Adolin Kholin]]'s first armor standby, ahead of [[Reep]]. Due to his position as first standby, Moore was presumably [[Alethi]] and well trained in the use of [[Shardplate]]. He donned the Plate in exhausting four-hour shifts during the defense of Azimir.

## Trivia
* Moore is likely named after Braydonn and/or Brett Moore, two members of the [[Dragonsteel Entertainment]] team.
