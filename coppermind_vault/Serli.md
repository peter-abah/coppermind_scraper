**Serli** is a female [[artifabrian]] on [[Roshar]]. She generally adorns herself with fractured gemstones. Serli is an ambitious, charming and talented, though lazy, artifabrian.{{rpg ref|ss|15}} She seems to be a powerful and somewhat well known artifabrian, as she is said to have the power to ban someone from an event like the [[Urithiru Grand Showcase]].{{rpg ref|ss|17}} The thing she wants most in life is to get rich enough to live a life of leisure.{{rpg ref|ss|15}}

To become wealthy, Serli planned to commit insurance fraud; she designed a fabrial, a pair of boots that lets its wearer reduce their weight, and hired a thief named [[Fane]] to steal it just before the first ever Urithiru Grand Showcase.{{rpg ref|ss|15}} After a group of people investigating the theft came to question her, she encouraged them to chase after a goat she saw dash out the showroom’s back door. Fane was supposed to meet her at an eatery named [[Talamin's Kitchen]], where she had reserved a private booth, and hand the fabrial back to her.{{rpg ref|ss|16}} It is unknown if the plot succeeded, though Fane is known to have managed to steal the fabrial.{{rpg ref|ss|15}}
