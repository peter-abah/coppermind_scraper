> Your people have a tradition, I believe. Bearn-gisel? Did I say that right?
> — Ulric to Ealstan

**Bearn-gisel** is a tradition, likely across all of [[Weswara]], but at least within [[Stenford]]. It involves the taking of a hostage, in order to keep a local lord under control. If the lord stays in line, they may be allowed to visit the hostage.

[[Ulric Stromfin]] refers to the tradition when talking with [[Ealstan]], just prior to kidnapping [[Wyrm]]. Ulric mistook Wyrm to be Ealstan's son, and took him [[Wellbury]] to be kept captive.
