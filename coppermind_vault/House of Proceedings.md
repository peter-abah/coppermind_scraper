The **House of Proceedings** is a building located in [[Elendel]] on [[Scadrial]]. This building houses the Elendel Senate.{{book ref|mb7|2}}

## Appearance
The House of Proceedings is a large building, fit to house the Elendel Senate. It has multiple private chambers for senators to use, although it is unknown how a senator gets one of these, as it is unlikely that they have a room for each of the senators. The main chamber, where the senate resides, is also large, with a podium lighted by large electric spotlights and observation balconies. This chamber also has special seats for visiting mayors or ambassadors.{{book ref|mb7|2}}
