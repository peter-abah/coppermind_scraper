The river **Human** is a (fictional) body of water running through the [[Roughs]] on [[Scadrial]]. It is as of right now unknown whether or not the river actually exists, as it is only mentioned in a story, and not shown to be on any maps of the Roughs. It is named for a member of the [[Koloss]], [[Human]], who survived the [[Catacendre]].

## Geography
The river Human runs through the Roughs. It is supposedly a vast waterway marking the border of what was once, before the third century PC, land belonging to the Koloss tribes. This river is said to flow extremely fast, with some people stating that its water flows at the same speeds as a train. Likewise, some (intentionally thick) stories claim it is the fastest flowing river on Scadrial. It is also supposedly full of rocks.
