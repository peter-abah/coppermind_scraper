*[[The Rithmatist]]* series is set on an Earth where the North American continent has been fractured into a large number of islands known as the [[United Isles of America]]. Sometime in the far past, the JoSeun people (who parallel the Korean people of our world), took over much of Europe. {{ref|name=smugglers}} In the history of the Rithmatist, the Mongols were turned to work for the JoSeun empire.{{book ref|Rithmatist|8}} It borrows elements of a "steampunk" setting, incorporating [[springwork]]s, a version of early industrial revolutionary technology but with more versatile applications.

## Nations
; [[United Isles of America]]: Collection of north American island states{{map ref|united isles|the United isles}}
; [[Britannia]]: Presumably the alternate Great Britain
; France: Presumably the alternate France
; Espania: Presumably the alternate Spain
; Egyptia: Presumably an alternate North Africa/Egypt
; [[JoSeun]]: An Asian empire
; [[Aztek Federation]]: South American nation{{book ref|Rithmatist|15}}{{book ref|Rithmatist|17}}

## History
Sometime in the far past, the JoSeun people, which are parallel to the Korean people of our world, took over much of Europe.{{ref|name=smugglers}} In the history of the Rithmatist, the Mongols were turned to work for the JoSeun empire.{{book ref|Rithmatist|8}} The conquest of the Americas by European imperialism seems to have foundered enough that the [[Aztek Federation]] is still a power in southern America.
