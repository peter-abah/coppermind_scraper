**Harris** is a professor of history (presumably [[Rithmatics|Rithmatic]] history) at [[Armedius Academy]].{{book ref|Rithmatist|4}}

They were unaware that [[Lilly Whiting]] had been taken by [[Harding|the Scribbler]] and became upset when she didn't show up for her history final. When informed, Principal [[Thomas York|York]] did not display any immediate concern,{{book ref|Rithmatist|4}} likely to keep the disappearance a secret and prevent a panic.{{book ref|Rithmatist|10}}
