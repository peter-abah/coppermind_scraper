> Professor Harris was furious when she didn't show up for her final, but the principal brushed it off.
> — [[Charlington]] telling his friends about [[Lilly Whiting]]'s disappearance

**Harris** is a professor of history (presumably [[Rithmatics|Rithmatic]] history) at [[Armedius Academy]].

They were unaware that [[Lilly Whiting]] had been taken by [[Harding|the Scribbler]] and became upset when she didn't show up for her history final. When informed, Principal [[Thomas York|York]] did not display any immediate concern, likely to keep the disappearance a secret and prevent a panic.
