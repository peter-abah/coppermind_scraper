**Leiko** (Callsign: **Lizard**) is a member of [[Nightmare Flight]] in the [[DDF]]. Lizard has bright blue eyes and waist-length black hair. Lizard participates in a large battle against the Krell when Spensa returns. Lizard is swarmed by several Krell, and though her squadmates try to help her she is killed.

## Trivia
* Lizard has challenged [[Nedd Strong]] to a tower-building contest with algae strips at some point.
