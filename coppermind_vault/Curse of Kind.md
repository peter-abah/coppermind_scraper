> When the Curse of Kind followed you, you learned to take what happened as it happened.
> — [[Axies]]' thoughts

The **Curse of Kind** is something that affects [[Axies]] the Collector; he specifically describes it as "following" him. Bad luck follows in the wake of those with the Curse of Kind, affecting people who interact with someone with the curse and possibly those with the curse as well. This effect is tied to [[Fortune]]. As a result of the curse, Axies has learned to be more accepting of and adjust to events that happen to him. The curse may be related to the [[Siah Aimian]]s or [[Aimia]] more broadly. It is similar to the Torment caused by wielding a [[Dawnshard]].
