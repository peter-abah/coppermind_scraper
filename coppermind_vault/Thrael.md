**"Thrael"** is an old word for slave in the [[Earth (Frugal Wizard)|alternate medieval dimension]].{{book ref|fwh|13}}

While planning how to confront a group of [[Hordamen]], [[Sefawynn]] tells [[Runian]] to call her "thrael" and order her around a lot, as she believed that would convince the Hordamen that Runian was an [[aelv]] who had performed a [[faeigerman|reverse binding]] on [[skop|her]].{{book ref|fwh|13}} Runian did so after they approached the Hordamen.{{book ref|fwh|14}}
