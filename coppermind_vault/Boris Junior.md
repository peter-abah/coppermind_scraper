**Boris Junior** is a resident of the [[Roughs]] on [[Scadrial]]. His brother is [[Boris the Third]].

[[Waxillium Ladrian]] shot Boris' father twice, for which Boris swore he would drink Wax's blood. Lady [[Steris Harms]] found record of the incident in the public records regarding Wax's exploits and contemplated inviting the Boris brothers to their wedding ceremony. She was compiling a list of his old enemies with the intent of inviting one to the ceremony so that she could prepare for the disturbance.
