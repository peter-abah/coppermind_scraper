The **Elantris trilogy** is the unofficial title for three books set on [[Sel]] following the [[Elantrian]]s and the [[Fjordell Empire]].

# *[[Elantris (book)|Elantris]]* follows prince [[Raoden]], princess [[Sarene]] and Gyorn [[Hrathen]] during the lead up and the culmination of the [[Invasion of Arelon]] and the [[Battle of Teoras]].
# [[Elantris sequel|The sequel]] will follow [[Kiin]]'s children as they interact with the [[Fjordell Empire]]'s conquest.
# We have no information about [[Elantris finale|the finale]]

Brandon had stated a wish to release the sequel in 2015, on the tenth anniversary of *Elantris's* publication, along with a re-release of *Elantris* itself. This timeline wasn't met; an update came in 2022, indicating that the 'loose plan' is for the two Elantris sequels to be released in between the releases of Mistborn Era 3.
