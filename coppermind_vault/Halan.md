**Halan** is an infant in [[Hallandren]] on [[Nalthis]] stricken with [[deathfever]].{{book ref|wb|12}}

Halan is extremely ill; he has lost so much weight that his skin is shriveled and he is not even strong enough to cry. When he is brought to the petition, [[Lightsong]] can see that Halan's [[Breath]] is so weak that he will be dead before the end of the day, if not almost immediately.{{book ref|wb|12}}

Lightsong declines Halan's mother's request and is frustrated by constantly turning away desperate petitioners. Halan and his mother are led away by the guards.{{book ref|wb|12}}
