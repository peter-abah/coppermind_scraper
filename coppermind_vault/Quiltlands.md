The **Quiltlands** is an open area in the [[Kingdoms of Light]] on [[Mirandus]]. The area is located north-west of [[Vitus Kirkas]], right by the [[Iron Coast]]. It reaches to the [[Quiet River]], opposite to [[Darkwall]].
