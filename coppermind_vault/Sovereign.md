**The Sovereign** is a historical figure in [[Southern Scadrial]] responsible for the creation of the [[Bands of Mourning]].

He helped the Southern society recover from the [[Catacendre|Ice Death]] by gifting them [[unsealed metalminds|medallions]] that enabled those touching them to tap [[brass|Feruchemical heat]]. He became their leader, both socially and spiritually, and later traveled with some of his priests to a [[Sovereign's temple|temple]] in the [[Seran Range]] of [[Elendel Basin]] where he stored the Bands of Mourning.
