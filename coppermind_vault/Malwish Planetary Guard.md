The **Malwish Planetary Guard** is a subdivision of the [[Malwish Empire|Malwish]] military. Their duties involve patrolling [[shadesmar]] and furthering Malwish goals in the greater [[Cosmere]]. The Planetary Guard was involved in the Malwish attempt to gain the use of a [[perpendicularity]] in the [[Drominad system]].{{book ref|iote|35}} It is likely that they are also involved in the Malwish' war on Rosharans.{{book ref|iote|36}}
