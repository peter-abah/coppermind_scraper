**Vexla** is a [[nimbleform]] [[singer]] on [[Roshar]]. She acted as a scout during the [[War of Reckoning]]. Vexla is wary of humans, but is also very interested in their culture, even speaking [[Alethi]]. Vexla was among the listeners who met the survivors of a failed expedition to a number of newly uncovered ruins.{{rpg ref|b9|26}}
