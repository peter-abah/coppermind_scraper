**Sot** is a resident of [[Hornhollow]].

After the [[Everstorm]] first hits, some [[singer]]s attack a grain storage outside Hornhollow and steal grain. During the change of the guard, Sot notices them, and raises the alarm when [[parshmen]], which scares the thieves off. However, only four sacks of grain remained.
