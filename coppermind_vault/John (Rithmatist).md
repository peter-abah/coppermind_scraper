**John** is a [[Rithmatics]] student at [[Armedius Academy]].

He and [[Luc]] were in Professor [[Layton]]'s mathematics class with [[Joel Saxon]], indicating that the two Rithmatists needed a remedial course on geometry basics. The two of them usually sat in the rear of the class, and they conveyed a deep disinclination to be in the same class as non-Rithmatists.
