**Uma** is a [[mistspren]] bonded to [[Dyel's mother]] on [[Roshar]].

==Appearance and Personality==
In the [[Physical Realm]], Uma appears as a collection of lights like those scattered by a prism.

==History==
When the daughter of her [[Knights Radiant|Radiant]] mistook [[Baon]] for [[Nale]], she cleared it up. Soon after, Dyel's mother was called on the [[Iriali#History|Iriali Long Trail]], and Uma was uncertain if she would be able to follow.
