**Corgi** is a [[taynix]] that [[Jorgen]] discovers in a cavern on [[Detritus]] along with several other taynix. Corgi is of the hyperslug variety of taynix, meaning it can use its [[Cytonics|Cytonic]] abilities to teleport. Corgi was carried around in a sling by a pilot in order to grow attached and bond with him, until [[FM]] took it and a few other taynix up to a [[Superiority]] transport ship with her.
