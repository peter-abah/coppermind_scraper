**Sween** is a town in southeastern [[Babatharnam]] on [[Roshar]], just north of the mountains that separate [[Azir]] and [[Sela Tales|Selay]] lands. Sween is located near the river that forms the border of Babatharnam and [[Yulay]], which flows into the [[Purelake]].
