**Edso** is a resident of [[Babilar]].{{book ref|Firefight|19}}

They are a musician (or possibly a DJ) that provided the music at the [[party in Babilar]]. [[Marco (Reckoners)|Marco]] and [[Infinity]] noticed that Edso's music had improved, and Infinity jokingly suggested that it may have been due to Edso getting heckled in the past.{{book ref|Firefight|19}}
