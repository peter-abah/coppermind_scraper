> I don't care for the hearts of mortals, Except for how they taste.
> — Xisis rebutting [[Tress]].

**Xisisrefliel**, also known as **Xisis** or **Foil** for short, is a [[dragon (cosmere)|dragon]], originally from [[Yolen]], who for a time lived on [[Lumar]] in the [[Crimson Sea]]. Because of this, the sea became known as 'the domain of the dragon.' Despite the official agreement of dragons not to meddle in [[cosmere]] events, Xisis maintains a significant involvement, and often makes servants of powerful people from other planets, in exchange for extending their lifespans.

## Appearance and Personality
### Appearance
#### Human
Like all dragons, Xisisrefliel has two main bodies, his draconic one, and his human one. In his human body, he has ebony skin, with a cool and polished sheen to it. He is bald, rightly believing that it matches well with his well tailored business suits. His eyes are silvery, chosen deliberately because of his heritage. Despite being older than the Shattering, and over ten thousand years old during the Space Age, Xisis doesn't appear like an old man.

#### Dragon
In his draconic form, Xisis has black scales, silvery hair, and [[Dragonsteel (metal)|dragonsteel]] ridges, claws and horns. He is very large, around the size of four humans standing on top of one another.

### Personality
Xisisrefliel is an incredibly old dragon, over ten thousand years old during the Space Age. This has had its effect on him; he's an incredibly cunning dragon, and is accustomed to looking ahead, generally further then anyone else. It has also made him fairly grouchy, although the dragon [[Illistandrista]] believes that he acts worse than he actually is. He is considered to be a terrible enemy, willing to chase someone across the entire cosmere for a slight. He also considers making people live in misery far more entertaining than killing them.

Xisis rejects the 'old school dragons', who spend all their time acting as gods. Rather, he is one of the dragons who wear suits, and run governments. That is not to say that he doesn't like being viewed as a god, he even has at least one whole planet worshipping him. Xisis often makes remarks about eating mortals, but he doesn't actually. He likes that there is a lot of misinformation about dragons.

As a primary patron of the [[Silverlight|Silverlight University]], Xisis is very interested in science, and he often goes to listen to the presentations given at the university. He is also somewhat of a researcher himself. Xisis is particularly interested in the decomposition cycle of the [[Aether]]s that engender the seethe on Lumar. This led him to travel to the planet, setting up a home base there to do research. While there, he also attempted to master the aethers; something he has likely achieved, as he is able to control the crimson spores that make up the sea in which he made his domain and to temporarily cure people of their [[spore eater]] affliction.

As a dragon, Xisis is nearly immortal. There are a few creatures in the cosmere whom he fears however, such as an [[Elantrian]] named [[Riina]]. He also likes drinking ruby wine, and, like many others of his kind, he dislikes [[Hoid]]. Over his lifespan, Xisis has acquired a lot of servants. He does not like putting too much effort into training his servants, and prefers they be well-liked with a good temperament. He is rather honest, feeling no need to lie; however, he is rather imperious, and the exact truth of his statements can be warped by his own self-image. He is rather honest about the way he personally feels he treats his slaves, believing that he treats them well.

## Attributes and Abilities
As a [[dragon (cosmere)|dragon]], he possesses an extremely long lifespan, and the ability to shapeshift between a chosen humanoid form and his draconic self.

Xisis has the ability to cure diseases including that of [[spore eater]]s. He claims that this is temporary, only lasting a year or two, unless the person remains in his vicinity. He can also extend the lifespans of mortals with ease, through unknown mechanics.

Xisis is also seemingly a Tenth Heightening [[Awakener]], able to Command Awakened cloth without words.

Through unknown means he is also able to control and shape the spores of the Crimson Sea to his whim, allowing him to form tunnels to his base of operations at the bottom of the ocean. This base is itself a series of tunnels made out of the crimson spores.

He is an accomplished pianist, able to improvise works at a level comparable to those of great composers. He uses his aptitude as a negotiating tool to manipulate his quarry's emotions.

## History
### Lumar
Xisis lived on [[Lumar]] in a palace at the bottom of the [[Crimson Sea]] for hundreds of years before the events of the exodus of the [[Sorceress]]. His arrival had some connection to the departure of the [[Iriali]], but the exact nature of this relation is unknown. During this time he researched the ecosystem at the bottom of the spore seas, as well as the water cycle and the decomposition of spores. 

To help with his chores and research, he had been making trades, taking a slave in exchange for a boon. [[Captain Crow]] took the *[[Crow's Song]]* above his palace and was able to get an audience with him in order to sell [[Tress]] to him. Tress, however, was able to convince him to take Captain Crow instead. When he offered Tress a boon, she asked if he could grant three lesser wishes. When he agreed, she asked for a new tablet for [[Fort]], glasses for [[Ann]], and information to track [[Salay's father]].

Like many [[dragon (cosmere)|dragon]]s, his home base is in [[Silverlight]].

### The Discovery of Patji's Eye 
After leaving Lumar, Xisis was given charge of a ship called the [[Dynamic Storyteller's Incredible Conveyance]]. He inherited the ship and its debts after the disappearance of [[Hoid]]. He typically had the crew do merchant runs through Shadesmar, and installed Crow as the ship's captain. [[Illistandrista|Starling]] visited Xisis to broker a deal with him, following a mutiny against Captain Crow. Xisis' guard, a [[peakspren]] named [[Spring]], had been instructed to tell Starling that Xisis was away, but Starling knew that he would be in Silverlight to listen to a lecture from [[Professor Lake]]. By this point, Xisis had already heard of the mutiny from Crow herself.

> Humans are generally only good as meals.
> — Xisis to Starling

Xisis' skyscraper in Silverlight has a special entrance for dragons, but due to her manacles, Starling was unable to fly up to see him, and Xisis made her wait for over half an hour before agreeing to speak to her. He considered her care for the other crew members to be silly, unbecoming of a dragon, and told her as such. They argued about whether or not [[Frost]] was still alive, and then Xisis began to play the piano. Xisis wanted to disband the crew of the *Dynamic* and send Starling away, but agreed to leave the crew intact and give Starling full ownership of the *Dynamic* if she uncovered proof of the [[Drominad]] [[Perpendicularity]].

Xisis later entered negotiations with [[Vathi]] and the [[Eelakin]] government, following the discovery of [[Patji's Eye]]. He agreed to publicize news of the [[Navigator|Navigators]], in exchange for better tariffs when the perpendicularity was eventually opened for trade.
