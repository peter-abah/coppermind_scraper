**Vennin** is a [[Trackt]] working under [[Ais]] in [[Kezare]] on [[Taldain]]. She assigns him to watch over [[Kenton]] while Ais herself is busy meeting with [[Heelis]]. When Ais returns to relieve him from his post he reports that Kenton has been studying while she was gone.
