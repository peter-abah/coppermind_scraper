**Sellafruit** is a kind of food found on [[Roshar]]. It can be preserved through drying.{{book ref|sa2|86}}

## History
During the battle with the [[Parshendi]] at the center of the [[Shattered Plains]], [[Sebarial]] stayed back from the fighting and snacked on dried sellafruit.{{book ref|sa2|86}}
