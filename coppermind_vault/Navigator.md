**Navigation** is a manifestation of [[Investiture]] on [[First of the Sun]] in the [[Drominad System]]. A **Navigator** is a person who has the ability to sense the [[Current]] of the [[Grand Knell]] and use it to navigate the [[Cognitive Realm]].

## Prerequisites

> Anyone? Do I bless anyone? Only those who are tested. And who survive.
> — [[Patji (being)|Patji]] and [[Dusk]]

Navigation is unique to the people of [[First of the Sun]]. The [[Initiation]] for this ability is not fully understood, but it is only known to affect [[trapper]]s who have survived working in the [[Pantheon]] and spent time near [[Patji's Eye]]. [[Patji (being)|Patji]] seems to have an active role in determining whether someone should be granted abilities. Some trappers cannot gain the ability to Navigate, and [[Sixth of the Dusk]] wonders if they could instead gain another unknown ability. 

Navigators need to hold [[Investiture]] in order to Navigate; this is typically accomplished by ingesting [[Luminist]]. The more Investiture the user has, the more powerful their sensing is. The ability to Navigate is constant until the user's Investiture runs out. 

It is unknown if Navigation is a manifestation of Patji's own native Investiture or a manifestation of [[Autonomy]] through Patji.

## Abilities
Navigators are able to sense pulses in the Cognitive Realm from the Grand Knell. Unlike a [[Seeker]], who can only get a bearing to the Grand Knell, a Navigator can sense subastral obstructions to the Current. Navigators can locate these disturbances from a great distance away, and can innately understand what is causing them. This is similar to how wayfinders use waves to navigate in the [[Physical Realm]], but with an exact opposite reaction, as disturbances add to the strength of the pulse rather than subtract. Navigators are able to sense things both in the unsea (the "surface" of the [[Cognitive_Realm#Emberdark|emberdark]]) and in the air. They are able to release a sound similar to a tuning fork by hitting their chest, which acts like a sonar. 

Navigators can use their abilities to chart an accurate course through the emberdark, in a way that no other known magic or technology can replicate. They can also share their ability to hear the pulses with a bonded [[Aviar]]. The bird does not need to hold its own Investiture.
