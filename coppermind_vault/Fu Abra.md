**Fu Abra** is a [[Purelaker]] village on [[Roshar]]. It is located somewhere on the Purelake and is the only village miles around it. A rare fish, known as a [[kolgril]], can be found near the village.{{book ref|sa1|i|1}} 

The buildings are built on stilts, however, they are only partially raised, with several inches of water being found covering the inside flooring. They have domed roofs, which make them resemble [[rockbud]]s.{{book ref|sa1|i|1}} 

[[Maib]]'s house is located near the center of the village. It is where [[Ishikk]] met, more than once, with [[Galladon|Grump]],{{wob ref|10085}} [[Baon|Blunt]],{{wob ref|9208}} and [[Demoux|Thinker]]{{wob ref|6683}} in their search for [[Hoid]]. Ishikk claims that Hoid hasn't been around.{{book ref|sa1|i|1}}
