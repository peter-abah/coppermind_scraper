**Unativi** is a [[peakspren]] sailor in [[Shadesmar]] on [[Roshar]]. He is the cousin of [[Ua'pam]], another peakspren bonded to [[Zu (Roshar)|Zu]]. He runs a barge and has some skill in manifesting goods via [[Stormlight]].{{book ref|sa4|26}}

## Appearance and Personality
Unativi is bald, like other [[peakspren]], with more cracks on the head than the other parts of body. He likes to wear a hat.{{book ref|sa4|22}} His deep cracks in his skin emanate a molten light which varies in intensity.{{book ref|sa4|29}}

Unativi doesn't trust humans. He doesn't approve of [[Ua'pam]] bonding [[Zu (Roshar)|Zu]] and believes that he can run from the conflict when [[Odium]]'s war reaches [[Shadesmar]].{{book ref|sa4|26}}

## History
In {{Rosharan date|1175}}, Unativi brought his barge to sail the human envoy from [[Urithiru]] to the [[honorspren]] stronghold of [[Lasting Integrity]]. He refused to take the group all the way to Lasting Integrity, instead sailing them to [[Nameless]] and leaving them there.{{book ref|sa4|22}}{{book ref|sa4|29}}

Unativi used part of his crew's payment of [[Stormlight]] to manifest goods from the ocean of beads. He got his cousin [[Ua'pam]] to help him with the task and berated him for running off to fight instead of working with the crew on the barge.{{book ref|sa4|26}} After reaching Nameless, Unativi left the barge with [[Shallan Davar]] while he and the crew looked for supplies.{{book ref|sa4|29}} On his way back to the barge, he met and talked with the [[honorspren]] [[Notum]].{{book ref|sa4|30}}
