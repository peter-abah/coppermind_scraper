The **Trackts** are the law enforcement agents of [[Lossand]] on the Dayside of [[Taldain]]. Their headquarters is in **Trackt Hall** inside of the [[Hall of Judgement]].{{book ref|ws|6|review=yes}}

The trackts are one of the eight Professions represented in the [[Taishin]] with the [[Lady Judge]], who is currently [[Heelis]], as their leader.{{book ref|ws|6|review=yes}} They are part of **The Hall of Judges, Trackts, and Scribes**.{{book ref|ws|part=ars}} Their day to day duties, however, are overseen by the Senior Trackt, formerly [[Ais]], who is currently unknown.{{book ref|ws|18|review=yes}} The trackts seek to be impartial when it comes to enforcing the law.{{book ref|ws|6|review=yes}}

## Appearance
Trackts all wear dark grey boots and pants with a lighter grey robe and a dark grey cape over that. They are all are armed with [[zinkall]]in and wear brown belts with silver buckles which have many pouches.{{book ref|ws|11|review=yes}}

## History
During the crisis in the [[Diem]], the trackts were seeking to capture the crime lord [[Sharezan]]. After the [[Taisha|Taishin]] gave [[Kenton]] two weeks to rally support for the Diem, Lady Judge Heelis removed Senior Trackt [[Ais]] from heading the search for [[Sharezan]] and assigned her as a bodyguard for [[Kenton]] to spy on him and report back about his actions and character.{{book ref|ws|6|review=yes}} In the meanwhile Ais put [[Tain]] in charge of the investigation.{{book ref|ws|13|review=yes}} After successfully raiding one of Sharezan’s dens and hearing that one of his main allies wants to split with the organization, the trackts offered sanctuary for them in return for information.{{book ref|ws|13|review=yes}} When that ally, Lord General [[Raagent]], tried to meet up with them, however, Sharezan caused an explosion at the meeting place.{{book ref|ws|14|review=yes}} Once Raagent went missing, he was found murdered by Sharezan with his eyelids cut off in his chilling cellar for wine.{{book ref|ws|16|review=yes}}

After hearing from Tain that [[Lokmlen]], one of Sharezan's associates who Ais captured when she was still heading the investigation, broke, Ais went with him to the supposed location of Sharezan's safehouse where Tain revealed that he is Sharezan and gave Ais the choice between rescuing her family or the children at the orphanage where she grew up. She told an unnamed trackt that she ran into on her way home to go to the orphanage and arrived at her house to see Kenton defeating Tain. Tain was then imprisoned to be tried for his crimes.{{book ref|ws|17|review=yes}}

After all of this took place, Ais decided to resign from the trackts and left Lossand with her family.{{book ref|ws|17|review=yes}}{{book ref|ws|18|review=yes}}
