The **Intensifier** is a [[Malwish]] weapon that fires a concentrated beam of [[Investiture]].{{book ref|iote|40}} This beam is intended to unravel permanent Spiritual bindings. The beam fires disruptive Investiture that can be positive or negative.{{book ref|iote|53}} The device consumes an incredibly large amount of power during operation.

## Description
The Intensifier is a large device reminiscent of a cannon.{{book ref|iote|51}} When activated, the beam of Investiture it fires looks like a tight beam of light.{{book ref|iote|56}} The device can fire both keyed or unkeyed Investiture with either positive or negative alignment.{{book ref|iote|56}} Negative aligned investiture causes slight warping in the air around the beam.

## History
The Intensifier was developed by the Malwish military to be used as both a weapon and a tool.{{book ref|iote|40}} Though developed in secret, a few arcanists were allowed to see it and knowledge of the device was known to some, including [[Eddlin]].{{book ref|iote|53}} It was used to open an ancient draconic cavern, an ancestral cavern that once housed a [[Dawnshard]]. The Dawnshard had since been moved, as the dragons believed too many of their kind knew where it was, but the device allowed the Malwish military into the cavern where it was stored for study and research.{{book ref|iote|40}}

The Intensifier was used during [[Dajer]]'s search for the [[perpendicularity]] in the [[Cognitive Realm#Drominad subastral|Drominad Subastral]]. The device was brought to Dajer as a way to subdue the [[Dakwara]] via an Investiture overload{{book ref|iote|40}}. However, the device was too weak to subdue the creature.{{book ref|iote|56}} The Intensifier ended up being used to weaken [[Illistandrista]]'s manacles as part of her deal with Dajer.{{book ref|iote|58}} The power consumed by the device caused technical failure in the room where the crew of the *[[Dynamic]]* were imprisoned and allowed them to escape.{{book ref|iote|40}} The Intensifier was destroyed by the [[Dakwara]] under command from [[Sixth of the Dusk]] after a Scadrian gunship the Dakwara placed down rolled over the device.{{book ref|iote|60}}
