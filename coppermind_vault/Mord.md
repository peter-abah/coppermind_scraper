**Mord** is a [[darkeyes|darkeyed]] resident of [[Hearthstone]] on [[Roshar]].

Mord likely came from a family of farmers, as he was among a group of youths (also including [[Jost]], [[Jest]], [[Tift]], [[Naget]], and [[Khav]]) that was tasked with removing [[diggerworm]]s from the [[lavis]] fields outside of Hearthstone. He hung out with the same group of boys after work hours were over, and once witnessed a fight between Kaladin and Jost.

[[Kaladin]] cited his name as an example of a 'solid, Alethi darkeyes' name, unlike his own.
