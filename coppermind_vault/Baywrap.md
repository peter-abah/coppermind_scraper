**Baywraps** are rolls of thin flat bread stuffed with boiled barley and vegetables, eaten by wealthy [[skaa]].

They are commonly consumed by [[Kelsier's crew]] in [[Renoux]]'s mansion or [[Clubs's shop]] during the [[Final Empire]]. 

Three baywraps is more than [[Vin]] could possibly eat at once.
