**Baywraps** are rolls of thin flat bread stuffed with boiled barley and vegetables, eaten by wealthy [[skaa]].{{book ref|mb1|6}}

They are commonly consumed by [[Kelsier's crew]] in [[Renoux]]'s mansion or [[Clubs's shop]] during the [[Final Empire]].{{book ref|mb1|6}}{{book ref|mb1|26}}{{book ref|mb1|27}} 

Three baywraps is more than [[Vin]] could possibly eat at once.{{book ref|mb1|6}}
