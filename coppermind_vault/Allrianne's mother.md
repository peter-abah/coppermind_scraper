**Allrianne's mother** is a noblewoman from the [[Western Dominance]] of [[Scadrial|Scadrial's]] [[Final Empire]]. 

She was married to [[Ashweather Cett]] and had two children, [[Allrianne]] and [[Gneorndin Cett|Gneorndin]]. She trained [[Allrianne]] in the ways of the [[Luthadel]] court despite residing in [[Fadrex City]]. Allrianne believed that her mother was grooming her to be an attractive bride with the intent to use her as a tool to further Ashweather's ambitions.
