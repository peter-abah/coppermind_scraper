**House Erikeller** is one of the ten Great Houses of [[Luthadel]] on [[Scadrial]] during the [[Final Empire]]. It was the sixth most powerful prior to the [[skaa rebellion]].

Its keep, Keep Erikeller, was on the western side of the [[River Channerel]], near Luthadel's Commercial District.

It likely is related to [[House Erikell]], due to the similar etymology.
