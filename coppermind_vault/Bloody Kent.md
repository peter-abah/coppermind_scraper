**Bloody Kent** is a fabled bounty hunter on [[Threnody]].

## History
After [[Silence Montane]] fabricated the story of the White Fox's death, [[Daggon]] began to instead tell stories of Bloody Kent to travelers in [[Silence's waystop]], including [[Lamentation Winebare]].
