**Kuma** is a foodstuff on [[Roshar]] in the [[Era of Solitude]].{{book ref|sa3|121}}

It is made of crushed [[lavis]] and spices, and is sometimes served in flatbread. After the [[Battle of Thaylen Field]], [[Bridge Four]] ate a meal of flatbread with kuma.{{book ref|sa3|121}}
