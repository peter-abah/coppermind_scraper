The **Askarki** are a group of humans on [[Roshar]]. The [[Emuli]] are said to be a "tribe" of the Askarki, but the Emuli are also ethnically [[Makabaki]]. This could suggest that the Askarki are not an ethnicity.{{book ref|sa1|55}} 

The origin of the Askarki people are unknown. It is unclear if they are an ancient group who have carried through to modern day, a purely modern group, or an ancient group who have faded from existence. It is unclear what the Askarki people's relationship has been with [[Makabakam]] and its people historically.{{book ref|sa1|55}}
