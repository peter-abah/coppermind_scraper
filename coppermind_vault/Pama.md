**Pama** is a carriage driver used by [[Amaram]] and a member of the [[Sons of Honor]].{{book ref|sa2|88}}

She drives the carriage taking Amaram and [[Talenelat]] away from the warcamps after the [[Battle of Narak]]. Amaram writes a letter to [[Restares]] about the return of the [[Voidbringer]]s, and the success of their organization. Amaram gives the letter to Pama to send via spanreed, which she would use when they stopped moving. She would later destroy the letter.{{book ref|sa2|88}}
