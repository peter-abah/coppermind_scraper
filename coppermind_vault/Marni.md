**Marni** is an employee at [[Jez's Duty]] in the [[Ten Rings]] section of [[Urithiru]] on Roshar.

Marni presumably has some sort of supervisory role at the winehouse, as [[Adolin]] thinks he needs to talk to Marni about the unflattering work uniforms worn by [[Hem]] and the other barmaids.
