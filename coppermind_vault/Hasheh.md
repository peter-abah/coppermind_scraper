Brightness **Hasheh** is a [[lighteyes|lighteyed]] scholar and tutor on [[Roshar]].

Hasheh worked as [[Shallan Davar]]'s tutor at the Davar estates in [[Jah Keved]] around the year . She was an expert in languages and taught Shallan some [[Azish]]. Shallan also noted that Hasheh taught her etiquette, and would chide her for having poor posture.

Hasheh abruptly left her position after one of [[Lin Davar]]'s unhinged attacks on [[Malise Gevelmar]]. Hasheh was one of a series of Shallan's tutors who quit their jobs due to Lin's instability, and Shallan generally remembered them as "stern matrons".
