**Faith** is a resident of [[Union]] on [[Canticle]] working as an official under the direction of the [[Cinder King]].

While attempting to deal with [[Elegy]] and [[Zellion]], the Cinder King instructs Faith to go to Union's command center and put the city into lockdown. They successfully lock down the city, preventing all ships from detaching from Union for one hour unless overridden by an order from the Cinder King himself.
