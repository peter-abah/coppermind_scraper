**Faith** is a resident of [[Union]] on [[Canticle]] working as an official under the direction of the [[Cinder King]].{{book ref|tsm|48}}

While attempting to deal with [[Elegy]] and [[Zellion]], the Cinder King instructs Faith to go to Union's command center and put the city into lockdown.{{book ref|tsm|48}} They successfully lock down the city, preventing all ships from detaching from Union for one hour unless overridden by an order from the Cinder King himself.{{book ref|tsm|51}}
