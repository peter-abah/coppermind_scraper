**Brell** is an officer in the [[Imperial Fleet]] of the [[High Empire]].{{book ref|firstborn}}

About a year prior to [[Varion Crestmar]] rebellion against the empire, Brell was assigned to flagship in the Fleet as [[Dennison Crestmar]]'s [[wikipedia:executive officer (military)|XO]]. When Dennison performed poorly in a battle, loosing over half of his ships, Brell remarked that Dennision "really is an idiot." Dennison overheard the remark and replied that he probably should punish Brell for insulting a [[High Officer]], but that he wasn't inclined to punish someone for speaking the truth. Dennison did, however, tell Brell that the empire must consider Brell to be a relatively incompetent officer, since they "wasted" him on serving under Dennison.{{book ref|firstborn}}
