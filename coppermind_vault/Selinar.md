**Selinar** is a [[lighteyes|lighteyed]] soldier and captain on [[Roshar]]. Selinar is a cruel man, with green eyes marking him as a lighteyes. He also has a half beard, and wears his red captain’s knot on his shoulder. Even by the standards of the Sadeas military, Selinar is an unlikeable, mean-spirited bully. He often has difficulties with another man under his command, [[Palinor]]. Selinar speaks Alethi and Herdazian.

## History
At some point while he was forty-two, he agreed to accompany Eliah Matal’s expedition to curry favor with the Matal family. The goal of the expedition was to research a group of newly uncovered ruins. As they drew close to the site however, the expedition was surprised by a chasmfiend, which attacked them while they were crossing a bridge. The captain, along with many others, fell into the chasm when the bridge broke. He survived the fall though.

Selinar, along with another surviving soldier, [[Varda]], got increasingly agitated as the group slowly got to their feet and searched for supplies. After Varda tried yelling for help, figuring that there would still be people on top of the plateaus. Selinar quickly told him to shut his mouth, realising that yelling would draw the chasmfiend back. Afraid that the beast would chase them all the way back to the warcamps, the captain drew his sword, pointing it at a [[Doral|bridgeman with a broken leg]]. Selinar stated that they would leave him as bait. Before Selinar managed to finish the poor man, a fight started between the various bridgemen and the two soldiers, who quickly began to feel the [[Thrill]]. It is unclear what happened afterwards, and whether Selinar survived.
