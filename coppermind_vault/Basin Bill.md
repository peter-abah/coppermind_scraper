**Basin Bill** is a resident of [[New Seran]] on [[Scadrial]]. He owns an establishment called [[Basin Bill's Old Time Pub & Playhouse]].{{file ref|New_Ascendancy_broadsheet.jpg|*The New Ascendancy* broadsheet}}

In {{date|342|PC}}, Bill placed an ad in *[[The New Ascendancy]]* broadsheet seeking adventurous people to join him in seeking lost cities of the [[Final Empire]]. Bill believed that hundreds of these cities survived the [[Final Ascension]], and that anyone who found them could become rich and famous by salvaging magical artifacts and other valuable items. Interested parties were instructed to apply at Bill's pub.{{file ref|New_Ascendancy_broadsheet.jpg|*The New Ascendancy* broadsheet}}

By the year {{date|348|PC}}, Bill also operated an agency called Basin Bill Tours and advertised in *[[The Two Seasons]]* of [[Bilming]] offering trips to the [[Sovereign's temple]]. These included daily re-enactments of [[Waxillium Ladrian]]'s discovery of the [[Bands of Mourning]] by [[Penelope Portreau]] and [[Trevva Cett-Venture]] and an optional day trip to local hot springs.{{file ref|The_Two_Seasons_broadsheet.jpg|*The Two Seasons* broadsheet}}
