> Looking for Adventure?
Basin Bill is looking for YOU!
> — Bill's ad in *[[The New Ascendancy]]*

**Basin Bill** is a resident of [[New Seran]] on [[Scadrial]]. He owns an establishment called [[Basin Bill's Old Time Pub & Playhouse]].

In , Bill placed an ad in *[[The New Ascendancy]]* broadsheet seeking adventurous people to join him in seeking lost cities of the [[Final Empire]]. Bill believed that hundreds of these cities survived the [[Final Ascension]], and that anyone who found them could become rich and famous by salvaging magical artifacts and other valuable items. Interested parties were instructed to apply at Bill's pub.

By the year , Bill also operated an agency called Basin Bill Tours and advertised in *[[The Two Seasons]]* of [[Bilming]] offering trips to the [[Sovereign's temple]]. These included daily re-enactments of [[Waxillium Ladrian]]'s discovery of the [[Bands of Mourning]] by [[Penelope Portreau]] and [[Trevva Cett-Venture]] and an optional day trip to local hot springs.
