**Shubalai** is a scholar who lives on [[Roshar]] during the [[Era of Solitude]].

Shubalai is so interested in the [[Nightwatcher]] that she visits her and records their entire conversation for posterity. [[Shallan Davar]] studied her account of that event while researching the various types of sentient [[spren]].
