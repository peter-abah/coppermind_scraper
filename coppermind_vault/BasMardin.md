**BasMardin** is a city on [[Scadrial]] where [[House Hasting]] was settled.

House Hasting fled there after the death of the [[Lord Ruler]], but the house's heir, [[Telden Hasting]], didn't stay in BasMardin because he feared for his safety because of [[koloss]] activities in that area and hence moved to [[Fadrex City]].
