**Logshine** is a type of beer from [[Scadrial]]. It is typically brewed in the [[Roughs]], but at least one liquor store in [[Bilming]] stocks it. After accidentally sending a truck tire through the window of the store, [[Wax]] bought two bottles of Logshine, which he considered the best beer there was, and brought them to share with [[Wayne]], who hadn't had any in years and greatly appreciated the gesture.
