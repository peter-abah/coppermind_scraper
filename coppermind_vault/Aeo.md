**Aeo** is a [[seon]] on [[Sel]]. Aeo and [[Ati (seon)|Ati]]'s masters both died at some point between the [[Reod]] and the restoration of [[Elantris (city)|Elantris]]. After the two seons recovered, [[Raoden]] decided to give them to [[Dashe]], who in turn gave Aeo to his daughter [[Matisse]].{{book ref|thoe}} The seon's name comes from [[Aon#Aeo|Aon Aeo]], which means bravery;{{book ref|elantris|part=ars}} [[Ashe]] and Raoden agree that Aeo is a good fit for Matisse.{{book ref|thoe}}
