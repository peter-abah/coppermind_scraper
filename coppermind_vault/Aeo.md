**Aeo** is a [[seon]] on [[Sel]]. Aeo and [[Ati (seon)|Ati]]'s masters both died at some point between the [[Reod]] and the restoration of [[Elantris (city)|Elantris]]. After the two seons recovered, [[Raoden]] decided to give them to [[Dashe]], who in turn gave Aeo to his daughter [[Matisse]]. The seon's name comes from [[Aon#Aeo|Aon Aeo]], which means bravery; [[Ashe]] and Raoden agree that Aeo is a good fit for Matisse.
