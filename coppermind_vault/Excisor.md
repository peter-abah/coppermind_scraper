An **Excisor** is a tool used by [[Southern Scadrian]]s to make [[Feruchemical]] [[unsealed metalminds|medallions]].{{book ref|mb6|21}}

==History==
===Gifted by the Sovereign===
Excisors are said to have been created by the [[Sovereign]], who gifted the devices to the Southern Scadrians. A [[Metalborn]] uses an Excisor as part of an unknown process to create a medallion.{{book ref|mb6|21}}

According to [[Allik Neverfar]], the Sovereign originally trained individuals known as Firemothers and Firefathers in the use of Excisors to create medallions of [[nicrosil]] and [[brass]]. The medallions were actually [[Feruchemy#Unsealed_Metalminds|unsealed metalminds]] that granted temporary [[Feruchemy]] to anyone who tapped them, even if they were not Metalborn. The first medallions were used as transferrable brassminds, providing the heat necessary for survival after the [[Catacendre|Ice Death]].{{book ref|mb6|21}}

===Expanded Use===
The Southern Scadrians later started experimenting with the devices, gaining the ability to create medallions that could provide access to other Feruchemical powers such as storing [[iron|weight]] or [[duralumin|Connection]].{{book ref|mb6|21}} After many years, they also determined a method to create medallions with multiple powers. This involved a Metalborn creating a medallion, then passing it to another Metalborn so they could add their power to the medallion, presumably using an Excisor at each step. Allik had medallions with two powers, but stated that examples with three powers were very rare and that all attempts at incorporating four powers had failed.{{book ref|mb6|21}} The number of Excisors that the Southern Scadrians possessed (and whether they were able to make more) is unknown.

==Cosmere Significance==
The Feruchemical ability of nicrosil to store [[Investiture]] is extremely powerful but poorly understood in the [[cosmere]], even by Feruchemists.{{book ref|mb6|part=ars}} The Excisors enhance nicrosil's utility by allowing anyone to use it. The medallions created with the Excisors are among very few known objects that can grant the use of Investiture to someone that does not otherwise have magical abilities; they have some similarities to [[Rosharan]] [[Honorblade]]s.{{wob ref|9408}} Although the medallions are known to have limitations in practice, in theory they could allow someone to use all thirty-two [[Allomantic]] and Feruchemical powers{{wob ref|8659}} or possibly even manifestations of Investiture that originate on other [[Cosmere#Planets|planets]] in the cosmere, such as [[AonDor]],{{wob ref|12895}} [[BioChromatic Breath]],{{wob ref|3236}} and [[Surgebinding]].{{wob ref|3236}}

==Trivia==
*Brandon has not confirmed or denied the notion that creating an Excisor results in someone's death.{{wob ref|13353}}
*[[Waxillium Ladrian]] theorizes about the creation and potential limitations of unsealed metalminds on several occasions,{{book ref|mb6|3}}{{book ref|mb6|17}}{{book ref|mb6|21}}{{book ref|mb7|9}} but it is possible that he jumped to some incorrect conclusions.{{wob ref|392}}
*In 2017, Brandon told a fan that he would explain the complex process of creating an unsealed metalmind after the release of *[[The Lost Metal]]*.{{wob ref|3345}} He wrote up several pages of details, but later lost his notes. Since the process is highly technical and must adhere to the relevant magic systems, he needs to recreate the writeup and align it to cosmere canon. He stated that he would have to do this prior to writing [[Mistborn Era 3]].{{wob ref|15981}} As of late 2025, he is writing Era 3 and has presumably canonized the process, but he has not yet released any further specifics about the creation of the medallions or the role of the Excisors.
