***The Fires of December*** is a [[cosmere]] novel by [[Brandon Sanderson]]. It is part of the series known as [[Hoid's Travails]], indicating that the story takes place on a minor planet and is told from [[Hoid]]'s point of view.

Also known as Secret Project Six, the novel was announced at Dragonsteel Nexus 2025. A Dragonsteel premium illustrated edition will be included in a crowdfunding campaign for *[[Hoid's Storybook Collection]]* in March 2026, with publication of both the premium and commercial editions expected in late 2026.

On December 4, 2025, Brandon read an extended excerpt from the book at Nexus, which was recorded as a livestream [https://www.youtube.com/live/RXyWtp8tuYY on his YouTube channel]. He also released the excerpt [https://mailchi.mp/brandonsanderson/fod-first-look in his newsletter].
