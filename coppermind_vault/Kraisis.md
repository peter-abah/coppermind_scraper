**Kraisis** is a brown haired man on [[Mirandus]] who [[Illarion]] groomed to be the [[Destined One]] from a young age.{{book ref|do1|1}}{{book ref|do1|3}}

## Attributes and Abilities

Kraisis has some magical ability taught to him by Illarion, being the one to create the doorway for [[Sir Ysla]] to travel to [[Earth (Dark One)|Earth]] under Illarion's instructions, to hunt down [[Paul Tanasin]] before he can come into his powers as the [[Dark One (title)|Dark One]].{{book ref|do1|1}}

Kraisis is a capable fighter, spending many of his waking hours training to be a great fighter. Kraisis proves his fighting ability when [[Feotora]] challenges him to a mock battle to vent her anger, and would have defeated her if not for Illarion's intervention.{{book ref|do1|3}}

## History

Kraisis was discovered as the next person to be the Destined One by Illarion, sometime during Kraisis' youth. Illarion trained Kraisis in using a sword and spells throughout his childhood.{{book ref|do1|2}}

Under Illarion's instruction, Kraisis recruited Sir Ysla and set up the ritual to transport the knight to [[Earth (Dark One)|Earth]], to track down and kill the Dark One Illarion proclaimed to be starting their rise.{{book ref|do1|1}}

Feotora brought word of Paul Tanasin's rise as the latest Dark One, when he gave himself away during the fight against a drull camp. She and Kraisis had a mock duel to relieve tension, before the King [[Gosovic]]'s death during the night. Kraisis helped lead a campaign to defeat Paul and the [[drull]] at [[Pitch]] soon after Feotora was crowned queen.{{book ref|do1|3}} Despite bringing Paul to his knees during the battle, Paul killed Kraisis, returning his soul to the soil to raise more drull and bring about their first victory against the [[Kingdoms of Light]].{{book ref|do1|4}}
