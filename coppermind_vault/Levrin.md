**Levrin** is a member of [[House Davar]]'s guard.{{book ref|sa2|61}} 

He was hired by [[Lin Davar]], along with [[Rin]], to replace the former Davar guards, including [[Beal]] and [[Ten]], sometime around the early days of Lin's involvement with the [[Ghostbloods]].{{book ref|sa2|61}} 

[[Balat]] describes him as having angry eyes, and remarks that he is not as friendly as his predecessors.{{book ref|sa2|61}}
