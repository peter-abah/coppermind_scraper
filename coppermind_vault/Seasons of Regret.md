***Seasons of Regret*** is a historical drama [[hion viewer]] program. Episodes are broadcast in the evening. The show is set in an "old fashioned" time period, approximately one hundred years prior to the [[battle of Kilahito]]. The program features Sir [[Ashinata]] and Lady [[Hinobi]], a couple who fall in love but are forbidden to be together due to Ashinata's status as ronin. After sharing a kiss in a feigned final episode, Ashinata announces that he has to leave forever, due to a threat set in motion by his dead, evil brother. He returns to Hinobi in a surprise finale episode.

*Seasons of Regret* is [[Yumi]]'s favorite program, and [[Nikaro]] considers it one of the best as well. Yumi also considers it to be the most scandalous program out of the three she watches. The pair misses the surprise final episode, but [[Izzy]] updates them on what happened. Some members of the [[Dreamwatch]] are watching *Seasons of Regret* when Nikaro visits their headquarters in [[Kilahito]], to warn them of the approaching army of stable [[nightmare]]s.
