***Seasons of Regret*** is a historical drama [[hion viewer]] program. Episodes are broadcast in the evening. The show is set in an "old fashioned" time period, approximately one hundred years prior to the [[battle of Kilahito]].{{book ref|yumi|17}} The program features Sir [[Ashinata]] and Lady [[Hinobi]], a couple who fall in love but are forbidden to be together due to Ashinata's status as ronin.{{book ref|yumi|25}} After sharing a kiss in a feigned final episode, Ashinata announces that he has to leave forever, due to a threat set in motion by his dead, evil brother. He returns to Hinobi in a surprise finale episode.{{book ref|yumi|33}}

*Seasons of Regret* is [[Yumi]]'s favorite program,{{book ref|yumi|25}} and [[Nikaro]] considers it one of the best as well.{{book ref|yumi|11}} Yumi also considers it to be the most scandalous program out of the three she watches.{{book ref|yumi|25}} The pair misses the surprise final episode, but [[Izzy]] updates them on what happened.{{book ref|yumi|33}} Some members of the [[Dreamwatch]] are watching *Seasons of Regret* when Nikaro visits their headquarters in [[Kilahito]], to warn them of the approaching army of stable [[nightmare]]s.{{book ref|yumi|37}}
