**Stormsummoner** is an [[Epic]]. He tried to fight [[Lifeforce]] and his troops at Denton. He summoned nearly a hundred tornadoes before [[Languish]] managed to dampen him. One of his tornadoes hurled a broken lamppost into [[Cloudbreaker|Cloudbreaker's]] belly, a wound that Lifeforce's abilities healed. The hole left in Cloudbreaker's belly was deadly, and later killed her when Lifeforce withdrew his healing power from her.{{book ref|lux|49}}
