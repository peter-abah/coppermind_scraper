The **Ornisaur quarry** is a quarry somewhere between the northern and southern continents on [[Scadrial]]. This quarry contains paintings of large feathered [[ornisaur]]s. Additionally, there is an [[aluminum]] skeleton of an ornisaur. During , [[Nicelle Sauvage]] and three of her companions visited the quarry. They found the aluminum skeleton, which they took with them. Later, this skeleton allowed two of Nicki's kandra companions to become an ornisaur, which led to her getting the [[Compass of Spirits]] back.
