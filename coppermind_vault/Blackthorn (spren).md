The **Blackthorn** is a [[spren]] on [[Roshar]] that personifies the stories people tell about [[Dalinar Kholin]]'s time as a warrior and general, before he sought out the [[Nightwatcher]]. He will serve as Retribution’s main general during his future wars.{{book ref|sa5|145}}

## History
While [[Dalinar]] was traversing the [[Spiritual Realm]], the Realm made a copy of Dalinar when he was attacking the [[Rift]].{{book ref|sa5|99}} Dalinar confronted and then [[Connection|Connected]] to the [[spren]], and gave it his memories, skills, and showed it what Dalinar did after the destruction of the Rift.{{book ref|sa5|145}}{{book ref|sa5|99}}

Shortly following the [[contest of champions|Contest of Champions]] and Dalinar's death, [[Retribution]] noticed the Blackthorn in the Spiritual Realm.{{book ref|sa5|145}} Due to the numerous amount of people thinking about Dalinar as the Blackthorn and Connecting with Dalinar, the spren took shape. Ultimately, the Blackthorn pledged to serve [[Retribution]] when he begins his conquest beyond [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa5|145}} Because of this knowledge and skill, the Blackthorn became Retribution's Champion, a manifestation of an alternate version of Dalinar, without the pains and weaknesses of the real person.{{book ref|sa5|145}}
