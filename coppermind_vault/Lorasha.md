**Lorasha** is an [[Alethi]] [[nimbleform]] [[singer]] on [[Roshar]]. She has dark carapace streaked with white. During the [[True Desolation]], she stole a precious gem from an Alethi noble. Sometime after this event, she paid fifty marks and became a passenger on a ship called the *[[Dalewillow (ship)|Dalewillow]]*. During the voyage, she was busy carving a wooden jewelry box. She also noticed that Fasi was hiding something from Nenal. Lorasha dislikes Alethi humans.
