**Pilevines** or **pile-vines** are a type of plant on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa1|57}}{{book ref|sa4|64}}

Pilevines produce red fruits with a stringy texture.{{book ref|sa1|57}} They can grow to "towering" heights in the [[Drunmu Basin]].{{book ref|sa4|64}}

Pilevine fruits are eaten by [[Alethi]] soldiers on the [[Shattered Plains]]. When the fruit becomes overripe, some of the soldiers throw them at [[bridgemen]]. During his time in [[Bridge Four]], [[Moash]] was hit in the face by a pilevine fruit.{{book ref|sa1|57}}
