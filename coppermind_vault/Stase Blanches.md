Lady **Stase Blanches** is a [[noble]] in [[Luthadel]] on [[Scadrial]] during the [[Final Empire]].

==Appearance and Personality==
She is beautiful and has blonde hair; she is seen wearing a green dress.

==History==
[[House Venture]] arranged for Stase to attend a ball at [[Keep Elariel]] in [[Luthadel]] with [[Elend]]. However, Elend ignored her in favor of [[Valette Renoux]], drawing Stase's ire.
