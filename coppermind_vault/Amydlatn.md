**Amydlatn** is a port city in eastern [[Thaylenah]] on [[Roshar]]. It is the closest port to [[New Natanan]] on the south.{{book ref|sa2|6}}

[[Shallan Davar]] visited there while traveling from [[Kharbranth]] to the [[Shattered Plains]] on the *[[Wind's Pleasure]]*.

According to [[Tozbek]], Amydlatn has a large collection of greatshells.{{book ref|sa2|1}} The ship stopped there and picked up the "[[new kid]]" and some others,{{book ref|sa2|6}} who were instructed by [[Tyn]] to assassinate [[Jasnah]].{{book ref|sa2|34}}
