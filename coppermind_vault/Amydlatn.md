**Amydlatn** is a port city in eastern [[Thaylenah]] on [[Roshar]]. It is the closest port to [[New Natanan]] on the south.

[[Shallan Davar]] visited there while traveling from [[Kharbranth]] to the [[Shattered Plains]] on the *[[Wind's Pleasure]]*.

According to [[Tozbek]], Amydlatn has a large collection of greatshells. The ship stopped there and picked up the "[[new kid]]" and some others, who were instructed by [[Tyn]] to assassinate [[Jasnah]].
