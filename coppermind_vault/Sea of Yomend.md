The **Sea of Yomend**, formerly known as the **Southern Sea**, is a body of water on [[Scadrial]]. It comprises most of the area between the former [[Southern Dominance]] and the [[Southern Islands]]. It is connected to the [[Sea of Lennes]].

In the [[Classical Scadrial|Classical era]], the [[Bennet]] people lived on the Southern Islands and were known to be excellent seafarers, but little else is known of their society. During the [[Final Empire]], there were some settlements on the northeastern shores of the Southern Sea, near the mouth of the [[River Channerel]] and the ashmount [[Doriel (ashmount)|Doriel]]. 

After the [[Catacendre]], the sea was renamed to the Sea of Yomend, presumably after [[Aradan Yomen]]. Two of the most important ports on Scadrial, [[Elendel]] and [[Bilming]], are situated on the Sea of Yomend. The prominent [[Irongate River]] flows through [[Hammondar Bay]] into the sea.
