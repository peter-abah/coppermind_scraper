**Fine** is a purple and orange [[taynix]] found on [[Detritus]].{{book ref|sf1|4}} 

Fine got its name when [[Jorgen]] said "Fine, but I'm not naming it."{{book ref|sf1|4}} Fine repeatedly flutes "Fine!", often in response to humans saying the word "fine".{{book ref|sf1|4}}{{book ref|sf1|8}}

Fine has the cytonic ability of communication and was used to communicate with [[Cuna]] in the novella [[Sunreach_(station)|Sunreach]].{{book ref|sf1|13}}{{book ref|sf1|14}}

[[Gill]] brought Fine to [[Sunreach]], triggering the realization that the taynix want to help.{{book ref|sf1|16}}

Jorgen brought Fine to [[ReDawn (planet)|ReDawn]] with him in case the people on [[Detritus]] needed to contact him.
