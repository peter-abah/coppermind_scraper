**Iron Seven Waystation** is a space station in outer space somewhere in the vicinity of [[Komashi]]. After the [[battle of Kilahito]], [[Hoid]] and [[Design]] made plans to steal a [[hion]]-powered spaceship and leave Komashi via Iron Seven Waystation. 

Given the name, it is possible that it is a [[Scadrian]] station.
