**Iron Seven Waystation** is a space station in outer space somewhere in the vicinity of [[Komashi]].{{wob ref|16407}} After the [[battle of Kilahito]], [[Hoid]] and [[Design]] made plans to steal a [[hion]]-powered spaceship and leave Komashi via Iron Seven Waystation.{{book ref|yumi|part=anotherepilogue}} 

Given the name, it is possible that it is a [[Scadrian]] station.
