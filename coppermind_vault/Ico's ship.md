**Ico's ship** is a merchant vessel in [[Shadesmar]] crewed by [[Reachers]], captained by [[Ico]].{{book ref|sa3|97}}{{book ref|sa3|99}} Very few crew members speak [[Alethi]].{{book ref|sa3|102}}

## Appearance
Like all ships in [[Shadesmar]], Ico's vessel does not have traditional masts, sails, or oars, and instead is pulled by multiple [[mandra]]s that are harnessed to the bow of the ship.{{book ref|sa3|97}} Lines of copper plating can be found embedded in the walls and flooring of the craft, which the [[Reacher]]s crew use to communicate.{{book ref|sa3|99}} Reachers walk around barefoot so that they can touch the lines in the floor.{{book ref|sa3|99}}

The ship has three levels; the hold, the lower deck, and the upper deck. Accomodation,{{book ref|sa3|99}} the brig,{{book ref|sa3|101}} and the hold{{book ref|sa3|99}} can all be found on the lower deck. Cabins are available for both the [[spren]] sailors and passengers, with the former being filled with hammocks, and the latter with bunks.{{book ref|sa3|99}} Ico, as captain, has his own personal rooms.{{book ref|sa3|101}} The brig is located in the stern of the ship and is where Ico detains any [[deadeye]]s, including his [[Ico's father|father]], who are travelling on the vessel.{{book ref|sa3|101}} A cramped hold can be found on the lower deck and is full of crates and barrels that are lashed to the walls and floor.{{book ref|sa3|99}} Harpoons are stored in racks along the sides of the upper deck.{{book ref|sa3|99}} 

## History 
Ico's ship stopped at [[Riino's lighthouse]] in {{Rosharan date|1174}} as part of its voyage through the [[Sea of Lost Lights]].{{book ref|sa3|99}} While docked at the peninsula, [[Kaladin]], [[Shallan]], [[Adolin]], [[Azure]], and their spren negotiated for passage with the ship's captain, Ico.{{book ref|sa3|101}} All aboard, the ship set sail for [[Celebrant]],{{book ref|sa3|99}} where it was due to unload its goods, and newly acquired passengers, before continuing further east. Despite their attempts, the group of humans were unable to convince the captain to take them to [[Cultivation's Perpendicularity]] or [[Thaylen City]].{{book ref|sa3|101}} After arriving in Celebrant, the ship underwent a standard inspection, run by [[Kyril]] and another [[Fused]]. During the inspection it was discovered that the ship had carried humans, leading Kyril to accidentally set it on fire out of anger. It is unknown if the ship was entirely destroyed in the blaze or if the fire was extinguished fast enough to save the vessel.{{book ref|sa3|102}}
