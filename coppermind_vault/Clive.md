**Clive** is one of [[Stephen Leeds]]' [[aspect]]s.

During the [[White Room]], Stephen spots Clive and [[Owen]] dressed to play golf.

Along with [[Arnaud]] and [[Mi Won]], Clive is among the aspects best equipped to think about higher-level chemistry. That is, after the death of [[Ignacio]] had stolen most of Stephen's chemistry knowledge.
