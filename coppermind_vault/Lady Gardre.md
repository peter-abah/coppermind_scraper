**Lady Gardre** is a member of the [[Elendel]] Senate on [[Scadrial]]. She served on the Governor's Council. [[Steris Harms]] suspects her to be the [[Set]]'s spy.

## Appearance and Personality
Lady Gardre is a plump woman. She is a reasonable and rational politician, much less of a war-hawk than her other counterparts in the Council. 

## History
After Steris informed Governor [[Varlance]] about a threat to the city, he established a Governor’s Select Council to investigate the issue. This Council included Lady Gardre, [[Darlin Cett|Lord Cett]] and [[Mari Hammondess|Lady Hammondess]]. Throughout the Council's discussions, Steris suspected one of them to be a spy for the Set but could not decide who. When the Council unanimously declared that war with [[Bilming]] was inevitable, Steris surmised that Gardre was most likely to be the spy, her reasonable nature a front for Set's subterfuge.

With a bomb threat on [[Elendel]] imminent, Gardre excused herself from the council shortly before they decided to use the [[Bands of Mourning]]. She seemingly did not return. This and Steris' deductions hint that she was actually the spy, but it is not explicitly confirmed.
