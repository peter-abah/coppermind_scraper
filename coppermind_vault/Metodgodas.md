> Forced to heel to the Bear's dictates, Fenris brought with him the implements of metodgodas, the end of gods.
> — [[Sefawynn]] tells [[Runian]] about the [[Battle of Badon]]

**Metodgodas** refers to the end of gods. Implements exist in the [[Earth (Frugal Wizard)|alternate medieval dimension]] that can bring this about.

During the [[Battle of Badon]], the [[Black Bear]] sought out [[Fenris]]. Whilst being forced to obey the Black Bear's orders, Fenris brought the implements of metodgodas with him to the battle. This caused the other gods to retreat, as they did not want to risk their own end, or the end of the world.
