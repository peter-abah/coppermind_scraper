**Harathdal** is a [[Terris]] steward on [[Scadrial]] during the [[Final Empire]].{{book ref|mb3|81}}

## History
Harathdal was the foremost of the Terris stewards at the time of the [[Battle of Hathsin]].{{book ref|mb3|81}} During the battle, he waited at the mouth of the caverns leading to the [[Kandra Homeland]] with [[Sazed]] and other leaders, hoping for [[Vin]] to save them from the [[koloss]] and the destruction of the planet.
