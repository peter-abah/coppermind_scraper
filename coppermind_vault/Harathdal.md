**Harathdal** is a [[Terris]] steward on [[Scadrial]] during the [[Final Empire]].

## History
Harathdal was the foremost of the Terris stewards at the time of the [[Battle of Hathsin]]. During the battle, he waited at the mouth of the caverns leading to the [[Kandra Homeland]] with [[Sazed]] and other leaders, hoping for [[Vin]] to save them from the [[koloss]] and the destruction of the planet.
