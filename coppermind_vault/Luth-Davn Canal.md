The **Luth-Davn Canal** is the main canal between [[Luthadel]] and [[Haverfrex]] on [[Scadrial]] during the [[Final Empire]]. It connects to the western bank of the [[River Channerel]].

==History==
[[Kelsier]] and [[Vin]] traveled along the canal's towpath when they were trying to reach the area near [[Holstep]] and the [[Arguois caverns]] as quickly as possible in order to intervene in a battle between the [[skaa rebellion]] and the Holstep and [[Valtroux]] garrisons.

[[Ashweather Cett]]'s army settled near the canal and used it as an easy way to get supplies from Haverfrex.
