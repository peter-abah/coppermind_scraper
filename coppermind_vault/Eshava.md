**Eshava** is a [[lighteyes|lighteyed]] [[Alethi]] woman from Highprince [[Aladar]]'s warcamp on the [[Shattered Plains]] of [[Roshar]].

She is an acquaintance of Prince [[Adolin Kholin]], and accompanied [[Toral]] and some other young lighteyes (including [[Danlan Morakotha]], [[Jakamav]] and [[Inkima]]) to a social gathering at a wineshop in the [[Outer Market]]. She expressed frustration regarding Highprince [[Dalinar Kholin]]'s restrictive social policies following the [[Alethi Codes of War]]. She even suggested that Dalinar should abdicate, angering Adolin. She unsuccessfully tried to change the subject, and Toral interceded to tell Adolin that many people shared Eshava's opinion.
