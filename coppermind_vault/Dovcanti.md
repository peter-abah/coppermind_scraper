**Dovcanti** is a poet from [[Roshar]] who lived approximately 1,500 years before the [[Battle of Narak]], or about three hundred years prior to the start of the [[Vorin]] calendar.

Dovcanti wrote an epic about the [[False Desolation]], describing clashes between humans and [[Voidbringer]]s after [[Aharietiam]]. The epic was considered pseudohistorical by the Vorin church because the [[ardent]]ia did not believe any Voidbringers survived Aharietiam. However, recent revelations about the [[Herald]]s' abandonment of the [[Oathpact]] and the restoration of the [[singer]]s by the [[Everstorm]] lend more credence to Dovcanti's work.
