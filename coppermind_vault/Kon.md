**Kon** is the common currency used in [[Nagadan]] on [[Komashi]]. It can commonly be found as a coin, such as a ten-kon coin, though it may take other forms. A bowl of noodles at the [[Noodle Princess]] costs 200 kon.{{book ref|yumi|5}}
