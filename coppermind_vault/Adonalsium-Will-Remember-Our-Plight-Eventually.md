**Adonalsium-Will-Remember-Our-Plight-Eventually** is a resident of [[Beacon]] on [[Canticle]].

He is tall, thin, and stern.

He is asked by [[Zeal]] to find sleeping quarters for [[Nomad]] and [[Elegy]], however Nomad refuses to go with him.

His name is awkward and multi-worded in Canticle's version of Threnodite.
