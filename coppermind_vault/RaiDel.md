**RaiDel** is a pepper grown in [[JinDo]] on [[Sel]].{{book ref|Elantris|47}} It is a key ingredient of [[RaiDomo Mai]].

In the year prior to the [[Invasion of Arelon]], RaiDel peppers had a poor season.
