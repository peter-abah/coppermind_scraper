**Turlm** is a [[Thaylen]] sailor and a member of the crew of the *[[Wandersail (1174_ship)|Wandersail]]*.{{book ref|sa3.5|11}}

==Appearance and Personality==
Turlm has a genial personality and always shares his booze. [[Lopen]] considers him a friend, despite his tendency to eat with his mouth open.{{book ref|sa3.5|11}}

==Attributes and Abilities==
He hails from [[Thaylen City]], and is a father to six daughters there. He is a competent sailor.{{book ref|sa3.5|11}}

==History==
Turlm was a member of the [[expedition to Akinah]]. As the *Wandersail* neared Akinah and breached the [[Akinah#Protection_Storm|storm surrounding the island]], he was swept overboard by an unexpected wave that washed over the deck. He was quickly saved by Lopen and [[Rua]], and was amazed and very grateful. [[Fimkn]] then sent him belowdecks for the remainder of the passage through the storm.{{book ref|sa3.5|11}}
