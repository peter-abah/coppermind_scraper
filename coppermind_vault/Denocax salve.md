**Denocax salve** is an ointment used by surgeons on [[Roshar]] during the [[Era of Solitude]].{{book ref|sa1|38}}

Denocax salve can be used to encourage skin growth on wounds, such as skin that has been stripped away by prolonged exposure to a [[highstorm]].{{book ref|sa1|38}}

After surviving his judgement by the [[Stormfather]] in 1173, a semi-conscious [[Kaladin]] thought that he should apply a denocax salve to his wounds.{{book ref|sa1|38}}
