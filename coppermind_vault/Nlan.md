**Nlan** is a [[Thaylen]] sailor on [[Roshar]] employed as a cook on the *[[Wandersail (1174_ship)|Wandersail]]*.

He was one of three crewmembers that accepted [[Rysn]]'s offer to stay ashore during the [[expedition to Akinah]]. [[Cord]] replaced him as the ship's cook during the expedition.
