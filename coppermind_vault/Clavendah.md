**Clavendah** is a [[Roshar#Wines|wine]] drunk on [[Roshar]].

During [[Assassination of Gavilar|Gavilar's feast]] the cook came to [[Navani]] and told her that they had run out of the Clavendah and the [[Ruby Bench]]. Navani realized that [[Dalinar]] had been taking wine from the kitchens again, and told the cook to go to the monastery and ask Sister [[Talanah]] for her secret wine reserves.
