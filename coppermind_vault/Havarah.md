**Havarah** is Highprince [[Dalinar Kholin]]'s bridgelord on the [[Shattered Plains]] of [[Roshar]].

As bridgelord, Havarah was responsible for the big [[chull]]-drawn bridges used by Dalinar during chasm runs against the [[Parshendi]].

Havarah is a short man who has some [[Herdazian]] ancestry, which is shown by his dark, crystalline fingernails. However, he does not wear a [[sparkflicker]].
