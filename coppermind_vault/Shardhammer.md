A **shardhammer**, also known as a **warhammer** is an enormous battle hammer that can only be wielded by someone in [[Shardplate]]. Contrary to what its name might suggest, it is not an ancient magical weapon like Shardplate or a [[Shardblade]], but rather, a more modern creation. Dalinar's warhammer is described as having a haft as "thick as a man's wrist" with the front of the head being larger than a man's fist. It could barely be lifted by two men and was carried on a cart.{{book ref|sa1|28}} A Shardbearer's hammer is a common weapon for someone who owns a set of plate, but no Shardblade.{{book ref|sa2|26}}

## Notable Mentions
Dalinar used his warhammer to dig a latrine ditch as he contemplated stepping down as Highprince. {{book ref|sa1|28}}

Sadeas recalls an incident one year after the assassination of Gavilar in which he slaughtered a large number of Parshendi with only his hammer.{{book ref|sa2|29}}

When [[Adolin]] posed as Dalinar to meet with [[Eshonai]] on the Shattered Plains, [[Teleb]] wore a Shardhammer strapped to his back.{{book ref|sa2|51}}

On a plateau run where Adolin assists other highprinces in a failed attempt to collect a gemheart, [[Jakamav]] carries a shardhammer as a member of his team.{{book ref|sa2|12}}

During Adolin's duel-gone-bad, [[Elit Ruthar|Elit]] wears the King's borrowed Shardplate and fights with a Shardbearer's hammer.{{book ref|sa2|56}}

Before either had Shardblades, Dalinar and Sadeas both use warhammers as their primary weapon. Specifically during the battle of [[The Rift]].{{book ref|sa3|11}}

During the [[Battle of Thaylen Field]] [[Hrdalm]] uses a warhammer while battling a [[Thunderclast]] with Adolin.{{book ref|sa3|120}}
