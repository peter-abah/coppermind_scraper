**Daughnin** is a criminal in [[Elendel]] on [[Scadrial]].{{book ref|mb5|7}}

Captain [[Reddi]] of the Fourth Octant brought Daughnin in, but they ultimately escaped justice because of Reddi's unfamiliarity with the law. A month after their release, [[Claude Aradel]] used Daughnin's case as an example of how [[Marasi]]'s background in the solicitors' office made her a beneficial addition to the police force.{{book ref|mb5|7}}
