> If you paid more attention to the very laws you are sworn to uphold, perhaps Daughnin wouldn't have gotten back on the street last month.
> — [[Claude Aradel]] to [[Reddi]]

**Daughnin** is a criminal in [[Elendel]] on [[Scadrial]].

Captain [[Reddi]] of the Fourth Octant brought Daughnin in, but they ultimately escaped justice because of Reddi's unfamiliarity with the law. A month after their release, [[Claude Aradel]] used Daughnin's case as an example of how [[Marasi]]'s background in the solicitors' office made her a beneficial addition to the police force.
