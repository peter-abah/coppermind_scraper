**Cessil** is an [[UrDail]] from [[ReDawn (planet)|ReDawn]]. 

They are with the [[dione]] pretending to be [[Rinakin]] when [[Alanik]] comes to free Rinakin. Cessil delivers a tray with tea to the false Rinakin, and the imposter tells them to not come back for the tray later.
