**Lucky** is an inhibitor [[taynix]] found on [[Detritus]].{{book ref|defiant|6}} 

Lucky is one of the only two [[Taynix#Inhibitor slugs|inhibitor slugs]] that [[Skyward Flight]] brings with them during the raid on the [[Superiority]] information nexus, and rides with [[Chubs]] in [[Nedd Strong|Nedd]]'s ship. Once the Superiority's inhibitor slug is killed, Lucky puts up an inhibitor field to prevent the Superiority from calling for help or bringing in reinforcements. However, the strike force is ambushed by hidden anti-aircraft guns in the city, and one of them fires a shot that pierces right through Nedd's shield.{{book ref|defiant|6}} Though Chubs sends Nedd to safety, Lucky is killed in the explosion.{{book ref|defiant|7}}
