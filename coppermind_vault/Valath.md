**Valath** is a city in [[Jah Keved]] on [[Roshar]]. It is located midway up the leeward side of the [[Horneater Peaks]].{{map ref|Roshar}}

Possibly part of the [[Valam princedom]], Valath is the location of the **Holy Enclave**. The Holy Enclave has a store of tomes and scrolls that nearly rivals the [[Palanaeum]]'s collection, and it is the home to [[curates]] which wear distinguishing blue robes and carry ornate staves bound with [[gemstone]]s.{{book ref|sa1|7}}{{book ref|sa3|100}} In addition to that, Valath is home to a major spanreed hub.{{rpg ref|slwg|57}}

## History
[[Lin Davar]] sent [[Rin]], one of his guards, to Valath to kill [[Helaran]].{{book ref|sa2|61}} Helaran had left a contact in Valath so that [[Balat]] could get in touch with him; [[Shallan]] encouraged her brother to take [[Malise Gevelmar]] and [[Eylita Tavinar]] with him and join Helaran.{{book ref|sa2|65}} Years later when she reached the [[Shattered Plains]], [[Shallan]] paid to send a message and a [[spanreed]] to her brothers via Valath.{{book ref|sa2|47}}

The curates from the Holy Enclave traveled to [[Vedenar]] in order to excommunicate [[Dalinar Kholin]] during his tour of the city on behalf of the [[coalition of monarchs]].{{book ref|sa3|100}}
