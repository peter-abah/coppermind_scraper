**Tingmars** are a brand of cigar made in [[Weathering]] in the Northern [[Roughs]] of [[Scadrial]].

[[Waxillium Ladrian]] favors the stout nature of Tingmars. During his stay in [[New Seran]], he was surprised to find one in a pocket of his [[mistcoat]], as he thought he was out of them.
