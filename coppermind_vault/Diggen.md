**Diggen** is the individual who named [[Diggen's Point]] in the [[Emerald Sea]] on [[Lumar]].{{book ref|Tress|1}}

Diggen left Diggen's Point shortly after naming it and never returned. As such, residents of the island do not remember who he was.{{book ref|Tress|1}}
