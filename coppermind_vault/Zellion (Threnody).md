**Zellion** was a preacher on [[Threnody]] who persuaded a group of Threnodites to leave the system with him to escape the [[Evil]] and the infighting that arose amongst those fleeing in its wake. The name Zellion is of [[Yolish]] origin meaning One Who Finds, though it is unknown if Zellion was native to Threnody or Yolen.

==History==
Zellion’s followers comprised those who believed in his teachings about Adonalsium and followed him to [[Canticle]]. The group traveled in technologically advanced ships, most likely through [[Physical Realm]] space as Canticle has no easy access to the [[Cognitive Realm]] through a [[perpendicularity]].

Upon arriving in the new system, the travelers created ships based on the ones that had brought them to the planet. Many of the first generation on Canticle died and rose, like all Threnodites under the right circumstances, as shades to become the people’s [[Chorus]]. It is unknown if Zellion was among this group.

Many generations later, some on Canticle believe that Zellion may have brought the people to Canticle specifically to keep them so focused on running from the sun that they would have no time to waste on internal conflict.

==Trivia==
*The name Zellion was originally the name of the main character in [[Brandon Sanderson|Sanderson’s]] fifth [[Unpublished works|unpublished novel]], [[The Sixth Incarnation of Pandora]].
*Before the publication of [[The Sunlit Man]], Sanderson used the name Zellion as his online handle in many video games. However, he expects that he will no longer be able to use the name now that it has been used in one of his published works.
