**Lord Redeen** is a nobleman on [[Sel]].

He attends a party at Duke [[Roial]]'s mansion in [[Arelon]], and falls into a pond due to excess drink. Roial asks [[Opa]] to ensure that Redeen is provided with some towels and a ride home.

## Trivia
* Redeen was known as "Redeem" in the original published version of *Elantris*, but his name was changed in the tenth anniversary edition.
