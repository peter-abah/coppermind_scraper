**Thaddius** is a [[Rithmatics]] student at [[Armedius Academy]].

He is in the class ahead of [[Melody]] and [[Joel]]. He and [[Virginia]] are attacked by a mob with bottles after church, and draw [[chalklings]] to disperse the crowd.
