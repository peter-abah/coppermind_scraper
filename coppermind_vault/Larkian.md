The **Larkians** were a people who lived in the [[State]] of [[Alornia]]. They were originally from a separate country than [[Kairominas]], but their king eventually abdicated to Kai. After this abdication they gave Kai some [[churnrock]] as a gift.

## Trivia
Separate from the universe of [[Perfect State]], [[Alcatraz Smedry]] mentions that a Hushlander would not recognize the difference between a Rounsfield tunic and a Larkian tunic, but then reveals that he made those items up.
