The **Elantrians** are a race of incredible grace and beauty that reside in the city of [[Elantris (city)|Elantris]] on the planet [[Sel]]. People of any age, who are of [[Aonic]] descent, and who reside in or near the border of [[Arelon]] or [[Teod]], are occasionally taken by the [[Shaod]], which changes them into Elantrians. This consists of physical and mental alterations, and gives the capacity to use [[AonDor]].

## Becoming an Elantrian
A person can become an Elantrian by being taken by the [[Shaod]]. The Shaod, or the Transformation, generally happens at night.{{wob ref|8409}} The people it chooses are seemingly random (though not completely){{wob ref|13071}} and may be from any age or class in society.{{book ref|Elantris|prologue}} The Shaod only takes people from [[Arelon]] or [[Teod]], and occasionally [[Duladel]].{{book ref|Elantris|19}} It is suspected that this is due to [[AonDor]]'s close [[Connection]] with Arelon and its geography in general.{{wob ref|411}} A person needs to have some genetic ties to Arelon and be in Arelon to be taken by the Shaod.{{wob ref|2173}} Immediate proximity to Elantris or Elantrians do not increase one's chances of being taken by the Shaod.{{book ref|Elantris|2}} A non-human could become an Elantrian.{{wob ref|11629}} There are ways to reverse the Shaod.{{wob ref|9891}}{{wob ref|15260}}

It is also possible to form this Connection by being invited into specific groups (presumably Elantrian groups, such as the [[Ire]]), though the exact mechanics of this are not yet known.{{book ref|tress|64}}

There is an upper limit to the number of Elantrians there can be at a given time.{{wob ref|8617}}

## Abilities and Appearance
{{image|Elantrians by ML Malandrino.jpg|side=right|width=250px|Elantrians}}

### Physical changes
Elantrians have "brilliant white" hair and "metallic silver" skin and appear to shine with an inner light.{{book ref|Elantris|prologue}} Elantrian bodies are fueled and constantly renewed by the [[Dor]],{{book ref|Elantris|19}} and if the Dor is blocked they quickly deteriorate. There are still some basic metabolic processes happening in the Elantrian body, but their bodies are mostly sustained by Investiture.{{wob ref|4321}} Elantrians heal quickly and can survive fatal injuries, such as decapitation.{{wob ref|13084}} They bleed silvery-white blood.{{book ref|Elantris|62}} They have superior strength and speed.{{book ref|Elantris|prologue}} Elantrians can still die of natural causes if left untreated.{{book ref|Elantris|25}} An Elantrian away from Sel would still be an Elantrian but many of the visible signs would fade away.{{wob ref|3407}} There are ways to restore this.{{book ref|mb7|54}} Their raw levels of Investiture are very high, even higher than a [[Returned]].{{book ref|Yumi|32}}

==== Reod Elantrians ====
During the Reod their silvery skin turned grey and became covered in dark splotches and their white hair falls out, along with other less readily apparent changes, such as their hearts ceasing to beat, breathing and eating were no longer needed, bleeding no longer happened, and their wounds refused to heal.{{wob ref|1942}} Some functions of the body like muscles and blood flow continue,{{wob ref|8079}} and sleep and brain functions continues to work very similarly to the way it did before.{{wob ref|2200}} Although unable to heal, a Reod Elantrian can only be killed by decapitation or burning, while hunger or most fatal wounds failed to kill them since the Dor kept their bodies functioning. Other attempts to kill them built up pain that never decreased, and could result in making them a [[Hoed]], leaving them filled with pain and unable to do anything other than moan. They burned much easier compared to a normal human.{{book ref|Elantris|18}} These effects were due to the transformation being incomplete, waiting to finish but unable to do so with an incomplete Aon.{{book ref|Elantris|49}} Reod Elantrian bodies act as if they are frozen at a certain point in time, which is why pain didn’t go away and things like hair did not grow.{{book ref|Elantris|25}}

Some potential advantages come from being a Reod Elantrian. Reod Elantrians do not need to breathe or eat.{{book ref|Elantris|13}} A [[Shardblade]] will register them as half alive, half dead. They would be a flicker and depending on when they are hit the Shardblade might cut a body part off or it might leave it dead.{{wob ref|1363}} A Reod Elantrian can still be detected by [[Heightening#Fourth Heightening|life sense]] but they would appear as a stutter, with a flashing sensation.{{wob ref|1362}}

### Mental changes
{{image|Hopeless of Elantris.png|side=right|width=300px|Elantrians suffering during the Reod}}
Elantrians have increased insight and mental abilities.{{book ref|Elantris|prologue}} They are also practically immortal, but being an Elantrian is emotionally and mentally exhausting and is one of the hardest forms of immortality that exist in the [[cosmere]].{{wob ref|5685}}

### Invested Art
Elantrians have access to [[AonDor]], a symbol-based magic that can be used in seemingly limitless ways.{{book ref|Elantris|4}} By drawing [[Aon|specific symbols]], usually in the air, Elantrians are capable of instantaneous travel, healing, turning garbage to food, creating light, creating fire, creating a telescope made of air, creating disguises, etc. With enough preparation, Elantrians can do things that are more impressive than [[Feruchemy]].{{wob ref|3603}}

An Elantrian's ability to use AonDor will diminish the further they travel from the city of Elantris.{{wob ref|8617}} It is possible to hack the magic system and get it to work on other planets.{{book ref|tress|64}}{{wob ref|5633}}{{book ref|mb7|54}}

==== Reod Elantrians ====
A Reod Elantrian has limited access to AonDor and cannot produce very powerful effects with it.{{book ref|Elantris|49}} There is a similar relationship between a Reod Elantrian and a dead [[spren]].{{wob ref|4392}}{{wob ref|5716}} A Reod Elantrian can be affected by emotional [[Allomancy]]{{wob ref|1364}} and it is easier to [[Iron|pull]] metal out of them compared to a normal human.{{wob ref|1365}} Reod Elantrians cannot be directly targeted by AonDor.{{book ref|Elantris|49}}

## Cultural Influences
### Arelon
The Elantrians have almost always ruled Arelon, with a short interruption during the [[Reod]].{{book ref|Elantris|7}} Before the Reod they were worshipped as deities by the people of Arelon. People from the furthest corners of Arelon would go to the Elantrians to receive blessings and be healed from disease.{{book ref|Elantris|60}} The Elantrians and their use of AonDor also served as the lynchpin of the Arelene economy for centuries. Their ability to cheaply create large amounts of food had supported massive urban populations.{{book ref|Elantris|5}} Fencing, specifically with a [[Syre]], never became popular in Arelon because the [[Elantrian]]s disapproved of anything resembling violence.{{book ref|Elantris|17}}

==== Elantris ====
The grand city of [[Elantris (city)|Elantris]] was constructed by Elantrians.{{wob ref|2230}} This city is accounted as one of the most beautiful cities on Sel. Elantris eventually became the capital of Arelon, and with that it became seat of the Elantrians. During the [[Reod]] Elantris was abandoned due to its association with the Elantrians. During this time it served as a place for the exiled Elantrians.

==== The Aonic script ====
As Elantrians are the only ones who can draw Aons, someone taken by the Shaod must have developed the Aonic writing system. Due to the association with the Elantrians the Aonic people view writing as a noble art.{{wob ref|2173}}
