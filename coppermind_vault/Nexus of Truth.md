The **Nexus of Truth** is a region on Roshar's [[subastral]] of the [[Cognitive Realm]].{{map ref|Shadesmar}} It is bordered by the [[Sea of Regret]], and leads to the [[Expanse of the Densities]].

In the [[Physical Realm]], the Nexus of Truth corresponds the oceans northwest of [[Iri]] and [[Shinovar]].{{map ref|Roshar}}
