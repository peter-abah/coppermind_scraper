The **Double Eye of the Almighty** (sometimes shortened to **Double Eye**) is a traditional [[Vorinism|Vorin]] symbol, about as old as Vorinism itself,{{book ref|sa2|6}} that represents the creation of life on [[Roshar]]{{book ref|sa1|part=ars}} and the [[Ten Essences]], and is also closely associated with [[Surgebinding]] and the [[Knights Radiant]].{{book ref|sa1|19}}

## Design
The core of the Double Eye of the Almighty is a set of ten circles (often thought of as "spheres"{{book ref|sa1|19}}) representing the [[Ten Essences]] along with lines connecting them. The Essences are ordered in a loop such that any two adjacent Essences correspond to orders of the Knights Radiant that share a [[Surge]]. Adjacent Essences in the sequence are connected by lines, as are opposite ones. There are also additional lines connecting Talus to Blood and Air to Lucentia.

The name "Double Eye" comes from the particular placement of the Essences: Pulp and Sinew are placed close to the center of the shape, evoking two pupils in an eye formed by the remaining Essences.{{book ref|sa2|6}}

In some depictions, smaller circles representing the ten [[Surge]]s are also included, placed in a circle interleaved with the Essences. Each Surge is connected to the two adjacent ones and to the Essences corresponding to the two Radiant orders that have access to that Surge. 

The complete Double Eye contains forty-seven paths connecting the Surges and Essences.{{file ref|TWoK_Front_Endsheet.jpg|*The Way of Kings* front endsheet}} The ten main nodes of the Double Eye are connected to one another by seventeen paths.{{file ref|TWoK_Front_Endsheet.jpg|*The Way of Kings* front endsheet}} Surges are connected to the main nodes, and to each other, via thirty minor paths, with each Surge having four minor paths radiating from itself.{{file ref|TWoK_Front_Endsheet.jpg|*The Way of Kings* front endsheet}}

## Significance
{{quote
|The preceding list is an imperfect gathering of traditional Vorin symbolism associated with the Ten Essences. Bound together, these form the Double Eye of the Almighty, an eye with two pupils representing the creation of plants and creatures. This is also the basis for the hourglass shape that was often associated with the Knights Radiant.
|Khriss's summary of the Ten Essences and the Double Eye{{book ref|sa1|part=ars}}}}

According to [[Khriss]], the Double Eye represents the creation of plants and animals on Roshar.{{book ref|sa1|part=ars}}. In modern Vorin belief, while the Double Eye doesn't seem to be actively used in worship, it is at least somewhat revered; the [[Devotary]] of Purity, for instance, considers images of the Double Eye to be suitable for [[Shallan]] to draw.{{book ref|sa1|33}} The Double Eye not only represents the creation of plants and animals, but more macrocosmically to the Essences and Surges which make up reality;{{book ref|sa2|6}}{{book ref|sa1|33}}{{book ref|sa3|96}}{{book ref|sa4|31}}{{book ref|sa3|part=ars}} microcosmically it also represents the makeup of the human body.{{book ref|sa1|7}} It also appears to be used directly as a symbol of the Almighty, with the Almighty being said to be the Double Eye itself.{{book ref|sa1|33}}

The ten main nodes in the Double Eye are primarily representative of the Ten Essences,{{wob ref|953}}{{book ref|sa3|96}} with some variants of the Eye being more complete, including the Ten Surges as well.{{book ref|sa3|96}} However, perhaps because of the association between the Essences and the ten orders of Knights Radiant, the Double Eye is also often used as an icon to represent the Knights Radiant.{{book ref|sa1|19}}{{book ref|sa2|55}} One prominent version of the Double Eye that is particularly closely associated with them depicts a pair of [[larkin]] in the background, suggesting a connection between larkin and the Knights.{{file ref|TWoK_Front_Endsheet.jpg|*The Way of Kings* front endsheet}}

Although the Double Eye does neatly describe the different orders and the Surges they can access, it is, to a large extent, a philosophical and cultural construct rather than an exact reflection of the laws of Surgebinding.{{wob ref|953}}{{wob ref|8476}}

Due to Rosharan belief in the Double Eye and its association with the Almighty, if [[Tanavast]] were to manifest on Roshar, he would appear with double pupils, even though he did not have them previously.{{wob ref|13420}}

## Trivia
* The Double Eye was inspired by the [[wikipedia:Tree of Life (Kabbalah)|Kabbalistic tree of life]].{{wob ref|7111}}
* The Double Eye was one of the first things [[Brandon]] designed for the art of *[[The Way of Kings]]*.{{wob ref|7111}}
* The chapter headings in books of [[The Stormlight Archive]] show an eye with two pupils.
* The Original Double Eye on The Way of Kings Prime is composed of 16 polygons and vaguely resembles a shattered pupil. The modern Double Eye, if counted without the Surges, creates 16 polygons out of their intersections.
