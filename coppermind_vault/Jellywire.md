**Jellywire** is a vine on [[Patji (island)|Patji]] in the [[Pantheon]] of [[First of the Sun]].

The vines deliver an effective paralytic on contact, rendering a person unable to move. The vines do not appear to cause pain, but the numbness takes some fifteen minutes to fade away. Experienced [[trapper]]s are able to weave the vines into nets to create traps. [[Vathi]] gets trapped in a jellywire net that [[Sixth of the Dusk]] set up outside one of his [[safecamp]]s.
