**Gift** is an [[Unkalaki]] on [[Roshar]]. He is [[Lunamor]] and [[Tuaka]]'s eldest son, and a twin brother to [[Cord]]. The young boy is very tall and pale-skinned. He is [[Vorin]], and wears a [[glyphward]] tied to his arm.{{book ref|sa3|37}}{{book ref|sa3|55}}. Per his culture's traditions, he has the honor of gathering food as his occupation.{{book ref|sa1|49}}{{book ref|sa3|37}}

Like his father and sister, Gift may be able to [[Sighted|see]] [[spren]].{{book ref|sa3.5|7}} Gift is the only one of his siblings who speaks enough [[Alethi]] to manage a passable greeting when he arrives on the [[Shattered Plains]].{{book ref|sa3|37}}

## History
Gift converted to [[Vorinism]], with his father's blessing, by [[Veden]] monks who came to the [[Horneater Peaks]] during the time when he was growing up there.{{book ref|sa3|55}} His father, who had left the Peaks with [[Kef'ha]] almost a year before, sent his family a message summoning them as soon as he had saved up enough wages. Gift and his family traveled for months, arriving at the [[Shattered Plains]] in {{Rosharan date|1174}}. Their caravan was raided by [[Voidbringer]]s on the Plains, so he and his family barricaded themselves behind a pile of furniture. During the attack, a member of his family shot a Voidbringer with an [[Unkalaki]] arrow. They were found shortly after this by [[Bridge Four]] and taken to [[Urithiru]]. Lunamor was surprised by how much Gift had grown during the time he was away.{{book ref|sa3|37}} After settling in in Urithiru, Gift helped his father serve food and drink to [[Bridge Four]].{{book ref|sa3|37}}{{book ref|sa3|55}} He liked [[Lift]] and often left food out for her to "steal".{{book ref|sa4|i|5}}

Along with his sister [[Cord]], Gift chose to stay at Urithiru when Lunamor returned to the Peaks with the rest of his family.{{book ref|sa4|12}}
