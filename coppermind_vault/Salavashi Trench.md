**Salavashi Trench** is a body of water in the [[Sea of Lost Lights]] and the [[Sea of Oracles]].

The trench is the reflection of the [[Sunmaker Mountains]] in the [[Physical Realm]], hence, no rivers in the Sea of Lost Lights move past the Trench. It appears to be named after the town [[Savalashi]] just south of the Sunmaker Mountains.
