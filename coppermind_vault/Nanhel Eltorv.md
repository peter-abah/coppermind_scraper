**Nanhel Eltorv** is a sailor on [[Roshar]] and a member of Captain [[Tozbek]]'s crew on the *[[Wind's Pleasure]]*. 

==History==
When the crew spotted a [[santhid]] during [[Jasnah Kholin]] and [[Shallan Davar]]'s trip, Nanhel thought that it was an omen of good luck.

Following the sinking of the *Wind's Pleasure*, Nanhel's fate is unknown, but he likely died.
