**Koolf** is a member of [[Bridge Four]] on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa1|21}}

During the [[War of Reckoning]], he became a bridgeman for [[Torol Sadeas]]'s army on the [[Shattered Plains]] under unknown circumstances, and he was assigned to [[Bridge Four]]. Once [[Kaladin]] made the decision to save Bridge Four, he drastically reduced the casualties that they suffered on bridge runs, but they still lost Koolf and [[Amark]].{{book ref|sa1|30}}
