**Paul Crew** was a [[Rithmatics|Rithmatist]] who was one of the final two participants in the [[Melee#1888 Crew-Choi duel|1888 Melee]].  He was favored to win as his team had been able to get him to the end with an almost perfect defense.  He lost to [[Adelle Choi]] when she bounced a Line of Vigor off of several lines left by previously eliminated Rithmatists.
