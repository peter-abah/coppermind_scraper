**Duluko** plants are a type of flora in the [[cosmere]]. They release sprigs that float through the air in a manner similar to dandelions, presumably as a method of spreading their seeds.{{book ref|yumi|24}}

## Trivia
*Duluko plants are mentioned as being "here" by [[Hoid]] as he recounts the story of [[Yumi]] and [[Nikaro]].{{book ref|yumi|1}} Although Hoid's audience is apparently [[Roshar]]an, Hoid's own location while telling the story is unclear. He is not on [[Scadrial]], [[Komashi]], or [[UTol]],{{book ref|yumi|24}}{{book ref|yumi|part=anotherepilogue}} and some other planets could be ruled out depending on the timeframe. Hoid travels extensively and he is known to utilize several methods of interplanetary communication, so the native habitat of duluko plants cannot be confirmed.
