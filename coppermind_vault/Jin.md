**Jin** is a member of the [[Ghostbloods]].

He is described as a golden-haired man with a bare chest beneath a flowing outer robe.

During one of [[Shallan]]'s interviews with the [[Ghostblood]]'s [[Mraize]] asks her to draw Jin after only seeing him for a short time. Jin also grumbles about the rain.
