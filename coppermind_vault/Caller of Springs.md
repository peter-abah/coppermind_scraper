The **Caller of Springs** is a *[[makay-im]]* [[Fused]] on [[Roshar]].

As a *[[makay-im]]*, or Deepest One, the Caller of Springs can use [[Voidlight]] to manipulate the [[Surge]] of [[Cohesion]] to meld with and move through stone.

Deepest Ones are commonly deployed as scouts and the Caller of Springs was tasked with searching for [[Navani Kholin]] during the invasion of [[Urithiru]]. They discovered Navani shortly after she and her scholars destroyed the first node protecting the [[Sibling]]. They summoned a security force to take her into custody and deliver her to [[Raboniel]].
