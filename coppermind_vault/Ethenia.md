**Ethenia** is an [[honorspren]] on [[Roshar]] who is bonded to [[Leyten]].

## Personality
According to Syl, Ethenia is known to have a mathematical mind, almost as much as [[Vienta]], and calls Ethenia a "bore". 

## Attributes and Abilities
As an [[Honorspren]], forming a [[Nahel bond]] with Ethenia made Leyten a [[Knight Radiant]] of the [[Order of Windrunners]]. Through their bond, Leyten is able to access the Surges of [[Gravitation]] and [[Adhesion]], and after Leyten reaches his Third Ideal, Ethenia is able to form a [[Shardblade]].

## History
===Second Battle of Narak ===
Leyten and Ethenia fought in the [[Second Battle of Narak]], working directly under [[Sigzil]]. When Leyten finds [[Moash]] and tries to fight him, Moash, who can see spren due to his Hemalurgic crystalline spikes, kills Ethenia with an anti-Stormlight knife, then stabs and kills Leyten with the same knife. Similar to how Leyten's death affects Sigzil, Ethenia's death affected Vienta.
