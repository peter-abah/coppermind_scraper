**Benru** is a town on [[Roshar]] that is on the western edge of the part of the border of [[Azir]] and [[Yulay]] that is not mountainous. It is west of [[Berqq]]. It is unclear if Benru is under Azir's or Yulay's jurisdiction.{{map ref|Eastern Makabak}}
