**Poloni** is a citizen of [[Diggen's Point]] on [[Lumar]]. He is a fishmonger who imports at least some of his goods from other islands in the [[Emerald Sea]].

[[Tress]] purchased a pound of salmon from Poloni which was imported from [[Erik Island]]. He mistakenly marked it down in price, thinking it was going bad, but in fact the fish in the next barrel were going bad.
