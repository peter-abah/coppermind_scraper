**Chaeyung** is a [[Torish]] woman who worked as an attendant to [[yoki-hijo]] [[Yumi]].{{book ref|yumi|2}}

==Appearance and Personality==
Chaeyung appears to be in her thirties and has a willowy figure with very short hair, which she wears in a bun.{{book ref|yumi|8}}{{book ref|yumi|18}}

==History==
Chaeyung is from Torio. She eventually became an attendant of Yumi, under [[Liyun]]. Chaeyung has a particular talent for combing the tangles out of Yumi's hair without hurting her.{{book ref|yumi|19}} Her duties included feeding, bathing, and dressing Yumi, as well as escorting her, fetching needs such as water, and performing various other acts of service. Following the activation of the [[father machine]], Chaeyung was turned into a [[nightmare]].{{book ref|yumi|34}}

Chaeyung continued to attend Yumi over the centuries that followed, at the direction of the machine, until the time of Yumi and [[Nikaro]]'s bond. When Nikaro noticed a man's [[flyer]] operating strangely and inquired as to the reason, Chaeyung quietly explained that the creations of the scholars from the [[Institute of Mechanical Solutions]] do not work as well as those created by a yoki-hijo.{{book ref|yumi|31}}
