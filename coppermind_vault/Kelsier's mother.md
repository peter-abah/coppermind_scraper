**Kelsier's mother** is a [[skaa]] on [[Scadrial]]. She is a resourceful mistress to a high-ranking [[noble]] lord.{{book ref|mb1|8}}

She deceived her lover about her skaa heritage, successfully hiding it for many years. Although her children [[Marsh]] and [[Kelsier]] were illegitimate, they grew up privileged until their father discovered the truth, at which point she was taken by [[obligator]]s{{book ref|mb1|8}}{{book ref|mb1|20}} and presumably executed. Marsh and Kelsier were very young at the time; the loss of their mother caused Marsh to [[Snapping|Snap]].{{book ref|mb1|20}} Their father did not report the mixed heritage of the boys to the [[Steel Ministry]], but they lived out their childhoods in constant fear of being discovered and killed.{{msh ref|2|2}}
{{quote
|You know how Kelsier feels about the nobility? Well, I feel the same way about the obligators. I'll do anything to hurt them. They took our mother&mdash;that’s when I [[Snapping|Snapped]], and that’s when I vowed to destroy them.
|Marsh{{book ref|mb1|20}}
}}
By the time Kelsier and Marsh reached adulthood, they had no living relatives except each other.{{book ref|mb2|7}} Kelsier's loathing of nobles was partly fueled by the treatment of his mother,{{book ref|mb2|33}} although he sometimes wondered what his life would have been like had he remained in noble society.{{msh ref|2|2}} Marsh focused his anger on the obligators{{book ref|mb1|20}} and dedicated his life to overthrowing the [[Final Empire]];{{book ref|mb1|7}} he did not agree with Kelsier's wanton violence against nobles.{{book ref|mb1|20}}
