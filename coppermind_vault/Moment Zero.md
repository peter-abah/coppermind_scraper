***Moment Zero*** is a non-[[cosmere]] novella by [[Brandon Sanderson]] released in December 2025 as a part of the *[[Tailored Realities]]* collection. It is an action sci-fi story set in the present day, featuring a pair of police detectives.

## Development
The novella was written in 2024 after the completion of *[[Wind and Truth]]*.{{bws ref|blogs/blog/stormlight-mini-giveaway-weekly-update|Stormlight Mini Giveaway + Weekly Update|date=2024-08-27}}{{wob ref|16781}} Early drafts included some social commentary about the state of policing in the United States, but this aspect was removed; beta readers felt that it was out of place and could not be adequately addressed in an action story.{{wob ref|16937}} Edits were completed in early 2025.{{wob ref|17014}}

## Release
The story was released for the 2025 [[Dragonsteel Entertainment|Dragonsteel Nexus]] convention as the "centerpiece" of *[[Tailored Realities]]*, a collection of non-cosmere short stories. Although Brandon classifies it as a novella, it could be considered a "short novel".{{wob ref|16781}} The draft version of the story was over 50,000 words.{{bws ref|blogs/blog/new-products-debuting-at-fanx-weekly-update|New Products Debuting at FanX! + Weekly Update|date=2024-09-24}}
