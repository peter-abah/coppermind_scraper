***Moment Zero*** is a non-[[cosmere]] novella by [[Brandon Sanderson]] released in December 2025 as a part of the *[[Tailored Realities]]* collection. It is an action sci-fi story set in the present day, featuring a pair of police detectives.

## Development
The novella was written in 2024 after the completion of *[[Wind and Truth]]*. Early drafts included some social commentary about the state of policing in the United States, but this aspect was removed; beta readers felt that it was out of place and could not be adequately addressed in an action story. Edits were completed in early 2025.

## Release
The story was released for the 2025 [[Dragonsteel Entertainment|Dragonsteel Nexus]] convention as the "centerpiece" of *[[Tailored Realities]]*, a collection of non-cosmere short stories. Although Brandon classifies it as a novella, it could be considered a "short novel". The draft version of the story was over 50,000 words.
