The **Windrunner monastery** is a [[Monastery of Truth]] dedicated to the [[Herald]] [[Jezrien|Yesoran]] and associated with the [[Order of Windrunners]]. It is located in the north of [[Shinovar]], just south of [[Ayabiza]].{{file ref|Map_of_Shin_Monasteries.jpg}}

## Location
The Windrunner Monastery is located in the north of Shinovar on the leeward slopes of the [[Misted Mountains]], south of Ayabiza.{{file ref|Map_of_Shin_Monasteries.jpg}}

## Structure
Like the other monasteries, the Windrunner monastery has a grand hall with a mural on its floor of its patron Herald Yesoran.{{book ref|sa5|83}}

## History
In ca. 1165, [[Szeth]] fought the monastery's [[Honorbearer]] [[Tuko-son-Tuko]] in order to become the Windrunner Honorbearer. After Tuko insisted on a fight to the death, Szeth slew him in a fight that lasted less than five minutes. After killing Tuko, Szeth felt sick, saying that he was used like an assassin in the night. [[Sivi-daughter-Sivi]], the [[Order of Willshapers|Willshaper]] Honorbearer, tells him that the other Honorbearers had Szeth kill Tuko because he had spoken of  rebellion. The Voice then told Szeth that the [[Ishar|Voice]] wished Szeth to fly to him in Ayabiza to receive answers.{{book ref|sa5|83}}
