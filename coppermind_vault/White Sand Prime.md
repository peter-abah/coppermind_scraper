***White Sand* Prime** is the first novel written by [[Brandon Sanderson]]. It is followed by *[[Lord Mastrell]]*. Brandon rewrote it from scratch, resulting in [[White Sand (prose)|*White Sand* (prose)]]. The graphic novel *[[White Sand]]* is the novel's final incarnation.

He considers it to be a Dune rip-off.
