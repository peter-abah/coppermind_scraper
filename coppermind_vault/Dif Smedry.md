**Dif Smedry** is [[Alcatraz Smedry]]'s cousin. He is extremely enthusiastic about everything, and seems to have a Talent that causes everyone it affects, himself included, to forget about random minor events in the past.

## Trivia
* Like most Smedrys, he is named after a famous prison. In this case, his name is a modified version of [[Wikipedia:Château d'If|Château d'If]], a now-defunct French prison notable for being a main setting in [[Wikipedia:The Count of Monte Cristo|The Count of Monte Cristo]].
