**Hughes Entrone** is a nobleman and member of [[House Entrone]] on [[Scadrial]]. He is a cousin and confidant to the incumbent Lord Entrone.

He was present at [[Winsting Innate's auction]], indicating that he was probably involved in illicit activities. He spoke to Winsting during the auction. He presumably died along with the rest of the attendees after a shootout was instigated by [[Bleeder]].
