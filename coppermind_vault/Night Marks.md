The **Night Marks** are a type of birthmark in the [[Earth (Frugal Wizard)|alternate medieval dimension]] that appear as three deep blue spots on a person's back. It is said that the bearers of the Night Marks have been chosen by [[Woden]], though some people also believe they are a curse. The Night Marks grant the bearer the ability to see the shadows of [[wight]]s at the corners of their vision. The bigger the shadow appears, the stronger the wight is.{{book ref|fwh|24}}

On their way to [[Maelport]], [[John West]] asked [[Sefawynn]] how she knew that there had been a wight following him. She explained that she had been born with the Night Marks, and so she could see the shadows of wights as she had been chosen by Woden. She also told him of how the ability scared her. She was worried that maybe one day she would turn too quickly and see a wight in full, which would kill her.{{book ref|fwh|24}}

[[Logna]] is also able to grant the ability to see the shadows of wights to people without the Night Marks.{{book ref|fwh|35}}
