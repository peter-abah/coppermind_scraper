**Northgrip** is a city in northeast [[Jah Keved]] on [[Roshar]]. It lies on a bay adjacent to the eastern [[Reshi Sea]], near the border with [[Herdaz]]. It is the largest city in northern Jah Keved.

==History==
[[Helaran Davar]] ordered his sister [[Shallan]] some expensive drawing supplies from a merchant in Northgrip that were later delivered to the Davar estate.

After arriving on the [[Shattered Plains]], Shallan attempted to send messages to her family, including a letter sent through Northgrip. Northgrip was far away from the battles in the [[Veden civil war]], and Shallan hoped that its remote location would improve the chances that a letter would get through the chaos to her family.
