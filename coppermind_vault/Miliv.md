**Miliv** was Citylord [[Wistiow]]'s head steward in [[Hearthstone]] on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa1|25}}

Miliv disliked [[Lirin]], the local surgeon from Hearthstone, for unknown reasons.{{book ref|sa1|25}} [[Lirin]]'s son, Kaladin, believed that many of the rumors about their family in Hearthstone were started by Miliv.

Miliv was married to a woman who worked as a nurse to Brightlady [[Laral Wistiow]].{{book ref|sa1|25}} After Laral's father died, the nurse didn't let [[Kaladin]] visit Laral because she believed that the boys from the town were inappropriate friends for Laral.

Sometime after Citylord [[Roshone]] arrived to replace Wistiow, Miliv died and was replaced as steward by [[Natir]].{{book ref|sa1|37}}
