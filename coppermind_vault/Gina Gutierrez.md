**Gina Gutierrez** is a detective in [[New Clipperton]]. Her [[dupe]] dropped a cup of coffee on seeing the [[Reality badge]]. Gina was leading the case looking for [[The Photographer]], however her and the team were struggling to find him as his methods of killing made it hard to pinpoint a time for [[Snapshot (event)|Snapshot]] detectives to be effective.
