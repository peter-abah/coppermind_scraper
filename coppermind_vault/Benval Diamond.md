The **Benval Diamond** is a precious gem once owned by [[Vstim]].

When [[Vstim]] visited [[Rysn]] at the [[Thaylen Gemstone Reserve]], he found the diamond by recognizing its reference number, "0013017-36", and considered it smaller than he expected.
