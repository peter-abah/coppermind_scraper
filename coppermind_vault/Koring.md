**Koring** is a city in [[Shinovar]] on [[Roshar]]. It is located in the south of Shinovar, near the [[Willshaper monastery]].

## Geography
Koring is located near the Willshaper monastery. It is near a lot of arable land and some hills, which are covered in thigh-high grass. At least one road leads to the city.

## Appearance and Layout
Koring is a small city, although it is considered midsize by Shin standards. It features wooden boardwalks. The buildings, of which there are about two hundred, are painted colorfully. Many permanent awnings are affixed. Metal is used for door hinges and window frames, notable because only warriors and shamans are allowed to touch metal.

When fortifications are built around it, they are ramshackle, but effective. They feature an elevated walkway behind [[Wikipedia: Palisade|palisades]], and have a gate.

## History
[[Szeth]] got to know Koring while training at the Willshaper monastery under [[Sivi-daughter-Sivi]]. He found their use of metal for building, even as limited as it was, to be blasphemous.

When [[Ishar|Ishu]] began corrupting the people of Shinovar through the Honorbearers in , Sivi resisted. The people in the surrounding region realized their difference from the corrupted people of the nearby regions and gathered together in Koring. Thousands of them gathered. Many of them were not ethnically Shin, though it is unclear for how long they had lived in Shinovar. They built wooden fortifications around the city. Occasionally, corrupted residents of nearby cities and towns (such as [[Mokdown]]) would attempt to sneak up on them during the night, to attack them. Those in Koring were successful in repelling the attacks. They were able to successfully farm during the day, despite being confined to the city at night, and produce more than enough food to sustain their population. Because of how difficult life in the fort was, the residents considered fleeing the country to the east, hoping they could escape Ishu's influence there.

When Szeth arrived on his mission to [[Cleansing of Shinovar|cleanse Shinovar]], he was surprised at the fortification, but glad to see the people had resisted corruption. He noticed how many of them had picked up weapons, becoming those who subtract, and how they continued to wear splashes in spite of this. He empathized with their difficult situation, dismissing his concerns about propriety. He declared to them that the [[Desolation]] had begun anew, and that Shinovar had failed its mandate to Truth. The people of the city acknowledged this with some shame, though they had already guessed it was the case.

After Szeth discovered Sivi's corpse and retrieved her [[Honorblade]], he returned to Koring to rally the people. He declared the Desolation, told them of his actions at the [[Stoneward monastery]] that freed the people of the Encilo region, and promised to free the rest of Shinovar. Those in Koring initially reacted with confusion, though they did begin to celebrate. [[Kaladin]] traded with the residents for food, and the two of them flew towards the [[Elsecaller monastery]].
