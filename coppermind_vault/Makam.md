**Makam** are a type of tree native to [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa1|30}}

Makam have a sap with a strong musky odor.{{book ref|sa1|30}} It is a dark brown hardwood, with grain that is barely visible. Makam is a strong and light wood. All mobile bridges are made of makam as it is light enough to be carried by the bridgemen, but strong enough to stand a heavy cavalry charge.
