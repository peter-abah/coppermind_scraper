**Marshal** is a member of the [[Fabian Augments]] cartel. He was with [[Ulric Stromfin]] and [[Byungho]] in [[Maelport]] when they came to the room of Ulric's base with the portal where [[Runian]] and [[Quinn]] were fighting. Initially seeming like he had subdued [[Ealstan]], Marshal was later killed by Ealstan when [[Logna]] disrupted the nanites in his body.
