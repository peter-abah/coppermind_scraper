**The Scribbler** is the name given to the person responsible for the disappearance of several [[Rithmatics|Rithmatic]] students from [[Armedius Academy]].

Their victims describe them as wearing a bowler hat and a cape and holding what appears to be a long cane. They attack with creatures that seem similar to wild [[chalkling]]s.
