**Ellar** is an [[Alethi]] sergeant on [[Roshar]]. He keeps his hair closely shaved, and people consider him to be belligerent, intolerant and self-important. Despite this, he wants to be a respected figure. Ellar generally views strangers with disdain, until they prove themselves useful or capable.

## History
Shortly before the [[Everstorm]]’s arrival, Ellar lead a large [[House Kholin|Kholin]] caravan consisting of ten wagons and a company of one hundred soldiers. This caravan also carried [[Bordin]], the madman [[Talenel]] and his [[Honorblade]]. Two days before they reached the [[Covenant Stone Crossroads]], the caravan was attacked by bandits. A few men were injured, but no one was killed. Shortly after arriving here, while preparing for a [[Highstorm]], Ellar noticed a [[Shin]] [[Taszo-son-Clutio|stranger]] standing next to the wagon carrying their prisoner, Taln. He ordered the man to move away. 

Shortly afterwards, the caravan was ambushed by bandits. The soldiers fought them off, but one person died, and, unbeknownst to the Alethi, the Honorblade was stolen. The rest of the journey was uneventful. After their arrival at the [[Shattered Plains]], Ellar ordered his men to bring the prisoner to the Highprince. Later, Ellar, having heard of a report regarding some potential enemy movement not far from the warcamps, ordered an expedition to find and destroy these [[listener]]s.
