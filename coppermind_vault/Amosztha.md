**Amosztha** is a wine made from grapes that originate in [[Shinovar]] on [[Roshar]]. It is burgundy in color and sweeter than traditional [[Alethi]] wines. [[Navani Kholin]] was fond of a particular vintage of amosztha, and she and [[Raboniel]] shared a bottle in [[Urithiru]].
