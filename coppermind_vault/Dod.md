**Dod** is a member of the [[Kholinar Palace]] Guard in [[Alethkar]] on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa3|83}}

During the [[Siege of Kholinar]], Dod was among the Alethi that were influenced by the [[Unmade]] in [[Kholinar]] at the time, and fought for the enemy side under Queen [[Aesudan]]. When [[Elhokar]]'s team mounted an assault on the palace, Dod went down with an injury and a surgeon began attending to him. Adolin passed by and recognized Dod, likely from Adolin's time living in the palace. Despite his injury, Dod only whispered to himself instead of crying or groaning.{{book ref|sa3|83}} The whispering could be because of the Unmade, or it could be that Dod was muttering a [[Death Rattle]].
