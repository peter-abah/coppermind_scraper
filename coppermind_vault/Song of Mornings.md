The **Song of Mornings** is a [[listener]] song. It is simple, and is meant to train young children for more complex rhythms and songs. [[Venli]] finds it satisfying, because since it is simple, you can add complexity to it, and sing the song's soul instead of just worrying about getting its lyrics and notes right. It is [[Jaxlim]]'s favorite song.

## History
After a heated discussion between [[Eshonai]] and Venli, they hummed to the [[Rhythm]] of Reconciliation, then to Longing, then to Joy. As Eshonai hummed, Venli sang the Song of Mornings, a reminder of a simpler time in their relationship. Although it was dusk, the song worked with the situation.

After encountering the listeners that had fled [[Narak]], Venli sang the Song of Mornings to encourage Jaxlim to remember who she was; Jaxlim had lost her mental faculties and volition is required to bond a [[Reacher]] and become a [[Knight Radiant]]. It worked, and Jaxlim recovered her memories.
