**Dlavil** is a member of the [[Ghostbloods]], working on [[Scadrial]] in the city of [[Elendel]]. He is from [[Silverlight]], but is of [[Southern Scadrian]] descent. He wears an intricate and fierce-looking Southern Scadrian mask, which he never takes off. It has grown in to the point where it's nearly a part of his skin. He is a brother to [[Iyatil]].{{book ref|mb7|epilogue|1}}

## History

Dlavil was stationed in [[Elendel]], and likely helped with the city's evacuation when it was threatened by [[Autonomy]]'s agents, per [[Kelsier]]'s orders.{{book ref|mb7|40}} Ten hours after the [[Set]]'s bomb detonated, Dlavil was inside the Ghostbloods' Elendel safehouse with Kelsier and several other Ghostbloods. When [[Marasi Colms]] refused Kelsier's offer to join their ranks, Dlavil advocated for silencing her. He argued that she knew too much to be left to her own devices, but [[TwinSoul]] and Kelsier disagreed. Kelsier ordered Dlavil to not act against Marasi or anyone else in Elendel without his order.{{book ref|mb7|epilogue|1}}
