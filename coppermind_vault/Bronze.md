**Bronze** is the internal mental pushing metal. An Allomancer burning bronze is able to detect the presence of Allomancy being used nearby, sounding like drum beats to the Seeker's ears. Feruchemists can use bronze to store wakefulness, and when used as a Hemalurgic spike, bronze steals Mental Allomantic powers.

Bronze is the alloy paired with [[copper]] in the Metallic Arts.

## Allomantic Use
A bronze [[Misting]] is known as a **Seeker**. When a Seeker burns bronze, they are able to detect nearby use of Allomancy or any other form of [[Investiture| Kinetic Investiture]].{{book ref|mb4|part=ars}}{{wob ref|6113}} The Seeker feels pulses, like beats of a drum, emanating from the person burning a metal. These Allomantic pulses are related to the [[Rhythm|tone]] of [[Preservation]],{{wob ref|14482}} and would seem familiar, though strange, to a [[singer]] burning bronze.{{wob ref|11606}}

With practice, a Seeker can distinguish metals from each other by pulse length (long pulses for internal metals, short for external metals), whether the beats seem to "push" or "pull" on the Seeker, and by a distinctive pattern to the pulses characteristic of their quadrant (Physical / Mental / Temporal / Enhancement).{{book ref|mb1|20}} This is how it is known that tin is a Pulling metal while pewter is Pushing.{{book ref|mb1|20}}

It is more difficult to distinguish pulse length differences (internal vs. external) than other aspects; Marsh says that it took him six months of practice gain the skill.{{book ref|mb1|20}} Additionally, determining where pulses originate from takes some amount of practice, though [[Vin]] was able to do so instinctively.{{book ref|mb1|20}}

A talented Seeker can glean additional information from minute changes in Allomantic pulses, including:
* Who a [[Soother]] or [[Rioter]] is targeting, and what emotions they intend to influence.{{book ref|mb2|20}}{{book ref|mb1|20}} 
* Whether an Allomancer is flaring their metals.{{book ref|mb1|20}}
* Whether an Allomancer is running low on metals (though this is highly difficult).{{book ref|mb1|20}}

Generally, the stronger and faster a metal is burned, the easier it is to detect. For example, pulses from flared metals are more noticeable, and a Seeker who could not ordinarily pierce [[Copper|copperclouds]] may be able to detect a [[Duralumin|duralumin]] or [[Nicrosil|nicrosil]]-enhanced burn through one.{{wob ref|11499}} 

### Savantism
If someone were able to burn the amount of bronze needed to become an Allomantic [[savant]] with the metal, their range would be expanded{{epigraph ref|mb3|16}} and they might be able to pierce copperclouds depending on their relative strength.{{wob ref|4303}} A savant would also be able to tell the difference between regular Allomancy and [[Compounding]].{{wob ref|2293}} Due to Seekers' tendency to constantly burn bronze, many have become savants unknowingly.{{epigraph ref|mb3|16}}

### Detecting Other Investiture
It is possible for a skilled enough Seeker to detect the use of [[Feruchemy]], though doing so is extremely difficult and requires a particular tweak.{{book ref|mb7|25}}{{wob ref|5495}}{{wob ref|6113}} This is because Feruchemy is far more internal than Allomancy, which draws upon the external power of [[Preservation]].{{wob ref|3417}} 

Indeed, it is theoretically possible for bronze Allomancy to detect any [[Investiture#Kinetic_Investiture|kinetic Investiture]],{{book ref|tsm|12}}{{wob ref|6649}}{{wob ref|6113}} though static investiture is difficult to detect.{{wob ref|11910}} Among other things, bronze can detect:

* The use of [[Surgebinding]], including being able differentiate between [[Surge|Surges]]{{wob ref|8851}}
* A [[Shardblade]] being summoned{{wob ref|7413}}
* [[Stormlight]] held by a surgebinder{{wob ref|12921}}
* The [[Rhythm]]s of [[Roshar]]{{wob ref|2031}}
* [[AonDor]]{{wob ref|3008}}
* [[Awakening]]{{wob ref|3008}}
* [[Shard]]s or [[perpendicularity|perpendicularities]]{{book ref|mb3|45}}{{book ref|mb2|57}}
* The [[Grand Knell]]{{book ref|iote|25}}

## Feruchemical Use
A bronze [[Ferring]] is known as a **Sentry**. Bronze is used to store wakefulness. It allows the Sentry to stay awake for extended periods of time, or to heighten awareness. A Sentry filling a bronzemind becomes drowsy, and may sleep in a coma-like trance.{{book ref|mb4|part=ars}}{{book ref|mb2|57}} It is the only type of metalmind a Feruchemist can store into while sleeping.{{book ref|mb2|50}} Caffeine can be used to generate extra wakefulness, but it would feel different upon being tapped.{{wob ref|14739}}

### Compounding
A bronze [[Compounding|Compounder]] would be able to use bronze to have a nearly infinite supply of a good night's sleep, and therefore would never need to sleep.{{wob ref|12772}}

## Hemalurgic Use
When used as a Hemalurgic spike, bronze steals Mental Allomantic powers ([[zinc]], [[brass]], [[copper]], and bronze).{{file ref| Hemalurgy table.jpg | Hemalurgy Table}}

### Bind Points
* Allomantic Zinc: Allomantic zinc can be stolen by placing a bronze spike between two ribs.{{book ref|mb3|5}}
* Allomantic Brass: Allomantic brass can be stolen by placing a bronze spike between two ribs.{{book ref|mb3|5}}
* Allomantic Copper: Allomantic copper can be stolen by placing a bronze spike between two ribs,{{book ref|mb3|5}} or through the heart.{{book ref|mb3|34}}
* Allomantic Bronze: Allomantic bronze can be stolen by placing a bronze spike between two ribs,{{book ref|mb3|5}} through the left earlobe,{{book ref|mb3|21}}{{epigraph ref|mb3|73}} or through the upper arm.{{book ref|mb3|58}}

## Fabrial Use
A [[fabrial]] with a bronze cage will create a warning fabrial.{{epigraph ref|sa4|8}}
