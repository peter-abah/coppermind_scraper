**Tarn**, referred to as "Old Tarn" by [[Jost]], is a farmer who lives in [[Hearthstone]] on [[Roshar]]. 

Circa , he discovered a patch of cultivated [[lavis]] polyps that were not growing correctly. He gave a group of young workers (including Jost, [[Jest]], [[Mord]], [[Tift]], [[Naget]], and [[Khav]]) the day off from [[diggerworm|worming]] the fields so that he and the other adults could decide whether or not to replant that area.
