The **Compass of Spirits** is an item on [[Scadrial]] around the year 348 [[Catacendre|PC]]. It allows for easy travel to (and perhaps from) the [[Cognitive Realm]].

## Appearence and Usage
The Compass of Spirits looks like a regular compass, although it has a keyhole. The key linked to the Compass is made of [[aluminum]]. The Compass also has a number of little rings. After the key is inserted into the keyhole and turned, these little rings will begin spinning. Then, they will stop spinning once they are full of ethereal light, likely some kind of [[Investiture]]. This light then inverts in on itself and pops. After this, the Compass and its holder are transported into another Realm. They leave a faint afterimage, which soon disappears like smoke from a burnt match.

## History
During the year 348 PC, [[Nicelle Sauvage]], an Northern Scadrian adventurer, managed to get her hands on the Compass. However, sometime after, an attempt was made to steal it by [[Vila Mecant]]. During the following confrontation, Nicelle managed to get the Compass back. She was tricked however, as the [[Nazrilof|Haunted Man]] pocketed the Compass and its key and traveled to Shadesmar. Nicelle decided to chase after him.
