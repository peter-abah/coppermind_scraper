**Melerand** is the name of a kingdom on [[Yolen]] in the [[cosmere]].{{wob ref|4623}}

## Trivia
*The name Melerand has not appeared in any published work, but it is used in *[[Dragonsteel Prime]]*.{{bws ref|dragonsteel-prime-chapter-25-bridge-four-1|*Dragonsteel Prime* Chapter 25: Bridge Four 1|date=c. 2000}}
*In *[[Alcatraz Versus the Evil Librarians (book)|Alcatraz Versus the Evil Librarians]]*, [[Nalhalla]] was originally named Melerand as an in-joke reference to the kingdom from *Dragonsteel Prime*. Brandon later decided that the two should not exist in the same continuum and it was renamed.{{wob ref|4623}}
