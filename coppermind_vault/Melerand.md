**Melerand** is the name of a kingdom on [[Yolen]] in the [[cosmere]].

## Trivia
*The name Melerand has not appeared in any published work, but it is used in *[[Dragonsteel Prime]]*.
*In *[[Alcatraz Versus the Evil Librarians (book)|Alcatraz Versus the Evil Librarians]]*, [[Nalhalla]] was originally named Melerand as an in-joke reference to the kingdom from *Dragonsteel Prime*. Brandon later decided that the two should not exist in the same continuum and it was renamed.
