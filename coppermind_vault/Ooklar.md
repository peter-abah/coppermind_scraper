**Ooklar** is a planet in the [[Superiority]]. When the allied forces of [[Detritus]], [[ReDawn (planet)|ReDawn]], and [[Evershore (planet)|Evershore]] cut off the Superiority's access to [[acclivity stone]], [[Ohz Burtim Winzik|Winzik]] withdraws security forces from Ooklar to join a fleet to crush them.
