The **Giants of Epelli** are mythological creatures within in the mythology of the [[Eelakin]] people on [[First of the Sun]]. [[Cakoban]] supposedly encountered them, and later escaped by sailing between their legs. He first sailed through the legs of one, and then the other, leading them to attack each other in their confusion. Cakoban then rode the waves of their falling clubs, and when their great bodies crashed to the ocean, both dead, he rode the grandest wave of all, which supposedly carried him for three days across the [[Cognitive Realm#Drominad subastral|endless sea]].
