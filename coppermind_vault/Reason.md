**Reason** is one of the sixteen [[Shard|Shards of Adonalsium]]. The original Vessel of the Shard is [[Euridrius]].{{book ref|sa5|115}}

They originally wanted only to hide from and survive the conflicts of other Shards;{{wob ref|2788}} this desire is only tangentially related to their Intent and comes from their knowledge of events in the cosmere.{{wob ref|326}} They managed to hide well enough that other Shards, like [[Honor]], were unable to find them.{{book ref|sa5|115}} However, they have realized over time that this desire may not be the most important thing.{{wob ref|12684}}{{wob ref|13231}}

## Trivia
* Reason's existence was first hinted at in 2011, when Brandon responded to the question "Give us the name of a Shard's Intent we have not seen before" with "there is one who just wants to hide and survive."{{wob ref|2788}} Their Intent was eventually revealed thirteen years later, in *[[Wind and Truth]]*, and was the last Shardic Intent to be revealed. Before their Intent was revealed, fans often referred to them as "the Survival Shard."
