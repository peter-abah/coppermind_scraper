**Reason** is one of the sixteen [[Shard|Shards of Adonalsium]]. The original Vessel of the Shard is [[Euridrius]].

They originally wanted only to hide from and survive the conflicts of other Shards; this desire is only tangentially related to their Intent and comes from their knowledge of events in the cosmere. They managed to hide well enough that other Shards, like [[Honor]], were unable to find them. However, they have realized over time that this desire may not be the most important thing.

## Trivia
* Reason's existence was first hinted at in 2011, when Brandon responded to the question "Give us the name of a Shard's Intent we have not seen before" with "there is one who just wants to hide and survive." Their Intent was eventually revealed thirteen years later, in *[[Wind and Truth]]*, and was the last Shardic Intent to be revealed. Before their Intent was revealed, fans often referred to them as "the Survival Shard."
