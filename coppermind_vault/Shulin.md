**Shulin** is a city in the [[Kholin princedom]] of [[Alethkar]] on [[Roshar]].{{map ref|Alethkar}} It is near the border to the [[Unclaimed Hills]].

It is possible that the royal cartographer, [[Isasik Shulin]], is from here.
