**Drawers** is a felon apprehended in [[Elendel]] as a part of [[Migs]]' gang.

[[Wax]] and [[Wayne]] apprehend Migs' gang of eight the morning of Wax's wedding. Wax kicks Drawers in the chin during the fight. Drawers gets up, gun in hand, but Migs orders him to stand down.
