Admiral **Jonnes** is a [[Malwish]] ambassador to [[Elendel]] on [[Scadrial]] who is sent to Elendel sometime between {{date|342|PC}} and {{date|346|PC}}.{{book ref|mb7|2}}

She had a special place on the Elendel Senate, seated on the Senate floor with the visiting mayors of the [[Elendel Basin#Outer Cities|Outer Cities]].{{book ref|mb7|2}} Jonnes feared a possible war with the Basin. She was once seen visibly shaken after a speech made by Vice Governor [[Adawathwyn]] about war with the Malwish, and she did not return to attend the rest of the session after its recess.{{file ref|The_Two_Seasons_broadsheet.jpg|*The Two Seasons* broadsheet}} During {{date|348|PC}}, the leaders of the [[Malwish Consortium]] decided to send Admiral [[Daal]] to Elendel to replace Jonnes as the Malwish ambassador. They believed that Jonnes had grown too familiar with the people of Elendel.{{book ref|mb7|9}}
