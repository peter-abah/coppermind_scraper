Admiral **Jonnes** is a [[Malwish]] ambassador to [[Elendel]] on [[Scadrial]] who is sent to Elendel sometime between  and .

She had a special place on the Elendel Senate, seated on the Senate floor with the visiting mayors of the [[Elendel Basin#Outer Cities|Outer Cities]]. Jonnes feared a possible war with the Basin. She was once seen visibly shaken after a speech made by Vice Governor [[Adawathwyn]] about war with the Malwish, and she did not return to attend the rest of the session after its recess. During , the leaders of the [[Malwish Consortium]] decided to send Admiral [[Daal]] to Elendel to replace Jonnes as the Malwish ambassador. They believed that Jonnes had grown too familiar with the people of Elendel.
