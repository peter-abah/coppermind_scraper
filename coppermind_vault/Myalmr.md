**Myalmr** is a natural scientist and the author and illustrator of *Western Voyages* on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa1|33}}

When [[Shallan]] first became [[Jasnah]]'s ward and studied under her in [[Kharbranth]], she was allowed one hour per day to study any topic of her choosing. She decided to study books of drawings by natural scientists, and mentioned *Western Voyages* as the latest book that she had been reading.{{book ref|sa1|33}}
