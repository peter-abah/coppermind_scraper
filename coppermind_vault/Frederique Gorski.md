Frederique Gorski is a member of [[ICON]] and a [[checkout]], and serves as the sniper in the fire team that kidnaps [[Holly_Winseed_(Provisional_Replica)|Holly’s provisional replica]]. She has grey hair, which she keeps keeps pulled back in a ponytail. She’s a former Olympic champion in the biathlon. She became disaffected and checked out when Poland gave up its sovereignty and joined the United World Government.
