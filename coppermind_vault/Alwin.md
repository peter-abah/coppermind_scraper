**Alwin** was the previous earl of [[Maelport]].{{book ref|frugal|7}} In the past, he would sometimes call upon [[Ealstan]] to fight for him.{{book ref|fwh|12}} He also used to send ships to patrol the shores near Maelport, and fight back the [[Hordamen]].{{book ref|fwh|13}} However, Alwin had aspirations of becoming [[bretwalda]], and as time went on, he cared less and less about the outer villages in his lands. He squabbled with the other earls, and Ealstan believed that Alwin's attention would be better served elsewhere.{{book ref|fwh|12}}

[[Ulric Stromfin]] and his right-hand man [[Quinn Jericho]], pretended to be trusted by Alwin and have his authority to force Ealstan to obey them while they searched for [[Ryan Chu]].{{book ref|frugal|7}}{{book ref|frugal|18}} After setting up Maelport as a base of operations, Ulric kept Alwin as prisoner in a pit.{{book ref|frugal|29}} Ulric killed Alwin before Ealstan, [[Runian]] and [[Sefawynn]] could rescue him.{{book ref|frugal|33}}{{book ref|fwh|37}}
