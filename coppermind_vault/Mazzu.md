**Mazzu** is a town in northwestern [[Azir]] on [[Roshar]], south of the mountains that separate Azir and [[Yulay]], and south of the mountain settlement [[Hazzel]].{{map ref|Eastern Makabak}} Mazzu appears to be near Azir's border with [[Desh]].{{map ref|Roshar}}
