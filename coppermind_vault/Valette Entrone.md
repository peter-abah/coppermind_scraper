**Lady Valette Entrone** is a noble in [[Elendel]].

She was courted by [[Waxillium Ladrian]] at some point in the past. After seeing Waxillium, Valette was courted by another man, who [[Gave Entrone]] described as dreadful. However, she eventually married, and the Entrones were all pleased with her union.
