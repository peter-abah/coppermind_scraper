**Trebor** is a security guard stationed on the ground floor of the building which [[Mitchell and Young]] resides within. [[Lin Yang-Tanasin]] knows Trebor by name and greets him on entrance to the building. Trebor is armed with a baton, and a pair of handcuffs.
