**Glasswater Deep** is a location in the [[Sea of Lost Lights]]. It is adjacent to [[Ravizadth]], [[Emberdark (settlement)|Emberdark]] and hits the [[Emberdark Channel]] and [[South Hallen Channel]]. It is near the [[Luminous Shallows]].

In the [[Physical Realm]], it is the northern part of the [[Frostlands]], lying on the same peninsula that [[Kharbranth]] is on to its west.
