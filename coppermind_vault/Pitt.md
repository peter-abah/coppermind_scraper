**Pitt** is a [[bridgeman]] on [[Roshar]] during the [[War of Reckoning]]. He later serves under [[Kaladin]] as the sergeant of Bridge Seventeen.

==Appearance and Personality==
He has strong features and short dark hair. Life as a bridgeman broke his spirit, and he recalls hoping that [[Bridge Four]] would fail when [[Kaladin]] instilled some pride into its members. After his [[bridge crew]] is liberated from [[Torol Sadeas|Sadeas]]'s control, he becomes earnest and hopeful.

==History==
Pitt became a bridgeman under unknown circumstances and was assigned to Bridge Seventeen, a notoriously motley crew. Like most bridgemen, he only cared about keeping his head down and surviving, and was not pleased when [[Kaladin]] began organizing and training [[Bridge Four]]. After Sadeas's bridge crews were freed, Pitt was among the forty men chosen to train with [[Teft]] to potentially become sergeants in Kaladin's new chain of command. During Pitt's training, he apologized to Kaladin for his previous attitude as a bridgeman, and Kaladin forgave him. Pitt was eager to take a leadership role in rehabilitating Bridge Seventeen.

Bridge Seventeen, like other bridge crews, was slow to adapt to its new status as a platoon of legitimate soldiers. Kaladin gave a speech to Pitt and his men in the chasms before a training session with Teft, which seemed to inspire them. They continued to make progress, and their members became noticeably more earnest. Pitt and the squad were complimented by Kaladin during one of his inspections, even though they still had a long way to go. Bridge Seventeen was among the first of the bridge crews to be ready for patrol duty, and some of the members were assigned to guard the leaders of [[House Kholin]] at the dueling arena.
