**KeSun** is a [[kandra]] from Scadrial and an ally of [[Nicelle Sauvage]]. She is implied to be close to another Kandra, [[Tabaar]], and often talk to him with compassion she reserves only for him.{{file ref|The Two Seasons broadsheet.jpg|*The Two Seasons* Broadsheet}}

## Attributes and Abilities
As a Kandra, KeSun is able to shape their flesh at will, creating different bodies. She also seem to have experience with [[True Body|True Bodies]]. Additionally, She is said to be able to ‘merge’ with other Kandra, such as Tabaar. This process takes about ten minutes, and it’s said to be somewhat nasty, with both KeSun and Tabaar preferring to do it unseen. She has some experience impersonating birds, and can even change her form to become a great [[Ornisaur]], although this requires them to merge.{{file ref|The Two Seasons broadsheet.jpg|*The Two Seasons* Broadsheet}}

## History
At some point during the third century PC, KeSun and Tabaar encountered a woman named [[Nicelle Sauvage]]. They took to travelling together, and, by 348 PC, they were longtime companions. They visited the [[Ornisaur Quarry]] together, where the Kandra found an aluminum skeleton of an [[Ornisaur]]. Afterwards, Nicki took them to find the [[Compass of Spirits]].{{file ref|The Two Seasons broadsheet.jpg|*The Two Seasons* Broadsheet}}

After finding and retrieving it, one of Nicelle's rivals, [[Vila Mecant]], stole the compass. She didn’t manage to take the key with her however, and Nicelle moved to hand the key to the device to Nazh so that he could keep it away from Vila while Nicelle persuaded her two companions to merge together into an Ornisaur, using the bones that they had found. With this done, they gave chase to Vila and Nicelle fought her off, managing to retrieve the compass in the process. After Nicelle fell off the great bird, KeSun and Tabaar managed to save her, and put her down on the ground safely.{{file ref|The Two Seasons broadsheet.jpg|*The Two Seasons* Broadsheet}}

Afterwards, she spoke to Nazh who held her and then moved in for a kiss before stealing the compass from Nicelle. He apologized before disappearing to the ghostly dimension. Realizing the betrayal, Nicelle was hurt and panicked, realizing that there was no way for them to locate Nazh, concluding their quest was lost. KeSun and Tabaar, however, stopped Nicelle and told her that there was another way to enter the ghostly dimension that they had never told her about.{{file ref|The Two Seasons broadsheet.jpg|*The Two Seasons* Broadsheet}}
