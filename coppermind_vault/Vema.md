**Vema** is a person living in [[Bilming]] on [[Scadrial]]. In the year {{date|348|PC}}, Vema was working at a company called [[Steel Kitchen]]. Their job in this company was to take orders for food delivery, which would then be given to the [[Feruchemy#Feruchemical Properties of Metals|Steelrunners]] to deliver.{{file ref|The Two Seasons broadsheet.jpg|*The Two Seasons* broadsheet}}
