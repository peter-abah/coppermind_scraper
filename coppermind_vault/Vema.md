**Vema** is a person living in [[Bilming]] on [[Scadrial]]. In the year , Vema was working at a company called [[Steel Kitchen]]. Their job in this company was to take orders for food delivery, which would then be given to the [[Feruchemy#Feruchemical Properties of Metals|Steelrunners]] to deliver.
