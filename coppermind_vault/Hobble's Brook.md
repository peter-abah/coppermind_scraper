**Hobble's Brook** is a river near [[Hobbleken]] and [[Hearthstone]].{{book ref|sa3|5}} 

After the [[Battle of Narak]], [[Kaladin]] passes Hobble's Brook on his way to Hearthstone, and reckons Hearthstone is about two hours away flying.{{book ref|sa3|5}} 

Hobble's Brook had trees sprouting along its bank, hungry for extra water, as the [[Weeping]] ended early.{{book ref|sa3|5}}
