**Nilto** is a man in [[Kezare]] also known as the "Lord Beggar". 

His face appears disfigured and is covered in wrappings. He is an acquaintance of the [[Darksider]] [[Loaten]]. [[Ais]] believes that Nilto is the crime lord known as [[Sharezan]].
