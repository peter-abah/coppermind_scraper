**Gul'zah** is a [[Burl (species)|burl]] who takes the pilot's test under the auspices of the [[Superiority]] [[Department of Protective Services]]. He protests the use of live-fire in the test, and is released from the program. He is later involved in public demonstrations against the same, and is exiled to the [[nowhere]] by the Department of Protective Services.
