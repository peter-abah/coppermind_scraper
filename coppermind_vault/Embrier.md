**Embrier** is a member of the [[Set]] in [[Bilming]] on [[Scadrial]]. He is described by [[Wayne]] as being stouter than the other member of the Set next to him.

He was tricked by [[Wayne]] disguised as [[Franis]] and [[VenDell]] disguised as [[Granks]] into bringing out the Sequence at a Set hideout, where it was then stormed by Bilming city constables.
