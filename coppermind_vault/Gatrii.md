**Gatrii** is a character from [[Arelon]] on [[Sel]] in early drafts of *[[Elantris (book)|Elantris]]*. He does not appear in the published novel.

He was one of Prince [[Eton]]'s highest-ranking generals. He is burly and wears silver armor.
He participates in a trial of [[Raoden]] and is tasked with beheading him. However, Eton had instituted a custom wherein the executor closed his eyes while delivering the killing blow, with a miss indicating that the accused was innocent all along. Gatrii does not strike Raoden, and [[Hrathen]] believes that he missed intentionally.
