**Ven** is the chief surgeon in [[Amaram]]'s army on [[Roshar]].

==Appearance and Personality==
He is a tall man, with a long face and a bulbous nose. He wears a loose-fitting white robe.

==History==
During Amaram's battles, Ven had oversight of a pavilion where surgeries were performed. He also trained apprentice surgeons, many of whom were former soldiers that had been injured in battle.

While serving in Amaram's army, [[Kaladin]] bribed Ven to ensure that the men in his squad would be the first [[darkeyes]] to receive medical attention.
