**Soul's March** is an observance on [[Roshar]] during the [[Era of Solitude]].

[[Dalinar Kholin]] notes that the [[Parshendi]] death song reminds him of the "curses of spirits on Soul's March". This implies that it is a periodic holiday or event of some kind, but the specifics are not known.
