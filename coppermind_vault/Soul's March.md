**Soul's March** is an observance on [[Roshar]] during the [[Era of Solitude]].{{book ref|sa1|65}}

[[Dalinar Kholin]] notes that the [[Parshendi]] death song reminds him of the "curses of spirits on Soul's March".{{book ref|sa1|65}} This implies that it is a periodic holiday or event of some kind, but the specifics are not known.
