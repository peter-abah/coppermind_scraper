**Jormo-son-Falk** is a [[Shin]] youth on [[Roshar]]. He is a soldier-in-training who fights [[Szeth]] in training while at the [[Stoneward monastery]].{{book ref|sa5|57}}

## Appearance and Personality
Jormo is light-skinned and very large, with a solid build. He is brash and overconfident. He projects toughness, and does not like to be shown up by people smaller or younger than he is.{{book ref|sa5|57}}

## History
While Szeth was at the Stoneward monastery, sometime around {{Rosharan date|1152}}, they engaged in a sparring match. Jormo was too confident in his better reach and leverage from his significantly larger build, and was easily caught off-guard by Szeth's careful approach to fencing. Szeth first allowed Jormo's momentum to carry him into a fall. On the second approach, Szeth dodged and hit Jormo in his side and thigh, hard enough to make Jormo fall over again, dazed. Jormo then grabbed Szeth's legs and brought him down, slamming Szeth's head against the ground. Some bystanders pulled Jormo off Szeth. Sergeant [[Szrand]] bereated Jormo for letting anger get the better of him, implying that Jormo was becoming exactly what the shepherds thought they were. Jormo was disciplined for this infraction. He may have attempted to jump Szeth to bully him further sometime in the days following.{{book ref|sa5|57}}

When Szeth was selected as an acolyte of the [[Stone Shamanism|Shamans]] in {{Rosharan date|1157}}, he thinks about how Jormo must feel, though he doesn't actually see Jormo's reaction.{{book ref|sa5|68}}
