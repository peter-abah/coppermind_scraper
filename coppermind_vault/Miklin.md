**Miklin** is a constable in [[Elendel]] on [[Scadrial]].

==Appearance and Personality==
Miklin's hair stands on end like 'grass growing from a pot'. Like many of his colleagues, he is skeptical of [[Marasi]]'s methods and questions her motivations, likely due to bitterness regarding her association with [[Aradel]] and [[Waxillium]].

==History==
Miklin ran the records office at the Fourth Octant precinct. He was a close friend of Captain [[Reddi]]'s, a rival of [[Marasi]]'s, and was therefore somewhat obstructive of Marasi's investigation into the dam breaking near [[Corbeau]]. He was tasked with collecting info about the dam breaking despite his opinion that it was a waste of time. When Marasi came to collect the information, he complained about the assignment and argumentatively asked her why she didn't give up police work to become a prosecutor. Despite his attitude, the dossier that Miklin provided was very thorough and included a number of pieces of evidence that led Marasi and [[MeLaan]] to believe that the dam's saboteur, [[Johnst]], was actually [[Bleeder]].
