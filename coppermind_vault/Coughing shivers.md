The **coughing shivers** is a disease on [[Sel]]. After Prince [[Raoden]] became an [[Elantrian]], his father, King [[Iadon]], claimed that he had died from the coughing shivers.{{book ref|Elantris|2}}

The coughing shivers disfigures its victims.{{book ref|elantris|11}} The disease is unlikely to kill young and fit men, although it can happen.{{book ref|Elantris|2}}
