The **coughing shivers** is a disease on [[Sel]]. After Prince [[Raoden]] became an [[Elantrian]], his father, King [[Iadon]], claimed that he had died from the coughing shivers.

The coughing shivers disfigures its victims. The disease is unlikely to kill young and fit men, although it can happen.
