**Whiskers** is a hyperslug [[taynix]] whom [[Jorgen Weight]] finds in the caverns of [[Detritus]]. Whiskers is [[Corbin|Catnip]]’s personal taynix. 

During the battle for [[Evershore (planet)|Evershore]], Whiskers is in a fighter with Catnip when a [[Superiority]] [[cytonics|cytonic]] with mindblades begins chasing them. Jorgen sends a cytonic communication to Whiskers, prompting Whiskers to teleport Catnip and the ship closer to the city of [[Dreamspring]], and out of immediate danger.
