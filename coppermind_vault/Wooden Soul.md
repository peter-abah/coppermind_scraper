The **Wooden Soul** is an [[Epic]] from a remote Punjabi village.{{book ref|calamity|8}} She is a puppeteer or animator Epic. Her only known power is to give "life" to marionettes and enable their movement by her mind.{{book ref|calamity|9}}

The Wooden Soul died about ten years prior to [[Dean Knighthawk]] allying himself with the [[Reckoners]].{{book ref|calamity|8}} Knighthawk's control over his mannequin, Manny, was via a [[motivator]] using some of the Wooden Soul's DNA.{{book ref|calamity|9}} He was astonished that [[David Charleston]] recognized the source of the power and was able to name the Wooden Soul.{{book ref|calamity|8}}
