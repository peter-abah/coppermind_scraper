**Gumfrems** are domesticated animals on [[Roshar]]. They are described as pasture animals that are similar to [[chull]]s, although gumfrems are relatively uncommon. They are raised in [[Alethkar]] for their [[gemheart]]s, presumably heliodors, which are small but can be used to [[Soulcast]] meat. 

Gumfrem farms existed in the [[Akanny]] region of the [[Sadeas princedom]]. Enough gumfrems to clog multiple rooms were taken to [[Urithiru]].
