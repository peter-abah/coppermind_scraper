***Riftwake***, formerly referred to as ***Skyward Legacy***, is the sequel series to the *[[Skyward (series)|Skyward]]* books. *Riftwake* will be written primarily by [[Janci Patterson]], with [[Brandon]] involved in the editing and revision process. The series will be set shortly after *[[Defiant (book)|Defiant]]*, following mostly the same cast of characters, and is certain to feature [[taynix]].

The series is planned to consist of three books. The first book, *Blightfall*, is planned for release in September 2026. The working title of the second book is *Songrise*. As of December 2025, *Songrise* is undergoing revisions, with an expected release in summer 2027.

*Blightfall* is written from the perspectives of [[Sadie]] and [[Arturo]]. [[Skyward Flight]] will become a special forces unit. Skyward Flight’s first responsibility is to help the [[DDF]] Diplomatic Corps make contact with other humans in the old [[Superiority]] human preserves—and what they find there may pose a new threat to their alliances, their galactic reputation, and the future of humanity itself.
