***Riftwake***,{{wob ref|17015}} formerly referred to as ***Skyward Legacy***, is the sequel series to the *[[Skyward (series)|Skyward]]* books.{{wob ref|16430}} *Riftwake* will be written primarily by [[Janci Patterson]], with [[Brandon]] involved in the editing and revision process.{{wob ref|16430}}{{wob ref|17014}}{{wob ref|16632}} The series will be set shortly after *[[Defiant (book)|Defiant]]*, following mostly the same cast of characters, and is certain to feature [[taynix]].{{wob ref|16430}}{{wob ref|16632}}

The series is planned to consist of three books.{{wob ref|16074}} The first book, *Blightfall*, is planned for release in September 2026.{{wob ref|16788}}{{wob ref|17019}} The working title of the second book is *Songrise*.{{url ref|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=l1xj9BepfCI&t=82s|text=Starting Ghostbloods + Weekly Update|site=Brandon's YouTube|date=2025-01-14}} As of December 2025, *Songrise* is undergoing revisions, with an expected release in summer 2027.{{wob ref|17019}}

*Blightfall* is written from the perspectives of [[Sadie]] and [[Arturo]]. [[Skyward Flight]] will become a special forces unit. Skyward Flight’s first responsibility is to help the [[DDF]] Diplomatic Corps make contact with other humans in the old [[Superiority]] human preserves—and what they find there may pose a new threat to their alliances, their galactic reputation, and the future of humanity itself.{{wob ref|16430}}
