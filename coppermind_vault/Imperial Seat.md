The **Imperial Seat** is the capital city of the [[Rose Empire]] on [[Sel]].

The city rolls across a group of seven large hills. A major faction house tops the outer six hills, while the [[Rose Palace]] dominates the central hill. The [[Sogdian Forest]] is nearby the city.

The city has a thriving clandestine smuggling trade and underground. Arbiter [[Frava]] is aware of its existence and may have dealings with it.
