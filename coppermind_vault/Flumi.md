**Flumi** is a young [[Eelakin]] woman on [[First of the Sun]] working as an aide to President [[Vathi]]. She works in Vathi's office on [[Patji (island)|Patji]] responding to the service phone and received the call informing Vathi that [[Dusk]] had returned through the [[perpendicularity]].
