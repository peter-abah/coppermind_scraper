**Snuggles** is a [[Taynix#Hyperslugs|hyperslug]] found on [[Detritus]], and is [[Jorgen Weight]]'s personal [[taynix]], along with [[Boomslug]]. Snuggles and [[Boomslug]] are almost always together, and she tends to bring him with her whenever she hyperjumps. Though Jorgen is unable to hyperjump himself, he can give Snuggles [[cytonics|cytonic]] or verbal commands to hyperjump him.

## History
Snuggles was assigned to Jorgen by [[Freyja Marten]] shortly after the mission to [[Sunreach (station)|Sunreach]].. Snuggles accompanied Jorgen when he met with [[Alanik]] upon her second visit to [[Detritus]]. In that meeting, Snuggles hyperjumped to fetch [[Gill]], revealing their natures as a hyperdrives to Alanik. Snuggles assisted Jorgen on the mission to [[ReDawn (planet)|ReDawn]], in his efforts to save his parents from a [[Superiority]] trap, and his mission to [[Evershore (planet)|Evershore]].
