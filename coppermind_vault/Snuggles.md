**Snuggles** is a [[Taynix#Hyperslugs|hyperslug]] found on [[Detritus]], and is [[Jorgen Weight]]'s personal [[taynix]], along with [[Boomslug]].{{book ref|sf2|4}} Snuggles and [[Boomslug]] are almost always together, and she tends to bring him with her whenever she hyperjumps.{{book ref|sf3|11}} Though Jorgen is unable to hyperjump himself, he can give Snuggles [[cytonics|cytonic]] or verbal commands to hyperjump him.{{book ref|sf3|3}}

## History
Snuggles was assigned to Jorgen by [[Freyja Marten]] shortly after the mission to [[Sunreach (station)|Sunreach]].{{book ref|sf3|2}}. Snuggles accompanied Jorgen when he met with [[Alanik]] upon her second visit to [[Detritus]]. In that meeting, Snuggles hyperjumped to fetch [[Gill]], revealing their natures as a hyperdrives to Alanik.{{book ref|sf2|4}} Snuggles assisted Jorgen on the mission to [[ReDawn (planet)|ReDawn]]{{book ref|sf2|15}}, in his efforts to save his parents from a [[Superiority]] trap,{{book ref|sf2|23}} and his mission to [[Evershore (planet)|Evershore]].{{book ref|sf3|6}}
