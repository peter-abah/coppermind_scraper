**Idkwyl Elariel** is a member of [[House Elariel]] in the 4th century after the [[Catacendre]]. She is an up and coming fashion designer in [[Bilming]].{{file ref|The_Two_Seasons_broadsheet.jpg|*The Two Seasons* broadsheet}}

In 348 [[Catacendre|PC]] Idkwyl made multiple [[Terris]] inspired designs. These designs were sold in the flagship store located in [[Bilming]].{{file ref|The_Two_Seasons_broadsheet.jpg|*The Two Seasons* broadsheet}}

Her designs were advertised in the *[[The Two Seasons]]* broadsheet published on Marewill 19, {{date|348|PC}}.{{file ref|The_Two_Seasons_broadsheet.jpg|*The Two Seasons* broadsheet}}

## Trivia
Idkwyl's name seems to suggest that she has Terris heritage.
