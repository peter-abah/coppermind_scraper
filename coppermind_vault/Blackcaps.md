The **Blackcaps** are the personal guard of Highprince [[Aladar]] on [[Roshar]] during the [[Era of Solitude]].{{book ref|sa3|46}}

The Blackcaps wear a black uniform and helm when they are on duty.{{book ref|sa3|46}} They are an elite force, and while they allow darkeyes to join, they are still very selective with their membership.

[[Skar]] tried to join the Blackcaps when he came to the [[Shattered Plains]].{{book ref|sa3|46}} Due to Skar's stature, the Blackcaps turned him down, although they claimed they would reconsider if Skar managed to find himself some weapons and armor. Skar stole the required equipment from the Blackcap quartermaster, believing that they would see his theft as a sign of his enthusiasm and cleverness. However, the Blackcaps instead branded Skar a slave and he was later sold to Highprince [[Torol Sadeas]] and made a bridgeman. It is likely that the Blackcaps accompanied Highprince Aladar on [[Dalinar Kholin]]'s expedition to [[Narak|center of the Shattered Plains]] and participated in the [[Battle of Narak]].{{book ref|sa2|76}} After the start of the [[True Desolation]], the Blackcaps took turns with other groups of soldiers going on plateau runs to collect [[gemheart]]s from [[chasmfiend]]s.{{book ref|sa3|46}}
