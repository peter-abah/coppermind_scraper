**Zetah the Voiced** is a [[Roshar]]an scholar.

In , Zetah wrote an essay about the [[Alethi]] King [[Elhokar Kholin]] swearing fealty to [[Dalinar]] as "highking" or emperor before leaving to attempt to reclaim [[Kholinar]]. Shortly before the [[Battle of Thaylen Field]], as Dalinar attempted to solidify the [[coalition of monarchs]], Queen [[Fen Rnamdi]] of [[Thaylenah]] came into possession of the essay and revealed Zetah's claims to the assembled leaders, provoking shocked reactions from [[Yanagawn]] and [[Noura]].

Zetah's information about Elhokar and Dalinar was factually correct, although it was presented with limited context in a way that troubled the other leaders. The timing of the essay coming into Fen's hands (at the same time that Noura received previously undisclosed information about [[Dalinar's visions]]) was orchestrated by [[Taravangian]], who had been seeking secrets that would undermine Dalinar's leadership of the coalition against [[Odium]].
