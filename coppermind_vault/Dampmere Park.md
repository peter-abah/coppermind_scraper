**Dampmere Park** is a park near [[Ladrian mansion]] in [[Elendel]] on [[Scadrial]].

It is located in the 4th Octant of the city, along the 4th-5th Canal.

After [[Tillaume]] tried to kill [[Waxillium Ladrian]] and his companions for the [[Set]], they fled through the park before waving down a carriage.
