**Dampmere Park** is a park near [[Ladrian mansion]] in [[Elendel]] on [[Scadrial]].{{book ref|mb4|10}}

It is located in the 4th Octant of the city, along the 4th-5th Canal.{{map ref|Elendel}}

After [[Tillaume]] tried to kill [[Waxillium Ladrian]] and his companions for the [[Set]], they fled through the park before waving down a carriage.{{book ref|mb4|10}}
