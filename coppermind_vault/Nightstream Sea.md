The **Nightstream Sea** is a body of water in the northeast corner of the [[Roshar]]an continent.{{map ref|Alethkar}} It separates [[Alethkar]] and the [[Unclaimed Hills]] from a small island chain.{{map ref|Roshar}} [[Hoel Bay]] is an inlet of the Nightstream Sea.{{map ref|Alethkar}}
