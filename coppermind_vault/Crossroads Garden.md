**Crossroads Garden** is a public park in the city of [[T'Telir]] on [[Nalthis]].

[[Vivenna]] referred to a green space in T'Telir known as "crossroads square" that was presumably part of the garden. She noted that it had more plant life and visitors than the more reverent [[D'Denir garden]].
