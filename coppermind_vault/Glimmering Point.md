**Glimmering Point** is an area of [[Elendel]] on [[Scadrial]] that contains some of the city's many docks. It is likely located in the Third Octant, on the [[Irongate River]].

[[Wayne]] pretended that he wanted to stop at Glimmering Point while manipulating a boatman into giving him a free ride.
