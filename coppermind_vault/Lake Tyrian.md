**Lake Tyrian** is a large body of water that surrounds the [[Ashmount]] Tyrian. It serves as a natural edge to the [[Central Dominance]] and the [[Western Dominance|Western]] and [[Southern Dominance]]s.

After the [[Catacendre]], it was changed to the [[Tyrian Sea]], which is relatively the same location.
