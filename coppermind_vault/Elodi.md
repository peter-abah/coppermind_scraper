> — Elodi to Jezrien

**Elodi** is the king of a [[singer]] nation on [[Roshar]] before the [[Herald]]s became the Heralds, and was friends with [[Jezrien]]. He tried to resist [[Odium]]'s influence.

## Appearance and Personality
Elodi is a tall, old malen singer, with black and red carapace, turning white at the edges. He has a long wrinkled face, with gemstones in his beard. He has a broad smile.

Elodi is a peaceable, kind king, who listens to the [[Wind]], and tries to keep the fragile peace between humans and singers. He desires peace, and for the humans to leave his people alone.

## History
Elodi was king of a singer nation, and was around for when the first humans arrived from [[Ashyn]]. He was friends with [[Jezrien]] and [[Kalak]], for at least thirty years. Fifty years after the arrival, he meets with Jezrien to discuss the rising tensions between humans and singers, including the murder and theft of a singer, and the mass killing of thirty humans. He urges the humans to go east, or else the singers.

> Your people encroach upon the hunting grounds to the east. Some among my kind are starting to refer to humans as pests. Like a cremling worm in our grain. I have not... heard of such vitriol. Ever."
> — Elodi on the growing strife between humans and singers.

He realizes the [[Wind]]'s voice is fading, and is trying to actively resist Odium's influence, recognizing it as a dark god. He warns Jezrien of war between the two kinds, and tells him of Odium's growing power over the singers. Finally, he leaves him and the humans, saying he will go east.

## Trivia
* Although their names are similar, [[El]] and Elodi are not the same person.
