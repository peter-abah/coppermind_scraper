**Spring** is a [[peakspren]] working as a security guard in [[Xisisrefliel|Xisis]]'s palace in [[Silverlight]]. He is large and wears a custom-tailored guard uniform that makes him look like an overinflated bag of chips. Like his kin, he is made of stone with a smoldering magma core that shines through cracks in his skin.

Spring often uses Xisis's common name, Foil, so as to avoid using the religious name. He is loyal to the [[Dragon (cosmere)|dragon]] and willing to lie on his behalf. Spring also has some familiarity with dragon technology as he is able to recognize [[Illistandrista|Starling]]'s manacles and their capabilities. Like other peaksprean, he uses the word "he" as a gendered version of the word "it", particularly for emphasis.
