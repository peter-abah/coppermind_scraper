**Spray** is a mare in the [[House Kholin|Kholin]] warcamp on the [[Shattered Plains]] of [[Roshar]] during the [[War of Reckoning]].{{book ref|sa2|25}}

==Appearance and Personality==
She is short, with a shaggy mane and brown coat. She is very calm and primarily interested in eating grass and other plants.{{book ref|sa2|25}}

==History==
[[Kaladin]] chose her as his mount when he was learning to ride a horse. Spray was docile but somewhat stubborn, and she was unwilling to go faster despite Kaladin's urging. [[Adolin Kholin]] was riding his [[Ryshadium]] [[Sureblood]] nearby and lightly mocked Kaladin, remarking that Spray was fast in her youth, but was at least fifteen years old and was best suited to children.{{book ref|sa2|25}}

Kaladin decided that he needed to try to ride a faster horse, and dismounted Spray in favor of [[Dreamstorm]], despite [[Jenet]]'s warnings against doing so. He was bucked off of Dreamstorm, and he returned to Spray.{{book ref|sa2|25}}
