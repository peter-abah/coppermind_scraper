> She once considered these stories fancies, no more real than things like the gloomdancer or sea hags from Thaylen mythology.
> — [[Vstim]], on [[Jasnah]]'s research of folk tales around the [[Sleepless]]

The **gloomdancer** is a mythological creature in [[Thaylen]]ah on [[Roshar]]. Modern Thaylens and Rosharan scholars generally consider them to be fictitious, alongside [[sea hag]]s and [[Sleepless|living heaps of cremlings]]. 

During the [[True Desolation]], there were increased reports of unexplainable events, causing [[Jasnah Kholin]] to reconsider the authenticity of some legends and folk tales. [[Vstim]] mentioned Jasnah's thoughts on [[Aimia]]ns and the gloomdancer to [[Rysn]] during a discussion of the unusual events that were occurring on the *[[Wandersail (1174 ship)|Wandersail]]* during the [[expedition to Akinah]].
