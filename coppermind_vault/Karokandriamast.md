**Karokandriamast the Mighty** is a [[Dragon (cosmere)|Dragon]] from [[Yolen]]. She was a large and powerful dragon, which likely contributed to her receiving the title of 'the Mighty'. She died approximately three centuries before the discovery of the [[Patji's Eye|Drominad perpendicularity]].{{book ref|iote|51}}

Starling saw her body, appearing as a statue, in the [[ancestral ocean]]. Here, her body was the color of new steel, before it is polished, marked with the char of the coals that had given it birth.{{book ref|iote|51}}
