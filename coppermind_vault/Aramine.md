Professor **Aramine** is an instructor at [[Elendel University]] on [[Scadrial]].

Professor Aramine likely taught on the subject of legal justice or criminal behavioristics. Lady [[Marasi Colms]] was one of Aramine's students at the university, and often thought over quotes from Aramine and her other professors when she was nervous.
