**Zawfix** is a city in northeastern [[Azir]] on [[Roshar]]. It lies at the foot of a mountain range between Azir and [[Yulay]].

## History
It was said that when the [[Sunmaker]] invaded Azir in the seventh century in the [[Vorin]] calendar, he murdered ten percent of the population, and that [[highstorm]]s piled bones as high as buildings in Zawfix. [[Yanagawn]] reminded [[Dalinar Kholin]] of this when they spoke about the possibility of a [[coalition of monarchs|coalition]] in one of [[Dalinar's visions]].

## Culture
### Architecture
Zawfix has shanties built inside of old mines to protect them from highstorms. When [[Lift]] encounters shanties in [[Yeddaw]], she recalls seeing them in Zawfix.
