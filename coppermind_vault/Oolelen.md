**Oolelen** is an [[ardent]] who lived on [[Roshar]] at some point during the [[Era of Solitude]].

Oolelen was an artist of great renown, best known for his paintings. He painted a [[highstorm]] blowing in from the east on the ceiling of the great eastern hall of the royal palace in [[Kholinar]].
