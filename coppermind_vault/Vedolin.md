**Vedolin** is a former [[Alethi]] merchant on the [[Shattered Plains]] of [[Roshar]]. He used to trade textiles in the Plains’ Outer Market. After losing his money on risky ventures, Vedolin took to begging and thievery to earn [[sphere]]s for a ride back to [[Alethkar]]. After losing his money, he became wary, desperate and slippery, taking every chance to improve his station and opportunities. He is also said to be skittish and a fast talker. Vedolin is a bedraggled Alethi man with long wet hair. Among others, a woman named [[Liss]] took advantage of his situation, making him spy for her. Vedolin knew full well that he was being exploited, but he saw every dubious opportunity as a way out of his dire situation.{{rpg ref|slsw|48}}
