**Ravizadth** is a settlement in the [[Sea of Lost Lights]]. It is near the [[Burning Gardens]] and is adjacent to the [[Glasswater Deep]].

On [[:file: Sea of Lost Lights.jpg|the map of the Sea of Lost Lights]] [[Nazh]] steals, he writes that [[spren]] fishing is illegal there, but that the jail is nicer than most.

In the [[Physical Realm]], Ravizadth is in the [[Tarat Sea]] and is near [[Kharbranth]].
