**Svel** is a [[Southern Scadrian]] crewmember of *[[Brunstell]]* on [[Scadrial]].{{book ref|mb6|22}}

[[Allik]] and Svel were crewmates on *Brunstell*, and they were both involved when a mutual friend was once dangled overboard for messing up the cistern three-step. Svel was part of the crew of *Brunstell* when it crashed during a storm in {{date|342|PC}}. During Allik's subsequent rescue from the [[Set]], he saw Svel's mask on the wall of the prison and knew that he'd died in captivity.{{book ref|mb6|22}}
