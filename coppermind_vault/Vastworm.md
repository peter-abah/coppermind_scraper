> — [[Kauri]] to [[Kimmalyn]]

**Vastworms** are enormous creatures that live in space and feed off of pure energy, including [[cytonics|cytonic]] energy. They are drawn to places where there are a lot of [[taynix]], unless the minds of the taynix are well shielded. The [[Superiority]] generally expends large amounts of resources to exterminate infestations of them before their numbers expand too much, though there are several hundred around the Superiority base [[Evensong]]. Vastworms can be warded off by using an [[Starfighter#Inverted Magellan Pulse|Inverted Magellan Pulse]], which uses a frequency they don't like.

## Appearance and Biology
Vastworms resemble giant worms, and are covered cilia-like hairs and black and white spots. They are several kilometers long, and will devour entire ships. A vastworm's throat is about twelve meters in diameter, and the inside is covered in long green tendrils. The tendrils are capable of draining a [[starfighter]]'s shield with a single touch. The throat forms a long continuous tunnel throughout its body, and it's rear end has a large opening like its mouth.

They can move incredibly fast despite their size, and undulate as they move. They sometimes coil up as a defensive posture. When struck with destructor blasts, blue lights start glowing along their body.

## Culture
The [[kitsen]] have legends about the vastworms, which their samurai supposedly fought against in perilous battles. According to [[Kimmalyn]], the Saint slew one at some point, though the holy witness spelled it as "wurm".
