**Gavam** is a woman living in [[Urithiru]] on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa4|i|11}}

By the time of the [[True Desolation]], she is an old widow. She sometimes forgets to collect her rations, but [[Lift]] leaves food out for her.{{book ref|sa4|i|11}}
