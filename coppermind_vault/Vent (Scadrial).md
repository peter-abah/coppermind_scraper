**Vent** is a skaa who lived in the [[Final Empire]] on [[Scadrial]].{{book ref|mb1|5}}

Vent knew [[Kelsier]] and [[Dockson]].{{book ref|mb1|5}} He is an extremely nervous and jumpy man. When Kelsier met Vin, he was reminded of Vent, although Dockson insisted that she was not as bad. As Kelsier and Dockson spoke of Vent in the past tense, it seems likely that he was dead by the point they met Vin.
