[[Diem#Organization|Underfen]] **Doril** is a female [[Sand mastery|sand master]] on [[Taldain]]. Her rank indicates that she is weak in sand mastery, and she appears to have been a cook for the [[Diem]].

She was an older woman with gray hair, and she was seen wearing an apron over her robes. She approached [[Taishin#The Lord Mastrell|Lord Mastrell]] [[Kenton]] asking when the next shipment of [[zaidon]] would arrive.{{book ref|ws|13|review=yes}} She was one of several people who interrupted Kenton with relatively mundane questions, which led [[Khriss]] to suggest that he delegate some of his duties to [[Elorin]] and [[Dirin]].{{book ref|ws|13|review=yes}}
