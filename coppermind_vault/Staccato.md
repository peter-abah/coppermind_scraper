The **staccato** is a type of plant native to [[Roshar]]. They are known to live in the chasms of the [[Shattered Plains]].

Staccatos are likely part of the [[rockbud]] family. They are described as "polyplike", with retractable tendrils that emerge between [[highstorm]]s.

The source of their name is not known, but the meaning of the [[wikipedia:Staccato|word]] makes it possible that it refers to a sound made by the plants, perhaps when they snap shut in anticipation of a storm.
