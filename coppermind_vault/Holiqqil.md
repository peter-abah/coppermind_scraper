**Holiqqil** is a town in northern [[Emul]] on [[Roshar]], located slightly south of [[Azir]] and west of [[Urithiru]], and lies along a river that eventually flows into the river that forms part of Emul's border with Azir.

In mid-1175, the town was under [[coalition of monarchs|coalition]] control, rather than [[Voidbringer]] control.
