**Odium's empire** is an empire on [[Ashyn]], then known as Alaswha, formed when [[Odium]] gives his support to a city-state on the planet and encourages their hegemonic expansion around eight thousand years before the [[Night of Sorrows]]. It is eventually destroyed during the scouring of Ashyn. 

## History
Eight thousand years before the Night of Sorrows, the empire was a mere city-state upon the surface of the planet. At this point it was likely ruled by [[Zoral]], who would come to be known as the Voidbringer. However, the city would be contacted by Odium who appeared as a god to worship, and as a beneficiary to their expansion. To these humans, he introduced himself as Passion. 

As a way to support this empire, Odium granted the people the power of unbound [[Surgebinding|Surges]], which gave them a tremendous military advantage over other city-states. Through their military might, and the support of a Shard, the empire quickly expanded across the planet, conquering many city-states whilst doing so.

After conquering several city-states, they ceased expansion, and offered other city-states the chance to send emissaries to them for a conference. Several took up their offer, sending delegates as requested. The empire gave an ultimatum to the delegates, that they would allow any who wished to join the empire to do so, with the implication that those that rejected it would be potential rivals, and thus targets. In response to this, a group of individuals, including [[Makibak]] and [[Nale]], would leave the city.

Honor granted that group the power to resist Odium's empire, due to them possessing a shared enemy. They would come to be a noteworthy faction fighting against the empire in the centuries to follow.

The empire was ultimately destroyed during the scouring of Ashyn, around seven thousand one hundred years before the Night of Sorrows. Zoral would perish during this event, and Jezrien would come to replace him as king, and help lead humanity to [[Roshar]].
