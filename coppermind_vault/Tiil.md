**Tiil** is a young [[Arelene]] boy who became an [[Elantrian]] sometime after the [[Reod]]. When [[Raoden]] established [[New Elantris]], Tiil and the approximately fifty other children were placed under the care of [[Matisse]], who teaches them to draw [[Aon]]s. During this time, he and the other children live in a building known as the [[Roost]].

When [[Elantris (city)|Elantris]] is attacked by [[Fjordell]] soldiers, Matisse convinces him and [[Teor]] to help keep the younger children moving and quiet by claiming that, since her father is a soldier, she is as well, and they can be her "subcaptains" if they do what she tells them.
