**Tim Vashin** is an inventor or industrialist in [[Elendel]].

According to the curses of a newsman, Tim was somehow responsible for motorcars becoming affordable rather than a luxury reserved for the wealthy. This led to congestion in the city and reduced safety for horses and coachmen.
