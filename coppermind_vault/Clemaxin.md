**Clemaxin** is a person in the [[cosmere]] who is said to have discovered the [[Sleepless]]. As a result, the Sleepless are sometimes referred to as "Clemaxin's Hordes."{{book ref|iote|23}}

The Sleepless are sapient, effectively immortal beings that have traveled between worlds and encountered multiple cultures.{{wob ref|12222}}{{wob ref|14312}}{{wob ref|14397}} Therefore, whatever interaction Clemaxin had with the Sleepless was not a true "discovery". It would likely be more accurate to describe Clemaxin as the first recorded person to publicly document their existence. [[Chrysalis]] seems to have a bemused attitude about the name "Clemaxin's Hordes" being derived from their supposed discoverer.{{book ref|iote|46}}
