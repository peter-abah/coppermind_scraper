**Clemaxin** is a person in the [[cosmere]] who is said to have discovered the [[Sleepless]]. As a result, the Sleepless are sometimes referred to as "Clemaxin's Hordes."

The Sleepless are sapient, effectively immortal beings that have traveled between worlds and encountered multiple cultures. Therefore, whatever interaction Clemaxin had with the Sleepless was not a true "discovery". It would likely be more accurate to describe Clemaxin as the first recorded person to publicly document their existence. [[Chrysalis]] seems to have a bemused attitude about the name "Clemaxin's Hordes" being derived from their supposed discoverer.
