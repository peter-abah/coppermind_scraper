**Karanak** is a city in southern [[Alethkar]] in [[Bethab princedom]]. It sits near [[Mevan Bay]] and the delta where the [[Deathbend River]] meets the [[Tarat Sea]]. As a major port, Karanak is of great strategic importance.

In the year following the [[Battle of Thaylen Field]], [[Coalition of monarchs|coalition]] forces took control of southwestern Alethkar along the coast of the Tarat Sea. Karanak was under coalition control at the time of [[Taravangian]]'s betrayal and coalition forces in southern Alethkar successfully regrouped at the city with minimal casualties. Not long after, the Fused closed in on the city, laying siege to it. During the siege, the defenders often clashed with a violent singer who calls himself “Brightlord” [[Hathalash]].
