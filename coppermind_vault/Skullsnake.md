**Skullsnakes** are a species of animal that lives in the [[Cognitive Realm#Drominad subastral|Drominad subastral]].{{book ref|iote|31}} [[Navigator]]s can sense skullsnakes, although they are easy to miss, as a Navigator can only spot them when they are directly opposite them in a [[Current|wave]].{{book ref|iote|34}} 

## Appearance and Anatomy
As their name suggests, skullsnakes resemble large snakes, with long and thin bodies. They can grow to truly massive sizes, large enough to swallow a small canoe and its occupants whole.{{book ref|iote|24}} While most of a skullsnake's skin is leathery, it has bony osteoderms on its head and down its spine. These plates are strong enough to deflect bullets.{{book ref|iote|31}} Their mouths are shaped somewhat like a beak; they also don't have any eyes, nor do they seem to have good hearing.{{book ref|iote|24}} Instead, they have the ability to sense [[Investiture]], which they use to hunt their prey. When they get hurt, skullsnakes make a high-pitched cry with several overlapping sounds, as if there are multiple screaming snakes.{{book ref|iote|29}}

The animal glow with Investiture which they rely on to swim the unsea in the [[Cognitive Realm#Emberdark|emberdark]].{{book ref|iote|33}} It is speculated that it cam from consuming Invested prey from [[Patji's Eye]].{{book ref|iote|33}} They have no organ with visible abundance of Investiture and their meat loses its Investiture fairly quickly after death. Skullsnake meat is safe for human consumption and tastes like pork. Additionally, its blood is salty, requiring distillation to drink.{{book ref|iote|33}}

## Behavior
Skullsnakes roam the [[Cognitive Realm#Emberdark|unsea]], a part of the Emberdark, hunting shoals of [[glowing fish]].{{book ref|iote|34}} Due to their lack of eyes, and seemingly other senses, it is theorized that they hunt exclusively by searching Investiture,{{book ref|iote|24}} known by the [[Eelakin|locals]] as searching for minds. Skullsnakes also tend to go a lot closer to land then their prey, the glowing fish.{{book ref|iote|34}} 

Skullsnakes are also able to climb onto land, slithering across the ground towards their prey.{{book ref|iote|54}} It is currently unclear what the relationship between the skullsnakes and the [[Dakwara]] is, but it is known that the latter has an entourage of skullsnakes. It is also capable of commanding them to carry out certain actions.{{book ref|iote|60}}
