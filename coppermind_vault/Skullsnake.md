**Skullsnakes** are a species of animal that lives in the [[Cognitive Realm#Drominad subastral|Drominad subastral]]. [[Navigator]]s can sense skullsnakes, although they are easy to miss, as a Navigator can only spot them when they are directly opposite them in a [[Current|wave]]. 

## Appearance and Anatomy
As their name suggests, skullsnakes resemble large snakes, with long and thin bodies. They can grow to truly massive sizes, large enough to swallow a small canoe and its occupants whole. While most of a skullsnake's skin is leathery, it has bony osteoderms on its head and down its spine. These plates are strong enough to deflect bullets. Their mouths are shaped somewhat like a beak; they also don't have any eyes, nor do they seem to have good hearing. Instead, they have the ability to sense [[Investiture]], which they use to hunt their prey. When they get hurt, skullsnakes make a high-pitched cry with several overlapping sounds, as if there are multiple screaming snakes.

The animal glow with Investiture which they rely on to swim the unsea in the [[Cognitive Realm#Emberdark|emberdark]]. It is speculated that it cam from consuming Invested prey from [[Patji's Eye]]. They have no organ with visible abundance of Investiture and their meat loses its Investiture fairly quickly after death. Skullsnake meat is safe for human consumption and tastes like pork. Additionally, its blood is salty, requiring distillation to drink.

## Behavior
Skullsnakes roam the [[Cognitive Realm#Emberdark|unsea]], a part of the Emberdark, hunting shoals of [[glowing fish]]. Due to their lack of eyes, and seemingly other senses, it is theorized that they hunt exclusively by searching Investiture, known by the [[Eelakin|locals]] as searching for minds. Skullsnakes also tend to go a lot closer to land then their prey, the glowing fish. 

Skullsnakes are also able to climb onto land, slithering across the ground towards their prey. It is currently unclear what the relationship between the skullsnakes and the [[Dakwara]] is, but it is known that the latter has an entourage of skullsnakes. It is also capable of commanding them to carry out certain actions.
