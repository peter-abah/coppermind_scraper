**Austrex** is the capital city of the [[Southern Dominance]] of the [[Final Empire]] on [[Scadrial]].{{17s ref|topic|59143|10th Anniversary Final Empire Map|date=May 9, 2017}}

Austrex is near the northern edge of the Southern Dominance. It lies on the confluence of the [[River Channerel]] and a canal{{msh ref|4|2}} that flows from [[Tremredare]] and [[Fadrex City]] in the [[Western Dominance]], and [[Longsfollow]].{{17s ref|topic|59143|10th Anniversary Final Empire Map|date=May 9, 2017}} It is upriver from [[Lekal City]].{{17s ref|topic|59143|10th Anniversary Final Empire Map|date=May 9, 2017}}

==Trivia== 
Austrex was first mentioned in the [[Mistborn Adventure Game]]'s [[A House of Ashes]], which is uncanonical, but the city was later included on an updated map of the Final Empire.
