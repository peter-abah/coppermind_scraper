**Highprince Sadeas** is a prize-winning hog from the [[Herdazian]] village [[Hurziko]]. Highprince Sadeas loves melons and hates longroot.{{rpg ref|ss|9}} The hog resides in a fenced enclosure near the eastern wall of a farmstead close to the village.{{rpg ref|ss|10}} During the [[True Desolation]], the village’s overseer, a [[Fused]] named [[Tashgarn]], declared that he was going to slaughter Highprince Sadeas for his breakfast. In response, a small group of people tried to free the hog.{{rpg ref|ss|9}} It is unknown if they managed to free Highprince Sadeas.
