**Aunt Glorf** Is a resident of [[Diggen's Point]] on [[Lumar]]. She is [[Tress]]' aunt, and best known for her desire to fight for the best deals in the market, as she'd been afraid of looking silly, by being taken advantage of. Similar to [[Fort]] she would bargain fiercely for even the smallest of items.{{book ref|tress|40}}
