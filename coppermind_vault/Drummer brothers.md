The **Drummer brothers** live in a small port on the eastern edge of [[Roshar]] in an area that used to be part of the kingdom of [[Natanatan]]. After the [[Everstorm]]'s advent, they scavenged a tattered sail from the sea and [[Puuli]] helped them stretch it out on the rocks.
