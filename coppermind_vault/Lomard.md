> Shameful. How long has it been since Dalinar won a gemheart? The only time he can get one is when the king lets them hunt it without competition.
> — Lomard to [[Vartian]].

**Lomard** is a [[lighteyes]] in the [[Shattered Plains|Sadeas warcamp]] during the [[War of Reckoning]].

He spoke with [[Vartian]] about how [[Dalinar]] and his sons were not winning any [[gemheart]]s, purposefully saying it within earshot of [[Adolin Kholin]]. Adolin wished to challenge Lomard, but knew that the [[Alethi Codes of War]] forbade it since he was on duty.
