**Lomard** is a [[lighteyes]] in the [[Shattered Plains|Sadeas warcamp]] during the [[War of Reckoning]].{{book ref|sa1|12}}

He spoke with [[Vartian]] about how [[Dalinar]] and his sons were not winning any [[gemheart]]s, purposefully saying it within earshot of [[Adolin Kholin]]. Adolin wished to challenge Lomard, but knew that the [[Alethi Codes of War]] forbade it since he was on duty.{{book ref|sa1|12}}
