**Bethab princedom** is one of ten [[Alethi]] princedoms that form [[Alethkar]].{{map ref|Alethkar}} Its house colors are crimson on orange.{{rpg ref|slwg|35}}

## Geography
It is located in the southwest of Alethkar, and shares boarders with [[Jah Keved]] to the west, the [[Frostlands]] to the south, the [[Eastern Crownlands]] to the east, [[Sebarial princedom]] to the north-east, and [[Hatham princedom]] to the north. It is the sole princedom with a connection to the [[Southern Depths]], via [[Mevan Bay]].{{map ref|Alethkar}}

## History 
Bethab princedom was one of the ten princedoms reunited into a single country under [[Gavilar Kholin]].  It is unknown whether Highprince Bethab fought for Gavilar or against him during the war for unification.

[[Dalinar]]'s forces stop in Dumadari on their way to the [[Shattered Plains]] after the formation of the [[Vengeance Pact]] before sailing on to [[New Natanan]]. Dalinar used the voyage as a cover to travel to the [[Valley]] and seek the [[Nightwatcher]].{{book ref|sa3|105}} 

[[Jasnah Kholin]] was in residence in Dumadari for an unknown amount of time in {{Rosharan date|1173}}. [[Shallan Davar]] travelled to the city to meet with the [[Alethi]] princess, however, by the time she arrived Jasnah had moved on.{{book ref|sa1|5}}

## Politics
Brightlord [[Bethab]] is the current Highprince of this princedom. He has, on several occasions, when negotiating business contracts with the king, included language that would favor him for succession of the throne.{{book ref|sa1|15}} [[Mishinah Bethab]], the Highprince's wife, is also highly involved in Alethi politics and will represent his interests on occasion.{{book ref|sa1|28}}{{book ref|sa3|104}}

The Bethab army is made up predominately of mercenary companies.{{book ref|sa2|67}}

It is implied that Dumadari is not well protected from raids via Mevan Bay.{{book ref|sa3|107}}
