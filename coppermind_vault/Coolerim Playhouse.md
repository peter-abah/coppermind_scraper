**The Coolerim Playhouse** is a well-known theater in [[Elendel]] on [[Scadrial]].{{book ref|mb4|2}}

## History
The Coolerim was one of Lady [[Steris Harms]]' favorite places to see a play.{{book ref|mb4|2}}

The [[Vanishers]] raided the Coolerim during a performance, robbing its patrons and kidnapping some of them. Lady [[Armal]], one of Steris's cousins, was among those that were kidnapped.{{book ref|mb4|2}}
