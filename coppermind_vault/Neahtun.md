**Neahtun** is a settlement in [[Weswara]]. Around three months ago, it was raided by [[Hordaland|Hordamen]]. Despite Neahtun's strong [[runestone]], the Hordaman [[Skop|skalds]] negated its power, and the rest of the Hordamen burned the place to the ground.
