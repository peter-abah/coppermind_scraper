**Tone** is a [[lightspren]] on [[Roshar]].{{rpg ref|b9|35}} She has the ability to 'breathe in' [[Stormlight]] to fuel her innate [[Surgebinding]] powers. However, it is currently unclear what these powers do.{{rpg ref|b9|22}}

## Appearance and Personality
She appears in the [[Physical Realm]] as a little pulsing comet of golden-white light. Tone is attracted to people who seek freedom. That includes fighting unjust laws, seeking liberty, and freeing others from bondage. Like all other lightspren, Tone has a strong affinity for [[singer]]s. She refuses to bond any person who supports any type of slavery or who makes frequent use of [[fabrial]]s that utilize captured [[spren]]. While searching for a person to bond, Tone won't actively speak with humans, however, she may convey emotions through rhythms. Her personality is plucky and optimistic, but she is a fierce opponent of oppression.{{rpg ref|b9|35}}

## History
Before the [[Recreance]], she bonded a human [[Knights Radiant|Radiant]]. Millennia later, during the [[War of Reckoning]], she tentatively sought another person who could possibly become a [[Willshaper]]. She was with the research party that got attacked by a [[chasmfiend]] and fell into the chasms.{{rpg ref|b9|35}}
