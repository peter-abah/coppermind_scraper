**Dymab** is a malen *[[Makay-im]]* [[Fused]] on [[Roshar]]. Dymab has made a mission of eliminating the anti-Fused resistance in [[Rall Elorim]], where he is one of the three singer monarchs. He managed to drive them out of the [[Dirgehollow]], although they escaped his grasp and immediately went searching for a new safehouse.
