The **Covenant Stone Crossroads** is a crossroads in the [[Unclaimed Hills]] on [[Roshar]]. It is named for the **Covenant Stone**, a large cliff protecting the crossroads. The cliff wall stands five hundred feet high and twice as long. It provides a natural barrier against [[highstorm]]s, making it a popular waypoint for caravans. The cliﬀ wall, a well-known landmark where promises are made, is covered in burned and painted [[glyph]]s. Since the [[War of Reckoning]] started, a number of business deals, minor truces, and oaths of fealty were conducted at the crossroads. Shortly before the arrival of the [[Everstorm]], bandits attacked two caravans resting at the Covenant Stone.
