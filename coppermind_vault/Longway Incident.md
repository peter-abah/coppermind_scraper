The **Longway Incident** is a task accomplished by [[Stephen Leeds]] with the help of his [[aspects]].

It involved his aspects [[Ivan]], a mechanic, and [[Chin]], a computer expert, who enabled him to disassemble, fix, and reassemble a computer system in a short amount of time in order to stop an explosion.
