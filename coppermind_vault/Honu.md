**Honu** is a type of [[lavis]] grain liquor on [[Roshar]].

In late 1173, during the [[Weeping]] at the [[Shattered Plains]], [[Zahel]] drank from a bottle of hard honu while he talked to [[Kaladin]] about washing the sand in the lighteyed dueling grounds. 

The existence of "hard" honu implies that there may be several different types of honu with a variety of alcoholic strengths. Lavis grows throughout Roshar and can also be used to make beer.
