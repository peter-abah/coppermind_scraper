**Cled** is a [[skaa]] elder on [[Scadrial]].{{book ref|mb2|52}}

He is elder of a small village which [[Vin]] visits on her way back to the [[Siege of Luthadel]].{{book ref|mb2|52}} Vin asked for [[pewter]], and a skaa says Cled might have some goblets that would have some.
