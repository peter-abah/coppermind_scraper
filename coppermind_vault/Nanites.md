**Nanites** are minuscule machines that augment human bodies by providing different healthcare, structural reinforcement, and other functions.{{book ref|frugal|2}}{{book ref|frugal|4}}

## Types of Nanites

There are many types of Nanites, all used for differing purposes. 

### Plating
In-weaved structures of tiny robots underneath your skin. These as well as bone-strengthening chemicals greatly increase the force your body can handle.{{book ref|frugal|2}} A weakness to these is that they can't protect as well against shock because they just transfer it into your inner body making the easiest way to bypass these is just to continuously punch them in the vitals using strength augments

### Strength Augments
It is implied that most cops or augmented people have increased strength that is needed to break through plating.

### Stealth Augments 
There are many types of these. A few of them are skin altering, hearing and sight enhancement and voice changing.{{book ref|frugal|9}}

### Healing Nanites
These types of Nanites run throughout your blood and help you survive and fight off diseases very easily. They can heal you by turning themselves into calcium to repair bones. They can also be transferred to others to help them heal, though they won't let the donor's nanite levels drop below 5%. While regenerating from a level of 10%, nanite levels remain low for about forty-eight hours.{{book ref|frugal|22}} As they heal they can no longer be used.
