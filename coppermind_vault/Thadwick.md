**Thadwick** is a member of the [[DDF]]'s Engineering Corps on [[Platform Prime]]. When [[Rodge McCaffery|Rig]] becomes busy because of his work with the [[taynix]], Thadwick picks up his work on the gun platforms, trying to get the gun emplacements working. When [[Matthew Cobb|Cobb]] is preparing to talk with [[Cuna]] using a [[Taynix#Commslugs|hypercomm]], Thadwick asks to consult with Rig about the communicator.
