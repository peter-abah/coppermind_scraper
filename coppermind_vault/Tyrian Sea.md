The **Tyrian Sea** is a body of water north of the [[Elendel Basin]] on [[Scadrial]].

It is located in the Northern [[Roughs]], north of [[Covingtar]]. Its location is about where the [[Ashmount]] Tyrian stood during the [[Final Empire]] period of [[Scadrial]]. It is presumably the same body of water as [[Lake Tyrian]].
