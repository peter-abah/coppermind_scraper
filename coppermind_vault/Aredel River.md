The **Aredel River** flowed through [[Arelon]].{{map ref|Arelon}} It began at [[Lake Alonoe]],{{glossary ref|Elantris|A}} ran beneath [[Elantris (city)|Elantris]],{{book ref|Elantris|4}}{{book ref|Elantris|32}} and formed a bay near [[Kae]] when it joined with the [[Sea of Fjorden]].{{book ref|Elantris|2}} Occasionally it was diverted to rinse out the sewers of Kae, because their plumbing ceased to function after the [[Reod]]{{book ref|Elantris|32}}

The Aredel River appeared on [[Aon#Eno|Aon Eno]], the character for water.{{book ref|Elantris|49}}

[[Raoden]] and [[Karata]] accessed it via a well inside Elantris, and allowed the current to carry them out of the city.{{book ref|Elantris|13}}
