The **Aredel River** flowed through [[Arelon]]. It began at [[Lake Alonoe]], ran beneath [[Elantris (city)|Elantris]], and formed a bay near [[Kae]] when it joined with the [[Sea of Fjorden]]. Occasionally it was diverted to rinse out the sewers of Kae, because their plumbing ceased to function after the [[Reod]]

The Aredel River appeared on [[Aon#Eno|Aon Eno]], the character for water.

[[Raoden]] and [[Karata]] accessed it via a well inside Elantris, and allowed the current to carry them out of the city.
