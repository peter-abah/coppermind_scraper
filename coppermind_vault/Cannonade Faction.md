The **Cannonade Faction** is a group of pirates in the [[belt]] of the [[nowhere]]. Joining with the [[Superiority]] after the other pirate factions assaulted the [[acclivity stone]] mining base.

For a short period of time, [[Hesho|one of their members]] was the pirate champion.
