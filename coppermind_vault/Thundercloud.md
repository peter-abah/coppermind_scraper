**Thundercloud** is a [[chasmfiend]] on [[Roshar]], and the leader of the group of chasmfiends that allied with the [[listeners]] after the [[War of Reckoning]].

## Appearance and Personality

> He sent to her that he thought he finally understood. She didn’t want him to eat humans because they weren’t fatty enough to be truly delicious, and he should focus on better meals.
> — Thundercloud

Thundercloud is the largest and most dangerous of the chasmfiends who left the [[Shattered Plains]] with the listeners. Like all chasmfiends, his face is composed of thick plating that appears like “chunks of broken rock,” and he has dark eyes that peer through junctions in the plates. His mandibles are roughly the thickness of a singer’s arms, and he can use these smaller foreclaws to pick up and carry allies--including people--over obstacles.

Thundercloud is highly intelligent and is capable of communicating with other chasmfiends and listeners via projected images and impressions. [[Venli]] can feel rhythms like Curiosity pulsing from him, and Thundercloud is able to communicate complex ideas with her, such as the relative tastiness of different foods. Thundercloud is willing to coordinate with listeners and will listen to their commands. He shows confusion at the idea of not eating humans, but trusts Venli's judgment on the matter.

## History
### Post War of Reckoning

> Or he was the first who had decided not to fight any longer--and when the largest and most dangerous of them decided the little things on the Shattered Plains had defeated him, the others had followed
> — [[Venli]]

Thundercloud was the first chasmfiend to decide not to fight the humans and listeners. His choice inspired around fifty other chasmfiends to make peace and join the listeners, led by [[Thude]], during the aftermath of the War of Reckoning and following the arrival of the [[Everstorm]].

### Second Battle of Narak
Thundercloud is one of the five chasmfiends who insisted on accompanying Venli and her group of listeners through the chasms on their expedition to [[Narak]]. He and the other chasmfiends assisted the listeners in navigating the chasms, using their mandibles to lift singers over obstacles.

After their discovery of the [[Well of Control]] and subsequent truce with [[El]]'s army, Thundercloud and the other four chasmfiends participated in the listeners deception at the [[Second Battle of Narak]], launching a mock assault, alongside Venli’s forces, against the humans. At Venli’s urging, he agreed not to kill or devour most of the humans, despite his natural predatory instincts. Instead, he focused on destroying fortifications--stomping through rooftops and sweeping aside walls with his tail--until the humans retreated.

## Relationships
### Venli
Thundercloud shows direct responsiveness to Venli’s commands, trusting her judgment over his instincts regarding the consumption of humans. He carries her over obstacles in the chasms of the Shattered Plains, and allows her to ride him into battle during the Second Battle of Narak.
