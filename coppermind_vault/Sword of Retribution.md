The **sword of retribution** is a mythological sword on [[Roshar]]. This weapon is said to have been wielded by the [[herald]] Nale. Due to its association with the Heralds and [[Vorinism]] the sword is often painted or carved as decoration in Vorin countries.{{book ref|sa1|18}}
