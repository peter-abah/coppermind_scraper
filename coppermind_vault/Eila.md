**Eila** is a city in [[Iri]] in northwestern [[Roshar]] during the [[Era of Solitude]].{{map ref|Roshar}} It is located in southern Iri at the foot of the [[Misted Mountains]], near Iri's borders with [[Shinovar]] and [[Babatharnam]].{{map ref|Roshar}}

The [[Eila Stele]], an ancient carving written in [[Dawnchant]],{{book ref|sa3|111}} presumably originated in or was discovered in the city.
