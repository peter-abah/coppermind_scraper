## Nation
Union is a nation on [[Canticle]]. It was created, and led, by the [[Cinder King]]. During its height it stretched almost the entire world, the only regions out of its control being the poles, where life is almost impossible. All major cities were conquered by the Cinder King.

### Military 
Union’s military consists of regular soldiers, and the [[Charred]]. The regular soldiers are equipped with guns powered by [[Sunheart|sunhearts]], and appear to make up the bulk of the Cinder King's forces.

The Charred are invested soldiers. They were once ordinary people, but the Cinder King inserted [[Sunheart|Cinderhearts]] into their chests, burning away their memories and establishing a connection to him, as well as investing them. This connection allows the Cinder King to impress his will upon the Charred, completely controlling their actions. 

The Charred primarily use melee weapons, such as batons, and machetes. They have also been known to use bombs, acting as suicide bombers.

### Politics
The nation is an autocracy, with the [[Cinder King]] as it's head.  He claims right to rule through might, and uses a mix of propaganda, intimidation, and violence to maintain his rule. An example of this is when he fights [[Sigzil|Nomad]] in hand to hand combat, and secretly has a scadrian weight device attached to Nomad, making it nearly impossible for Nomad to fight back. While some people are aware of his propaganda, it is unknown how well spread the information is.

He maintains control over the populace through the use of his invested soldiers, [[Charred]], which only he can create and who only listen to him. Due to their increased strength, endurance his complete control over them, he is able to asert his control.

While all major cities were merged with the capital city, called Union, there are still some small towns which remained separate, while still being under his control. He controlled them via a couple of officials, as well as some Charred 

### Economy
Union has access to many prisoners and captives, and these are sacrificed to make sunhearts, which power their cities. They also have many prospectors just behind the great Maelstrom to find and collect these sunhearts.

Their cities rely on agriculture for food, they sow and harvest every day, just before dawn, with the reflected sunlight. They also have hunters.

They harvest metal from iron fields, places on the planet where molten metal coats the surface.

## City
The capital city of Union goes by the same name. The Cinder King planned for everyone to join and live in the city. While almost all major cities have been added to Union, there are still some towns which roam the planet independently, as they are too small and in bands that are not as hospitable. [[Beacon]] stands as a notable exception, being the largest remaining city to resist being integrated. 

Union consists of thousands of small ships that hover about 40 feet off the ground, and constantly move forward across the surface of the planet. The ships come in a variety of sizes, shapes, and function, and can be rearranged based on the needs of the city, such as when some are arranged to form an arena.

In the lower levels of the ship that makes up the heart of Union there is an unornamented metal staircase that leads to a large hallway with hundreds of [[sunheart]]s. This room is guarded by a single charred. At the end of this hallway is the room where new charred are made.

The Cinder king has the ability to put the city on lockdown for a period of time, this way, without the password, no one can leave the city until the time is over.

### Corridor 
Union flies along the equator, although it has at times left this position.
