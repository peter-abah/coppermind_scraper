**Sir Ashinata** is a fictional character featured in the [[hion]] viewer program *[[Seasons of Regret]]*, which is a historical drama. He carries a hion blade, and is typically displayed using blue hion lines. As ronin, Ashinata is a wandering warrior forbidden to be with the woman he loves, Lady [[Hinobi]].{{book ref|yumi|25}}

After his first kiss with Lady Hinobi, Ashinata announces that he has to leave forever, due to a threat set in motion by his dead, evil brother. He returns to Hinobi in a surprise finale episode.{{book ref|yumi|33}}
