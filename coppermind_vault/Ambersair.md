An **Ambersair** is a revolver on [[Scadrial]].{{book ref|mb4|14}}

[[Ranette]] made an Ambersair for [[Waxillium Ladrian]], which he lost while fighting [[Miles Dagouter]] on a train.{{book ref|mb4|14}} After Wax was given [[Vindication]], he typically used a [[Sterrion]] as his secondary weapon,{{book ref|mb5|11}} and was not seen using an Ambersair again.
