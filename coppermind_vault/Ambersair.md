An **Ambersair** is a revolver on [[Scadrial]].

[[Ranette]] made an Ambersair for [[Waxillium Ladrian]], which he lost while fighting [[Miles Dagouter]] on a train. After Wax was given [[Vindication]], he typically used a [[Sterrion]] as his secondary weapon, and was not seen using an Ambersair again.
