**Kunona** is a [[listener]] on [[Roshar]]. She used to arrange small plants and rocks in beautiful ways to make a diorama. [[Venli]] was fond of her and her work, later being reminded of it while walking through nature. She died during the birth of the Everstorm.{{book ref|sa5|70}}
