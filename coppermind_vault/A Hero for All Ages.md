***A Hero for All Ages*** is a historical operetta in post-[[Catacendre]] [[Scadrial]], written by [[Sablerfils]] and [[Buissonommes]].

A performance at [[Clarvonne's Theater]] in [[Elendel]] was advertised in *[[The House Record]]* on the 1st of Doxil, . The title is a play on the term "[[Hero of Ages]]", and the operetta presumably tells the story of [[Vin]] and [[Elend]] during the [[Final Empire]]. The advertisement appears to depict the couple reaching toward each other while wearing [[mistcloak]]s. Vin is held back by a group of [[Steel Inquisitors]], and Elend by a group of [[skaa]]. 

According to an account by [[Nicelle Sauvage]] published in *[[The Two Seasons]]* in , the [[kandra]] [[Tabaar]] once played the role of [[Human]] in the play. She reminded him of his masterful performance in an effort to boost his confidence.
