**Lanacin the Surefooted** is a historical figure on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa1|51}}

He is said to have killed a [[Shardbearer]] and won a [[Shardblade]].{{book ref|sa1|51}}{{book ref|sa2|44}} He was presumably [[Alethi]], as there is a monument to Lanacin in southwest [[Kholinar]].{{map ref|Kholinar}}

[[Kaladin]] knew of his story, and believed that the stories of men like Lanacin and [[Evod Markmaker]] indicated that opportunities for [[darkeyes]] had decreased over time in Alethkar.{{book ref|sa2|44}}

Either Lanacin the Surefooted or [[Evod Markmaker]] actually poisoned a Shardbearer to obtain their Shards, although this isn't well known.{{wob ref|15880}}
