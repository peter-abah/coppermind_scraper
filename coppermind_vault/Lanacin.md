**Lanacin the Surefooted** is a historical figure on [[Roshar]].

He is said to have killed a [[Shardbearer]] and won a [[Shardblade]]. He was presumably [[Alethi]], as there is a monument to Lanacin in southwest [[Kholinar]].

[[Kaladin]] knew of his story, and believed that the stories of men like Lanacin and [[Evod Markmaker]] indicated that opportunities for [[darkeyes]] had decreased over time in Alethkar.

Either Lanacin the Surefooted or [[Evod Markmaker]] actually poisoned a Shardbearer to obtain their Shards, although this isn't well known.
