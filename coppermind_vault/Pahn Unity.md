The **Pahn Unity** was the coalition of nations that were united against Hallandren during the [[Manywar]].{{book ref|wb|26}} It is possible many [[Pahn Kahl]] joined in the Unity to oppose the Hallandren who had long occupied their land, hence the name of the coalition. It may have been referred to as a "unity" because the Pahn Kahl religion had five gods in a unity.{{wob ref|6964}}

It may have included [[Gys]], and almost surely included [[Tedradel]].

The leader of the Pahn Unity was killed in the [[Battle of Twilight Falls]].{{book ref|wb|26}}
