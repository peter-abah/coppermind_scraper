**Brekv** is a [[Thaylen]] sailor on [[Roshar]]. He works on the crew of a scouting vessel captained by [[Smta]].

When [[Yalb]] spotted the *[[First Dreams]]* in the distance, he climbed up to join Brekv, who was manning the [[Skyeel#Cultural References|eel's nest]], to ask him what he could see with his spyglass. Brekv told Yalb that the ship was not flying a banner and asked if he wanted to report it to the captain.
