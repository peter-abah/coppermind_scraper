**Jix** is a guard for [[House Davar]] in [[Jah Keved]] on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa2|45}}

==Appearance and Personality==
Jix is a [[lighteyed|lighteyes]] of the eighth [[dahn]]. He has a gut, but is very strong. He rarely combs his hair and often chews [[yamma root]].{{book ref|sa2|45}} While performing his guard duties, he wears a weapon, likely a sword.{{book ref|sa2|48}}

Despite being of a low dahn, he exhibits the typical lighteyed disdain for [[darkeyed]] people.{{book ref|sa2|45}} He is loyal to House Davar and serves [[Lin]] for over a decade, and he has a good rapport with the Davar children.{{book ref|sa2|48}} He presumably has a penchant for gambling.{{book ref|sa2|45}}

==History==
Jix accompanied the Davar household to the [[Middlefest Fair]] in {{rosharan date|1170}}. He followed [[Shallan]] when she slipped away from Lin and walked around the festival. He grudgingly accompanied her into the gambling pavilion, and was relieved when he realized that she was just looking for [[Balat]] in order to deliver a letter from [[Eylita Tavinar]]. Several people in the gambling pavilion said hello to Jix.{{book ref|sa2|45}}

Jix and his fellow guard [[Ekel]] were present when [[Mill]] attempted to extort Lin over [[Jushu Davar]]'s debts. He was armed and prepared to fight, if necessary. Jix seemed a bit baffled that Lin refused to pay Mill and allowed him to take Jushu.{{book ref|sa2|48}}

Jix and the other Davar household guards that [[Shallan]] had grown up with were later replaced after the [[Ghostblood]]s took interest in [[Lin Davar]] and [[Luesh]] was installed as his house steward.{{book ref|sa2|61}}{{book ref|sa2|65}} The guards who replaced Jix and his colleagues were far more brutal and bloody, and the Davar children wished that the old guards were still around.{{book ref|sa2|61}}
