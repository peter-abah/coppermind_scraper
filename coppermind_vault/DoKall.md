**DoKall** is a substance used on [[Taldain]]'s [[Dayside]] to make objects water resistant.{{book ref|ws|9|review=yes}}

The shells of [[terha]] are doused with DoKall from infancy in order to keep their shells tough and resilient.{{book ref|ws|9|review=yes}}
