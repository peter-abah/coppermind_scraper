**Mara** is [[Lirin]]'s apprentice in [[Hearthstone]].

When [[Kaladin]] returns to Hearthstone after the first [[Everstorm]], Mara has been an apprentice surgeon for a few years.

Lirin implies that they are not yet ready to be a full surgeon, questioning whether Mara would be able to take over for him if he left.{{book ref|sa3|6}}
