**Cyn** is a member of [[Kaladin]]'s squad in Highmarshal [[Meridas Amaram]]'s army on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa1|1}}

During a battle in [[Alethkar]] in 1172, Cyn and [[Korater]] were told by [[Kaladin]] to escort the wounded [[Cenn]] to a medic.{{book ref|sa1|1}} However, the [[Shardbearer]] [[Helaran Davar]] rode by Cyn as he was retreating to the medics with Cenn, killing Cyn with his [[Shardblade]] as he passed.{{book ref|sa1|47}}
