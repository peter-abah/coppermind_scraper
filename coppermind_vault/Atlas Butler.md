**Atlas Butler** is a paramedic who works in Boston. He attempts to save [[Dan]] from a deadly injury inflicted by [[Lovestruck]], but fails. He wears a white long-sleeved shirt with a blue patch on the shoulder. His eyes are bright blue, like gemstones. Later, Jax tells Prof that he wants to save people like Atlas.
