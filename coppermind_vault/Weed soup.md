**Weed soup** was a dish served in [[Hraggen]] on [[Sel]] during the [[Late Era]].{{book ref|elantris|35}} Most people did not think that it tasted very good. As it is often referred to as **Hraggish weed soup**, it's possible that there are other varieties of weed soup.

[[Kiin]] knew how to prepare weed soup, a fact disliked by his family.{{book ref|elantris|26}}

King [[Eventeo]] was forced to eat weed soup by [[Sarene's mother]], in order to lose weight and to become healthier.{{book ref|elantris|35}}
