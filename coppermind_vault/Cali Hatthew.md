**Cali Hatthew** is a constable in [[Elendel]] on [[Scadrial]]. She joined the force in {{date|346|PC}}. In {{date|348|PC}} when [[Marasi Colms]] planned a sting operation against the [[Set]] in [[Bilming]], Cali begged to come. She and another constable from Elendel died in the conflict that broke out when the Set decided to resist arrest. Their deaths weighed heavily on Marasi and negatively affected her confidence.{{book ref|mb7|26}}
