**Chip Erikell** is an accused criminal in [[Elendel]] on [[Scadrial]].

They were suspected of smuggling in the Third Octant of [[Elendel]] by [[Claude Aradel]] and the constabulary. They were present alongside a number of criminals and corrupt nobles during [[Winsting Innate's auction]] for his senate vote. They were killed during the auction as a result of [[Bleeder]]'s actions, and [[Waxillium Ladrian]] identified their corpse on sight.
