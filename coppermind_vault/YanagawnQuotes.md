## Quotes

> We thought we’d be safe after driving the enemy back in Emul. Even with the contest, will it ever end?
> — Yanagawn to Adolin after discovering an impending invasion of his homeland.

> I mention it, not to dwell upon it, but to remember a time when I was commonly put into dangerous situations. I did not handle it well then. I often wonder … how would I handle it now?
> — Yanagawn to Adolin and Noura after mentioning his status as a commoner before being elevated to Prime.

> Yanagawn is a *good* emperor. He has the heart to be an excellent one. If you'd seen the progress he's made … If you knew the previous emperors, all excellent candidates on paper, who became shells … Do you realise how long it’s been since we had a Prime who truly understood the needs of the common people? We *can’t lose him*.
> — Noura to Adolin about Yanagawn

> Your power is absolute, but you do not exercise it all. You must not.
> — Noura to Yanagawn when he wanted to return and fight the Thaylen battle

> I would say that hope defines us, Jasnah. Without it, we are not human.
> — Yanagawn to Jasnah, as they plan to take back [[Urithiru]]
