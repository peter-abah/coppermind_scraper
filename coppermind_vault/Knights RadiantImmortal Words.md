This page lists all known versions of the Ideals of the Knights Radiant, also known as the **Immortal Words**. For information on the Immortal Words in general, see [[Knights Radiant#Immortal Words]]. For details about the meaning of the Ideals of a specific order as well as the powers they gain with each Ideal, see the appropriate section on that order's individual page.

## The First Ideal

> Life before death. Strength before weakness. Journey before destination.
> — The First Ideal of the Knights Radiant

## Subsequent Ideals of Individual Orders
After the First Ideal, each Order has unique words that serve as a path to increasing the [[Nahel bond]] and becoming a Knight Radiant. The exact words used may vary, but generally, the concepts implied by them are shared. A Surgebinder progressing along the path will know the words when they are ready to say them. Each order has four sets of words before the final culmination of the Nahel bond. 

### Ideals of the [[Order of Windrunners]]

> I will protect those who cannot protect themselves.
> — The Second Ideal of the Windrunners, as sworn by [[Kaladin]] and [[Lopen]]

> I will protect even those I hate, so long as it is right.
> — The Third Ideal of the Windrunners, as sworn by [[Kaladin]]

> I will protect even those I hate, even if the one I hate most is myself.
> — The Third Ideal of the Windrunners, as sworn by [[Teft]]

> I’ve got to protect people, you know? Even from myself.
> — The Third Ideal of the Windrunners, as sworn by [[Lopen]]

> I accept that there will be those I cannot protect.
> — The Fourth Ideal of the Windrunners, as sworn by [[Kaladin]]

> I will protect myself, so that I may continue to protect others.
> — The Fifth Ideal of the Windrunners, as sworn by [[Kaladin]]

### Ideals of the [[Order of Skybreakers]]

> I will put the law before all else.
> — A general form of the Second Ideal of the Skybreakers

> I swear to seek justice, to let it guide me, until I find a more perfect Ideal.
> — The Second Ideal of the Skybreakers, as sworn by [[Szeth]]

> I swear to follow the will of Dalinar Kholin. This is my oath.
> — The Third Ideal of the Skybreakers, as sworn by [[Szeth]]

> I will cleanse the Shin of their false leaders, so long as Dalinar Kholin agrees.
> — The Fourth Ideal of the Skybreakers, as sworn by [[Szeth]]

> I am my own agent. I make my own choices. I. Am. THE LAW!
> — The Fifth Ideal of the Skybreakers, as sworn by [[Szeth]]

### Ideals of the [[Order of Truthwatchers]]

> I will reveal truth to all who seek it.

### Ideals of the [[Order of Lightweavers]]
The [[Order of Lightweavers]] acknowledge no Ideals other than the First Ideal shared by all Knights Radiant. Members of the Lightweavers do however need to speak "Truths" to advance, which is similar to the process of speaking the Ideals in other Orders. However, instead of a shared set of beliefs like the other orders, the Truths of Lightweavers seem to be acknowledgements of personal truths, especially those that are hard to accept or ones that have been kept secret.

### Ideals of the [[Order of Elsecallers]]
The [[Order of Elsecallers]] acknowledge no Ideals other than the First Ideal shared by all Knights Radiant. Members of the Elsecallers do however need to speak "Aspirations" to advance, which is similar to the process of speaking the Ideals in other Orders. However, instead of a shared set of beliefs like the other orders, the Aspirations of Elsecallers seem to be themed towards individual growth and self-improvement.
