**Jomari** is a [[Darkeyes|darkeyed]] man on [[Roshar]]. He is proud, impulsive and ambitious, believing himself to have a great destiny. Despite this, or perhaps because of it, he was sent to [[Torol Sadeas|Sadeas]]’ bridge crews. He is also said to be an Agent, although what exactly this means is unclear.
