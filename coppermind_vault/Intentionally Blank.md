***Intentionally Blank*** is the name of a podcast and video series produced by [[Dragonsteel Entertainment]] that features [[Brandon Sanderson]] and [[Dan Wells]]. They discuss a variety of topics in pop culture including books, movies, TV series, and video games. They also discuss their own writing, and Brandon sometimes has to catch himself before accidentally revealing details of unreleased books.{{wob ref|15419}}{{wob ref|15469}}

The podcast series was started in summer 2021 and new episodes are usually posted each week.{{bws ref|intentionally-blank-episode-1-popcornture-vs-stubad|text=Intentionally Blank Episode 1 &mdash; Popcornture vs. Stubad|date=2021-06-09}} [[Adam Horne]] produces; sound editing and engineering are performed by Daniel Thompson. After a few audio-only episodes, the discussions are now captured on video and posted to Brandon's YouTube channel.
