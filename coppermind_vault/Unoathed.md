The **Unoathed** are a group on [[Roshar]] that have formed a close relationship with [[deadeye]] [[spren]]. After the formation of [[Retribution]] and his revocation of [[Stormlight]], they are the only individuals, aside from the [[Knights Radiant]], able to bond a [[Shardblade]] and regrow [[Shardplate]]. According to [[Mayalaran|Maya]], this is a side-effect of the reformation of the [[Oathpact]].

## History

The Unoathed were formed when Maya brought dozens of deadeyes and their associated armor spren through [[Shadesmar]] to [[Azimir]], where [[Adolin Kholin]], Emperor [[Yanagawn]], and their allies were making a last stand to defend the throne room against [[Odium]]'s forces. The spren formed Shardblades and Shardplate in the [[Physical Realm]] when they arrived; although many of the defenders were untrained in combat, the sheer power of the Shards allowed them to hold until the [[contest of champions]] had begun, at which point Azimir was officially free from Odium, according to the [[contest of champions#Contract|terms]] of the contest.

Afterward, Yanagawn considered all of the Unoathed to be of a sufficiently high rank to speak directly to him, which was a rare privilege in Azish society. [[Noura]] disliked the familiarity that they displayed as a result, but it meant that he could have real friends for practically the first time since becoming emperor.

Maya brought thirty-seven deadeyes in total, meaning that the Unoathed would be able to grow their numbers considerably if they desired, but they decided to be deliberate in doing so.

Many years later, on [[Canticle]], a [[Scadrian]] scientist used the word "unoathed" in the context of [[Nomad]]'s allegiance in the [[cosmere]]-wide arms race going on at the time, though that may have been a generic usage of the word and not a reference to this specific group.
