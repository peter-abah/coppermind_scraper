**Deeli** is a [[lighteyed]] woman on the [[Shattered Plains]] of [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa1|12}}

At one point in {{Rosharan date|1173}}, Deeli courted Brightlord [[Adolin Kholin]], and even met his father Highprince [[Dalinar]], but their relationship did not work out.{{book ref|sa1|12}} During their relationship, she once claimed to be suffering from 'womanly reasons' four times in a single month.{{book ref|sa3|21}}

Deeli is short and has a fondness for silver hair ribbons.{{book ref|sa1|12}}
