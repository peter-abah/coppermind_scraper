**Ruli** is one of [[Taravangian]]'s granddaughters on [[Roshar]]. She has a gap-toothed smile.{{book ref|sa3|122}}

In a letter sent to Taravangian while he is in [[Urithiru]], [[Karavangia]] enclosed a sketch of herself, Ruli, and [[Gvori]]. Ruli had included a small drawing of some flowers.{{book ref|sa3|122}} Ruli is not the grandchild who was trapped following a rockfall.{{wob ref|11637}}
