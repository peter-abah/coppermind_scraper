**Ruli** is one of [[Taravangian]]'s granddaughters on [[Roshar]]. She has a gap-toothed smile.

In a letter sent to Taravangian while he is in [[Urithiru]], [[Karavangia]] enclosed a sketch of herself, Ruli, and [[Gvori]]. Ruli had included a small drawing of some flowers. Ruli is not the grandchild who was trapped following a rockfall.
