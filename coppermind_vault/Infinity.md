**Infinity** is a resident of [[Babilar]].{{book ref|Firefight|19}}

==Appearance and Personality==
She is blonde and perky, and she wears the painted clothing common in Babilar. She and her friends have an indifferent attitude towards [[Epic]]s, despite the death and destruction that they often bring about.{{book ref|Firefight|19}}

==History==
She is an acquaintance of [[Mizzy]]'s and attends the [[party in Babilar]] with [[Marco (Reckoners)|Marco]], [[Lulu]], and [[Calaka]]. She attempts to spray [[David]] with paint to help him fit in at the party, but he nervously overreacts, jumping away from her.{{book ref|Firefight|19}}

Mizzy asks the group about a mutual friend named [[Steve]]. Infinity believes that he was one of the eighty people killed{{book ref|Firefight|17}} in a recent attack by [[Obliteration]], but she and Marco quickly change the subject to the quality of [[Edso]]'s party music. David is stunned by both the apathy of the Babilarans and their disdain for the [[Reckoners]] and anyone else who tries to fight Epics. Infinity tells him that more people would survive if they simply let the Epics do what they want until they get bored and move on.{{book ref|Firefight|19}}
