**Loremothers** are women who maintain the oral history of the [[Eelakin]] people on the planet [[First of the Sun]]. The do so under the direction of the [[kingmaker]]s. As storytellers, they teach the Eelakin about their history and culture. Loremothers traditionally wear a costume made of feathers, arranged in a particular old style, with long sleeves. However, as the planet industrialised, many loremothers took to wearing more modern costumes. 

The official duties of the loremothers are reciting stories for the chiefs and kingmakers but they also provide their services to the general public. Every year, the loremothers gather to repeat their stories and check them for errors. Due in part because of these covens, the stories of the loremothers are considered to be incredibly accurate, even able to remain (largely) accurate after millenia. Loremothers are also somehow forced to be impartial. At least one loremother is also called **worldsinger**, but whether this is the same as a loremother, a subset of loremothers or an entirely different thing which this loremother happened to be a part of is unknown. The only currently named Loremother is [[Frond]].
