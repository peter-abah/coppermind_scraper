A **zinkall**, plural **zinkallin**, is a ranged weapon wielded by [[Dayside|Daysiders]] on [[Taldain]]. It utilizes pneumatics to fire small arrows at high speed.

==Characteristics==
A zinkall looks like a set of thin reed-like pipes held together with rope. It is mounted on the forearm, near the wrist. There are usually four or five tubes, although some zinkallin may have many more. Each tube contains a front-loaded arrow that can be launched by the wielder by flicking their wrist to release compressed air, pushing the arrow out of the tube at high speed. Each firing chamber has its own compressed air, and the user must use a levered pump on the top of the zinkall to recharge for the next shot. The arrows are small and are sometimes referred to as darts. The arrowheads can be carved from sandling carapace, making them [[terken]], and the arrows usually have fletching on the back of their shafts. Extra arrows can be carried in a small quiver attached to a belt or sling. 

Zinkallin are common weapons on Dayside, being the preferred weapon for duels, battles, and self-defense. They are seen used by [[Kerzta|Kerztian]] warrior-priests, [[Lossand]]in [[trackt]]s, and members of [[Sharezan]]'s gang. They appear to be quite accurate, although proficiency may take some practice. Zinkallin can be used in very close quarters and the arrows can be fired in rapid succession. They are very convenient and portable, but lack the power of bows and crossbows. Their exact range is unknown, but they are typically used at short range to wound an opponent before engaging them. Most users wield a single zinkall to allow the use of a melee weapon in their other hand, usually a sword. In some nations of [[Darkside]], they have been made largely obsolete by the discovery of gunpowder.

==History==
Zinkallin are seen many times during the events that led to [[Kenton]] becoming [[Lord Mastrell]]. They were used by the Kerztians in their attack on the [[Diem]], by both [[Lokmlen]] and [[Ais]] during their fight in the market, and by several assassins who tried to kill Kenton.

[[Khrissalla]] found the weapons interesting and noted that they operated on the same basic principles as a pistol. [[Jon Acron]] was surprised that the Daysiders had pneumatic technology.

[[Tain]] gave Ais a defective zinkall before luring her into a trap.
