**Fourleaf** is a plant on [[Roshar]] whose sap can be used as an antiseptic.{{book ref|sa1|2}}

It is known to scare away [[rotspren]]. The plant is most potent when harvested directly after the [[Weeping]].{{rpg ref|slwg|12}}

[[Kaladin]]'s father [[Lirin]] taught him of the usefulness of fourleaf sap when training him in surgery.{{book ref|sa1|2}}
