Granny **Hilde** is the operator of a passwall in [[Luthadel]] on [[Scadrial]]. 

## Appearance and Personality
Granny Hilde is a small, elderly woman, who is nearly blind and uses a cane to walk. She has surprisingly dexterous fingers for her age to count coins with. The grizzled woman implies that she has many grandsons.

In the underground, people know her to just take coin and not ask questions, although she can grumble a bit.

## Passwall
Her passwall is located within a few minutes' ride from [[House Venture#Keep Venture|Keep Venture]]. The tunnel is half burrowed and half cut from the wall's stones themselves, and large enough to lead a horse through. A couple of burly men are needed to open and close the false stone wall on the outside.

## History
She used to give [[Ham]] sweets when he was a child, which was actually a quiet but well-targeted way of getting publicity for her passwall, since Ham was already involved in the underground by then. As a result, Ham became quite fond of her. He later used her passwall to sneak his wife [[Mardra]] and their children in and out of the city when they came to visit him.

During the [[Siege of Luthadel]], [[Felt]] informed [[Elend]] that passwall keepers such as Hilde were letting people out of Luthadel, mostly at night, defying Elend's orders.

When [[Elend]] wanted to visit [[Jastes Lekal]] in his [[koloss]] army, Ham led him through Granny Hilde's passwall. Elend paid her three [[clip]]s extra to post someone who could watch for his return; Hilde said she could probably fetch one of her grandsons for this task.
