**Gaino** is a resident of [[Kilahito]] on [[Komashi]]. He is a weightlifter and a friend of [[Tojin]], as well as a patron of the [[Noodle Pupil]].

Gaino gives Tojin some advice regarding the lat press, which Tojin finds very helpful. [[Yumi]] watches Tojin and Gaino interact while trying to get a better read on the former's character and interests. Tojin removes his shirt to show his muscles as evidence of the workout's effectiveness, and Gaino asks him what his reps were. Tojin responds that he did four sets of twenty reps.
