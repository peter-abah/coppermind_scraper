Callsign **Jager** is a member of [[Nightmare Flight]] along with [[Nose]], [[Tunestone]], [[Sushi]], [[Bog]], and [[Nord]].{{book ref|Skyward|46}}{{book ref|sky1|9}}.

They graduated [[Flight School]] in the previous class to [[Skyward Flight]].{{book ref|sky1|9}}

[[Jorgen Weight]] hoped to use it as his own callsign until finding out that it was in use.{{book ref|Skyward|9}}

During their training, Skyward Flight was paired with Nightmare Flight, however, Jager was not present during this exercise. The flightleader, Nose, offered [[Spensa]] and [[FM]] spots on Nightmare Flight as they were down several members, suggesting that Jager may have died.{{book ref|sky1|46}}
