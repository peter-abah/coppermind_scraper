Callsign **Jager** is a member of [[Nightmare Flight]] along with [[Nose]], [[Tunestone]], [[Sushi]], [[Bog]], and [[Nord]]..

They graduated [[Flight School]] in the previous class to [[Skyward Flight]].

[[Jorgen Weight]] hoped to use it as his own callsign until finding out that it was in use.

During their training, Skyward Flight was paired with Nightmare Flight, however, Jager was not present during this exercise. The flightleader, Nose, offered [[Spensa]] and [[FM]] spots on Nightmare Flight as they were down several members, suggesting that Jager may have died.
