**Chananar** is a soldier in the [[coalition of monarchs]] on [[Roshar]].

## History
[[Navani Kholin]] asked Chananar to secretly watch her workstation. Navani had set the workstation as a bait for whoever was sending her secret [[spanreed]] messages. [[Dabbid]] took the bait and tried to replace Navani's lost spanreed. Chananar observed Dabbid doing so, and caught him in the act along with a couple other soldiers. Chananar then took the spanreed [[fabrial]] from Dabbid and gave it to Navani.
