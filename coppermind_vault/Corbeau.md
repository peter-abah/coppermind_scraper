**Corbeau** is a rural settlement in the eastern [[Elendel Basin]] on [[Scadrial]].{{map ref|Elendel Basin}}{{file ref|House Record broadsheet.jpg|text=*The House Record* broadsheet}}{{ref|group=fn|text=Corbeau does not appear on all versions of the Elendel Basin map; the dam and nearby reservoir are shown on the map from *[[Shadows of Self]]* as annotations by [[Nazh]].}}

## Geography
It is located at the eastern edge of the Basin. It lies north of the [[Irongate River]] on a small tributary.{{map ref|Elendel Basin}}

## Landmarks
The most notable feature of Corbeau is a nearby dam that creates a large reservoir near the foot of the [[Faleast Range]]. The reservoir provides water that irrigates one of the Basin's largest grain-producing regions.{{file ref|House Record broadsheet.jpg|text=*The House Record* broadsheet}}

## History
In {{date|342|PC}}, the dam in Corbeau was destroyed by a saboteur.{{book ref|mb5|14}} This caused dozens of deaths{{book ref|mb5|14}} and extensive flooding in the region, such that commodities markets predicted a spike in future grain costs due to the harvest being cut in half.{{file ref|House Record broadsheet.jpg|text=*The House Record* broadsheet}} The panic over the impending shortage of grain caused all food to suddenly increase in price, despite the fact that there were reserves that could be released.{{book ref|mb5|6}} This added to the turmoil in Elendel, which was already reeling from a labor strike and the murder of [[Winsting Innate]].{{book ref|mb5|12}}

Governor [[Replar Innate]]'s promise to send aid to the region, supported by the [[Argien-Ohr Financial Circle]] and Lord [[Chapmot Heviers]],{{file ref|House Record broadsheet.jpg|text=*The House Record* broadsheet}} was not well-received by the people that were suffering in Elendel.{{book ref|mb5|6}}

Working on a hunch, [[Marasi Colms]] investigated the dam breaking incident.{{book ref|mb5|12}} She and [[MeLaan]] concluded that a local farmer named [[Johnst]] who had been tried and executed for sabotaging the dam was actually [[Bleeder]].{{book ref|mb5|14}}
