**Wink** is one of [[Alcatraz Smedry]]'s [[Mokia]]n advisors during the battle for [[Tuki Tuki]]. He advises Alcatraz along with the other two advisors, [[Dink]] and [[Mink (Alcatraz)|Mink]], with whom he is not related.{{book ref|a4|70706}} 

## Appearance and Personality
Wink is tall and thin, with a long neck and spindly arms. Alcatraz likens his appearance to a bottle of soda.{{book ref|a4|70706}}

## History
During the battle and prior to meeting up with Alcatraz, Wink’s mind was affected by a Librarian disharmony grenade, which confused his brain. He continued to give good advice, but about random and un relevant topics.{{book ref|a4|70706}} At Alcatraz’ orders, Mink and the other counselors went to fetch him some backpacks full of explosive teddy bears, six of  their fastest warriors, and a pair of very long stilts. They hesitated briefly, but did as asked.{{book ref|a4|6.02214179 × 10^23}}
