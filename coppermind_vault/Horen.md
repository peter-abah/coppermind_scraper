**Horen** is an [[Elantrian]] on [[Sel]]. He lives in [[New Elantris]] during [[Raoden]]'s exile there.

He is a thin-limbed, anxious man who avoids conflict. He presumably has the same splotchy gray skin and thinning white hair as other Elantrians that are affected by the broken [[Shaod]].

Horen was not particularly loyal to any of the gang leaders in Elantris. He observed an argument between [[Karata]] and [[Dashe]] about attacking [[Aanden]] but stayed out of it. It is not known if he survived to see the restoration of Elantris and [[AonDor]].
