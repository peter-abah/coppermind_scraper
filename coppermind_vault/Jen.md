**Jen** is a medieval historian and [[Runian]]'s former partner.

## History
Jen, John, and [[Ryan Chu]] were friends at least as early as high school. During that time, Jen quickly found an interest in history.

At some point, Jen began dating John. Two weeks later, she started secretly cheating on John with Ryan. Jen and John often argued during their relationship.

Eventually, Jen infiltrated [[Ulric Stromfin]]'s organization, using her knowledge as a medievalist to involve herself with his dimension projects. During her infiltration, she pretended be on a trip in Europe and let John think she had died in an accident.

When the [[hordamen]] began their attack on [[Maelport]], Jen escaped with Ryan through the dimensional portal to the police department headquarters.
