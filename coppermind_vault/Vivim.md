**Vivim trees** are a plant on [[Roshar]] with intertwining spindly branches. They are present in [[Alethkar]].

[[Kaladin]] flies over a valley filled with a vivim forest upon leaving [[Hearthstone]], near when the [[Everstorm]] first hit.
