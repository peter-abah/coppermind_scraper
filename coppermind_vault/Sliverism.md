**Sliverism** is a religion on [[Scadrial]] that arose after the [[Final Ascension]].

According to [[Steris Harms]], they are known to revere [[Ironeyes]], A.K.A. Marsh.{{book ref|aol|4}}{{book ref|mb1|10}} Although Marsh won't admit it, a part of him—the part that's similar to his brother, [[Kelsier]]— enjoys being seen as a god of death.{{wob ref|5082}}

It can be inferred that Sliverism is an evolution of the dominant religion of the [[Final Empire]], brought to the new world by [[Obligator|obligators]] such as [[Aradan Yomen]] that survived the Final Ascension, as the [[Lord Ruler]] was referred to the "Sliver of Infinity" in religious terms. This is a possible motive for worshiping Ironeyes, as the old religion revered [[Steel Inquisitors]], of which Ironeyes is the last member.
