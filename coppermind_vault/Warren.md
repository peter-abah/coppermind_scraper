**Warren** is an [[Azish]] master [[Skybreaker]] wearing the garb of a [[Marabethian]] lawkeeper, with his chest bare and a patterned short cloak . He is formally referred to as **Master Warren**.  He announced the start and end of the pole fight test in the [[Purelake]].

## Attributes and Abilities
As a master Skybreaker, Warren has access to the [[Surge]]s of Gravitation and Division. This allows him the ability to make use of the Basic and Reverse Lashings, as well as the ability to degrade objects.{{book ref|sa3|part=ars}} He also has the ability to take in [[Stormlight]], allowing him to recover from all but the most severe of injuries.

## History

Warren, along with [[Ki]], traveled from the Skybreaker fortress with [[Szeth]] and twenty other Skybreaker squires to a testing ground on the Purelake. He announced a test of martial competence where the Skybreaker hopefuls, using their [[Lashing]]s, had to fight by throwing colored bags of powder at each other within a contest area marked by wooden poles. He explained that no other weapons were allowed and the winner would be the squire with the cleanest uniform at the end of the test. When the sun had gone down he shouted an end to the test. Szeth showed great skill with his Lashings and ultimately won the test by exploiting a loophole--washing his uniform prior to the end. Warren and Ki concluded that he was beyond the skill of the other squires. Warren told Szeth he should have his own [[spren]] soon.{{book ref|sa3|98}}

Warren accompanied [[Nale]], Szeth, and most of the other Skybreakers on their journey to [[Marat]], flying southward for three days. Upon their arrival in [[Marat]], he landed and conferred briefly with Nale and the other masters before flying to a nearby town with all the Skybreakers except for Szeth and Nale.{{book ref|sa3|106}}
