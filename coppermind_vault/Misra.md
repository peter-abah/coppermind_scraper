Misra and Ranette were dating in . Ranette quipped that Misra might shoot her if she were to go to a bar with [[Wayne]] during the [[Elendel]] riots. It is unclear when the couple broke up, but by  Ranette was in a relationship with her future wife, [[Jaxy]].
