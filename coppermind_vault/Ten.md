**Ten** is one of [[House Davar]]'s guards in [[Jah Keved]] on [[Roshar]] prior to [[Lin Davar]]'s association with the [[Ghostbloods]].

Ten had worked for the Davars for a number of years, but by , [[Luesh]] had taken over as house steward and purged all of Lin's old guardsmen. [[Balat Davar]] compared the old guards such as [[Beal]] and Ten with the newer, more violent guards including [[Levrin]] and [[Rin]]. Balat remarked that Beal and Ten were friendly and liked to joke around.
