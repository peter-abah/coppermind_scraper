**Isaeuc's Bend** is a city in the northern [[Roughs]] on [[Scadrial]]. It is northeast of [[True Madil]].

==Trivia==
*Its name is probably a reference to the artist of the maps in the [[Mistborn (series)|Mistborn series]], [[Isaac Stewart]].
