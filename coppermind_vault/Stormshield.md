A **stormshield** is a large shield used by the [[listener]]s on [[Roshar]]. Stormshields are designed to protect their carrier from a [[highstorm]], although they don’t always succeed in this task.

[[Eshonai]], along with a number of other listeners, prefers to have a stormshield with her when she goes out into a highstorm. Eshonai secures the stormshield on her back while jumping chasms, then unhooks it when a highstorm arrives.
