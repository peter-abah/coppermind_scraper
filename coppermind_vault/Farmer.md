The **Farmer** is a leader among the [[Shin]] on [[Roshar]]. He wears a many-colored robe and is referred to as colors-nimi by [[Szeth]]. During an invasion by raiders before the [[True Desolation]], Szeth asks him how he knows what is right or wrong. He responds by saying that the difference between humans and animals is that the [[spren]] move the animals, and there is no good answer. Szeth is not completely satisfied with this answer and continues to search for a moral guide. Later, after Szeth kills for the first time, the Farmer encourages Szeth's family to send him to train as soldiers as well.
