> Ah, my dear Brightlord Taselin. Still engaged in your experiment to prove a maximum threshold of human idiocy? Good for you! Very empirical of you.
> — [[Wit]] to Taselin

Brightlord **Taselin** was an [[Alethi]] noble on the [[Shattered Plains]] of [[Roshar]].

Taselin attended one of King [[Elhokar Kholin]]'s feasts, where his intelligence was mocked by [[Wit]].

He walked with a waddle.
