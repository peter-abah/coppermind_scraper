**Feral** is an [[Epic]]. She has mutation powers, and can cause animals to mutate into horrifying and dangerous beasts. She attacks the [[Wikipedia: Cameron Park Zoo|Cameron Park Zoo]], and later [[Hershel]] and his special forces squad have to put the creatures down. Feral's current status is unknown.{{book ref|lux|16}}
