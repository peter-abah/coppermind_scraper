> Is my defense of the Summer Hill really the "Burden by which the Hero shall be dubbed"?
> — [[Alendi's logbook]]

The **Summer Hill** was a location on [[Scadrial]] before [[Rashek]] reshaped the world. Prior to traveling to the [[Well of Ascension]], [[Alendi]] defended the hill, which some saw as a fulfillment of a line of the [[Terris Prophecies]].
