**Jenin** is one of [[Dalinar's elites]]. He was present during the attack where they recruited [[Teleb]].{{book ref|sa3|3}}

He is referred to as a striker.{{ref|group=fn|text=The term "striker" is not defined in the text, but in American military slang it can refer to an soldier who serves as an officer's personal servant for extra pay.{{url ref|url=https://www.dictionary.com/browse/striker|text=Striker definition|site=Dictionary.com}}}} When Dalinar and the elites approached Brightlord [[Yezriar]], Jenin unhooked his shortbow and shot Yezriar's horse. Jenin lamented that it was a terrible waste of money, and Dalinar said he would buy Jenin two horses when they were finished.{{book ref|sa3|3}}
