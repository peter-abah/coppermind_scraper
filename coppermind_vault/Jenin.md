> Hate shooting horses. Like throwing a thousand broams into the storming ocean, Brightlord.
> — Jenin to [[Dalinar Kholin]]

**Jenin** is one of [[Dalinar's elites]]. He was present during the attack where they recruited [[Teleb]].

He is referred to as a striker. When Dalinar and the elites approached Brightlord [[Yezriar]], Jenin unhooked his shortbow and shot Yezriar's horse. Jenin lamented that it was a terrible waste of money, and Dalinar said he would buy Jenin two horses when they were finished.
