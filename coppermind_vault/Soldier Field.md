**Soldier Field** is an old football stadium{{book ref|Steelheart|33}} that the Reckoners used to fight [[Steelheart (Epic)|Steelheart]] in.{{book ref|Steelheart|35}}

There is nothing of real importance around Soldier Field, and thus usually no one goes near it.{{book ref|Steelheart|33}} Like the rest of the city, it was turned to steel during the [[Great Transfersion]].

The Reckoners use Soldier Field as a place to fight Steelheart. They create nooks and sniper nests using the tensors, and replace the old headlights (which were turned to steel) with new UV headlights.{{book ref|Steelheart|34}}
