**Trike** is a type of fish that lives in the [[Sea of Fjorden]] on Sel. 

While discussing crop farming in [[New Elantris]], [[Galladon]] and [[Raoden]] decide to ask [[Sarene]] for trike. They correctly predict that she will assume they want it for food and spitefully provide rotten fish, concealing their true intent to use it as fertilizer.
