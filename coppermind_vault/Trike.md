**Trike** is a type of fish that lives in the [[Sea of Fjorden]] on Sel.{{glossary ref|Elantris|K}}{{ref|text=The original spelling of this fish, "kike", still appears in the glossary on Brandon's website; however, the glossary has not been updated with spelling changes made in the tenth anniversary edition of *Elantris*.}} 

While discussing crop farming in [[New Elantris]], [[Galladon]] and [[Raoden]] decide to ask [[Sarene]] for trike.{{book ref|elantris|25}} They correctly predict that she will assume they want it for food and spitefully provide rotten fish, concealing their true intent to use it as fertilizer.{{book ref|elantris|41}}
