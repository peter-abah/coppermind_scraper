The **Seran River** is a river on [[Scadrial]] in the [[Elendel Basin]] and the [[Roughs]].{{book ref|bom|7}}

## Geography
The river cuts through the [[Seran Range]] in a straight line, having carved various gorges and canyons into the mountains.{{book ref|bom|7}} It flows from an unknown origin in the Roughs northwest to [[Elendel]] and the [[Sea of Yomend]].{{map ref|Elendel Basin}} The water is ice cold.{{book ref|bom|8}}

## Commerce and Transportation
The river is used to transport goods to and from Elendel.{{book ref|bom|5}} A major railway that connects Elendel to various cities (including [[Vindiel-Cameux]], [[Doriel (city)|Doriel]], and [[New Seran]]) lies along the river's path.{{map ref|Elendel Basin}} The railway crisscrosses the river with large latticework bridges.{{book ref|bom|7}} [[Waxillium Ladrian]] remarks on the width of the river and the heavy population on its shores.{{book ref|bom|5}} 

## Trivia
* Prior to the [[Catacendre]], the similarly-named [[River Seran]] existed on a different part of the continent, in the [[Eastern Dominance]] of the [[Final Empire]].{{map ref|Final Empire}}
