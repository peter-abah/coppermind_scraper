**Grent** is a soldier and guard in the army of [[Straff Venture]] after the [[Collapse]] on [[Scadrial]].

==History==
He was posted as a door guard outside of Straff's tent in the Venture camp outside of [[Luthadel]]. He assisted Straff by fetching [[Amaranta]] when Straff believed himself to be poisoned. He was also present when Straff woke up, and gave him updates on the recent actions of Lord [[Janarle]] and the state of the [[koloss]] army. Grent was shocked when Straff told him that he planned to retreat rather than attack the supposedly weakened city of Luthadel; Grent was unaware of [[Zane]]'s betrayal.
