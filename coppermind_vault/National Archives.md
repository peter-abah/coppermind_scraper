The **National Archives** is a building in [[Elendel]] on [[Scadrial]].

The National Archives are located in the Second Octant, near the [[Field of Rebirth]] and across from the governor's mansion. Presumably, the archives store important historical documents.

Governor [[Replar Innate]] liked to address people from the governor's mansion. On at least one such occasion, in , the constables kept an eye on the people from the steps leading up to the National Archives.
