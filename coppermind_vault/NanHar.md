**NanHar** was an ancient king of [[Jah Keved]].{{book ref|sa3|53}} 

He died from an illness while on campaign in what would later become [[Triax]], and his brother [[NanKhet]] inherited his throne.{{book ref|sa3|53}}

NanHar is written of in a history by [[Natata Ved]] two centuries after NanKhet's death.{{book ref|sa3|53}}
