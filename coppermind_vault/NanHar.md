**NanHar** was an ancient king of [[Jah Keved]]. 

He died from an illness while on campaign in what would later become [[Triax]], and his brother [[NanKhet]] inherited his throne.

NanHar is written of in a history by [[Natata Ved]] two centuries after NanKhet's death.
