**Mikaim** is a [[listener]] on [[Roshar]].

During the [[War of Reckoning]], she was one of [[Venli]]'s scholars and their surgeon. She went to [[Jaxlim]]'s home after the elderly listener was found wandering around on the outer plateaus with a wounded head. Mikaim took care of Jaxlim, bandaging her wound and giving her a medication to calm her and help her sleep.
