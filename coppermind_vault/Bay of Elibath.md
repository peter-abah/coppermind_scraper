The **Bay of Elibath** is a body of water north of [[Alethkar]] on [[Roshar]].

The Bay of Elibath separates Alethkar from the northwest peninsula of [[Herdaz]] and the [[Reshi]] island of [[Akak]]. It borders the princedoms of [[Sadeas princedom|Sadeas]], [[Aladar princedom|Aladar]], and [[Roion princedom|Roion]]. The bay feeds into the [[Steamwater Ocean]]. The Alethi port of [[Dalilak]] sits on the Bay of Elibath.

During the [[Heraldic Epochs]], the bay bordered both [[Rishir]] and [[Alethela]].
