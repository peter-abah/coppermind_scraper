The ***MW-232*** is a class of [[Malwish]] military spacecraft. The MW-232s serve as dogfighters to the Malwish, and although they are built for combat in space, they are capable of fighting in Shadesmar as well. The Malwish often claim that the MW-232 is the best fighter in the entire [[cosmere]], with both a regular [[aether]] engine for propulsion, and the ability to [[Steel|Push]] and [[Iron|Pull]] on starship anchors in a proper [[Scadrian]] [[steelfield]], making them capable of outmaneuvering any ordinary ship. The MW-232 is capable of vaporizing a [[Sleepless]], and each fighter also has a sort of [[Sleepless interference machine]].

The MW-232 only makes use of Scadrian technology, and this, along with the pilots' training, has led to the fact that they don't rely on [[Lodestar_(technology)|lodestar]]s to pilot their craft. The Malwish Colonel [[Dajer]] had six MW-232s with him while trying to find a [[perpendicularity]] in the [[Drominad system]], he eventually lost one of them. Additionally, [[Leonore]] flew an MW-232 before she deserted.

MW-232s -- and all starfighters -- are expensive, but recently, their price has risen more.
