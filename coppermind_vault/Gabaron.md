> — [[Sigzil]], referring to Gabaron's death

**Gabaron** is an [[Alethi]] soldier on [[Roshar]]. He is a [[lighteyes]] with a large ego and little to no concern for those serving under him. He has a tendency to blame incidents concerning his armour on his armorers and may even have them punished by sending them to the [[bridge crew]]s.

During the [[War of Reckoning]], [[Leyten]] served as an apprentice armorer in Gabaron's retinue. Gabaron's armor failed during a battle, and he took an arrow to the shoulder. Looking for someone to blame for the incident, he sent Leyten to the bridge crews. As this was his pattern of behavior, his armor ended up poorly maintained. During the fighting two bridge runs later, Gabaron's [[wikipedia:cuirass|cuirass]] snapped off due to a poor connection to its straps, and he died.
