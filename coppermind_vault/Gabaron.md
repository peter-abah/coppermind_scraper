**Gabaron** is an [[Alethi]] soldier on [[Roshar]]. He is a [[lighteyes]] with a large ego and little to no concern for those serving under him. He has a tendency to blame incidents concerning his armour on his armorers and may even have them punished by sending them to the [[bridge crew]]s.{{book ref|sa5|78}}

During the [[War of Reckoning]], [[Leyten]] served as an apprentice armorer in Gabaron's retinue.{{book ref|sa5|78}} Gabaron's armor failed during a battle, and he took an arrow to the shoulder. Looking for someone to blame for the incident, he sent Leyten to the bridge crews.{{book ref|sa1|63}} As this was his pattern of behavior, his armor ended up poorly maintained. During the fighting two bridge runs later, Gabaron's [[wikipedia:cuirass|cuirass]] snapped off due to a poor connection to its straps, and he died.{{book ref|sa5|78}}
