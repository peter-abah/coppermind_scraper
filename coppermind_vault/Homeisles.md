The **homeisles** are the homeland of the [[Eelakin]] people on [[First of the Sun]], a planet in the [[Drominad System]].

## Geography and Ecology
The homeisles are made up of a large number of islands, There are great differences between the islands, with some of them being fairly large, and others being little more then tiny and barren rocks. There are also several atolls. Most of the islands are inhabited. [[Sixth of the Dusk]] mentions that the homeisles superficially resemble the [[Pantheon]] archipelago. It takes approximately three weeks rowing to travel from the homeisles to the Pantheon. It is unknown how far the homeisles are from the mainland, but the latter is a frozen wasteland while the former experiences warm and muggy summers.

Not very much is known about the flora and fauna of the homeisles. It has a very different ecology than the Pantheon islands. Birds are known to live on the homeisles. The homeisles are also home to vibrant coral reefs. The Eelakin have also domesticated dogs.

## History
It is unclear how the homeisles came into existence. However, Eelakin myths tell of their creation. The Eelakin hero [[Cakoban]] followed a brilliant shooting star, which led him past the cave of the terrible [[Dakwara]]. Cakoban valiantly defeated that beast, and the Dakwara was forced agree to Cakoban’s request to protect his daughter and kin for a hundred years. With a respect gained for people, when it came to their land, it fulfilled Patji’s wish and created the islands. Actually, Cakoban led the Eelakin to First of the Sun, where they traveled through the perpendicularity and left the Pantheon for the homeisles. For millennia afterwards, the Eelakin lived on the isles, until they entered the industrial era and were surprised to meet the [[Ones Above]]. Not long after, the Eelakin, with outside help, figured out space travel and the homeisles became a hub of activity.
