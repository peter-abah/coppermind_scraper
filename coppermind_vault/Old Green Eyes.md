**Old Green Eyes** is a [[chasmfiend]] on [[Roshar]] during and after the [[War of Reckoning]]. It is likely that they were given their name by the [[Alethi]], as there are tavern stories about them in the warcamps. These stories say that Old Green Eyes is the largest, meanest beast on the Plains. After the war, an Alethi [[lighteyes]] announced that he was willing to pay handsomely for anyone able to bring him its head. However, the [[listener]]s also wanted to find Old Green Eyes and guide them to the [[Shattered Plains|eastern floodplains]] to live with them.{{rpg ref|slwg|160}}
