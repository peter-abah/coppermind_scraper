**Palafruit** are a type of fruit found on [[Roshar]]. They have pits, and they are eaten by biting into them. Palafruit are juicy and can stain clothes. At least some of them are small and green. A dozen palafruit is considered a large quantity to eat at once.

Pits from the fruit were occasionally thrown at bridgemen by soldiers from the [[Alethi]] army during the [[War of Reckoning]]. [[Adolin Kholin]] fed [[Gallant]] a palafruit before departing on the expedition to [[Lasting Integrity]], and he gave him another one later in the journey. [[Wit]] brought palafruit with him when he joined [[Dalinar]], [[Navani]], [[Fen]], and [[Jasnah]] in their discussion about Dalinar seeking the power of [[Honor]] prior to the [[contest of champions]]. Once [[Lift]] was discovered eavesdropping, she helped herself to the snacks and ended up eating at least a dozen palafruit.
