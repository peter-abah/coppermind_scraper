**Mourn's Vault** is a settlement in the [[Sadeas princedom]] of [[Alethkar]] on [[Roshar]]. It is on the border of Alethkar and [[Herdaz]], and is northwest of [[Hearthstone]]. Mourn's Vault is not a pleasant area of Alethkar.

[[Tarah]] moved to Mourn's Vault from Amaram's army to work as a scribe under Highprince [[Torol Sadeas]]'s house.
