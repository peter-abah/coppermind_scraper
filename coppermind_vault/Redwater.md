**Redwaters** are a type of creature native to [[Longbrow's Straits]] on [[Roshar]]. They are predators, and would pose a danger to humans that encounter them in the water.{{book ref|sa2|1}}

Captain [[Tozbek]] was worried about redwaters when [[Shallan Davar]] wanted to be lowered into the water in order to get a better look at a [[santhid]].{{book ref|sa2|1}}
