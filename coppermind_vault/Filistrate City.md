**Filistrate City** is a city presumably in the [[Emerald Sea]] on [[Lumar]]. [[Salay]]'s father arrived there just after she had searched for him there and left. Tress received the information that he was there from [[Xisis]] as one of her three boons.{{book ref|tress|53}}
