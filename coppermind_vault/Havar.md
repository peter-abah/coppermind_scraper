Brightlord **Havar** is an officer in Highprince [[Dalinar Kholin]]'s army on the [[Shattered Plains]] of [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa1|68}}

## Personality and Appearance
Havar wears a beard,{{book ref|sa1|68}} and is a good soldier who does his job well.{{book ref|sa2|37}}

## History
Havar accompanies [[Dalinar]] to a [[firemoss]] den the night [[Adolin]] is born. He buys a round of drinks and sits next to [[Bashin]].{{book ref|sa3|49}}{{expand}}

Havar served as the commander of the rearguard in the Kholin army,{{book ref|sa1|68}} and was good friends with both Dalinar and [[Adolin Kholin]].{{book ref|sa2|37}} In {{Rosharan date|1173}}, Havar fought in the [[Battle of the Tower]], where the Kholin army was betrayed by Highprince [[Torol Sadeas]].{{book ref|sa1|68}} Havar took a serious gut wound during the fighting. He survived long enough to be rescued by [[Bridge Four]], but died shortly thereafter, coughing up blood. Havar was one of around six thousand Kholin casualties in the fighting, along with [[Perethom]], [[Ilamar]].{{book ref|sa2|37}} [[Kaladin]] promoted [[Nacomb Gaval]] to temporarily replace Havar for the purpose of expeditiously evacuating the [[Tower (Roshar)|Tower]].{{book ref|sa1|68}} Havar's death, along with the others served as one of the sources of Adolin's hatred for Sadeas.{{book ref|sa2|37}}
