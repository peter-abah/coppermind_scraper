**Zoroaster** is the spiritual leader of a religion in the [[Earth (Frugal Wizard)|alternate medieval dimension]]. He preaches of the god, [[Ahura Mazda]]. Zoroaster is not a god himself, but many mistake him as being one.{{book ref|frugal|15}}

After rescuing [[Yazad (Frugal Wizard)|Yazad]] from the [[Hordamen]], [[Ealstan]] asked him why he had come to [[Weswara]]. Yazad replied that he had come to teach the people about the true god, a god above gods, Ahura Mazda. [[Sefawynn]] noted that she had heard of Zoroaster, but Yazad corrected her, saying that Zoroaster is not a god himself.{{book ref|fwh|15}}

## Trivia
* Zoroaster was a real-world Iranian spiritual leader, and is considered the founder of Zoroastrianism.{{wp ref|Zoroaster}}{{wp ref|Zoroastrianism}}
*Followers of Zoroastrianism believe in an all-knowing deity called Ahura Mazda.{{wp ref|Zoroastrianism}}{{wp ref|Ahura Mazda}}
