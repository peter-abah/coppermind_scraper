A **qido** is a type of waterskin used on the [[Dayside]] of [[Taldain]]. It has a horn-like shape. [[Sand mastery|Sand master]]s attempting the [[Mastrell's Path]] are allowed to carry only a single qido during the test. The plural of qido is qidoin.
