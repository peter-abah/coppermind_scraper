A **qido** is a type of waterskin used on the [[Dayside]] of [[Taldain]]. It has a horn-like shape. [[Sand mastery|Sand master]]s attempting the [[Mastrell's Path]] are allowed to carry only a single qido during the test.{{book ref|ws|1|review=yes}} The plural of qido is qidoin.{{book ref|ws|3|review=yes}}
