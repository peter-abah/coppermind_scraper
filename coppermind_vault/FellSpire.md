**FellSpire** is a location on [[Scadrial]], one that existed at least shortly after the [[Ascension of the Lord Ruler|Ascension]]. It is not clear if the place still exists.

King [[Wednegon]] and his soldiers are routed for the first time at FellSpire by the [[Lord Ruler]] and his [[koloss]] armies. This begins a series of defeats for Wednegon, which he blames on a lack of food, due to the [[Deepness]].
