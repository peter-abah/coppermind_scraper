The **Nirovah Valley** is located in southeastern [[Shinovar]] on [[Roshar]].

It contains the town of [[Clearmount]], along with other scattered towns and homesteads. There are prairies for grazing sheep in the region. The area also has plains with wild horses, as well as simple earthen buildings. The [[Stoneward]] monastery gives a perspective over the valley.
