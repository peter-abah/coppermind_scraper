**ShalRim** is a [[Dayside]]r fabric on [[Taldain]]. It is known for being strong as well as comfortable. [[Raagent]] preferred ShalRim over [[Darkside]] materials for his bed sheets. The material is strong enough that [[Sharezan]] was able to use Raagent's ShalRim blanket to securely tie him up.
