**Jito** is a city in [[Nagadan]] on [[Komashi]]. A contingent of the [[Dreamwatch]] was relocated from Jito to [[Kilahito]] when a stable [[nightmare]] was found in Kilahito in the days before the [[battle of Kilahito]].
