Old **Dent** is a vendor on [[Scadrial]].

Dent sells fried potatoes from a roadside cart near central [[Elendel]]. He cooks the potatoes to order, and they are served in a small or large pouch with an optional sauce. He is familiar with [[Wayne]].{{book ref|mb6|4}}

Wayne passed by Dent's cart while it was very busy and helped him serve the customers (while also sneaking a few potatoes for himself). Dent observed Wayne manipulating one of the customers into "trading" him his wallet.{{book ref|mb6|4}}
