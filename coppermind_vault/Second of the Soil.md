**Second of the Soil** is the vice president of the interior of the [[homeisles|homeisler]] government and trusted advisor to President [[Vathi]]. He often acts as Vathi's aide; fetching [[Dusk]] for the talks with the [[Ones Above]], discussing political strategy and relaying important messages to Vathi, and seeing off important guests and diplomats.

## Appearance and Personality
Soil has lanky legs and carries his [[Aviar]] on top of his head instead of his shoulder. This unusual visage is prone to mocking caricatures in the broadsheets.

Born in the city, Soil has little understanding of the jungles of the [[Pantheon]].
