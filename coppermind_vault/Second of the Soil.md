{{spoilers|iote}}
**Second of the Soil** is the vice president of the interior of the [[homeisles|homeisler]] government and trusted advisor to President [[Vathi]].{{book ref|iote|7}}{{book ref|iote|61}} He often acts as Vathi's aide; fetching [[Dusk]] for the talks with the [[Ones Above]],{{book ref|iote|7}} discussing political strategy and relaying important messages to Vathi,{{book ref|iote|61}} and seeing off important guests and diplomats.{{book ref|iote|epilogue}}

## Appearance and Personality
Soil has lanky legs and carries his [[Aviar]] on top of his head instead of his shoulder.{{book ref|iote|7}} This unusual visage is prone to mocking caricatures in the broadsheets.{{book ref|iote|61}}

Born in the city, Soil has little understanding of the jungles of the [[Pantheon]].{{book ref|iote|61}}
