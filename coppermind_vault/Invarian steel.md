> That's Invarian steel, [...] stronger, lighter. It let me shave the thickness between chambers, increase the number without making it too big.
> — [[Ranette]]

**Invarian steel** is a metal alloy on [[Scadrial]]. It is silvery in color. [[Ranette]] uses it to make [[Vindication]], her [[Allomancer]] specific handgun. It is stronger and lighter than ordinary steel. This allows her to increase the number of chambers in the cylinder without making the pistol too large.
