**Lunamor's family** are an [[Unkalaki]] family related to [[Numuhukumakiaki'aialunamor]].

## History
Lunamor left the [[Horneater Peaks]] with [[Kef'ha]] and was enslaved by [[Torol Sadeas]] before being sent to [[Bridge Four]]. After the [[Battle of the Tower]], he became a member of [[House Kholin]]'s personal guard, and then a squire to the [[Knight Radiant]] [[Kaladin Stormblessed]]. At some point he sent word back to the mountains for his family to join him on the [[Shattered Plains]]. By the time they reach the Plains, the warcamps had migrated to [[Urithiru]]. The family's caravan was attacked by [[Voidbringer]]s and they defend themselves behind a pile of furniture. During the attack one of them shot a Voidbringer with an [[Unkalaki]] arrow. They were found by [[Bridge Four]] during a patrol and reunited with Lunamor in [[Narak]] before being taken back to [[Urithiru]]. Shortly after the evacuation of the residents of [[Hearthstone]] most of the family traveled back to the Peaks, leaving [[Cord]] and [[Gift]] behind.
