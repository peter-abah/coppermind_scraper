**Kalay** is a female [[ardent]] on [[Roshar]]. During the [[War of Reckoning]], she resided in the [[House Kholin|Kholin]] warcamp on the [[Shattered Plains]]. She is highly critical of the [[Knights Radiant]]. She works with another ardent called [[Shansan]].
