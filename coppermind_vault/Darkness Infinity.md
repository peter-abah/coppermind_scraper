**Darkness Infinity** is an [[Epic]] who is killed in [[Ildithia]] during [[Prof]]'s fight with [[Tavi]]. Darkness Infinity had the power to teleport between shadows. She also has the tendency to change her Epic name fairly often, always coming up with "something better". "Darkness Infinity" is the fourth name she has come up with so far, and David claims that each of her names is progressively worse.
