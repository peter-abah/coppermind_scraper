The **First Insurance Bank** is a bank in the Second Octant of [[Elendel]] on [[Scadrial]]. It has a stone archway outside the building, which [[Waxillium Ladrian]] uses as a vantage point to catch up to Governor [[Replar Innate]]'s coach after the latter's departure from [[Lady ZoBell's party]].{{book ref|mb5|12}}
