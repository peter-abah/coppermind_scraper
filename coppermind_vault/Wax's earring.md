**Wax's earring** is a [[Hemalurgy|Hemalurgically]] charged [[Pathism|Pathian]] earring, created from the melted-down spikes of a [[Steel Inquisitor]].{{book ref|mb6|3}}{{wob ref|9983}} It was given to him by the [[kandra]] [[MeLaan]].{{book ref|mb5|13}} It is small and simple, with a head stamped with ten interlocking rings, the symbol of the Path.{{book ref|mb4|3}}

It allows [[Harmony]] to communicate with Wax, speaking through [[Ruin]] and hearing Wax's thoughts through [[Preservation]].{{book ref|mb5|7}}{{wob ref|4156}} Although the earring is a Hemalurgic spike, it has very little charge and grants practically no abilities.{{book ref|mb6|3}}{{wob ref|6210}}

## History
MeLaan personally delivered the earring to Wax many years before his return to [[Elendel]].{{book ref|mb5|13}} He wore it frequently, especially when praying or doing something important,{{book ref|mb4|15}} and often touched it while thinking things over.{{book ref|mb4|11}}{{book ref|mb4|15}} He began to wear it less after finding out it was a Hemalurgic spike{{book ref|mb5|7}} and becoming angry at Harmony for letting [[Lessie]] die.{{book ref|mb5|18}}

In order to take down [[Paalm]], Wax asked [[Ranette]] to reforge the earring into a bullet.{{book ref|mb5|17}}{{book ref|mb5|19}} He shot Paalm with it, delivering a second Hemalurgic spike into her body and allowing Harmony to start taking control of her, though she committed suicide to avoid falling under his control again.{{book ref|mb5|25}}{{book ref|mb5|26}}

After that, Harmony and the kandra attempted to deliver him a new earring, but he refused to begin wearing one again.{{book ref|mb5|epilogue}}{{book ref|mb6|4}}{{book ref|mb6|6}}{{book ref|mb6|16}}
