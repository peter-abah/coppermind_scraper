The **Jotun Line** is a popular carnival ride found in [[Kilahito]]'s carnival. It consists of a steel post extending high into the air with two-person pods that are lifted to the top before turning and coming down the other side. It is a slow moving ride with occasional pauses for the riders at the top to enjoy their view while new riders board at the bottom.{{book ref|yumi|28}} When [[Nikaro]] and [[Yumi]] ride the Jotun Line, they are ushered to the front of the long line because of Yumi's apparent service as a [[painter]].{{book ref|yumi|28}}
