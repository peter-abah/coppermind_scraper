**Haverfrex** was a city and location of one of the [[Lord Ruler]]'s main canneries. It likely supplied one of the Lord Ruler's storage caches.

It was held by [[Ashweather Cett]] during the [[siege of Luthadel]], giving him the advantage of easy supply along [[Luth-Davn Canal]] which ran between the cities.
