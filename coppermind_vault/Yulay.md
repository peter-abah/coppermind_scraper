**Yulay** is a nation located near the [[Purelake]] on [[Roshar]].

## Geography
Yulay's territory primarily consists of a narrow strip of land between the southern shore of the [[Purelake]] and several mountainous areas. It borders [[Tu Bayla]] to the east and [[Babatharnam]] to the west. The southern border with [[Azir]] is mostly defined by mountains, with the exception of one large pass, which has the towns of [[Benru]] and [[Berqq]] at the border. These two towns are under Yulay’s administration, and serve as the hub of Yulay’s trade with Azir.

Other notable locations in Yulay are [[Yian Dion]], [[Domistar]], and [[Uarr Dion]], all of which are on the shores of the Purelake. Many unnamed rivers flow through Yulay and end in the Purelake.

## History
During the [[Silver Kingdoms]] era, Yulay was part of [[Sela Tales]]. After Sela Tales collapsed Yulay became an independent nation.

At some point after the [[Recreance]] people in Yulay began talking about the [[Knights Radiant]] and hoping they would return.

[[Sigzil]] told stories about Yulay and other Selay areas to [[Bridge Four]].

Once [[Iri]] surrendered to [[Odium]]’s forces, the nation moved to conquer much of the Purelake and its environs. The [[Fused]] have stationed a few of their Regals in the area near Yulay, though they are more concerned with their advancing troops than with the territory itself.

## Culture
The people from Yulay are [[Selay]] and as such they likely have the skin ripples Selay people have. As Selay, they also have bronze skin and dark hair. It can also be assumed that they speak a mix of Selay and the local tribal languages. It’s possible they have shards as the Selay are known to have multiple sets.

The people of Yulay were once concerned Azir would try to invade them to gain access to the Purelake, but they’ve since become confident the Azish Empire has no interest in doing so. The people of Yulay prize the traits of hopefulness and patience. They’ve long believed the Knights Radiant would return.
