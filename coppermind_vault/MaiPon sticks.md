**MaiPon sticks** are an eating utensil used in [[JinDo]]. Based on the name, they likely originated in [[MaiPon]]. They come in pairs, with one stick having a sharp pointed tip and the other having a flat shoveled end.

[[Sarene]] had never used MaiPon sticks before arriving in [[Arelon]], and had trouble eating with them when [[Kiin]] made [[HaiKo]] for her first meal in Arelon. [[Kaise]] found this to be unbecoming of a princess.

Something similar to MaiPon sticks are used in both [[Torio]] and [[Nagadan]] on [[Komashi]]; when telling the story of [[Yumi]] and [[Painter]], [[Hoid]] refers to them as MaiPon sticks for the convenience of his audience.
