King **Raoshem** was the first king of [[Elantris (city)|Elantris]].

When the [[Aonic]] peoples migrated to [[Arelon]] they found Elantris empty and abandoned. Initially it was viewed with superstition, people believed it was haunted. King **Rhashm**, who renamed himself Raoshem, strove to overcome the fears of his people and settled Elantris as the center of his kingdom. Several decades later people started being taken by the [[Shaod]], becoming [[Elantrian]]s.
