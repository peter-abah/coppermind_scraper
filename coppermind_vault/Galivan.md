Lord **Galivan** is one of [[Ashweather Cett]]'s generals during the [[Siege of Luthadel|Siege]] and [[Battle of Luthadel]].

When [[Allrianne Cett]] returned to visit her father, Galivan and [[Detor]] were discussing strategy in an open-sided pavilion which contained a table with maps on it.
