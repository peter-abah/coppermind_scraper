**Hema-Dak** lives with her children in a small port on the eastern edge of [[Roshar]] in an area that used to be part of the kingdom of [[Natanatan]]. Her sister was sick with a fever when the [[Everstorm]] came through, and [[Puuli]] babysat for her so she could visit her sister and take her broth.
