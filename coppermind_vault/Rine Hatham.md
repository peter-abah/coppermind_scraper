Highlord **Rine Hatham** is a [[lighteyes]] living in [[Alethkar]] on [[Roshar]]. He is presumably related to Highprince [[Hatham]].

King [[Gavilar Kholin]] promised Rine that he would have an audience during his [[Assassination of Gavilar|feast]] to discuss a border dispute involving some misdrawn maps, but Gavilar was busy and did not make time to meet with him before it was too late.
