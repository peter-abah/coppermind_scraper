**Sourmelon** is a fruit that grows in the highlands of [[Duladel]] and [[JinDo]] on [[Sel]].

They are bright red, and able to fit in a pocket.

[[Lukel]] somehow manages to get a contact that lets him get a shipment of sourmelons a whole month before the harvesting season begins. Lukel partners with [[Roial]], who sends his half of the melons to [[Svorden]], much to the chagrin of [[Ahan]], whose shipments will arrive too late and overripe.
