**Sourmelon** is a fruit that grows in the highlands of [[Duladel]]{{glossary ref|Elantris|S}} and [[JinDo]]{{book ref|Elantris|25}} on [[Sel]].

They are bright red, and able to fit in a pocket.{{book ref|Elantris|26}}

[[Lukel]] somehow manages to get a contact that lets him get a shipment of sourmelons a whole month before the harvesting season begins.{{book ref|Elantris|26}} Lukel partners with [[Roial]], who sends his half of the melons to [[Svorden]], much to the chagrin of [[Ahan]], whose shipments will arrive too late and overripe.{{book ref|Elantris|32}}
