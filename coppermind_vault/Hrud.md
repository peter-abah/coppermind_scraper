**Hrud** is a member of [[Camon]]'s thieving crew in [[Luthadel]] on [[Scadrial]]. He is a flat-faced and quiet man.{{book ref|mb1|4}}

Hrud was with the rest of the [[Camon's crew|crew]] who told [[Vin]] the story of the Survivor of Hathsin shortly before [[Kelsier's crew]] arrived at their safe house. He distrusted [[Kelsier]], spreading rumors of his unnatural abilities. Hrud, [[Milev]], and the rest of the group left, leaving Vin behind to attend the meeting with Kelsier's crew.{{book ref|mb1|4}}
