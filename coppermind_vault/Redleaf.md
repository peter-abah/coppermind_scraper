**Redleaf** is an [[Epic]] killed by the [[Reckoners]] in Canada. He is presumably Canadian, based on his nickname and location. His powers are unknown.

[[David Charleston]] once heard a rumor that [[Jonathan Phaedrus]] (Prof) had killed Redleaf by shooting him in midair after jumping out of a moving airplane. When David mentioned this story to [[Abraham Desjardins|Abraham]], however, Abraham revealed that he was present for the operation and that the rumors were exaggerated. Prof's actions were pre-planned, and he had actually jumped from a helicopter.
