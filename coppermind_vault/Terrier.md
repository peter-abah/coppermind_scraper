Callsign **Terrier** is the flight leader of [[Riptide Flight]]. There are five other members in his flight.{{book ref|Skyward|52}} He swears by the North Star.{{book ref|Skyward|52}}

He participated in the battle to defend [[Alta]] base. On [[Spensa]]'s command, Terrier and his flight engaging with the black [[varvax|Krell]] fighter ships that defended the [[lifebuster]]. [[Arturo Mendez|Arturo]] and [[Nedd Strong|Nedd]] fought alongside Riptide flight during this fight. He ordered the [[DDF]] ships away from the Krell bomber when the lifebuster was dropped.{{book ref|sky1|52}}
