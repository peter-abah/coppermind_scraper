A **kathari** is a large pink fruit grown in [[Hraggen]] on [[Sel]].

When the [[New Elantris|New Elantrians]] got new clothing that was dyed strange colors, [[Galladon]] did not want [[Raoden]] to make fun of him looking like a kathari fruit.
