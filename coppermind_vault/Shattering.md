The **Shattering of [[Adonalsium]]** (or simply the **Shattering**) was a pivotal moment in the history of the [[cosmere]]. Adonalsium, an entity of immense power considered to be the creator of the cosmere,{{wob ref|16915}} was killed using the [[Dawnshard]]s.{{msh ref|3|2}}{{book ref|sa3.5|19}}{{book ref|tsm|21}} It was shattered into what became known as [[Shard]]s of Adonalsium, sixteen godlike powers that have been the dominant influence in the cosmere since they were taken up by [[Yolen|Yolish]] co-conspirators in the plot.{{book ref|sa5|21}} The Shattering took place over ten thousand years before the [[True Desolation]].{{book ref|sa4|i|1}}{{book ref|tsm|11}}{{book ref|sa5|100}}

## Prior to the Shattering
It is not clear how different the cosmere was prior to the Shattering, but the Shattering did have an effect on the magic.{{wob ref|8357}} An example is the [[highstorm]]s,{{wob ref|8624}} natural phenomena that are central to the life cycle of many of the native inhabitants of Roshar; the highstorms were later infused with the power of Honor.

The people who killed Adonalsium on Yolen{{wob ref|11602}} all had different motivations, including a desire for power and a belief in the necessity of the action.{{msh ref|3|2}} [[Hoid]] was there at the Shattering and was offered a Shard, but he refused.{{book ref|sa4|99}}{{wob ref|3337}} Hoid participated in the plan to kill Adonalsium,{{book ref|tress|39}} although at one point [[Tanavast]] expressed that only he and fifteen other participants did "something terrible" on Yolen.{{book ref|sa5|122}} [[Frost]] was also present;{{book ref|iote|47}} his role (if any) is unclear, but he later seems to express regret about causing destruction in a letter to Hoid.{{article ref|Letters#The_Second_Letter|Frost's letter to Hoid}}

According to Tanavast, Adonalsium did not fight back, and let itself be Shattered. He assumed it was done in order to prevent the destruction that could come to be if it did fight back.{{book ref|sa5|113}}

Almost all of the Investiture in the cosmere was divided between and became associated with a Shard,{{wob ref|8605}} with some exceptions like the [[Aether]]s and the Dawnshards.{{book ref|mb7|42}}{{book ref|sa3.5|19}}{{wob ref|15933}} All of the sixteen Shards were created at once, and they all started with the same amount of [[Investiture]].{{book ref|mb7|20}}{{wob ref|1292}}{{wob ref|5313}} 

It was not random which Vessel got which Shard;{{wob ref|5490}} some chose Shards that they were particularly Connected with, while others chose Shards that they believed they could control well.{{wob ref|15953}} It is possible for Adonalsium to have Shattered into different Shards than the Shards currently in existence.{{wob ref|6634}}

Following the Shattering, the Vessels spread across the cosmere, Investing into various planets, with Ruin and Preservation themselves creating the planet of [[Scadrial]].{{au ref|scadrian}}

Various Shards fought amongst themselves for various reasons, leading to the death of Vessels and Splintering of the Shards. In particular, this applies to Devotion, Dominion, and Ambition.{{book ref|mb7|20}}{{au ref|selish}}{{au ref|Threnodite}} All of these Splinterings had strong and lasting effects upon the planets they were Invested within, and are also largely the result of Odium having gone to kill every Shard he could.{{book ref|mb7|20}}{{au ref|selish}}{{epigraph ref|sa4|27}}{{wob ref|8881}}{{wob ref|1662}} Ruin and Preservation were also later fused into one Vessel after the death of their previous holders, creating the hybrid Shard, [[Harmony]].{{book ref|mb3|82}} Honor and Odium were also later fused into one Vessel creating the hybrid Shard, [[Retribution]].

### Reasoning
The reasoning for the Shattering differs between each individual involved. Some wanted Adonalsium's power, while others felt it was necessary.{{rpg ref|slwg|81}}

* **Koravellium Avast** participated in the Shattering due to the belief that one god could not supply the needs of everyone, thus necessitating the existence of a plethora of deities to truly meet everyone's needs.{{book ref|sa5|i|4}}
* **Tanavast** believed that the Shattering was for Adonalsium's own good.{{book ref|sa5|100}}
