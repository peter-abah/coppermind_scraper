{{spoilers|iote}}
**Tumak** is one of several former monarchs in [[Xisisrefliel|Xisis']] employ in his palace in [[Silverlight]]. Once the emperor of his nation, Tumak likely came to Silverlight in the hopes of being granted a boon by the [[Dragon_(cosmere)|dragon]] such as an extended lifespan. He is serving as Xisis' servant and cup bearer until such time as Xisis feels he is obedient enough to be set to more interesting tasks.{{book ref|iote|32}}

Tumak is a tall man and looks like he may be [[Rosharan]], though his clothing is unfamiliar potentially indicating he is from one of its less prominent nations.{{book ref|iote|32}}
