**Sakin** is a resident of a small port on the eastern edge of [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa3|i|1}}

Sakin's village is located within the former kingdom of [[Natanatan]], and Sakin is presumably [[Natan]] like the other townsfolk, including [[Puuli]].{{book ref|sa3|i|1}}

She moved to a fine stone hut behind the northern cliffs of the village after marrying her third husband, and she had been looking forward to growing a garden there. When the [[Everstorm]] came, her husband was unfortunately lost at sea; she had lost her first two husbands the same way. Puuli briefly stopped to comfort her in her grief while he was gathering scrap wood after the storm.{{book ref|sa3|i|1}}
