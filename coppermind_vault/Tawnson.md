**Tawnson** is a member of staff in [[Renoux Manor]].{{book ref|mb1|17}}

He is a servant who once notified [[Renoux]] that members of [[Kelsier's crew]] had arrived.{{book ref|mb1|17}} To signal this discreetly, Tawnson simply said that a carriage arrived from [[Luthadel]].{{book ref|mb1|17}}
