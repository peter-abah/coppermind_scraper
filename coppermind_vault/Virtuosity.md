**Virtuosity** is a [[Shard]] of [[Adonalsium]] who [[Splinter#Formation|Splintered]] herself. The Vessel was an unknown female,{{book ref|yumi|1}} she is possibly one of the scholars Hoid questioned about talent.{{book ref|wok|epilogue}}

## Intent
Virtuosity's [[Intent]] concerns artistic intent, talent, and appreciation.{{wob ref|15500}}

It appears to have some association with mathematics, as its [[Investiture]] and [[Splinter]]s are attracted by numbers in the [[Artist's sequence of progress]].{{book ref|yumi|4}}

## Associated Magics
### Hijo
{{for|Hijo}}

[[Hijo]] are spirits found on [[Komashi]].{{book ref|yumi|17}} They naturally reside underground, and are attracted by thoughts, emotions, and artistic expressions.{{book ref|yumi|17}} They are also attracted to expressions of the [[Artist's sequence of progress]], especially as it relates to artistic expression.{{book ref|yumi|4}}

Hijo are a major part of the lives of people all over Komashi, and are the key to their survival on the planet.{{book ref|yumi|17}} They can be bound by a [[yoki-hijo]] into useful tools and devices,{{book ref|yumi|4}} or be utilized to produce and supply [[hion]].{{book ref|yumi|part=anotherepilogue}} The hijo are related to Komashi's strange geothermal activity, apparently being directly responsible for the intense heating of the ground in the region of [[Torio]].{{book ref|yumi|4}}

## History
Like all Shards, Virtuosity was created at the [[Shattering]] of [[Adonalsium]] and picked up by an unknown Vessel.{{msh ref|3|2}}{{wob ref|15504}} In the millennia that followed, Virtuosity primarily spent her time exploring various artistic expressions throughout the [[cosmere]], and consequently never settled in one location for very long.{{wob ref|15530}}

At some point, Virtuosity Splintered herself for unknown reasons in the [[UTol system]], in the vicinity of [[Komashi]].{{book ref|yumi|1}} [[Design]] asserts that the entire region is filled with Splinters of Virtuosity in the wake of this.{{book ref|yumi|17}} [[Hoid]] believes that her Splintering may have contributed to the odd way [[UTol]] is visible through the [[shroud]] on Komashi.{{book ref|yumi|1}} Either her Splintering remained unknown to the cosmere’s general public, or she was reconstructed after the disappearance of Komashi’s shroud and went into hiding.{{ref|text=[[Ed]], an Arcanist, states that it is unknown where Virtuosity is residing, meaning that she is either actually moving around, or they don’t know about her Splintering.}}{{book ref|iote|42}}

## Trivia

* The word "Virtuosity" is often mistaken to be related to the notion of "[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Virtue virtue]". The term actually refers to the characteristics of a [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Virtuoso virtuoso]: someone having outstanding talents or technical abilities, especially in the arts.
* Brandon considered naming the Shard "Artistry", but felt the name "Virtuosity" fit with other Shard names, like [[Odium]], better.{{wob ref|15500}}
* A future book will explore Virtuosity's story.{{wob ref|15555}}
* The sacred number of Virtuosity is most likely 13.{{wob ref|15498}}
* The Push-Pull theme can be seen in all magic systems, but is most pronounced with Virtuosity's Investiture.{{wob ref|16165}}
