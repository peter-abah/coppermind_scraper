**Arik** is a member of [[Bridge Four]] on [[Roshar]].

Arik survived the bridge runs until after [[Kaladin]] decided that he would try to save the men of Bridge Four. He was killed in the same bridge run as [[Maps]], and Kaladin was unable to save either of them.
