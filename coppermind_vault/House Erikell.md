**House Erikell** is one of the ten great houses of [[Luthadel]] on [[Scadrial]] during the [[Final Empire]].{{book ref|mb1|9}} It may be related in some way to [[House Erikeller]], but this is not confirmed.

House Erikell was aligned with [[House Venture]] and sold them armaments such as swords and dueling canes; however, they were selling the same weapons at a slight discount to [[House Tekiel]]. [[Kelsier]] learned of this situation and related an embellished version to [[Straff Venture]], hoping to drive a wedge between Erikell and Venture.{{book ref|mb1|19}}

## Post-Catacendre
House Erikell survived the [[Final Ascension]] and some members of the house are mentioned as living in the [[Elendel Basin]]. Erikell remained a powerful house, as it was connected to several industries (including armaments and insurance){{book ref|mb6|9}}{{book ref|mb6|2}} and high-ranking political officials. They were aligned with [[House Hasting]] and typically disagreed with [[Waxillium Ladrian]]'s positions on policy, particularly regarding the [[Elendel Supremacy Bill]]. They were involved in a bribery scandal with [[Melstrom]], a senator representing Elendel dockworkers.{{book ref|mb7|2}}

When the [[Nightstreet Gang]] robbed a train en route to [[New Seran]], they stole cash intended to cover Erikell payroll.{{book ref|mb6|9}} 

===Known Members===
*[[Chip Erikell]] was a smuggler in the Third Octant of [[Elendel]]; he was killed by [[Bleeder]] at [[Winsting Innate's auction]].{{book ref|mb5|4}}
*[[Lord Erikell]], the head of the house, was present at [[Lady ZoBell's party]] and spoke to Governor [[Replar Innate]].{{book ref|mb5|9}}
*After a water tower fell onto the church where [[Wax]] and [[Steris]] were holding their wedding, an unnamed Erikell insurance assessor argued with a priest about payment of their policy.{{book ref|mb6|2}}
