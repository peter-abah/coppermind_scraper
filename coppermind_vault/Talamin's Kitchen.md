**Talamin's Kitchen** is an eatery in [[Urithiru]]. Talamin’s Kitchen is popular with Urithiru’s criminals. [[Serli]] frequents the place, and she reserved a private booth for herself and [[Fane]] to meet after their theft.{{rpg ref|ss|16}}
