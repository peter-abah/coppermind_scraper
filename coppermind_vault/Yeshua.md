> Yeshua. He was crucified by the Romans?
> — [[John West]] asks [[Yazad (Frugal Wizard)|Yazad]]

**Yeshua** is the figurehead of a religion in the [[Earth (Frugal Wizard)|alternate medieval dimension]]. Followers of the religion are known as "Yeshuans", and are likely to be the dimension's version of Christians. Many centuries ago, the Romans in the medieval dimension attempted to crucify Yeshua, but [[Ahura Mazda]] rescued him. The Yeshuans then formed a coalition with the followers of Ahura Mazda, and fought together for a short time. The two religions are like cousins, and many people mix them up.

After rescuing [[Yazad (Frugal Wizard)|Yazad]], and learning that he was a preacher for Ahura Mazda, [[John West]] asked him what he knew about Christianity. Yazad assumed that John was referring to Yeshuans, and when John asked if Yeshua had been crucified, Yazad gave him a brief overview of the religion's history.

==Trivia==
*The name "Jesus" comes from a Greek translation of the Hebrew name, "Yeshua".
