**Denka** is a nation on the Dayside of [[Taldain]].

Denka is located on the northeast edge of the Dayside continent. It is bordered by [[deep sand]] to the west, [[Seevis]] to the south, and the northern [[Border Ocean]] to the north.{{map ref|Dayside}}
