**Compassion's grandsons** are Threnodites living in [[Beacon]] at the end of the [[Cinder King]]'s rule. Their grandmother, [[Compassion]], is the eldest member of the [[Greater Good]], Beacon's ruling council. They are present when [[Zellion]] gains entry to the [[Scadrian]] research facility and bring a chair for Compassion to sit on.{{book ref|tsm|43}}
