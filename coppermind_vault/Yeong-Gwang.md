The ***Yeong-Gwang*** was a ship in the old fleet which carried humanity to [[Detritus]].

The residents and descendants are known as **Yeongian**, and are mostly ethnically Chinese or Korean.

After landing, they formed a clan and used their hydroponics knowledge to become underground farmers in a cavern close to Igneous. The food grown by this clan is of high value and is only available to those who have multiple achievement or industry merits. [[Bim]] is from that clan.
