**Unity Day** is a [[Herdaz|Herdazian]] holiday on [[Roshar]]. Every year, during the holiday, the king invites commoner representatives from each of the 203 Herdazian ranches to attend their designated chimney in the royal palace, releasing synchronized trails of smoke to represent the unity of the Herdazian people.
