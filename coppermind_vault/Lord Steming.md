**Lord Steming** is a noble in [[Elendel]] on [[Scadrial]].

He attended [[Wax]] and [[Steris]]'s interrupted wedding, and fell and hurt his head trying to escape when the water tower tipped over. According to Wax, his minor injury was the worst that anyone sustained despite the chaos.{{book ref|mb6|2}}
