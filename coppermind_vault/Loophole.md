**Loophole**  is an [[Epic]] that lives in [[Ildithia]]. She is lean and has short hair.{{book ref|calamity|27}} She throws lavish parties and loves fancy cupcakes. Three pastry chefs were executed by Loophole for producing subpar cupcakes.{{book ref|calamity|26}}

## Attributes and Abilities
===Epic Abilities===
;Size Manipulation: She has the ability to change the size of things that she touches, including herself. She can do it very quickly, even being able to shrink bullets as they hit her, making them virtually harmless. Objects return to their normal size after a few minutes if she leaves them alone. [[David Charleston]] did not consider her a [[High Epic]], but reconsiders this when he sees the power of this ability in action.{{book ref|calamity|43}} 
;Augmented Speed: She has slightly augmented speed.{{book ref|calamity|43}}

===Weaknesses===
Loophole has a fear of sneezing. When anyone sneezes in her presence, anyone or any objects altered in size return to normal size.{{book ref|calamity|43}}

## History
Nothing is known of her life before she became an Epic. She was known as being one of the most powerful Epics in Ildithia.{{book ref|calamity|18}} Loophole spent her time arranging parties and being a mercenary, offering her services to any powerful Epics that would pay her.{{book ref|calamity|43}}

Before [[Limelight]] came to Ildithia, Loophole was working for [[Larcener]], who allowed her to throw parties every week in [[Sharp Tower]]. Prof allowed her to continue after he became the dominant Epic of the city.{{book ref|calamity|24}} In the final battle between the Reckoners and Prof, she aided Prof by grabbing a [[motivator]] from [[Cody]]. She fought [[David Charleston|David]] during the battle and fell into a narrow chasm while small; she then accidentally expanded due to her Epic weakness, crushing herself to death.{{book ref|calamity|43}} After her death, David briefly saw an alternate reality version of Loophole fighting [[Firefight (Epic)|Firefight]].{{book ref|calamity|44}}
