> Foreigners were so stupid. Of course [[Nu Ralik]] was their god, but you always pretended that he wasn't. Vun Makak &mdash; his younger, spiteful brother &mdash; had to be tricked into thinking you worshiped him, otherwise he’d get jealous. It was only safe to speak of these things in a holy grotto.
> — [[Ishikk]] on the [[Purelaker]] religion

**Vun Makak** is a deity in the religion of the people of the [[Purelake]] on [[Roshar]].

Vun Makak is the younger brother of the true god of the Purelakers, [[Nu Ralik]]. Vun Makak is spiteful and jealous, and the Purelakers believe that they must trick him into thinking that they worship him instead of his brother. Therefore, they do not speak Nu Ralik's name outside of their [[holy grotto]]s, always swearing by Vun Makak in their day-to-day interactions. 

> I swear it by Vun Makak, may he watch over me and curse me as he pleases.
> — Ishikk

Vun Makak is usually thought to blight or curse his "followers", and he is also associated with lying. One man invokes his name after he is paralyzed by [[Szeth]].
