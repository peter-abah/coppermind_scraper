**Vun Makak** is a deity in the religion of the people of the [[Purelake]] on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa1|i|1}}

Vun Makak is the younger brother of the true god of the Purelakers, [[Nu Ralik]].{{book ref|sa1|i|1}} Vun Makak is spiteful and jealous, and the Purelakers believe that they must trick him into thinking that they worship him instead of his brother. Therefore, they do not speak Nu Ralik's name outside of their [[holy grotto]]s, always swearing by Vun Makak in their day-to-day interactions.{{book ref|sa1|i|1}} 
{{quote
|I swear it by Vun Makak, may he watch over me and curse me as he pleases.
|Ishikk{{book ref|sa1|i|1}}
}}
Vun Makak is usually thought to blight or curse his "followers", and he is also associated with lying.{{book ref|sa1|i|1}} One man invokes his name after he is paralyzed by [[Szeth]].{{book ref|sa3|92}}
