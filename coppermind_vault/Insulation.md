**Insulation** is an [[Epic]]. They were the second Epic to control [[Conflux]] after [[Bastion (Reckoners)|Bastion]], eventually giving him away to some unknown party before [[Steelheart (Epic)|Steelheart]] gained control of him.

Conflux always thought Insulation was a stupid name for an Epic.
