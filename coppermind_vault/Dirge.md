**Dirge** is a resident of [[Beacon]] on [[Canticle]].{{book ref|tsm|23}} He is a member of a team of five engineers led by [[Solemnity Divine]]. [[Nomad]] considers the engineering team to be "overachievers" that are quite knowledgeable and skilled.{{book ref|tsm|26}}{{book ref|tsm|34}}{{book ref|tsm|30}} However, Canticle's brutal environment and lack of [[cosmere]] awareness does not afford them the opportunity to study certain concepts that would be considered elementary on some other planets.{{book ref|tsm|23}}

Dirge participated in testing the prototypes of Nomad's steam-powered engine and helped refine the design.{{book ref|tsm|23}}
