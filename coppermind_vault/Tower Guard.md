The **Tower Guard** is a security force in [[Urithiru]] on [[Roshar]].

The Tower Guard was presumably created by the [[coalition of monarchs]] at some point after the [[Battle of Narak]]. When Urithiru was invaded by [[singer]]s, Queen [[Navani]] asked after Commander [[Lyon]] of the Tower Guard when she was strategizing about how to defend the tower. She was informed that Lyon was one of the Radiants that had fallen unconscious due to [[Raboniel]]'s partial corruption of the tower's defenses.
