A **Kelzi** is a wealthy landowner on the [[Dayside]] of [[Taldain]].{{book ref|ws|7|review=yes}}

The Kelzin form the upper class in [[Lossand]], and were partially responsible for encouraging the [[Taishin]] to disband the [[Diem]].{{book ref|ws|7|review=yes}}

[[Trell (Taldain)|Trell]]'s boss is a Kelzi named [[Karmat]].{{book ref|ws|12|review=yes}}
