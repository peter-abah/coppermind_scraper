**Doc Slush** is a Reckoner doctor who lives in [[wikipedia:Lafayette, Louisiana|Layfayette, Louisiana]] as part of the Louisiana cell. He has a severe limp, which prevents him from participating in most combat missions. When [[Paige]] falls into a coma after the fall of [[Lux (city)|Lux]], [[Jax (Reckoners)|Jax]] takes Paige to see him. Doc Slush tries everything he can think of, but is unable to get her to wake up.
