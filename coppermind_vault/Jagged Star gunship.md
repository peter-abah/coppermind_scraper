The ***Jagged Star gunship*** is a type of gunship in the **Jada** class of [[Malwish]] military spacecraft. The Jagged Star serves as a long range cruiser, meant for both firing at other capital ships and supporting military operations on the ground. The front of the gunship is outfitted with a large cannon, which glows with a red and harsh light before firing. This cannon isn't its only weapon however. As it’s also meant to support military operations on the ground, it has the ability to serve as a full base of operations; it carries pods to serve as living quarters or research stations and has an amazingly powerful engine capable of supplying enough power to use a large weapon like the [[Intensifier]]. The gunship's pods are also useful because due to them, it won't have to take its scientists and engineers with it into battle. 

As a capital ship, it dwarfs many other ships, including starfighters and even large cargo ships like the [[Dynamic]]. The Jagged Star gunship has multiple bridges, each capable of flying the ship, and a layer of [[silver]] in its hull, a standard precaution. The ship does take a long time to reach its top speed. The [[Malwish Planetary Guard]] had one stationed in the [[Drominad system]] as part of a mission to gain the use of a new [[perpendicularity]] there.
