**Mel** is a young barmaid at [[Jez's Duty]] in the [[Ten Rings]] section of [[Urithiru]] on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa4|12}}

On the day of [[Jor]] and [[Kryst]]'s wedding party, Mel served [[Adolin]], [[Shallan]], and [[Kaladin]] drinks. After refilling Veil's glass, she took Kaladin's order; perplexed that he would only order an [[Roshar#Wines|orange]], she offered him some yellow. Veil chased her away, but when she returned with Kaladin's order, she offered him yellow again. This time Adolin spoke up to decline the offer, and Mel beamed at him, ecstatic to have a [[highprince]] speak to her.{{book ref|sa4|12}}
