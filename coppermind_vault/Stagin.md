**Stagin** is a man from the [[Roughs]] that owns a still and brews whiskey.

Whiskey from Stagin's still was a favorite of [[Waxillium Ladrian]] when he was a lawman in the Roughs. Wax used it in his vials of [[Allomantic]] [[steel]] shavings in lieu of water or some other liquid. When Wax returned to [[Elendel]] to take his place as leader of [[House Ladrian]] after his [[Edwarn Ladrian|uncle's]] "death", he bemoaned the fact that his supply of Stagin's whiskey would soon run out.
