**Haka'alaku** is a city in [[Shadesmar]] located around where [[Cultivation's Perpendicularity]] is prior to her departure on the [[Night of Sorrows]].

The city is built on seven islands that are connected by wooden platforms. It seems to have few long-term structures, instead consisting mostly of tents and shanties.

It is ruled by [[peakspren]], but is welcoming to outsiders; anyone who lives in the city for long enough is granted citizenship and the city's senate includes several humans. The city is occupied by the [[Fused]] sometime after the summoning of the [[Everstorm]], but they allow the local government to continue running the city.

Several months after the Night of Sorrows, [[Shallan Davar]] traveled to Haka'alaku to verify that Cultivation's Perpendicularity had disappeared and confront the [[Ghostblood]] [[Felt]].
