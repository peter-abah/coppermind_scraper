**Macil** is a [[chromium]] [[Misting]] (aka a [[Leecher]]) and one of the women captured by the [[Set]] on [[Scadrial]].

## History
Macil was kidnapped by the [[Vanishers]] and delivered to the [[Set]] to be a part of the [[Community]]. The Set told her that she had been chosen by random lottery to be saved from a catastrophic [[ashmount]] eruption.

After [[Armal]] Rioted [[Gave Entrone]]'s shame. Macil Leeched his [[Allomantic]] reserves so that Armal may tie him up. Marasi and the Allomancers of [[Wayfarer]] drained the [[perpendicularity]] just before [[Autonomy]]'s forces could enter Scadrial through it. After resting, Marasi led Macil and the other Allomancers out of the caverns and into [[Bilming]].
