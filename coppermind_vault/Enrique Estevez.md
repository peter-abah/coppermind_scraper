**Enrique Estevez** is investigated for the murder of the mayor's nephew in [[Snapshot]]. [[Chaz]] and [[Anthony Davis|Davis]] wait for him to leave the murder scene in a liquor store across the street and then follow him to find the weapon that he dumps in a storm drain. Davis texts Maria the location of the weapon and gets Enrique convicted IRL.{{book ref|Snapshot|1}}

## Appearance
Enrique is a thin, tall man that looks more intimidating in real life than his mugshot.{{book ref|Snapshot|1}}
