The **Expanse of the Densities** is the [[Roshar]]an term for  the [[Sel]]ish subastral of the [[Cognitive Realm]].{{wob ref|8595}}{{wob ref|10301}}{{rpg ref|slwg|83}} After [[Splinter]]ing [[Devotion]] and [[Dominion]], [[Odium]] pushed their [[Investiture]] into the Cognitive Realm.{{au ref|sel}} Due to the contained nature of the space, that Investiture is in an incredibly dangerous and highly pressurized state, similar to plasma.{{wob ref|8241}} This Investiture fills the region as a deadly storm known as the [[Dor]] and is the reason Rosharans call the region the Expanse of the Densities.{{wob ref|10301}} As a result of this high concentration of Investiture outside of the [[Spiritual Realm]], there may be noticeable [[wikipedia: time dilation|time dilation]] in the region.{{wob ref|14822}}

The Cognitive Realm on Sel extremely dangerous as anyone who comes there unprepared would be ripped apart by the power of the Shards.{{wob ref|7212}} It is possible to pass through the Dor and survive, though by what means it is unclear.{{book ref|sa3|97}}{{book ref|sa1|i|1}}{{book ref|mb7|39}}{{book ref|mb7|42}} There are people who guide others safely through the subastral.{{wob ref|16891}} It strains [[Hoid]]'s resources to safely travel there, but he has done it on occasion.{{wob ref|9500}}{{book ref|elantris|part=postscript}}

It can be accessed from [[Devotion's Perpendicularity]] near [[Elantris (city)|Elantris]],{{book ref|elantris|25}}{{wob ref|8649}}{{book ref|elantris|part=postscript}} the other [[Perpendicularity]] on Sel,{{wob ref|13093}} or by traveling there from another region of the Cognitive Realm;{{wob ref|1392}} it is adjacent to the [[Nexus of Truth]] on the Rosharan map of [[Shadesmar]].{{map ref|Shadesmar}}
