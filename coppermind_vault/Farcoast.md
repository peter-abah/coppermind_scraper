**Farcoast** is a city in [[Alethkar]] in [[Vamah princedom]].{{map ref|Alethkar}} It is near the border to [[Jah Keved]].

## History
After the [[Parshendi]] assassination of King [[Gavilar Kholin]], Highprince [[Vamah]] called on Farcoast to join him in fighting the [[War of Reckoning]] on the [[Shattered Plains]].{{book ref|sa2|24}} As many as one in every two men from Farcoast that were of fighting age joined the cause. Spirits were high for the war, and those who did not enlist often had their bravery questioned by their fellow citizens. [[Red (Roshar)|Red]], a man from Farcoast, enlisted with five of his friends, although all of them but Red were killed in the fighting.
