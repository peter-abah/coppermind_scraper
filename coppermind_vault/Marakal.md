Brightlord and Brightness **Marakal** are a [[lighteyes|lighteyed]] couple on the [[Shattered Plains]] of [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa1|22}}

During one of King [[Elhokar]]'s feasts, they were among the members of the Alethi nobility that [[Wit]] mocked as they entered. Wit joked that Brightness Marakal's hairstyle was embarrassing and that her husband made him sick.{{book ref|sa1|22}}
