**Vedlew** is a [[Terrisman]] elder on [[Scadrial]].{{book ref|mb3|22}}

Vedlew is the senior elder of the Terris refugees staying at the [[Pits of Hathsin]] after the [[Collapse]] of the [[Final Empire]].{{book ref|mb3|22}} [[Sazed]] questions him and the other Terris leaders about the [[Mist|daymists]]. They tell him that the [[Snapping|mistsickness]] does not affect the Terris people, and that they think it is a myth.

Vedlew wonders why Sazed no longer openly wears his metalminds, and he and the others wish for Sazed to stay with the Terrismen and lead them.{{book ref|mb3|22}}
