> It seems odd, to me, that you should work so hard during the [[Lord Ruler]]'s time, always wearing your [[metalmind]]s in secret, despite the danger. Yet, now that you are free to do as you wish, you carry them in your pack
> — Vedlew to [[Sazed]]

**Vedlew** is a [[Terrisman]] elder on [[Scadrial]].

Vedlew is the senior elder of the Terris refugees staying at the [[Pits of Hathsin]] after the [[Collapse]] of the [[Final Empire]]. [[Sazed]] questions him and the other Terris leaders about the [[Mist|daymists]]. They tell him that the [[Snapping|mistsickness]] does not affect the Terris people, and that they think it is a myth.

Vedlew wonders why Sazed no longer openly wears his metalminds, and he and the others wish for Sazed to stay with the Terrismen and lead them.
