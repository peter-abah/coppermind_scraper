**Mitchell and Young** is a law firm based in New York City. Notably, [[Lin Yang-Tanasin]] works here and represents [[Malmahan|Mr. Caligo]] before they disappear to [[Mirandus]]. The partners of the firm push Lin for a plea deal in Mr. Caligo's case, not wanting a fight with the governor due to the public reception around the case.
