Commander **Lyon** is the leader of the [[Tower Guard]] in [[Urithiru]] on [[Roshar]]. He is a [[Knight Radiant]] of an unknown order.{{book ref|sa4|40}}

He formed a [[Nahel bond]] with an unknown [[spren]] a month before the [[singer]] invasion of [[Urithiru]]. Like the other Knights Radiant, he fell unconscious when [[Raboniel]] partially corrupted the tower's defenses.{{book ref|sa4|40}}

## Trivia
* Lyon was named after [[Howard Lyon]], who has created multiple pieces of art for [[Dragonsteel Entertainment]], including works for *[[The Stormlight Archive]]* and *[[Tress of the Emerald Sea]]*.
