House **Yomen** is a major socio-political entity on [[Scadrial]] after the [[Final Ascension]]. They are counted as a powerful house, with the majority following religion of [[Sliverism]].{{book ref|aol|4}} The Yomens are known to follow [[Sliverism]].{{book ref|mb4|2}} This likely stems from Aradan Yomen's status as an [[Obligator]] during the [[Final Empire]], and his worship of [[The Lord Ruler]] and his [[Steel Inquisitors]].{{book ref|mb3|32}}{{book ref|mb3|60}} Notable members include [[Aradan Yomen]], [[Joshin Yomen]], and [[Mi'chelle Yomen]]. 

## Property
The house owns a large [[Yomen mansion|mansion]] in [[Elendel]] city, which was the location of a notable Yomen [[Yomen-Ostlin wedding dinner|wedding]].{{book ref|mb4|2}} They also have several smaller property investments in [[Elendel]]. Joshin Yomen in particular bought run-down buildings and renovated them, causing crime to drop substantially in the area.{{book ref|mb4|9}}

## History
### After the Catacendre
House Yomen was established by Aradan Yomen, or his descendants, shortly after the Catacendre. The House quickly grew in power, becoming a great noble House. It also got a seat on the Elendel Senate as soon as the latter was established.{{book ref|mb7|2}} 

==== Yomen-Ostlin wedding ====
During the year {{date|341|PC}} Lord Joshin Yomen married Lady Mi'chelle Ostlin. The [[Yomen-Ostlin wedding dinner|wedding]], a large event attended by most of Elendel’s upper crust, was hosted at Yomen manor. During the first course the wedding was robbed by [[Miles Dagouter]] and the [[Vanishers]], who kidnapped [[Steris Harms]] and temporarily Marasi, killed Lord [[Peterus]], and stole plenty of jewelry from everyone, including the members of House Yomen themselves, with the most noteworthy possession stolen being the bridal necklace.{{book ref|mb4|4}}{{book ref|mb4|5}}

## Politics
As a powerful Noble House, the Yomens have a seat on the Elendel Senate. House Yomen, through senator Mi'chelle Yomen, was the first house to vote against the [[Elendel Supremacy Bill]].{{book ref|mb7|2}} Lady Yomen is also known to be a good friend to [[House Ladrian]].{{book ref|mb7|33}} This is likely a direct effect of Wax and his allies' heroic actions at the [[Yomen-Ostlin wedding dinner|Yomen-Ostlin wedding]], as well as his involvement with the investigation and capture of the Vanishers.{{book ref|mb4|5}}
