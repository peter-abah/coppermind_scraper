Brightlord **Habatab** is a [[lighteyes]] on the [[Shattered Plains]] on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa1|22}}

In 1173, Habatab attended one of the feasts held by King [[Elhokar Kholin]].{{book ref|sa1|22}} There he encountered [[Wit]], who mocked Habatab's decision to wear a red shirt to the feast while sunburned.
