> Ah, Brightlord Habatab! How thoughtful of you to wear a red shirt with a sunburn like that!
> — [[Wit]] to Brightlord Habatab

Brightlord **Habatab** is a [[lighteyes]] on the [[Shattered Plains]] on [[Roshar]].

In 1173, Habatab attended one of the feasts held by King [[Elhokar Kholin]]. There he encountered [[Wit]], who mocked Habatab's decision to wear a red shirt to the feast while sunburned.
