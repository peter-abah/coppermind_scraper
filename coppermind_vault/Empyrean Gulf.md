The **Empyrean Gulf** is a body of water in the [[Sea of Souls]], part of the [[Shadesmar|Rosharan subastral]] of the [[Cognitive Realm]]. It is surrounded by peninsulas with a variety of settlements and locations: one that contains [[Perpetual Sobriety]] and [[Ageless Beauty]], one with [[Justice Untarnished]] and the [[Oathbound Spires]], and one with the lighthouse [[Abiding Light]]. 

In the Physical Realm, the Empyrean Gulf corresponds to the nation of [[Emul]].
