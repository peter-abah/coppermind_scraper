The **Waelish** are a people on the [[Earth (Frugal Wizard)|alternate medieval Earth]]. They are enemies of the [[Weswarans]]. They are ruled by a king, the [[Black Bear]], who has ruled unnaturally long. His kingdom is a dark forest.

The war between the Waelish and the Weswarans led to the [[battle of Badon]], where the monstrous wolf [[Fenris]] killed the goddess [[Friag]].

The Waelish are this alternate dimension's version of the Britons. [[John West]] recognizes the word as related to "Welsh".
