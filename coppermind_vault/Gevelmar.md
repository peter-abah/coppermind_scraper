Brightlord **Gevelmar** is a [[lighteyes|lighteyed]] [[Veden]] lord on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa2|39}}

In {{Rosharan date|1169}}, Gevelmar arranged for his youngest daughter [[Malise Gevelmar|Malise]] to marry the much older [[Lin Davar]]. He sent Malise to the Davar estate, along with the appropriate writs of marriage.{{book ref|sa2|39}} Gevelmar was a rural lord of little consequence,{{wob ref|1161}} likely of the fifth or sixth [[dahn]]. The marriage was presumably political in nature since Lin needed a new wife and House Davar still had some prestige at the time.{{book ref|sa2|45}}

[[Tavinar]] knew of Gevelmar.{{book ref|sa2|39}}
