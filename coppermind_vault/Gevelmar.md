Brightlord **Gevelmar** is a [[lighteyes|lighteyed]] [[Veden]] lord on [[Roshar]].

In , Gevelmar arranged for his youngest daughter [[Malise Gevelmar|Malise]] to marry the much older [[Lin Davar]]. He sent Malise to the Davar estate, along with the appropriate writs of marriage. Gevelmar was a rural lord of little consequence, likely of the fifth or sixth [[dahn]]. The marriage was presumably political in nature since Lin needed a new wife and House Davar still had some prestige at the time.

[[Tavinar]] knew of Gevelmar.
