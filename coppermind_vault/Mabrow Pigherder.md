**Mabrow Pigherder** is a resident of [[Hearthstone]] in [[Alethkar]] on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa1|37}}

Their house is close to the center of town. In {{Rosharan date|1168}}, a teenaged [[Kaladin]] ran past it on his way to catch up with [[Lirin]] before he confronted [[Roshone]].{{book ref|sa1|37}}
