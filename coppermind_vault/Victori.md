**Victori** is a carmaker in [[Elendel]] on [[Scadrial]]. In {{date|348|PC}}, they were the first carmaker to bring a convertible model to market. [[Call]] showed [[Wayne]] a picture of the new model; Wayne wanted to buy one, and he also agreed to buy a five percent stake in the company. Wayne suggested having the stars of his proposed [[noseball]] league drive the cars in order to promote them.{{book ref|mb7|16}}
