**Lorain** is an [[Order of Edgedancers|Edgedancer]] on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa4|39}}

==Appearance and Personality==
She is short, and has dyed hair.{{book ref|sa4|39}}

==Attributes and Abilities==
Lorain shares a [[Nahel bond]] with an unknown [[cultivationspren]]. She uses the [[Surge]] of [[Progression]] to heal people.{{book ref|sa4|39}}

==History==
Lorain worked in a medical clinic in the [[Urithiru]] markets with other Edgedancers and nurses. When [[Raboniel]] attacked [[Urithiru]] and reversed the effects of the [[Sibling]]'s protection [[fabrial]] to interfere with [[Knights Radiant|Radiant]] powers, Lorain and another Edgedancer fell unconscious. Lorain hit her head, causing some bleeding. A nurse that was working in the clinic attended to her wound and was confused by what had happened; when [[Kaladin]] came to investigate, the nurse theorized that there may have been a [[dazewater]] leak.{{book ref|sa4|39}}
