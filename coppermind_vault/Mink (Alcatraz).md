**Mink** is one of [[Alcatraz Smedry]]'s [[Mokia]]n advisors during the battle for [[Tuki Tuki]]. She advises Alcatraz along with the other two advisors, [[Dink]] and [[Wink]], with whom she is not related.{{book ref|a4|70706}}

## Appearance and Personality
Mink is short and compact, with her arms pulled in at her side and her chin nestled down level with her shoulders. Alcatraz likens her appearance to a can of soda.{{book ref|a4|70706}}

## History
During the battle, she gives Alcatraz information on the Mokian defenses such as the dome, and how long they’ll hold.{{book ref|a4|70706}} At Alcatraz’ orders, Mink and the other counselors went to fetch him some backpacks full of explosive teddy bears, six of  their fastest warriors, and a pair of very long stilts. They hesitated briefly, but did as asked.{{book ref|a4|6.02214179 × 10^23}}
