**Nor'Tallon** is a city on the [[Dayside]] of [[Taldain]]. It is the capital of [[Tallon]].{{book ref|ws|part=ars}} It is located in a mountain range east of [[KraeDa]], at the northern headwaters of the [[Nor'Tallon River]].{{map ref|dayside|Taldain Dayside}}{{book ref|ws|part=ars}}

## Trivia
* In the original *[[White Sand]]* maps, Nor'Tallon was a nation on the eastern coast of Dayside with [[Seevis]] to the north and [[Tallon]] to the south, with its western borders extending over the mountain range along the Nor'Tallon River.{{url ref|url=https://coppermind.net/w/images/archive/20230828023207%21Dayside_map.jpg|text=Previous Dayside Map|site=The Coppermind|date=2016-09-09}} However, an updated map in the *White Sand* omnibus edition merged Nor'Tallon into Tallon, with the former expressly defined as a city in the glossary.{{book ref|ws|part=ars}}
