**Riil** is an [[Elantrian]] on [[Sel]]. He was originally a laborer on a [[Arelon#Plantations|plantation]] and had been studying with the journeymen in hopes of becoming an apprentice before he was taken by the [[Shaod]].

[[Raoden]] recognizes that Riil's intelligence and workman build and ethics are a valuable combination. He gives Riil, [[Maare]], and [[Mareshe]] the task of first finding a way to build a roof with their limited resources and then building said roof over one of the larger buildings in [[New Elantris]].
