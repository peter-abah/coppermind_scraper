**Esandi** is a [[Dhatri]]an [[aetherbound]] who serves the [[Malwish Empire]].

==Appearance and Personality==
She has light brown skin and appears to be ethnically Dhatrian. However, she wears a Malwish uniform and appears to be a member of their military.

==Attributes and Abilities==
She is able to speak Malwish, with a noticeable accent.

One of her palms has a visible living aether bud of an unknown variety. She serves the Malwish despite some sort of restrictions on aetherbound serving [[Scadrian]]s. As of the discovery of the [[Dakwara]], she is the only aetherbound who serves the Malwish.

==History==
It is unknown how Esandi came to live on Scadrial and serve the Malwish. She was familiar with Colonel [[Dajer]], and owed him a favor related to unspecified past events.

During Dajer's mission to find a [[perpendicularity]] in the [[Drominad System]], he contacted Esandi via datapad seeking to verify information about the capabilities of an aetherbound with a cracked or disconnected bud. He also asked Esandi to show [[Illistandrista|Starling]] the living aether bud on her palm. Although Esandi cautioned Dajer that doing so revealed closely guarded state secrets, she acquiesced.

Dajer's ulterior motive in revealing Esandi's abilities was to intimidate and/or impress Starling by confirming that the Malwish had an aetherbound in their service. Starling was indeed surprised that an aetherbound could serve [[Scadrian]]s; she had previously discussed this possibility with her crew (including [[Ed]], [[Leonore]], and [[Aditil]]) and they had concluded that such service was forbidden, presumably by the [[Aether#Primal_Aethers|primal aethers]]. When Starling asked how the aethers allowed Esandi to serve the Malwish, she simply responded that there were ways around the restriction.
