Sister **Talanah** is an ardent in the [[Kholinar]] monastery on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa4|prologue}}

During [[Assassination of Gavilar|Gavilar's feast]] the cook came to [[Navani]] and told her that they had run out of the [[Clavendah]] and the [[Ruby Bench]]. Navani realized that [[Dalinar]] had been taking wine from the kitchens again, and told the cook to go to the monastery and ask Talanah for her secret wine reserves.{{book ref|sa4|prologue}}
