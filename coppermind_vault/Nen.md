**Nen** is an [[Alethi]] merchant on [[Roshar]]. He has dark wavy hair. Nen deals in polearms for the [[Shattered Plains|warcamps]]. He is habitually optimistic; after arriving at the [[Covenant Stone Crossroads]], he assured [[Svalka]] that [[Tet Rebin]] would arrive soon. Similarly, after noticing a few strangers standing near their caravan, Nen immediately started playing host. Not long after finding Rebin, the caravan in which Nen traveled was attacked by bandits. They, with the help of some Alethi soldiers, managed to repel the bandits however. After the attack, Nen gave several new friends of his a weapon of choice, either a javelin, longspear or shortspear. He is friends with an arms merchant named [[Yarren]].
