**Nen** is an [[Alethi]] merchant on [[Roshar]]. He has dark wavy hair.{{rpg ref|slsw|20}} Nen deals in polearms for the [[Shattered Plains|warcamps]]. He is habitually optimistic; after arriving at the [[Covenant Stone Crossroads]], he assured [[Svalka]] that [[Tet Rebin]] would arrive soon. Similarly, after noticing a few strangers standing near their caravan, Nen immediately started playing host.{{rpg ref|slsw|21}} Not long after finding Rebin, the caravan in which Nen traveled was attacked by bandits. They, with the help of some Alethi soldiers, managed to repel the bandits however.{{rpg ref|slsw|29}} After the attack, Nen gave several new friends of his a weapon of choice, either a javelin, longspear or shortspear.{{rpg ref|slsw|33}} He is friends with an arms merchant named [[Yarren]].{{rpg ref|slsw|36}}
