**Digball** is a team sport played on [[Detritus]]. 

It is played on hoverbikes fitted with [[acclivity ring]]s that are too small for ships and involves the use of a ball. Each bike can go three-D in short bursts, however, is only allowed a certain amount of airtime during the game. There is an organised sports program known as the Digball League, which is either made up of, or encompasses, a student league. Carrier is the only known position.

[[Hurl]] is one of the best Carriers in the student league.

Tickets are awarded to workers with exemplary merits.
