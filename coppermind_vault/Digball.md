**Digball** is a team sport played on [[Detritus]].{{book ref|Skyward|23}}{{book ref|Skyward|31}} 

It is played on hoverbikes fitted with [[acclivity ring]]s that are too small for ships and involves the use of a ball. Each bike can go three-D in short bursts, however, is only allowed a certain amount of airtime during the game.{{book ref|Skyward|31}} There is an organised sports program known as the Digball League, which is either made up of, or encompasses, a student league. Carrier is the only known position.{{book ref|Skyward|23}}{{book ref|Skyward|31}}

[[Hurl]] is one of the best Carriers in the student league.{{book ref|Skyward|23}}

Tickets are awarded to workers with exemplary merits.{{book ref|Skyward|31}}
