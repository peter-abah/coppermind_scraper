**Zedzil** is a [[Skybreaker]] squire under the Skybreaker masters [[Ki]] and [[Warren]].

He competes against [[Szeth]] in a martial competence test over the [[Purelake]], to test their ability with their [[surge]]s. The squires have to throw bags of coloured powder at each other and avoid being hit. Zedzil attempted to throw a bag at Szeth but it fell back onto him instead, and then was hit again by one of Szeth's bags. After which he stopped targeting Szeth.{{book ref|sa3|98}}
