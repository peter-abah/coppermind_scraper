**Guntua** is a [[heklo]] pirate in the [[belt]] of the [[nowhere]], and is a pilot in the [[Broadsider Faction|Broadsiders]]. 

## History
Guntua used to work at [[Surehold]], and was part of the large group that defected alongside [[Peg]]. She joined up as a pilot in the Broadsider Faction, and piloted a powerful two-seat striker, which was equipped with formidable destructors and a twin booster. It was the best of Peg's ships. After a fea years in the Broadsiders, Guntua decided she wanted to take a step back from flying, and take a break to work on the ground crew. After [[Spensa Nightshade]] became the pirate champion, she was allowed to take Guntua's old fighter.
