**Chanasha Hasareh** is a merchant on [[Roshar]]. She found modest success hiring out her [[chull]] teams to caravans crossing the [[Shattered Plains]] after most of the [[Alethi]] relocated to [[Urithiru]]. 

Chanasha is [[lighteyes|lighteyed]], although she is of a low [[dahn]]. She is presumably of the [[Vorin]] faith, as she wears a [[safehand]] sleeve. She is not the smiling type, and seems to be known as a shrewd negotiator. She always carries a reed in her pocket and never lets a day go by without reconciling her accounts. Her name is derived from the herald [[Chanaranach]].

She was bribed to hide in a secure location for a month while [[Shallan]] impersonated her as part of an attempt to infiltrate the [[Sons of Honor]].
