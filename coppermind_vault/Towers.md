**Towers** is a card game on [[Roshar]], with many variations. It is used to teach war strategy to generals. [[Adolin]] teaches [[Yanagawn]] to play. [[Rlain]] is good at playing towers. During the occupation of [[Urithiru]], Rlain tries to show some women who are gossiping about him that he is a person like them by telling them that he likes to play towers and [[runaround]], however he is unsuccessful and their gossip just turns in a darker direction.

## Rules of Play
The game can be played with two or more players. Cards represent armies which can be deployed on the table and retreated back to a player's hand. Positioning of cards compared to other cards on the table influences their stats. A player loses a game if all their armies are eliminated or they voluntarily give up. As Towers is usually played in best of three formats and eliminated cards can carry over from game to game, it is usually advised to not overcommitt cards to a single game. There are scenarios where this will happen, of which some are intended to teach the game. It is tradition to end a game with a handshake.

## Examples of variants
* Flatface - prefered variant of [[Adolin Kholin]]. Considered good for learning strategic planning. Possibly the most basic variant as the cards do exactly what the glyphs on them indicate.
* Deliver
* Vanquish
* Crosswise Chull - complex variant
* Bubbly Belch - complex variant
