**Tuma** is a planet under the control of the [[Superiority]]. It is prone to acidic rainstorms but has several excellent recourse farms nearby, some of which are used to farm methane. It is mostly automated, and is a very cheap place to live. The [[varvax]] [[Nuluba]] used to live there, doing customer analysis for the methane utility farms.
