**Leroy** was one of [[Stephen Leeds]]' [[aspects]]. It is not known what his special skill is.

He was on a skiing vacation when Stephen went looking for [[Sandra]], but returned sometime around when [[Tobias]] died. Later, like other aspects, he turned into a nightmare aspect or was killed by one.
