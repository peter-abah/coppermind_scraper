**Leroy** was one of [[Stephen Leeds]]' [[aspects]].{{book ref|legion3|2}} It is not known what his special skill is.

He was on a skiing vacation when Stephen went looking for [[Sandra]],{{book ref|legion3|2}} but returned sometime around when [[Tobias]] died.{{book ref|legion3|8}} Later, like other aspects, he turned into a nightmare aspect or was killed by one.
