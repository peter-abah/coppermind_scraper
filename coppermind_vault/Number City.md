**Number City** is the [[Cryptic]] capital and one of the largest cities in the [[Rosharan subastral]]. Its true name is a massive number--easily recitable by Cryptics and incomprehensible to all others, who unlike the city's inhabitants, are unable to commit it to memory. Difficulty with remembering the city's name has led it to be nicknamed "Number City".

Most Cryptics live in Number City and rarely venture outside of the city. It is the only Cryptic city in [[Rosharan subastral|Shadesmar]] and the spren have no desire to expand beyond what they already own.
