**Douglas Venture** is a member of [[House Venture]] in the [[Elendel]] nobility on [[Scadrial]].

Venture is a man that cannot handle his liquor. He was also a sort-of friend of High Lord [[Waxillium Ladrian]]. Wax's fiancée, Lady [[Steris Harms]], was concerned about the possibility of a disturbance at their wedding, and attempted to plan a small controlled problem that they could handle. Wax suggested that they invite Douglas Venture so that he could cause a drunken disturbance at the after-party. Steris agreed and Venture was invited.
