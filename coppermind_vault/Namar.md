**Namar** is a person on [[Roshar]] who developed a theory on how [[spren]] could be categorized.{{book ref|sa2|3}}

Namar believes that spren can be divided into two groups, those associated with human emotions and those associated with physical forces.{{book ref|sa2|3}}

[[Jasnah Kholin]] and [[Shallan Davar]] both subscribed to Namar's theory and briefly discussed it during Shallan's wardship.{{book ref|sa2|3}}
