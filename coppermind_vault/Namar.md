**Namar** is a person on [[Roshar]] who developed a theory on how [[spren]] could be categorized.

Namar believes that spren can be divided into two groups, those associated with human emotions and those associated with physical forces.

[[Jasnah Kholin]] and [[Shallan Davar]] both subscribed to Namar's theory and briefly discussed it during Shallan's wardship.
