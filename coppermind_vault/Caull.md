**Caull** was a young man from [[Hearthstone]] in [[Alethkar]] on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa1|44}} 

Caull was born to a man named [[Taleb]].{{book ref|sa1|44}} In {{Rosharan date|1168|missing-date=y}}, when Highmarshal [[Meridas Amaram]] visited to Hearthstone to recruit men for his army, Citylord [[Roshone]] chooses Caull to be one of the men conscripted, along with [[Agil]], [[Habrin]], [[Jorna]] and [[Tien]].
