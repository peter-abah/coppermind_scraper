The ***Penguinator*** is flying vessel shaped like a penguin that runs on [[Silimatic]] technology.

[[Alcatraz Smedry|Alcatraz]], [[Leavenworth Smedry|Grandpa Smedry]], [[Shasta Smedry|Shasta]], and [[Kazan Smedry|Kaz]] use the *Penguinator* to travel from [[Mokia]] to the [[Highbrary]].
