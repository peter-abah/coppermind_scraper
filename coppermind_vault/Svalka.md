**Svalka** is a fashionable [[Thaylen]] woman on [[Roshar]]. She is a Thaylen cloth merchant with drooping white eyebrows and a caring demeanor. Svalka owns a wagon marked with a bright-blue glyph resembling a spool of thread, a sign of her profession. Shortly before the arrival of the [[Everstorm]] on Roshar, she traveled from [[Alethkar]] proper to the [[Shattered Plains]], intending to sell her wares to the Alethi armies. Upon reaching the [[Covenant Stone Crossroads]], she began to worry, as her friend [[Tet Rebin]] and his daughter [[Hana (Roshar)|Hana]] should have arrived at the Crossroads hours ago.{{rpg ref|slsw|20}} Not long after this, the caravan in which she traveled was attacked by bandits. They, with the help of some Alethi soldiers, managed to repel the bandits however.{{rpg ref|slsw|29}}
