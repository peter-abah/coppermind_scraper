**Royal Defender** is a title traditionally given to a full Shardbearer of [[Thanadal princedom]] in [[Alethkar]] on [[Roshar]]. The Royal Defender's [[Shardplate]] is painted yellow.

The tradition of the Royal Defender lasted for centuries. Even though Thanadal had other Shards, only the Royal Defender was given both Blade and Plate. In , Brightlord [[Resi]] was the holder of the Shards and the title.
