***Artifabrication*** is a set of six books on [[Roshar]] written by Britt the Good. It is presumably about the creation of [[fabrial]]s.{{book ref|sa5|101}}

[[Navani Kholin]] acquired a worn copy of *Artifabrication* not long after she met [[Gavilar]], [[Dalinar]], [[Torol]], and [[Ialai]]. The books were still in her study in [[Kholinar]] shortly after the [[Alethi]] discovered the [[listener]]s on the [[Shattered Plains]]. She later noted the books in her study when [[Odium]] tormented her with visions from her past during her trip to the [[Spiritual Realm]].{{book ref|sa5|101}}
