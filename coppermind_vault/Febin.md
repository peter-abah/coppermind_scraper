**Febin** is an [[Alethi]] man who is a member of [[Dalinar's elites|Dalinar Kholin's elites]] on [[Roshar]] during the [[Era of Solitude]].

Febin and [[Kadash]] served as bodyguards for [[Gavilar Kholin]] after he and [[Dalinar]] conquered [[Rathalas]] during the [[Alethi reunification]].
