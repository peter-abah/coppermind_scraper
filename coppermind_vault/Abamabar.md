**Abamabar** was a location on [[Roshar]] during the [[Heraldic Epochs]].

[[Nohadon]] claimed to have walked from Abamabar to [[Urithiru]] in [[The Way of Kings (in-world)|*The Way of Kings*]].{{book ref|twok|58}} [[Dalinar Kholin]] believed that this was a long distance. It is unknown whether Abamabar was a city or not, but it seems likely as Urithiru was a city.
