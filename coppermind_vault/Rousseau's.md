**Rousseau's** is a retailer which sells shoes on Yomen Street in the Third Octant of [[Elendel]].

A servant at [[Lady ZoBell's party]] is questioned by [[Wayne]] after the fact, and he asks her where she purchased her shoes, and she got them at Rousseau's.
