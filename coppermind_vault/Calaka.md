**Calaka** is a resident of [[Babilar]].{{book ref|Firefight|19}}

## Appearance and Personality
He is tall and has blue hair. He does not think that the [[Reckoners]] should be fighting against the [[Epic]]s and believes that standing up to them only makes things worse for everyone else.{{book ref|firefight|19}}

## History
Calaka was at the [[party in Babilar]] with [[Infinity]], [[Marco (Reckoners)|Marco]], and [[Lulu]]. When [[Mizzy]] introduced [[David]] to the group, Calaka made a derogatory comment about David's drab appearance, and she punched him in the arm as retribution. Taking a glass of glowing juice, he asked David where he was from and then told Mizzy he didn't think [[Steve]] was at the party. Infinity mentioned Steve being caught up in [[Obliteration]]'s explosions a few days before, so Calaka and the others raised a glass in his memory. Calaka expressed his disgust with the Reckoners' actions, and then the group moved on to discussion of [[Edso]]'s music.{{book ref|firefight|19}}
