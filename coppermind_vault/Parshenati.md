The **Parshenati** (rough translation: *those who destroy*) are a group of anti-authoritarian singers seeking to redefine the derogatory name given to their [[listener]] cousins. They are mostly active in the occupied [[Alethi]] city of [[Revolar]], where they have seized control of several districts in the city.{{rpg ref|slwg|152}}
