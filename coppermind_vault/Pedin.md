**Pedin** is a member of [[Bridge Four]].

He and [[Mart]] are killed by [[Szeth]] at the [[Battle of Narak]].

In , as [[Kaladin]] tries to get to the [[Battle of Thaylen Field]] from [[Shadesmar]], Kaladin fights the [[Fused]] and remembers Pedin, alongside [[Malop]], [[Jaks]], [[Beld]], [[Rod (Roshar)|Rod]], [[Mart]], [[Goshel]], [[Nalma]], [[Dallet]] and his squad, and [[Tien]], in an attempt to say his next oath, but he fails to.
