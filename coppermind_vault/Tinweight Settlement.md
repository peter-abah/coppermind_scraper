**Tinweight Settlement** is a mining town on [[Scadrial]]. It is located about thirty miles southwest of [[Weathering]] in the Northern [[Roughs]]. The settlement is located next to a mine where metal ore can be found (likely [[tin]], and possibly others). The mineworkers live in poor conditions, and deadly accidents at the mine are common.

[[Wayne]] grew up in Tinweight Settlement. His family was among the poorest in town, and they lived in a one-window house very close to the mine. [[Wayne's father]] and uncle [[Gregr]] died in mining accidents, forcing [[Wayne's mother|his mother]] to take a job in the mine. She was killed in yet another mining accident when Wayne was eleven.
