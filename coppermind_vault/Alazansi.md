Brightking **Alazansi** was a mythological king on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|twok|55}}

Alazansi was in a story that involved a fleet of one hundred ships{{book ref|twok|55}} [[Leyten]] requested that [[Sigzil]] tell that story while [[Bridge Four]] was on chasm duty.
