**Kondeon** is a city in [[Arelon]]. It lies along the [[Aredel River]], and is about halfway between [[Elantris (city)|Elantris]] and [[Lake Alonoe]]. It is in the domain of the [[Arelon#Plantations|Rii Plantation]].
