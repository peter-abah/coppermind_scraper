**Gilles and Gilles** is a clothes shop located on Canton Avenue and Troncheau Way in [[Elendel]] on [[Scadrial]]. They tailor exquisite and tasteful dress clothes for fashionable people.

Their shop is featured in an [[Jak|Allomancer Jak]] story, where Jak mentions that his shirt was given to him by the owner's daughter.
