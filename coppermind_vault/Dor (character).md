**Dor** is a [[singer]] on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa4|1}}

In early {{Rosharan date|1175}}, they were part of the [[Fused]]'s forces stationed in [[Hearthstone]] under the command of [[Abiajan]]. They were one of the inspectors on duty when [[Lirin]] attempted to sneak the [[Herdaz]]ian general [[Dieno enne Calah]] into town by using the general as a litter carrier. Although Dor was slightly more vigilant than their colleagues, they failed to notice Dieno, partly due to a planned distraction by [[Toralin Roshone]]. Dieno's party was allowed to enter the town just before Abiajan ordered all of the inspectors to close off the city limits because [[Lezian]] had arrived seeking [[Kaladin]].{{book ref|sa4|1}}
