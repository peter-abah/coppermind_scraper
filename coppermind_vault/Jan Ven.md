**Jan Ven** is a [[Sho Del]] in the [[cosmere]].{{book ref|mb7|epilogue|6}}

Like all Sho Del, she has chalk-white skin and hair, four arms, and reptilian eyes. She works as a guide and a boatwoman in the [[Cognitive Realm]]; [[MeLaan]] hires her when she travels through Shadesmar on behalf of [[Harmony]]. Jan Ven is empathetic and willing to accomodate MeLaan, as well as explain various aspects of Cognitive Realm culture and mechanics to her.{{book ref|mb7|epilogue|6}}
