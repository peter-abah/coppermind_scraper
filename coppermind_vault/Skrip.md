A **skrip** is a species of [[cremling]] on [[Roshar]].

Skirps have flat tails, two large claws, and long antennae.

When [[Shallan]] meets [[Wit]] in [[Kholinar]], they visit a tavern where Wit is meant to be entertaining, and are served [[soulcast]] grain cakes each topped with a steamed skrip. Shallan describes the dish as "not terrible", and ends up eating Wit's food in addition to her own.
