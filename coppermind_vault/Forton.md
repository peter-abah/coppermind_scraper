**Forton** is a [[Hroven]] alchemist and apothecary on [[Sel]], known for his potions that can have supernatural effects.{{book ref|elantris|part=ars}}{{book ref|Elantris|18}}

== Appearance and Personality==
Forton has a mouse-like face with large ears. He also has a persistent cough, likely because of various substances that he likes to smoke. These same substances also cause Forton to be absolutely content with his life.{{book ref|Elantris|18}} Like many in Hrovell, Forton speaks a dialect of [[Fjordell]] that is thick, harsh, and rough sounding.{{book ref|Elantris|18}}

Forton is a man of contradictions. While he is a devoted follower of the [[Jeskeri Mysteries]] and is considered a mystic of sorts in his town, he also professes to be staunchly [[Derethi]]. His knowledge of Derethi doctrine is impressive but lacking in some areas, such as his belief that [[gyorn]]s could simply pray to Jaddeth to have their words directed to anyone with a [[seon]]. [[Hrathen]] can easily see that his piety is an act, likely intended to appease the [[Fjordell Empire]].{{book ref|Elantris|18}} 

## Attributes and Abilities
Forton is an apothecary of unparalleled skill and intelligence.{{book ref|Elantris|18}} There is no one in [[Opelon]] who can match Forton's skills in making antidotes, elixirs, potions, and poisons. He is able to create potions with specific effects in less than a day, sometimes through combining existing formulas.{{book ref|Elantris|18}}

In fact, Forton's potions are [[Investiture|Invested]],{{wob ref|826}} likely drawing on the power of the [[Dor]]. It is unknown how exactly Forton creates these potions, how they use Investiture, or where he learned his skills. Selish magic is known to be highly location-dependent,{{au ref|sel}} so it is likely that the [[Invested Art]] used by Forton is specific to Hrovell, but it is not known whether other Hroven people can make similar potions.

Forton is also bound to an unnamed seon, allowing for instantaneous communication across the world.{{book ref|Elantris|18}}

## History
Years before the [[Invasion of Arelon]], Hrathen was poisoned by a political enemy with a poison that had no known antidote. Hrathen went to Forton for help and was cured. Hrathen kept in touch with Forton via seon, ordering potions, antidotes, and the like. He was so skilled and such a useful tool that Hrathen overlooked the fact that he was a heathen.{{book ref|Elantris|18}} While attempting to convert [[Arelon]] to [[Shu-Dereth]], Hrathen commissioned a potion that could simulate the appearance of an [[Elantrian]] who had been taken by the [[Shaod]] after the [[Reod]].{{book ref|Elantris|18}}{{book ref|Elantris|30}} Hrathen used this potion both on himself{{book ref|Elantris|30}} and [[Sarene]]{{book ref|Elantris|38}} to create the appearance of a miracle healing and gain leverage on [[Eventeo]], Sarene's father and king of [[Teod]].{{book ref|Elantris|45}} Forton's potion worked exactly as requested, although the effects lasted slightly longer than Hrathen expected.{{book ref|elantris|36}}
