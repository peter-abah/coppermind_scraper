**Dean** is a member of the [[Ghostbloods]], stationed in [[Elendel]]. He is the organization's chemist, producing the organization's [[Aluminum|aluminum]] via electrolysis. He believes that someday aluminum would become easy enough to produce that it will become cheaper than [[Tin|tin]].{{book ref|mb7|40}}
