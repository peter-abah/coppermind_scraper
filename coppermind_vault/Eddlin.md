**Eddlin**, or **Ed** for short, is an arcanist of [[Vax|Vaxilian]] heritage serving on the [[Dynamic]].

## Appearance & Personality
Ed is dark-skinned with curly black hair. He typically dresses in robes and a large, floppy hat, and carries around the large bag common among arcanists.

He is very talkative and curious, constantly searching for new information on the cosmere. He often rambles about topics he finds interesting, and takes notes on any interesting experiences he has aboard the *Dynamic*. The [[Grand Apparatus]], [[ZeetZi]]'s homeworld, is something he finds especially fascinating. He is a loyal member of [[Illistandrista|Starling]]'s crew, and typically a very friendly and witty person. He can sometimes be overeager. He is also courageous, and stood up for his friends against the [[Malwish]] military, armed with nothing but a heavy tome.

Ed follows [[the Path]], a [[Scadrial|Scadrian]] religion, as he thinks the [[Shard]] of this religion is 'nice'. He keeps a Pathian earring on his person. 

## Attributes & Abilities
Ed is a highly talented arcanist, and well-informed on matters such as [[Realmatic Theory]], [[Investiture]], and the various planets of the [[cosmere]]. He can provide a lot of information off the top of his head, and teaches the other members of his crew about [[Evil|Threnodite entities]] and is able to analyze and authenticate ancient [[Iriali]] artifacts with ease. [[Xisisrefliel|Xisis]] and [[Hoid]] hold his skills in high regard. Ed knows many other arcanists based in [[Silverlight]], and collaborates with them on occasion. [[Khriss]] is an idol of his, and he has a lot of respect for her research and work.  

He is able to speak multiple languages, including formal [[Yolen|Yolish]] religious [[dragon (cosmere)|draconic]], which he slips into when he is drunk.

He is able to fire a gun, but has terrible aim.

## History
Ed was born and raised in Silverlight. He joined the crew of the *Dynamic* under Hoid, and worked there for a number of years before Starling became captain. As part of his work, he would write reports on things he observed and share them with other arcanists back in Silverlight. 

He backed Starling when she led a mutiny against Captain [[Crow]], and worked with the others to dodge a type 1-6 Threnodite entity and rescue an Iriali family from it. His knowledge of Investiture was crucial to this achievement. He helps the Iriali family ([[Crux]],[[Nol]] and their child) settle down on the ship after they escaped. 

Once the *Dynamic* has returned to Silverlight, Ed plans to report his experiences with the entity to his fellow scholars. Before he can go, Starling asked him to look at a map given to her by the Iriali. Ed recognized it as a genuine piece of the Iriali [[Iriali#History|Long Trail]]. The map spoke of a [[perpendicularity]] in the [[Drominad system]]. He took the map to his colleagues to discuss it further.

The crew followed the map to an [[Dakwara's island|island]] in the [[Cognitive Realm#Emberdark|emberdark]], where they were taken prisoner by Malwish Scadrians. There was another entity on this island- the [[Dakwara]]. He discussed the nature of the [[Cognitive Realm]] with the other crewmembers. The Malwish then threatened to execute [[Leonore]], and Ed tried to help defend her by using a tome as a weapon. He and ZeetZi ended up in a scuffle to free Leonore, but this was just a distraction- the Malwish put [[Nazrilof|Nazh]] in a shade prison, and let Leonore go.

Leonore was in a bad mood following this encounter, and Ed attempted to cheer her up by talking about an interesting planet he'd read about. The crew began to plan an escape, and Ed shared his knowledge about the [[Eelakin]]. They worked in secret to create a plan, and eventually proposed their idea to Starling. This plan ultimately failed, but with Ed's knowledge of the [[Intensifier]], the crew figure out another. 

After Starling used the Intensifier to create a power cut, Ed and [[Aditil]] freed Nazh from the shade prison. He went along with Starling to free [[Chrysalis]], engaging in small gunfights on the way. 

[[Dusk]] led the crew of the *Dynamic* to [[Patji's Eye]], the perpendicularity on [[First of the Sun]]. They spent six months of shore leave there, and left the planet after the inaugural voyage of the [[Navigator]]s. Ed and the others promised Starling that they would find a way to free her from her manacles and return her draconic powers to her. The crew decided to travel to the Grand Apparatus in order to achieve this.

## Relationships
### Starling
Ed holds a great deal of respect for Starling as his captain. She, in turn, believes him to be an immensely competent arcanist. Like the rest of the crew, Ed wants to help free Starling from her manacles in order to restore her powers to her.
