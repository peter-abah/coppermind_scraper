**Lirdan** is a soldier from the [[Aladar princedom]] on [[Roshar]]. He was employed in the [[Aladar|Highprince]]’s army during the tail end of the [[War of Reckoning]]. At some point, Lirdan went to a gambling den called the [[Red Rockbud]]. Here, he drunkenly joined a game of [[Pieces]] with the owner, [[Nel]] and another person. It is unknown what the outcome of the game was.
