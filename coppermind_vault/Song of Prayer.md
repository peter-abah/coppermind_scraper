The **Song of Prayer** is a song that can be used by [[singer]]s to create [[Voidlight]] [[spheres]].{{book ref|sa4|67}} 

The role of the song in the creation of Voidlight spheres is unknown. However, [[Navani]]'s research into the [[Rhythm#The Pure Tones|pure tones of Roshar]] allowed her to manipulate Voidlight,{{book ref|sa4|69}} even mixing it with other [[Lights]] inside of a sphere.{{book ref|sa4|84}} The tone and rhythm of the Song of Prayer is therefore likely related to the creation of Voidlight spheres.

[[Odium]]'s forces are not seen using the Song of Prayer in battle, even when they are running low on Voidlight.{{book ref|sa4|5}} It is unclear why this is the case, but [[Fused]] and [[Regal]]s seem to have abundant spheres available to them{{book ref|sa4|2}}{{book ref|sa4|67}} and it may be easier or faster to simply retrieve more spheres rather than create new ones. [[Venli]] knew that singing the Song of Prayer could draw Odium's attention, and did not dare to try and create her own Voidlight spheres during the occupation of [[Urithiru]].{{book ref|sa4|67}}
