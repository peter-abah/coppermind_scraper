**Fritz Casas** is one of the artists of the graphic novel adaptation of [[Brandon Sanderson]]'s *[[White Sand (prose)|White Sand]]*. He is credited as the artist of *[[White Sand|White Sand Volume 3]]*. His other work for Dynamite Comics includes *Red Sonja: Vulture's Circle* and *The Blood Queen*.{{url ref|url=https://comicvine.gamespot.com/fritz-casas/4040-62516/|text=Comic Vine profile|site=Comic Vine|date=date unknown}}
