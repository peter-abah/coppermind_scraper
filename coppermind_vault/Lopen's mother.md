**Lopen's mother** is a [[Herdaz]]ian woman on [[Roshar]].

==Appearance and Personality==
Lopen's mother is ethnically Herdazian. She speaks [[Alethi]] with a thick accent. She can be extremely forceful in her personality, especially in her own household. She likes to cook and feed people, and she keeps [[axehound]]s.{{book ref|sa2|87}}

Lopen describes his mother as "wise".{{book ref|sa2|87}} She encouraged his self-deprecating jokes back when he was still missing his left arm, telling him to laugh before others could laugh at him.{{book ref|sa2|12}} She also appears to have some influence among her relatives. One of Lopen's cousins, [[Punio]], decided to join [[Bridge Four]] after hearing Lopen's mother mention that they needed help.{{book ref|sa2|87}}

==History==
At some point during the [[War of Reckoning]], she and her family moved to Little Herdaz, a section of Highprince [[Sebarial]]'s warcamp on the [[Shattered Plains]]. When a wounded King [[Elhokar]] was recovering in Little Herdaz, Lopen's mother forced the king to eat despite his objections. She said he looked pale, and she did not want Elhokar found when he was not properly fed.{{book ref|sa2|87}}

She presumably moved to [[Urithiru]] along with most of the population of the warcamps after the tower city's rediscovery.{{book ref|sa3|18}}
