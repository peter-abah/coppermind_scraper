**Shar** is a [[darkeyed]] girl on [[Roshar]]. She lives in [[Urithiru]] during the [[Fused]] occupation of the tower.

She is the daughter of a seamstress and her uncle is a [[Windrunner]]. She is friends with [[Adin]], and has a bossy personality.

Shar glares at some warform [[singers]] near the end of the occupation.
