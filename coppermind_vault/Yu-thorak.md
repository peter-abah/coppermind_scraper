**Yu-thorak** are a species of semi-marine [[greatshell]] found in the seas around [[Aimia]].{{rpg ref|aa|8}} The [[Sleepless]] seem to have a way of controlling them.{{book ref|sa3.5|epilogue}}

## Appearance and Anatomy
Yu-thorak are a type of greatshell. They can grow to prodigious size, standing as tall as a building,{{rpg ref|aa|8}} and look somewhat like a slug.{{book ref|sa3.5|17}} The frontmost six of their twelve skittering limbs can get as thick as tree trunks and sharp as spears, while their other legs are shorter, providing the creatures with speed and support. They have two sets of beady black eyes. Each set moves independently, granting them excellent vision both on land and underwater. Yu-thorak have huge hooked beaks and segmented carapace that protects them from most weapons.{{rpg ref|aa|8}} Yu-thorak seem to bond with luckspren.{{book ref|sa3.5|17}} Under their carapace, they are a pink-white color.{{book ref|sa3.5|18}}

## Ecology
Yu-thorak are found on Roshar’s beaches and coastal shallows, especially around Aimia. They generally swallow their prey whole, and yu-thorak begin to digest their prey while they are still alive. While most mobile in the water, yu-thorak are still dangerous on land, scooting their bulbous bodies along the shoreline in search of prey.{{rpg ref|aa|8}} The Sleepless use them to protect Aimia from unwanted visitors.{{book ref|sa3.5|epilogue}}
