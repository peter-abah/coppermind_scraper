***Dialogues*** is a famous historical work on the topic of political theory on [[Roshar]] during the [[Era of Solitude]].{{book ref|twok|33}}

The [[Palanaeum]] had a copy of *Dialogues*, which was located in the same room as *[[Shadows Remembered]]*.{{book ref|twok|33}}

In {{Rosharan date|1173}}, Princess [[Jasnah Kholin]] sent Brightness [[Shallan Davar]] to fetch and then read the copy of *Dialogues* in the Palanaeum.{{book ref|twok|33}} As it was near *Shadows Remembered*, a book Jasnah had been reading, Shallan spent some time reading *Shadows Remembered* while she was there.
