The **Core Archipelago** is an island chain in the [[Emerald Sea]] on [[Lumar]].{{book ref|tress|7}} It is the home of the [[Verdant King]], who lives in a palace on one of the islands,{{book ref|tress|8}} presumably in the city of [[Kingsport]].

## Geography
The archipelago is a good distance from [[Shimmerbay]]{{book ref|tress|21}} and far from [[Diggen's Point]].{{book ref|tress|8}}{{book ref|tress|2}} The number of islands and their size are both unknown, but [[Huck]] implies that some islands are as large as forty miles wide.{{book ref|tress|30}}

## History
The [[Charlie's father|duke]] of Diggen's Point would periodically visit the king to report on his territory.{{book ref|tress|4}} A "cursed" [[Hoid]] claimed to have visited the archipelago during his time on Lumar.{{book ref|tress|42}}

The archipelago was the claimed destination of the *[[Oot's Dream]]* after it forged a king's writ to buy six kegs of [[Brick]]'s ale on Diggen's Point.{{book ref|tress|7}}{{book ref|tress|9}} [[Tress]] posed as a royal inspector hoping to gain passage to the king's island on the ship as part of her plan to free [[Charlie]].{{book ref|tress|8}}
