**Professor Lake** is an [[arcanist]] and professor at the [[Silverlight]] university.

When [[Starling]] visited [[Xisis]]'s palace in Silverlight to discuss the piece of the [[Iriali]] Long Trail record she was given, she noted that Xisis must be present in Silverlight because Professor Lake would be giving a presentation about a new planet in three days and Xisis wouldn't want to miss it.

## Trivia
* Professor Lake is named after Eric Lake, one of Brandon's beta readers, [[17s-user:1|one of the founders]] of the [[17thshard.com|17th Shard]], and [[User:Chaos2651|a staff member]] on the Coppermind.
