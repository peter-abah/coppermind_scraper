The **Happy Accident** is a bar that [[Holly Winseed (Provisional Replica)|Holly Winseed’s Provisional Replica]] goes to. It has a real neon “open” sign hanging in the window. The interior is a normal, blank white, to allow for easy theming, except for a sizable collection of physical landscape paintings on the walls. It’s default theming is vintage-style, with lots of neon, and a long, polished wood bar. It is well constructed theme, with a good amount of smoke in the air for atmosphere. Though the interior is largely themed, the drinks served at the Happy Accident are real.
