**Darlin Cett** is a member of [[House Cett]] on [[Scadrial]]. He is either the Lord of House Cett during {{date|348|PC}}, or acts as the representative of House Cett on the Elendel Senate.{{book ref|mb7|31}}

## Appearence and Personality
Darlin Cett is a well dressed man,{{book ref|mb7|38}} with slicked back, thinning hair.{{book ref|mb7|31}} He also uses powders, likely to whiten his skin as is fashionable during {{date|348|PC}}. Darlin is knowledgable about Elendel politics, and he is somewhat of a war hawk, believing that they should deal the first strike to the Outer Cities.{{book ref|mb7|38}} However, after realising that there was a bomb pointed at [[Elendel]], he immediately made arrangements to flee, valuing his own life above those of the common people.{{book ref|mb7|43}}

## History
Darlin was the senator for House Cett during {{date|348|PC}}. After [[Steris Ladrian]] asked the Senate to approve of a [[Elendel#Senate|Select Council]] to deal with the issue of the Outer Cities, Darlin voted in favour of this proposal, knowing that he would be included in the council.{{book ref|mb7|31}} During the meeting this council held, Darlin argued for a preemptive strike against the Outer Cities, believing that if they waited, they soon wouldn’t be able to win anymore.{{book ref|mb7|38}} After receiving a message detailing the presence of an Outer Cities’ bomb that was pointed at Elendel, Darlin immediately made arrangements to flee the city.{{book ref|mb7|43}}
