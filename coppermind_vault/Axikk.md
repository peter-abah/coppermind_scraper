**Axikk** is a vizier in the [[Azish]] government on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa2|i|9}} 

==Attributes and Abilities==
Axikk was presumably a high-ranking vizier based on his candidacy for Prime and his presence in the emperor's quarters with other important viziers such as Dalksi and [[Noura]].{{book ref|sa2|i|9}} 

==History==
Axikk was one of the potential [[Prime Aqasix]] candidates during the Azish succession crisis following the assassination of two Primes in one week by [[Szeth]].{{book ref|sa2|i|9}} [[Lift]] overheard him in the [[Bronze Palace]] talking to a number of other viziers. Like the other candidates, Axikk intentionally wrote a bad application essay in an attempt to avoid elevation to Prime. Additionally, he claimed to be disqualified due to a bad back. This excuse was refuted by [[Dalksi]], who pointed out that [[Ashno|Ashno of Sages]] had a bad back, but was considered one of the greatest [[Emuli]] Primes.{{book ref|sa2|i|9}}

He is implied to be one of the viziers who attempts to teach Lift during her time in the Bronze Palace after the ascension of [[Yanagawn]];{{book ref|sa2|10}} Lift mentions a vizier that she refers to as "Big A" because of the choking sounds in his name.{{book ref|sa2.5|13}} He was likely one of the many viziers present during [[Dalinar Kholin]]'s diplomatic visit to Azimir.{{book ref|sa3|65}}
