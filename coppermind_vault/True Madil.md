**True Madil** is a city in the Northern [[Roughs]] of [[Scadrial]]. 

==Geography==
It is situated at the confluence of two rivers that flow into the Basin. It is served by the only railway that extends into the Northern Roughs. The nearest city in the Basin is [[Drypost]], around 125 miles to the south. 

## History
Circa , True Madil was the largest city in the Northern Roughs.

[[Miles Dagouter]] served as the lawman of True Madil for around fifteen years. Under Miles's leadership, law enforcement in the town maintained an alliance with [[Weathering]] and [[Far Dorest]]. Miles carried a lighter inscribed with the seal of the lawmen of True Madil on his person. He kept using the lighter during his time as leader of the [[Vanishers]], even though it made the other members of the gang uncomfortable. 

True Madil had a very secure prison. After [[Waxillium Ladrian]] apprehended the criminal [[Ape Manton]], he imprisoned him in the jail there.
