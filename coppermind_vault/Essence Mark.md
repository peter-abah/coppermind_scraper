An **Essence Mark** is a particular type of [[soulstamp]] that is designed to [[Forgery|Forge]] a person's memories, history, and soul for a brief period of time.{{tes ref|2}} Essence Marks are among the most powerful and most complex soulstamps a Forger can create.{{tes ref|2}} In [[realmatic]] terms, an Essence Mark reforges the spiritual aspect of an individual to match the new history.{{tes ref|12}} 

Essence Marks are considered blasphemy by a number of cultures, including the [[Grand]]s of the [[Rose Empire]], the [[Mulla'dil]] Strikers, and the [[Dzhamar]]ians.{{tes ref|30}}{{tes ref|76}} 

## Construction & Use
Essence Marks are the most difficult type of soulstamps to create, typically requiring years worth of development and construction, and are required to be attuned to a specific individual in order to function.{{tes ref|2}} When a human is soulstamped, the transformation lasts a relatively short period of time, and the best crafted stamps last approximately a day. This is due to the fact that the identity of a person, as opposed to an object, is constantly growing, changing and shifting.{{tes ref|30}} It may also be due to the fact that all humans are naturally Invested with a small amount of Investiture,{{wob ref|6881}} and Investiture resists change by other Investiture.{{wob ref|2675}} However, through large amounts of practice and research, it is possible to make Essence Marks that last far longer.{{book ref|mb7|53}}{{book ref|mb7|epilogue|1}}{{wob ref|16022}} If used continuously for years, the person may no longer need the Essence Mark to maintain the transformation.{{tes ref|58}}

To create an Essence Mark, one must make hundreds of stamps, each one representing a small portion of the history or personality which you would wish to change.{{tes ref|2}} These stamps must be tested individually to make sure that each will take, and then networked together into a lattice on some external object. The lattice will be connected to the final stamp, which will act as a keystone for the other stamps, and will be the only one necessary to stamp. The lattice must maintain in contact with the skin of an individual to maintain its effects.{{tes ref|58}} A deep knowledge of the individual and the new history you wish to write is necessary to create the stamps for an Essence Mark.{{tes ref|2}} An [[Gold#Allomantic_Use|Augur]] would find their Allomantic powers "useful" for making Essence Marks for themselves.{{wob ref|11153}}
