**Chip** is a [[steel]] [[Misting]] (aka a [[Coinshot]]) and a member of the [[Elendel]] constabulary on [[Scadrial]].{{book ref|mb5|19}}

He and the Fourth Octant's messengers are sent by [[Claude Aradel]] to tell the other constable-generals to meet at the Governor's mansion to coordinate a city-wide curfew. Because Chip is a [[Coinshot]], he is able to move through the city very quickly to get the message out.{{book ref|mb5|19}}
