**Mela** is a [[Windrunner]] on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa4|111}}

## Attributes and Abilities
As a Windrunner, Mela is able to heal herself with [[Stormlight]] and to use the [[Surge]]s of [[Gravitation]] and [[Adhesion]].{{book ref|sa4|part=ars}} She is competent at fighting with a spear.{{book ref|sa4|111}}

## History
Mela was part of the force of [[Windrunner]]s that went to [[Emul]] as part of the [[coalition of monarchs|coalition's]] army. She accompanied [[Dalinar]], [[Szeth]], [[Sigzil]], [[Lyn]], [[Leyten]], and another Windrunner to [[Tukar]] to meet with [[Ishar]]. When Ishar drew his [[Honorblade]] on Dalinar, the five Windrunners fought him. Ishar, however, a master duelist, managed to [[Connection|Connect]] each of them to the ground and drain away their [[Stormlight]]. Szeth countered Ishar's tactics by severing his Connections and collapsing Dalinar's [[perpendicularity]] with [[Nightblood]], and Dalinar and the Windrunners Lashed themselves into the air.{{book ref|sa4|111}}

After Szeth and Nightblood prompted Ishar to retreat into [[Shadesmar]], the group returned to the ground and looked through Ishar's pavilion. They were perplexed by the strange corpses they found; after Mela called Dalinar over to a body, the [[Stormfather]] recognized it as a [[Cryptic]] and realized that all of the corpses were [[spren]] in bodies that should not be possible in the [[Physical Realm]]. The group grabbed whatever they could find in the tent that looked to be of use and quickly retreated to Emul.{{book ref|sa4|111}}
