**Invention** is a [[Shard]] of [[Adonalsium]] that has not been [[Splinter#Formation|Splintered]] and whose original Vessel is [[Chan Ko Sar]].{{book ref|sa5|115}} The Vessel may be one of the scholars [[Hoid]] questioned about talent, who answered that the highest talent is the invention of marvelous devices.{{book ref|wok|epilogue}}

Invention created a number of planets, and [[Starling]] believes that they are more technologically advanced than [[Scadrial]].{{book ref|iote|40}} The lawnarks native to the [[Grand Apparatus]] are mentioned to be 'of Invention'.{{book ref|iote|42}}

## History
Like all Shards, Invention was created at the [[Shattering]] of [[Adonalsium]], and picked up by the Vessel [[Chan Ko Sar]]. After Ascending, Chan Ko Sar traveled the cosmere creating "great marvels."{{book ref|sa5|115}}

Some time around the [[True Desolation]] [[Harmony]], on the advice of [[Hoid]], reached out to Invention about the threat posed by [[Odium]]. After that initial contact, Harmony was unable to locate Invention again.{{epigraph ref|sa4|22}}{{epigraph ref|sa4|24}}

## Trivia
* If Brandon were to become a Vessel, Invention is one of the Shards he has said he might choose.{{wob ref|14733}}
