**Limafruit** is a type of small fruit with a green rind found on [[Roshar]]. It can be eaten without any preparation and is small enough to eat whole.

[[Lift]] stole a limafruit off a young [[lighteyed]] man's table as she was following [[Gereh]]'s red [[Aviar|chicken]] in [[Urithiru]].
