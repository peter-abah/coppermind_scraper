**Gub** is a journeyman potter in [[Urithiru]] on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa4|i|11}}

He works alongside [[Alalan]] in Master [[Liganor]]'s pottery store. Liganor once handled all the glaze work, but now Gub does it in his place.{{book ref|sa4|i|11}}
