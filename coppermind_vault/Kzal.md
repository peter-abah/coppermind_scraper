**Kzal** is an [[Azish]] [[vizier]] on [[Roshar]]. He attended the meeting of [[coalition of monarchs|monarchs]] in [[Urithiru]] where [[Wit]] revealed that [[Odium]] was sending forces against the capital cities [[Azimir]], [[Thaylen City]], and [[Narak]] in a bid to capture each of their corresponding nations before the commencement of the [[contest of champions]] settled their borders. When [[the Mink]] was working on tactics to defend against this attack, Kzal confirmed to him to that Azimir had three thousand soldiers in the city to defend it.
