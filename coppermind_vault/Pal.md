> They want us to farm, I'm tired of farming.
> — Pal explaining why she and [[Nam]] clear rubble.

**Pal** is a [[singer]] in [[Kholinar]].

She and [[Nam]] are one of the few [[singers]] who survived the [[Siege of Kholinar]] together with [[Moash]].
They join Moash and [[Khen]] who are clearing rubble in Kholinar. Both of them wear [[warform]] at that time.

It isn't clear for how long Pal and Nam have been in [[Khen]]'s group.
