**Tamukeks** are a type of bone that can be used for inter-[[Realmatic Theory|Realm]] and interplanetary communication, similar to a [[seon]].{{wob ref|14644}} Most notably they are used to interact with [[Dragon (cosmere)|dragons]] for purposes of prayer.{{book ref|iote|56}}{{wob ref|16187}}{{wob ref|16190}} They have some similarities to [[singer]] [[gemheart]]s.{{wob ref|10319}}

[[Hoid]] has possession of a tamukek as of the [[Night of Sorrows]], which he used to contact [[Frost]] and his sister.{{book ref|sa5|18}} He would later use another to contact "[[Spirit Shores|the meditative realm of the dragons]]" for "wisdom of the ancient dead"; this gave him insight on [[Dalinar Kholin]]'s recent actions and their implications.{{book ref|sa5|147}} An interference cloud made by a [[Sleepless interference machine]] has the side effect of preventing dragons from convening with their followers through a tamukek.{{book ref|iote|56}}

## Trivia
* When writing *[[Rhythm of War]]*, Brandon debated whether to have the communication box that [[Mraize]] gave to [[Shallan]] contain a seon or a tamukek, but eventually settled on making it a seon because they have personalities whereas tamukeks are just bones.{{wob ref|14644}}
*Tamukeks (then spelled "Tamu Keks") originated in the book *[[Dragonsteel Prime]]*.{{wob ref|16190}}
