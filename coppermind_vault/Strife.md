**Strife** is a pilot in the [[DDF]] on [[Detritus]]. It is unclear whether Strife is a callsign or this pilot's name.

Strife fought against the [[varvax|Krell]] in the [[Battle of Alta]] and did not survive the battle, however, if they did, they would be considered a [[First Citizen]]. It is unclear which flight Strife belonged to or how they died, however, it is possible that they were a part of [[Hope Flight]].

[[Ironsides]] may have greatly disliked Strife, however, it is unclear.
