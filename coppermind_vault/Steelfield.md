A **steelfield** is a [[Scadrial|Scadrian]] invention that grants their ships enhanced maneuverability. Steelfields seem to only be usable in space.{{book ref|iote|35}} Their most common use is in war, where a steelfield, with proper starship anchors placed inside it, enables the Scadrian starships, such as the [[MW-232]], to outmaneuver any ordinary ship.{{book ref|iote|38}} However, it appears that a steelfield is also incredibly useful outside of battle, such as for moving large objects around.{{book ref|iote|48}}
