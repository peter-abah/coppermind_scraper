**Garha** is a hot caffeinated drink created in [[Fjorden]] on [[Sel]].

[[Sarene]] drinks some garha with [[Roial]] and [[Lukel]] while recuperating from an incident involving the [[Jeskeri Mysteries]]. It is likely that the drink is popular in [[Teod]], as Sarene knew that it would help her stay awake.
