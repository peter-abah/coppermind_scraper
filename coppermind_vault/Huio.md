**Huio** is a [[Herdazian]] member of [[Bridge Four]] on [[Roshar]] during the [[Era of Solitude]] and a [[Windrunner]] of the Third Ideal.{{book ref|sa2|46}}

## Appearance and Personality
{{sidequote
|Short joke? Be glad not short temper.
|Huio to [[Lopen]]{{book ref|sa3|111}}
|side=right|size=250px
}}
Huio is one of [[Lopen]]'s cousins. He is a short,{{book ref|sa3|111}} bald,{{book ref|sa3.5|11}} beefy man with thick arms.{{book ref|sa2|46}} He is known among Bridge Four for his propensity to visit prostitutes.{{book ref|sa3|35}} His lack of fluency in Alethi is a source of embarrassment to him,{{book ref|sa3|55}} though he gets a sense of satisfaction and pride out of his cooking skill.{{book ref|sa3|37}} He enjoys eating sandwiches with the bread in the middle.{{book ref|sa3.5|5}} He is a curious and scientifically minded person, and has a fondness for taking apart fabrials to experiment with them and better understand how they work, though this frequently results in him breaking the fabrial beyond repair.{{book ref|sa3.5|7}}

## Abilities and Attributes
Huio is a [[Windrunner]], quick to pick up on how to draw in [[Stormlight]]. This allows him to use Stormlight and access the [[Surge]]s of [[Surgebinding#Gravitation|Gravitation]] and [[Surgebinding#Adhesion|Adhesion]]. He is also fairly skilled in field medicine--at least, more so than [[Teft]]--and a more than adept cook.{{book ref|sa3|i|4}}{{book ref|sa3|37}} After Huio swore the Third Ideal of the Windrunners, his spren [[Caelinora]] gained the ability to become a [[Shardblade]], which can be summoned in the form that he chooses, including a hammer.{{book ref|sa3.5|18}} Along with [[Rock]] and [[Lopen]], Huio is one of the only members of Bridge Four that can swim.{{book ref|sa3.5|3}} He is an [[arcanist]] and familiar with Old Iriali.{{book ref|iote|30}}

## History
Late in 1173, Lopen brought Huio and another of his cousins, [[Rod (Roshar)|Rod]], to join Bridge Four.{{book ref|sa2|46}} He ended up often being on kitchen duty with [[Rock]], requesting the duty more and more as the others became squires. Huio once added a handful of [[lazbo]] to some [[shiki]] he was stirring for Rock, much to Rock's horror. When Rock tasted it, however, he realized Huio had improved on the original recipe. He even began to ask Huio's advice on some recipes.{{book ref|sa3|37}} Because of this, he was one of the most capable members of Bridge Four to help Rock when cooking.{{book ref|sa5|33}}

Huio also pulled shifts guarding the Kholins with the rest of Bridge Four.{{book ref|sa3|104}} He was part of the group that went with [[Dalinar]] to [[Thaylen City]] to check on [[Amaram]]'s progress helping rebuild the city from the damage from the [[Everstorm]] and to attend a conference with Queen [[Fen]] and the other monarchs who had joined their coalition. The soldiers from Bridge Four were with Dalinar in the conference, so they heard the translation of the [[Eila Stele]]; this demoralized the group enough that they left Dalinar behind and returned to [[Urithiru]] on their own.{{book ref|sa3|111}} When they returned to the Bridge Four barracks, they discovered the aftermath of [[Taravangian]]'s plot to steal back [[Jezrien]]'s [[Honorblade]]: Rock and [[Bisig]] were gravely injured, and [[Eth]] was dead. Huio, the best field medic present, attended to Rock until they could get him to [[Renarin]].{{book ref|sa3|i|14}}

When he began earning a stipend as a Radiant, he sent most of it to his family to help out the poorer cousins. A large part of this went to [[Rod (Roshar)|Rod]]'s family.{{book ref|sa3.5|7}}

### Expedition to Aimia
A few months after the [[Battle of Thaylen Field]], Lopen and Huio were chosen by [[Kaladin]] to join the Thaylen [[expedition to Akinah]] aboard the *[[Wandersail (1174 ship)|Wandersail]]* as they were capable of swimming,{{book ref|sa3.5|4}} and did not yet have their Blades.{{book ref|sa3.5|13}} Huio spent the early portions of the trip assisting the crew with shipboard duties.{{book ref|sa3.5|5}} Because of Huio's struggles with Alethi, Lopen frequently pulled his cousin into conversations to translate jokes. Huio tinkered with Rushu's spanreed and the aluminum foil - an activity that ultimately resulted in the discovery of aluminum-modified [[fabrial]]s.{{book ref|sa3.5|7}} 

After discovering [[Nikli]]'s plot to sabotage the expedition, Lopen and Huio were tasked with flying the porter to shore for incarceration.{{book ref|sa3.5|10}}

At some point during Lopen's absence, Huio began fighting the large Yu-thorak that was attacking the *Wandersail*. Lopen arrived just in time to rescue Huio from its jaws. The two fought the creature to give the crew time to escape. After Lopen fell to the ground injured when his assault failed, Huio swore the Third Ideal to save Lopen and summoned [[Caelinora]] as a Shardhammer to shatter the monster’s arm. Shortly after, Huio killed it with a Shardspear through the head. The two escaped while [[Rysn Ftori|Rysn's]] negotiation continued underground.{{book ref|sa3.5|15}}

Back at the ship, Lopen and Huio discussed the Third Ideal. Lopen congratulated his cousin on beating him to the ideal, although struggled to do so as he had an inkling that Huio's oath had been related to him and thought Huio might harbor ill will for him. Huio, however, explained that he did not hate Lopen and that the Third Ideal didn't have to involve hatred - although the constant ribbing and teasing from his cousin could be a bit much.

### After the Expedition

Following Kaladin's retirement, Huio and a majority of the Bridge Four Windrunners joined Dalinar's forces on the battlefield in [[Emul]].{{book ref|sa4|23}} Huio played cards with his cousin, Lopen, while not in combat.{{book ref|sa4|54}} Huio, along with the rest of Bridge Four, later gathered together for [[Teft|Teft's]] funeral.{{book ref|sa4|114}}

At some point after returning from the expedition to Aimia, Huio went into business with Rysn. Together, they registered a Thaylen patent for aluminum-modified fabrial technology.{{book ref|sa5|i|15}}

Many years later, Huio becomes associated with a number of [[arcanist]]s at the [[Silverlight]] university. He, [[Argent]], and [[Jess (Silverlight)|Jess]] are mentioned by [[Eddlin]] when looking to verify an ancient Iriali map.{{book ref|iote|30}}
