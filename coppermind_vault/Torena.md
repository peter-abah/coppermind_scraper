**Torena** is a noble in the Arelene court on [[Sel]].{{book ref|Elantris|14}}

She is daughter of Earl [[Ahan]] and Lady [[Seaden]].{{book ref|Elantris|14}} She is usually a quiet member of the court gatherings, but helps start the fencing lessons, being the first volunteer.{{book ref|Elantris|17}} She starts courting [[Shuden]] prior to the [[invasion of Arelon]].{{book ref|Elantris|26}}{{book ref|Elantris|32}}{{book ref|Elantris|44}} She participated in Sarene's [[Widow's Trial]], and is the first of the women to participate.{{book ref|Elantris|25}}{{book ref|Elantris|26}} She and [[Kaise]] act as Sarene's train-bearers during her marriage to [[Roial]].{{book ref|Elantris|38}} She dances and rides with Shuden.{{book ref|Elantris|32}}{{book ref|Elantris|47}}

She disappeared after her father betrayed the group, and only reappeared before the battle.{{book ref|Elantris|56}}{{book ref|Elantris|58}} She attends the funeral with her father.{{book ref|Elantris|epilogue}}

## Appearance and Personality
She's a quiet member of the court ladies, and Sarene considers her to be intelligent. She has a surprisingly slim figure given her parents' weight.{{book ref|Elantris|14}} Her hair is red-gold.{{book ref|Elantris|17}}

## Trivia
* Torena was originally meant to be revealed as [[Eventeo]]’s spy in [[Arelon]] but this was eventually cut as it distracted from the book's climax.{{wob ref|2195}} It is unclear if she was still Eventeo's spy in the final draft.
