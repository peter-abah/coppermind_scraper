**Kenton Street** is a street in [[Luthadel]] on [[Scadrial]].

==Location==
Kenton Street is in north-central Luthadel. It connects to a notable fountain square known as the [[Square of the Survivor]].

==History==
Prior to the [[Collapse]], Kenton Street was relatively quiet. It housed shops intended exclusively for the [[noble|nobility]], including tailoring and jewelry shops. During the rebellion against the [[Lord Ruler]], Lord [[Renoux]] (actually a disguised [[OreSeur]]) proposed that [[Vin]] buy new earrings from Kenton Street to replace [[Vin's earring|the stud that she always wore]]. Vin opposed the idea and chose to keep her earring.

After the Collapse, [[Elend Venture]] abolished the restrictions on [[skaa]] merchants and wages. As a result, Kenton Street became a lively market, with merchants employing criers and jugglers to try to attract attention for their wares.

During the [[Siege of Luthadel]], [[Tindwyl]] took [[Allrianne]] and Vin dress-shopping on Kenton Street. At the time, Vin noted that the merchants were charging as high as five [[boxing]]s for a box of old apples. Later during the siege, Elend ordered [[Hammond]] to gather the Kenton Street clothing merchants and make them come see the skaa refugees, hoping that the poor conditions would convince the merchants to lower their prices.
