The ***Defiant*** is a [[DDF]] Phoenix-class battlecarrier captained by [[Becca Nightshade]]. It serves as the flagship of the Defiant coalition in their attack on the [[Superiority]] forces at [[Evensong]].{{book ref|sky4|30}} It was destroyed with Becca Nightshade on it, killing her.{{book ref|sky4|46}}

The ship was launched from the surface of [[Detritus]], able to maneuver past the planet's platforms. It was equipped with bays for fighters and the bridge{{book ref|sky4|30}}, long-range, ultra-speed missiles, and could communicate via cytonics and radio communication.{{book ref|sky4|32}} The ship also had shields like starfighters.{{book ref|sky4|39}}

While similar to the original *Defiant,* the bridge of the DDF ship had a slightly different layout. The bridge crew had multiple different stations with a leather command chair at the center. The ship was fabricated on Detritus centuries before the battle, with the DDF rebuilding it. Because of this, the ship was equipped with unchangeable protocols that required a human to be in command instead of an AI. Officers aboard the ship included navigation{{book ref|sky4|34}} and communication officers.{{book ref|sky4|39}}
