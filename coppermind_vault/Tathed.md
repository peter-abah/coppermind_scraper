**Tathed** is a [[Terrisman]] who lives in [[The Village]] on [[Scadrial]].

## History
When a constable came to the Village to speak to Elder [[Vwafendal]] about the recent arson in the area, [[Idashwy]] overheard Tathed asking about the situation. She later told [[Waxillium Ladrian]] what she had heard.
