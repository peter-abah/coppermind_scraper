**Gabrathin** is a scholar on [[Roshar]] during the [[Era of Solitude]].

Nothing of Gabrathin's works is known, but they likely had to do with logic or the related arts. He was a prolific writer.

In 1173, Princess [[Jasnah Kholin]], upon interviewing Brightness [[Shallan Davar]] to be her ward, wanted Shallan to have already read Gabrathin's works, but Shallan knew nothing of Gabrathin. Shallan later went to [[Artmyrn]]'s bookstore and requested a book by Gabrathin.
