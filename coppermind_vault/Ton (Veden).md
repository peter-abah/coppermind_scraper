**Ton** is a [[Bav]] mine worker on [[Roshar]] who once encountered [[Took]] and [[Szeth]].{{book ref|sa1|i|3}}

==Appearance and Personality==
Like other miners, Ton is often covered in dirt and grime. He likes to drink beer in a local tavern when he is not working. He has a Bav accent and swears on the [[Dustmother]]'s name.{{book ref|sa1|i|3}}

==History==
Ton lived in the town of [[Ironsway]] in Bavland and worked in the nearby mines. He was one of the miners drinking with [[Amark]] when Took and [[Szeth]] visited a tavern in town. Took held Szeth's [[Oathstone]] and boasted about it to the miners. Took ordered Szeth to pour Ton's beer over his own head, but Ton did not want to waste his beer. Ton was surprised by Szeth's manner of speaking, as he was not familiar with people from [[Shinovar]].{{book ref|sa1|i|3}}
