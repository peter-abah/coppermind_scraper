**Olin Tober** is a professor at [[Elendel University]] on [[Scadrial]]. He is knowledgable about modern and ancient dog breeds, and wrote multiple letters to [[Soonie Industries]] and [[The Two Seasons]] broadsheet about the inaccuracy in the depiction of the Ascendant Warrior’s wolfdog [[kandra]], stating it was historically inaccurate to make it a wolfhound instead of a wolf dog, since wolfhounds did not exist pre-Catacendre. He released a "Tober's accurate Soonie Pup", which sold for a fivespin with a trade-in of the other kind of Soonie pup, and sponsored [[Nicki Savage]]'s broadsheet column with it. His version included a patented zippered pocket, presumably inspired by the ability of kandra to store items within their bodies.{{file ref|The Two Seasons broadsheet.jpg}}
