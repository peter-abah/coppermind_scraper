**Schrib Welfor** is an author and a working man in [[Bilming]] on [[Scadrial]]. The Bilming native wrote a novel called *The Man Who Electrified Time*, which was advertised to be available in all "fine" bookstores on the front page of *[[The Two Seasons]]*, as published on Marewill 19, {{date|348|PC}}.{{file ref|The Two Seasons broadsheet.jpg|*The Two Seasons* broadsheet}}
