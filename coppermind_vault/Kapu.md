**Kapu** is a youth living in the Eelakin capital city on [[First of the Sun]]. At some point in time, shortly before the Drominad perpendicularity became publicly available, he visited the park that had been constructed to showcase the original state of the [[Pantheon]] Islands. Here, he listened to a [[loremother]], named [[Frond]], who already knew his name.  She told him about the story of [[Cakoban]], which Kapu had clearly heard before, being able to summarise it for another girl. He did ask Frond how an ocean could be endless however, not understanding that part. After she was done with her story, she sent him back to his parents, giving him a piece of candy.
