> Your demands are about as reasonable as the ones made of the Ten Heralds on Proving Day!
> — An exclamation by [[Shallan]]

It is possible that **Proving Day** was how the [[Herald]]s were chosen from among the population of [[Roshar]].

It is implied that the tasks asked of the Heralds were difficult, if not impossible.

==Trivia==
Proving Day is an equivalent of [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bar_and_Bat_Mitzvah Bar Mitzvah] in [[Vorin]] mythology.
