**Burl** is a [[bronze]] [[Misting]] (aka a [[Seeker]]) from [[Scadrial]] in the employ of [[Kelesina Shores]]. 

==History==
Burl was at or near Kelesina Shores's mansion during [[Kelesina Shores' party|her party]] in [[New Seran]] and was tasked with keeping watch for the use of metals. During the party, [[Wax]] used a coin to [[Steel]]push his way onto a balcony in order to covertly follow Kelesina and eavesdrop on her.  [[Kelesina Shores's steward|Kelesina's Terriswoman steward]] soon received a report from Burl that someone had used metals in the area. Although Burl worked for Kelesina, it is not clear if he was aware of her association with the [[Set]].
