**Dryport** is a city in the [[Elendel Basin]] on [[Scadrial]].{{map ref|Elendel Basin}}

It lies a short distance east of [[Elendel]] on the [[Irongate River]].{{map ref|Elendel Basin}} The etymology of its name is unclear, as it is not considered to be a major port;{{book ref|mb6|15}} there may be a connection to another city in the Basin named "[[Drypost]]".{{map ref|Elendel Basin}}

It has a dedicated branch line of the Basin's railway system.{{map ref|Elendel Basin}} [[Waxillium Ladrian]] boards a train in Dryport and confronts [[Edwarn Ladrian]] after deducing his location using coded notes taken by [[Miles Dagouter]].{{book ref|mb4|epilogue}}
