The **Truthwatcher monastery** is a [[Monastery of Truth]] dedicated to the [[Herald]] [[Pralla]] and associated with the [[Order of Truthwatchers]]. It is located in the northwest of [[Shinovar]].

## Location
The Truthwatcher monastery is located in the northwest of Shinovar, at the base of the western mountains.

## History
[[Vambra-daughter-Skies]] is the Truthwatcher [[Honorbearer]] during [[Szeth|Szeth's]] quest to become an Honorbearer, in roughly ca. 1157. By the time Szeth undertakes his pilgrimage to [[Cleansing of Shinovar|cleanse Shinovar]], Vambra is no longer the Truthwatcher Honorbearer and has instead been replaced by a different woman. [[Kaladin]] kills her to protect Szeth when they visit the [[Lightweaver monastery]] and Szeth picks up the Truthwatcher [[Honorblade]] from her disintegrated body. After leaving the Lightweaver monastery, Szeth, Kaladin, and Nale visit the Truthwatcher monastery as a formality, even though they have its Honorblade. From there, they head to the [[Dustbringer monastery]].
