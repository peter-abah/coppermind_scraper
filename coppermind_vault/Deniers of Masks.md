The **Deniers of Masks** are a group of people from [[Southern Scadrian|southern]] [[Scadrial]]. They are likely the Maskless mentioned on the Scadrial map.

They are implied to avoid wearing the masks commonly worn by the people who live in the South. The Deniers are dangerous for some unknown reason, and [[Allik Neverfar]] believes that the [[Bands of Mourning]] could be used to fight them. This term does not refer to the inhabitants of the [[Elendel Basin]] and the surrounding areas, as the Southern Scadrians simply refer to them as barbarians.
