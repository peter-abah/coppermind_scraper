**Mosaic** is a [[Cryptic]] on [[Roshar]] bonded to [[Vathah]]. In [[Shadesmar]], her head pattern has sharp lines in it and often includes rapid sections that look like [[women's script]].{{book ref|sa4|26}}

In {{Rosharan date|1175}}, Vathah and Mosaic were chosen by [[Shallan Davar]] to accompany an ambassadorial group from the [[coalition of monarchs]].{{book ref|sa4|20}} Mosaic and the other Cryptics enjoyed watching Shallan sketch on the journey, though their unwavering attention and delight in her mistakes unsettled the [[Order of Lightweavers|Lightweaver]].{{book ref|sa4|26}} The group entered Shadesmar at [[Urithiru]] and traveled to [[Lasting Integrity]], where the [[honorspren]] denied most of them entry.{{book ref|sa4|21}}{{book ref|sa4|36}} It is unclear if Vathah and Mosaic then remained camped outside the fortress or if they returned to Urithiru with news of [[Adolin Kholin]]'s impending trial.{{book ref|sa4|75}}
