> — Mosaic

**Mosaic** is a [[Cryptic]] on [[Roshar]] bonded to [[Vathah]]. In [[Shadesmar]], her head pattern has sharp lines in it and often includes rapid sections that look like [[women's script]].

In , Vathah and Mosaic were chosen by [[Shallan Davar]] to accompany an ambassadorial group from the [[coalition of monarchs]]. Mosaic and the other Cryptics enjoyed watching Shallan sketch on the journey, though their unwavering attention and delight in her mistakes unsettled the [[Order of Lightweavers|Lightweaver]]. The group entered Shadesmar at [[Urithiru]] and traveled to [[Lasting Integrity]], where the [[honorspren]] denied most of them entry. It is unclear if Vathah and Mosaic then remained camped outside the fortress or if they returned to Urithiru with news of [[Adolin Kholin]]'s impending trial.
