***Light and Gemstones*** is a book on [[Roshar]] written by Chanosha. It is presumably about how [[Stormlight]] and [[polestone]]s can be used to make [[fabrial]]s.

[[Navani Kholin]] acquired a worn copy of *Light and Gemstones* not long after she met [[Gavilar]], [[Dalinar]], [[Torol]], and [[Ialai]]. The book was still in her study in [[Kholinar]] shortly after the [[Alethi]] discovered the [[listener]]s on the [[Shattered Plains]]. She later noted the book in her study when [[Odium]] tormented her with visions from her past during her trip to the [[Spiritual Realm]].
