> I coated the bolt’s tip with black blood. I did that right, didn’t I? That way, the poison will kill him for sure.
> — [[Sebruki]], to [[Silence]]

**Blackblood** is the name of a poison used on [[Threnody]]. [[Sebruki]] coats a crossbow bolt with it, intending to kill [[Chesterton Divide]].
