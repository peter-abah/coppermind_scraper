**Tobias** is a member of [[Red Young]]'s team of bounty hunters from the [[Fortress Town]]s of Threnody.{{book ref|sfs|2}}

==History==
Red took a search party of fortfolk to the [[Forests of Hell]] hunting for [[Chesterton Divide]], hoping to collect the bounty on his head. Red mentioned that Tobias had gone out scouting ahead of the group and had discovered that [[Silence]] and [[William Ann Montane]] had already killed Chesterton.{{book ref|sfs|2}} Tobias was presumably present when the gang found Silence and took Chesterton's body; all of the fortfolk later fell victim to a booby trap placed by Silence and were killed by [[shade]]s.{{book ref|sfs|2}}
