**Tashgarn** is a Yazed-im [[Fused]] on [[Roshar]]. He is considered to be petty, villainous and single-minded. While on a boring mission, he is often sleepy and cranky. Tashgarn has also has a hard time staying awake after dark. During the [[True Desolation]], this slow-moving but menacing Fused was deployed to [[Herdaz]]. There, he was assigned to oversee a small village named [[Hurziko]], far away from the front lines. He resented this post, as he wanted to lead battles in the vanguard again. To demoralise the Herdazians, Tashgarn announced that he planned to slaughter the village’s prized hog ‘[[Highprince Sadeas (hog)|Highprince Sadeas]]’ for his breakfast.{{rpg ref|ss|9}} While it is known that a group of people attempted to stop him, it is unclear whether or not they succeeded.
