**Lemex's nurse** is a [[Terris]]woman [[worldhopper]] who acts as a nurse to [[Lemex]] when he is poisoned during the [[Pahn Kahl rebellion]] on [[Nalthis]].{{wob ref|3092}}{{book ref|Warbreaker|10}}

When [[Vivenna]] came to visit Lemex on his deathbed, his solemn nurse told her that nothing more could be done to help him. Vivenna dismissed the nurse before Lemex gave her all of his [[Breath]]s.{{book ref|Warbreaker|10}} With the aid of [[Denth]]'s crew, the nurse disposed of Lemex's body later that night.{{book ref|Warbreaker|13}} Denth later mentioned that he paid off the nurse while dealing with Lemex's property, claiming that she had not really known who Lemex was anyway.{{book ref|Warbreaker|19}}

## Trivia
* When asked about background worldhoppers, Brandon frequently referred to a Terriswoman without revealing any other details about her.{{wob ref|10409}}{{wob ref|10546}}{{wob ref|6349}}{{wob ref|2105}}{{wob ref|10496}} After some time, fans narrowed down the possibilities and directly asked if Lemex's nurse was this Terriswoman worldhopper.{{wob ref|6354}}{{wob ref|1815}} Brandon has confirmed that she is, although there is nothing in the text of *Warbreaker* to clue readers into her mysterious identity.{{wob ref|3092}} 
* Brandon has implied that she has access to some form of [[Investiture]].{{wob ref|10496}}
* Fans continue to speculate about the nurse's identity, often mentioning [[Aslydin]]{{wob ref|3358}}{{wob ref|6493}} or [[Axindweth]],{{book ref|sa4|77}} but Brandon has not confirmed anything and may still be waiting to reveal more details in a future book.{{wob ref|1815}}
