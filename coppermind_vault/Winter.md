**Winter** is a [[National Assembly Leader]].

After multiple members of the National Assembly are blown up by the [[Superiority]], Winter attempts to contact [[Jorgen Weight]] via radio. However, Jorgen commands [[Ashwin]] to blow her off by using Section 57 of the [[Defiant Defense Force|DDF]] Communications Policy, which gives the DDF three days to respond to her.
