The **Highbrary** is the main office of the [[Librarian]]s.

It is located in a bunker under the city of [[wikipedia: Washington, D.C.|Washington, D.C.]] and is disguised as the [[:Wikipedia: Library of Congress|Library of Congress]]. It is possibly an attempt by the Librarians to replicate the [[Library of Alexandria]]. It was destroyed by [[Leavenworth Smedry]], who initiated the self-destruct sequence thinking that higher ranking Librarians would turn it off. Unfortunately, [[Biblioden]] made it so that the Highbrary would, in fact, be destroyed.
