The **Plains of Thrist** are a large area of land in the [[Blackened Lands]], covering a good portion of the south-east of the area. The [[Quiet River]] runs along the southern side of the plains. The [[Warfields of Karkun]] and [[Spectre's Watch]] are both found nearby.{{map ref|Mirandus}}
