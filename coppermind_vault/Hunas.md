The **Hunas** were a group of people who entirely destroyed Rome in the [[Earth (Frugal Wizard)|alternate medieval dimension]].{{book ref|fwh|15}}

After rescuing [[Yazad (Frugal Wizard)|Yazad]] from a group of [[Hordamen]], [[Runian]] asks him about the history of Christianity in that dimension. Yazad tells him how the Romans tried to crucify [[Yeshua]], but was rescued in time by [[Ahura Mazda]]. Yazad notes that that happened many centuries ago, before the Hunas completely destroyed Rome.{{book ref|fwh|15}}

## Trivia
*"Hunas" or "Huna" was the name given by real-world ancient Indians to a group of occupying Central Asian tribes.{{wp ref|Huna people}}
