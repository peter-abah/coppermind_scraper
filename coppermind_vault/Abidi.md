**Abidi** the Monarch is a [[Fused]] of the [[Shanay-im]]  on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa5|9}}

## Appearance and Personality
{{sidequote|Abidi the Monarch, she recognized by the patterns. Unlucky, to encounter him of all Heavenly Ones. Abidi liked pain.|[[Shalash]] upon recognizing Abidi{{book ref|sa5|84}}|side=right|size=350px}}
Abidi's face looks almost like a white glyph, with only specks of black showing through.{{book ref|sa5|9}} He announces to his enemies that he will one day rule the world,{{book ref|sa5|9}} and he is recognized by others (including [[Shalash]]) as a particularly cruel Heavenly One who likes pain.{{book ref|sa5|84}} He wants glory, as after [[Adolin Kholin]] wounds him he refuses to return to [[Braize]] so that he can come back healed, as he wants to personally oversee the fall of [[Azimir]].{{book ref|sa5|41}} Abidi believes bathing in the blood of Radiants quiets the voices of a thousand years of pain in his mind, thereby keeping him sane.{{book ref|sa5|63}} He is very confident, and refuses to believe Adolin is not a Radiant when Adolin bests him in combat.{{book ref|sa5|63}}{{book ref|sa5|133}}

## Attributes and Abilities
Abidi is one of the [[Shanay-im]].{{book ref|sa5|9}} This allows him to use the Surge of [[Gravitation]].{{book ref|sa4|2}}{{book ref|sa5|9}} Like all Fused, he can also breathe in [[Voidlight]]; this allows him to heal from most wounds, such as being stabbed through the chest,{{book ref|sa5|11}} as long as he has Voidlight.{{book ref|sa5|9}} Abidi is one of the best Fused duelists and can fight with [[aluminum]]-lined maces{{book ref|sa5|41}} and even a [[Shardblade]], despite there not being dead Shardblades before the [[Recreance]].{{book ref|sa5|130}}

## History

Abidi's actions throughout the first part of the [[True Desolation]] are unknown. He is first encountered in [[Shadesmar]] by [[Shallan]], Adolin, and their companions when he ambushes them as they travel from [[Lasting Integrity]] to the [[Azimir]] [[Oathgate]] on day one of the final ten days before the [[Contest of champions]].{{book ref|sa5|7}}{{book ref|sa5|9}}{{book ref|sa5|11}} The assault was an uncharacteristically direct and lethal attack for the Shanay-im, and Abidi nearly kills Shallan in the bead ocean.{{book ref|sa5|9}}{{book ref|sa5|11}} She wounds him by manifesting her illusions physically, and Adolin finishes him off by stabbing him between two pieces of carapace, forcing him to flee.{{book ref|sa5|9}}{{book ref|sa5|11}} While not deadly, the strike cracks his gemheart, preventing him from flying unless he returns to Braize to be reborn.{{book ref|sa5|41}}{{book ref|sa5|129}}

Abidi next encounters Adolin on the battlefield where he commands singer forces in the assault on Azimir.{{book ref|sa5|41}}{{book ref|sa5|95}} He and Adolin face off several times as his army slowly progresses through their siege of the oathgate platform.{{book ref|sa5|41}}{{book ref|sa5|63}} On day seven of the final ten days before the Contest of Champions his forces break out of the platform and Abidi encounters Shalash and [[Talenel]].{{book ref|sa5|84}} Shalash recognizes him as an especially sadistic fused when he orders the slaughter of the wounded in a hospital.{{book ref|sa5|84}} He escapes in terror when Taln suddenly awakens and slays many of his fellow Fused.{{book ref|sa5|84}} On day nine, Abidi's forces are able to kill the Shardbearer using Adolin’s [[Shardplate]] and Abidi claims the armor for himself.{{book ref|sa5|125}} By the end of day nine, his forces break through the Azish defences and take the city.{{book ref|sa5|125}}

On day ten, Abidi lays a trap for Adolin, [[Yanagawn]], and their companions in the [[Bronze Palace]] throne room to stop them from sitting the emperor on his throne.{{book ref|sa5|128}}{{book ref|sa5|129}}{{book ref|sa5|130}} Adolin confronts him there and is nearly defeated by Abidi when he is  unable to summon [[Maya]] as a Shardblade because of the room’s aluminum lining.{{book ref|sa5|130}} However, Adolin’s platespren return to him at the last moment, stripping Abidi of his stolen Plate.{{book ref|sa5|133}} In the ensuing reversal, Adolin kills Abidi, throwing him through the doors to the throne room and crushing his ribcage with a plate enhanced stomp.{{book ref|sa5|135}}
