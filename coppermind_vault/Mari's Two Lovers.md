**"Mari's Two Lovers"** is a humorous song on [[Roshar]] about a woman named Mari and her relationship with two twin brothers. Mari believed that the brothers were actually the same person.{{book ref|sa1|27}}

[[Dunny]] and [[Rock]] sang this song while [[Bridge Four]] was on chasm duty.{{book ref|sa1|27}}
