**Ravamak** is an alcoholic drink on Scadrial. It appears to come from one of the various [[Southern Scadrian]] peoples, likely the [[Malwish Empire|Malwish]]. It has an incredibly high alcohol percentage, with some people experiencing a headache for three days, or beginning to talk gibberish after a single cup.
