**Apinya Pan** is a woman who bought a personal wizard dimension for their own use. The [[The Frugal Wizard's Handbook for Surviving Medieval England (in-world)|Frugal Wizard's Handbook]] details how she set up a free city in her dimension, with modern advancements such as health care and better food, to bring security and stability to millions in a time of darkness. Her story was documented in 'Case Studies in Hope: Ten People Who Changed the World in Their Personal Wizard Dimensions', which was also released in a limited illustrated edition.{{frugal iw ref|FAQ:What If I’m Still Worried about the Ethics of Essentially Colonizing the British Isles, Influencing the Course of History for an Entire People?}}
