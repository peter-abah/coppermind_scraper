***Nearer the Flame*** is a fictional work on [[Roshar]]. It is a first person novel and follows a man who descends into insanity after watching his children starve.{{book ref|TWoK|8}} It very likely is not considered a romance novel and can be purchased from the [[Artmyrn]]'s bookshop in Kharbranth.{{book ref|sa1|8}}

[[Shallan]] sarcastically requested a copy of it from Artmyrn, who tries to sell her romance novels, instead of books she requested.{{book ref|sa1|8}}
