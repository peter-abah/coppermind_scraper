**Jess** is a [[skaa]] from the [[Tresting plantation]] in the [[Final Empire]] on [[Scadrial]].{{book ref|mb1|prologue}}

She had an attractive daughter and feared that she would eventually be taken by [[Themos Tresting]]. Tresting did send for the girl, but she was rescued when [[Kelsier]] killed him and the rest of the plantation staff.{{book ref|mb1|prologue}}
