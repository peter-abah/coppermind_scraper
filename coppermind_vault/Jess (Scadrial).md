**Jess** is a [[skaa]] from the [[Tresting plantation]] in the [[Final Empire]] on [[Scadrial]].

She had an attractive daughter and feared that she would eventually be taken by [[Themos Tresting]]. Tresting did send for the girl, but she was rescued when [[Kelsier]] killed him and the rest of the plantation staff.
