**Ukrit** is a [[National Assembly Leader]].

Ukrit confronted [[Ironsides]], along with NALs [[Algernon Weight|Weight]] and [[Valda Mendez|Mendez]], about her leadership of the [[Defiant Defense Force]], taking particular issue with the number of damaged ships that require repair. During the meeting, Ironside proposed that the DDF send their entire force of fighters to capture a falling shipyard full of acclivity rings.
