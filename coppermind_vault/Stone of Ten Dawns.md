The **Stone of Ten Dawns** is a legendary [[perfect gemstone]] on [[Roshar]].

According to [[Mraize]], during the days of the original [[Knights Radiant|Radiants]] some [[honorspren]]&mdash;who are known to covet perfect gemstones&mdash;believed the stories of the Stone of Ten Dawns and spent lifetimes hunting it. However, [[Kaladin]] asked the [[Reacher]] [[Ico]] about the Stone of Ten Dawns during a discussion of the value of perfect gemstones in [[Shadesmar]], and Ico was not familiar with it.

[[Vstim]] told [[Rysn]] that a large perfect ruby known as the [[King's Drop]] was rumored to be a piece of the Stone of Ten Dawns. The rumor implies that the Stone was shattered at some point.
