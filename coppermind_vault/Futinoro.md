**Futinoro** is a city in [[Nagadan]] on [[Komashi]] that is completely destroyed by a force of several dozen stable [[nightmare]]s thirty years prior to the [[battle of Kilahito]].{{book ref|yumi|7}}

To the people of Nagadan, the attack is believed to have been the consequence of [[painter]]s not showing proper vigilance at their jobs. It stands as a warning sign to the dangers of not halting nightmares before they become fully stable. According to [[Liyun]], who is present during the city's destruction, the attack is directly orchestrated by the [[father machine]] to curb the efforts of some [[hijo|spirits]] that managed to contact the residents of Futinoro. The machine has the entire city destroyed in order to prevent the truth from being discovered.{{book ref|yumi|36}}
