**Takanda** is a [[nightmare]] [[painter]] in [[Kilahito]] on [[Komashi]]. When [[Nikaro]] was looking for help in [[battle of Kilahito|defending Kilahito from a coordinated attack]] by the nightmares, [[Tojin]] proposed asking Takanda and his team of painters to join them because Takanda owed him a favor.{{book ref|yumi|38}}
