**Gregorh** is a young king that ruled an unknown land on [[Roshar]].

## History
Gregorh became king of his country at a very young age. He began a pointless war with other kingdoms that had once been allies of his father. The history of Gregorh and his foolish actions was infamous during the [[Era of Solitude]].

In 1173, Brightness [[Shallan Davar]] made reference to Gregorh to prove the point to Princess [[Jasnah Kholin]] that youthful immaturity is not always a catalyst for positive change.
