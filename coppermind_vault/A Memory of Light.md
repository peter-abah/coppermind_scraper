***A Memory of Light*** is the fourteenth and final volume of [[The Wheel of Time]] series by Robert Jordan, which [[Brandon Sanderson]] was chosen to complete after Jordan's death. It is also the third part of what was to be one book of the same name, but which grew too large and had to be split into three parts.{{ref|name=torpr}}{{ref|name=splitting-a-memory-of-light}} It was released on January 8, 2013.
