**Thomas York** is the principal of [[Armedius Academy]].

Despite being in charge of one of the eight Rithmatic Academies of the [[United Isles of America]], he is notably not a [[Rithmatics|Rithmatist]]. 

He had a close relationship with [[Trent Saxon]], [[Joel Saxon|Joel's]] father. After Trent died in a [[springrail]] accident, he awarded Joel a scholarship for free tuition so he could afford to attend Armedius.
