The **Song of Histories** is one of the songs detailing the oral history of the [[listener]]s. It is the way in which the listeners are able to keep their history and pass it on to the younger generations.{{epigraph ref|sa2|30}}{{epigraph ref|sa2|25}} It is at least 127 stanzas long.{{epigraph ref|sa2|25}} Both the [[Song of Renunciation]] and the [[Song of Destruction]] contain pieces that are part of the Song of Histories, but the Song of Histories is slightly more complicated.{{book ref|sa5|112}}
