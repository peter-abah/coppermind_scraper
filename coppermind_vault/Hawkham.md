**Hawkham** is an [[Epic]] from [[Wikipedia: Boston|Boston]]. He uses force redirection to survive bulletfire, and the bullets that bounce off him are usually ripped apart in midair. [[Newton]], however, is able to change the direction of the bullets instead.
