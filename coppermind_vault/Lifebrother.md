The **Lifebrother** is a [[spren]] from [[Stone Shamanism]] that [[Taravangian]] and [[Pozen-son-Nash|Pozen]] reference. 

Pozen indicates that the Lifebrother is part of the [[Shin]] [[Honorbearer]] chain of command and refers to the Lifebrother as the "spren of soil." The placement in the chain of command above the Honorbearers and [[Heralds]] indicates the Lifebrother is highly revered in Stone Shamanism.{{book ref|sa5|57}}

Taravangian wonders if the Lifebrother sent Szeth to him perhaps because the position of [[Truthless]] is unique to [[Shinovar]] or the Lifebrother is associated with good fortune. Szeth's response to Taravangian about the Lifebrother which mentions Vorin gods is most likely not associating the Lifebrother with [[Vorinism]], but rather refuting Tarvangian's claim that the Lifebrother would send Szeth to perform these bloody acts.{{book ref|sa1|71}}
