Brother **Tadet** was an ardent with [[House Kholin]] on the [[Shattered Plains]].

He was short and [[Dalinar]] asked him to individually interview the ten soldiers that [[Torol Sadeas]] questioned to find out the exact words Sadeas had used and any other details.
