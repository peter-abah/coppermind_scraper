The **Night** is an ancient [[spren]] on [[Roshar]] who was created by [[Adonalsium]] alongside the planet, the [[Wind]], and the [[Stone]]. Her role was to shroud or possibly to nurture the singers.

## Attributes and Abilities
### Nahel Bond (Historical)

Prior to the creation of the [[Nightwatcher]] from the Night, she was one of three Bondsmith spren. As such, it would follow that forming a Nahel bond with her may have granted a Bondsmith power over [[Connection]] and other Spiritual attributes, but the full scope of its effects are unknown.

## History
When [[Cultivation]] arrived on Roshar, she found the Night's song to be particularly beautiful, which helped convince her to stay on Roshar despite the fact that she and [[Honor]] had originally planned to find an uninhabited planet to settle on.

Even in the earliest days of Roshar, few people loved or even spoke of the Night. Instead, the [[singer]]s claimed her siblings Wind and Stone, as well as the spren, as their gods. As a result, the Night eventually left Roshar, though how this affected the planet or where she went are unclear. As a replacement, Cultivation created the [[Nightwatcher]] from some of the same essence.

When in a vision of the [[Recreance]], [[Dalinar Kholin]] seemed to feel the presence of the Night, though she would have already left Roshar by that time.
