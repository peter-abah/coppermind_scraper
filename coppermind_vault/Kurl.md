**Kurl** are a form of small crustacean on [[Roshar]].

They are similarly sized to [[axehound]]s, and look like a combination of a crab and a tortoise. They do not live in the [[Shattered Plains]].

According to [[Kaladin]], the male kurl watches over their eggs.

[[Shallan]] mentions seeing a man with a trained kurl who will dance and wave its arms for fish.
