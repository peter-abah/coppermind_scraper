**Jasten** is a skaa member of the [[Luthadel Assembly]], and a canal worker.

Both he and his brother [[Thurts]] vote for [[Ferson Penrod]] in the vote for king during the [[Siege of Luthadel]]. According to [[Elend Venture]], neither of them liked the [[Church of the Survivor]], and so weren't swayed by Elend's devotion to the Church.
