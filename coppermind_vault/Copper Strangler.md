The **Copper Strangler** is a serial killer that operates in [[Elendel]] on [[Scadrial]].{{book ref|mb4|9}}

## History
### Life
The Strangler was an ordinary man.{{book ref|mb4|9}} He had a good family and friends as well as a job that paid him a satisfactory wage. However, inexplicably, the Copper Strangler began killing women. He would strangle them with copper wire and then sink their bodies in the canals to hide them. He probably committed at least a few of these crimes in the Eighth Octant of Elendel.{{book ref|mb4|5}} Eventually, Lord [[Peterus]], who was a simple constable at the time, joined in the investigation and captured the Copper Strangler himself.{{book ref|mb4|8}}

### Legacy
The capture of the Copper Strangler led to acclaim, awards, and promotions for Lord Peterus,{{book ref|mb4|8}} probably resulting in his position as chief constable of the Eighth Octant.{{book ref|mb4|5}} Peterus was also invited to become a lord and a member of Elendel's high society.{{book ref|mb4|8}}

Years later, the Copper Strangler was still a case that fascinated criminologists, such as Lady [[Marasi Colms]]{{book ref|mb4|9}}. The question of why such a normal man could do such terrible things for no apparent reason still puzzled them.
