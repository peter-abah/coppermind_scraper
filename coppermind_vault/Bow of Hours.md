The [[Bow of Hours]] is a legendary bow in [[Unkalaki]] culture on [[Roshar]]. When someone is able to draw the bow, it heralds "years of change".

==History==
According to [[Cord]], her father [[Lunamor]] was able to draw the bow in the [[Horneater Peaks]] at the start of the new millenium. She mentioned that [[spren]] helped provide Lunamor the strength needed to draw the bow. She also referred to him as "the Fal'ala'liki'nor", although it is unclear how this title was related to the bow.

Lunamor's ability to draw the Bow of Hours may have been related to his use of a [[grandbow]] during the [[Battle of Thaylen Field]], but he appeared to consider that incident a violation of an unknown oath, and did not discuss it any further.
