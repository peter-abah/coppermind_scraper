**Rodeo** is an inhibitor [[taynix]] found on [[Detritus]].

Rodeo accompanies [[Arturo Mendez|Arturo]] on his mission with [[Spensa Nightshade|Spensa]] to the [[Harkil]] supply depot. Once [[Kimmalyn]] rescues the two [[Superiority]] inhibitor slugs, Rodeo puts up an inhibitor field to prevent the workers at Harkil from calling for help or getting reinforcements.
