**Kazilah** is a historical figure and possible member of the [[Knights Radiant]] mentioned in *[[Words of Radiance (in-world)|Words of Radiance]]*.{{epigraph ref|sa2|51}} They were found to be guilty of fraternizing with "unwholesome elements," possibly some sort of agent of [[Odium]], and executed for it.
