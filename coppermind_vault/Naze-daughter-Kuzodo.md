> We are uncertain the effect this will have on the parsh. At the very least, it should deny them forms of power. Melishi is confident, but Naze-daughter-Kuzodo warns of unintended side effects.
> — From drawer 30-20, fifth emerald

**Naze-daughter-Kuzodo** is a [[Shin]] woman from the time of [[Ba-Ado-Mishram]]'s capture on [[Roshar]]. She is quoted in the [[Urithiru gem archive]] and is presumably a [[Knight Radiant]].

> Your warnings in the past have been useful, Naze, but this **must** be done. Otherwise, Garith will destroy us all. Are you in?
> — [[Melishi]]

Naze warned of unintended side effects to [[Melishi]]'s plan to trap Ba-Ado-Mishram in a [[perfect gemstone]], and her warnings were taken seriously enough to warrant a mention in the gem archive in [[Urithiru]]. During [[Dalinar Kholin]]'s time in the [[Spiritual Realm]] prior to the [[contest of champions]], he witnessed a vision of the day in which Melishi trapped Ba-Ado Mishram. Naze was standing in the pavilion during one of [[Garith]]'s peace talks with the [[singers]], and Dalinar took her place for the duration of the meeting. He sat at the table with the other Knights Radiant after the singers left, and the others accepted Naze's presence there and followed Dalinar's attempts to direct the conversation. Melishi approached Naze after the talk was over to see if he had her support for his plan. Despite the warnings that Naze had given, Melishi expressed that they had to move forward. Dalinar, in Naze's place, gave his assent.
