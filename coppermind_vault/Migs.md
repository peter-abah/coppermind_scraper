**Migs** is a gang leader in [[Elendel]] on [[Scadrial]].

Migs ran a crew of eight crooks. In {{date|342|PC}}, the crew attempted a heist of some kind and things went seriously wrong. Migs shot two constables, although they survived their wounds.{{book ref|mb6|prologue}}

Migs and his gang holed up in their hideout, located in an old pub. They barricaded themselves in overnight; the constabulary attempted to negotiate, telling Migs he would not be hanged for his crimes, but he refused to surrender. In the morning, [[Waxillium Ladrian]] and [[Wayne]] were called in to raid the hideout despite the fact that Wax's wedding to [[Steris Harms]] was scheduled later in the day. Wax easily broke through the crew's barricade, drawing their gunfire and deflecting it with a [[steel]] bubble. Meanwhile, Wayne sneaked in undetected; he punched Migs and knocked out a number of his thugs (including [[Guillian]] and [[Slink]]) while Wax kicked [[Drawers]] in the chin. Migs indicated that he would rather die than be apprehended, and questioned why Wax had not killed them all with his [[Allomantic]] powers. Wax told Migs that he wasn't worth killing, and finally convinced him to surrender.{{book ref|mb6|prologue}}
