**Shimmerbay** is a port city in the [[Emerald Sea]] on [[Lumar]], and is located far from [[Kingsport]].

[[Crow]] decided to sail to Shimmerbay so [[Tress]] could "escape" when she tricked Crow into thinking that she--if she had the opportunity--would tell the [[Verdant King|king's]] men that the *[[Crow's Song]]* crew killed everyone on the *[[Oot's Dream]]*, and thus are [[deadrunner]]s. At Shimmerbay, Tress decided to become a pirate and joined the *Crow's Song* as their [[sprouter]].
