**Erinor** is a [[Order of Stonewards|Stoneward]] and the husband of [[Darcira]] on [[Roshar]]. He was ordered to help [[Shallan]] raid a [[Ghostbloods|Ghostblood]] hideout. During the raid, Erinor spoke to the stones, which gave him the impression that there was a secret exit down into the chasms. While they were exploring it, they received the signal and Erinor melted the ceiling so that the [[Order of Windrunners|Windrunners]] could enter the basement to capture the Ghostbloods.
