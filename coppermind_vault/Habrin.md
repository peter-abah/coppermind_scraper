**Habrin** is a youth of [[Hearthstone]] in [[Alethkar]] on [[Roshar]].

Habrin was born to a man named [[Arafik]]. In , when Highmarshal [[Meridas Amaram]] visited to Hearthstone to recruit men for his army, Citylord [[Roshone]] chooses Habrin to be one of the men conscripted, along with [[Agil]], [[Caull]], [[Jorna]] and [[Tien]].
