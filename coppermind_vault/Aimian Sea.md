The **Aimian Sea** is a body of water that separates the islands of [[Aimia]] from the coast of [[Shinovar]] on the continent of [[Roshar]]. It is a large, deep channel that is connected to the [[Endless Ocean]].{{map ref|Roshar}}

[[Kaza]] and the crew of the *[[First Dreams]]* crossed the Aimian Sea from [[Liafor]] in hopes of reaching [[Akinah]] and discovering a wealth of [[gemheart]]s.{{book ref|sa3|i|4}}
