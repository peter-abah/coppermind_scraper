The **Red Rockbud** is a gambling den in the [[Aladar]] warcamp on the [[Shattered Plains]] of [[Roshar]]. It is noted to be among the warcamps’ most notorious, and the den is run by a [[Bavland]]er spy named [[Nel]].{{rpg ref|slwg|164}} The Red Rockbud takes the appearance of a Soulcast bunker located at the crater rim of the Aladar warcamp. True to the den’s name, a cluster of [[rockbud]]s, whose rocky shells have been carelessly slapped with crimson paint, grow around the bunker. It is customary for anyone new to the place to first meet the boss before they are let it.{{rpg ref|slsw|41}} Shortly before the arrival of the [[Everstorm]], [[Liss]] met with a representative of the [[Stone Shamans]] at the Red Rockbud.{{rpg ref|slsw|38}}
