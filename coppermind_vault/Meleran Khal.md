Captainlord **Meleran Khal** is the youngest son of [[General Khal]] and [[Teshav]].

## Appearance
Meleran Khal is a powerfully built man who is balding by his thirties. He is around the same height as Adolin.

## History
Meleran Khal was born the youngest son of Teshav and Khal sometime around 1144, after his brothers [[Halam]] and [[Aratin]]. Little of his history is known, but it likely he accompanied the rest of his family in the [[War of Reckoning]] and followed them to [[Urithiru]].

While King [[Elhokar]] was infiltrating [[Kholinar]], he asked [[Shallan Davar]] to use Lightweaving to disguise [[Adolin Kholin]] as Meleran Khal multiple times, since the two have a similar height.
