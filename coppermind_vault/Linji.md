**Linji** is a figure who attempts to travel around the world without the use of [[Aviar]]. Presumably a figure in stories on [[First of the Sun]], it is unclear whether they are a historical figure or merely fictional. [[Hoid]] is aware of Linji's story and refers to it on at least one occasion.
