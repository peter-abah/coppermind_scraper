**Tinalar** is an [[Alethi]] [[Shardbearer]] who lived on [[Roshar]] during the [[Era of Solitude]].{{book ref|wor|14}}

Tinalar possessed a [[Shardblade]], but no [[Shardplate]].{{book ref|wor|14}} Considering the young [[Adolin Kholin]] to be merely a well-dressed fop, Tinalar dueled him for Shards, hoping to embarrass Adolin's father, [[Dalinar Kholin]], and win Adolin's Plate. However, Adolin won the duel and claimed Tinalar's Shardblade as his own.{{book ref|wor|14}} Tinalar's Blade is the same one that Adolin later came to know as [[Mayalaran]].{{book ref|sa3|120}}
