**Outer cities** is a term used for certain settlements on two planets of the [[cosmere]].
* On [[Sel]], it refers to the four smaller cities that surround Elantris, including [[Kae]]; see [[Elantris (city)#Outer Cities]].
* On [[Scadrial]], it refers to cities in the Elendel Basin other than [[Elendel]], such as [[New Seran]], [[Bilming]], and [[Rashekin]]; see [[Elendel Basin#Outer Cities]]. The term is sometimes capitalized in the Scadrial broadsheets.
