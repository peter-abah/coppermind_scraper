**Outer cities** is a term used for certain settlements on two planets of the [[cosmere]].
* On [[Sel]], it refers to the four smaller cities that surround Elantris, including [[Kae]];{{book ref|elantris|8}}{{book ref|elantris|60}} see [[Elantris (city)#Outer Cities]].
* On [[Scadrial]], it refers to cities in the Elendel Basin other than [[Elendel]], such as [[New Seran]], [[Bilming]], and [[Rashekin]];{{book ref|mb6|4}}{{book ref|mb6|epilogue}} see [[Elendel Basin#Outer Cities]]. The term is sometimes capitalized in the Scadrial broadsheets.{{file ref|House Record broadsheet.jpg|*The House Record* broadsheet}}{{file ref|New_Ascendancy_broadsheet.jpg|*The New Ascendancy* broadsheet}}
