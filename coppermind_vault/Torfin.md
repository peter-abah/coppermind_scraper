**Torfin** is a member of [[Bridge Four]] on [[Roshar]].

## Attributes and Abilities
After Kaladin swears the [[Immortal Words|Third Ideal]] of the [[Order of Windrunners]], Torfin becomes one of his [[squire]]s. He is able to breathe in [[Stormlight]] whenever Kaladin is nearby, giving him healing powers and the ability to fly using the [[Surge]] of Gravitation. Unfortunately, he is scared of heights.

By , Kaladin no longer has any squires, and "almost all" of the original Bridge Four members have bonded a [[windspren]]. This implies that Torfin is a full [[Knight Radiant]].

## History
Torfin has been a member of Bridge Four since its origins as a wartime bridge running crew under [[Torol Sadeas]]. How he came to be a bridgeman is unknown.

After the crew was sent on chasm duty in the [[Shattered Plains]] to loot [[Parshendi]] corpses, they were quiet and nervous, but Kaladin engaged Torfin, [[Drehy]], and other members of the crew in conversation to break the tension. Torfin was later seen training with the rest of Bridge Four in the warcamps. When [[Moash]] abruptly stopped carrying a bridge, Torfin yelled at him, not realizing that Moash stopped due to his shock at seeing Kaladin's rapid recovery after his judgement by the [[highstorm]].

Torfin was in [[Narak]] when the [[Alethi]] began occupying the area around .
