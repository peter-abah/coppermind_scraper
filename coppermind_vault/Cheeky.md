**Cheeky** is a hyperslug [[taynix]] found on [[Detritus]].{{book ref|sf2|10}} They are [[Sadie]]'s personal slug, though mental commands have to be given to Cheeky through [[Jorgen Weight|Jorgen]], as Sadie is not a cytonic.{{book ref|sf2|10}}{{book ref|sf3|12}}

## History
Sadie adopted Cheeky as her personal hyperslug sometime after the mission to rescue [[Cuna]] from [[Sunreach (station)|Sunreach]]. Cheeky accompanied Sadie on the mission to [[ReDawn (planet)|ReDawn]]. During the first clash with the Unity faction, [[Jorgen]] used Cheeky to rescue Sadie from a light net by teleporting their ship out of the net.{{book ref|sf2|10}} Sadie also brought Cheeky on the mission to [[Evershore (planet)|Evershore]]. Cheeky stayed behind in Sadie's ship while negotiations with the [[kitsen]] took place. When the [[Superiority]] attacked, Sadie and [[Kimmalyn]] were stuck out in the open and couldn't get to their fighters. Jorgen had Cheeky hyperjump to Sadie and then take her to her fighter so she could join the battle.{{book ref|sf3|12}}
