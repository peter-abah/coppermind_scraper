**Chief Roberts** is in charge of the 42nd Precinct in [[New Clipperton]]. When meeting up with politicians, he would wear a suit which never suited him as they always seemed too tight. After [[Chaz]] and [[Anthony Davis|Davis]] showed off the [[Reality badge]] to the precinct, the chief came up personally to take a closer looks at Chaz's. After seeing the truth that he was a [[dupe]] in a [[Snapshot (event)|Snapshot]], *Roberts* went back into his office and shot himself.
