**Kaminah** is the oldest [[ward]] of [[May Aladar]]. She serves as [[Adolin Kholin]]s aide-de-camp during the [[Battle of Azimir]].

## Appearance & Personality
Kaminah is a young Alethi woman with curls of light brown hair, pointing to some partial foreign ancestry. She is atleast seventeen years old at the time of the Battle of Azimir.
Although initially somewhat nervous about her promotion to aide-de-camp due to her complete lack of experience, she rapidly takes to the role with eagerness, showing initiative and looking for ways to be helpful.

## History
Kaminah arrived at Azimir together with her Teacher May Aladar who was going to assist Adolin Kholin as his head scribe. When Adolin assigned May to command his archers he promoted Kaminah to his new aide-de-camp. Kaminah rapidly took to her new role and and relayed Adolins orders and messages during the siege. She also saw to that he got enough food and sleep, directly ordering him to go to bed on atleast one occassion. When asked about what it meant to control Azimir by Adolin, she offered to write to Urithiru to try and find out more.

When the Oathgate defense finally completly broke during the seventh day of the siege, Kaminah, along with Mays other wards, made it to the Emperors hidden saferoom where she received the news of Naraks fall and [[Thaylenah]]s treaty via [[spanreed]].

## Relationships
===May Aladar===

> — Adolin reassuring Kaminah about her promotion

Kaminah is the oldest ward of May Aladar.

===Adolin Kholin===

> To bed. You fought all night, straight through the Everstorm as it passed. Brightlord Colot say I’m supposed to tell you that you have to sleep.
> — Ordering Adolin to rest

Adolin gives Kaminah a battlefield promotion to his aide-de-camp shortly before start of the siege of Azimir. He later notes that she seems to have quickly grown into her new role and does her job well.

===Colot===
Colot gives Kaminah advice on her role as Adolins aide and pushes her to be more confident in it by giving her the task of ordering Adolin to rest. This somewhat backfires as she then also gives him the same order.
