**Cali** is a [[Skybreaker]] squire under Skybreaker masters, [[Ki]] and [[Warren]].

She competes against [[Szeth]] in a martial competence test over the [[Purelake]], to test their ability with their [[surge]]s. The squires have to throw bags of coloured powder at each other and avoid being hit. During the test Szeth bends a pole out of Cali's reach preventing her from  getting a bag.
