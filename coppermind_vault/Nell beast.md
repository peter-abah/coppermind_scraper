**Nell beasts** are a species of animal found on the island of [[Patji (island)|Patji]] on [[First of the Sun]]. They are likely herbivorous, as they sometimes feed on oceanside weeds. Their natural predators include [[deepwalker]]s, which attack them when they are near the shore. They are small enough that a single deepwalker might kill a dozen of them in a single strike.{{book ref|iote|4}}

## Trivia
* This species was instead called krell in the novella *[[Sixth of the Dusk (novella)|Sixth of the Dusk]]*. Their name was changed to nell beasts in *[[Isles of the Emberdark]]*.
