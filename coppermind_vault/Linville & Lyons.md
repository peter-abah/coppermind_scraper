**Linville & Lyons** is an accounting firm in [[Elendel]] on [[Scadrial]].

[[Waxillium Ladrian]] shot up their storefront while chasing the [[Marksman]], doing over 400 [[boxing]]s of damage. After the incident the firm hired a solicitor to seek compensation for their losses.
