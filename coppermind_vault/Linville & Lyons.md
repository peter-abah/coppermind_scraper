**Linville & Lyons** is an accounting firm in [[Elendel]] on [[Scadrial]].{{file ref|House_Record_broadsheet.jpg|*The House Record* Broadsheet}}

[[Waxillium Ladrian]] shot up their storefront while chasing the [[Marksman]], doing over 400 [[boxing]]s of damage. After the incident the firm hired a solicitor to seek compensation for their losses.{{file ref|House_Record_broadsheet.jpg|*The House Record* Broadsheet}}{{book ref|mb5|2}}
