**Jakkik** is a surgeon on [[Roshar]].

## Attributes and Abilities
Jakkik is a skilled surgeon, knowledgeable in his craft. Adolin trusts him to clean his wounds, and accepts a potentially deadly stimulant (made from [[firemoss]]) from him.

## History
On the day of the [[contest of champions]], Jakkik resided in [[Yanagawn]]'s saferoom in [[Azimir]]. Prior to this, he was wounded severely in the thigh, likely when the troops surrounding the dome broke at the death of the armor standby using Adolin's armor. Jakkik then cleaned Adolin's stump and gave him tincture of firemoss -- as a way to dull the pain -- before Adolin left to reclaim the throne room and save [[Azir]].
