**Molinar** is a child mentioned in one of the records in the [[Urithiru gem archive]].{{epigraph ref|sa3|59}} Since their father and sibling have [[Thaylen]] styled names, it is likely Molinar is Thaylen or part-Thaylen.

Their mother was planning to depart [[Urithiru]] and was given the opportunity to leave a message. She chose to leave a record of her family. A ruby was used to record the message, so she was likely a [[Dustbringer]].{{epigraph ref|sa3|59}}

She felt Molinar, as well as Molinars's sibling [[Kmakra]], were the true gemstones of her life.{{epigraph ref|sa3|59}}
