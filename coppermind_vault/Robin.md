**Robin** is a member of [[Stardragon Flight]] in the [[DDF]]. It is unclear whether Robin is their name or callsign.

As [[Alanik]] and [[Skyward Flight]] are departing [[Detritus]] to aid the [[UrDail]] against the [[Superiority]], [[Jeshua Weight]] sends Stardragon Flight&mdash;including Robin&mdash;out to try to stop them. Robin radios [[Jorgen Weight]] to ask what he is doing, though he does not respond before Alanik [[hyperjump]]s the flight to [[ReDawn (planet)|ReDawn]].
