**Lekal City** is a city in the [[Southern Dominance]] of the [[Final Empire]].{{ref|name=map}} It lies on the [[River Channerel]] and is downriver from [[Austrex]].{{ref|name=map}}
 
==History==
Prior to the [[Collapse]], Lekal City was a country estate, with a manor house but lacked a true palace.{{book ref|mb3|4}} It was still notable enough to be on maps dated 1021, shortly before the Collapse.{{ref|name=map}} When the [[Lord Ruler]] died, a shantytown sprung up around Lekal City's main manor house.{{book ref|mb3|4}}
 
It is not clear if [[Jastes Lekal]] made this his capital of his kingdom during the [[Siege of Luthadel]] or perhaps the Southern Dominance's capital, [[Austrex]]. Regardless, following Jastes Lekal's demise, King [[Audil Lekal]] was centered out of Lekal City.{{book ref|mb3|4}}
 
[[Sazed]] and [[Breeze]] traveled to Lekal City as ambassadors of the [[Elend Venture]]'s [[New Empire]] prior to the [[Siege of Fadrex City]]. Sazed sought Audil Lekal's surrender, making the Lekal kingdom a vassal to the Empire. Audil eventually signed the treaty, realizing he was beaten.{{book ref|mb3|4}}
 
Nearer to the [[Catacendre]], Lekal City was destroyed from lava from a nearby [[Ashmount]] (presumably the nearby Ashmount Zerinah), and along with it, the entire Southern Dominance was said to be burning.{{book ref|mb3|61}}
