**Dison's Valley** is located in southeastern [[Shinovar]] on [[Roshar]] near the [[Nirovah Valley]].

[[Neturo-son-Vallano]] says that Dison's Valley is on "the other side" of the town of [[Clearmount]] compared to his family's homestead, farther inland. Neturo suggests driving the sheep to Dison's Valley when he hears horns announcing the arrival of raiding soldiers on the coast, since the family will be safer there.
