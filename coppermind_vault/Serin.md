**Serin** is Lady Judge [[Heelis]]'s secretary in [[Kezare]] on the [[Dayside]] of [[Taldain]]. He brings news to Heelis from [[Ker Kedasha]] saying that the [[A'kar]]'s support is crumbling in the wake of the [[Diem]]'s reinstatement.{{book ref|ws|18|review=yes}}
