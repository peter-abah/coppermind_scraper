Callsign **Dodger** is a pilot in the [[DDF]].

When Spensa started flight school, they held the record for most kills in one engagement: twelve kills and nine assists. Spensa joked to Hurl that it would be arrogant for her to try and beat Dodger's record, so instead she would aim for an equal number of kills and assists.
