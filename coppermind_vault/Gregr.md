**Gregr** is a miner on [[Scadrial]]. He is [[Wayne]]'s uncle; it is unclear which of Wayne's parents is his sibling. During Wayne's childhood, Gregr lived with his family in their one-window home  next to a mine in [[Tinweight Settlement]]. It is possible that he moved in after the death of Wayne's father in a mining accident, but the specific timeline is unclear. 

Wayne felt that Gregr did not care about him or [[Wayne's mother|his mother]], and implied that Gregr was abusive. Gregr died in a mining accident at some point before , being crushed by a number of rocks. After Gregr's death, Wayne sometimes kicked a table that Gregr had built for the nearly unfurnished home, hoping to spite him in case he was watching from the afterlife.
