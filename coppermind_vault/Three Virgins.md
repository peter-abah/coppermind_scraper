The **Three Virgins** are characters from a folk tale on [[Sel]] known in [[Teod]] (and possibly [[Arelon]]).

While speaking with [[Sarene]], [[Kiin]] colloquially invokes the Three Virgins as a simile for someone who is very surprised.{{book ref|elantris|26}}
