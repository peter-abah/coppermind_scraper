***Skyward*** is a series of novels written by [[Brandon Sanderson]] set in the [[Cytoverse]], taking place centuries after *[[Defending Elysium]]*. The first book, *[[Skyward]]*, was released on November 6, 2018 and the second book, *[[Starsight]]*, was released on November 26, 2019.

### Skyward Flight Novellas
In addition to the main series novels, there are three novellas, each co-written by [[Janci Patterson]] and each following a different point-of-view character. They are set between *[[Starsight]]* and *[[Cytonic (book)|Cytonic]]*. Their initial release was with ebook and audiobook versions only, but a combined print volume of all three novellas, entitled *Skyward Flight*, came out on April 5, 2022.

===Riftwake===
A sequel series taking place after the events of the Skyward series, co-written by Janci Patterson. It is set to be a trilogy of novels, and to follow different characters than Skyward or the Skyward Flight Novellas.

## Development
*Skyward* was originally planned as a YA novel set in the [[cosmere]]. Brandon began working on it in October 2017 after some difficulty with *[[Apocalypse Guard]]* caused that book to be delayed, and updated his website to read "Mystery Project". When he revealed the title of this new project he also announced that it was no longer planned as part of the cosmere because the story needed to reference Earth to work properly. The trilogy was officially announced in State of the Sanderson 2017. In an AMA leading up to the release of the first book, Brandon stated that the trilogy would likely become a four book series.
