**Nenal** is a [[Kharbranthian]] apothecary on [[Roshar]]. She has tawny skin and a quiet intensity, softening only around [[Fasi]].{{rpg ref|ss|7}} Sometime during the [[True Desolation]], Nenal paid fifty marks and boarded a ship called the *[[Dalewillow (ship)|Dalewillow]]*.{{rpg ref|ss|8}} On the voyage, she attempted to surprise Fasi, her lover, with a proposal. She hid a [[fabrial]] inside of a crate labelled [[longroot]]. However, the captain, after dealing with a [[cremling]] infestation, discovered the fabrial and asked a few people to search for its owner.{{rpg ref|ss|6}} They questioned the passengers, and Nenal grew uneasy.{{rpg ref|ss|7}} It is unclear if these people foiled her plans, but the pair were engaged that same night.{{rpg ref|ss|8}} Nenal owns a jewelry box containing a diamond bridal crown.{{rpg ref|ss|6}}
