**Nenal** is a [[Kharbranthian]] apothecary on [[Roshar]]. She has tawny skin and a quiet intensity, softening only around [[Fasi]]. Sometime during the [[True Desolation]], Nenal paid fifty marks and boarded a ship called the *[[Dalewillow (ship)|Dalewillow]]*. On the voyage, she attempted to surprise Fasi, her lover, with a proposal. She hid a [[fabrial]] inside of a crate labelled [[longroot]]. However, the captain, after dealing with a [[cremling]] infestation, discovered the fabrial and asked a few people to search for its owner. They questioned the passengers, and Nenal grew uneasy. It is unclear if these people foiled her plans, but the pair were engaged that same night. Nenal owns a jewelry box containing a diamond bridal crown.
