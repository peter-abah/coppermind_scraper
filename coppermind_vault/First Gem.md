The **First Gem** is a topaz with magical properties{{wob ref|5301}} that [[Hoid]] once carried and from which he took his name for a while.{{wob ref|6065}}{{wob ref|8609}}

The First Gem was referenced by [[Patji (being)|Patji]]{{wob ref|12761}} in [[Letters#Second_Oathbringer_Letter|their letter to Hoid]], whom they addressed as "Cephandrius, bearer of the First Gem."{{epigraph ref|sa3|42}} The fact that Hoid was a [[Dawnshard]] at some point{{wob ref|14300}} is related to his title as Bearer of the First Gem.{{wob ref|14355}}

## Theories
{{theory}}
{{unpublished|book=Dragonsteel prime}}
A character named "Topaz" appears in the unpublished work [[Dragonsteel Prime|*Dragonsteel* Prime]].{{bws ref|dragonsteel-prime-chapter-31-bridge-four-4|Dragonsteel Prime Chapter 31: Bridge Four 4|date=c. 2000}} Although *Dragonsteel* Prime is not canonical, Brandon has confirmed that some individuals in the [[cosmere]] still know Hoid by that name.{{wob ref|5882}}

Hoid told [[Kaladin]] that he was once named for a beautiful rock that became "completely worthless" for his wearing it.{{book ref|sa1|57}} If this is a reference to the First Gem, it likely indicates that it no longer holds its original magical properties. [[Frost]] also mentions a gemstone being "dead" in his [[Letters#The Second Letter|letter to Hoid]].{{epigraph ref|sa2|60}} 

Although topaz is a [[polestone]] on [[Roshar]], Frost and Patji's knowledge of the gem, its name, and Hoid's status as one of the oldest beings in the cosmere{{wob ref|3083}} all hint that the First Gem has importance beyond Roshar.
