**Chan Ko Sar** is the original Vessel of the [[Shard]] [[Invention]]. [[Tanavast]] describes them as traveling the [[cosmere]] and creating marvels.{{book ref|sa5|115}}

## Abilities and Attributes
{{for|Invention#Associated Magic|the powers of Invention}}

As a [[Vessel]] of the [[Shard]] [[Invention]], Chan Ko Sar is immortal and immensely powerful.

## History
Like all original [[Vessel]]s, Chan Ko Sar is a native of [[Yolen]].{{wob ref|7939}} They participated in the [[Shattering of Adonalsium]] along with sixteen other people, and took up one of the [[Shard]]s, [[Invention]].{{book ref|tress|56}} Three thousand years after the Shattering, Chan Ko Sar was creating wonderful things when [[Honor]] contacted them for help against [[Odium]] and they rejected Honor.{{book ref|sa5|115}}
