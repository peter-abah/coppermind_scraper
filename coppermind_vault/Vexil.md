**Vexil the Wise** is the Prime of  the [[Makabaki]] kingdom of [[Emul]] on [[Roshar]] during the [[Era of Solitude]].{{book ref|sa3|96}}

As with many of the Makabaki monarchs, Vexil is often referred to simply as **Emul**.{{book ref|sa3|96}}

## Personality
Little is known of Vexil's personality. He has a passionate hatred for both the [[Tukar|Tukari]] and their ruler [[Tezim]].{{book ref|sa3|96}} He is also a grateful man, very appreciative of the [[Alethi]] offer to help him secure the last of his lands. As ruler of Emul, he obviously seeks to promote his kingdom whenever possible, but is ultimately realistic enough to accept that his personal priorities are not those of the other kingdoms of Roshar.

## History
Little is known of Vexil's early life. At some point he and the princess of [[Yezier]] engaged in a romantic relationship that eventually came to an end.{{book ref|sa3|122}} He became Prime sometime after [[Ashno|Ashno of Sages]] had completed his time as Emuli Prime.{{book ref|sa1|interlude|7}} Vexil spent a portion of his rule embroiled in the [[Eighty's War]] with the kingdom of Tukar{{book ref|sa3|i|14}} and its god-priest Tezim.{{book ref|sa1|54}} By late 1173, Vexil's power in the kingdom was waning with the kingdom invaded and refugees flooding into neighboring [[Tashikk]].{{book ref|edgedancer|3}}

Following the arrival of the Everstorm, and an assault by the [[singers]] as they moved through Emul to [[Marat]], Vexil was even further weakened.{{book ref|sa3|65}} He and Ashno traveled to [[Azimir]]{{book ref|sa3|65}} and accompanied the leaders and representatives of the rest of the Makabaki Empire through the [[Oathgate]] to [[Urithiru]].{{book ref|sa3|96}} Upon his arrival, Vexil sought to advocate for his kingdom.{{book ref|sa3|96}} In a meeting with the rest of the rulers of the coalition kingdoms, Vexil asked that their next move be to reclaim his land lost to the [[singer]]s and then conquer the kingdom of Tukar. This proposal was dismissed, as the coalition wished to focus on securing what lands they had first. Alethi Highprince [[Aladar]] offered the aid of several of his experts in fortification to help Vexil defend what lands were left to Emul, but not to yet try to reclaim any lost lands. Vexil gratefully accepted Aladar's offer.

Prior to the [[Battle of Thaylen Field]], Vexil traveled to [[Thaylen City]] to the meeting of coalition leaders before the battle. He spoke to Aladar there about tactics.{{book ref|sa3|111}} After the revelations about Dalinar's role as highking, conversations with Odium, and the potential dangers of Surgebinding, Vexil seems to have departed the city, possibly with the Azish.{{book ref|sa3|113}} After the battle, Vexil seems to have returned to Thaylen City and spoke to the princess of [[Yezier]], as their guards cleaned up rubble.{{book ref|sa3|122}} This spurred rumors that the two were rekindling their romantic relationship.
