**Jom** is a cobbler in [[Kholinar]].

He broke his arm before the [[Siege of Kholinar]]. 

[[Grund]] knew of Jom, and suggested to [[Shallan Davar|Veil]] that Jom would be a good candidate to give extra food to, along with many other candidates. However, the [[Grips]] killed all of Grund's friends for the food and terrorized anyone who received food, so it is possible Jom is now dead.
