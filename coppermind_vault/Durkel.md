**Durkel** is the father of [[Allriandre]] and her sisters on [[Scadrial]]. Durkel is his surname.{{book ref|mb7|58}} He worked as an accountant in the [[Roughs]] and would scribe letters for the miners and other workers.{{book ref|mb4|10}} He was killed by a sixteen year old [[Wayne]] during a robbery.{{book ref|mb5|5}}
