**Durkel** is the father of [[Allriandre]] and her sisters on [[Scadrial]]. Durkel is his surname. He worked as an accountant in the [[Roughs]] and would scribe letters for the miners and other workers. He was killed by a sixteen year old [[Wayne]] during a robbery.
