**Inis Julien** is a senator from [[Elendel]] on [[Scadrial]].{{file ref|New Ascendancy broadsheet.jpg|*The New Ascendancy* broadsheet}}

==History==
In {{date|342|PC}}, Julien was part of a delegation from Elendel that was invited to [[Bilming]] to view the unveiling of new warships known as "[[Pewternaut]]s". The ships were personally developed by the mayor of Bilming, Lord [[Bastien Severington]]. Julien was subsequently quoted on the front page of *[[The New Ascendancy]]* mocking the ships, which he believed to be superfluous.{{file ref|New Ascendancy broadsheet.jpg|*The New Ascendancy* broadsheet}} Julien's comments may have contributed to the resentment of Elendel that Severington later displayed during a speech at [[Kelesina Shores's party]].{{book ref|bom|14}}
