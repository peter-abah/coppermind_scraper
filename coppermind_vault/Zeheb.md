**Zeheb** is an [[ardent]] from [[Hearthstone]]. She brings [[Kaladin]] a scroll case with maps of [[Alethkar]] when he stops in Hearthstone before following the trail of the transformed parshmen. Kaladin is a little surprised to see her because he thinks she has "always seemed older than the stones themselves." {{book ref|sa3|7}}
