**Olo** is a [[seon]] on [[Roshar]] who has worked with [[Hoid]].

## Appearance and Personality
Olo's appearance is not described, but all known seons share the same appearance; he is presumably a melon-sized glowing ball of light with his namesake [[Aon#Olo|Aon Olo]] at the center. The meaning of Aon Olo is unknown.

He likes to be of service.

## Attributes and Abilities

Olo likely has the typical abilities of a seon. He can communicate instantly with other seons.

He is also a [[worldhopper]] and is one of only a few seons known to have left Sel.

### Work with Hoid
It is unclear when and how Olo encountered [[Hoid]]. Hoid is known to have spent time on Sel, but he is not seen there with Olo. Hoid has specifically mentioned the existence of unbound seons and [[Ala]] says that Olo "worked for" Hoid. However, Ala also refers to Olo's "master". It is therefore uncertain if Olo is unbound.

## History
In , Olo spent some time in [[Urithiru]] while working for Hoid. [[Pattern]], while on the [[Lasting_Integrity#Urithiru_Emissary_Delegation_(1175)|trip to Lasting Integrity]], used [[Ala]] to speak to Hoid, presumably through Olo. Not long afterward, Olo's master suggested that he leave the tower. Olo decided to make his way toward the [[Shattered Plains]]. A few months later, [[Shallan Davar]] asked [[Ala]] to check to see if Olo could to go to [[Azimir]]. Shallan did not explain why, but she was hoping to contact her husband, [[Adolin Kholin]]. Ala suspected that Olo would help her.

After [[Stormfall]], Hoid woke up on [[Scadrial]] and wished he had a seon for communication; Olo was presumably still on Roshar.
