**Igneous Cavern** is the largest human-inhabited cavern on [[Detritus]]. It is home to the apparatus that produce the [[DDF]] ships.{{book ref|Skyward|prologue}} In addition, it houses reserve ships for the DDF in event of an emergency such as a [[Lifebuster]].

Glorious Rises of Industry is a suburb in Igneous. It is where [[Spensa]], her [[Spensa's mother|mother]], and [[Gran-Gran]] live.{{book ref|sky1|1}} It is also the home of [[Rig]].{{book ref|sky1|17}}

Igneous has a starship museum that celebrates warriors of the past.{{book ref|sky1|4}} The museum has mock cockpits which patrons can sit in to experience what it would be like to be in a starfighter.{{book ref|sky1|4}}

The public elevators that can take people and cargo to the surface can be found near the center of Igneous. The government buildings are close to the elevators and are used to host the pilots entrance test.{{book ref|sky1|5}} One can elect to take the pilot entry exam when they are seventeen.{{book ref|sky1|5}} The test goes for four hours and can cover material from any number of classes taken by students.{{book ref|sky1|5}}
