**Lady Farthing** is a noblewoman from [[New Seran]] on [[Scadrial]]. She owns [[Farthing Mansion]]. Some of Lady Farthing's jewelry was stolen by the [[Set]] in  during a robbery of her mansion, and she subsequently coordinated with the Uptown Precinct to offer a reward for its return. These details were published in an edition of *[[The New Ascendancy]]*.
