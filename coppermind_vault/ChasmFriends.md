The **ChasmFriends** are a group of young [[chasmfiend]]s who live in the "[[Shattered Plains|Crackity Plains]]" of [[Roshar]].

They are anthropomorphized, and their comedic adventures are non-canon to *[[The Stormlight Archive]]*.

## Publication History
The ChasmFriends were introduced in *The ChasmFriends Get a Pet!*, one of the "*Stormlight* Lost Tales" Story Decks at Dragonsteel Nexus 2024. The short story is credited to Brandon and [[Dan Wells]], although the whole team came up with the idea and Dan primarily wrote the story. The art was done by [[Coppermind:Artists/Anna Earley|Anna Earley]].

The story will be included in *[[Hoid's Storybook Collection]]*, which is expected to be published in late 2026. The crowdfunding campaign for the collection includes pins of each ChasmFriend in blind bags.

==*The ChasmFriends Get a Pet!* ==
### Summary
Cleverclaws captures a "Shoutie", which is actually a terrified human. The ChasmFriends are not very familiar with humans and struggle to understand his reactions as they try to make him happy. Their attempts to cheer him up include shouting at him, making very loud music, taking him underwater, and trying to feed him food that they like, including a human corpse. They eventually realize that he should be returned to the plateaus, but they unfortunately place him in the middle of a group of [[listener]]s, unaware that they are a different type of Shoutie.

## Known ChasmFriends
*Burpslurper: A baby ChasmFriend, he is constantly eating. His carapace is orange.
*Cleverclaws: He is smart and values friendship. His carapace is light green and teal.
*Darren: The largest ChasmFriend, he is almost always asleep. His carapace is purple.
*Howlerina: She likes music and dancing, and enjoys being fancy. Her carapace is light blue.
*Shredhead: She is very energetic and seeks adventure. Her carapace is red and yellow.
