**Dolk-son-Dolk** is a boy on [[Roshar]]. He likely lives in or near the town of [[Clearmount]]. He calls [[Elid]] and [[Szeth]] "trunk and branch", as a reference to their relative builds. Szeth believes that both Dolk and his father, Dolk, are idiots.{{book ref|sa5|24}}
