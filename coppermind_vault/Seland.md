**Seland** is a woman who lives in [[Alethkar]] on [[Roshar]]. During the [[Siege of Kholinar]], she was one of the impoverished people that [[Shallan Davar]] (as Veil) attempted to supply with food.{{book ref|sa3|74}} She and [[Ma]] had been squatting in a decrepit building with [[Grund]] before they suddenly moved away,{{book ref|sa3|72}}{{book ref|sa3|74}} possibly due to a fear of the [[Grips]].
