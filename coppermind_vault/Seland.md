**Seland** is a woman who lives in [[Alethkar]] on [[Roshar]]. During the [[Siege of Kholinar]], she was one of the impoverished people that [[Shallan Davar]] (as Veil) attempted to supply with food. She and [[Ma]] had been squatting in a decrepit building with [[Grund]] before they suddenly moved away, possibly due to a fear of the [[Grips]].
