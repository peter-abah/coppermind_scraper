The ***Whistlebow*** is a ship that traveled the twelve seas on [[Lumar]].

## History
Little is known about the history of the *Whistlebow*. At some point, [[Charlie]] came into contact with [[Hoid]] while he was serving as the ship's cabin boy, before he joined the crew of the *[[Crow's Song]]*. Charlie had Hoid deliver a beautiful, unchipped porcelain cup back to [[Tress]] aboard the *Whistlebow*. This was the first of the four cups Charlie sent to Tress, however it is unknown which ships delivered the other three.
