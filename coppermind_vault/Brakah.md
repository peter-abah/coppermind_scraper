**Brakah** is an Admiral in the Imperial Fleet of the [[High Empire]]. He and [[Terarn]] attended the Imperial Academy with [[Varion Crestmar]]. They requested to serve under his command as High Admiral, and became his most trusted advisors. They are present when [[Sennion Crestmar]] attempts to assassinate Varion.
