**Talata** is a [[House Kholin|Kholin]] scribe who lives on the [[Shattered Plains]] during the [[War of Reckoning]].

[[Dalinar]] tells [[Adolin]] that Talata needs to review Highprince [[Bethab]]'s [[chull]] contracts with [[Elhokar]]. Bethab had previously tried to slip language into a contract that would give him favor in a succession. Talata is presumably the best scribe in the Kholin warcamp for this task, as Dalinar asks for her by name.
