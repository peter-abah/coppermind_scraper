**Adam Horne** is an employee of [[Dragonsteel Entertainment]]. He was hired as [[Brandon Sanderson]]'s executive assistant circa January 2015.{{bws ref|brandon-sanderson-newsletter-for-january-2015|Brandon Sanderson Newsletter for January 2015|date=2015-1-7}}

He is involved in a wide variety of tasks, including publicity, merchandise, and updating Brandon's website. He often responds to fan mail and other inquiries, and formerly sent out copies of *[[Aether of Night]]* and *[[White Sand (prose)|White Sand]]* before their distribution methods were changed. He is involved in the production of leatherbound editions, and administered the 10th anniversary *[[The Way of Kings]]* Kickstarter.{{bws ref|only-two-stretch-goals-left|TWoK Kickstarter stretch goal update|date=2020-08-04}} He can often be seen (or at least heard) on Brandon's YouTube livestreams reading questions from fans.{{wob ref|13771}}

He is married to Brandon's sister Jane{{wob ref|14716}} and they have a corgi named [[Lopen]].{{tweet ref|945727751989878784|text=Adam's reply to Brandon's picture|date=2017-12-26}}
