**Thermoli** is an outer village in the [[Elendel Basin]]. It is west of [[New Seran]], at the edge of the mountains that form that border between the Basin and the Southern [[Roughs]].

The village is the source of the Thermolian accent, known for its distinctive "v" sounds.

Professor [[Hanlanaze]] grew up in Thermoli.
