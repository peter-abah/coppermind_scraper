**Ms. Torrent** is the librarian at [[Armedius Academy]].

She stops [[Joel]] from entering the [[Rithmatics]] section of the library as it is forbidden for non-Rithmatists to enter without an escort. When Joel returns with [[Melody]], Ms. Torrent lets them into the section reluctantly and then lets them check out one of the books [[Nalizar]] had requested be sent to his office. She acts similarly when they return the book.
