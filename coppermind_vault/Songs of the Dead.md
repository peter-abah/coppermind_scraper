***Songs of the Dead*** is a contemporary fantasy novel by [[Brandon Sanderson]] and [[Peter Orullian]] about a musician named Jack Solomon who dies and wakes up in a new reality where music and light are considered magic. Brandon describes the character as a "heavy metal musician who becomes a necromancer".

After a long period of development, the book is planned for release in 2026 as part of a trilogy called *[[The Strata Wars]]*. Peter will write the remaining books in the series.

==Development==
### Original Draft and Hiatus
Brandon originated the story as an urban fantasy about a necromancer pizza delivery man and referred to it as ***Death by Pizza***. He first mentioned the story in 2010 and wrote the majority of a novel-length story before 2013, but it had many flaws that prevented it from being published. Brandon noted that the title was a bit too silly for the subject matter and was likely to change. Few details of the story are known; Brandon mentioned that necromancers would increase their power after death and resurrection. 

Brandon continued to occasionally mention the book at signings and in his annual [[State of the Sanderson]] blog posts. After looking at it again as a potential reading for the *[[Firefight]]* tour in 2015, Brandon decided that it didn't work, and simply considered it as practice in the urban fantasy genre. Although the story did see some minor development later that year, it was put on hiatus and stayed there for several years. The book was never officially trunked, but it took a back seat to other commitments including *[[The Rithmatist]]* and *[[Alcatraz]]*.

### Revival of Project and Collaboration
In 2018, Brandon mentioned that he was once again actively working on the necromancer novel. He noted that the pizza delivery aspect of the story did not fit the setting and magic system, and he realized that a better choice would be the culture of heavy metal music. The updated novel featured an American metal singer living in London who discovers his latent necromancy powers. In the 2018 State of the Sanderson, he revealed that he had completed a new outline and world guide and had done additional work on the project throughout the year. Some elements of the revamped magic system were culled from an unpublished work called *[[Unpublished_works#.22Trunked.22_Works|The King's Necromancer]]*.

Brandon also announced that the novel would be written as a collaboration with novelist [[Peter Orullian]], whom he had known since at least 2010. Brandon did the worldbuilding and outline, but Peter helped brainstorm the new setting, wrote the first draft, and developed the individual characters. Brandon felt that Peter was an ideal collaborator because he is a heavy metal musician who has toured internationally. Since this was Brandon's first true collaboration, the two authors had to figure out a way to make it work with both of them performing revisions, the first of which Brandon was working on completing at the end of 2018. Brandon intended to make sure that the novel did not take an "over-the-top metal" tone in the style of *[[wikipedia:Brütal Legend|Brütal Legend]]* or *[[wikipedia:This Is Spinal Tap|This Is Spinal Tap]]*. Since the pizza delivery angle had been removed, Brandon jokingly referred to the revamped story as ***Death Without Pizza*** before Peter suggested the current name.

Peter completed a second draft in June 2019, but the project stalled slightly due to Brandon's commitments on *[[Rhythm of War]]*. By the end of 2019, Brandon was working through another revision and was hoping that a final draft would be completed in 2020.

### Return to Minor Project Status
Peter and Brandon spent a lot of time on the novel in 2020; Brandon emphasized that he wanted to make sure his first foray into the urban fantasy genre was successful. It made it into final revisions and was sent to editors for feedback, but ultimately did not find a home. By the end of 2021, *Songs of the Dead* reverted to minor project status. Peter continued to make revisions, but Brandon called it a "difficult write" that was still not quite working, and it remained in limbo.

### Handoff to Peter, Another Revival
Near the end of 2023, Brandon announced that he had completely handed the project over to Peter, clarifying that he was happy with it but simply did not have adequate time to remain involved. After Peter completed more revisions and the book was sent to publishers, the *Strata Wars* trilogy was officially announced in 2025, with the first book arriving in 2026. Peter oversaw the publishing deal and will write the remaining books in the trilogy. Brandon is excited to see the project finally come to light, and calls it "Peter's book" since he had the passion to keep it moving forward.
