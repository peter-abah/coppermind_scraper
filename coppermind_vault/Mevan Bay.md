**Mevan Bay** is a section of the [[Tarat Sea]] that borders southern [[Alethkar]] and [[Jah Keved]] as well as part of the [[Frostlands]].

The port city of [[Dumadari]] lies on the north shore of the bay. The [[Deathbend River]] forms a delta that empties into the eastern section of the bay near the Alethi city [[Karanak]]. Several other small rivers empty into the bay, including the rivers that form part of the borders between Jah Keved, Alethkar, and the Frostlands.
