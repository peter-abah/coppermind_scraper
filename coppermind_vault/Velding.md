> I haven’t been able to get anyone into the Velding in over a month, and Domi only knows what Wyrn and the gyorns are scheming in there. Sending spies to Fjorden these days is almost the same as sending them to die.
> — [[Eventeo]] to [[Sarene]]

**The Velding** is a location on [[Sel]] which is being spied upon by [[Teod]]. King [[Eventeo]] had not been able to get any spies there in a month, and worried what the [[Fjordell]] planned there. 

It is possible the Velding is a place in [[Fjorden]], but it could be located in any place in the Fjordell Empire. Or, it could be a place outside of the Empire, but of interest to the Fjordell.
