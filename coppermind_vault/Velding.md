**The Velding** is a location on [[Sel]] which is being spied upon by [[Teod]].{{book ref|Elantris|17}} King [[Eventeo]] had not been able to get any spies there in a month, and worried what the [[Fjordell]] planned there. 

It is possible the Velding is a place in [[Fjorden]], but it could be located in any place in the Fjordell Empire. Or, it could be a place outside of the Empire, but of interest to the Fjordell.
