**Zapriel tea** is a beverage on [[Lumar]]. It originates on the [[Dromatory Isles]], and is worth more than gold, by weight.{{book ref|Tress|35}}

[[Captain Crow]] drinks stolen Zapriel tea on the *[[Crow's Song]]*.{{book ref|Tress|35}}
