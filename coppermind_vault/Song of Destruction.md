The **Song of Destruction** is one of the [[listener]] songs on [[Roshar]]. It was created by the first listeners after leaving their gods. The Song of Destruction is often used while marching to war or preparing for a fight. The words are similar to the [[Song of Renunciation]], and sung to the same tune. As such, it recounts the story of how the listeners left their gods, but voiced to Destruction.
