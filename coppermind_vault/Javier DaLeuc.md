**Javier DaLeuc** is an actor on [[Scadrial]].

In Cladence and the following month of , he starred in a run of *[[The Den of the Survivor]]*, a play about the overthrow of the [[Final Empire]] that was being shown at the [[Uptown Trio Theater]] in [[New Seran]]. Along with his co-star, [[Penelope Portreau]], he was mentioned in an advertisement for the performances in *[[The New Ascendancy]]*.
