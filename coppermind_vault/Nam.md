**Nam** is a [[singer]] in [[Kholinar]].{{book ref|sa3|122}}

Along with [[Pal]], they are one of the few [[singers]] who survived the [[Siege of Kholinar]] together with [[Moash]]. They join Moash and [[Khen]] who are clearing rubble in Kholinar. Both of them wear [[warform]] at that time.{{book ref|sa3|122}}

It isn't clear for how long Nam and Pal have been in [[Khen]]'s group.
