**Brick's tavern** is a tavern and brewery owned and operated by [[Brick]] on [[Diggen's Point]] on [[Lumar]]. Brick is assisted by his cousin, who helps him keep watch over the tavern when the crowd is getting rough.

== Economics and Culture==
Brick's tavern is a regular meeting spot for many of Diggen's Point's locals. It also serves as a brewery where Brick produces an ale that is considered to be among the best in the region. He spends time brewing on the weekends, often assisted by [[Rod (Lumar)|Rod]]. Sailors from other islands visit the tavern seeking Brick's ale, and merchants purchase it in barrels.

There are generally two crowds that patronize Brick's tavern. Early in the evening, there is usually a happy and boisterous crowd that is often roaring, cheerful, and festive. On nights where Brick expects things to get particularly rowdy, his cousin will stand by the door and act as a bouncer. As the evening drifts on, the lively crowd is replaced by a colder, darker, and quieter group of people seeking company, rather than companionship. Brick keeps the tavern open until 2 a.m.

### Darts
The men that frequent Brick's tavern regularly play darts, though with slightly unusual rules. By coincidence, the wood grain in the wall around the dartboard roughly forms a human shape. There are knots that look like facial features, grain that resembles hair, and the dartboard resembles a family crest worn on the chest. Once the patrons noticed this, they decided that it actually looks like [[Charlie's father|the duke]]. Two knots in the wood a few feet below the dartboard can be interpreted as the testes of the duke, and are the real targets for the game. A good dart throw will result in winces from any witnesses.

## History
[[Lem]] was a regular patron at Brick's tavern and was well known for helping out many of the other townspeople (including Brick, Rod, [[Jule]], [[Gremmy]], and [[Sor]]) whenever they needed it. He went to the tavern to tacitly recruit some of his friends into the plot to sneak his daughter [[Tress]] onto the *[[Oot's Dream]]*.
