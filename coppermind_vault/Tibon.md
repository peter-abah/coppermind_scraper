**Tibon** is a lighteyed [[Alethi]] soldier on [[Roshar]].

He was a member of the [[Cobalt Guard]]. He--along with [[Marks (Roshar)|Marks]], another member of the Cobalt Guard--guarded [[Adolin]] during his date with [[Janala]], and waited with [[Falksi]] outside the tent of the leatherworkers [[Yis]] and [[Avaran]].
