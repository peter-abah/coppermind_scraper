> We could imitate a reverse binding. That's where a faeigerman overpowers a skop by use of her true name.
> — [[Sefawynn]] explains to [[Runian]]

A **faeigerman** is someone with the ability to perform a reverse binding. This involves overpowering a [[skop]] by using their true name, and turning them into the faeigerman's slave. In the [[Earth (Frugal Wizard)|alternate medieval dimension]], there are stories about people who have this ability, which are also known to the [[Hordamen]]. An [[aelv]] may also have this ability.

As part of their plan to confront a group of Hordamen, [[Sefawynn]] suggests that [[Runian]] could appear to be more like an aelv if they imitate a reverse binding. She tells him to order her around a lot, to call her "[[thrael]]", and that she would act the part.
