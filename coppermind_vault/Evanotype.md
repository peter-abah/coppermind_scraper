An **evanotype** is a photograph created through an early form of image capture technique invented on [[Scadrial]] after the [[Catacendre]].

Evanotypes are captured using special cameras. Early evanotype cameras were large and required the subject to stand still. The camera used by the [[kandra]] explorer [[ReLuur]] was a newer model that could take action pictures and was small enough to fit in ReLuur's pack. Even later, by , Scadrians had figured out how to make moving images. It is not clear when evanotypes were invented, but by around  they were relatively common, as people living in the [[Roughs]] had access to them. A few years before , the city of [[Bilming]] saw the rise of **evanoplays**. It is unknown what these are exactly, but [[Jak]] invested in them.

## Evanoscope
In , a new device known as an **evanoscope** was developed. It was an image projector that used lights to enlarge and display an evanotype on a given surface, such as a wall. Both [[Wayne]] and [[Marasi]] were shocked to see an evanoscope in action, and [[VenDell]] believed that they would be quite popular.

[[Harmony]] let slip to the kandra the concept of moving images, although he refused to expand on the subject. Moving evanoscope images were later discovered by an organisation known as the [[Set]].

## Trivia
*Evanotypes are presumably inspired by real-world photographic processes invented in the 19th century (such as the [[wikipedia:daguerrotype|daguerreotype]], [[wikipedia:Ambrotype|Ambrotype]], and [[wikipedia:tintype|tintype]])  that use metals and chemicals to create monochromatic images.
