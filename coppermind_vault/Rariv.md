**Rariv** is a servant at [[Mercystar]]'s palace in [[Hallandren]] on [[Nalthis]] circa 327.

When [[Vasher]] infiltrates the palace, he encounters a group of four servants in the hallway. Vasher disables [[Taff]] with an [[Awakening|Awakened]] rope and throws [[Nightblood]] (in his sheath) at the group. [[Lolan]] picks up the sword while Rariv and another servant run for help. Taff ends up dead; Rariv, Lolan, and the third surviving servant recount their version of the events to [[Lightsong]] when he decides to investigate.
