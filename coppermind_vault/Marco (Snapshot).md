**Marco** is a police officer in [[New Clipperton]] who works at the 42nd Precinct headquarters. He was on bad terms with [[Anthony Davis]] and refused to speak with him when he entered the precinct the time prior to Davis and [[Chaz]]'s [[Snapshot (event)|Snapshot]] investigation into [[the Photographer]]. Marco's dupe reacted to seeing a [[Reality badge]] by opening his mouth in shock and patting at his body.{{book ref|snapshot|4}}
