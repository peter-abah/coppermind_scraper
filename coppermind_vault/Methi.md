**Methi** is a type of fruit native to [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa1|22}} Rotting methi has a distinctively unpleasant off-red color.{{book ref|sa4|98}}

It is sometimes steamed and served to [[lighteyed]] women with chicken and a reddish-brown sauce.{{book ref|sa1|22}}
