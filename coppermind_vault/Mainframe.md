**Mainframe LLC** is an entertainment and production company founded by [[Brandon Sanderson]] and [[Max Epstein]].{{url ref|name=catalog|url=https://www.awfulagent.com/catalogs/|site=awfulagent.com|text=JABberwocky Catalog & Rights Guide|date=2021-06-14}}{{sots ref|2020}}
{{sidequote|side=right|size=300px
|The main goal of Mainframe, then, is to take ideas I have that I (so far) haven’t been able to do anything with, or to take stories I want to see done more as a television show, then try to make audio-drama-style expansions to them.
|Brandon Sanderson{{sots ref|2020}}
}}
Mainframe is focused on the production of spoken word entertainment, including audio original novellas, novels, and dramas. [[wikipedia:Recorded Books|Recorded Books]] was a launch partner of the company's. Most of Mainframe's works will also be printed in a prose format.{{sots ref|2020}}

Brandon and Max founded the company after realizing how difficult it can be to get projects completed in the film/television space, leaving some of Brandon's ideas unused. He partnered with Max, who has experience in Hollywood, to create their own company where they could produce original works and also extend the universe of some of Brandon's existing series. Brandon's work in this area may lead to a full-fledged film production company in the future.{{sots ref|2020}}

==Projects==
All of Mainframe's projects to date have had Brandon's involvement, but he has collaborated with several other authors.

* *[[The Original]]*, coauthored with [[Wikipedia:Mary Robinette Kowal|Mary Robinette Kowal]]{{sots ref|2020}}
* *[[Lux (book)|Lux]]*, a *[[The Reckoners|Reckoners]]* audio original coauthored with [[Steven Michael Bohls]]{{bws ref|cover-reveal-for-lux-a-texas-reckoners-novel|Cover Reveal for Lux: A Texas Reckoners Novel|date=2021-06-24}}
* *[[Sunreach]]*, *[[ReDawn (novella)|ReDawn]]*, and *[[Evershore (novella)|Evershore]]*, audio versions of the [[Cytoverse]] novellas coauthored with [[Janci Patterson]]{{sots ref|2020}}{{ref|name=catalog}}
* *[[Stephen Leeds: Death & Faxes]]*, a serial podcast-like extension of the *[[Legion (series)|Legion]]* universe with a writer's room and Max Epstein as "showrunner"{{ref|name=catalog}}
* *[[Dark One: Forgotten]]*,{{ref|name=catalog}} an audio drama coauthored with Dan Wells{{wob ref|14985}}
** A sequel to *Forgotten* titled *[[Dark One: Prophetic Histories]]* was in development for several years,{{ref|name=catalog}} but was later cancelled{{wob ref|17029}}
* *[[Dawnshard (novella)|Dawnshard]]* (audiobook){{wob ref|15362}}
* A second audiobook edition of *[[Edgedancer (novella)|Edgedancer]]* that includes [[Lift]]'s interlude from *[[Words of Radiance]]* as a prologue{{reddit ref|path=r/Stormlight_Archive/comments/waedq0/comment/ii4z4x7|text=Peter Ahlstrom's explanation|date=2022-07-29}}
