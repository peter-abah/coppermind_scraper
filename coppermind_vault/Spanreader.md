A **spanreader** is a device in the [[cosmere]] that can communicate across vast, interplanetary distances. It presumably derives its name from the [[spanreed]], a [[Roshar]]an communication [[fabrial]]. Spanreaders are known to be used by the [[Malwish Empire]] in the [[Cognitive Realm]],{{book ref|iote|38}} but it is not clear if they invented them. The technology that powers spanreaders and the details of their capabilities are unknown. 

## History
[[Dajer]] used a spanreader to attempt to verify [[Starling]]'s claim that the *[[Dynamic]]* had left [[Crow]] behind in [[Silverlight]] before coming to the [[Dakwara's island]].{{book ref|iote|38}} He asked an aide to pay an information broker in the city for this intelligence, implying that such brokers have their own spanreaders.

## Trivia
*Spanreaders were developed during the space age of the cosmere. Interplanetary communication had previously been limited to [[seon]]s and [[tamukek]]s, both of which are quite rare.{{wob ref|16187}} Spanreaders were presumably designed to be a scalable replacement to these methods, facilitating communication as societies expanded beyond their home planets.
*Brandon had previously referred to spanreeds as proof-of-concept that an [[wikipedia:ansible|ansible]] (faster-than-light communication device) is possible in the cosmere,{{wob ref|2701}} foreshadowing the existence of devices like spanreaders.
