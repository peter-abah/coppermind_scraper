***Stephen Leeds: Death & Faxes*** is a story in the [[Legion (series)|Legion]] universe created by [[Brandon Sanderson]]. It was developed by Brandon's [[Mainframe]] production company as an audio original.{{url ref|name=catalog|url=https://www.awfulagent.com/catalogs/|site=awfulagent.com|text=JABberwocky Catalog & Rights Guide|date=2021-06-14}} The audio rights are held by Recorded Books{{ref|name=catalog}}{{sots ref|2020}} and it was released as an Audible Original on June 7, 2022.{{url ref|name=audible|url=https://www.audible.com/pd/Stephen-Leeds-Death-Faxes-Audiobook/B09ZLN963G|text=Publisher's Summary|site=Audible.com}} The story takes place chronologically between *[[Legion (book)|Legion]]* and *[[Legion: Lies of the Beholder]]*. The plot focuses on [[Stephen Leeds]] tracking down a mysterious hacker named [[Enoch]] that uses unusual technology to breach the Internal Revenue Service.{{ref|name=audible}}
