**Jule** is a citizen of [[Diggen's Point]] on [[Lumar]].

Jule once lost the roof of his house during a storm - [[Lem]] helped him replace it. He has, on more than one occasion, paid off [[Gremmy]]'s bar tab at [[Brick]]'s tavern.
