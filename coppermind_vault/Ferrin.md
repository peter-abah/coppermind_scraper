The **ferrin** is a species of bird on [[Sel]] that is commonly found in the marshes of [[JinDo]]. Ferrins are particularly loud and talkative birds, and can be heard screaming at each other across entire marshes.

The JinDo have a proverb about ferrins: "A two-headed ferrin would never leave its nest." It is used when a person has found a new hobby or romantic interest and is suddenly less available than usual.
