**Runaround** is a card game on [[Roshar]]. 

[[Rlain]] liked to play both [[towers]] and runaround, and [[Bisig]] praised his bluffing skills. During the occupation of [[Urithiru]], Rlain noticed some women gossiping about him and attempted to find some common ground by telling them that he liked to play card games like runaround. However, his attempt was unsuccessful and their gossip turned in a darker direction.
