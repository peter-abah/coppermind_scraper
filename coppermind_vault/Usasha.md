**Usasha** is a [[nightmare]] [[painter]] who participates in the [[battle of Kilahito]], of which she is the sole casualty. Preferring to remain anonymous, [[Nikaro]] has his friends credit Usasha as the one who mobilized the painters to defend [[Kilahito]] so that the city would give her the honors.{{book ref|yumi|part=anotherepilogue}}
