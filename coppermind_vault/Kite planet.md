> Another where people fly on kites, as if every man were a Windrunner.
> — [[Mraize]]

A **Shardworld** was the home of a kite-based magic system. The [[Ghostblood]] [[Mraize]] was aware of this planet.

==Trivia==
At one point, Brandon was planning a YA novel to be set on this planet. A story here may end up featuring [[Starling]].
