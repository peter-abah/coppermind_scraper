**Basin Tires** is a business that makes tires in [[Bilming]] on [[Scadrial]].
==History==
At some point they hired [[Tobal Copper]], a chemist specializing in manufacturing and rubber. They fired him for erratic behavior in the year . Tobal filed a lawsuit against them for refusing to listen to his discoveries about the end of the world. The lawsuit was dismissed before reaching the first stage of trial. During Tobal's employment there, he stole the occasional document, all of which added to reveal the Set's plan to build a bomb. In reality, it was a front for the [[Set]], as underneath the building was an entrance to their underground lab and bunker complex. [[Marasi]], [[Prasanva|TwinSoul]], and [[Shai|Moonlight]] used the building to infiltrate the complex. It is unknown what happened to the business after the events surrounding the Set's defeat.
