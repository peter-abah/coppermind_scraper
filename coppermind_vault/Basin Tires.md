**Basin Tires** is a business that makes tires in [[Bilming]] on [[Scadrial]].{{book ref|mb7|29}}
==History==
At some point they hired [[Tobal Copper]], a chemist specializing in manufacturing and rubber. They fired him for erratic behavior in the year {{date|343|PC}}. Tobal filed a lawsuit against them for refusing to listen to his discoveries about the end of the world. The lawsuit was dismissed before reaching the first stage of trial.{{book ref|mb7|29}} During Tobal's employment there, he stole the occasional document, all of which added to reveal the Set's plan to build a bomb.{{book ref|mb7|33}} In reality, it was a front for the [[Set]], as underneath the building was an entrance to their underground lab and bunker complex. [[Marasi]], [[Prasanva|TwinSoul]], and [[Shai|Moonlight]] used the building to infiltrate the complex.{{book ref|mb7|39}}{{book ref|mb7|42}} It is unknown what happened to the business after the events surrounding the Set's defeat.
