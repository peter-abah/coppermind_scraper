A **grandbow** is an incredibly powerful bow created by attaching an [[Fabrial#Augmenter|augmenter fabrial]] to a large bow. Invented by the [[Alethi]] on [[Roshar]], they are sometimes called **Shardbows** because users typically must be enhanced by the strength of [[Shardplate]] in order to fully draw them.

## Appearance and Functionality
Grandbows are constructed from steel and are about as tall as a man. A small gemstone attached to the bow by an [[artifabrian]] provides [[Stormlight]] and ensures that the metal does not warp under the stress of being drawn. The arrows used with a grandbow are as thick as three fingers, and are quite long.

## History
Grandbows were designed by [[Alethi]] [[artifabrian]]s led by [[Navani Kholin]] during the [[War of Reckoning]]. They were often used during [[chasmfiend]] hunts to injure the creatures before the hunters moved in for the final kill. In , [[Highprince]] [[Torol Sadeas]] used a grandbow to attack the chasmfiend that surprised the Alethi during a hunt, while Highprince [[Dalinar Kholin]] as well as [[Adolin]] and King [[Elhokar]] attacked it with their [[Shardblade]]s. Some of the Alethi that had Plate but no Blade (including [[Rust Elthal]] and [[Serugiadis]]) sometimes practiced with grandbows as an alternative to a [[shardhammer]] or another melee weapon.

[[Meridas Amaram]] used a grandbow during his fight with [[Kaladin]] at the [[Battle of Thaylen Field]]. Amaram lost the bow when Kaladin scared off his horse, and it was retrieved by [[Lunamor]]. Lunamor was able to draw the bow and kill Amaram, who had been joined with [[Yelig-nar]], with arrows through the head and chest. He is the only person to have drawn a grandbow without the aid of Shardplate, and it is not known how he did so. His daughter [[Cord]] referred to [[spren]] strengthening Lunamor's arm when he drew the [[Bow of Hours]] in the [[Horneater Peaks]], but Lunamor declined to speak about the subject.

Amaram's grandbow was passed onto Cord, who was also a skilled archer. [[Cord]] used the grandbow to kill [[Leshwi]] before the assault on [[Hearthstone]]. During a [[Skybreaker]] attack at the Battle of [[Emul]], Cord used the bow to shoot an arrow directly into [[Nale]]'s face, but he easily healed from the injury. Nale was also able to catch an arrow that Cord had fired from mere feet away.

## Living Shardbows
It is possible that a bonded [[Knights Radiant|Radiant]] [[spren]] may be manifested as a living Shardbow instead of a [[Shardblade]], although this seems to be uncommon and has not been seen since at least the [[Recreance]].
