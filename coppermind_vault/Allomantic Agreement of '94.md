The **Allomantic Agreement of '94** is a law regulating the use of [[Allomancy]]. It is in effect as of {{date|342|PC}}, so it must have been enacted in year 194 or 294 (the apostrophe in the title implies that it was not in year 94).{{book ref|mb5|14}}

One of its provisions is the prohibition of using [[zinc|Rioters]] and likely [[brass|Soothers]] for commercial gain, i.e. to influence customers into buying by manipulating their emotions. However, there is no law against paying Allomancers for their services, e.g. [[Steel#Allomantic Use|Coinshots]] as couriers or [[Soothing parlor]]s.{{book ref|mb5|14}}

Significant government resources are used to enforce this law, with entire departments watching for violations of it.{{book ref|mb5|14}}
