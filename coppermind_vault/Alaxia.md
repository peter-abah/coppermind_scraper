**Alaxia** is the stern-looking wife of [[Natir]] on Roshar. 

Natir is the steward of Citylord [[Roshone]] in [[Hearthstone]]. Roshone comes from [[Dalilak]], so Alaxia probably does as well.

Alaxia is the one who reads names off of Roshone's list of people who will be conscripted into [[Amaram]]'s army, including [[Tien]].
