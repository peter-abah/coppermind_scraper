**Colot** is a Captain of the Fifth Battalion of the [[House Kholin|Kholin]] army on [[Roshar]].

## Appearance and Personality
Colot is [[lighteyes|lighteyed]] and lanky, with streaks of red in his hair. 

He is referred to as a "good man" by [[Skar]]. He believes his status as a lighteyes can be a hindrance.

## History
### Kholin army
Colot was an archer in the Kholin army in a battalion with a number of archers; he was a captain, also commanding some scouts, scribes, and bridgemen. 

He was good friends with Captain [[Tallan]] of the Eighth Company, which acted as the Fifth Battalion's policing force during the [[Battle of Narak]]. After the battle, he volunteered his battalion to help [[Bridge Four]] scout [[Urithiru]]. He arrived on the scene with reinforcements just after the death of [[Vedekar Perel]]; [[Dalinar]] asked him to help secure the perimeter and to notify [[Elhokar]] and [[Sebarial]] of the death.

### Radiant squire
Colot was among a group of potential [[Knights Radiant]] squires that [[General Khal]] sent to [[Kaladin]]. Kaladin was hesitant to recruit lighteyes, but [[Skar]] vouched for Colot and pointed out that [[Renarin]] was also lighteyed. Colot and the other lighteyed recruits submitted to orders during the tryout without complaint, causing Skar to reflect on Kaladin's and [[Teft]]'s prejudiced perception of lighteyes.

[[Rlain]] later saw Colot and nine other new recruits to Bridge Four training with [[Peet]] after gaining the ability to breathe in [[Stormlight]]. Colot was the only lighteyed squire in the group, and remained one of only a few members of Bridge Four who were originally lighteyed.

After becoming a squire, Colot's name was known to [[Shallan]]. While disguised as a messenger from the [[Shattered Plains]], she invoked his name to seem more authentic during her attempt to deliver a letter from Elhokar to [[Aesudan]].

When the group of [[honorspren]] that followed Sylphrena's example and left to search for a radiant to bond, before the delegation to [[Lasting Integrity]], they reject Colot.  He speculates his rejection was due to his status as a lighteyes when the honorspren wanted someone darkeyed to be more like Kaladin though he admits he doesn't know the reason.

### Battle of Azimir
After Adolin sees that Colot has decided not to remain a Windrunner squire, he takes him to the [[Battle of Azimir]]. Adolin cites Colot's military knowledge as a reason for handpicking him. Colot acts as Adolin's second in command during the battle, reporting to him directly.

In the fight to take back Azimir, Colot became one of the original ten [[Unoathed]], summoning Shardplate and a Shardblade from deadeyes brought by [[Maya]] and taking back the Azimir throne room from the singer army, winning the country back as the sole country not covered by the [[Everstorm]] during the [[True Desolation]], besides Urithiru.
