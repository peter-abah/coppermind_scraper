Lady **Aving Cett** is a member of [[House Cett]] in [[Elendel]] on [[Scadrial]].{{book ref|mb4|1}}

## Appearance and Personality
She is older and has gray hair that she wears in a bun.{{book ref|mb4|1}} She owns expensive jewelry.{{book ref|mb4|1}} She is presumably the matriarch or a high-ranking member of her house, as she is the primary host of a party thrown at Cett Mansion.{{book ref|mb4|1}} Like most nobles, she is perplexed by [[Waxillium Ladrian]]'s lack of decorum.{{book ref|mb4|1}}

## History
During a party hosted by House Cett in {{date|341|FA}}, Aving stumbled upon Waxillium on a balcony. Wax was an eligible bachelor, and Aving urged him to come inside, claiming that there were people who wanted to meet him.{{book ref|mb4|1}} Wax promised that he would come inside momentarily, but instead used the [[Metallic Arts]] to disappear from the party without informing anyone.{{book ref|mb4|1}} Wax later agreed with [[Tillaume]] that he needed to send a letter to Lady Cett apologizing for his early departure.{{book ref|mb4|1}}
