**Xorm** is a [[deadeye]] [[spren]] on [[Roshar]] who went with [[Maya]] through [[Shadesmar]] to [[Azimir]] shortly before the [[Night of Sorrows]] began.{{book ref|sa5|135}} Xorm formed a [[Shardblade]] that [[Yanagawn]] took up in defense of Azimir, thereby becoming one of the [[Unoathed]].{{book ref|sa5|136}} Like all deadeyes, Xorm began healing as a result of [[Ba-Ado-Mishram]]'s release.{{book ref|sa5|147}}
