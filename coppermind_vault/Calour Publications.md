**Calour Publications** is a publisher in [[Elendel]] on [[Scadrial]]. It is owned by [[Lesan Calour]] and family, and is also referred to as Lesan Calour & Daughters & Sons. The company is headquartered in the 6th Octant of Elendel, at 211 Morise near the [[Elendel#Hub|Hub]] at the center of the city.{{file ref|House_Record_broadsheet.jpg|*The House Record* Broadsheet}}

The company publishes *[[The House Record]]* broadsheet. In the 1st of Doxil {{date|342|PC}} edition, they placed an advertisement in the broadsheet seeking samples from novelists, as they were looking to publish books similar to *[[The Horribles]]* and *[[Fear & Ferociousness]]*.{{file ref|House_Record_broadsheet.jpg|*The House Record* Broadsheet}}
