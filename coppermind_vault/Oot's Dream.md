The ***Oot's Dream*** is a boat that travels the twelve seas on [[Lumar]]. It is a vessel used by smugglers pretending to be merchants, who forged writs from the king to avoid paying taxes. 

## Appearance
The *Oot's Dream* is a small smuggling vessel disguised as a merchant ship. It has a single, small cannon on the foredeck. The ship has two masts, and is described best as being similar to a [[Wikipedia:brigantine|brigantine]].

## History
[[Tress]] snuck aboard the *Oot's Dream* while imitating a Royal Inspector, and consequently spooked the crew and captain, who believed she was onto them and poisoned her so they could capture her. She awoke and met [[Huck]] the rat who was also held captive. Soon after, the ship sank after being attacked by the *[[Crow's Song]]*, which used cannonballs with sabotaged timers, designed to sink instead of incapacitating the ship. [[Tress]] and [[Huck]] were the only survivors. Although the captain of the *Crow's Song* left another man alive on a lifeboat, he died before he could fulfill Captain [[Crow]]'s plan of making known which ship sank the *Oot's Dream*.
