**Abrial** is a [[Highprince]] of [[Jah Keved]].

In 1173, Abrial attended one of King [[Hanavanar]]'s feasts in [[Vedenar]]. During the feast, [[Szeth]] attempted to assassinate the king on [[Taravangian]]'s orders. During the fighting, Abrial was likely killed along with several other Veden highprinces, although reports of his death were at least initially unconfirmed.
