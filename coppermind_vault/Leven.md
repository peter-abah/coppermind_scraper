**Leven** is a cobbler from [[Hearthstone]] on [[Roshar]].

During the *[[Fourth Bridge]]*'s evacuation of Hearthstone in , [[Kaladin]] almost collided with Leven and his wife while chasing [[Leshwi]]. Leven and his wife screamed in terror, and Kaladin realized that he needed to stay away from bystanders.
