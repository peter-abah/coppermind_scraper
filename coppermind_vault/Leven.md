**Leven** is a cobbler from [[Hearthstone]] on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa4|5}}

During the *[[Fourth Bridge]]*'s evacuation of Hearthstone in {{Rosharan date|1175}}, [[Kaladin]] almost collided with Leven and his wife while chasing [[Leshwi]]. Leven and his wife screamed in terror, and Kaladin realized that he needed to stay away from bystanders.{{book ref|sa4|5}}
