**Caelinora** is an [[honorspren]] on [[Roshar]]. She is bonded to [[Huio]].{{book ref|sa3.5|7}}

## Attributes and Abilities
As an honorspren, Caelinora presumably has the inherent abilities of her species, including flight and shapeshifting. She can initiate a [[Nahel bond]], which allows her to transition to the [[Physical Realm]] and makes her bonded partner a [[Order of Windrunners|Windrunner]].

## History
Caelinora likely bonded Huio after the [[Battle of Thaylen Field]], as Huio had only recently become a [[Knights Radiant#Squires|Radiant squire]] at that point.{{book ref|sa3|46}} 

Huio and Caelinora were selected to join the [[expedition to Akinah]] in late {{rosharan date|1174}}. By that time, Huio had sworn the first two [[Order of Windrunners#Ideals of the Windrunners|Ideals of the Windrunners]].{{book ref|sa3.5|18}} Although Caelinora rarely appeared to [[Lopen]], she was visible to [[Cord]] since she was one of the [[Sighted]].{{book ref|sa3.5|7}}

Huio swore the Third Ideal during the [[Sleepless|hordeling]] battle on the shores of Akinah, advancing his bond with Caelinora;{{book ref|sa3.5|18}} this surprised Lopen, who was happy for the pair but a bit envious at first.{{book ref|sa3.5|19}}{{book ref|sa3.5|epilogue}} Huio wielded Caelinora as a massive living [[Shardblade|Shardhammer]] that could instantly transform into a Shardspear at his command.{{book ref|sa3.5|18}}{{book ref|sa3.5|19}}
