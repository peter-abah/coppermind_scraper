**Cael** is a commander in [[Dalinar]]'s army.

## History
He is head of the field command during the [[Battle of Narak]]. Both he and [[Apara|his wife]] are present during the battle. He reports of [[Aladar]]'s victory to [[Dalinar]], who sends Cael to order general retreat to the [[Oathgate]] plateau. He also points out the [[Everstorm]] to Dalinar. He dies when a [[highstorm]] collides with the Everstorm.
