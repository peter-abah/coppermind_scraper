**Flend** is a [[Thaylen]] sailor on the *[[Wandersail (1174_ship)|Wandersail]]*.

He was a member of the [[expedition to Akinah]] and had the day shift in the [[Skyeel#Cultural References|eel's nest]]. [[Rysn]] encouraged [[Nikli]] to ask Flend about [[Spren#Sailorspren|sailorspren]] in order to fit in better with the crew.
