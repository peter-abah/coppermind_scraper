Captain **Bedes** is an young [[noble]] in [[Luthadel]].

He is left in charge of the Steel Gate during the [[Battle of Luthadel]] after Lord [[Penrod]] calls away the gate's initial commander Lord [[Culee]].{{book ref|mb2|51}} [[Sazed]], who is also assigned to that gate, tries to encourage Bedes to order and lead the soldiers. Bedes does not believe that he can lead them, and asks Sazed to help.

During the [[koloss]] attack, Bedes is thrown by one and presumably doesn't survive.{{book ref|mb2|52}}
