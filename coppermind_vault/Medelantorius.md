**Medelantorius** is a [[Dragon (cosmere)|dragon]] and the original Vessel of the [[Shard]] [[Valor]].

## Abilities and Attributes
{{for|Valor#Associated Magic|the powers of Valor}}

As a [[Vessel]] of the [[Shard]] [[Valor]], Medelantorius is immortal and immensely powerful.

## History
Like all original [[Vessel]]s, Medelantorius is a native of [[Yolen]].{{wob ref|7939}} Before the [[Shattering of Adonalsium]], she was a warrior.{{book ref|sa5|115}} She also dated [[Hoid]] at some point.{{wob ref|16895}} Medelantorius participated in the [[Shattering of Adonalsium]], and took up one of the [[Shard]]s, [[Valor]].{{book ref|sa5|115}}
