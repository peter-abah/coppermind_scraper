**Joshua Bilmes** is [[Brandon Sanderson]]'s agent, who runs [http://awfulagent.com/ JABberwocky Literary Agency].

The revision of [[White Sand]] was what made Joshua pick up Brandon as a client, though Joshua did not think the novel was good enough, and wanted to see some of Brandon's other work. Joshua was instrumental in the revision process of [[Elantris (book)|Elantris]], where he persuaded Brandon to cut the [[Mad Prince]].{{wob ref|2139}}

He prefers to be called "Joshua", rather than "Josh".
