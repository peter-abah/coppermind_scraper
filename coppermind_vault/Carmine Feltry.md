Lady **Carmine Feltry** is a resident of [[Elendel]] on [[Scadrial]].

She is planning on opening a racing circuit exclusively for motorcars on the old fairgrounds near the [[Irongate River]].

==Trivia==
*Her last name is similar to that of Elendel politician [[Alloran Feltri]], but there is no confirmed connection between them.
