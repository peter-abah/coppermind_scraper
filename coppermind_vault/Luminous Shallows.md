**Luminous Shallows** is a location in the [[Sea of Lost Lights]], near the [[Glasswater Deep]] and the [[Caretaker of Laughter]].

In the [[Physical Realm]], this corresponds to the southern peninsula of [[Frostlands]], the section that contains the [[Shallow Crypts]].
