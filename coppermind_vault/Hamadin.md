**Hamadin** is a legendary [[Alethkar|Alethi]] warrior on [[Roshar]] who was said to have led fifty soldiers against ten thousand [[Jah Keved|Veden]] troops. His story was widely known, even to [[Thaylenah|Thaylen]] soldiers that were fighting in the [[Battle of Thaylen Field]].
