**Tasni** is an [[Alethi]] woman on [[Roshar]]. She is easygoing, honest and looks sun-weathered. Tasni is the captain of the [[Dalewillow (ship)|Dalewillow]].{{rpg ref|ss|6}} Tasni owns a leather-bound atlas worth 100 marks.{{rpg ref|ss|8}} On a voyage at some point during the [[True Desolation]], Tasni discovered a [[cremling]] infestation on her ship. After dealing with the swarms, she found a [[fabrial]] hidden inside of a crate labelled ‘[[longroot]]’. Tasni, realising that one of her passengers didn’t want it to be found, tasked a small party of people with finding the owner. They realised that the fabrial belonged to someone named [[Nenal]], who was going to use it to propose to her lover, [[Fasi]].{{rpg ref|ss|6}} It is unclear whether or not the plot was foiled, but that same night, the pair were engaged anyway.{{rpg ref|ss|8}}
