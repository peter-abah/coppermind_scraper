The **School of Storms** is an educational institution on [[Roshar]].

Enrollment in the School of Storms grants scholars the enviable benefit of access to the [[Palanaeum]] in [[Kharbranth]], indicating that the school is likely located nearby. Based on the name, the curriculum may include training for [[stormwarden]]s. 

One of [[Taravangian]]'s granddaughters, [[Gvori]], was accepted to study at the school around the advent of the [[True Desolation]].
