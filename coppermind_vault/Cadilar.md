Brightlord **Cadilar** is an elderly [[Alethi]] noble on the [[Shattered Plains]] associated with [[Highprince]] [[Vamah]].

On , Cadilar attended one of the feasts held by King [[Elhokar Kholin]]. There he encountered [[Wit]], who compared Cadilar's wizened face to that of Cadilar's own horse.

On , the operator of a waystop on Cadilar's lands allows [[Kaladin]] to shelter from a [[highstorm]], along with the [[singers]] who he was accompanying.
