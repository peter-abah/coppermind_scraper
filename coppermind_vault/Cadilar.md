Brightlord **Cadilar** is an elderly [[Alethi]] noble on the [[Shattered Plains]] associated with [[Highprince]] [[Vamah]].{{book ref|sa1|22}}{{book ref|sa3|23}}

On {{Rosharan date|1173|missing-date=y}}, Cadilar attended one of the feasts held by King [[Elhokar Kholin]].{{book ref|sa1|22}} There he encountered [[Wit]], who compared Cadilar's wizened face to that of Cadilar's own horse.

On {{Rosharan date|1174|missing-date=y}}, the operator of a waystop on Cadilar's lands allows [[Kaladin]] to shelter from a [[highstorm]], along with the [[singers]] who he was accompanying.{{book ref|sa3|23}}
