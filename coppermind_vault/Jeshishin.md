**Jeshishin** is a *[[shanay-im]]* [[Fused]] on [[Roshar]].

During the [[True Desolation]], Jeshishin was present when [[Raboniel]] proposed her plan to invade [[Urithiru]] at a meeting with the [[Nine]]. He had participated in a previous attack on the tower that was rebuffed, and demanded to know how she planned to make it into the well-protected heart of the tower. Raboniel told him that [[Taravangian]]'s espionage would allow her the access needed to successfully repurpose the tower's defenses.
