An **Ornisaur** is a type of creature on Scadrial. However, it is unknown if they actually existed. According to Nicelle Sauvage, there is a [[Ornisaur Quarry|quarry]] that contains paintings of large feathered Ornisaurs. The quarry also contained an aluminum Ornisaur skeleton. Ornisaurs have long and thin bones, with bat like wings. They are also said to have feathers, and a long and sharp beak. The Kandra Tabaar and KeSun merged to become a great, albeit featherless, Ornisaur, and this event led to one of Nicelle’s stories being named ‘The Flight of the Ornisaur’.{{file ref|The Two Seasons broadsheet.jpg|*The Two Seasons* Broadsheet}}
