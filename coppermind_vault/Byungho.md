**Byungho** is a member of the [[Fabian Augments]] cartel. He was with [[Ulric Stromfin]] and [[Marshal]] in [[Maelport]] when they came to the building with the portal where [[Runian]] and [[Quinn]] were fighting. He and Marshal fought with [[Ealstan]] outside, while Ulric entered the building to speak with the fighters. Byungho was killed by [[Ealstan]] when [[Logna]] disrupted the nanites in his body.
