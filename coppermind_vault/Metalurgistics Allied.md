**Metalurgistics Allied** is a firm hiring out [[Metalborn]] in [[Elendel]] on [[Scadrial]].{{file ref|Elendel_Daily_broadsheet.png|*The Elendel Daily* Broadsheet}}

They are located on Carronberry Square in the 7th Octant of Elendel. They primarily hire out [[Misting]]s, but [[Ferring]]s can usually be supplied with advance notice. [[Jarrington]] is in charge of recruitment, and Metalborn interested in employment are directed to speak with him.{{file ref|Elendel_Daily_broadsheet.png|*The Elendel Daily* Broadsheet}}
