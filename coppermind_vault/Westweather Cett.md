Lord **Westweather Cett** is the heir to the Cett fortune in [[Elendel]], circa .

[[Wax]] headbutts him at a party and breaks his nose, after Cett demands to duel him.

Wax later refers to him "that smarmy little man with the ridiculous mustache", though this may not be a fully accurate description of his appearance.
