**Mvylrn** is a [[Thaylen]] gambler on [[Roshar]]. He has a broad grin and wears many glittering rings—which are all cheap knockoffs. Mvylrn generally wants to hide his gambling losses and is receptive to bribery. At some point during the [[True Desolation]], he paid fifty marks and became a passenger on a ship called the *[[Dalewillow (ship)|Dalewillow]]*. During the voyage, Mvylrn noticed [[Nenal]] sneaking down to the cargo hold.
