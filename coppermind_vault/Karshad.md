> Ker’Naisha’Totar’Kersha!
> — 'May the [[Sand Lord]] bless us!' in Karshad

**Karshad** is a holy language spoken by members of the Priest [[DaiKeen]], the clergy of the [[Ker'reen]] religion in [[Kerzta]] on [[Taldain]].

When [[Khriss]] and her team first arrived on [[Dayside]], she and [[Cynder]] had learned some Karshad by studying Daysider books. When Cynder spoke the language to the locals, they seemed to understand but quickly fled in fear. The Darksider team then overheard a priest (possibly the [[A'kar]]) speaking Karshad, and realized that they had inadvertently learned a holy language. Cynder was considered to be [[Elis]]'s foremost authority on Daysider linguistics, and his ignorance of the common language was symptomatic of the schism between the two regions.
