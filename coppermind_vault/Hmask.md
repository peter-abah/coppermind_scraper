**Hmask** is a [[Thaylen]] member of the [[Cobalt Guard]] who later becomes one of the [[Unoathed]] on [[Roshar]].

==Appearance and Personality==
Hmask is a bearded fellow with long white Thaylen eyebrows and a white mustache. Hmask is often seen happy and smiling.  In difficult situations where others see the negative aspects of a situation, Hmask can be seen with an eager smile, ready to take on the challenge.

==Attributes and Abilites==
Hmask explains his loyalty to [[Adolin Kholin]] is because in the [[Battle of Thaylen Field]] Adolin had saved Hmask's son from a breaking and crumbling building. Hmask's loyalty as a soldier was demonstrated on the second day of the Battle for [[Azimir]] when Hmask had volunteered to be close to Adolin, even though it wasn't his shift. Roughly two hours into the assault, Adolin had to rush forward through the middle of the enemy position in order to save another Shardbearer who had fallen while under attack. After fighting off many of the [[Direform]]s and reaching the other Shardbearer, Adolin looked around and saw that Hmask had followed him across the enemy fortification.

==History==
Hmask is initially one of Adolin’s support squad during his first confrontation with the fused named [[Abidi]] the Monarch, during the Battle for Azimir. Adolin is tripped to the ground during the fight and Hmask puts himself between the fused and Adolin, giving Adolin time to recover.

After the confrontation, Adolin inquired of [[Colot]], a member of his Cobalt Guard, about the identity of the man who had protected him. After hearing, he was a particularly enthusiastic foreign soldier who seemed specifically loyal to Adolin, Hmask was promoted to a full member of the Cobalt Guard and given a uniform.

During one of his training sessions with [[Yanagawn]], Adolin noticed Hmask standing guard and remarked to Yanagawn that he would like to know Hmask's personal story. Yanagawn, knowing the Theylan language, helped facilitate Hmask being able to explain, using concise language, that Adolin had saved his son in the Battle of Thaylen Field while fighting a [[Thunderclast]].
