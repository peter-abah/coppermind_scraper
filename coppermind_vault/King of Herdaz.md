The **King of Herdaz** is the Herdazian monarch ruling before and during the True Desolation. The king is beloved by the people. Other then that, little is known about him and his family; he has no daughters, and he is from a powerful Reshi house. It is also known that either he or one of his sons (if he has any) has a [[Shardblade]] and a set of [[Shardplate]].

Once per year, the king invites commoner representatives from each Herdazian ranch to attend their designated chimney in the capital, releasing synchronized trails of smoke to represent unity. The Herdazian monarchy refuses to get involved in land disputes. Under enemy occupation, the king takes it on himself to personally burn a glyph in each of the 203 palace chimneys during [[Unity Day]]. The single wisps of smoke periodically signal the king’s devotion to his people, as he believes war makes commoners of everyone.
