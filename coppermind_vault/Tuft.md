**Tuft** is a beggar that lives in the slums of [[T'Telir]] on [[Nalthis]].{{book ref|wb|54}}

## Appearance and personality
Tuft is missing an eye and does not cover the empty socket with a patch.{{book ref|wb|54}} He is usually one of the first people to arrive on the street corners to beg in the morning.{{book ref|wb|54}} He is presumably [[Idrian]], as [[Denth]] remarks that "everyone" who lives in the slums hails from Idris.{{book ref|wb|31}} He is gruff when interacting with [[Vivenna]], saying he "don't owe nobody nothing" and calling Vivenna a "plant" for living as a beggar when she didn't have to.{{book ref|wb|54}}

## History
[[Vivenna]] meets Tuft at some point when she is living on the streets as a [[Drab]]; he kicks her out of his usual begging spot three times.{{book ref|wb|54}} She later visits him seeking clues to the location of [[Nightblood]]. He is not cooperative until Vivenna gives him a valuable handkerchief. He then directs her to search a tenement near the Third Dock, where she eventually finds Nightblood with [[Chapps]].{{book ref|wb|54}}
