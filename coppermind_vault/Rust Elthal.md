**Rust Elthal** is an officer and a [[Shardbearer]] in [[Dalinar Kholin]]'s army on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa1|69}}

## Appearance and Personality
Rust is a limber man, and he has a distinguished face with a thin moustache.{{book ref|sa1|69}}

As an officer of Dalinar's army, he proves himself to be a competent and loyal leader. Dalinar trusts him with a set of [[Shardplate]], and with it he becomes a greater asset to the army.{{book ref|sa2|81}}

## History
Rust Elthal was part of Dalinar's army on the [[Shattered Plains]] during the [[War of Reckoning]]. He was the senior-most officer to survive the [[Battle of the Tower]], where he fought close by Dalinar. He injured his arm during the fighting. After the army was rescued by [[Bridge Four]], Dalinar directed Rust to take the wounded back to his warcamp and to tell [[Teleb]] to put the camp on alert.{{book ref|sa1|69}}

A few months later, Rust received a set of Shardplate when [[Adolin Kholin]] won several sets during his duels with the other [[highprince]]s' Shardbearers.{{book ref|sa2|66}}{{book ref|sa2|81}} During the [[Battle of Narak]], he and [[Serugiadis]], under the command of Teleb, fought at the head of Highprince [[Sebarial]]'s army with their Shards.{{book ref|sa2|81}} As the [[Alethi]] armies retreated to the [[Oathgate]] at the end of the battle, Rust helped guide some of Dalinar's mechanical bridges into position to span the chasm.{{book ref|sa2|86}} He then relocated to [[Urithiru]] with the majority of the Alethi warcamps.{{book ref|sa3|2}}{{book ref|sa3|64}} Rust and Serugiadis practiced with [[Shardbow]]s on the plateau at the base of the tower on the day Dalinar first visited [[Azimir]] to discuss joining the [[coalition of monarchs]].{{book ref|sa3|64}}
