**Khesh** is a bridgeman on [[Roshar]]. He is a physically imposing, albeit slow-witted, man. At some point during the [[War of Reckoning]], Khesh, originally a soldier, was assigned to [[Bridge Nine]]. He wasn’t branded though. Later, while he was twenty-four, he was part of an expedition to research a group of recently uncovered ruins. The expedition was surprised by a [[chasmfiend]] while crossing a bridge though, and Khesh was thrown into the chasm when the bridge broke. The bridge fell on top of him, although he survived the fall.
