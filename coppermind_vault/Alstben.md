**Alstben** is a [[Thaylen]] sailor on the *[[Wandersail (1174_ship)|Wandersail]]*. He is a tall man and likes to spike his eyebrows.

He was a crew member of the *Wandersail* for the [[expedition to Akinah]]. When the ship encountered what appeared to be a dead [[santhid]], Alstben took it as a bad omen and wanted to abort the mission.
