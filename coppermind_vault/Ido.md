**Ido** is the leader of a pirate faction in the [[belt]] of the [[nowhere]].{{book ref|sky3|29}} 

## History
Ido attended the duel to determine the pirate champion, along with the other faction leaders. After the duel, when [[Peg]] proposed a unified assault on the [[Superiority]] base of [[Surehold]], Ido was initially skeptical. He didn’t want to anger the Superiority, or suffer retribution. Peg explains that Surehold has a reality icon and reality ashes, which helps to convince Ido. Ido conferred with his faction members, and eventually agreed to join the assault.{{book ref|sky3|29}} With the help of Ido and his faction, the assault succeeded, and they took control of Surehold.{{book ref|cytonic|35}}
