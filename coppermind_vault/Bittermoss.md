**Bittermoss** is a type of food from [[ReDawn (planet)|ReDawn]]. [[Alanik]] compares the algae strips eaten on [[Detritus]] to it, noting that both have a deep and earthy taste, though the algae strips have a somewhat sharper aftertaste.{{book ref|sf2|5}}
