**Kevron's** is a "quick-eats" cafe in [[Bilming]] on [[Scadrial]]. It began operation around the year {{date|348|PC}} and is located at the corner of 2nd and Nellis. It ran an advertisement in *[[The Two Seasons]]* seeking a [[bendalloy]] [[Allomancy#Types of Allomancers|Misting]] cook; as well as a high salary and bonuses, applicants were promised a bendalloy stipend, good hours, one day off per week, and days off for [[The Path|Harmontide]] or [[Church of the Survivor|Survivorday]]. They were asked to apply in person at the cafe.{{file ref|The_Two_Seasons_broadsheet.jpg|*The Two Seasons* broadsheet}}
