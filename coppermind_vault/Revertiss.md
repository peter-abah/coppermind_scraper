**Revertiss** is a famous [[Fjorden|Fjordell]] dish on [[Sel]].{{book ref|elantris|8}}{{glossary ref|Elantris|R}}

Shortly after [[Sarene]] arrived in [[Arelon]], [[Kiin]] made revertiss for lunch. The promise of revertiss encouraged Sarene to leave [[Iadon]]'s court and go to Kiin's house for lunch.{{book ref|elantris|8}}
