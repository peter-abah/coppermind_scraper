**Revertiss** is a famous [[Fjorden|Fjordell]] dish on [[Sel]].

Shortly after [[Sarene]] arrived in [[Arelon]], [[Kiin]] made revertiss for lunch. The promise of revertiss encouraged Sarene to leave [[Iadon]]'s court and go to Kiin's house for lunch.
