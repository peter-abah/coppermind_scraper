**Crispmelons** are a type of fruit native to [[Roshar]].

[[Gallant]], [[Highprince]] [[Dalinar Kholin]]'s [[Ryshadium]],  is fed crispmelons as a reward for good behavior, but will only eat them when he feels he deserves them.
