Callsign **Sushi** is a member of [[Nightmare Flight]], along with [[Nose]], [[Bog]], [[Nord]], [[Jager]], and [[Tunestone]].

As part of their training, Skyward Flight were paired with Nightmare Flight for a routine debris tagging mission, during which they are attacked by the [[varvax|Krell]]. Sushi, and flightmate Nord, were assigned to accompany [[Jorgen]] during the tagging exercise.

Later, Nightmare Flight participated in the Second Battle of Alta.
