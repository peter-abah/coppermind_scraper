Callsign **Sushi** is a member of [[Nightmare Flight]], along with [[Nose]], [[Bog]], [[Nord]], [[Jager]], and [[Tunestone]].{{book ref|Skyward|46}}{{book ref|sky1|9}}

As part of their training, Skyward Flight were paired with Nightmare Flight for a routine debris tagging mission, during which they are attacked by the [[varvax|Krell]]. Sushi, and flightmate Nord, were assigned to accompany [[Jorgen]] during the tagging exercise.{{book ref|Skyward|46}}

Later, Nightmare Flight participated in the Second Battle of Alta.{{book ref|sky1|48}}
