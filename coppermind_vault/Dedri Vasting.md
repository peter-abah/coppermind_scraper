**Dedri Vasting** is a [[noble]]man on [[Scadrial]] during the [[Final Empire]].

He is one of the members of the [[Luthadel Assembly]] after the [[Collapse]]. He is present for the [[Battle of Hathsin]] and was one of the only surviving members of [[Luthadel]]'s government at that time.
