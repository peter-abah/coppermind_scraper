**Dawncity** is a term used to describe a city on [[Roshar]] that is old enough to be able to trace the origin of said city all the way back to the [[Dawnsingers]].

As of , discussing the Dawncities had recently become fashionable among the [[Alethi]] [[lighteyes]]. In fact, Highprince [[Hatham]]'s wife studied the Dawncities. [[Au-nak]] believed that the city of [[Sesemalex Dar]] was a Dawncity, due to the complex stone patterns that the city was built within. In [[Dalinar]]'s [[Dalinar's visions|visions]], he saw no sign of any of the Dawncities. The Alethi city of [[Kholinar]] and the [[Iriali]] city of [[Rall Elorim]] are known to have been built on the site of a dawncity.

It is believed that they may have been created by the Dawnsingers making use of an early form of [[Stoneshaping]] to shape cities in the stone. They may also have been created with the use of [[Surgebinding]], although they are beyond the scope of what a single or group of Radiants could create using the power of [[Soulcasting]].
