The **Battle of Aheleha** was a large and destructive battle fought at [[Aheleha]] by the [[Malwish Empire]] and at least one other unknown nation, likely Rosharan in origin. It is not known precisely when this battle happened, but it must have been shortly before the discovery of the [[Patji's Eye|Drominad Perpendicularity]]. The Malwish had troops on the ground, and the other side is known to have set the very sky ablaze, creating so much smoke that it suddenly seemed midnight. Colonel [[Dajer]] fought, and was injured, at Aheleha.
