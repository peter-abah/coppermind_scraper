The **Battle of Aheleha** was a large and destructive battle fought at [[Aheleha]] by the [[Malwish Empire]] and at least one other unknown nation, likely Rosharan in origin.{{ref|text=In Isles of the Emberdark, the Malwish are mentioned to be at war with Rosharans.}} It is not known precisely when this battle happened, but it must have been shortly before the discovery of the [[Patji's Eye|Drominad Perpendicularity]].{{ref|text=Dajer fought at Aheleha, and he’s not terribly old during Isles of the Emberdark.}} The Malwish had troops on the ground, and the other side is known to have set the very sky ablaze, creating so much smoke that it suddenly seemed midnight. Colonel [[Dajer]] fought, and was injured, at Aheleha.{{book ref|iote|38}}
