**Bloodivy** is a plant native to [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa1|2}}

Named for its crimson color, it can be dried and ground to support the immune system and act as an expectorant for phlegm{{rpg ref|slwg|12}}.

Administering two crushed handfuls of bloodivy per day is an effective treatment for the [[grindings]].{{book ref|sa1|2}}
