**Fimkn** is a [[Thaylen]] sailor and medic on the *[[Wandersail (1174_ship)|Wandersail]]*.{{book ref|sa3.5|11}}

He was a member of the [[expedition to Akinah]] and became friends with [[Lopen]] during the journey. After Lopen rescued [[Turlm]], who had fallen overboard, Fimkn came over to check on Turlm and then sent him belowdecks as another sailor had already come up to replace him.{{book ref|sa3.5|11}} During the fight against the [[Sleepless]] on [[Akinah]], Fimkn worked to bind the wounds of one of the men that was wounded.{{book ref|sa3.5|18}}
