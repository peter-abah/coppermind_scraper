**Nistar** is a [[Regal#Nightform|nightform]] [[Regal]] on [[Roshar]]. Nistar translates into "mystery".

## History
During the [[singer]] occupation of [[Urithiru]], [[Lezian]] posted Nistar and [[Urialin]] to guard the [[Knights Radiant|Radiant]] infirmary where [[Lirin]] and [[Hesina]] worked, likely at [[Raboniel]]'s suggestion.

## Trivia
* Their name likely comes from the Hebrew word "nistar" (נסתר) which means "hidden".
