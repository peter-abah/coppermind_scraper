**Scragglebark** is a type of plant on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa1|46}}

[[Rock]] compares [[Kaladin]]'s beard to scragglebark branches.{{book ref|sa1|46}}

Scragglebark can be used to make an arrow's fletching, which is what the [[listeners]] did in the [[War of Reckoning]].{{book ref|sa1|65}}
