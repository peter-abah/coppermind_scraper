**Scragglebark** is a type of plant on [[Roshar]].

[[Rock]] compares [[Kaladin]]'s beard to scragglebark branches.

Scragglebark can be used to make an arrow's fletching, which is what the [[listeners]] did in the [[War of Reckoning]].
