The **Quiet River** is centrally located on [[Mirandus]]. The Quiet River starts at [[Uls Ydin]], moving to the north-west, separating the [[Blackened Lands]] from the [[Kingdoms of Light]]. The [[Backflow]] connects to the Quiet River near the [[Warfields of Karkun]].{{map ref|Mirandus}}
