**Elors Durnsed** is the leader of the [[United Tradeworkers Union]] on [[Scadrial]].{{file ref|Elendel_Daily_broadsheet.png|*The Elendel Daily* broadsheet}}

In {{date|341|PC}}, Durnsed was the leader of the trade union during tense negotiations with the [[Noble Houses Collective]]. The talks hit an impasse, but Durnsed abruptly withdrew his opposition to closing the negotiations, allowing the existing contract to continue.{{file ref|Elendel_Daily_broadsheet.png|*The Elendel Daily* broadsheet}}

Many rank-and-file union members were enraged by Durnsed's capitulation, which he did not explain. Rumors swirled that Durnsed had been corrupted during secret meetings with representatives of the nobles; a political cartoon in  *The Elendel Daily* depicted him as a sycophant willing to accept bribes.{{file ref|Elendel_Daily_broadsheet.png|*The Elendel Daily* broadsheet}} Durnsed's actions contributed to the labor unrest in Elendel that continued for several years.{{file ref|New_Ascendancy_broadsheet.jpg|*The New Ascendancy* broadsheet}}
