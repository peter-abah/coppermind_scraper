**Helenntion** was a member of the Council of [[Khlennium]] on [[Scadrial]]. He was one of the last survivors of the Council, although the timeline of Khlennium's dissolution after the [[Ascension of the Lord Ruler]] is unclear.

[[Tindwyl]] found a useful piece of information in his biography while she and [[Sazed]] were researching the [[Terris Prophecies]]. The biography contained a direct quote from a prophecy that had previously been overlooked because the text used the Khlenni term *Rabzeen* to refer to the Hero of Ages.
