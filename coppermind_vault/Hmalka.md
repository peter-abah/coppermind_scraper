**Hmalka** is [[Rysn]]'s superior at the [[Thaylen Gemstone Reserve]].

Shortly before the [[Battle of Thaylen Field]], she was visited by [[Vstim]]; she told him she was satisfied with Rysn's work but concerned for her happiness.
