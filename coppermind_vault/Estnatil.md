**Estnatil** is a [[Thaylen]] [[Shardbearer]] and a highguard who lived in [[Thaylen City]] on [[Roshar]] during the [[Era of Solitude]].

Estnatil possesses a [[Shardblade]] and as such is a member of one of the eight Thaylen houses to own Shards. 

Estnatil likely received his Shard from his father in his youth.  After receiving his Shard, he served as a member of the highguard, protecting the Thaylen throne. During the [[Battle of Thaylen Field]], Estnatil was standing on the city wall when the [[thunderclast]] [[Kai-garnis]] broke it. He was killed and he and his Shardblade were buried beneath the rubble of the wall. After patching the city wall, [[Jasnah Kholin]] detected his [[Shardblade]] and [[Soulcast]] the rubble covering it to smoke. [[Adolin Kholin]] gave Estnatil's Blade to Fen's son [[Kdralk]], ordering him to gather men and attempt to hold the Low Ward of Thaylen City.
