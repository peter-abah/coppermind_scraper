> You don't survive like the Mink has without learning to wiggle out of situations others could never escape.<br>
Is no like he needs us a lot. The Ganlos Riera herself couldn’t catch him!
> — Two Herdazian soldiers discussing [[Dieno enne Calah]]'s evasiveness

The **Ganlos Riera** is a female individual on [[Roshar]]. It is not clear if "Ganlos Riera" is a title, a name, or some other descriptor.

The Ganlos Riera is likely related to [[Herdaz]] in some way; the [[Dieno|Mink]]'s soldiers explain to [[Kaladin]] that the general is one of the few people that would be able to elude the Ganlos Riera.
