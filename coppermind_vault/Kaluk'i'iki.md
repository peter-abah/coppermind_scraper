**Kaluk'i'iki**, Unkalaki for “she who the many hands of death cannot grasp”, are Unkalaki huntresses. They lead the Unkalaki hunting parties. This matriarchal tradition teaches girls and women to hone their reflexes, and above all, to trust that their feelings are objectively informed by a multitude of subtle signs.

Kaluk’i’iki train alone for years to earn their own respect before asking hunters to join them in the wilds. When the Unkalaki warriors are away from home, it is considered to be the duty of the kaluk’i’iki and their hunters to defend their villages. Kaluk’i’iki assist the [[worldhopper|strange gods]] who arrive on Roshar through [[Cultivation's Perpendicularity]] with traveling down the mountains into [[Jah Keved]]. In exchange, the kaluk’i’iki ask to be paid in otherworldly gifts, which they share with the village’s hunters.
