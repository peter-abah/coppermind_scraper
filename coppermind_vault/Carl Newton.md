**Carl Newton** is a [[checkout]] and a member of [[ICON]]. He has a nasal voice, and [[Holly Winseed (Provisional Replica)|Holly]] calls him Nasal Man in her head. He has curly red hair, muscles, and a piercing in his left nostril. He was a former lab technician, and his lab equipment was stolen two weeks after he checked out.{{book ref|og|10}} He joined ICON, and was part of the fire team that kidnapped Holly’s Provisional Replica.{{book ref|og|5}}{{book ref|og|10}}
