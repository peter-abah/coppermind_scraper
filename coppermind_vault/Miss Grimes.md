**Miss Grimes** is the steward of [[House Ladrian]] in [[Elendel]] on [[Scadrial]].{{book ref|mb4|2}}

## Appearance and Personality
Miss Grimes is a plump, middle-aged, matronly woman.{{book ref|mb4|2}}{{book ref|sa5|epilogue}} While performing her duties, she usually wears the black dress and white apron of a member of the Ladrian household staff.{{book ref|mb4|2}} At other times, she wears drab, formal clothing that she tries to spruce up with a splash of color.{{book ref|sa5|epilogue}} She wears glasses, possibly just for reading.{{book ref|sa5|epilogue}} 

She takes her duties as house steward seriously and is rather unflappable in her role. She is in charge of hiring members of the staff and the day-to-day operations of Ladrian Mansion.{{book ref|sa5|epilogue}}{{book ref|mb4|2}} She keeps the House Lord informed about any matters that need their attention; this is not limited to the mansion, and can also include issues with their business ventures or interactions with other houses.{{book ref|mb4|2}}

Miss Grimes is a motherly figure to Wax, fussing over his marital status and giving him her advice whether he wants it or not. She claims to have a "[[Brass|Soother]]'s sense" for things related to marriage and romance. She knows Wax's family well and still sees him as a young man in need of guidance. This is partly because she considers any man who is unmarried to be a child, which is ironic considering that she herself is unmarried.{{book ref|mb4|2}} Like [[Tillaume]], Miss Grimes disapproves of Wax's behavior when he slips back into his old [[Roughs]] habits.{{book ref|mb4|3}}

## History

Miss Grimes became steward of [[House Ladrian]] circa {{date|321|PC}}, around the time that [[Waxillium]] left [[Elendel]] for the [[Roughs]].{{book ref|mb4|1}}{{book ref|mb4|2}} She was familiar with Wax's parents, particularly his mother. She may have served as a confidant of sorts for his mother, who spent much of her time worrying about Wax after he left for the Roughs.{{book ref|mb4|2}} She is presumably familiar with other members of House Ladrian, including Edwarn and [[Telsin]].

When Wax returned to take control of his house in {{date|341|PC}} after his [[Edwarn Ladrian|uncle's]] apparent death, Miss Grimes retained her position as house steward. As Wax was preparing to meet with Lord [[Jackstom Harms]] and Lady [[Steris Harms|Steris]] to discuss his potential betrothal, Miss Grimes accompanied him to the room and tried to help him maintain a positive attitude. At his prodding, she informed him that the [[Vanishers]] had stolen a second shipment of steel from House Ladrian the previous evening. She had hoped to withhold this information because she thought it would distract him in the meeting with the Harms family.{{book ref|mb4|2}}

Around the same time, Miss Grimes interviewed [[Hoid]] when he applied to become House Ladrian's new coachman. Hoid had not been on the planet long, and had previously been posing as a beggar. Hoid found Miss Grimes rather staid, but was somewhat impressed when she ignored most of his usual jokes and enigmatic statements. Miss Grimes hired Hoid for the job, noting that he seemed competent, if a bit odd. She mentioned that it had been difficult to fill the position after the accident that resulted in the "deaths" of Edward and Telsin.{{book ref|sa5|epilogue}} Hoid remained in the position for several years.{{book ref|mb7|20}}

Later in {{date|341|PC}}, when [[Tillaume]] attempted to assassinate Wax on [[Edwarn Ladrian|Mister Suit]]'s orders, he drugged Miss Grimes to knock her out and hid her in a closet with [[Limmi]] so they wouldn't be injured in the explosion. Wax carried the two of them out into the garden behind the manor, where he hoped they would be discovered by his coachman [[Krent]] or one of the constables.{{book ref|mb4|9}}

In {{date|342|PC}}, [[Harmony]] suddenly spoke to Wax about his earring being a [[Hemalurgic]] spike. Harmony foresaw that Miss Grimes would have to take the Ladrian coach in for repairs if Wax got spooked and fired a bullet into the furnishings.{{book ref|mb5|7}}
