**Lessie** is a resident of the [[Roughs]] and is married to [[Waxillium Ladrian]].

While hunting [[Bloody Tan]] in his lair in the town of [[Feltrel]], Lessie and Wax split up. Bloody Tan took Lessie hostage and used her as a human shield. When Wax attempted to shoot him, Bloody Tan pulled Lessie into the path of the bullet. It struck her in the head, killing her instantly.
