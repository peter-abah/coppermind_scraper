**Walin** is a prisoner at the [[Pits of Hathsin]] on [[Scadrial]]. Like most prisoners in the Pits he has many long cuts on his arms.{{book ref|mb1|32}}

## History
Walin was sent to the [[Pits of Hathsin]] as punishment for trying to steal food from his plantation master.{{book ref|mb1|32}}

He was barely fed at the Pits, his fingers kept searching for geodes instinctively and he was too tired to care about entering the mists or getting beaten. Walin usually climbed up to the surface to sleep, but as he was an older man, he had to look out for younger, stronger men who might attack him to steal his [[atium]] geode.
He wasn't sure if such a life was better than being dead.{{book ref|mb1|32}}

Walin was eventually freed by [[Kelsier]], who was about to destroy the mines, just after he managed to find an atium geode the final day of the week. Although he first thought that Kelsier wanted to steal his geode, after being encouraged by Kelsier, Walin left the Pits to sell the atium to secure his freedom.{{book ref|mb1|32}}
