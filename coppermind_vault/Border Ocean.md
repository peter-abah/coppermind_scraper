The **Border Ocean** is a massive ocean that covers a large portion of [[Taldain]].{{map ref|Dayside}} It surrounds the [[Dayside]] continent and separates it from [[Darkside]].{{book ref|ws|part=ars}} It comprises the Northern Border Ocean and Southern Border Ocean, although there is no obvious separation between the two sections.{{map ref|Dayside}}

The Border Ocean is navigable by experienced sailors, but entering the wrong currents can prevent ships from making the crossing between continents.{{book ref|ws|prologue}} Directly between Dayside and Darkside, along the center of the ocean, is a powerful, perpetual storm known as the [[terminal storm]].{{book ref|ws|prologue}}{{book ref|ws|part=ars}} The terminal storm includes thunderstorms and constant driving rain; it can take several days to sail through it, depending on wind conditions.{{book ref|ws|prologue}} 

The sea is full of oxygen-producing algae, which provide much of the oxygen in Taldain's atmosphere.{{wob ref|4160}}

## History
Travel through the Border Ocean from Darkside to Dayside was not only difficult due to environmental conditions, it was also forbidden by Emperor [[Skathan]] of the [[Dynasty]].{{book ref|ws|prologue}} However, it was not impossible to make the journey, as evidenced by the presence of Darksiders in places like [[Lonzare]]. Prince [[Gevalden]] of [[Elis]] came to Dayside in search of the [[Diem|sand masters]], but supposedly died under mysterious circumstances, prompting Duchess [[Khrissalla]] to form her own expedition. Khrissalla's team sailed across the ocean with some difficulty; Khriss was particularly fascinated by the terminal storm.{{book ref|ws|prologue}} The team crossed back over the Border Ocean on their return to Darkside.{{book ref|ws|18}}
