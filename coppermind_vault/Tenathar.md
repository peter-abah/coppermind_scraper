**Tenathar** is a ranked duelist in [[Alethkar]] on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa3|88}} He is presumably a [[lighteyed]] member of the Alethi nobility in [[Kholinar]]. 

Circa {{Rosharan date|1166|2}}, Tenathar agreed to a dueling match with [[Adolin Kholin]], who was fifteen at the time. It is implied that Tenathar was the first ranked opponent that Adolin ever dueled, and Adolin considered the match his first step towards winning a [[Shardblade]]. Adolin eagerly sent his father [[Dalinar Kholin|Dalinar]] a message to tell him the news of the bout, and then personally invited him to attend the match.{{book ref|sa3|88}} The outcome of the duel is unknown. Years later, Adolin recalled being quite nervous before his first ranked duel.{{book ref|sa4|87}}
