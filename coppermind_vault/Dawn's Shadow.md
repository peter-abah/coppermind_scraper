**Dawn's Shadow** is a location, presumably a settlement, on the coast of far eastern [[Roshar]].{{map ref|Roshar}}

It is located east of the [[Unclaimed Hills]], on a sea connected to the [[Ocean of Origins]].{{map ref|Roshar}} It is situated in the former [[Silver Kingdoms|Silver Kingdom]] of [[Natanatan]].{{map ref|Silver Kingdoms}} The nearest population center is the city-state of [[New Natanan]].{{map ref|Roshar}}

Although eastern Roshar has been described as a "crumbling"{{book ref|sa3|i|1}} empty wasteland,{{book ref|sa3|67}} Dawn's Shadow is known to some mapmakers in [[Alethkar]].{{book ref|sa2|60}}
