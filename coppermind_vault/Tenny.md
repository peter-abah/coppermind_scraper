**Tenny** is a stuffed [[kandra]] owned by [[Maxillium Ladrian]] on [[Scadrial]]. Tenny is a [[Soonie pup]], a stuffed animal representing [[TenSoon|TenSoon]] the kandra's pre-Catacendre wolfhound form. "Tenny" is likely a childlike play on the name "TenSoon" that Maxillium created. Maxillium often hugs Tenny when he is sad.
