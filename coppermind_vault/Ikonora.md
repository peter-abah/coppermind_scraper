**Ikonora** is a [[nightmare]] [[painter]] in [[Kilahito]] on [[Komashi]]. When [[Nikaro]] was looking for help in the anticipated [[battle of Kilahito]], [[Akane]] proposed asking Ikonora to join them. Akane figured that with the painters Ikonora could gather, they might have twenty or thirty nightmare painters.
