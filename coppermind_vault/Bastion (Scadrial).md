**Bastion** is an arms manufacturer in [[Elendel]] on [[Scadrial]]. In , Bastion made a new semiautomatic rifle, which the Elendel constabulary promptly bought and gave to their constables to train with. It is likely that they were among the first to create semiautomatic guns. [[Marasi Colms]] used a Bastion rifle during the raid she and [[Wayne]] undertook of the smuggling base set up below Elendel.
