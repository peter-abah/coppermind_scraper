**Tet Rebin** is a red-haired [[Veden]] man on [[Roshar]].{{rpg ref|slsw|24}} He is a balding{{rpg ref|slsw|24}} wine merchant with a teenage daughter named [[Hana (Roshar)|Hana]]. Rebin is good friends with a [[Thaylen]] merchant named [[Svalka]].{{rpg ref|slsw|20}} Rebin’s wine business is his entire livelihood, and losing even a single wagon has the potential to cripple him.{{rpg ref|slsw|25}} He owns some expensive wines, such as an auburn wine worth 150 marks,{{rpg ref|slsw|33}} and supplies wine to the [[Ornery Chull]].{{rpg ref|slsw|36}}

Shortly before the [[Everstorm]] arrived on [[Roshar]], Rebin and his daughter traveled to the warcamps on the [[Shattered Plains]]. Close to the [[Covenant Stone Crossroads]], their wagon got stuck in a pool of wet crem, and Rebin sent Hana to the crossroads to see if anyone could help.{{rpg ref|slsw|24}} A few people came to help him, but while they were there, trying to free the wagon, they got ambushed by bandits. It’s unclear if Rebin survived the encounter, though the bandits were repelled with the help of a nearby caravan of [[Alethi]] soldiers.{{rpg ref|slsw|29}} Rebin planned to stay at an inn called the Ornery Chull after arriving in the warcamps.{{rpg ref|slsw|33}}
