The **underbloods** are a group of people in [[Newcago]]; possibly a gang, or servants of some [[Epic]] from another city. They seem to be opposed to [[Steelheart]]'s regime, as [[Curveball]] and his underlings fight with them occasionally.{{book ref|steelheart|2}}

The underbloods kill one of Curveball's men, [[Eddie Macano]], two days before [[David]] attempts to blend in by pretending to be on site on orders from Eddie. David isn't aware of that, which blows his cover.{{book ref|steelheart|2}}
