**Carlee** is a young [[noble]]woman in the [[Final Empire]] on [[Scadrial]].{{book ref|mb1|23}} 

She is married to Lord [[Tyden]]. She has a purring voice, and rarely speaks except to make jabs at her husband. She is present at a ball at [[Keep Hasting]] and hints that Tyden foolishly fell victim to a robbery or scam perpetrated by [[skaa]].{{book ref|mb1|23}}
