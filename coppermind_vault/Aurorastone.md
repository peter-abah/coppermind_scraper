**Aurorastones** are magical stones found in [[Alornia]] that are used to power various mechanisms.{{book ref|ps|1}} The stones are capable of being charged via [[Lancing]], and are used to maintain Lancing feats without [[Kairominas]] needing to be around. It is implied that the power of an Aurorastone diminishes over time, and that Kai must then recharge them with Lancing.{{book ref|ps|4}}

The known uses for Aurorastone include weather control, automated carriages resembling cars within Alornia cities, and enchanted swords.
