**Callowfield yellow** is a dye extracted from a species of tree native to [[Chakath]] in the [[Western Dominance]] of [[Scadrial]] during the [[Final Empire]].{{book ref|mb1|9}} It is presumably used to dye wool, which is a major export of Chakath.{{book ref|mb1|9}}
