**Neshan** is a scholar and [[artifabrian]] on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa4|46}}

They worked in [[Urithiru]] under [[Navani Kholin]]'s direction, and were in the room containing the [[Sibling]]'s pillar when [[Raboniel]]'s forces invaded the tower. Neshan and both of their wards were killed in the fighting, to Navani's dismay.{{book ref|sa4|46}}
