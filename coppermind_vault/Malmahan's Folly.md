**Malmahan's Folly** is a tower in the [[Blackened Lands]] on [[Mirandus]].{{book ref|do1|2}} It is located near the [[Backflow]] and the [[Warfields of Karkun]].{{map ref|Mirandus}} 

It is clearly related to [[Malmahan|Malmahan the Uncalled]] somehow. The tower was burned seven times, and its structure still remains. There is a legend that Malmahan's soul still is alive at the tower.{{book ref|do1|2}}

When [[Paul Tanasin]] first arrived on [[Mirandus]], he and [[Feotora]] traveled near Malmahan's Folly.{{book ref|do1|2}}
