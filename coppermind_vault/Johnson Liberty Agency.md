**Johnson Liberty Agency** is an insurance agency in [[Newcago]] before the rise of [[Steelheart (Epic)|Steelheart]]. They insured the [[First Union Bank (Newcago)|First Union Bank]]. Tia uses their records to try to determine what Steelheart's weaknesses are.
