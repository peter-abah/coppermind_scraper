**Fu Namir** is a city on the border of [[Yulay]] and [[Tu Bayla]] on [[Roshar]] during the [[Era of Solitude]].

It is located on the southern coast of the [[Purelake]].

In , several members of the [[Seventeenth Shard]] hired [[Ishikk]] to search for [[Hoid]] in Fu Namir.
