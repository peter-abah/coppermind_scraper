The **Yomen-Ostlin wedding dinner** is the reception following the wedding of [[Joshin Yomen]] and [[Mi'chelle Ostlin]] in [[Elendel]] on [[Scadrial]].

==Background==
The wedding between two [[noble]]s from well-connected houses was a significant event in Elendel high society. Nearly the entirety of the upper class was invited to the dinner. [[House Yomen]] intentionally excluded some of their political enemies in an attempt to relegate them to outsider status; some unlikable people such as Lord [[Shewrman]] were invited solely to maximize the insult to those who were not.

Wax and [[Steris Harms]] attended as the official start of their relationship. [[Jackstom Harms]] and [[Marasi]] both accompanied Steris, while [[Wayne]] gatecrashed as a waiter. [[Hoid]] appeared at the dinner dressed as a beggar; he was there simply to congratulate friends.

==History==
The event was held in the grand ballroom of [[Yomen Manor]]; it was filled with over a hundred tables. The attendees were dressed in their finest clothes and jewelry. Wax made some awkward conversation with Marasi and embarrassed Steris in front of Lord Harms. He talked with Marasi and Wayne about the Vanishers, and they realized that the dinner could be a prime target for a robbery since wealthy people with noble blood had become scared of taking trains. Just as Wax and Wayne made this realization, heavily armed [[Vanishers]] invaded the ballroom, stealing many valuables from attendees. They also attempted to kidnap women with [[Allomantic]] heritage on behalf of the [[Set]], including Steris and Marasi. 

Wax and Wayne initially laid low, hoping to avoid bloodshed. However, Lord [[Peterus]] loudly stood up to the bandits, and he was shot and killed by [[Miles Hundredlives]]. Lord Peterus's death prompted chaos in the ballroom, forcing Wax and Wayne into action. Using a [[bendalloy]] speed bubble, they planned and executed a counter-attack, rescuing Marasi and forcing the Vanishers into retreat. Twenty-five bandits were killed and another six were captured. Wayne was seriously injured and Wax was forced to come to his aid, and some of the bandits were able to escape with Steris. The constabulary arrived and berated Wax for his vigilante efforts, despite the fact that he saved numerous lives without causing further casualties.

==Aftermath==
The events of the dinner were a turning point for many of the attendees. It was the first time that Marasi was in a real fight, and it resulted in her working closely with Wax and Wayne. Steris was kidnapped but ultimately saved by Wax, leading her to become more affectionate towards him. The dinner was also when Wax began to suspect that Miles was involved with the Vanishers. His investigation culminated in the discovery of an insurance fraud scheme by [[Edwarn Ladrian]]; he later learned about the full extent of the kidnapping scheme and the existence of the [[Community]].
