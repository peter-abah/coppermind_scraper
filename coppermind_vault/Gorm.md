> — The Hordaman captain is cut off by [[John West]]

**Gorm** is a [[Hordaland|Hordaman]] raider.  He was among the group confronted by [[John West]], [[Sefawynn]], and [[Ealstan]], after landing by boat on a beach near [[Stenford]]. Gorm was crudely ordered by the Hordaman captain to take [[Sefawynn]] back to their boat and to put her with [[Yazad (Frugal Wizard)|Yazad]].
