**Adis** is a member of [[Bridge Four]] on [[Roshar]].

Adis ran on the edge of the deathline during one of [[Kaladin]]'s first bridgeruns as bridgeleader, along with [[Corl]], [[Leyten]], [[Murk]], and Kaladin himself. He was hit with Parshendi arrows during the final approach and collapsed. Adis was killed, although Kaladin initially believed he could have lived and sent bridgemen to look for him.
