**Adis** is a member of [[Bridge Four]] on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa1|17}}

Adis ran on the edge of the deathline during one of [[Kaladin]]'s first bridgeruns as bridgeleader, along with [[Corl]], [[Leyten]], [[Murk]], and Kaladin himself.{{book ref|sa1|17}} He was hit with Parshendi arrows during the final approach and collapsed. Adis was killed, although Kaladin initially believed he could have lived and sent bridgemen to look for him.
