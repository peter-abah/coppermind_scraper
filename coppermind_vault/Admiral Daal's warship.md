**Admiral Daal's warship** is a Malwish airship on [[Scadrial]] around the year 348 [[Catacendre|PC]].{{book ref|mb7|9}}

## Appearence
The warship is very large.{{book ref|mb7|7}} The hull of the ship is made from a dark wood type. Its corridors are round, looking like tubes. The ship also has electric lights.{{book ref|mb7|9}} 

The ship has a docking bay of sorts, where skimmers can dock and people can enter the ship. This bay seems to have doors that can open to the outside. There are also several skimmers onboard, these skimmers look a little like open topped fishing boats.{{book ref|mb7|9}}

As all other airships seen have them, it is likely that the ship has large pontoons, which have fans attached to it.{{book ref|mb6|18}} These fans are powered by [[Allomancy]], keeping the ship afloat.{{book ref|mb6|21}} This is possible due to the reduced weight of the ship, which works because of [[Iron]] [[Feruchemy]].{{book ref|mb6|29}}

## Usage
It is a warship, meaning that it is likely employed in war. The ship generally flies very high up in the sky, with even its low altitude being higher then a [[Allomancy|Coinshot]] can reach.{{book ref|mb7|9}}

## History
It is unknown when Daal's warship was constructed. At some point, either directly after its construction or a while after it, the ship became Admiral [[Daal]]'s personal ship. As Daal's personal ship it likely went on a number of campaigns.{{book ref|mb7|9}}

In the year 348 PC Daal was appointed ambassador of the [[Malwish Consortium]]. He took the ship with him to the [[Elendel Basin]] and met [[Waxillium Ladrian]] on it.{{book ref|mb7|9}} As he resided in [[Elendel]] the ship would stay here as well. Eventually the ship would leave Elendel and carry Daal, along with some Elendel officials, to safety.{{book ref|mb7|43}}
