The **Val** is a class of [[starfighter]] used by scout teams in the [[DDF]].{{book ref|sky1|41}} 

Val-class fighters are not heavily armed, as they are designed for scanner avoidance and speed. They have a single 131 Stewart [[destructor]]; larger ships carry as many as four destructors, often using the more powerful 138 Stewart model.{{book ref|sky1|41}}{{book ref|sky1|32}}{{book ref|sky1|17}} The scanning sensors on the Val-class have a longer range and are more sensitive to detail than those used on other DDF ships.{{book ref|sky1|41}}

The members of [[Ranger Flight]] fly Val-class ships.{{book ref|sky1|32}}
