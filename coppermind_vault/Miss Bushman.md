> Poets were given respect here, it seemed. Miss Bushman, my middle school English teacher, would have been proud.
> — [[John West]]

**Miss Bushman** is [[John West]]'s middle school English teacher. She is presumably highly appreciative of poetry. On noting [[Oswald]] hold a door for and bow to [[Sefawynn]] due to her being a [[skop]], John remembered Miss Bushman and thought she would have been proud of the respect shown to poets.

## Trivia
* Miss Bushman is likely named for Brandon's wife, [[Emily Sanderson|Emily]], whose maiden name is Bushman and who used to be a middle school English teacher.
