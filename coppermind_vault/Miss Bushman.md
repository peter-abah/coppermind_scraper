{{quote|Poets were given respect here, it seemed. Miss Bushman, my middle school English teacher, would have been proud.|[[John West]]{{book ref|fwh|6}}}}

**Miss Bushman** is [[John West]]'s middle school English teacher. She is presumably highly appreciative of poetry. On noting [[Oswald]] hold a door for and bow to [[Sefawynn]] due to her being a [[skop]], John remembered Miss Bushman and thought she would have been proud of the respect shown to poets.{{book ref|frugal|6}}

## Trivia
* Miss Bushman is likely named for Brandon's wife, [[Emily Sanderson|Emily]], whose maiden name is Bushman and who used to be a middle school English teacher.{{wob ref|2240}}{{bws ref|brandon-sanderson-newsletter-for-july-2006|Brandon Sanderson Newsletter for July 2006|date=2006-07-26}}
