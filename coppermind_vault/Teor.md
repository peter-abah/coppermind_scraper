**Teor** is a young [[Elantrian]] boy on Sel. He is one of the children under the care of [[Matisse]] in [[New Elantris]].{{book ref|thoe}}

==Appearance and Personality==
Teor presumable has the same sickly appearance as other Elantrians during the period of the failed [[Shaod]]. He is not a small child but he does not have his own home like the teenaged Elantrians, and is likely around eleven or twelve years old. He likes to stay up past his bedtime and does not enjoy schoolwork.{{book ref|thoe}} His name is based on [[Aon#Teo|Aon Teo]], meaning royal or majestic.

==Attributes and Abilities==
Teor was alive during the restoration of Elantris.{{book ref|thoe}} He presumably gained the inherent powers of an Elantrian, including the ability to use [[AonDor]].{{book ref|elantris|61}}

==History==
It is unknown when exactly Teor came to Elantris. During the period of [[Raoden]]'s leadership, Teor lived in the [[Roost]] with around fifty other children and his caregiver, Matisse. He was often curious about what the adults were up to and liked to stay up late and watch them through the windows. One night, he and his friend [[Tiil]] saw a number of adults practicing AonDor (despite it not working at the time) and wanted to stay up to watch them. Teor put on a "wide eyed" expression to try and gain sympathy, but Matisse threatened the boys with extra schoolwork drawing Aons in order to get them to go to bed.{{book ref|thoe}}

A short time later, the [[invasion of Arelon]] commenced and Matisse and [[Idotris]] quickly tried to hide the children. Matisse enlisted Teor and Tiil to help calm the younger children down. Teor briefly questioned her authority, but soon began following her instructions. [[Ashe]] then led the children to the library and successfully hid them until the [[Fjordell]] invaders were defeated.{{book ref|thoe}}
