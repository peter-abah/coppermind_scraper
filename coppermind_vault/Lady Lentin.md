**Lady Lentin** is a woman in [[Elendel]] on [[Scadrial]].

She was present at [[Winsting Innate's auction]], which was attended by crime lords and corrupt [[noble]]s; based on her title, she presumably fell into the latter category. She was killed during a shootout that resulted in the deaths of all of the auction guests. During their investigation of the crime scene, [[Wax]] and [[Wayne]] concluded that she was probably the first person killed as part of [[Bleeder]]'s plan to sow chaos in the room.
