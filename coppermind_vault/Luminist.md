**Luminist**{{book ref|iote|21}} is an [[Invest]]ed paste derived from processed [[glowing worm]]s.{{book ref|iote|11}} When applied to an object the paste infuses it,{{book ref|iote|29}} allowing it to interact with the unsea of the [[Cognitive Realm]].{{book ref|iote|11}} One coating of the paste lasts about five days before losing efficacy.{{book ref|iote|29}} Luminist can also Invest a living being, either as a coating or through consumption.{{book ref|iote|33}} Doing the latter on top of surviving the trials of [[Patji (being)|Patji]] allowed [[Sixth of the Dusk]] to be [[Initiation|Initiate]]d as a [[Navigator]].
