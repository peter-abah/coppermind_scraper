**Luminist** is an [[Invest]]ed paste derived from processed [[glowing worm]]s. When applied to an object the paste infuses it, allowing it to interact with the unsea of the [[Cognitive Realm]]. One coating of the paste lasts about five days before losing efficacy. Luminist can also Invest a living being, either as a coating or through consumption. Doing the latter on top of surviving the trials of [[Patji (being)|Patji]] allowed [[Sixth of the Dusk]] to be [[Initiation|Initiate]]d as a [[Navigator]].
