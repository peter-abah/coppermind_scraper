**Glowing worms** are a kind of [[Investiture|Invested]] parasitic worm endemic to [[Patji (island)|Patji]] in the [[Pantheon]] islands on [[First of the Sun]]. They are a critical factor in the transformation of birds into [[Aviar]] and can be processed into a [[Luminist|paste]] that allows objects to float on the [[unsea]].

## Ecology
The worms live inside the fruit of the [[Cakoban's finger]] trees that surround [[Patji's Eye]]. When the fruit is eaten by a young bird, the worms survive and form a symbiotic relationship with the Aviar, allowing it to form a [[Nahel bond]] and bestow a talent dependent on the bird's species.

Such a symbiotic relationship would not be possible if a human consumed the worms because human gut flora is too different from that of Aviar, having not lived long enough on First of the Sun to become part of that system. However, a human who consumes a paste made from the worms may in some circumstances become able to sense the [[Current]] in the [[Cognitive Realm]]

## History
The worms' relationship with Aviar was known to the [[trapper]]s of Patji (and perhaps trappers more broadly) but was kept secret from the [[Eelakin|homeislers]] until discovered by [[Vathi]]. About two years later, it was accidentally discovered that the worms were able to float on the not-water in the unsea and that a paste—later called luminist—could be derived by mashing up the worms allowing coated objects to likewise float for a time.
