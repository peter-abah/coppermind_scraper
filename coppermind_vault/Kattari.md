A **kattari** is a variety of short sword on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa3|65}} 

It is triangular in shape, and it is popular in western Roshar. When [[Dalinar Kholin]] visits [[Azimir]] in [[Azir]], the guards that accompany [[Noura]] are armed with kattari. Dalinar has always wanted a kattari.{{book ref|sa3|65}}

## Trivia
*The description of the kattari bears some similarity to a [[Thaylen]] blade known as a [[gtet]].{{book ref|sa2|i|3}} 
*A real-life triangular dagger from India known as the katar was originally known as a "kaţţāri" in the [[Wikipedia: Tamil language|Tamil]] language.{{wp ref|Katar (dagger)}}
