The **kolgril** is a fish native to the [[Purelake]].{{book ref|sa1|i|1}}

The locals of the Purelake believe that the kolgril is very lucky. It is said to cure aching joints for a month after being eaten, and some also report that it allows you to read the shapes of the clouds to determine when friends are going to visit.{{book ref|sa1|i|1}} It is likely that these rumors are true, to an extent, as many of the fish in the Purelake have formed symbiotic bonds with [[spren]]{{wob ref|2109}}, making them [[Investiture|Invested]] in some way--not unlike [[Ryshadium]]{{wob ref|5171}} or [[Aviar]].{{wob ref|8201}}

[[Ishikk]] caught a kolgril one morning and offered it as a gift to [[Maib]], who had a particular liking for the fish due to aches in her fingers, in order to put her in his debt and avoid her ongoing attempts to bait him into marriage.{{book ref|sa1|i|1}}
