The **cutaway vine** is a dangerous plant species found on the island [[Patji (island)|Patji]] on [[First of the Sun]].{{book ref|iote|4}}

The vine grows in the shape of a net in the tops of trees; if disturbed, the net will fall on its prey, anchoring itself with stinging barbs and then taking a few hours to retract.{{book ref|iote|4}} It has a distinctive curl which allows [[trapper]]s to identify it and avoid it.{{book ref|iote|20}}

[[Sixth of the Dusk (character)|Sixth of the Dusk]] made use of a cutaway vine to hide his canoe after arriving on Patji. Knowing that other trappers would steer clear of the vine, he activated the trap and hid his boat in the underbrush near the tree.{{book ref|iote|4}} After the [[First Company]] settled Patji, taming the island, they chopped up most cutaway vines, salting their roots to prevent them from growing back.{{book ref|iote|13}}
