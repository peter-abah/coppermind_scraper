**Senator Vindel** is a member of the [[Elendel]] Senate on [[Scadrial]]. During the vote for the [[Elendel Supremacy Bill]] in {{date|348|PC}}, [[Waxillium Ladrian]] was unsure how Vindel would vote; however, Vindel likely voted in favor of the bill.{{book ref|mb7|2}}
