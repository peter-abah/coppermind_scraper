A **lorist** is a person who studies [[Epic]]s.{{book ref|r2|6}}

Lorists are very secretive and do very little actual fieldwork for the [[Reckoners]]. They can't risk getting caught lest they should be tortured and reveal what little information they actually have about the Epics. Their studies may be valuable as the information can help reveal an Epic's weakness.{{book ref|r2|26}}

[[Tia]] is a lorist, and [[David]] would like to be a lorist, but would rather continue doing fieldwork for the Reckoners.{{book ref|r2|26}}
