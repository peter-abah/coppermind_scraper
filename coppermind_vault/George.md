**George** is a [[wikipedia:stegosaurus|Stegosaurus]] who was part of the group of dinosaurs captured alongside [[Charles]] by [[Librarian]]s after they washed up on the shore on their research trip. *George* agreed with Charles that they great at disturbances. *George* appeared to be reading a book to the other dinosaurs when [[Alcatraz]] and the others went to check on the 'disturbance'.{{book ref|a1|16}}
