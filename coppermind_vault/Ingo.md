> Boiled ingo tea, with mint. Smells of home. With a start, he realized ... he’d probably never live in that home again, would he? He’d thought perhaps he would return in a few years.
> — [[Taravangian]]'s thoughts while drinking ingo tea

**Ingo** is a type of tea on [[Roshar]].

[[Taravangian]] drinks it with mint and its smell reminds him of his home, [[Kharbranth]]. [[Maben]] served him a hot cup of the tea while they were in [[Urithiru]] discussing [[Dalinar]]'s recovery; on this occasion, the reminder of home prompted Taravangian to ponder his mortality.
