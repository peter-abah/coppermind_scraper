**Enelan** is a historical scholar on [[Sel]]. They lived during the fourth century, around a hundred years before the [[Reod]]. Like many scholars of the past, their name was derived from [[Aon#Ene|Aon Ene]], meaning wit and intelligence.

Enelan is considered a great scholar of [[AonDor]]. They considered [[Aon#Omi|Aon Omi]] to be the "most perfect" of Aons.

> No wealth of gold and silver can purchase a keen mind, but the man of wit will often find treasures beyond what mere lucre can provide.
> — A quote attributed to Enelan
