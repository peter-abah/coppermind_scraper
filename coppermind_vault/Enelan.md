**Enelan** is a historical scholar on [[Sel]]. They lived during the fourth century, around a hundred years before the [[Reod]].{{wob ref|7591}}{{wob ref|7594}} Like many scholars of the past, their name was derived from [[Aon#Ene|Aon Ene]], meaning wit and intelligence.{{wob ref|7594}}

Enelan is considered a great scholar of [[AonDor]]. They considered [[Aon#Omi|Aon Omi]] to be the "most perfect" of Aons.{{wob ref|7591}}
