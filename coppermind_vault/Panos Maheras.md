**Panos Maheras** is an engineer at [[I3]]. Panos is an open-source enthusiast and an avid maker. He participates regularly in online forums for things such as [[wikipedia: Linux|Linux]] and in maker conventions. He believes in a very extreme version of freedom of information, even going so far as to not leave a password on his computer. He is of Greek descent.

He was killed in a ski accident and his body was stolen.
