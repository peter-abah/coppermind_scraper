**Silverwood** is an area of bushland or forest, that is located north of [[Mod Isilva]] in the southern part of the [[Kingdoms of Light]]. [[Vitus Kirkas]] is located to the north-west of Silverwood, and [[Shieldreach]] to the west.{{map ref|Mirandus}}
