**Kalleb** is a [[Order of Windrunners|Windrunner]] [[squire]] to [[Sigzil]] on [[Roshar]].

He has a short, dark beard and the typical abilities of a squire, including healing and [[Lashing]]s.

## History
Kalleb was present for the Windrunner clash with enemy [[Skybreaker]]s at Narak Prime during the [[Second Battle of Narak]]. He and [[Fent]] checked on Sigzil during the battle. Kalleb observed that the ploy to divert the opposing forces to Narak Three was working.

He and other squires subsequently accompanied Sigzil to a platform where [[Moash]] had laid a trap for them. All of the squires were forced to retreat due to Moash's [[suppression fabrial]] that prevented [[Surgebinding]]. Kalleb presumably lost his powers after Sigzil renounced his [[Knights Radiant|Radiant]] oaths.
