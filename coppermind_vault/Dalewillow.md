**Dalewillow** is a species of tree on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa2|63}}

Dalewillows have stone bark and long fronds like streamers, which twist when blown in the wind.{{book ref|sa2|63}}

Brightness [[Shallan Davar]] thought that a tree she sketched in [[Shattered Plains|Sebarial's warcamp]] was related to the dalewillow, as it has similar fronds.{{book ref|sa2|63}} The [[Ghostblood]], [[Mraize]], left a note for Veil inside the tree.
