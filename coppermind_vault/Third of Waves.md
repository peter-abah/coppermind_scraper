{{spoilers|iote}}
**Third of Waves** is the [[First Company]] medical vice president and a participant in the talks with the [[Ones Above]] on [[First of the Sun]]. He is a squat man with a bright red [[Aviar]] giving him enhanced color perception, likely similar to that granted by the [[Heightening#Third Heightening|Third Heightening]].{{book ref|iote|7}}

As the medical vice president, Waves was involved in trading with the Ones Above for medicine to treat the Aviar plague as well as human medicine that lead to a sharp reduction in infant mortality in the [[homeisles]].{{book ref|iote|7}}
