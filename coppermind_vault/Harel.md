**Harel** is a [[singer]] on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa4|96}}

Harel is part of the group of singers who hope to escape the [[Fused]] with [[Venli]]. During the occupation of [[Urithiru]], they packed supplies the group would need if they fled and hid them in a storage room.{{book ref|sa4|96}}
