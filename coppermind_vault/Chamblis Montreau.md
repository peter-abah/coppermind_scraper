**Chamblis Montreau** is a brand of alcohol in [[Elendel]] on [[Scadrial]].

In , the company ran an advertisement in *[[The House Record]]* in conjunction with Gentleman [[Jak]] promoting **Chamblis 328**, presumably a wine or other alcohol that was bottled in . Jak praised the drink within his story, and an advertisement at the end of the piece included his endorsement of drinking Chamblis 328 while smoking [[Doxonar (brand)|Doxonar]] brand cigars.
