**Lady Mulgrave** is a noblewomen that lives in [[Elendel]] on [[Scadrial]]. 

During the time of [[Bleeder]]'s terrorist attacks in Elendel, Lady Mulgrave attended [[Lady ZoBell]]'s [[Lady ZoBell's party|party]]. Lady [[Steris Harms]] gave her a very particular smile as she and Lord [[Waxillium Ladrian]] passed her at the party.
