**Westbrook Lane** is a street in [[Urteau]] on [[Scadrial]].{{book ref|mb3|35}}

[[Edgard Ladrian|Breeze]] spent some time in a tavern on Westbrook Lane gathering information. [[Spook]] told [[Durn]] to send a message to Breeze in that tavern, to let Breeze know that the Urteau underground would be willing to work with the [[New Empire]].{{book ref|mb3|35}}
