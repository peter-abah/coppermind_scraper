**Hraggen** is a nation under the dominion of the Fjordell Empire on the continent of Opelon during the [[Late Era]]. The Hraggish has been under [[Fjorden]]'s control for a long period of time, possibly because they were just to the southeast of Fjorden. Hraggen is a relatively small nation, adjacent to [[Jaador]], [[Hrovell]], and [[Geant]]. Many of their words are in Fjordish, so it's likely that their ethnicity is Fjordell.

One city in Hraggen is [[Gmordsom]], perhaps its capital.

## Cuisine
Hraggish chefs are excellent, well-known throughout Sel.

### Foods
*[[Kathari]] - A large pink fruit grown in Hraggen
*[[Weed soup]] - Very healthy, supposedly tastes awful
*Meatwraps - Served cold
