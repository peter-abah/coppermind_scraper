**Talani** is a [[Darkeyes|darkeyed]] ardent on [[Roshar]]. She is dedicated, thoughtful and sympathetic, with a scholarly heart and great inner strength. She has devoted her life to the [[Vorin]] church, but finds herself questioning the religion’s doctrine of conquest and competition. During the [[War of Reckoning]], she was employed as an envoy. In addition to her work, she researched the [[listener]]s, wishing to know more about their culture.{{rpg ref|b9|38}}
