**Bjendal** is a [[Cosmere#Planets|planet]] in the [[cosmere]], and its system is considered one of the primary systems.{{book ref|mb7|39}}

The [[Ghostbloods]] consider the planetary system of Bjendal to be of primary importance, likely meaning that a shard resides or has resided there. In {{date|348|PC}}, interplanetary travel to Bjendal through the [[Cognitive Realm]] was disrupted, likely as a result of something happening to the [[perpendicularity]] on Bjendal.{{book ref|mb7|39}}
