> I climb! I climb the wall of grief toward the light, locked away above! I climb, the weight of my darkened twin on my back, and seek the captive! The light I love! I ... Storms ... the light I love!
> — Tuko-son-tuko's [[Death Rattle]]

**Tuko-son-Tuko** is a [[Shin]] [[Honorbearer]] on [[Roshar]] who wields the [[Windrunner]] [[Honorblade]]. He was rebellious, gathering acolytes to him who were expelled from other monasteries to train as an army. He was slain by [[Szeth]].
