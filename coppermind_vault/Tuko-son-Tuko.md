**Tuko-son-Tuko** is a [[Shin]] [[Honorbearer]] on [[Roshar]] who wields the [[Windrunner]] [[Honorblade]].{{book ref|sa5|83}} He was rebellious, gathering acolytes to him who were expelled from other monasteries to train as an army. He was slain by [[Szeth]].
