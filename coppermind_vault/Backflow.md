The **Backflow** is a river in the [[Blackened Lands]] on [[Mirandus]]. It flows from the [[Uls Karkun]] mountain range, through the [[Rust Banks]], past [[Pitch]] and [[Malmahan's Folly]], finally exiting into the [[Quiet River]].
