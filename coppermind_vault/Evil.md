The **Evil**, also referred to as a **Type 1-6 Threnodite entity**, **Nephilim**, or **Eidolith**, are [[Splinter]]s of [[Ambition]] that live on [[Threnody]].

## Appearance and Characteristics

> You don’t just want me dead. You want me broken and terrified first. Nazh is right—and for once, Ed is wrong. You’re not just some force of nature… You’re evil.
> — [[Starling]] on the Evil

The Evil is formed by entities of almost pure [[Investiture#Anti-Investiture|anti-Investiture]], caused by the Splintering of Ambition. They can be influenced by the thoughts of mortals, though this functions differently when they are close to the [[Grand Knell]]. Entities tend to stay away from populated areas to avoid this.

They are attracted to Investiture and seek it out, apparently as prey. They appear in Shadesmar as large translucent white beings, moving in a strange juddering manner and leaving afterimages behind as they move. They are vaguely humanoid, with many limbs and lacking eyes. They tend to stay close to the Threnodite subastral, near the [[Grand Knell]].

## Powers and Abilities
Due to their anti-Investiture nature, the Evil are capable of killing even highly [[Investiture|Invested]] beings such as [[Dragon (cosmere)|dragons]]. When touching others, they will quickly dissolve into a black mist. They are able to grow hundreds of extra arms when needed. They are able to show people visions when others look into their eye sockets.

Certain manifestations of Investiture, as well as some technology, tend to fail when entities are nearby, such as [[Feruchemy|Feruchemical]] healing or wireless signals. They are resistant to most conventional and Invested weapons. Entities are strong enough to easily break through [[Allomancy|Allomantic]] bullet shields, [[Awakening|Awakened]] deflection shields, or [[AonDor|Aonic]] force fields. However, some Arcanists have had luck overfeeding the things by hitting them with Investiture.

## History

> Smiling was wasted energy. And love...love was for the people back in Homeland. The people who'd died to the Evil.
> — Silence's grandmother's perspective on life as remembered by her [[Silence|grandaughter]]

Before the Splintering of [[Ambition]], over 7700 standard years before the [[True Desolation]], the Evil did not exist in the state it would later become; as it was warped by the clash between [[Odium]] and Ambition.

Sometime after the people of Threnody discovered the continent that contained the [[Forests of Hell]], an unknown cataclysm occurred in their Homeland. A being or force referred to only as the Evil appeared and somehow managed to kill the majority of people living on their continent. The survivors were desperate enough to flee across the ocean to the Forests that they had once believed to be hell, where the [[Forescout]]s had already begun to settle. The Forescouts helped care for the refugees and acclimatize them to their new home, coexisting with the [[shade]]s and following the [[Simple Rules]]. Much of the government, cultures, and technology of the old continent, what was now called the [[Fallen World]] was lost. Ideals such as justice, kindness, or moral living became things of the past, worship of the [[God Beyond]] became less common, and much knowledge, such as the use of gunpowder, was nearly forgotten in the Forests. The people who survived in the Forests were a harsh, hardy people that focused more on practicalities and less on things such as love and family.

Despite the fact that less than a century had passed since the Evil made its appearance on Threnody, the true nature of the Evil had already become surrounded by myths and legends by the time that [[Silence Montane]] ran her [[Silence's waystop|waystop]]. Some of the [[Forescout]]s, such as [[Silence's grandmother]] believed that one day they would be the ones to destroy the Evil. Others, who told stories about the [[White Fox]], said that he had been to the Fallen World and shaken hands with the Evil there.

As of the rebellion against the [[Cinder King]], the Evil was still on Threnody, though others have long since started wandering the Cognitive Realm.
