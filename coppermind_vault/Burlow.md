Old **Burlow** is a man who lives in [[Weathering]] in the [[Roughs]].

Old Burlow had a daughter who was murdered by [[Pars the Deadman]]. Her corpse was left in the center of Weathering to taunt [[Waxillium Ladrian]], who was hunting Pars.
