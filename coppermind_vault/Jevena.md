**Jevena** is King [[Gavilar Kholin]]'s head [[ardent]] on [[Roshar]].

## Appearance & Personality
She is an older, bony woman who is short sighted and has to wear glasses. She relates choices made, by herself and others, to the [[Vorin]] faith and has a tendency to use all available opportunities to preach about the [[Almighty]].

## History
Despite her deep commitment to the [[Almighty]], Jevena readily accepted [[Evi Kholin]], regardless of her pagan beliefs, when she was first betrothed to [[Dalinar Kholin]]. Jevena saw an opportunity to convert the woman to [[Vorinism]].

She led King [[Gavilar]]'s funeral, held in the [[Kholinar Palace#The Catacombs|Royal Catacombs]] in [[Kholinar]], giving a eulogy for the deceased king as well as reading the ritual sayings, a collection of traditional and personal [[ketek]]s. Like many of those in attendance, she found the [[Soulcaster]] to be unnerving and preferred to keep at a distance when they transformed the king's body into stone. After the Soulcaster was finished and the statue of Gavilar was in place, King [[Elhokar]], in front of those who had been in attendance, swore the other [[Highprince]]s to war against the [[Parshendi]] and created the [[Vengeance Pact]].
