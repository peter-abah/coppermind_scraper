**Darkwall** is a riverside area in the [[Blackened Lands]] near the exit of the [[Quiet River]], with [[The Raxian Desolation]] to the north. [[The Sentinels]] in the [[Kingdoms of Light]] look across the Quiet River to the Darkwall.
