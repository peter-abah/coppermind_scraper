**Great-grandmother Glorf** is [[Tress]]'s great-grandmother on [[Lumar]]. She shares a name with both Tress and Tress's [[aunt Glorf]]. It is reported that she once waved from on top of [[Diggen's Point]] at a king who was sailing past miles away.
