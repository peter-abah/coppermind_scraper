**Murkwood** is an [[Epic]] formerly known as **Lincoln**.{{book ref|Firefight|39}} His powers and weakness are unknown.

He was friends with [[Jonathan Phaedrus]] and [[Tia]] prior to the formation of the [[Reckoners]]. He attempted to form a superhero team alongside Phaedrus, [[Amala]], and [[Abigail Reed]].{{book ref|firefight|39}} Along with the others on the team, he would frequently observe [[Calamity (Epic)|Calamity]] in the sky through a telescope.{{book ref|firefight|41}} He eventually succumbed to the corrupting influence of Calamity and turned evil.{{book ref|calamity|10}} His subsequent fate is unknown, although it is possible that he is alive since the younger Reckoners know of him.{{book ref|firefight|39}}

==Trivia==
*Prof notes that Murkwood "always did love those sparking books" when discussing his Epic name.{{book ref|firefight|39}} This implies that his codename is a reference to [[wikipedia:Mirkwood|Mirkwood]], a forest prominently featured in J.R.R. Tolkien's work, and that Tolkien's Middle-earth stories exist in the alternate universe of the Reckoners.
