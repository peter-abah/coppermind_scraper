**Pepe** is a kid selling drugs for the Primeros who [[Anthony Davis|Davis]] uses to get access to [[Horace]], his boss. Davis tricks the [[dupe]] of *Pepe* into thinking he swallowed one of the pills he bought, to convince him he's not a cop, however this convinces *Pepe* that him and [[Chaz]] are feds instead.
