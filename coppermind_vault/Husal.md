**Husal** is a [[listener]] and a member of the [[Listener#Culture|First-Rhythm Family]]‘s [[Council of Five]] on [[Roshar]].

When [[Eshonai]] returned from one of her expeditions with a message from the [[Alethi]], the Council of Five discussed forming a united [[listener]] nation to deal with them. Attuning the [[Rhythm#Normal Rhythms|Rhythm]] of Anxiety, Husal objected to the idea of having a king like the humans.
