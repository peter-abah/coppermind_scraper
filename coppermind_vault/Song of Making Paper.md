The **Song of Making Paper** is a [[listener]] song that describes the process of making paper.{{book ref|sa4|45}} The listeners make paper from dried [[rockbud]] pulp following a harvest.{{book ref|sa2|i|1}} Few among all the listener families know the song, [[Jaxlim]] being one of them.{{book ref|sa4|45}} As a result, paper is precious and rare among the listeners.{{book ref|sa2|i|1}} With the song, Jaxlim helps [[Eshonai]] perfect the process of making paper.{{book ref|sa4|45}}
