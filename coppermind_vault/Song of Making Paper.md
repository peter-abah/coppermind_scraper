The **Song of Making Paper** is a [[listener]] song that describes the process of making paper. The listeners make paper from dried [[rockbud]] pulp following a harvest. Few among all the listener families know the song, [[Jaxlim]] being one of them. As a result, paper is precious and rare among the listeners. With the song, Jaxlim helps [[Eshonai]] perfect the process of making paper.
