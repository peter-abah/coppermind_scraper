**Ashwin** is a member of the Communications Corps on in the [[Defiant Defense Force]] on [[Platform Prime]].

Shortly after the explosion of the [[Superiority]] ship in which members of the [[National Assembly]] were trapped, Ashwin makes radio contact with [[National Assembly Leader]] [[Winter]] who wants to speak to [[Jorgen Weight]]. However, Jorgen declines and asks Ashwin to tell Winter that the DDF has three days to respond to her request, according to Section 57 of the DDF Communications Policy.
