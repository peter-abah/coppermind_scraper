**Dynamo**  is an [[Epic]] that lives in [[Ildithia]], who succumbs to [[Limelight]]'s reign.
He is a teenager who wears a loose necktie and a hat.

He has sound manipulation powers, and can act as an impromptu PA system.

## History
Dynamo originally served [[Larcener]] in Ildithia. After watching Limelight easily kill another Epic with flight powers, Dynamo swore his alleigance to Limelight and helped him find Larcener. He helped Limelight to project his voice when attacking a market and threatening to kill all the people inside.
