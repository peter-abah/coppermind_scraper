**Ruddy** is an underworld [[copper]] [[Misting]] (aka a [[Smoker]]) known to [[Kelsier]] and [[Dockson]].

According to Kelsier, he is a good Smoker but not a good man, and so when Kelsier asks for a Smoker for his [[Kelsier's crew|crew]] and Dockson suggests Ruddy, Kelsier discards the idea and hires [[Clubs]] instead.
