**Babatharnam** is a kingdom in western [[Roshar]].{{map ref|Roshar}} Its capital, [[Panatham]], contains an [[Oathgate]],{{book ref|sa3|8}} and people from Babatharnam are called Babath. 

## Geography and Ecology
Babatharnam borders the [[Purelake]], [[Yulay]], [[Marabethia]], and [[Rira (country)|Rira]].{{map ref|Roshar}} Cities in Babatharnam include its capital of [[Panatham]]{{map ref|Roshar}} and [[Sween]].{{map ref|Eastern Makabak}}

Babatharnam has two unnamed rivers running through it, and the border with Yulay is also an unnamed river.{{map ref|Roshar}}

Babatharnam has peculiar trees which, when a [[highstorm]] approaches, completely lay down, trunk and all, as if the tree was on a hinge.{{book ref|sa1|46}} 

Babatharnam has an area of around 1,107,000 km<sup>2</sup>.{{url ref|url=https://www.17thshard.com/forum/blogs/entry/634-roshar-physical-characteristics-and-areas/|text=Roshar Physical Characteristics And Areas|site=17th Shard}}

## History
During the [[Heraldic Epochs]] Babatharnam was part of the [[Silver kingdoms|Silver Kingdom]] [[Sela Tales]]. All the Silver Kingdoms had a specific purpose during peacetime, teaching the others their art during a desolation so the Babath, among others, were experts in an art. At some point following Aharietiam, Sela Tales broke into a large amount of smaller states, one or more of which eventually became what is present-day Babatharnam.{{map ref|Roshar}}

Around the year 1120, the Monavakah dynasty was established and began assassinating rivals to stay in power. This caused a great deal of unrest in the country.{{book ref|sa1|46}}

During the initial months of the [[True Desolation]], the Most Ancient of Babatharnam refused to cooperate with [[Dalinar Kholin]].{{book ref|sa3|24}} Once [[Iri]] surrendered to [[Odium]]’s forces, the nation moved to conquer much of the Purelake and its environs. The [[Fused]] have stationed a few of their Regals in the area, though they are more concerned with their advancing troops than with the territory itself.{{rpg ref|slwg|63}}

## Politics
Babatharnam is a gerontocracy, meaning the older one is, the more authority one has.{{book ref|sa1|46}} Every man gets an opportunity to rule, if they live long enough. Anyone can become the **Most Ancient**--the Babath king. 

However, in practicality, during the present day, Babatharnam is ruled by the **Monavakah Dynasty**, which executes or exiles any person old enough to challenge them.{{book ref|sa1|46}} This has ensured that the Monavakahs have remained in power for fifty years. [[Sigzil]] has visited Babatharnam, and recalled that the nation has a great deal of unrest from the Monavakah Dynasty's assassinations.{{book ref|sa1|46}}

## People
As the Babath are ethnically [[Sela Tales|Selay]]{{book ref|sa3|i|1}}{{map ref|Roshar}} they probably have the Selay ripple patterns on their skin.{{book ref|sa1|52}} The people here also have bronze skin and dark hair. They speak a mix of Selay and local tribal languages.{{rpg ref|slwg|62}} The women are known to possess a complex pattern of veins sitting shallowly beneath their skin, which many foreigners find unnerving.{{book ref|sa1|46}}

The Babath are human-[[Aimian]] hybrids, and thus are one of many ethnicities on Roshar known to have non-human ancestry.{{wob ref|457}}

### Culture
Its people place great value on thoughtful and precise communication. This sometimes leads to misunderstandings and offense from careless outsiders.{{rpg ref|slwg|63}} Sigzil has managed to be imprisoned three times in Babatharnam, though he suspects the Babath use any excuse to imprison a foreigner, as his [[Hoid|master]] had a good deal of money.{{book ref|sa1|46}}

The Babath probably have shards, as the Selay are known to have multiple sets.{{book ref|sa1|23}}

The Babath people are known to eat snails.{{book ref|sa1|46}}
