## Background
The **Emuli offensive** is a military operation conducted by the [[coalition of monarchs]] to liberate [[Emul]] from the control of [[Odium]]'s forces.. Raised as an alternative by The [[Dieno|Mink]] to [[Dalinar]]'s desire to retake [[Alethkar]] as a strong offensive of the [[Coalition of monarchs]], the **Emuli offensive** presents a much stronger advantage to the [[coalition of monarchs]] than the liberation of [[Alethkar]], along with being a major strategic goal as a staging ground for the eventual liberation of [[Alethkar]]. Despite [[Veden]] betrayal at the hands of [[Odium]] carried out by the orders of [[Taravangian]], the offensive is successful in liberating [[Emul]] employing a "hammer-and-anvil"" approach to push the [[singer]] army into the forces of the mad herald [[Ishar]].. [[Raboniel]] uses the offensive as an opportunity to lead an invasion of [[singers]] to [[Urithiru]] while the only [[Knights Radiant]] who can resist the [[Suppression fabrial]] at [[Urithiru]]: [[Dalinar]] and [[Jasnah]] at [[Urithiru]] are occupied with the offensive .

## Major  Events

Ensuring  the invasion of [[Urithiru]] wasn't not interrupted by [[Jasnah]] or [[Dalinar]], [[Odium]] set in motion a distraction to prevent them from investigating the disturbances at [[Urithiru]]. A simultaneous assault of the [[Skybreakers]] and betrayal from the [[Veden]] forces was orchestrated when [[Taravangian]] was instructed to, fulfill his agreement with [[Odium]] to spare [[Kharbranth]],  order his troops to betray the [[Coalition of monarchs|coalition]] force's . The attack was short lived and [[Taravangian]] was arrested just 15 minutes after sending the order. 

The Veden army was soon after was almost destroyed in its entirety, even though they were reinforced by a small force of singers and Skybreakers. After this battle, the Coalition would decide to leave Dalinar and the Alethi in charge. At the meeting where this was decided, Jasnah dueled the [[Highprince]] [[Ruthar]], who was the last holdout of [[Torol_Sadeas|Sadeas]]’s faction, and this led to Ruthar’s ‘death’, though he was healed by [[Renarin]], and his removal as a Highprince. Jasnah would use this event to outlaw honor duels in Alethkar. 

Some time later Jasnah herself would go onto the battlefield disguised as a regular Shardbearer by having her [[Shardplate]] appear as regular dead Plate. This was one of a series of battles that led to the Coalition’s victory. During the campaign, Dalinar grew curious about his powers and decided to seek out the Herald [[Ishar]], who was leading nearby [[Tukar]] as Tezim, and they would meet briefly in Tukar towards the end of the campaign. After this meeting, Dalinar would encounter Odium and would enter the contract for the Contest of champions, which had been drafted by [[Hoid|Wit]] during the campaign. 

After the contract was made, Taravangian was killed by [[Szeth]] while Odium was present. This allowed Taravangian to use the Cognitive aspect of Nightblood to kill Rayse, allowing him to pick up the Shard.

After the Offensive ended, the [[Order of Stonewards|Stoneward]] [[Dami]] would swear the Fourth Ideal, unlocking his Shardplate and making him the third [[Knights Radiant|Radiant]] to so after Jasnah and Kaladin.
