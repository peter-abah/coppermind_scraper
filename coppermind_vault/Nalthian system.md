The **Nalthian system** is the name given to the planetary system containing the [[Cosmere#Planets|planet]] of [[Nalthis]]. In addition to Nalthis, it includes one gas giant, one outer planet, a comet belt, and a [[Cognitive Realm|cognitive]] anomaly manifesting in the [[Physical Realm]].{{map ref|Nalthian system}} Notably, both of the non-inhabited planets are named like the [[Returned]], such as [[Lightsong|Lightsong the Brave]].

## Planets
;[[Nalthis]]
Nalthis is the only planet in the system known to be inhabited -- it has a large human population and a [[Shard]], [[Endowment]].{{wob ref|5943}} Humans on Nalthis differ from those elsewhere in the [[cosmere]] in that they are born with [[Breath]], Endowment's [[Investiture]]. Breath provides a number of passive benefits, as well as fuel for the local magic system, [[Awakening]].{{book ref|wb|part=ars}}

Nalthis has one moon, **Rrendos**. It also shares its orbit with an unspecified [[cognitive anomaly]], although it's unknown whether it passes through it once a year, or if the anomaly follows the planet's motion.{{map ref|Nalthian system}}

;Farkeeper the Bright
A red gas giant, and the largest planet in the Nalthian system. It has at least six moons orbiting it.{{map ref|Nalthian system}}

;Nightstar the Hidden
A small, violet outer planet, located at a considerable distance from Farkeeper. A comet belt lies beyond Nightstar, marking the system's edge.{{map ref|Nalthian system}}

## Astronomy
The Nalthian sun is a yellow star, similar to those of [[Scadrial]] and [[Sel]]. The system is located in "The Giver" constellation.{{wob ref|8089}} Its closest known stellar neighbor is the [[Selish system]], with [[Rosharan system|Greater Roshar]] a bit further away.{{file ref|Cosmere constellation map.jpg|Star chart of the cosmere}}

The local name of Nalthis' [[subastral]] is unknown. It's been speculated that it corresponds to what Rosharans name the [[Expanse of Vibrance]], in which case it would directly neighbor [[Shadesmar]]. There are, in fact, regular caravans travelling between the two subastrals, carrying both goods and people.{{wob ref|11992}} The Nalthian system is extremely popular with [[worldhoppers]], to the point of there being a customs check in the vicinity of the local [[perpendicularity]].{{wob ref|9501}} This sole perpendicularity is located on Nalthis, in the jungles of the nation of [[Hallandren]].{{wob ref|9833}}
