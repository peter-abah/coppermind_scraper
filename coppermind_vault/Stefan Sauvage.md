**Stefan Sauvage** is a [[Scadrian]] nobleman and the father of [[Nicelle Sauvage]].{{map ref|New Seran}}{{file ref|New Ascendancy broadsheet.jpg|*The New Ascendancy* broadsheet}}

## History
In {{date|336|PC}}, Lord Sauvage commissioned a large tapestry map of [[New Seran]].{{map ref|New Seran}} He included a secret pocket in the map that contained a letter to his daughter Nicelle (also known as Nicki Savage). The letter supposedly included information that Stefan had learned about lost [[Metallic Arts|metal]] beings created by the [[Lord Ruler]] that he called [[kalki]]s. By {{date|342|PC}}, he had presumably died, as Nicelle referred to the map as her inheritance.{{file ref|New Ascendancy broadsheet.jpg|*The New Ascendancy* broadsheet}}

[[Nazh]] stole the map from Nicelle, but after a brief struggle that tore the map in two, she was able to get back the piece that had the hidden pocket. Nazh escaped with the "better" part of the map.{{file ref|New Ascendancy broadsheet.jpg|*The New Ascendancy* broadsheet}} Nicelle was known to embellish her stories,{{wob ref|7415}} but Nazh did in fact annotate a ripped map of New Seran.{{map ref|New Seran}} The details of Stefan's letter remain unknown, and it is not clear if Nazh was aware of it.
