**Mod Isilva** is a town or city located in the south of the [[Kingdoms of Light]]. The [[Silverwood]] surrounds Mod Isilva in the north, and a mountain range reaching from [[Highguard (Dark One)|Highguard]] in the west borders Mod Isilva along its southern side. Mod Isilva is shown as smaller than [[Vitus Kirkas]], with a single distinct tower and large walls to protect it.
