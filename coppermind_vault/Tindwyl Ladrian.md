**Tindwyl Ladrian** is a young girl on [[Scadrial]]. She is the second child of [[Steris Harms]] and [[Waxillium Ladrian]], and is a couple of years younger than her brother [[Maxillium]]. Her given name was likely suggested by her mother due to her preference for traditional names. She shares the name of [[Tindwyl|a prominent Keeper]] who heroically sacrificed her life defending [[Luthadel]] shortly before the [[Catacendre]].

In , the children stayed at [[Jackstom Harms|their grandfather]]'s estate with their governess [[Kath]] while Wax tried to prevent a bombing and Steris prepared [[Elendel]] for potential disaster.
