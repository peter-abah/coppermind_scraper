Brightlord **Morakotha** is a [[lighteyes|lighteyed]] [[Alethi]] noble on the [[Shattered Plains]] of Roshar.{{book ref|sa1|28}}

He presumably hails from the [[Kholin princedom]] and is of high birth, as his daughter [[Danlan]] worked with King [[Elhokar]]'s scribes in [[Kholinar]].{{book ref|sa2|46}}

During the [[War of Reckoning]], Morakotha lived in the Alethi warcamps on the Shattered Plains. In {{Rosharan date|1173}}, Danlan came to the warcamps to visit her father for a few months. She courted [[Adolin]] and became one of [[Dalinar]]'s scribes.{{book ref|sa1|28}} Her visit may have been a cover for her activities with the [[Diagram (group)|Diagram]].{{book ref|sa2|46}}
