**Truefast** is a holiday or observance of unknown length in [[Elendel]] on [[Scadrial]]. According to [[Wayne]], two-penny drinks sell exceptionally well on the day after Truefast. Wayne's observation combined with the word "fast" in the name imply that people abstain from alcohol during Truefast.
