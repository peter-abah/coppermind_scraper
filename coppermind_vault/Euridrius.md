**Euridrius** is the original Vessel of the [[Shard]] [[Reason]], who is hiding from the other Shards.{{book ref|sa5|115}}

## Abilities and Attributes
{{for|Reason#Associated Magic|the powers of Reason}}

Euridrius is a [[Shard]] of [[Adonalsium]], making them one of the most powerful entities in the [[cosmere]].{{book ref|sa5|115}}

## History
Nothing is known about Euridrius's origins and life prior to the [[Shattering]], other than that they came from [[Yolen]].{{wob ref|7939}} For unknown reasons, they and a group of others broke the [[cosmere]]'s creator deity, [[Adonalsium]], into sixteen pieces.{{msh ref|3|2}} Of those sixteen, they took up [[Reason]], and departed from [[Yolen]].
