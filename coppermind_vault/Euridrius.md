**Euridrius** is the original Vessel of the [[Shard]] [[Reason]], who is hiding from the other Shards.

## Abilities and Attributes

Euridrius is a [[Shard]] of [[Adonalsium]], making them one of the most powerful entities in the [[cosmere]].

## History
Nothing is known about Euridrius's origins and life prior to the [[Shattering]], other than that they came from [[Yolen]]. For unknown reasons, they and a group of others broke the [[cosmere]]'s creator deity, [[Adonalsium]], into sixteen pieces. Of those sixteen, they took up [[Reason]], and departed from [[Yolen]].
