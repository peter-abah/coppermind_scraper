**Yulip** is a [[koloss]]-blooded on [[Scadrial]]. He is said to look like a frog, and is either a member of the [[Set]] or an associate of various members. He was mentioned in a conversation between [[Wayne]] and [[Embrier]] during a sting planned by the Elendel constabulary in Bilming.
