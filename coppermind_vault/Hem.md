**Hem** is a barmaid at [[Jez's Duty]] in the [[Ten Rings]] section of [[Urithiru]] on [[Roshar]].

She is young, tall, and has unusually light hair.

[[Shallan|Veil]] and [[Adolin]] thought Hem was attractive despite her unflattering work uniform. They pointed her out as a possible romantic fling for [[Kaladin]], who did not seem interested.
