> We'd set foot upon that road in the jungles of Evasti in my State. After about half an hour of travelling on the enchanted road, the trees had started to change to these pines and aspens. Eventually, the road spat us out here.
> — [[Kairominas]]

**Evasti** is the name of a region in the [[State]] of [[Alornia]] known for its jungles. An enchanted road within the jungles becomes the first pathway to a [[State#Border States|Border State]] that [[Kairominas]] uses.
