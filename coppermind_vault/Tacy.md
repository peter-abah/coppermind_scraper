> Go home to Tacy and the kids. Forget about this dimension; pretend it never existed.
> — [[Runian]] to [[Quinn]]

**Tacy** is [[Quinn]]'s partner, and the mother of their children. At some point during his life as John West, [[Runian]] did something for Tacy that made Quinn feel like he owed him. When the men saw each other again in the other dimension, Runian refused to kill Quinn because he didn't want to leave Tacy alone with the kids. Runian later sent Quinn back home through a portal with greetings for Tacy.
