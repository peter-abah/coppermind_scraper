The **Markivian barrow-wolf** is a species of creature in the [[Cytoverse]]. After flying up into the upper reaches of the sky in the [[Nowhere]] and suffering its effects, [[Chet]] says that he feels like he’s been used as a chew toy by a Markivian barrow-wolf.
