**Telanium** is a super-light metal with a silvery texture that is very resistant to corrosion. It is an extremely versatile and strong material, to the point where most modern weaponry has difficulty even scarring it, though [[mindblade]]s can easily destroy it.

[[Evensong]] is made entirely out of telanium.
