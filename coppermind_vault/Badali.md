**Badali** is a [[Order of Stonewards|Stoneward]] on [[Roshar]]. He is an affable older man with a powdery beard and a cheerful expression.{{book ref|sa4|27}}

When [[Navani]]'s scholars found a room in [[Urithiru]] with no doors but a large exterior window, Badali used his powers to create an entrance and then guarded the location at least until Navani arrived.{{book ref|sa4|27}}

==Trivia==
Badali's name is a reference to [https://badalijewelry.com/ Badali Jewelry], which makes licensed cosmere jewelry, in addition to other fantasy properties.
