**Trap** is a [[skaa]] [[copper]] [[Misting]] (aka a [[Smoker]]) on [[Scadrial]].

[[Kelsier]] envies his ability to know his emotions are not the result of emotional Allomancy. He has been known to be a member of [[Kelsier's crew]], being known and liked by many of the other members.

He is caught and beheaded by an [[Inquisitor]] shortly before Kelsier's return to [[Luthadel]] after departing the [[Pits of Hathsin]]. As a result of his absence, [[Clubs]] is employed.
