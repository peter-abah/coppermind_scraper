An **aelv** is a supernatural being from another realm.

## Appearance and Anatomy

Unlike a [[landswight]], an aelv is visible to people of [[Earth (Frugal Wizard)|Earth]]. They look like a regular human, though noted for appearing more feminine than a traditional man. They typically are clean-shaven, and are not overly muscular, with delicate facial features. Overall their appearance stands out as being more perfect than a regular person.{{book ref|frugal|4}}

## Behavior

An unbound aelv is considered dangerous, needing to be appeased or bound to try stop them doing anything.{{book ref|frugal|4}} They like to mock and play with mortals.{{book ref|frugal|10}} They shy away from a [[skop]]'s boasts however,{{book ref|frugal|5}} and can be bound by invoking their ancient name.{{book ref|frugal|6}}

### Abilities

An aelv is able to control a person or overpower them.{{book ref|frugal|4}}
