> Regalia has no form of protective powers, not directly. That's what makes someone a High Epic. Steelheart's skin was impenetrable; the Clapper warped air around him so anything stabbing or hitting him was teleported to his other side; Firefight reincarnates when killed.
> — [[David]], explaining the definition of [[High Epic]]

The **Clapper** is a [[High Epic]]—an Epic with protective abilities. The Clapper warps air around him, meaning attacks targeted towards him are teleported to his other side.
