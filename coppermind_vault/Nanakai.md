**Nanakai** is a [[nightmare]] [[painter]] in her forties living in [[Kilahito]] on [[Komashi]]. During the [[Battle of Kilahito]], one of the nightmares took advantage of her nervousness to push forward and wound her with a slash to her side. Two others helped her as she fell back into the circle the painters had created, and [[Nikaro]] briefly stepped up to take her place.
