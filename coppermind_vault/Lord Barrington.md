**Lord Barrington** is the namesake of a common type of carriage in [[Elendel]] on [[Scadrial]].{{book ref|mb5|14}}

The standard fully-enclosed horse-drawn carriage is called a [[Barrington carriage|Barrington]]. [[House Ladrian]]'s coaches are Barringtons, as are many of the coaches at [[Ashweather Carriage and Coach]].{{book ref|mb5|14}}
