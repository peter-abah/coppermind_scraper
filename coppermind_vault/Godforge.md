The **Godforge** is the location where the [[honorspren]] [[Sylphrena]] was born. It functions as a kind of throne room for the [[Stormfather]].{{book ref|sa5|126}} It is unknown what happened to this location after the death of the Stormfather.{{book ref|sa5|144}} 

As the Stormfather and Syl are both [[spren]], the Godforge is likely somwhere in [[Shadesmar]].
