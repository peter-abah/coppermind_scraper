> I poofed into existence fully formed by the Stormfather, right in the middle of the Godforge—which is basically his throne room

The **Godforge** is the location where the [[honorspren]] [[Sylphrena]] was born. It functions as a kind of throne room for the [[Stormfather]]. It is unknown what happened to this location after the death of the Stormfather. 

As the Stormfather and Syl are both [[spren]], the Godforge is likely somwhere in [[Shadesmar]].
