**Argent** is an [[arcanist]] working at the [[Silverlight]] university. When [[Ed]] tells [[Starling]] that he needs to check in as part of his contract, he mentions that he wants to see Argent's face when he learns that Ed saw an [[Evil| entity]].{{book ref|iote|30}}

Ed then asks Starling's permission to show Argent, [[Jess (Silverlight)|Jess]], and [[Huio]] the map to [[First of the Sun]]'s [[perpendicularity]] for verification.{{book ref|iote|30}}

## Trivia
* Argent is named after the real-life Argent, one of Brandon's beta readers and a staff member on [[User:Argent|the Coppermind]] and [[17s-user:488|the 17th Shard]].
