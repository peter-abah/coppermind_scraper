**Alicago** is an island on [[Scadrial]] that was once hit by a tsunami. The island comes up in a discussion on how sunken ships might slow the incoming water of a tsunami, whereas unsunken ships provide an additional danger that might be slammed into a city. It is unclear which of these was the case when Alicago was hit.{{book ref|mb7|70}}
