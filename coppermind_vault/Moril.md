[[Diem#Organization|Underlestrell]] **Moril** is a [[Sand mastery|sand master]] on [[Taldain]].{{book ref|ws|prologue}}

==Appearance and Personality==
Moril is of average height and build. She has medium-length red hair, fair skin, and she dresses in the typical white robes of a sand master, with a purple sash signifying her rank as an underlestrell.{{book ref|ws|prologue}} As a sand master, she is presumably [[Lossand]]in.

She and her friend [[Yeeden]] are minions of [[Drile]]'s and are openly disrespectful to [[Kenton]] due to his low rank in the [[Diem]].{{book ref|ws|prologue}}

==Attributes and Abilities==
Based on her rank, Moril is capable of controlling at least six ribbons of sand.{{book ref|ws|part=ars}}

==History==
Moril and Yeeden accosted Kenton shortly before his attempt at the [[Mastrell's Path]]. They called him "[[Lonsha]]" as an epithet due to his [[Darksider]] heritage, and Moril also referred to him as "daddy's boy". They had heard [[Dirin]] speaking highly of Kenton's ability to control a single ribbon of sand and were very skeptical. Yeeden challenged Kenton to a game of [[Zo'Ken]]. Overconfident due to his ability to control nine ribbons, Yeeden wagered twenty-five red [[lak]] on the game, and both he and Moril were shocked when Kenton easily won. They walked away grumbling just as Drile approached and told them they should not be sparring with acolents.{{book ref|ws|prologue}}
