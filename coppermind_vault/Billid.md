> Billid claimed... to have found old Skybreaker oaths. I thought it ridiculous at the time...
> — [[Nale]]

**Billid** is a [[Order of Skybreakers|Skybreaker]] on [[Roshar]]. He is presumably bonded to an unknown [[highspren]].

He and a group of followers dissented with [[Nale]]'s leadership, so they broke off from the Order. Billid said he had discovered "old Skybreaker oaths," a claim which Nale doubted at the time but later came to realize could have been true. Nale found through the centuries that he could usually persuade dissenters to return to the Order, but his failure with Billid caused [[Ishar]] to despise both Nale and the Skybreakers.
