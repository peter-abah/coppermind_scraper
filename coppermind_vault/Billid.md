**Billid** is a [[Order of Skybreakers|Skybreaker]] on [[Roshar]]. He is presumably bonded to an unknown [[highspren]].{{book ref|sa5|119}}

He and a group of followers dissented with [[Nale]]'s leadership, so they broke off from the Order.{{book ref|sa5|119}} Billid said he had discovered "old Skybreaker oaths," a claim which Nale doubted at the time but later came to realize could have been true.{{book ref|sa5|128}} Nale found through the centuries that he could usually persuade dissenters to return to the Order, but his failure with Billid caused [[Ishar]] to despise both Nale and the Skybreakers.{{book ref|sa5|119}}
