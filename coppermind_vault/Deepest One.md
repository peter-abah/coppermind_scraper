The **Deepest Ones** are unknown beings in the [[Forests of Hell]] on [[Threnody]].{{book ref|sfs|2}}

It is unknown if the Deepest Ones actually exist or if they are just a myth. [[Silence Montane]], who is not afraid to battle large numbers of [[shade]]s, fears even a single Deepest One; she seems to believe that it is nearly impossible to escape if one attacks. They seem to be drawn by fighting, perhaps specifically fighting between [[shade]]s and humans.{{book ref|sfs|2}}

==Trivia==
* The Deepest Ones are planned to be heavily involved in a future [[Threnody]] novel, tentatively titled *[[The Night Brigade]]*.{{wob ref|11828}}
