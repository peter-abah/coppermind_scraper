**Blaze** is a scout pilot in the [[DDF]]. As a scout she flies a [[Val-class]] ship.{{book ref|Skyward|41}}

She is sensitive to the fact that some people consider scouts more cowardly than fighter pilots and has given derogatory looks in the past.{{book ref|Skyward|41}}

As part of their training, [[Skyward Flight]] paired with the scouts during a [[varvax|Krell]] battle to gain experience in scouting. Blaze was in charge of giving the cadets instructions on the best way to spot enemy ships, as well as outlining some of tactics often used by the Krell.{{book ref|Skyward|41}}
