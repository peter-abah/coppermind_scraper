**Thomton Delacour** is possibly a fashion designer or tailor in [[Elendel]] on [[Scadrial]].

[[Wayne]] told a boatman who questioned his [[Roughs]] coat that his outfit was actually couture designed by Thomton with a 'northern outlands inspiration'. Since Wayne was posing as an indignant noble, it is possible that he made up the name on the spot to help mislead the boatman.
