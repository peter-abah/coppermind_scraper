**Hessi** is a scholar and author on [[Roshar]]. She is the author of *[[Mythica]]*, a work which investigates historical information about the [[Unmade]] and explores their characteristics.

==History==

Hessi was presumably alive on Roshar during the arrival of the [[Everstorm]]; she was a contemporary of [[Jasnah Kholin]] and [[Shallan Davar]] mentioned that *Mythica* was a recent work. Shallan told [[Adolin]] and [[Kaladin]] about some of Hessi's controversial hypotheses in the book, noting that *Mythica* was poorly received and was hard to find. Hessi herself knew that aspects of *Mythica* would be contentious, such as her assertions that the [[Unmade]] were still alive and that [[Moelach]] was responsible for [[Death Rattle]]s. Subsequent events proved Hessi to be correct on a number of occasions, indicating that Hessi was a gifted researcher, as she was apparently able to meet the challenge of separating fact from fiction in the many tales and stories about the Unmade.

==Trivia==
*[[Oathbringer/Epigraphs#Hessi's_Mythica|Selections from *Mythica*]] are used as the epigraphs for part four of *[[Oathbringer]]*.
