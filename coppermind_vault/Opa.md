**Opa** is a [[seon]] on [[Sel]] who was bonded to Duke [[Roial]].

## Appearance and Personality
Like all seons, Opa is a sentient, melon-sized ball of light. He has formal mannerisms and a stern, unemotional voice.

## History
After the [[Reod]], many [[Arelene]] people became fearful of [[Elantrian]] magic, and this fear extended to seons. Since it was no longer fashionable to have a seon, Roial began keeping Opa at his home rather than bringing him wherever he went. [[Aon#Opa|Aon Opa]] means "flower," and Opa fittingly oversaw the gardeners who tended to the expansive grounds of Roial's estate in [[Kae]].

During Roial's eclipse viewing party, Opa reported to Roial that Lord [[Redeen]] got drunk and fell into a pond. Roial instructed Opa to ensure that Redeen was dried off and sent home. [[Sarene]] was surprised to see Opa, as Roial had never mentioned having a seon. She was also somewhat taken aback by Opa's somewhat aloof personality, which she attributed to the post-Reod sentiment about seons. Opa's fate after Roial's death is unknown.
