**Adawathwyn** is a former vice governor of [[Elendel]] under Governor [[Varlance]].{{book ref|mb7|2}} Adawathwyn is a [[Terris]] [[Zinc#Feruchemical_Use|Sparker]]. 

## Appearance and Personality
Adawathwyn has curly brown hair{{book ref|mb7|2}} and dark brown eyes{{book ref|mb7|38}}. She outwardly appears to be just a token representative for the Terris people{{book ref|mb7|2}}, but Varlance is in her pocket for most of his term.{{book ref|mb7|38}} He relies on her for almost every action, and she frequently pushes him around on major decisions. 

## History
Adawathwyn became the Vice Governor of Elendel under Governor [[Varlance]]. She was involved in almost every policy decision he took.

After [[Steris]] informed her of a bomb threat to Elendel, she established a Council to investigate it but also maneuvered to exclude House Ladrian.{{book ref|mb7|31}} The Council consisted only of three senators, [[Darlin Cett|Lord Cett]], [[Mari Hammondess|Lady Hammondess]] and [[Lady Gardre]].

When [[Reddi|Constable-General Reddi]] invites Steris to the meeting anyway as a subject matter expert, Adawathwyn was not happy.{{book ref|mb7|38}}. When the Council proposed a war that Reddi was opposed to, she planned to use a [[wikipedia: Conscription|draft]] to force them. Her actions seem to benefit the [[Set]] but Steris believed she was secretly encouraged but not directly influenced by them.{{book ref|mb7|38}}

Adawathwyn and the Council finally believe Steris after [[TenSoon]] revealed himself, disguised as a police officer. As a [[Zinc#Feruchemical_Use|Zinc]] Ferring, she planned to use the [[Bands of Mourning]] to safely dispose of the bombs.{{book ref|mb7|43}} However, Tensoon revealed that the bands are drained, so Adawathwyn could not try the plan. Considering the destruction of the city imminent, Adawathwyn and the other Senators in the Council fled the city, leaving behind Steris and Varlance to evacuate everyone else.

After the crisis was over, Adawathwyn was fired by Governor Varlance.{{book ref|mb7|epilogue|2}}
