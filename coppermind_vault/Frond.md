**Frond**, also called **Mother Frond** is a [[loremother]] from the outer isles on [[First of the Sun]] living in the [[homeisles]] capital. As a loremother, her official duties to recite histories for chiefs and kingmakers on the outer isles often keeps her away from the capital.{{book ref|iote|3}}

## Appearance and Personality
Frond is a heavyset older woman with deep brown weathered skin that is darker than many homeislers. Unlike many other loremothers who wear more modern clothing, she wears a feathered headdress and cloak with long sleeves after the traditional style.

Frond is always looking for a chance to teach, and can often be found doing so at the children's amphitheater in the park; her favorite spot in the city. She is fond of both children and [[aviar]], giving out candies and seeds to them respectively.

Frond is concerned about the [[Ones Above]] and believes that someone like [[Dusk]] who is like the people from her stories should be the one to deal with them.{{book ref|iote|3}}
