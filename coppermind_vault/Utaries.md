**Utaries** is a planet in the [[Reaches]] that is part of the [[High Empire]].{{book ref|firstborn}}

Prior to [[Varion Crestmar]]'s graduation from the [[Academy]], the planet had rebelled against the empire and was outside of its control. During his [[Reunification War|re-conquest of the Reaches]], Varion prioritized retaking Utaries, as it easily could have rallied other planets to its cause, and successfully captured it early in the campaign.{{book ref|firstborn}}

The planet is the home of the pilot [[Charisa of Utaries]], one of the first rebel commanders to join Varion.{{book ref|firstborn}}
