**Utaries** is a planet in the [[Reaches]] that is part of the [[High Empire]].

Prior to [[Varion Crestmar]]'s graduation from the [[Academy]], the planet had rebelled against the empire and was outside of its control. During his [[Reunification War|re-conquest of the Reaches]], Varion prioritized retaking Utaries, as it easily could have rallied other planets to its cause, and successfully captured it early in the campaign.

The planet is the home of the pilot [[Charisa of Utaries]], one of the first rebel commanders to join Varion.
