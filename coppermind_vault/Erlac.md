**Erlac** is a city in the [[Eastern Dominance]] in the [[Final Empire]].{{ref|name=rename}} Erlac is located southeast of the [[Black Lake]] and the [[Ashmount]] Faleast, and northwest of [[Urbene]].{{ref|name=rename}}
 
It at least existed in the year {{date|1021|fe}}, shortly prior to the [[Collapse]].{{ref|name=rename}}{{17s ref|topic|59143|10th Anniversary Final Empire Map|date=May 9, 2017}}

==Trivia==
On the original version of the Final Empire map in the 10th Anniversary Mistborn release, [[Suisna]] was located at Erlac's current location.{{17s ref|topic|59143|10th Anniversary Final Empire Map|date=May 9, 2017}} Erlac is named after [http://www.17thshard.com/forum/profile/1-chaos/ Eric Lake], who noted a mistake to Isaac Stewart that Suisna had to be located in the Central Dominance, not the Eastern Dominance.{{tweet ref|867933863443447809|One city is in the wrong place.|date=May 25, 2017}} Isaac changed this in a reprint of the map, and now the city in the Eastern Dominance is named Erlac in Eric's honor.{{ref|name=rename}}
