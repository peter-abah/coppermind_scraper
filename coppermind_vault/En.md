**En** is a former slave in [[Tvlakv]]'s caravan on [[Roshar]].

After [[Shallan Davar]] took ownership of Tvlakv's slaves, En became her footman when she borrowed [[Turinad Sebarial]]'s coach in the [[Alethi]] warcamps. En said he had some previous experience performing the job, and seemed happy in his new position.
