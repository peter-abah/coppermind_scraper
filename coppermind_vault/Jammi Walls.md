> Ill repute. I'm gonna tell Jammi you said that, Marasi. She worked hard for her reputation.
> — [[Wayne]] to [[Marasi]]

**Jammi Walls** is a friend of [[Wayne]]'s in [[Elendel]] on [[Scadrial]]. Jammi is a prostitute, and her great reputation allows her to charge roughly four times as much as everyone else. Wayne asked her what he should do after [[MeLaan]] told him that she was going to break up with him soon. Jammi thought that it meant that MeLaan wanted Wayne to try harder in their relationship, although Wayne didn't believe that to be true.
