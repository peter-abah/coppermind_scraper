**Savrahalidem**, also known as **Savri** to those close to her, is the queen of Kharbranth. She is [[Taravangian]]'s daughter and has multiple children.

## History
Savri disavows her father, [[Taravangian]], after his betrayal at the Battle in [[Emul]]. [[Kharbranth]] declared itself neutral in the conflict, with their surgeons aiding whichever side petitions them.
