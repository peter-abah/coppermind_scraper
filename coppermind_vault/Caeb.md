**Caeb** was a historical resident of [[Roshar]]. He fought alongside the [[Knights Radiant]] during the [[Heraldic Epochs]].

==History== 
Caeb was seen in one of [[Dalinar Kholin]]'s visions. He and Dalinar were among a group of around twelve soldiers that accompanied an unnamed female Radiant who wore red-accented [[Shardplate]]. The soldiers were likely Radiant [[squire]]s, as Dalinar was surprised to see many of them glowing faintly with [[Stormlight]].

Caeb's Radiant team joined several others to search the [[Purelake]] for a [[spren]] corrupted by [[Sja-anat]]. Caeb was the first person to catch a glimpse of the spren. Dalinar subsequently found the red-eyed spren in the water, and a Radiant told Caeb to run back to the checkpoint.
