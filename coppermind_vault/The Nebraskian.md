***The Nebraskian*** is the working title for the third and final book in a planned [[The Rithmatist|Rithmatist]] trilogy. 

Neither it nor the second book, *[[The Aztlanian]]*, have been written, so information about the story is scarce. The title is also subject to change.

The Rithmatist series was planned to be a trilogy, but Brandon has not returned to it for several reasons. It's possible that the final book may be dropped.
