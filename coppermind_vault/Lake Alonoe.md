**Lake Alonoe** is a body of water located near the center of [[Arelon]] on the continent of [[Opelon]] on [[Sel]], created by the flow of rivers from the southwestern mountains. The [[Aredel River]] flows from it and empties into the northern [[Sea of Fjorden]]. It is among the largest lakes on the continent and fed the [[Aredel River]].

Lake Alonoe, along with the [[Atad Mountains]], the northern coastline, and the [[Chasm]], is one of the four essential parts to [[Aon#Aon|Aon Aon]]. It is represented by a dot in the center of the Aon.
