**Lak** is the currency of all nations in Dayside on [[Taldain]]. They are coins that vary in color, made of rare and valuable semi-precious gemstones, cut into a circular shape. Lak are referred to by their color, and when no color is mentioned, blue lak are presumed.

The average daily salary for middle and upper-class worker is two-hundred-fifty lak, often given as fifty green lak or twenty-five red lak.

Some merchants deal in both lak and [[Kerzta|Kerztian]] currency.
