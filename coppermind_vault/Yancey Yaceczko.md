> Good show indeed. It's always a pleasure.
> — Yancey Yaceczko

**Yancey Yaceczko** is a member of the [[Elendel]] Senate on [[Scadrial]]. He is described as good-natured and patient.

When [[Waxillium Ladrian]], a fellow senator, finishes his speech against the [[Elendel Supremacy Bill]], he notes Yancey as one of the senators who'd actually been listening. Yancey then gives positive remarks to Wax, appreciating the show he put on.
