**Akak** is one of the [[Reshi Isles]], located just to the north of [[Alethkar]].{{map ref|Roshar}}

[[Dalinar Kholin|Dalinar]], as general, fought and destroyed the Akak Reshi,{{book ref|sa1|56}} some time around the year 1155, and claimed the island for Alethkar.{{book ref|sa3|52}} 

The [[zatalef]] is a [[Wikipedia: cephalopod| cephalopod]] that lives at least in Akak, and perhaps in other Reshi Isles.{{file ref|Alethi Glyphs Page 1.jpg|Page of Alethi glyphs}}
