**Akak** is one of the [[Reshi Isles]], located just to the north of [[Alethkar]].

[[Dalinar Kholin|Dalinar]], as general, fought and destroyed the Akak Reshi, some time around the year 1155, and claimed the island for Alethkar. 

The [[zatalef]] is a [[Wikipedia: cephalopod| cephalopod]] that lives at least in Akak, and perhaps in other Reshi Isles.
