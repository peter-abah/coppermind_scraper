[[Kelzi]] **Karmat** is the boss of [[Trell (Taldain)|Trell]] and his team of builders on [[Taldain]].

After one of Karmat's buildings collapsed, [[Kenton]] saved the workers and told them take the rest of the day off. He asked Trell to refer the Kelzi to himself if Karmat has any problems with the decision.
