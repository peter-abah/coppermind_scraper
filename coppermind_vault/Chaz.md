Detective Chavez, known as **Chaz**, is a police officer in [[New Clipperton]] who partners with [[Anthony Davis|Davis]] in the [[Snapshot (event)|Snapshots]].{{book ref|Snapshot|1}}{{book ref|Snapshot|10}} He worked as a traffic officer in Mexico City and New Clipperton for less than a year before being transferred to Snapshot duty replacing Davis's retiring partner. {{book ref|Snapshot|3}}{{book ref|Snapshot|4}}

Chaz sneaks off to sleep with [[Molly (Snapshot)|Molly]], Davis's ex-wife, whenever Davis spends time with [[Hal Davis|Hal]].

## Appearance & Personality
Chaz is known as a bully and an aggressive person, racking up seven complaints in the few months he worked as a traffic officer in New Clipperton.{{book ref|Snapshot|4}} Chaz says he became a cop because he liked the power.{{book ref|Snapshot|3}} After a bar fight, Chaz needed a few fake teeth but decided to replace them all so they would match.{{book ref|Snapshot|2}} 

Chaz likes mustard on his burritos and has been trying to get Davis to like it for 3 years.{{book ref|Snapshot|2}}
