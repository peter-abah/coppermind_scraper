**Weevilwax** is a substance on [[Roshar]] that is used as an adhesive, a seal, an artistic medium, and for making candles.

## Examples of usage
*[[Jasnah Kholin]] uses it to seal a message to her hired assassin, [[Liss]].
*[[Shallan Davar]] uses some of Jasnah's wax to hang a map of [[Urithiru]] on a wall when researching its location.
*A note from the [[Ghostbloods]] to Shallan was stuck to a wall outside her rooms with weevilwax.
*A standing mirror in [[Dalinar Kholin]]'s rooms in [[Urithiru]] is decorated with images of [[windspren]] that appear to be carved from weevilwax and then [[Soulcast]] into hardwood.
*After the loss of [[Stormlight]], one of the conference rooms in the [[Bronze Palace]] uses weevilwax candles.
