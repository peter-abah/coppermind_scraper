Agil was born to a man named [[Marf]]. In , when Highmarshal [[Meridas Amaram]] visited to Hearthstone to recruit men for his army, Citylord [[Roshone]] chooses Agil to be one of the men conscripted, along with [[Caull]], [[Habrin]], [[Jorna]] and [[Tien]].
