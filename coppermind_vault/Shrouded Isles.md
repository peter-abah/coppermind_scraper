The **Shrouded Isles** are a series of islands on [[Scadrial]]. They lie in the ocean west of the continent that formerly contained the [[Final Empire]] and now contains the [[Elendel Basin]]. They are similar in size and shape to the [[Southern Islands]], but are more remote. Very little is known about them.
