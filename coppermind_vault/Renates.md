> One only need look to the near past to note such blunders as the Massacre at [[Devanex]], the revision of the [[Deepness Doctrine]], and the relocation of the Renates peoples.
> — *Book of the False Dawn*, written by Deluse Couvre

The **Renates** were a people on [[Scadrial]] during the [[Final Empire]].

Towards the end of his rule, the [[Lord Ruler]] relocated the Renates peoples for unknown reasons. In the *[[Book of the False Dawn]]*, [[Deluse Couvre]] mentioned their relocation as one of several colossal leadership errors that the Lord Ruler had made in the near past. While most of the Lord Ruler's mistakes had been covered up and could only be found in a [[Keeper]]'s [[metalmind]]s or in obscure, forbidden texts, the relocation of the Renates was known because of its recency.
