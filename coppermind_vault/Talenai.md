**Talenai** is a *[[Mavset-im]]* [[Fused]] on [[Roshar]]. They are proud, sarcastic and pragmatic. As one of the Mavset-im, they are slight but athletic. The carapace under their skin creates flowing ridges that accent the deep red patterns along their face and arms. While undercover in [[Marabethia]], Talenai met a [[Order of Lightweavers|Lightweaver]] named [[Caelith]] and slowly fell in love. After Caelith was discovered, Talenai killed another Fused to allow him to escape capture. In response, [[Odium]] removed Talenai’s powers; when Talenai fled, [[Order of Skybreakers|Skybreakers]] captured and sentenced them to death by Marabethian custom. Before they were eaten by [[Yu-nerig]] however, they were rescued by a group of people employed by Caelith. After being rescued, Talenai gave the [[coalition of monarchs]] information about Odium’s forces and supply caches in the region.
