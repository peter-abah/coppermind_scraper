**Gord** is a person in [[Wayfarer]], a city in the [[Set]]'s [[Community]] underneath [[Bilming]] on [[Scadrial]]. He is nearby when [[Marasi Colms|Marasi]] infiltrates the Community, but he does not see her. This is fortuitous for Marasi, as [[Fialia]] and [[Drenya]] believe that Gord will go straight to [[Gave Entrone]] with any news of an outsider.
