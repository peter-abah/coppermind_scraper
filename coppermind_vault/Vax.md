**Vax** is a planet in the [[cosmere]].{{rpg ref|slwg|84}}

Little is known of Vax, its peoples, or any Shards that may or may not be located there. All that is certain is that it has a manifestation of [[Investiture]] in which people are Initiated differently than on [[Sel]], [[Scadrial]], [[Nalthis]], or [[Taldain]].{{book ref|elantris|part=ars}}

On Scadrial, when [[Ruin]] is killed by [[Vin]] while she is holding [[Preservation]], [[Ati]]'s soul is finally freed from [[Ruin]] and appears in the Cognitive Realm. Right before moving on to the Beyond, Ati looks around Scadrial's subastral and seems to wonder aloud if it is Vax.{{msh ref|6|8}} [[Leras]] would recognize Vax.{{wob ref|12779}} [[Khrissalla| Khriss]] is aware of Vax and how people are Initiated on or within it.{{book ref|elantris|part=ars}}

For a time, it was not clear whether Vax was a planet, or simply a place.{{wob ref|6586}}{{wob ref|15251}}{{book ref|elantris|part=ars}}

"True Vaxilians," presumably people from Vax, are a mortal race distinct from humans and [[Sho Del]]. They, like humans and Sho Del, are said to fear the [[Sleepless]].{{book ref|iote|46}} [[Eddlin]] is a Vaxilian.
