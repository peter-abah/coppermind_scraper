The **Svordish university** is a center of learning in [[Svorden]], a former sovereign nation in northeastern [[Opelon]] that was annexed by the [[Fjordell Empire]].

==Description==
Svorden is described as the "cultural epicenter" of eastern Opelon, and the university is well-known. People from other nations reference contemporary research by Svordish scientists and the complexity of the education in their schools. Svordish literature is sometimes read even in the distant [[Rose Empire]]. The university keeps human bodies for study. 

Although Svorden is part of the Fjordell Empire, the university allows people from [[Arelon]] to enroll, as [[Lukel]] is a recent graduate.

==History==
[[Jalla]] and Lukel met while attending the university together. [[Hrathen]] has visited the university.
