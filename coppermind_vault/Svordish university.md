The **Svordish university** is a center of learning in [[Svorden]], a former sovereign nation in northeastern [[Opelon]] that was annexed by the [[Fjordell Empire]].{{book ref|Elantris|5}}

==Description==
Svorden is described as the "cultural epicenter" of eastern Opelon, and the university is well-known.{{book ref|Elantris|18}}{{glossary ref|Elantris|S}} People from other nations reference contemporary research by Svordish scientists and the complexity of the education in their schools.{{book ref|Elantris|8}}{{book ref|Elantris|33}} Svordish literature is sometimes read even in the distant [[Rose Empire]].{{tes ref|2}} The university keeps human bodies for study.{{book ref|elantris|18}} 

Although Svorden is part of the Fjordell Empire, the university allows people from [[Arelon]] to enroll, as [[Lukel]] is a recent graduate.{{glossary ref|Elantris|L}}

==History==
[[Jalla]] and Lukel met while attending the university together.{{glossary ref|Elantris|L}}{{book ref|Elantris|5}} [[Hrathen]] has visited the university.{{book ref|Elantris|18}}
