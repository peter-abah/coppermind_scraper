The **Tisark** seems to be a group of [[Odium]]'s followers sent to secure the [[Thaylen City]] [[Oathgate]] during the [[Battle of Thaylen Field]] on [[Roshar]].

[[Renarin]] later sees a group of [[Fused]] holding the Oathgate; it is possible that Tisark is the name for a particular faction of the Fused.
