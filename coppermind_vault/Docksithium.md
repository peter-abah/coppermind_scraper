The **Docksithium** is a document on post-[[Catacendre]] [[Scadrial]]. It was allegedly written by one of the [[Preservers]], [[Dockson]], but is considered apocryphal, likely because Dockson died before the Catacendre. There is at least one copy stored in a room of the museum underneath the [[Field of Rebirth]].{{book ref|mb5|19}}{{book ref|mb2|53}}

[[Wax]] tried to read it once and found it very dull.{{book ref|mb5|19}}
