**Lord Stanton** is the lord of a noble house in [[Elendel]] on [[Scadrial]].{{book ref|mb4|1}}

==History==
Soon after High Lord [[Waxillium Ladrian]]'s return to [[Elendel]] from the [[Roughs]], he attended an event hosted by Lord Stanton.{{book ref|mb4|1}} Rather than spend time with the other nobles, Wax chose to leave the party and explore the [[mist]]s, leaping and moving about with his [[Allomancy]] and [[Feruchemy]]. This was an insult to Lord Stanton and his hospitality, so Wax later sent him a letter apologizing for his misconduct. This event and others, including a similar incident at a party hosted by Lady [[Aving Cett]], led to Wax developing a reputation for wild behavior and boorishness.{{book ref|mb4|2}}

==Trivia==
*There is a street in Elendel named Stanton Way, presumably after House Stanton.{{book ref|mb5|12}}
