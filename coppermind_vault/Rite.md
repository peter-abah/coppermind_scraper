**Rite** is the current [[Lord Artisan]] of [[Lossand]]. He is the Taisha in charge of the [[Lossand#The_Draft|Draft]]. He resides at the Artisan Conference Center  and leads the manufactured goods industry of Lossand. He was selected to be Lord Artisan by a vote of the guild's members.

## History
In the first vote of the [[Taishin]] Rite voted for the [[Diem]]'s dissolution. After Kenton, at the advising of [[Khriss]], promised the services of sand masters to the Artisans Guild, and with the help of a loan from the Guild, repaid the Diem's 150,000 [[lak]] debt; Rite agreed to vote for the Diem.
