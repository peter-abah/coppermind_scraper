**Koron** is an [[Alethi]] accent. It is from near the [[Sunmaker Mountains]] in Central [[Alethkar]], though the etymology of the name Koron is not clear, as there are not towns or regions in the area with a name similar to Koron.

Koron accents are slow drawls, with "to" sounding like "ta." [[Rial]] has a Koron accent.
