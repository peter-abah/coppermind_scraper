A **Lodestar** is an appointed leader of a community on [[Canticle]] who navigates a city along its route. In some communities, the Lodestar also acts as a visionary who guides the long term plans of the city. On [[Beacon]], a Lodestar is appointed by a vote of the [[Greater Good]].

==History==
The term originates on [[Threnody]] with the [[Zellion (Threnody)|first Lodestar]] who led a group of Threnodites to Canticle to escape internal strife in the aftermath of the [[Evil]].
