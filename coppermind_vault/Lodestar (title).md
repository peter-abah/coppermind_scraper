A **Lodestar** is an appointed leader of a community on [[Canticle]] who navigates a city along its route.{{book ref|tsm|8}} In some communities, the Lodestar also acts as a visionary who guides the long term plans of the city.{{book ref|tsm|11}}{{book ref|tsm|20}} On [[Beacon]], a Lodestar is appointed by a vote of the [[Greater Good]].{{book ref|tsm|20}}

==History==
The term originates on [[Threnody]] with the [[Zellion (Threnody)|first Lodestar]] who led a group of Threnodites to Canticle to escape internal strife in the aftermath of the [[Evil]].{{book ref|tsm|11}}{{book ref|tsm|15}}
