**Audrey** is one of [[Stephen Leeds]]' [[aspect]]s; an expert on handwriting and cryptography.{{book ref|legion|1}}{{book ref|legion2|9}}

## Appearance and Personality
Audrey has long dark hair and self-described as "curvaceous". She has a pretty face.{{book ref|legion|1}}{{book ref|legion2|8}}

She didn't mind being an hallucination, and joked about it.{{book ref|legion|1}} As the most self-aware of Leeds's aspects, she got away with things he wouldn't have expected.{{book ref|legion2|8}} 

Steve later understand that knowing she is imaginary was not self-awareness but her imagined psychosis. This means, she would think she was imaginary even if she was real. All aspects are insane in some way, this time her insanity just aligned with reality.{{book ref|legion2|14}} [[Tobias]] thinks she is a sort of compensating factor in Leeds psychology, a way to blow off a steam.{{book ref|legion2|8}} She has a habit of visiting her sister in Springfield.{{book ref|legion|1}}

## Attributes and Abilities
Audrey is an expert on handwriting, able to recognize if two texts were written by the same hand, or it was an attempted forgery.{{book ref|Legion|1}} She also seems to keep record of handwriting of people, and can compare a text with those memories to find the author.{{book ref|Legion|3}}

She also has cryptography knowledge. The research she knew described decoding messages by noticing intentional changes in handwriting. The knowledge is deepened after further reading.{{book ref|Legion2|8}}{{book ref|legion2|9}}

Although an expert in handwriting analysis, her penmanship was awful.{{book ref|Legion2|8}}

On her spare time, she learned biochemistry equations and was trying to figure out the method Panos used to create a virus; but [[Stephen Leeds|Stephen]] erases that memory to avoid overloading himself.{{book ref|Legion2|8}}

## History
Audrey was created after [[Stephen Leeds]] studied handwriting analysis. He also did some tangential research in cryptography which she also absorbed.{{book ref|legion2|8}}

She helps him on discovering the true authors of several writings/letters. For example, she was able to expose an undercover student who wanted to get close to him to write a report by saying he had a case. Audrey was able to confirm his writing was the same as the supposed letter from his girlfriend.{{book ref|legion|1}}

She does not go out on missions. The I3 investigation company mission was the second one she had been to.{{book ref|legion2|19}} 

After acquiring deeper cryptography knowledge, she went out with Stephen Leads for the investigation of I3 company. Her first contribution was coming up with a "hacking" trick to get Zen's phone number; an assassin that was tailing them while driving.{{book ref|legion2|9}} She also explained that people are less secure than the encryption systems they use, which also helped Leeds figure out how the corpse was taken out the facilities.{{book ref|legion2|13}}

Audrey figured out that Panos was still working on viruses even after they had shut down that research on I3.{{book ref|legion2|14}}

Later on, when Leeds was separated from most of his aspects, Audrey remained in the car with him. She was scared, crying and did not help much at the beginning. But during their captivity she figured out where the body was.{{book ref|legion2|19}} She was able to figure out the cleaning lady was actually Panos mother, whose zero in the time stamp was very distinctive.{{book ref|legion2|21}}

## Relationships
### Stephen Leeds
She is not one of Stephen's closest aspects, usually staying home while Stephen, [[Ivy]], [[Tobias]] and [[J.C.]] go out on cases. She seems to prefer this, seeming to assume she'll get shot at.
