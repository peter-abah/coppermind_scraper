The **Dashir boys** are criminals which High Lord [[Waxillium Ladrian]] pursued during his time as a lawman in the [[Roughs]] of [[Scadrial]]. 

Lady [[Steris Harms]] compiled biographies of many of Wax's old enemies, including the Dashir boys, in order to prepare for their wedding. She hoped to invite one of them to the wedding ceremony so that she could plan for a disturbance.
