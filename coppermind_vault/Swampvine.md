**Swampvine** is a plant on [[Patji (island)|Patji]] in the [[Pantheon]] of [[First of the Sun]].

It releases a poisonous gas when disturbed, but the gas cloud disperses quickly and is only dangerous for a short time. The gas is only harmful if it is inhaled.
