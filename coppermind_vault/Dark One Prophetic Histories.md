***Dark One: Prophetic Histories*** was the planned second installment of the audio originals set in the [[Dark One (franchise)|Dark One]] universe. It was developed by [[Mainframe]] and the rights were held by Recorded Books.{{url ref|name=catalog|url=https://www.awfulagent.com/catalogs/|site=awfulagent.com|text=JABberwocky Catalog & Rights Guide|date=2021-06-14}}

*Prophetic Histories* was conceived as an audio novelization of Brandon's original outline that was also used as the basis for the *Dark One* graphic novels.{{reddit ref|brandonsanderson|hdot60|fvou8lu|date=2020-06-22|text=The Original (audio book) release date, and an unexpected new story!!!}}

As of 2025, the project is cancelled and will not be made in any form.{{wob ref|17029}}
