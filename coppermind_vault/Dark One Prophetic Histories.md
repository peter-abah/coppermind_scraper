***Dark One: Prophetic Histories*** was the planned second installment of the audio originals set in the [[Dark One (franchise)|Dark One]] universe. It was developed by [[Mainframe]] and the rights were held by Recorded Books.

*Prophetic Histories* was conceived as an audio novelization of Brandon's original outline that was also used as the basis for the *Dark One* graphic novels.

As of 2025, the project is cancelled and will not be made in any form.
