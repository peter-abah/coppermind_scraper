**Varala** was a scholar on [[Roshar]].

They translated *[[The Song of the Last Summer]]* and claimed that [[Urithiru]] was inaccessible by foot, along with [[Sinbian]].

This is, of course, because the primary means of traveling to Urithiru was via the ten [[Oathgate]]s.
