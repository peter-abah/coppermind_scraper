**Kinaler** is a steelworker and a member of the [[Luthadel Assembly]] on [[Scadrial]].

He was worried that the [[Central Dominance]] needed good relations with the surrounding kingdoms and regarded an alliance with [[Ashweather Cett]] to be the best way to preserve [[Luthadel]].
