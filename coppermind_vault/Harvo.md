**Harvo** is a [[singer]] on [[Roshar]].

Harvo is one of the [[listener]]s and survived at least most of the [[War of Reckoning]]. When [[Venli]] left [[Urithiru]] to find to the listeners, [[Rlain]] asked her to give his regards to Harvo and [[Thude]] if they were still alive.
