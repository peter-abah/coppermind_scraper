**Isom** is a [[Order of Lightweavers|Lightweaver]] and a member of the [[Unseen Court]] on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa4|20}}{{book ref|sa4|50}}

He was in [[Torol Sadeas|Sadeas]]'s army, but fled because his captain constantly beat him. He then joined [[Vathah]]'s crew and was recruited by [[Shallan]] on her way to the [[Shattered Plains]].{{book ref|sa4|26}} During [[Raboniel]]'s invasion of [[Urithiru]], [[Lyn]] thought she saw Isom and [[Teft]] on a balcony holding a sign with the [[glyphs]] for "patience" and "progress;" however, this was a deception created by the [[Fused]].{{book ref|sa4|50}} Eight days before the [[contest of champions]], Shallan asked Isom to tail [[Adolin]] and covertly keep an eye on him. Isom reported that Adolin had gone to check on [[Gallant]] rather than going straight to the next meeting of monarchs. When Adolin did head up to the meeting, he got on the lift at the same time as the group from [[Azir]], leaving no room for Isom. Isom made a signal to Shallan that he would follow Adolin up in the next lift.{{book ref|sa5|17}}
