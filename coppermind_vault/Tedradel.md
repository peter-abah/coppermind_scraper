**Tedradel** is a kingdom on [[Nalthis]].{{book ref|wb|32}} It has existed for at least three hundred years.{{book ref|wb|32}} 

## Geography
Tedradel lies east of the [[Bright Sea]], directly across from [[Hallandren]].{{book ref|wb|25}} Not much is known of Tedradel’s climate, although it is known to be either a mountain, prairie, steppe or a desert climate.{{book ref|wb|32}}

## Government
### Monarchy
Tedradel is a kingdom, and as such it is ruled by a monarch.{{book ref|wb|32}}

### Foreign Policy and International Relationships
==== Hallandren ====
Tedradel’s opinion of Hallandren hasn’t improved much since the [[Manywar]], which saw Hanald, the predecessor of Hallandren, and Tedradel go to war with each other{{book ref|wb|32}}{{book ref|wb|1}} Their opinion of Hallandren is low enough that [[Yarda]] considers it possible that Tedradel would go to war with Hallandren again, should Idris be attacked.{{book ref|wb|1}} 

Tedradel still has many traders in T’Telir,{{book ref|wb|9}} and likely other Hallandren cities as well.

==== Idris ====
Tedradel has good relations with Idris.{{book ref|wb|1}} These nations also have many similarities, such as in fashion, with both nations preferring covering clothing.{{book ref|wb|25}}

==Culture==
### People
The people of Tedradel have brown skin, and they often have long black beards tied into a cylindrical shape with a leather cord.{{book ref|wb|25}} 

### Fashion
Tedradel fashion is similar to Idrian fashion, with dresses being long, covering everything from the ankle to the neck.{{book ref|wb|25}} There is a type of dress sold in Hallandren that is tube-like and tightly fitted.{{book ref|wb|25}}

## Economy
Tedradel lies on the shores of the [[Bright Sea]], as such they have access to its trade routes.{{book ref|wb|25}}{{book ref|wb|9}} They have many traders in [[Hallandren]].{{book ref|wb|9}}

It is possible that they also export fine pottery, as some is to be found in Hallandren that comes from across the [[Bright Sea]], where Tedradel was located.{{book ref|wb|3}}

## History
### Establishment
Tedradel was established at an unknown date before the [[Manywar]].{{Book ref|wb|32}}

### Tensions with Hanald
A little more then three hundred years before the [[Pahn Kahl rebellion]], a short time before the Manywar, tensions with [[Hanald]] were at an all time high.{{Book ref|wb|32}} This was because of two main reasons.

Hanald had a monopoly on the dye trade, which, at the time, was one of the most profitable trades.{{Book ref|wb|32}} Other kingdoms, including Tedradel, wished to profit from this trade as well, but Hanald didn’t allow them to.{{Book ref|wb|32}}

Hanald also turned its attention to the relatively new art of [[Awakening]].{{book ref|wb|32}} During this time they gained powerful awakeners and [[Lifeless]], which other kingdoms didn’t have.{{Book ref|wb|32}} Many different discoveries were made during this time, including the command to make Lifeless with a single breath and [[Ichor-alcohol]]. These discoveries allowed nations to build far larger and cheaper armies,{{book ref|wb|32}} and become far more powerful, raising tensions even higher. 

At some point, one of the [[Returned]] in Hanald had a vision of war.{{book ref|wb|32}} This ended up being the catalyst for a war, as it prompted [[Strifelover]] to strike preemptively against the other nations.{{book ref|wb|32}}

### The Manywar 
Soon after [[Kuth]] and [[Huth]] were attacked, Tedradel was dragged into the war, opposing [[Hanald]].{{book ref|wb|1}} It is unknown if they joined the [[Pahn Unity]], although they did fight on the same side.{{book ref|wb|1}} Sometime after the destruction of Kuth and Huth, Strifelover withdrew his phantoms, taking back control of his own kingdom. He then made peace with the remaining kingdoms, including Tedradel.{{book ref|wb|32}} However, since the Manywar, Tedradel's hatred and animosity towards Hallandren has not lessened greatly.{{book ref|wb|1}}
