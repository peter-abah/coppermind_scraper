**Olid** served as [[Quellion]]'s foreign minister in [[Urteau]] after the [[Collapse]].

Olid brought news that [[Elend Venture]], emperor of the [[New Empire]], had captured [[Vetitan]] and obtained a new [[koloss]] army. He had heard rumors that Elend sent a member of the Survivor's crew, [[Spook]],to offer them a peace treaty. Olid advised Quellion to take it, as he did not believe that Urteau could defeat Elend and his armies.
