The **Siege of Luthadel** was a major event following the end of the [[Final Empire]] on [[Scadrial]].{{book ref|mb2|16}}

The armies of [[Straff Venture]], [[Ashweather Cett]], and the [[koloss]] army of [[Jastes Lekal]] besieged [[Luthadel]], King [[Elend Venture]]'s throne.{{book ref|mb2|1}}{{book ref|mb2|10}}{{book ref|mb2|19}} Originally, Straff Venture's numerically superior force was poised to take control of the city, leading to some diplomatic overtures between Cett and Elend in response.{{book ref|mb2|35}} However, this balance was upset by the entry of the koloss army, which had the potential to defeat any of the human armies present.{{book ref|mb2|42}}{{book ref|mb2|44}} Elend was deposed and the city raised [[Ferson Penrod]] to be king.{{book ref|mb2|38}} [[Vin]] intimidated the opposition through the use of [[duralumin]]-enhanced emotional [[Allomancy]] upon Venture and an assault upon Cett's force within Luthadel's walls.{{book ref|mb2|50}}{{book ref|mb2|43}} However, Vin's apparent need to seek the [[Well of Ascension]] and save Scadrial from the [[Deepness]] led her to leave the city, accompanied by Elend and [[Spook]], on a journey to [[Terris]] before the battle for the city began.{{book ref|mb2|48}}{{book ref|mb2|49}}

The [[Battle of Luthadel]] was triggered by the lack of control that Lekal held over his koloss force, which attacked the city of its own accord.{{book ref|mb2|51}} The koloss force successfully breached the city walls, which, with their numerous apertures in the eight city gates, were difficult for the defenders to hold.{{book ref|mb2|52}} During this assault, [[Dockson]], [[Clubs]], and [[Tindwyl]] were all killed by the koloss.{{book ref|mb2|53}}{{book ref|mb2|54}}

Upon realizing that the Well was in Luthadel and that she, Elend and Spook were sent away for their own safety, Vin returned to the city and took control of the koloss using emotional Allomancy.{{book ref|mb2|51}}{{book ref|mb2|54}} Her swift execution of Straff Venture and her control over the koloss army allowed Elend to retake control of Luthadel and be crowned Emperor of the [[New Empire]].{{book ref|mb2|55}}
