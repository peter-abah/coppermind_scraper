A **skop** is a person who tells stories and protects people from malevolent [[landswight]]s and other entities.{{book ref|frugal|13}} They are known as **skalds** to the [[Hordamen]]. Hordamen are the name of [[Wikipedia: Vikings| Vikings]] in this alternate dimension. Skops are losing their power of wights and mostly pretending to help, main example being [[Sefawynn]].{{book ref|frugal|14}} Hordamen skops seem to be more effective, and can use their boasts to intimidate landswights and weaken the protection of [[runestone]]s.{{book ref|frugal|13}}
