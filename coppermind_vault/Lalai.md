Brightness **Lalai** is a lighteyed woman on [[Roshar]].

Lalai is a cousin of [[Highprince]] [[Torol Sadeas]]. She served as one of King [[Elhokar Kholin]]'s clerks, in [[Jasnah Kholin]]'s absence. Lalai and Jasnah did not get along well.
