Brightness **Lalai** is a lighteyed woman on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa1|28}}

Lalai is a cousin of [[Highprince]] [[Torol Sadeas]].{{book ref|sa1|28}} She served as one of King [[Elhokar Kholin]]'s clerks, in [[Jasnah Kholin]]'s absence. Lalai and Jasnah did not get along well.
