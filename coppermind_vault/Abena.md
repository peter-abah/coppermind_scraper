**Abena** is a [[Darkeyes|darkeyed]] woman on [[Roshar]]. She is compassionate, perceptive and independent. Once a bailiff, she at some point decided to travel to the [[Shattered Plains]] to get a new life. She now works as a scout for the [[Alethi]] armies, and she aims to become a highly respected one.{{rpg ref|b9|37}}
