**Candlemore** is a lighthouse in the [[Sea of Lost Lights]]. It lies in the [[Sea of Oracles]], and is near the [[Salavashi Trench]].

On [[:file: Sea of Lost Lights.jpg|the map of the Sea of Lost Lights]] that [[Nazh]] stole, he writes that he rode from [[Celebrant]] to Candlemore on a [[mandra]], and that someone (likely [[Khriss]]) owes him some silver pieces for a bet.

In the [[Physical Realm]], it is likely very close to [[Kelathar]], north of the [[Sunmaker Mountains]].
