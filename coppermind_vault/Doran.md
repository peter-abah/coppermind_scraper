**Doran** is an operative in the [[Phone Company]]. When [[Jason Write]] travels to [[Evensong]] to retrieve [[Denise Carlson]], he asks [[Lanna Write]] if Doran is available as a support operative instead of her as part of his act to hide his relationship with Lanna from the [[United Intelligence Bureau]]. At the time, Doran was unavailable as she was on maternity leave.{{book ref|elysium}}
