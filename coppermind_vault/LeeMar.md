**LeeMar** is a [[kandra]] on [[Scadrial]].

As of , LeeMar had been impersonating [[Cassileux]] on the [[Elendel]] constabulary for sixteen months, ever since the latter's death during a raid on the [[Nomad Gang]]. LeeMar was one of several kandra infiltrating the constables, and she kept an eye on [[Marasi]], occasionally having lunch with her. During the constables' investigation in the caverns below Elendel, LeeMar discretely recovered a note from the [[Ghostbloods]] which [[Moonlight]] left for Marasi expressing their interest in her. She gave it to [[VenDell]] to pass on to Marasi, at which point he told Marasi about Cassileux's true nature.
