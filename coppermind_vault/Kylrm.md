**Kylrm** is a guardsman on [[Roshar]].

He is the head guard of [[Vstim]]'s merchant train.

During Vstim's visit to [[Shinovar]], Kylrm is suspicious of the Shin, and they outnumber him and the rest of the guards.

On the way to the [[Reshi]] island, Klyrm plays with [[Rysn]]'s parasol. She is surprised at him not warning her about the island, or its inhabitants. He and [[Nlent]] accompany Rysn up to see the king of the isle, keeping hold of his [[gtet]].
