Inspector **Harding** is a federal inspector of the [[United Isles of America]].{{book ref|Rithmatist|4}}

## Attributes and Abilities
He often rides an Equilix Horse{{book ref|Rithmatist|11}} and uses spring rifle as weapon.{{book ref|Rithmatist|24}} With the [[Rithmatics|Rithmatic]] powers granted by a [[Forgotten]], he draws with a chalk on the tip of the rifle.{{book ref|Rithmatist|23}}{{book ref|Rithmatist|24}} He seems to have the inherent ability to draw quite perfect circles and lines; however, he knows nothing of drawing a proper defense and can be easily fooled in a duel.{{book ref|Rithmatist|24}}

## History
Prior to working as a federal inspector, Harding was a soldier at the [[Tower of Nebrask]]. One day, he went into a breach with [[Andrew Nalizar]] to save [[William Muns]], and was possessed by a [[Forgotten]].

He is sent to [[Armedius Academy]] to investigate the disappearance of [[Lilly Whiting]], which becomes the case of the Scribbler.
[[Thomas York|Principal York]] assigns him Professor [[Fitch]] to assist in the investigation.{{book ref|Rithmatist|6}}

Harding, however, was the Scribbler. [[Chalkling#Wild Chalklings|Wild chalklings]] under the command of his Forgotten had consumed William Muns, and he traveled to Armedius to continue this work.{{book ref|Rithmatist|24}} He first attacked and consumed [[Lilly Whiting]], to spread panic between the students. Then, he insisted that students stay at school under his protection, abducting [[Charles Calloway]], the son of a knight senator, at his home to further discourage any thoughts on leaving the school.{{book ref|Rithmatist|23}} After all students were concentrated at the school as he had planned, he went on, attacking and consuming the students methodically. Eventually, he is stopped by [[Fitch]], [[Melody]] and [[Joel]], and the people consumed by his wild chalklings are released.{{book ref|Rithmatist|24}}
