**Arintol** is a solicitor in [[Elendel]] on [[Scadrial]].

## History
[[Wayne]] was told by people he spoke to on the street that Arintol was an honest solicitor. While in the guise of a corrupt constable, Wayne recommended Arintol's services to a captured [[Vanishers|Vanisher]], a man named Sindren. He told [[Sindren]] to get the constables to promise him freedom with Arintol in the room and then confess his knowledge of the Vanishers' activities.
