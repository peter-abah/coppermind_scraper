**Xinyi** is a commander in the [[DDF]] of a unknown species.{{book ref|sky4|34}}

## Appearance and the Personality
Nothing is known about her appearance, age, race, or species, but is referred to by female pronouns. She is hesitant to use the onboard computer system of the *[[Defiant (DDF flagship)|Defiant]]* until Becca orders her to use it. After the order she and the rest of the crew become less distrusting of it.{{book ref|sky4|34}}

==History==
She was assigned to the *Defiant* for unknown reasons and by a unknown person. On the ship she gave tactical information to Becca Nightshade such as the time to engagement,{{book ref|sky4|34}} and she presumably left with the rest of the *Defiant’s* crew at the [[Battle of Evensong]].{{book ref|sky4|43}}
