**Lake Iri** is a body of water in northwest [[Roshar]].{{map ref|Roshar}} Most of the lake's mass lies in southern [[Rira (country)|Rira]], while the rest lies in eastern [[Iri]]. The lake itself forms part of the border between the two nations. At least two small rivers drain into Lake Iri from the Rira side. Annual harvests from this lake include large, fatty fish and a water vegetable known as [[dripgrass]].{{rpg ref|slwg|52}}
