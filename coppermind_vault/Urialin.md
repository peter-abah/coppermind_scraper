**Urialin** is a [[Regal#Nightform|nightform]] [[Regal]] on [[Roshar]]. Urialin translates into "light".{{book ref|sa4|83}}

## History
During the [[singer]] occupation of [[Urithiru]], [[Lezian]] posted Urialin and [[Nistar]] to guard the [[Knights Radiant|Radiant]] infirmary where [[Lirin]] and [[Hesina]] worked, likely at [[Raboniel]]'s suggestion.{{book ref|sa4|83}}
