**Powder**  is an [[Epic]] that tried to conquer [[wikipedia: Canada|Canada]]. He could cause unstable materials like gunpowder to explode just by looking at them.

He attempted to conquer Canada during the second year of Epics, and attacked their military bases. When he attacked the base in [[wikipedia: CFB Trenton|Trenton]] where [[Abraham]] was stationed, he killed Abraham's entire squad, but Abraham survived due to being imprisoned awaiting a court-martial.
