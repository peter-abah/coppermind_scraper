Queen **Kamiko** is a monarch of the [[Free Kingdoms]].

## Appearance and Personality

Kamiko is an Asian-looking woman of around fifty years of age. She dislikes people disagreeing with her.

## History

After the [[Hushlands]] defeat at taking the Free Kingdoms nation of [[Mokia]], Queen Kamiko met via [[Communicator's Glass]] with [[Leavenworth Smedry]] alongside other monarchs of the Free Kingdoms to discuss sending people to help the [[House Smedry|Smedrys]]. Kamiko did not like the way [[Alcatraz]] spoke to her and the other monarchs, and cut off communication. Alcatraz used his [[Smedry Talent|Talent]] to reconnect communication with the monarchs.
