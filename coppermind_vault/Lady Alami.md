> — [[Hoid]] taunting the lady

**Lady Alami** is an [[Alethi]]  noblewoman, present on the [[Shattered Plains]] around the end of the [[War of Reckoning]] in 1173.

Two days after a [[highstorm]] where [[Dalinar Kholin]] first witnessed the [[Dalinar's visions#Starfalls|Starfalls vision]], King [[Elhokar]] held a [[feasting basin|feast]]. The King's Wit, [[Hoid]], insulted Lady Alami's intelligence there.
