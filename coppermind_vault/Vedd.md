**Vedd** is a [[Darkeyes|darkeyed]] soldier on [[Roshar]]. He is a protective, short-tempered and self-sacrificing veteran soldier. However, after being disgraced and made to join the bridge crews of the [[Torol Sadeas|Sadeas]] military, his main focus is helping the other members of [[Bridge Nine]] survive and escape. Vedd is a grizzled warrior who shines in combat, and he isn’t afraid of being in the thick of it.{{rpg ref|b9|38}}
