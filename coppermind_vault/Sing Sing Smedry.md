**Sing Sing Smedry** is a [[House Smedry|Smedry]] with the [[Smedry Talent|Talent]] to trip and fall down. He is the older brother of [[Australia Smedry]] and [[Aydee Ecks Smedry]].

## Appearance and Personality
Sing Sing is six and a half feet tall and has dark hair and dark skin. He is quite large.{{book ref|a1|3}} While in [[Hushlands]] he wears a kimono as a disguise,{{book ref|a1|4}} but wears a tunic when in the [[Free Kingdoms]].{{book ref|a1|3}}{{book ref|a3|5}}

Sing is cheerful, good-natured, and loyal, showing great kindness to [[Alcatraz]] on their first mission together. He loves books and learning and shows great enthusiasm for his job.{{book ref|a1|11}}

## Attributes and Abilities
Sing has the Talent of tripping and falling to the ground, which he is able to use to great effect. One feature of his Talent is that he tends to trip whenever danger is nearby (and when tripping would be helpful).{{book ref|a1|8}} This is categorized as a Knowledge Talent on the [[Smedry Talent|Incarnate Wheel]].{{book ref|a2|10}}

{{quote|The hefty Mokian quite convincingly stumbled on the welcome mat right inside the doors. He cried out, hopping on one foot—a teetering, lumbering mound with the kinetic energy of a collapsing building.|Alcatraz{{book ref|a1|7}} }}

Additionally, Sing is an anthropologist specializing in ancient (Hushlander) weaponry, and he prefers to use guns over more “advanced” weapons.{{book ref|a1|4}} He is very interested in [[Hushlander]] culture, though he despises the [[Librarian]]s.{{book ref|a1|6}} Although not an [[Oculator]], he uses Warrior's Lenses effectively.{{book ref|a1|4}}

===Alcatraz Smedry===
Sing treats [[Alcatraz]] with a degree of deference and respect, admiring his young cousin's [[Oculator]] powers and powerful [[Smedry Talent|Talent]]. However, he also mentors Alcatraz, providing him with encouragement and information about the Librarians and the Free Kingdoms.
