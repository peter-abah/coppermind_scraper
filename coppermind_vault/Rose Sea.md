The **Rose Sea** is an ocean on [[Lumar]] and is the source of [[roseite spores]]. It borders the [[Emerald Sea]] and four other seas. Unlike the [[Crimson Sea]] and [[Midnight Sea]], the Rose Sea is safely inhabited.{{book ref|tress|10}} Like all seas on Lumar, it is pentagonal in shape.{{book ref|tress|42}}

Both [[Ann]] and [[Fort]] claim to have sailed on the Rose Sea.{{book ref|tress|23}}
