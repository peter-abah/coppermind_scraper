The **Rose Sea** is an ocean on [[Lumar]] and is the source of [[roseite spores]]. It borders the [[Emerald Sea]] and four other seas. Unlike the [[Crimson Sea]] and [[Midnight Sea]], the Rose Sea is safely inhabited. Like all seas on Lumar, it is pentagonal in shape.

Both [[Ann]] and [[Fort]] claim to have sailed on the Rose Sea.
