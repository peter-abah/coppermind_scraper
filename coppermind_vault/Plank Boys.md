> Do you think you'd have fallen in with the Plank Boys if your mother hadn't died in that accident?
> — [[Waxillium Ladrian]] to [[Wayne]]

The **Plank Boys** are a criminal group or gang in the [[Roughs]] on [[Scadrial]].

[[Wayne]] joined the Plank Boys for a period of time following his [[Wayne's mother|mother's]] death and considered some of the other members his friends. They recognized Wayne's growing potential and talked him into branching out into robbery with guns; the ensuing incident led to [[Durkel]]'s death and indirectly brought about Wayne's association with [[Waxillium Ladrian]]. Waxillium later brought up the Plank Boys to illustrate to Wayne how creating a world in which fewer children are left to grow up alone could lead to fewer criminals.
