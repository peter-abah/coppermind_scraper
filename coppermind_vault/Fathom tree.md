**Fathom** is a type of tree native to [[Roshar]].{{book ref|twok|38}}

Fathom bark can be used to reduce cranial swelling{{book ref|twok|38}} and also as a painkiller{{book ref|sa2|76}}. It can be administered by chewing, or it can be prepared for topical use by boiling until soft then grinding it into a paste{{rpg ref|slwg|12}}.

==History==
After surviving his judgement by the [[Stormfather]] in {{Rosharan date|1173}}, a semi-conscious [[Kaladin]] thought that he should take fathom bark to reduce the swelling of his brain.{{book ref|sa1|38}} He later took fathom bark to manage the pain in his leg after he was injured in the chasms of the [[Shattered Plains]] by a [[chasmfiend]].{{book ref|sa2|76}} While Kaladin was secretly tending to an unresponsive [[Teft]] in [[Urithiru]], fathom bark was one of the medical supplies that he procured with [[Kuno]]'s help.{{book ref|sa4|49}}

[[Tukks]] chewed fathom bark habitually.{{book ref|sa3|91}}
