**Fathom** is a type of tree native to [[Roshar]].

Fathom bark can be used to reduce cranial swelling and also as a painkiller. It can be administered by chewing, or it can be prepared for topical use by boiling until soft then grinding it into a paste.

==History==
After surviving his judgement by the [[Stormfather]] in , a semi-conscious [[Kaladin]] thought that he should take fathom bark to reduce the swelling of his brain. He later took fathom bark to manage the pain in his leg after he was injured in the chasms of the [[Shattered Plains]] by a [[chasmfiend]]. While Kaladin was secretly tending to an unresponsive [[Teft]] in [[Urithiru]], fathom bark was one of the medical supplies that he procured with [[Kuno]]'s help.

[[Tukks]] chewed fathom bark habitually.
