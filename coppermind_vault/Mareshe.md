> I ... am a craftsman. An artisan. I can make anything, for [[Domi]] Himself has granted me the soul of an artist.
> — Mareshe

**Mareshe** is an [[Elantrian]] from [[Sel]]. He was taken by the [[Shaod]] after the [[Reod]] and became an important figure in [[New Elantris]].

## Appearance and Personality
Mareshe is a spindly man in his late forties. He is relatively short, with a small face. When first taken by the incomplete Shaod, he had a gaunt face, nervous eyes, and the typical sickly look of an Elantrian. He is later seen in the typical bright-colored clothing of New Elantris, although he added several sashes in an attempt to look more "artistic".

He speaks with a somewhat formal air, typically addressing Raoden as "my lord". He is egotistical and haughty, and his boasting particularly annoys [[Galladon]]. He initially follows Raoden out of fear, but he quickly comes to trust him and works diligently to accomplish whatever task he is assigned. He is always eager to impress Raoden with his various skills.  Mareshe becomes one of Raoden's closest advisors and a leader in New Elantris, although he is sometimes a bit frustrated that he is not privy to all of Raoden's secrets.

## Attributes and Abilities
Mareshe was a jeweler by trade and claims to be a skilled jack-of-all-trades. He is surprisingly able to substantiate these claims with many innovative uses of the minimal resources of [[Elantris (city)|Elantris]]. He is intelligent, organized, and pragmatic. He is able to make shoes that fit perfectly using only leather from book bindings, later enhancing them with thick soles studded with nails for traction. He creates an earthen building material that is strong enough to reinforce structures and create new roofs, makes ropes out of torn rags, and devises makeshift sleds that allow heavy items to be transported within the city.

He is able to tell stories in a dramatic (albeit longwinded) fashion that can hold people's attention and gain their trust. He does not have much experience with acting, although he thinks he is good at it.

Assuming that he survived the [[Invasion of Arelon]] ([[Mareshe#Invasion of Arelon|see below]]), he gained the typical powers of a restored Elantrian, including a magically enhanced physiology and the ability to use [[AonDor]].

## History
### New Elantris
Mareshe was taken by the Shaod two days after Raoden and deposited in Elantris. He was presumably Arelene, as he almost recognized Raoden despite the effects of the Shaod. Mareshe was rather terrified, and gladly accepted Raoden and Galladon's help in evading [[Shaor]]'s men. He soon became the first person that Raoden recruited to his cause of improving life for Elantrians. Asked about his profession, he told Raoden that he was a jeweler, a craftsman, and an artisan. Raoden assigned him to make shoes for the Elantrians, a difficult task since they had to fit their bearers perfectly to avoid blistering wounds that would never heal. Despite a lack of raw materials, Mareshe was able to make high quality shoes using leather from old book covers.

Mareshe's speaking skills were valuable in recruiting newcomers to Raoden's cause. At Raoden's direction, Mareshe later worked with other Elantrians such as [[Riil]], [[Maare]], and [[Kahar]] to set them on tasks that improved Elantris and provided them with purpose. Mareshe informed Raoden of [[Sarene]]'s arrival in the city for her [[Widow's Trial]] and met with her posing as "[[Aanden]]". He unintentionally came off as pompous and blustering, allowing [[Karata]] to do the actual negotiating. Mareshe subsequently devised a sled system that allowed heavy boxes of goods from Sarene to be moved around the city. He also kept track of the resources that came from Sarene, and helped Raoden think of unconventional ways to use them. He later used the feral men who had followed Shaor as laborers to further improve the city.

> [Galladon] disappears like this sometimes. Karata too. They'll never tell me where they’ve gone. Always so secretive! ‘You’re in charge, Mareshe,’ they say, then go off to have secret conferences without me. Honestly!
> — Mareshe, to Matisse

Mareshe slept in the chapel in New Elantris. He was dubious about Raoden's theory that some of the [[Hoed]] in the chapel had been true Elantrians before the [[Reod]]. 

### Invasion of Arelon
Just before the [[Invasion of Arelon]], Mareshe went to the [[Roost]] late at night looking for [[Dashe]]. [[Matisse]] admonished him for being too loud. Upon learning that Dashe was not around, he complained about people like Galladon and Karata disappearing and being secretive.

During the invasion, Galladon saw Mareshe among the Hoed; Mareshe had an enormous, ragged hole torn in his chest, presumably from a [[Dakhor]] monk. Since Mareshe's body was mostly intact and he was not honored at the memorial for those that fell during the invasion, it is likely that he survived to see the restoration of Elantris and [[AonDor]].

## Relationships
### Raoden
Mareshe is a dedicated follower of Raoden, his friend and confidant, and a driving force behind the improvements to New Elantris. Raoden acknowledges that it was a stroke of luck that Mareshe came to Elantris immediately after his arrival, and he puts a great deal of trust in Mareshe to help run the city. Raoden does not fully share what he found at [[Devotion's Perpendicularity]] with Mareshe, primarily because he thinks it is too dangerous.

### Galladon
Galladon and Mareshe have rather incompatible personalities; Galladon is sarcastic and rude to Mareshe when they first meet. Galladon tends to doubt that Mareshe can actually live up to his boasting; he eventually recognizes Mareshe's ingenuity, but refuses to acknowledge it as he knows that doing so would stroke his ego. Galladon is upset by seeing Mareshe as a Hoed during the Invasion of Arelon.
