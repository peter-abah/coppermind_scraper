**Tek** is a carrier parrot on [[Roshar]] owned by [[Neturo-son-Vallano]] and [[Zeenid-daughter-Beth]]. They are capable of flying a note from their homestead to the town of [[Clearmount]].

When [[Szeth]] discovered a new stone on their property, Neturo used Tek to send a note to Zeenid about the situation.
