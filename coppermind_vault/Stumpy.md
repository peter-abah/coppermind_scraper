**Stumpy** is a [[tonk]] used by [[Khriss]]. During Khriss’s journey to visit the [[Diem]], she used this tonk. Along the way she accidentally spilled some water on its horn causing it to melt. This is why she gave the tonk the nickname ‘Stumpy’ Stumpy was sold with the other tonks when Khriss crossed the [[Ry'Do Ali River]] into [[Kezare]].
