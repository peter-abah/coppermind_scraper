**Stumpy** is a [[tonk]] used by [[Khriss]]. During Khriss’s journey to visit the [[Diem]], she used this tonk. Along the way she accidentally spilled some water on its horn causing it to melt. This is why she gave the tonk the nickname ‘Stumpy’{{book ref|ws|1|review=yes}} Stumpy was sold with the other tonks when Khriss crossed the [[Ry'Do Ali River]] into [[Kezare]].{{book ref|ws|4|review=yes}}
