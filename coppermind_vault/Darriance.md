**Darriance** is [[Waxillium Ladrian]]'s house butler at the Ladrian mansion in [[Elendel]] on [[Scadrial]].{{book ref|mb5|2}}

Darriance performs the usual duties of a butler, such as acting as a doorman and taking people's coats.{{book ref|mb5|7}} He takes messages and packages for Wax when he is away from the mansion, and [[Ranette]] trusts him enough to leave new equipment with him.{{book ref|mb5|7}} Darriance also helps Wax practice using a [[steel]] bubble by shooting at him while Wax wears extensive padding and armor.{{book ref|mb5|10}}

[[Wayne]] is amused by Darriance's adherence to protocol and is perpetually trying to provoke him, but Darriance just brushes it off.{{book ref|mb5|8}} Wayne jokes that Darriance will snap and try to kill everyone someday.{{book ref|mb6|4}}
