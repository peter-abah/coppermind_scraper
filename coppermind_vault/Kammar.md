**Kammar** is an ancient fighting stance developed on [[Roshar]].

Kammar is designed to defeat enemies without harming them, and so is considered a peaceful form. It focuses on grabbing people and immobilizing them by using their own weight against them. Only the hands are  used in kammar.

[[Szeth]] is trained in kammar, so it is possible that this is a [[Shin]]-specific art. The grabbing motions are ideal for [[Surgebinding|infusing]] people with [[Stormlight]], so Szeth is dangerous even with it alone, and without a [[Shardblade]].
