Lord **Detor** is one of [[Ashweather Cett]]'s generals during the [[Siege of Luthadel|Siege]] and [[Battle of Luthadel]].{{book ref|mb2|50}}

When [[Allrianne Cett]] returned to visit her father, Detor and [[Galivan]] were discussing strategy in an open-sided pavilion which contained a table with maps on it.{{book ref|mb2|50}}
