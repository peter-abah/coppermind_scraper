**Soomi** is a resident of [[Babilar]].{{book ref|Firefight|31}}

She is among the survivors from a fire and collapse of a building in Babilar. The group are rescued by the [[Reckoners]] and are told to stay away from the city for a few days to keep [[Regalia]] from knowing of the teams activities.
