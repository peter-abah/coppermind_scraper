The **Calzer** is a large, repeating gun used by the [[High Empire]]. It fires blue bolts and can be powered from the same source that powers weather regulators, though it consumes power quickly.

When [[Varion Crestmar]] launches his rebellion against the empire, imperial forces eventually get a Calzer working and use it to fire on him, but are unable to break through his advanced shielding before running out of power.
