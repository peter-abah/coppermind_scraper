The **First Company Expeditionary Navigators** is a subdivision of the greater [[First Company]], a mercantile business on [[First of the Sun]]. This subdivision Is focused on employing and training [[Eelakin]] to navigate the Emberdark regions of [[Shadesmar]]. It was suggested that they be named Trappers, as a continuation of tradition and as a way to embrace the future. This idea was discarded however, as they didn't actually trap anything.{{book ref|iote|epilogue}}

## Work and Abilities
The company has a monopoly on Eelakin navigators, who can find their way in the emberdark. Thus, they can charge high prices for their work. They do business with corporations, individuals and governments alike. Due to the incredibly important service they provide, several of the Cosmere's great powers, such as various Scadrian and Rosharan nations, rely on them. This also protects them from being conquered and absorbed into any one nation, as the others would try and prevent it. The First Company has made a trade deal with the dragon [[Xisisrefliel]]. The terms of this deal state that the dragon will spread the news of the Navigators, provide the Company with free translation devices, [[Lodestar_(technology)|Lodestar]]s and the ability to travel to [[Silverlight]]. In turn, Xisis is given the right of first negotiation for reduced tariffs.{{book ref|iote|epilogue}}

The Navigators employed by the First Company are all Eelakin who have spent considerable time near the [[Drominad System|Drominad]] [[perpendicularity]]. Their presence near the perpendicularity has changed them to be able to feel the 'current' of the Cosmere, the [[Knell]]. This gift allows them to be able to accurately navigate in the emberdark, the dangerous regions of Shadesmar that are unknown and inbetween planets. It is unclear whether or not a person needs to be near the physical side of the perpendicularity to gain this gift, or if a person can be on both sides. The First Company has plans to experiment with this however. It has also developed several training programs regarding survival and wayfinding techniques.{{book ref|iote|epilogue}}

## History
The First Company Expeditionary Navigators, or just the Expeditionary Navigators, were created as a response to the Eelakin learning of the wider Cosmere and of their own abilities. A man named [[Sixth of the Dusk]] gathered various trappers and began training them in his art. After approximately six months, the first members made a solo inaugural excursion into the emberdark. When they returned to the Drominad perpendicularity, which served as their homebase, they were welcomed by a large number of officials, chiefs, [[kingmaker]]s and senators. President [[Vathi]] was among those to welcome the Navigators.{{book ref|iote|epilogue}}
