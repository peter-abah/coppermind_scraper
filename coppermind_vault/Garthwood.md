> Except, koloss didn't take bribes. They just killed. Fatren shuddered, thinking of Garthwood. That city had been bigger than his own, but fewer than a hundred survivors had made their way to Vetitan.
> — [[Fatren]], thinking about the destruction of Garthwood.

**Garthwood** is a city in the [[Final Empire]] on [[Scadrial]]. It is in the [[Southern Dominance]], southeast of [[Vetitan]].

Three months before [[koloss]] attacked [[Vetitan]], koloss attacked Garthwood. Garthwood was larger than Vetitan--which meant that it had many more than Vetitan's seven thousand residents--but less than a hundred survivors eventually made it to Vetitan.
