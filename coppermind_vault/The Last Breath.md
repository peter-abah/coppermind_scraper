**The Last Breath** is a quick, lively song with a simple and easy tune. [[Wayne]] particularly likes the song, and the pianoforte is playing it when Wayne and [[Ranette]] first meet. Wayne occasionally whistles the tune, and has a street musician play it for him one day when on his way to talk to Ranette.
