The **Oathbound Spires** is a location in the [[Sea of Souls]], part of the [[Shadesmar|Rosharan subastral]] of the [[Cognitive Realm]]. It is located on a peninsula north of the [[Astral Banks]] and [[Lasting Integrity]], and west of [[Justice Untarnished]].{{map ref|Sea of Souls}} In the Physical Realm, it is near [[Sesemalex Dar]], and its peninsula is a river that separates [[Emul]] and [[Tukar]].{{map ref|Roshar}}

The Oathbound Spires's name and proximity to [[Lasting Integrity]] may suggest the place is under [[honorspren]] control.{{map ref|Sea of Souls}} 

It is unclear exactly if the Oathbound Spires is some sort of settlement or not.
