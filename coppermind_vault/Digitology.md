**Digitology** is a method of judging people based on the width of their fingers on [[Roshar]]. A [[stormwarden]] named [[Elthebar]] suggested that the gear ratio of the [[fabrial]] lifts of [[Urithiru]] could somehow be related to the fingers of the people who crafted them.{{book ref|sa3|44}}
