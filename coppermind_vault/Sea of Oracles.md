The **Sea of Oracles** is a region of the [[Sea of Lost Lights]]. It is north of [[Celebrant]], [[Emberdark (settlement)|Emberdark]], and the [[Burning Gardens]]. To the south, it is bordered with the [[Glasswater Deep]]. 

The [[Salavashi Trench]] is a portion of the Sea of Oracles. 

In the [[Physical Realm]], the Sea of Oracles corresponds to at least parts of Alethkar north of the [[Deathbend River]]. It is also north of the [[Tarat Sea]], so it seems the Sea of Oracles goes into [[Jah Keved]]. The sea's western boundary is unclear.
