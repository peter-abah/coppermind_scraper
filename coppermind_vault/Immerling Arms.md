**Immerling Arms** is an armament manufacturer in [[Elendel]] on [[Scadrial]], established in {{date|314|PC}}.{{file ref|Elendel Daily broadsheet.png|*The Elendel Daily* Broadsheet}} They primarily specialize in a patented "Break-Frame Mechanism" for loading bullets into the cylinder, as opposed to the typical swing-out design favored by most of manufacturers of the period. They are described as slightly inferior to the custom firearms made by [[Ranette]].{{book ref|mb4|2}}

## Models
### Model .44
The Immerling .44 is a long-barreled revolver primarily marketed towards lawmen and constables.{{file ref|Elendel Daily broadsheet.png|*The Elendel Daily* Broadsheet}}

### Model .44-S
The Immerling .44-S is a snubnose variant of the model .44. It is designed to be easier to conceal, although the accuracy suffers due to the shorter barrel. [[Wax]] carries one in a shoulder holster when he does not want to appear overtly armed.{{book ref|mb4|2}}
