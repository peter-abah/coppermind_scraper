> You'll be glad you stayed for PECTIN-ADE!
> — Slogan

**Pectin-Ade** is a type of snack on [[Scadrial]].

It comes in eight flavors and costs ten [[clip]]s a box. In an advertisement in *[[The House Record]]*, they claim to be the [[Elendel Basin|Basin's]] favorite snack.
