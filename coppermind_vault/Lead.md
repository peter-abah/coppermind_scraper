**Lead** is a soldier employed on [[Diggen's Point]] on [[Lumar]]. His duties include guarding the [[duke]]'s mansion.

[[Tress]] bribed Lead and [[Snagu]] with homemade pies to let her onto the grounds of the duke's mansion to meet with [[Charlie]].
