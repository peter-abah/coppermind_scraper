**Cordwood** is a type of wood on [[Roshar]]. It is implied to be stiff and heavy.

[[Rysn]] describes her legs as "dragging like cordwood" when she is trying to drag herself away from the [[Fused]] stealing the [[King's Drop]] from the [[Thaylen Gemstone Reserve]].
