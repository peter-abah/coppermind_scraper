**Biord** is a [[Scadrian]] special operative working for the [[Malwish Empire]] under Colonel [[Dajer]]. Dajer sent her and a fellow soldier through the cave system on the [[Dakwara's island]] in [[First of the Sun]]'s [[subastral]] where the pair both died from [[masher wasp]] stings after angering the hive. Her corpse was swiftly covered by fast-growing moss which hid it from the drones sent to look for them. It was eventually uncovered by [[Dusk]] who was impressed she had survived so long.
