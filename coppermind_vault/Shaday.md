**Shaday** is a Highlord and member of the [[House Kholin|Kholin]] army in [[Alethkar]] on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa3|83}}

==Attributes and Abilities==
As a Highlord, he is presumably a [[lighteyes]] of the third [[dahn]]. He is a high-ranking military leader, as he discusses strategy with King [[Elhokar]], [[Azure]], [[Adolin]], and other Highlords.{{book ref|sa3|83}}

==History==
He was part of a strike team that attempted to retake the [[Oathgate]] during the [[Siege of Kholinar]].{{book ref|sa3|83}} Shaday held out hope that the [[Voidbringer]]s and members of the corrupted [[Kholinar#Palace_Guard|Palace Guard]] would turn on each other, but Elhokar dismissed the possibility.{{book ref|sa3|83}} Although a few individuals made it to the Oathgate,{{book ref|sa3|85}} the Kholin army was overrun by the [[Singers]].{{book ref|sa3|84}} Shaday's fate is unknown, but he was likely killed or enslaved along with the other defenders of Kholinar.
