> [[Kelsier]] stepped up to the island's edge, the ribbon of [[Preservation]]'s essence curling past him and leading onto the island, like a mythical string showing the way home from the grand maze of Ishathon.
> — Kelsier's thoughts in the [[Cognitive Realm]]

**Ishathon** is a mythical location spoken of in stories on [[Scadrial]]. The myth is [[Scadrial]]-specific.

There was said to be a great maze at Ishathon, and in stories a string was used to find the way out.

When the [[Cognitive shadow]] of [[Kelsier]] followed a thread of [[Preservation]]'s essence to meet [[Khriss]] and [[Nazh]] on the island in the [[Cognitive Realm]] that corresponds to [[Lake Luthadel]], he compared following Preservation to being guided by the mythical string from the maze of Ishathon.

## Trivia
Although this myth is obviously based on myths on Earth, it developed independently on [[Scadrial]]. Brandon deliberately put it in the novel to demonstrate how often similar stories can spawn in different isolated cultures.
