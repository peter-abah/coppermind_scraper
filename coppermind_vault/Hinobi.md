**Lady Hinobi** is a fictional character featured in the [[hion viewer]] program *[[Seasons of Regret]]*, which is a historical drama. Hinobi is typically displayed using magenta [[hion]] lines. She is in love with Sir [[Ashinata]], a ronin whom she is forbidden to be with.

After her first kiss with Ashinata, the warrior announces that he has to leave forever, due to a threat set in motion by his dead, evil brother. Ashinata returns to her in a surprise finale episode.
