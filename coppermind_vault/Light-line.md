A **Light-line** is a technology device that takes the form of a bracelet.{{book ref|skyward|prologue}} It can project weightless glowing ropes that can be attached to two different objects. While being created, the lines must be in contact with the device, but multiple lines can then exist alone until the power given to the lines have run out.{{book ref|skyward|prologue}} This device is especially useful when exploring cave systems or descending heights, as demonstrated by [[Spensa]] and her father's usage.{{book ref|skyward|prologue}} It is quite durable, needing only to be charged once a while to function over ten years.{{book ref|sky1|3}}

It resembles a mini version of the [[Light-lance]] used on [[DDF]] [[starfighter]]s.{{book ref|sky1|14}} They are used by pilots and can be requisitioned from the quartermaster.{{book ref|sky1|14}} Spensa's light-line is unknown to the DDF, and although Cobb is aware of it, he has not reported it.{{book ref|sky1|14}}

Technology such as light-lines{{book ref|sky1|3}} survived the crash of the Defiant onto Detritus, as well as subsequent destruction of the fleets electronic archive, and have been passed down through the generations.{{book ref|sky1|3}} Before the crash, light-lines were used by the engineers while they worked on ship machinery in zero gravity.{{book ref|sky1|14}}
