**Bendal** was a [[Steel Inquisitor]] near the end of the [[Final Empire]]. 

He swore that [[Tevidian]] had fathered a half-breed child, and hunted [[Vin]]'s half-brother, [[Reen]], to get him to admit it. Reen died promising Vin had starved to death long ago, but Bendal remained convinced one was out there.{{book ref|mb1|37}} 

After Vin Soothed Prelan [[Laird]] for [[Camon]] and [[Camon's crew|his crew]], Bendal hunted her, to eventually bring the evidence against Tevidian. However, Vin escaped the Canton of Finance Headquarters because [[Kelsier]] threw Bendal off Vin's trail.{{book ref|mb1|2}}{{book ref|mb1|3}}

Bendal was the lead Inquisitor who stopped Kelsier and Vin during their infiltration of [[Kredik Shaw]].{{book ref|mb1|14}}{{book ref|mb1|33}}

Bendal eventually fought Kelsier at [[Square of the Survivor|fountain square]] and was decapitated.{{book ref|mb1|34}}
