**Douglas** is a talking [[wikipedia:Tyrannosaurus|T-Rex]] from [[Nalhalla]].{{book ref|a1|9}}

## Appearance and Personality

*Douglas* is an orange tyrannosaurus rex, around six foot in height. They wear a vest over a pair of trousers.{{book ref|a1|9}}

## History

*Douglas*, alongside several other dinosaurs from the [[Free Kingdoms]], went to the [[Hushlands]] to write a paper about life there, but was captured by [[Librarian]]s and placed in cages. They were captured rather easily when their plan of entry was washing up on a popular beach pretending to be dead fish. They and their companions were to be killed and have the bones enlarged, before being placed inside rock so human archaeologists could dig them up, further perpetuating the myth of dinosaurs being large and extinct.{{book ref|a1|9}}

[[Alcatraz]] freed *Douglas* and their friends to make a distraction, however Alcatraz misunderstood that a dinosaurs idea of a distraction was to sit around reading after moving six books out of their place in the library.{{book ref|a1|16}}

Sometime later, *Douglas* was once again captured in the Hushlands with their fellow dinosaurs: [[Charles]], [[Margaret]] and [[Mary]]. They had gone to the Hushlands after hearing Alcatraz's speech to see and study the Hushlanders reaction to extreme stress. *Douglas* bit their lip while in captivity, which they blame on their rather large teeth. This scared many Librarians who didn't want to approach them, believing *Douglas* to be some mysterious creature of great danger. Alcatraz convinced Charles and the other dinosaurs to pretend to be making a big fuss while Alcatraz 'dealt' with them, to make it appear convincing he was resolving the monster problem. He then got *Douglas* to bite his lip again so there would be a lot of blood to show the Librarians.{{book ref|a5|15}}
