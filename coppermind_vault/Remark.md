**Remark** is a cadet in either [[Inkwell Flight]] or [[Firestorm Flight]] who is offered a [[scout]] position prior to graduation.{{book ref|sky1|42}} As a scout he would fly a [[Val-class]] ship.{{book ref|Skyward|41}} It is unclear whether Remark is a callsign or this cadet's name.

He is boisterous and has a bowl cut.{{book ref|sky1|42}} He is excited to join the scouts after graduating from [[Flight School]] and talks with the other cadets about his offer during dinner in the mess hall.{{book ref|sky1|41}}

The current status of Remark is unknown, however, if he did graduate he may have been one of the twelve scouts combined into a single Flight during the [[Second Battle of Alta]]. As three quarters of this Flight were killed during the fight, he may now be dead.{{book ref|sky1|48}}
