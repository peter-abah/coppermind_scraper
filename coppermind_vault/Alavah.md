**Alavah** is a [[singer]] on [[Roshar]].

During the [[True Desolation]], they were part of [[Venli]]'s group that wished to escape the [[Fused]]. After being transferred from [[Kholinar]] to [[Urithiru]], they were assigned to secretly pack supplies with [[Ron]] in case the group needed to quickly escape the tower.
