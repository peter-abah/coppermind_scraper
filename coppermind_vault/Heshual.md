**Heshual** is a [[Regal]] on [[Roshar]] during the [[True Desolation]]. He was formerly known as **Govi** before bonding a [[Voidspren]]. He is the leader of a group of four guards assigned to the [[Kholinar Palace]] gemstone vault.

==Appearance and Personality==
While speaking to [[El]], Heshual seems to struggle to find the correct [[rhythm]]. El finds him timid and is slightly perturbed that he holds a prestigious position as a guard. However, El attributes Heshual's behavior to the general lack of passion among the newly freed singers.

==Attributes and Abilities==
Heshual wears [[envoyform]] and seems to have an understanding of the interactions between [[Polestones]] and [[Lights]], including [[Anti-Light]].

==History==
Around , Heshual encountered [[El]], who wanted to access the Kholinar palace vault. He deferred to the [[Fused]]'s request and accompanied El inside the vault. However, he was quickly shocked and horrified as El took up the gemstone that held the remainder of [[Jezrien]]'s soul and destroyed it using [[Anti-Stormlight]]. Heshual panicked and ran to get the other guards.

El thought that the [[Nine]] must not have warned Heshual of his rebirth, and hoped they would not punish him too harshly due to his ignorance. However, Heshual likely escaped any punishment since [[Odium]] immediately appeared and elevated El above the Nine as the commander of his armies.
