**HaiKo** is a city in [[JinDo]] on [[Sel]]. It is located in the northeast corner of JinDo, situated on a river and at the foot of a mountain range. It is on the eastern border with [[Fjorden]] and near the northern border with [[Duladel]].{{map ref|Sycla}} It is the only city marked on known maps of JinDo.

The city has a namesake dish cooked using lots of grease, as is common for JinDo food.{{book ref|Elantris|5}} [[Kiin]] learned how to make the dish from a traveling merchant.{{book ref|Elantris|5}} Kiin's version contains shrimp, and [[Lukel]] and [[Sarene]] find it delicious.{{book ref|Elantris|5}}

## Trivia
* In early editions of *[[Elantris (book)|Elantris]]*, a map of Arelon was published depicting HaiKo in the south of JinDo.<ref>[[:File:Arelon.jpg|Map of Arelon (original)]]</ref> However, new maps were added in the tenth anniversary edition that superseded the original maps.
