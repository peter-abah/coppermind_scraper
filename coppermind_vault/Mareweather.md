**Mareweather** is a post-[[Catacendre|Catacendric]] holiday celebrated in the [[Elendel Basin]] and the north of [[Scadrial]].{{book ref|mb5|17}}

The holiday is celebrated at some point in the summer and involves some sort of celebratory dinner.{{book ref|mb5|17}}
