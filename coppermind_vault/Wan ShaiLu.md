**Wan ShaiLu**, known as **Shai**, is a [[MaiPon]] woman from [[Sel]]. She has a reputation throughout the [[Rose Empire]] as being one of the best [[Forger]]s alive.{{tes ref|prologue}} She was imprisoned in her homeland, but escapes after restoring [[Ashravan]]'s health.{{tes ref|98}} She becomes a [[worldhopper]] and leaves Sel, and is unable to return.{{book ref|mb7|47}}

## Appearance and Personality
{{image|TES Shai.jpg|side=left|width=200px}}
{{for|/Gallery|more images}}

Shai is small of stature like the rest of the MaiPon people. She has black hair that lays straight and long.{{tes ref|2}}{{book ref|mb7|20}} She wears spectacles because of her bad eyesight.{{tes ref|2}} She is implied to have epicanthic folds, and has prominent cheekbones.{{book ref|mb7|20}} Her arms are layered with shallow cuts from the [[Bloodsealing|Bloodsealer]] [[Weedfingers]], who needs her blood to keep her imprisoned.{{tes ref|5}} Her voice is on the deeper end for a woman, and she uses the expression "Nights!" as an expletive.{{book ref|mb7|20}}

She loves the thrill, the accomplishment and the wonder of Forgery. However, at times, she is tempted by a simple life where she doesn't have to hide or lie.{{tes ref|76}} She constantly quotes her **Uncle Won** and **Aunt Sol** although they don't really exist. They are figments of her imagination created for one of her Essence Marks.{{tes ref|76}} She believes her Forgeries are works of art and she gets angry when contradicted on that.{{tes ref|3}}

She worships the [[Unknown God]].{{tes ref|2}} She holds a deep hatred for [[Autonomy]], and by extension [[Bavadin]], due to her ideals and actions. This hatred is enough that it can sometimes cloud her judgement.{{book ref|mb7|20}}

### Elantrian
As an Elantrian, she has a very high opinion of herself, seeing herself as a divinity worthy of praise and offerings. In this form, she has the glowing skin of an Elantrian and wears her hair up in a bun.{{book ref|mb7|53}}

## Attributes and Abilities
Shai is a very talented artist,{{tes ref|prologue}} but generally uses her ability to make forgeries of paintings. She reads people very well and is a master manipulator.{{tes ref|70}} She has good control of her emotions. She is very knowledgeable in art history and has a very good understanding of [[Realmatic Theory]].{{tes ref|12}}

Shai is one of the best [[Forgery|Forgers]] ever to live, especially in [[Forgery#Soul Forging|Soul Forgery]]. [[Forgery#Resealing|Flesh Forgery]] is one of the few branches of Forgery she hasn't studied in depth because getting a Flesh Forgery wrong usually means the subject dying.{{tes ref|2}}

### Essence Marks
During her imprisonment in the [[Imperial Seat]], Shai had five [[Essence Mark]]s, which took her over eight years to create.{{tes ref|76}} As of her time on Scadrial, she has created at least one more.{{book ref|mb7|53}}

In the decades to centuries between her escape from the Rose Empire, and her work against the Set alongside Marasi, she gained a lot of skill in her use of Essence Marks. She is now able to create Essence Marks that last far longer than the mere day{{tes ref|30}}{{tes ref|98}} that her old ones last for, enough so that she believes they could be potentially permanent, even without hemerine reapplication.{{book ref|mb7|53}}{{book ref|mb7|epilogue|1}}{{wob ref|16022}} Therefore bypassing, through unknown means, the limitations posed by the shifting Identity of sentient beings.{{tes ref|30}} These are not necessarily permanent, and might wear off some day.{{wob ref|16022}}

### Universal Stamps
Shai carries two soulstamps that are designed to work on any object for a limited time. One creates a doorway, and the other repairs broken objects.{{book ref|mb7|42}} This is still limited by the feasibility of a Forgery, to a degree. If a Forgery with a Universal Stamp is completely infeasible, the stamp would not properly take.

## History
{{image|The_Forger’s_Masterpiece_by_AutomaticMoon.jpg|Shai attempts to recreate the soul of the Emperor Ashravan|side=right|height=350px}}

Shai first started inquiring about being a Forger when she was eight years old.{{tes ref|76}} As an apprentice, she received training in a variety of mundane forgeries such as art and writing;{{tes ref|17}} she also learned information useful in Forgery, such as how to identify different types of stone.{{tes ref|2}} Her first trainer in Forgery was [[Tao]], who taught her the value of respecting people even as you manipulated their perceptions.{{tes ref|42}} She noted that apprentice Forgers spend much of their time learning about human nature before they are even allowed to touch soulstone.{{tes ref|30}} She also had other mentors, and she learned the finer mechanics of creating and applying soulstamps.{{tes ref|12}} Her training was performed "half in secret", as is typical in MaiPon.{{tes ref|17}}

Over time, she created five different [[Essence Mark]]s. Each of these marks took years to create and were essentially irreplaceable.{{tes ref|2}}{{tes ref|98}} For example, as part of the creation of one of the Essence Marks, Shai spent a year training with the insular [[Teullu]] people, a training so intense that she nearly died on many occasions.{{tes ref|98}}

### Infiltration of the Rose Palace
[[Han ShuXen]], an old painter who had tutored Shai as a child, asked her to destroy his masterpiece *[[Lily of the Spring Pond]]*. ShuXen hated the Rose Empire and didn't want his painting, which he had painted for a woman he once loved, in their hands. He helped Shai create a counterfeit.{{tes ref|76}}

Shai managed to destroy the original painting in the Arbiter [[Frava]]'s office near the [[Imperial Gallery]], replacing it with a fake and planting another lower-quality counterfeit in her room in order to hide the theft.{{tes ref|30}} With the help of the [[Imperial Fool]], she also broke into the [[Rose Palace]]'s Imperial Gallery by impersonating a noblewoman to steal the [[Moon Scepter]]. However, the Fool betrayed her. He stole Shai's copy of the Moon Scepter, swapped it for the original, then escaped after giving Shai up to the palace guards. Shai was captured and sentenced to public execution for theft.{{tes ref|2}}

{{image|TES - Shai and Gaotona by Johanna Rupprecht.jpg|[[Gaotona]] and Shai|side=left|height=300px}}

### The Emperor's Soul
A day before her execution, she was asked by the council of five arbiters to Forge a soul in exchange for her freedom. Shai didn't think it was possible to create a soulstamp for an entire person's soul in the hundred days they gave her, but she accepted their offer in the hope that it would give her time to escape. She was taken before the Emperor [[Ashravan]] and told that he took a crossbow bolt in the head from assassins sent by the [[Glory Faction]] and despite surgeons [[resealing]] the wound, he is in a vegetative state. She was told to replace the emperor's soul with a [[Forgery]].{{tes ref|2}}

She was given a room barely better than a cell which, over time, she transformed into the finest room in the whole palace with Forgery.{{tes ref|76}} She received her Forgery tools except for her Essence Marks. [[Gaotona]] volunteered to be her test subject for the soulstamps she created for Emperor Ashravan.{{tes ref|30}} The arbiters hired a [[Bloodsealer]] to make sure of her cooperation.{{tes ref|3}} As they work edtogether, Gaotona and Shai grew fond of each other and eventually became friends. Shai started to collect information on Ashravan's personality and memory to use for the soulstamp.{{tes ref|5}}

Shai concluded that the other arbiters were planning to kill her before the soulstamp was done and have another Forger finish her work. She started lacing her notes with subtext about the arduousness of the task to deter the other Forger, [[Nyen]].{{tes ref|58}} All the arbiters except Gaotona proposed that she create a backdoor to the emperor's soul that would allow him to be controlled by the arbiters. Shai played along but eventually refrained from doing it, even for herself.{{tes ref|17}}

As her hundred days passed, Shai was constantly tempted to escape but was driven by the challenge of the task.{{tes ref|76}} On day ninety-seven, just before she finished the soulstamp, she enacted her plan to get past the Bloodsealer. The next day, Captain [[Zu (Sel)|Zu]] tried to kill her, a move she had expected from some of the guards and managed to evade by Forging traps into her room. She escaped and made her way to Ashravan's chambers, desperate to see if she had managed to Forge a soul from nothing. When it succeeded, Gaotona gave her back her Essence Marks and she used them to escape the palace.{{tes ref|98}} It later turned out that she didn't reForge Ashravan's soul exactly the way it was and made small changes, nudging him on the path to being a better ruler.{{tes ref|epilogue}}

After she escaped, Shai planned to track down the Imperial Fool who betrayed her.{{tes ref|epilogue}} She eventually tracked him down, and they kept an eye on each other for a long time.{{book ref|mb7|20}}{{wob ref|9631}}

### Resistance against Trell
{{image|Moonlight by Caio Santos.png|side=right|width=300px|Turning into an Elantrian}}

At some point after her release from the Rose Empire, she became a member of the Ghostbloods and worked on [[Scadrial]] in order to resist the oncoming threat of [[Trell (Trellism)|Trell]], working under the codename of Moonlight.{{book ref|mb7|36}}

Six years after the first interaction between the Elendel Basin and the Southern Scadrians, she found [[Marasi Colms]] in a series of underground caverns, crashing a [[Set]] smuggling operation. She secretly gave her a letter, informing her that the Ghostbloods were watching her and were impressed by her accomplishments.{{book ref|mb7|15}}  

Several days later, she met with Marasi, acting as her driver for a sting operation on the Set smuggling operation in [[Bilming]]. Introducing herself as a member of the Ghostbloods using the code name Moonlight, she explained to Marasi the nature of Trell and [[Autonomy]].{{book ref|mb7|20}}

Shai assisted Marasi, [[Wax]], and [[Wayne]] in their investigation and pursuit of the Set until being discovered by [[Telsin]], then an avatar of Autonomy.{{book ref|mb7|29}}{{book ref|mb7|34}} When the group separated, Shai was tricked by Marasi into bringing her back to the Ghostblood hideout in Bilming, which Marasi believed may have been her intention regardless.{{book ref|mb7|36}} Shai introduced Marasi to the small crew of Scadrian Ghostbloods—including to Kelsier, via Seon—who believed that Shai had taken on Marasi as her apprentice.{{book ref|mb7|39}}{{book ref|mb7|40}} 

With some research, Shai, the Ghostbloods, and Marasi discovered an entrance to the Set's underground [[Wayfarer|caverns]]. She took TwinSoul with them to infiltrate the complex, and—at Kelsier's request—three large jars of purified Dor, which Shai looked upon with awe.{{book ref|mb7|40}}

She was forced to use a Stamp to change her past and turn herself into an Elantrian, also changing her personality and giving her the abilities of an Elantrian. She used [[AonDor]] in order to create an illusion of Marasi, letting her escape.{{book ref|mb7|53}} Following this, she vanished and the Ghostbloods sent out a mission to look for her.{{book ref|mb7|epilogue|1}}

## Relationships

### Bavadin
She holds a deep hatred for Bavadin, fundamentally disagreeing with her ideals and actions.{{book ref|mb7|20}} They have met before at an unknown point.{{book ref|mb7|29}} 

### Kelsier
Kelsier is her mentor, and she holds a deep respect for him.{{expand}} 

### Marasi
She has a great interest in Marasi, and was the one who brought her into the Ghostblood's fold.{{book ref|mb7|20}} 

### Prasanva
She works with Prasanva in Bilming.{{book ref|mb7|39}} She is willing to try to help him get back to his homeworld, but he rejects her offer, stating that it is too dangerous.{{book ref|mb7|42}}{{expand}}

### Gaotona
Gaotona held a strong dislike for Shai, seeing her forgery as an abomination, and felt that she was wasting her immense talent.{{tes ref|30}} But over time, she managed to change his view on art, and make him grow a fondness for her, making him wish to save her from execution, even if he didn't feel as if he was on her side.{{expand}}

### Hoid
She despises Hoid for deceiving her and running away with the Moon Scepter, replacing it with her forgery.{{tes ref|2}} After escaping from the Rose Empire, she began to hunt after him to enact her revenge, an act that she eventually succeeded at.{{book ref|mb7|20}}{{wob ref|9631}} Their current relationship is unclear, but she does wonder to herself if she was keeping watch over him, or he over her, but she knows that they are in reality just keeping watch over the same third parties.{{book ref|mb7|20}}

### Kaise
She works with Kaise in Bilming.{{book ref|mb7|39}}{{expand}}
