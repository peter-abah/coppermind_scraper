The **Terringul 27** is a type of handgun on [[Scadrial]]. [[Waxillium Ladrian]] wears one as a backup strapped to the inside of his calf. It is not a particularly high caliber gun, but it has a long barrel, which makes it more accurate. It holds at least six rounds.

Wax used hollow-point bullets in his Terringul 27 because they would lodge into things, giving him good anchors for [[Steel#Allomantic Use|Steelpushing]] and reducing the chance of the bullets going through his targets and injuring bystanders.

When attacking the [[Vanishers]] hideout and attempting to rescue [[Marasi]], Wax used his Terringul 27 after his primary weapons ran out of ammunition. He used the gun's hollow point bullets to create anchors, and he also shot [[Tarson]] and several other Vanishers. When he ran out of bullets, he discarded the gun and Pushed off of it during his escape.
