**Sisisru** is a female [[Aviar]] being raised by [[Sixth of the Dusk (character)|Sixth of the Dusk]] on [[Patji (island)|Patji]], an island in the [[Pantheon]] on [[First of the Sun]].

She lives in a nesting box with other Aviar in one of Dusk's [[safecamp]]s. She is not one of Dusk's primary Aviar companions, and her species and Aviar talent are unknown.
