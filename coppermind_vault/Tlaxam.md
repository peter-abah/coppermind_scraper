**Tlaxam** is a [[listener]] and [[keeper of songs]]. After the [[War of Reckoning]], he bonded a [[Reacher]] and joined the [[Order of Willshapers]].{{rpg ref|slwg|160}} After the [[Listener#The Five|Five]] assassinated the [[Alethi]] king [[Gavilar Kholin]], and ordered all the listeners to relocate to [[Narak]], Tlaxam and a number of other listeners chose to stay behind. 

These listeners fled into the western chasms, wary of the Alethi but skeptical of the need for war. Tlaxam and his people traveled as nomads, seeking shelter in whatever safe hollows they could find in the chasms. After Tlaxam became a Willshaper, he began using his powers to protect his people during highstorms by shaping stone across their tunnels. It is unknown if he and his people joined the listener nation founded after the [[Second Battle of Narak]].{{rpg ref|slwg|160}}
