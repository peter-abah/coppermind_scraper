**Larn** is a soldier in [[Amaram]]'s army in [[Alethkar]] on [[Roshar]].

Larn fights at [[Kaladin]]'s side when Kaladin battles to protect [[Cenn]]. Kaladin launches himself out of formation to catch up to Cenn, and Larn called out in surprise. He likely died when [[Helaran Davar]] killed Kaladin's squad.
