> Claiming it will revolutionize security and transportation, Reshelle Tekiel announced her House's new vault-style train car, intended for the transportation and protection of valuable goods via railway
> — *[[The Elendel Daily]]* on the [[Breaknaught]]

**Reshelle Tekiel** is a member of [[House Tekiel]] on post-[[Catacendre|Catacendric]] [[Scadrial]].

After House Tekiel suffered severe losses to the [[Vanishers]], they developed a vault-like train car known as the [[Breaknaught]], which Reshelle was responsible for announcing to the public.
