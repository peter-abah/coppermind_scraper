**Lesan Calour** is a publisher in [[Elendel]] on [[Scadrial]].

They own a publishing company known as Lesan Calour & Daughters & Sons, also referred to as [[Calour Publications]]. The company, which is headquartered in the 6th Octant of Elendel, publishes novels and *[[The House Record]]* broadsheet.
