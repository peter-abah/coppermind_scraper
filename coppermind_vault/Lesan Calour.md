**Lesan Calour** is a publisher in [[Elendel]] on [[Scadrial]].{{file ref|House_Record_broadsheet.jpg|*The House Record* Broadsheet}}

They own a publishing company known as Lesan Calour & Daughters & Sons, also referred to as [[Calour Publications]]. The company, which is headquartered in the 6th Octant of Elendel, publishes novels and *[[The House Record]]* broadsheet.{{file ref|House_Record_broadsheet.jpg|*The House Record* Broadsheet}}
