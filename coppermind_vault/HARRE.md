**HARRE** is an acronym for Heuristic Algorithm and Reasoning Response Engine and the name of the [[AI]] that co-pilots a [[mechanized infantry unit]] with Captain [[Karith Marvudi]]. They were assigned to liaise with the [[Panesthian]]s on [[Milacria]] and help with their defense against a [[Self-Replicating Machine Infestation]] outbreak near [[Panesthian city|one of their cities]].
