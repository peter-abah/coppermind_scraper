> — [[Colot]]

**Grubs** is an [[Alethi]] sergeant at arms on [[Roshar]]. [[Colot]] instructed [[Kaminah]] to use Grubs in a threat to get [[Adolin Kholin]] to sleep during the [[Battle of Azimir]]. Colot claimed that Grubs, who presumably was present at the battle, had a rope he chiefly used to tie officers in place.
