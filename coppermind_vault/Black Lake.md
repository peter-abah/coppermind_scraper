The **Black Lake** is a large lake near the border of the [[Eastern Dominance]] and [[Central Dominance]] of [[Scadrial]] prior to the [[Catacendre]]. It is located southeast of [[Luthadel]] and northeast of the [[Ashmount]] Faleast. [[Vin]] reads about a  black lake in [[Alendi's journal]], this seems to be referring to something other than the modern Black Lake.
