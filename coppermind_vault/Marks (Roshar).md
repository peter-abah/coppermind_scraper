**Marks** is a lighteyed [[Alethi]] on [[Roshar]] and a member of the [[Cobalt Guard]].

He, along with [[Tibon]], guarded [[Adolin]] during his date with [[Janala]], and waited with [[Falksi]] outside the tent of the leatherworkers [[Yis]] and [[Avaran]].
