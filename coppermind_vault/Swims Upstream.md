Kitsen Unity Ship ***Swims Against the Current in a Stream Reflecting the Sun***, often referred to as the ***Swims Upstream***, is a [[Superiority]] [[starfighter]], used by a crew of [[kitsen]] training to fight [[delver]]s. It is captained by [[Hesho]] until his death, after which [[Kauri]] assumes command. The starfighter is 50% larger than the average Superiority starfighter due to the large crew of kitsen it accommodates, and is built like a battleship.

==Functions==
The ship has multiple gun emplacements, a captain's chair, an instruments department, and multiple other departments needed to run a battleship.
