**The Coven** are a group of six [[Epic]]s ruling the south of the [[Fractured States]]. They have a loose alliance, and would sometimes help another member kill a rival Epic, which according to [[David]] was Epic for being "best-buds."

## Known Members

;[[Creer boys|Hanjah]]: Twin of the Mad Pen. Hanjah has the powers of air pressure control, pain manipulation, and precognition.
;[[Creer boys|Mad Pen]]: Twin of Hanjah. Mad Pen also has the powers of air pressure control, pain manipulation, and precognition.
;[[Larcener]]: Ruler of [[Ildithia]]. Larcener has the power of assuming, meaning he can steal any Epic's powers and gain those powers as well.
