Lord **Cimines** is a scholar in post-[[Catacendre]] [[Scadrial]].

According to [[Marasi]], he once conducted a sociological study that found that people in crowds give constables a wide berth, mimicking the behavior of groups of animals in the vicinity of a predator. During a trip to [[New Seran]], [[Wax]] realizes that his height, clothing, and weapons make him very conspicuous even though he is not identifiable as a constable, and people indeed seem to get out of his way.
