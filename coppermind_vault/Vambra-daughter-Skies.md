**Vambra-daughter-Skies** is a [[Truthwatcher]] [[Honorbearer]] in [[Shinovar]] on [[Roshar]].

## Appearance and Personality
Vambra has long golden hair. She is young to be an Honorbearer, at not much older than [[Szeth]].

## History
Vambra became the Truthwatcher Honorbearer before Szeth undertook his quest to become one himself. She is no longer the Honorbearer -- and is, presumably, dead -- by the time Szeth [[Cleansing of Shinovar|cleanses Shinovar]] in 1175.
