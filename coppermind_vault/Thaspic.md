**Thaspic** is a [[Purelaker]] on [[Roshar]].

Thaspic is a dark-skinned, scrawny man who spoke with [[Ishikk]] while doing his laundry. Thaspic lost a shirt while washing, but doesn't seem troubled by this, and points Ishikk to [[Maib]]'s place, where [[Seventeenth Shard|Ishikk's foreigners]] are at.
