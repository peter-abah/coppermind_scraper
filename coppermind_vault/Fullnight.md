**Fullnight** is a horse on [[Roshar]]. He is a black gelding ridden by a young [[Dalinar Kholin]] during a battle against [[Yezriar]] in .

Dalinar asked for Fullnight to be brought to him after he was hit by an arrow fired by [[Teleb]]. Dalinar rode Fullnight up to the ridge where the archer was firing from, while dodging more arrows. After the battle was over, Dalinar rode into town on Fullnight.
