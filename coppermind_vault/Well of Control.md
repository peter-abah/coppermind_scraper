The **Well of Control** is a [[perpendicularity]] associated with [[Odium]], and later [[Retribution]].

## Location and Appearance

> We must travel to the Well of Control, within the shroud of the fragments of the dead moon. There, we will find our destiny. We cannot stop him from destroying us. It is time.
> — [[Sleepless]]

The Well of Control is located at the [[Shattered Plains]], hidden beneath a rocky cavern with no exits.

The Well appears as a pool, roughly thirty yards wide, full of thick liquid. As Odium's perpendicularity, it is a shimmering golden color, while as Retribution's it is a brilliant blue-black. It pulses with a [[rhythm]] that is difficult to hear, as it is located within the fragmentary remnants of Roshar's fourth moon, which can block even a Shard's sight. Being [[Connection|Connected]] to both [[Honor]] and [[Odium]] may help a person sense its rhythm.

## Purpose
Those who use the Well of Control are able to gain the powers of Odium. This allows the user to [[Connection|Connect]] to the [[singer]]s and [[spren]] of [[Roshar]], Connect to certain lands of Roshar, or create their own version of [[Fused]].

## History and Mythology
During the [[False Desolation]], [[Ba-Ado-Mishram]] found the Well of Control and used its powers to Connect to the singers on Roshar. [[Ishar]] would later find and take up some of the powers of Odium from the Well.

After Odium and Honor combined to make [[Retribution]], the Well of Control drained, before refilling with Retribution's [[Investiture]].
