The **Torrington 11940** is a type of pistol used by [[Ulric Stromfin]]. It is a military grade weapon with force ejectors that can punch through an inch of steel. It can use rounds that are anti-nanite, and comes standard with a suppressor. Ulric uses a modified version without the suppressor, and generates a lot of smoke to appear more imposing.
