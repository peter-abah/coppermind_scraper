**Copper** is the internal mental pulling metal. An Allomancer burning copper is able to hide Allomantic pulses emanating from those around them, and is immune to emotional Allomancy. Feruchemists can use copper to store memories, and when used as a Hemalurgic spike, copper can steal mental fortitude, memory, or intelligence.

The paired alloy of copper used in the Metallic Arts is [[bronze]]. It is also a component in [[duralumin]], which is the alloy of [[aluminum]].

## Allomantic Use
A copper [[Misting]] is known as a **Coppercloud** or **Smoker**. When an Allomancer is burning copper, the pulses from nearby Allomancers cannot be detected by most [[Bronze#Allomantic Use|Seekers]].{{book ref|mb4|part=ars}} Allomancers with a [[Hemalurgy|Hemalurgic]] spike that enhances their natural ability with bronze, genetically more powerful Allomancers, such as those created with [[lerasium]], and bronze [[savant]]s are all capable of "piercing" a coppercloud.{{epigraph ref|mb3|73}}{{wob ref|4303}} [[Nicrosil#Allomantic Use|Nicrosil]] can also allow a Mistborn or a Seeker to pierce a coppercloud for a brief moment; [[duralumin#Allomantic Use|duralumin]] would also work for a Mistborn.{{wob ref|7760}} Multiple Smokers burning copper in the same area will make the resulting coppercloud harder to pierce.{{wob ref|7762}} As few Allomancers have the ability to pierce a coppercloud, Smokers are of central importance when Allomancers are trying to hide. If a Seeker is inside of a coppercloud, they will be unable to receive any pulses, even from those inside of the coppercloud.{{book ref|mb1|9}}{{wob ref|5496}} Copper is the slowest burning metal of the basic eight Allomantic metals.{{wob ref|7820}}

Copperclouds can also block other uses of Investiture. On Scadrial, this is most commonly seen in its ability to protect the user from emotional Allomancy;{{book ref|mb1|14}} protecting others in the cloud's boundaries is very difficult but not impossible.{{wob ref|6884}} Copperclouds also block other methods of detecting Investiture, such as an [[Awakener]]'s [[Heightening|life sense]] or a [[voidspren#secretspren|secretspren's]] ability to sense the use of Investiture.{{wob ref|6817}}{{wob ref|8897}} Additionally, a coppercloud can interfere with more passive uses of Investiture; for example, it can prevent a [[singer]] from attuning a Rhythm and affects how spren act.{{wob ref|1600}}{{wob ref|12639}}

### Savantism
Savanthood strengthens the coppercloud, making it harder to pierce, and is less debilitating for Smokers compared to other metals.{{wob ref|14917}}

## Feruchemical Use
A copper [[Ferring]] is known as an **Archivist**. Copper is used to store memories.{{book ref|mb4|part=ars}} This ability drives the core purpose of the [[Keeper]]s, a group of Terrismen dedicated to preserving knowledge. Since the memories in a coppermind do not degrade with time{{book ref|mb2|4}} (although they are vulnerable to [[Ruin]]'s manipulation while being transferred{{wob ref|9695}}), it is perfect for the collection of enormous quantities of facts. Keepers often create "index" copperminds to find individual pieces of information much faster.{{book ref|mb2|4}}

When an Archivist stores a memory in a coppermind, it immediately disappears from their own memory (although it can be stored at partial strength to allow them to keep a vague recollection, at the cost of the stored memory being weaker).{{book ref|mb2|12}}{{wob ref|1114}} Memories stored in a coppermind are filed individually and are not seen as one whole reserve to the Feruchemist.{{wob ref|13242}}

The longer a memory sits outside of a coppermind, the more it degrades, though this is due to the brain's own imperfections rather than a result of the magic.{{book ref|mb2|15}}{{wob ref|1699}} As such, Keepers undergo extensive training in memory retention to keep them as pristine as possible.{{wob ref|7794}}

Copperminds cannot store muscle memory.{{wob ref|11670}}

### Compounding
{{theory}}
Copper is unique among Feruchemical metals in that the individual bits of Feruchemical charge (the memories) are distinct from one another, as opposed to a metal like [[steel]] in which no bit of stored speed is discernible from another. Thus the possible effect of compounding copper is unclear. [[Rashek]] was said to have had a flawless memory,{{book ref|mb1|6}} possibly the effect of copper compounding, though this ability could have just been propaganda or a non-magical talent like that of his uncle [[Kwaan]].{{epigraph ref|mb2|42}}

## Hemalurgic Use
When used as a Hemalurgic spike, copper steals mental fortitude, memory, and intelligence.{{book ref|hoa|part=ars}}{{file ref| Hemalurgy table.jpg | Hemalurgy Table}} 

### Kandra Blessing
The [[kandra]] can use a pair of these spikes as a [[Blessing]] and call them the **Blessing of Presence**.{{book ref|mb3|2}} Like all kandra Blessings, the Blessing gives the [[mistwraith]]s sentience, with the added benefit of mental stability.{{epigraph ref|mb3|38}} This capability includes the ability to focus despite physical discomfort or distraction, exceptionally sharp memory, resistance to madness, and the inability to lapse into unconsciousness due to shock.{{book ref|mb3|2}}{{book ref|mb3|24}} This Blessing also counters at least some of the mental weakness that is caused by Hemalurgy; kandra with this Blessing are much less vulnerable to being controlled by Ruin than others.{{wob ref|5597}}

## Fabrial Use
Copper can probably be used in [[Rosharan]] [[fabrial]]s. Its effect is unclear, although it might be able to negate alerter fabrials, which are made using bronze. It has not yet been discovered by Rosharan artifabrians.

[[Reacher]]s use copper lines in their ships to communicate between one another.{{book ref|sa3|99}}
