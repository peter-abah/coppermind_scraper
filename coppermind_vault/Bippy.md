**Bippy** is a [[flamespren]] on [[Roshar]]. It has too-large eyes that glow red, and blazing red "fur" with a distinctive tuft on its head.

In , [[Ardent]] [[Rushu]] performed research on [[spren]] and [[fabrial]]s. At [[Navani]]'s suggestion, they attempted to "domesticate" [[Spren#Lesser_Spren|lesser spren]] by offering them some kind of reward. Rushu's initial experiments focused on flamespren, and they quickly realized that the spren responded to positive human thoughts about them, such as being given names and compliments. They could also be calmed by using tuning forks made to emit a specific [[Rhythm#The_Pure_Tones_and_Shardic_Rhythms|tone]]. Rushu and their team were soon able to coax the spren into willingly entering and exiting fabrials.

When Rushu demonstrated their findings for Navani and the [[Sibling]], they were able to identify Bippy by its tufted head, and used it as an example of the rapid advancement in their research. The spren directly responded to its name and followed Rushu's finger when prompted, seemingly glowing brighter when it was praised. The behavior of Bippy and the other flamespren represented a breakthrough, since lesser spren had previously been considered non-sapient. Domesticated spren could be cultivated and trained instead of simply trapped, significantly expanding the potential for widespread fabrial use. The Sibling felt that domesticating spren like Bippy was unnatural, but Navani argued that there could be a mutual benefit.
