**Au-lam** is a man who lives in a small port village on the eastern edge of [[Roshar]] that contains the lighthouse [[Defiance]]. The village is in an area that used to be part of the kingdom of [[Natanatan]], and Au-lam is ethnically [[Natan]].

He lost his barn when the [[Everstorm]] hit the village. [[Puuli]] helped him clean up and took some of the destroyed boards to use as fuel for the lighthouse.
