**Dragdown** is an [[Epic]] who works with [[Limelight]]. They have unknown powers, but are assumed to be a lesser Epic. Prof assigns them and [[Fabergé]] to spread rumors of an underground resistance against him. Limelight's orders are part of a plan to draw [[Larcener]] out of hiding.
