**Natir** is an [[Alethi]] steward on [[Roshar]].

==Appearance and Personality==
He is tall and has slender hands. He wears a long black coat and a purple cravat.

==History==
Natir hails from [[Dalilak]] in the far north of Alethkar. He is married to a stern-looking woman named [[Alaxia]].

In , he was hired as the head steward of Citylord [[Roshone]] in [[Hearthstone]]. Natir was brought in due to [[Miliv]]'s death.

Natir was present when [[Amaram]] visited Hearthstone seeking new soldiers for his army.
