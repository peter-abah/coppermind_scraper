**Natir** is an [[Alethi]] steward on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa1|37}}

==Appearance and Personality==
He is tall and has slender hands.{{book ref|sa1|37}}{{book ref|sa1|44}} He wears a long black coat and a purple cravat.{{book ref|sa1|37}}

==History==
Natir hails from [[Dalilak]] in the far north of Alethkar.{{book ref|sa1|37}} He is married to a stern-looking woman named [[Alaxia]].{{book ref|sa1|44}}

In {{rosharan date|1168}}, he was hired as the head steward of Citylord [[Roshone]] in [[Hearthstone]].{{book ref|sa1|37}} Natir was brought in due to [[Miliv]]'s death.{{book ref|sa1|37}}

Natir was present when [[Amaram]] visited Hearthstone seeking new soldiers for his army.{{book ref|sa1|44}}
