Lord **Idren Seeris** is a [[noble]] on Scadrial. He is a cousin of [[House Elariel]].{{book ref|mb1|30}}

He wears eyeglasses.{{book ref|mb1|30}}

He regularly attended balls in [[Luthadel]] and danced with [[Valette Renoux]] on several occasions. When Valette approached him at the last ball at [[Keep Venture]], he and the group of people standing with him (including [[Yestal]] and [[Triss]]) snubbed her and walked away. Idren seemed apologetic, but the group quickly reassembled after Valette moved on. Valette realized that Idren and the rest of the nobles were trying to consolidate power while they could, and were only speaking with their close allies.{{book ref|mb1|30}}
