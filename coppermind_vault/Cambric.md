The **cambric** are a member species of the [[Superiority]] and one of five species currently at the head of the government. They do not have a large presence on [[Starsight (station)|Starsight]].{{book ref|sky2|12}}

Given the similarities in their names, they may come from the planet [[Cambri]], the homeworld of the [[taynix]].{{book ref|sky2|27}} If so, that might explain how they developed faster-than-light travel and rose to the top of the Superiority.
