> When Simol was informed of the arrival of the [[Edgedancers]], a concealed consternation and terror, as is common in such cases, fell upon him; although they were not the most demanding of orders, their graceful, limber movements hid a deadliness that was, by this time, quite renowned; also, they were the most articulate and refined of the [[Knights Radiant|Radiants]].
> — From [[Words of Radiance (in-world)|Words of Radiance]], chapter 20, page 12

**Simol** is a historical figure mentioned in *[[Words of Radiance (in-world)|Words of Radiance]]*. He lived on [[Roshar]] at some point prior to the [[Day of Recreance]].

Simol was presumably a person of some importance, and the [[Order of Edgedancers]] visited him or his lands. When he heard that they were coming, he was terrified.
