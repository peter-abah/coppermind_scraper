**Simol** is a historical figure mentioned in *[[Words of Radiance (in-world)|Words of Radiance]]*.{{epigraph ref|sa2|46}} He lived on [[Roshar]] at some point prior to the [[Day of Recreance]].

Simol was presumably a person of some importance, and the [[Order of Edgedancers]] visited him or his lands. When he heard that they were coming, he was terrified.{{epigraph ref|sa2|46}}
