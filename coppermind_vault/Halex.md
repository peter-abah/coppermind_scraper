> If you think credentials will get you in free, then you must be new to the force.
> — Mistress Halex to [[Wax]]

Mistress **Halex** is a [[brass]] [[Misting]] (aka a [[Soother]]) and the proprietor of a [[Soothing parlor]] on Decan street in [[Elendel]] on [[Scadrial]].

She wears a matronly gown and a hat that is likely lined with [[aluminum]]. She is unruffled by the presence of a constable in her establishment.

Mistress Halex opened her Soothing parlor in 341 [[catacendre|PC]]. In 342, [[Chapaou]] fled to the parlor after his disturbing encounter with [[Bleeder]] and paid for a private session. The [[Coinshot]] that also worked for [[Ashweather Carriage and Coach]] knew where he was and directed [[Waxillium Ladrian]] to the parlor. Mistress Halex greeted Wax and, after seeing his credentials, took him back to Chapaou's room. She urged Wax to take Chapaou with him, as he was upsetting the other customers.
