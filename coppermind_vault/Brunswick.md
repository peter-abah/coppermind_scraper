**Brunswick** is the steward for the [[duke]] of [[Diggen's Point]] on [[Lumar]].

## History

Three days after finding out that [[Charlie]] had been captured by the [[Sorceress]], [[Tress]] went to Brunswick with a plea for more information. Because Brunswick liked Tress's pies, he told her that a ransom letter had been received from the sorceress, but that the duke believed this to be a trick and the king had officially declared Charlie as dead.
