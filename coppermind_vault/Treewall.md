**Treewall** is a settlement in [[Weswara]].

==History==
After the defeat of [[Ulric Stromfin]] in [[Maelport]], [[Runian]] and [[Sefawynn]] traveled to Treewall to tell the tale of the [[Ealstan|new earl]]'s victory. The townspeople gave Runian and Sefawynn a private chamber to use during their stay. Here, the pair set up their bedrolls, and put solar panels on the roof to power [[Ryan Chu]]'s laptop, which they had recovered.

Runian and Sefawynn told their story to the townspeople through a puppet show, with Runian using his augments to create sound effects. [[Logna]], although she was unseen, also contributed by flaring fire when she was being discussed in the story. Over the course of the tale, the initially hostile townspeople began to engage and they grew excited. Logna believed that the townspeople of Treewall would allow Sefawynn to restore their [[runestone]], and maybe even send some of their children to Maelport for training to become [[skop]]s.

Logna snuck into Runian and Sefawynn's private chamber after they had finished telling their story. She formed herself into the body of a young man, and logged onto the laptop. She checked Runian's unfinished memoirs, opened the encyclopedia contained in a locked file, and began to research dimensional portals.
