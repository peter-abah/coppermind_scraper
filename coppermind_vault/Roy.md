**Roy** is a member of [[Enforcement]] and an acquaintance of [[David Charleston]].{{book ref|r1|8}}

Roy is one year older than David and was raised in the [[Havendark Factory]] alongside him. The Factory's manager, [[Martha]], assigned Roy the role of "adjunct", which required him to help supervise the younger children and deal with conflicts and bullying. David respected him for performing this role.{{book ref|r1|8}}

After graduating from the Factory, Roy joined Enforcement. He and [[Refractionary]] were part of the Enforcement Core that searched David's apartment when he became a suspect in the assassination of [[Fortuity]]. When David returned to his apartment and was accosted by Enforcement, Roy believed that David was innocent and tried to persuade his commander to let him go. When the Reckoners engaged the Enforcement Core in combat and killed several of them,  David spared Roy and told him that he was part of a conspiracy led by an Epic called Limelight, thus throwing Enforcement off of the Reckoners' trail.{{book ref|r1|8}}{{book ref|r1|9}}

After Steelheart was killed, Roy continued to serve as an Enforcement officer, helping to maintain order in the liberated Newcago. He and David remained on good terms.{{book ref|r2|4}}{{book ref|r2|5}}
