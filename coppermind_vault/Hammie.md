**Hammie** is a resident of [[Hearthstone]] on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa1|16}} 

Hammie told [[Kaladin]] when they were young that he thought it was silly for [[deathspren]] to be scared of a little water, since they were good at killing people anyway.{{book ref|sa1|16}}  Kaladin told his father [[Lirin]] this, and Lirin explained to him that this knowledge came from the [[Herald]]s.
