**Precilia** is either an [[Order of Edgedancers|Edgedancer]] or a [[Order of Truthwatchers|Truthwatcher]] on [[Roshar]]. Precilia taught [[Rahel]] [[Regrowth]] secretly because the latter was afraid to make it known that she was a [[Order of Truthwatchers|Truthwatcher]].{{book ref|sa5|39}}

## Trivia
Precilia is a Tuckerization of Priscilla Spencer, an Art Coordinator in Dragonsteel's Creative Development Department.  Priscilla considers herself a Truthwatcher.
