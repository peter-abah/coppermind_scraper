Dr. **Florin Malin** is a former government official in the [[Elendel Basin]] on [[Scadrial]].{{file ref|New Ascendancy broadsheet.jpg|*The New Ascendancy* broadsheet}}

==History==
Malin was the Minister of Science and Technology of the Basin. He worked with the mayor of [[Bilming]], Lord [[Bastien Severington]], to develop warships known as "[[Pewternaut]]s" with armored hulls and gun turrets. By the time Severington unveiled the new ships in {{date|342|pc}}, Malin had died under unknown circumstances.{{file ref|New Ascendancy broadsheet.jpg|*The New Ascendancy* broadsheet}}
