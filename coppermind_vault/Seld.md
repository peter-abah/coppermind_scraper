***Seld*** is a series of books on [[Roshar]] with at least fourteen volumes. It was among the many literary works that [[Jasnah Kholin]] consulted during her research of the [[Voidbringer]]s and [[Unmade]]. *Seld*'s authorship is unknown, but it wasn't written in [[Alethi]]; one of the people who translated it was [[Gashash-son-Navammis]], who was considered a trustworthy source by Jasnah. The single known quote, however, was questioned by Jasnah, and she was considering translating it herself.

## Story
It is unknown what these books are about specifically, they do however reference the [[Voidbringers]] in some way.
