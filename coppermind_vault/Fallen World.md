The **Fallen World** is the larger of two continents on [[Threnody]].{{au ref|threnodite}} It is controlled by the [[Evil]].{{book ref|sfs}}{{rpg ref|slwg|85}} The Fallen World is dangerous to visit. Expeditions in the [[Physical Realm]] have disappeared, and it is also holds peril in the [[Cognitive Realm]].{{au ref|threnodite}}

The [[Homeland]]—the origin of the ancestors of [[Silence Montane]] and possibly the other [[Forescout]]s—is also mentioned as a place lost to the Evil; Homeland may be a synonym for Fallen World or it could be a specific part of that continent.{{book ref|sfs}}

[[Daggon]] claimed that the [[White Fox]] had been to the Fallen World, although his stories were often apocryphal.{{book ref|sfs}}
