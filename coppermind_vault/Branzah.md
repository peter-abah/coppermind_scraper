**Branzahs** are a type of plant native to the [[Shattered Plains]] of [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa1|17}}

Branzahs live in cracks and crevices in the rock.{{book ref|sa1|17}} They are flowering plants with claw-like branches. After a [[highstorm]], they extend their branches out of the cracks they live inside.
