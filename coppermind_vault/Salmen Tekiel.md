**Salmen Tekiel** was a [[noble]]man on [[Scadrial]] during the [[Final Empire]], who may or may not have existed.

Kelsier spread a rumor to [[Hoid]] of a covert relationship between him and [[Shan Elariel]] to try and create controversy. Kelsier may have invented the name.
