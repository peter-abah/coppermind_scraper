**Angola Dartmoor** is the queen of [[Mokia]]. When the [[Knights of Crystallia]] evacuated her husband King [[Talakimallo]] and daughter Princess [[Kamali]], she was left to lead the defense..

## Appearance and Personality
When Alcatraz meets Angola she is wearing a flower in her long blond hair that matches her yellow and black Mokian wrap. She is tall and thin and looks much like an older version of her sister [[Bastille]]. 

## Relationships
Angola is the daughter of [[Brig Dartmoor]] and [[Draulin]]. This makes her [[Bastille]] and [[Rikers Dartmoor|Rikers]]'s sister.

## Trivia
* Her name is a reference to the [[wikipedia:Louisiana_State_Penitentiary|Louisiana State Penitentiary]], which is nicknamed The Angola Plantation.
