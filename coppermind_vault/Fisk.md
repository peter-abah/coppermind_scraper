**Fisk** is a [[Shardblade|Shardbearer]] in [[Dalinar]]'s army and captain of the [[Cobalt Guard]] on [[Roshar]]. His Blade is named [[Loremaker]].

## History
Fisk was promoted to captain of the Cobalt Guard sometimes after the position was vacated by [[Kaladin]]. [[Adolin]] lent his Shardplate to Fisk while on his mission to [[Shadesmar]] in . While in [[Emul]], Fisk coordinated a perimeter of soldiers around [[Dalinar]]'s tent, shortly after the duel between [[Jasnah]] and [[Ruthar]]. Dalinar and Fisk are able to communicate with only nods.
