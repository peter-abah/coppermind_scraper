**Fisk** is a [[Shardblade|Shardbearer]] in [[Dalinar]]'s army and captain of the [[Cobalt Guard]] on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa4|21}}{{book ref|sa4|50}} His Blade is named [[Loremaker]].{{book ref|sa4|50}}

## History
Fisk was promoted to captain of the Cobalt Guard sometimes after the position was vacated by [[Kaladin]].{{book ref|sa4|50}} [[Adolin]] lent his Shardplate to Fisk while on his mission to [[Shadesmar]] in {{Rosharan date|1175|4}}.{{book ref|sa4|21}} While in [[Emul]], Fisk coordinated a perimeter of soldiers around [[Dalinar]]'s tent, shortly after the duel between [[Jasnah]] and [[Ruthar]]. Dalinar and Fisk are able to communicate with only nods.{{book ref|sa4|50}}
