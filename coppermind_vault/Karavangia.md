**Karavangia** is [[Taravangian]]'s middle granddaughter{{book ref|sa3|122}} and is named after him.{{wob ref|11637}}

She was accepted for wardship, and had sketched an image of her, [[Ruli]], and [[Gvori]], which had been sent to [[Taravangian]].{{book ref|sa3|122}}

She is likely in Taravangian's model of [[Kharbranth]] in the [[Spiritual Realm]] at the moment,{{book ref|sa5|i|12}} if she was in Kharbranth when it was destroyed.
