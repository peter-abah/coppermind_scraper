**Karavangia** is [[Taravangian]]'s middle granddaughter and is named after him.

She was accepted for wardship, and had sketched an image of her, [[Ruli]], and [[Gvori]], which had been sent to [[Taravangian]].

She is likely in Taravangian's model of [[Kharbranth]] in the [[Spiritual Realm]] at the moment, if she was in Kharbranth when it was destroyed.
