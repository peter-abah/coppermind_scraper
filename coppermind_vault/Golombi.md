**Golombi** is a [[Roshar]]an scholar of [[Stormlight]].{{book ref|sa3|i|5}}

Golombi's work suggested that Stormlight-infused [[polestone|gemstones]] could enhance food growth in dark rooms more than candlelight. However, there was an issue with efficiency, and Golombi also thought that the results of testing may have been compromised.{{book ref|sa3|i|5}}

[[Taravangian]] and [[Adrotagia]] knew of Golombi's work. They briefly discussed its relevance while pondering the food sources used by the [[Knights Radiant]] at [[Urithiru]].{{book ref|sa3|i|5}}
