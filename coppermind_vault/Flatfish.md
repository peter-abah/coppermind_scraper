**Flatfish** is a type of fish on [[Evershore (planet)|Evershore]].{{book ref|sf3|7}} They also likely exist on [[Detritus]], as [[Freyja Marten]] owns flatfish caviar and feeds it to [[Doomslug]].{{book ref|sf1|3}} The flatfish has photophores, which are mildly venomous. They are poisonous to humans to eat, but likely not for [[kitsen]]. The kitsen usually remove the photophores before consumption.{{book ref|sf3|7}}
