**Flatfish** is a type of fish on [[Evershore (planet)|Evershore]]. They also likely exist on [[Detritus]], as [[Freyja Marten]] owns flatfish caviar and feeds it to [[Doomslug]]. The flatfish has photophores, which are mildly venomous. They are poisonous to humans to eat, but likely not for [[kitsen]]. The kitsen usually remove the photophores before consumption.
