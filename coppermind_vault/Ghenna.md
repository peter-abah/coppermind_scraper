**Ghenna** is a scribe who works at the [[Grand Indicium]] in [[Yeddaw]] in [[Tashikk]] on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|Edgedancer|14}} She is presumably a higher order scribe.{{book ref|Edgedancer|13}}{{book ref|Edgedancer|20}}

## Appearance and Personality
Ghenna wears a [[shiqua]] that covers her entire body, including her face.{{book ref|Edgedancer|14}} She also wears glasses.{{book ref|Edgedancer|13}} She is described as being very overweight.{{book ref|Edgedancer|13}} She is pretty, and younger than Lift initially assumed.{{book ref|Edgedancer|14}} She is a fairly serious person and seems to take her job seriously as well; she only suffers Lift's flippant attitude due to the orders of the [[Prime Aqasix]].{{book ref|Edgedancer|14}}{{book ref|Edgedancer|20}} Lift liked to insult Ghenna, but seemed to be fond of her, at one point giving her an extended hug.{{book ref|Edgedancer|15}}

## History
{{sidequote|side=right|size=250px
|Half the time, I get the sense that even kings are confused by the world.
|Ghenna{{book ref|edgedancer|18}}
}}
Ghenna intercepted [[Lift]] when the girl tried to get into the Grand Indicium. She did not initially believe that she was from the palace in Azimir.{{book ref|Edgedancer|13}} She changed her mind after [[Yanagawn]] sent a [[spanreed]] instructing the scribes to listen to Lift.{{book ref|Edgedancer|13}}{{book ref|Edgedancer|14}} She then generally obeyed Lift's requests, unless orders from the Prince of Tashikk superseded Yanagawn's authority.{{book ref|Edgedancer|14}}

Ghenna went on to assist Lift in contacting the Prime Aquasix via spanreed on a few different occasions as they updated each other on their activities and the [[Everstorm]].{{book ref|Edgedancer|14}}{{book ref|Edgedancer|20}} She helped Lift investigate [[Nale]]'s activities in Yeddaw and visited [[Tashi's Light Orphanage]] after Lift discovered that the [[Stump]] was a [[Surgebinder]].{{book ref|Edgedancer|14}}{{book ref|Edgedancer|20}}
