**Malal** is a [[singer]] on [[Roshar]] who is one of [[Leshwi]]'s soldiers. They are known to [[Venli]] and have moral qualities that set them apart from the type of soldier who serves [[Lezian]].

Venli noted that Malal and [[Rothan]] were some of the singers who did not participate when Lezian and his troops began attacking the defenseless [[Knights Radiant|Radiants]] and civilian humans in [[Urithiru]].
