**Mari Hammondess** is a member of [[House Hammondess]] on [[Scadrial]]. It is unknown if she is the head of House Hammondess during , or if she simply acts as the representative of House Hammondess on the Elendel Senate.

## Appearance and Personality
Mari Hammondess is considered to be attractive. She has a small gap between her front teeth, of the sort men may describe as cute. She also has flawless skin, delicate features, and long eyelashes. Mari is knowledgeable about Basin politics, and she is at least somewhat of a war hawk. She believes that Elendel should attack the Outer Cities while they can still win. However, after realizing that there was a bomb pointed at [[Elendel]], she immediately made arrangements to flee, valuing her and her family's own lives above those of the civilians.

## History
Mari was the acting senator for House Hammondess during . She presumably voted on the [[Elendel Supremacy Bill]], although it is unknown whether she voted for or against the bill. Mari likely voted in favor of [[Steris Ladrian]]'s proposal for a [[Elendel#Senate|Select Council]] to deal with the issue of the Outer Cities. Afterwards, she was appointed to this council, and left with the other members to a private meeting. During this meeting, Mari considered it necessary to launch a preemptive strike against the Outer Cities, believing that if Elendel waited, they would lose their advantage. After receiving a message detailing the presence of an Outer Cities bomb that was pointed straight at Elendel, Mari decided to immediately flee the city, along with her family.
