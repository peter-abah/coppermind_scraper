**Memist vines** are a dangerous type of carnivorous plant found on the [[Pantheon]] islands on [[First of the Sun]]. They grow in patches,{{book ref|iote|8}} and can sense when a creature walks underneath them. When this happens, the vines emit a chemical, called memist, that knocks any the creature unconscious. They then kill their prey, although it is not entirely clear how they do this, and feed on them.{{book ref|iote|43}}
