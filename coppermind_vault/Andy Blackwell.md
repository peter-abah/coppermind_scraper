**Andy Blackwell** is a member of [[ICON]]. She speaks with a slight lisp, and has sleek blond hair. She was a competitor in extreme ironing before she checked out. As part of ICON, she uses her scuba training to disable desalination plants. She was part of the fire team that kidnaps [[Holly Winseed (Provisional Replica)|Holly’s Provisional Replica]] When Holly was trying to escape, she threw a cutting board at Andy’s throat, crushing her trachea and killing her.
