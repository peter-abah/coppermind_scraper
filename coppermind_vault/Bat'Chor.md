**Bat'Chor** is a friend of [[Hoid]]'s. For a period of time, he was the closest thing that Hoid had to a best friend, but due to Hoid's long lifespan and travels around the [[cosmere]], this is no longer the case.

## Trivia
*Bat'Chor appears a major character in [[Dragonsteel Prime|*Dragonsteel* Prime]] as a member of the Ke'Chan people on [[Yolen]]. He was planned to be canonized in a rewrite of *Dragonsteel* Prime called *The Eternal War*, part of the original [[Dragonsteel (series)|*Dragonsteel* series]]. However, [[Brandon]]'s publishing plans have changed significantly over the years, and the series has been pushed back in the overall Cosmere release timeline. As of 2024, it is tentatively scheduled to be written after the second arc of *[[The Stormlight Archive]]*.
*Brandon has stated that many of the details from his various drafts of *Dragonsteel* will be reworked. Unlike other *Dragonsteel* Prime characters such as [[Hoid]], [[Frost]], and [[Jerick]], Bat'Chor has not been mentioned in published canon. However, Brandon has implied that he already exists in the cosmere.
