A **starmark** is a manifestation of [[Investiture]] on the Darkside of [[Taldain]].{{book ref|ws|part=ars}} Those who use this system are called **Starcarved**. Starmarks are highly connected to [[sand mastery]].{{book ref|ws|part=ars}}

## Prerequisites
It is unknown how someone gains starmarks, but only a few are able to access the power. Those who gain the power must first be shown by another Starcarved, possibly in a testing process similar to how the Diem finds acolents that have the ability to master sand.{{book ref|ws|part=ars}}

## Mechanics
The magic primarily functions through tattoos and its effects are not nearly as noticeable as [[sand mastery]],{{wob ref|14993}}{{wob ref|1509}} although with current information the latter may no longer be true. Starcarved are charged by weekly pulses of light and Investiture from [[Taldain system|Ridos]]. There are several different colors of Starmark. Some applications of starmarks allow the starcarved to make razor sharp blades or create a shield that can withstand bullets.{{book ref|ws|part=ars}}

Starmarks do not work and are not visible on Dayside.{{book ref|ws|prologue}}{{book ref|ws|11}}

## Trivia
* Starmarks may be a type of tattoo, possibly using ink only visible under UV since they cannot be seen while on Dayside.{{wob ref|14993}}
