> Within a heartbeat, Alezarv was there, crossing a distance that would have taken more than four months to travel by foot.
> — Another folktale, this one recorded in *Among the Darkeyed*, by Calinam. Page 102. Stories of instantaneous travel and the Oathgates pervade these tales.

**Alezarv** was a person existing in the time prior to the [[Recreance]] on [[Roshar]]. They may have been a member of the [[Knights Radiant]]. 

At some point, Alezarv used an [[Oathgate]]. This was recorded in the text *Among the Darkeyed* (suggesting perhaps Alezarv was darkeyed) by [[Calinam]] and [[Jasnah Kholin]] included it in her [[Jasnah's notebook|notebook]].
