**Fladrn** is a [[Thaylen]] admiral on [[Roshar]].

## Appearance and Personality
Fladrn has grey hair and spiked eyebrows. He is commanding and composed, not batting an eye at seeing [[Fen]] and [[Kmakl]] unclothed.

## History
At the beginning of the second of the ten days before the [[contest of champions]], he informed Fen and Kmakl of a breakout in [[Jah Keved]] by enemy [[Skybreakers]] and the force's moves toward [[Thaylen City]] in an exploitation of a loophole in the contract between [[Odium]] and [[Dalinar]].
