**Nightstorm Seven** is a member of [[Nightstorm Flight]] and is the brother of [[Nedd Strong|Nedd]] and [[Vent (Skyward)|Vent]].{{book ref|sky1|19}} Both his name and his callsign are unknown. He flies a blue [[Sigo]]-class figher, his flight number is seven and he is wingmates with his brother, Vent.{{book ref|sky1|26}} 

Both of Nedd's brothers died in a fight around a falling old shipyard. Attempting to help his brothers, Nedd followed them inside the shipyard. Despite his efforts, both of Nedd's brothers were killed.{{book ref|sky1|26}}
