**Inkwell Flight** is one of two other cadet Flights training at the [[DDF]] [[Flight School]] along side [[Skyward Flight]]. By the time of this Flight's graduation they have lost their flightleader, though it is unclear as to how.{{book ref|sky1|42}}

[[Drama]] is a cadet in either Inkwell Flight or [[Firestorm Flight]]. If she is part of Inkwell Flight then only four members remain in her Flight in the time period just before graduation.{{book ref|sky1|42}} If she is not, then there are only two members of Inkwell Flight at this point in time.{{book ref|sky1|42}}

[[Remark]] is also a cadet in either Inkwell Flight or Firestorm Flight.{{book ref|sky1|42}}

[[Spensa]] and [[FM]] eat dinner with members of Inkwell and Firestorm Flight not long after [[Arturo]] is pulled from Flight School.{{book ref|skyward|42}} During dinner, the three Flights discuss whether they would be combined into one larger Flight due to their reduced numbers and if so, which Flight name they would keep.{{book ref|sky1|42}}
