**Namakudo** is an assistant cook in the employ of [[Design]] at the [[Noodle Pupil]] in [[Kilahito]] on [[Komashi]]. When Design was busy introducing [[Masaka]] to [[Yumi]] and [[Nikaro]], Namakudo had to step out of the kitchen to help take orders from a rush of [[nightmare]] [[painter]]s coming off shift.
