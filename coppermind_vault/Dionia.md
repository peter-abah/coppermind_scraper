**Dionia** was the [[Arelon|Aonic]] term for influenza.{{glossary ref|Elantris|D}} Its name is based off of [[Aon#Dio|Aon Dio]], which means cold.

When Duke [[Telrii]]'s daughter [[Shaor|Soine]] was taken by the [[Shaod]], he claimed she had died of dionia, but there were rumors around the court about what had actually happened.{{book ref|Elantris|22}}
