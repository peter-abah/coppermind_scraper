> You mean...Myrddin?
> — [[Sefawynn]], after [[John West]] tells her he is a wizard

**Myrddin** is likely the [[Earth (Frugal Wizard)|alternate medieval dimension]]'s version of Merlin. He is a man who knows things that others consider strange or dangerous. Myrddin is also likely to be [[Waelish]], as [[Sefawynn]] thinks [[John West]] is Waelish for claiming to be like him.

When John told Sefawynn that he was a wizard, she didn't understand what he meant. John tried to give examples of what a wizard could do, such as magic, writing, and having knowledge of strange and dangerous things, and he mentioned Merlin as an example. Sefawynn hadn't heard of Merlin, but asked John if he meant Myrddin instead. When he affirmed that was who he meant, Sefawynn became convinced that John was Waelish.

## Trivia
* The wizard "Merlin" is derived from the Welsh legend of "Myrddin".
