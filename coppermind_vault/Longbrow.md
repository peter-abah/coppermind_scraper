**Longbrow** is a [[Thaylen]] historical figure who sailed the seas of [[Roshar]] in the mid-to-late s.

A proud figure, Longbrow is regarded as boastful yet likable by [[Nikliasorm]], who sailed with him on at least one occasion.

Longbrow died circa . [[Longbrow's Straits]] are likely named in his honor.
