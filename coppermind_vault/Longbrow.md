**Longbrow** is a [[Thaylen]] historical figure who sailed the seas of [[Roshar]] in the mid-to-late {{rosharan date|700}}s.{{book ref|sa3.5|17}}

A proud figure, Longbrow is regarded as boastful yet likable by [[Nikliasorm]], who sailed with him on at least one occasion.{{book ref|sa3.5|17}}

Longbrow died circa {{rosharan date|774}}.{{book ref|sa3.5|17}} [[Longbrow's Straits]] are likely named in his honor.
