**Mudbeer** is an alcoholic drink on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa1|25}}

It is served in taverns throughout western Roshar, people often turning to it in the depths of winter.{{book ref|sa1|25}} Even the winehouses of [[Urithiru]] serve the brew.{{book ref|sa4|12}}

The [[Unkalaki]] use mudbeer to duel. When two people disagree, they compete to see who can drink the most and still sing. Usually, they both end up too drunk to remember what they were arguing about in the first place.{{book ref|sa1|27}}

When [[Kaladin]] decides to help [[Bridge Four]] escape, he promises them that they will all be drinking mudbeer in some tavern within a few weeks.{{book ref|sa1|49}} The evening that [[Lunamor|Rock]] announces to Kaladin his intention to return to the [[Horneater Peaks]], he and his family drink mugs of mudbeer in [[Jez's Duty]].{{book ref|sa4|12}}
