Lord **Yonatan** was a [[lighteyes]] on the [[Shattered Plains]] on [[Roshar]].

He was mocked by [[Hoid|Wit]] along with Brightlords [[Tumul]] and [[Habatab]], and Lady [[Meirav]] his newly-wedded wife. Wit said that Yonatan's hat was impressive, and doubled as a tent at nighttime.

## Trivia
* [[Meirav]] and Yonatan are the names of two of King Saul's children in the Hebrew Bible.
* He was named after Brandon's editor's nephew, as a wedding gift.
