**Elrao** is a member of the [[Ire]],{{msh ref|5|2}} a group of [[Elantrian]]s from [[Sel]]{{wob ref|5870}} that have taken up residence in the [[Cognitive Realm]].{{wob ref|5671}}

## Appearance and Personality
Like many of the other members of the [[Ire]], Elrao is incredibly old.{{wob ref|7647}} He has silvery skin that glows faintly, typical of [[Elantrian]]s.{{book ref|elantris|prologue}}{{msh ref|5|2}} Although aging works differently in the [[Cognitive Realm]],{{wob ref|718}} Elrao has the appearance of a bald, unnaturally old man.{{msh ref|5|2}} As a member of the Ire, he wears a traditional hooded white robe with silver embroidery.{{msh ref|5|2}} Elrao is cautious, and seems to question the ambition of [[Alonoe]] more than his colleagues.{{msh ref|5|3}} His name is based on [[Aon#Rao|Aon Rao]].

==History==
Elrao was present when the Ire met in their fortress in the [[Cognitive Realm]] after [[Kelsier]] (as a [[Cognitive Shadow]]) spooked the group when he was noticed snooping nearby.{{msh ref|5|2}} Elrao was seen drinking [[Dor|an unusual glowing liquid]] during the meeting.{{msh ref|5|2}} He questioned [[Alonoe]]'s desire to move up the timetable of their planned attempt to take [[Preservation]]'s power using [[Ire's orb|a special orb]], although he ultimately went along with her plans.{{msh ref|5|2}}

Elrao was one of the members of the Ire that set off on the expedition to find Preservation. He carried a decoy orb, as Alonoe kept the real one.{{msh ref|5|3}} As [[Kelsier]] began to sow chaos, Elrao became fearful of [[Ruin]]'s interference and suggested turning back, but was overruled by Alonoe. After Kelsier separated Alonoe from the group, he tricked the rest of the party into thinking she had somehow disintegrated, and Elrao led the rest of the party in a hasty retreat.{{msh ref|5|3}}
