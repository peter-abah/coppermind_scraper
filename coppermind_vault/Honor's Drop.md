**Honor's Drop** is a [[perfect gemstone|perfect ruby gem]].

It was given to the [[Order of Elsecallers|Elsecallers]] for protection prior to the abandonment of [[Urithiru]]. 

After the [[Recreance]] it is unclear where Honor's Drop ended up. However, it may be the original name for the ruby [[King's Drop]] found in the [[Thaylen Gemstone Reserve]], which Vstim says is rumored to be a chunk off the [[Stone of Ten Dawns]].
