**Malad-son-Zent** was a [[Shin]] soldier during the [[Last Desolation]] who fought at [[Aharietiam]]. He wore [[lavis]] fiber wraps with hogshide sandals and fought with a bronze tipped spear.{{book ref|sa3|38}}

[[Dalinar Kholin]] took the place of Malad in one of his earliest [[Dalinar's visions|visions]], in which he witnessed another Shin man discover the abandoned [[Honorblade]]s.{{book ref|sa3|38}}
