**Twelveday** is a holiday on [[Lumar]].{{book ref|tress|39}} Presumably it is connected to its twelve moons, which are worshiped on the planet.{{book ref|tress|1}} On the day of the festival some ports in the [[Emerald Sea]] use gunfire powered by [[zephyr spores]].{{book ref|tress|39}}
