> Remember What the Doctors Say! Drink Vif Sparkle Tonic Every Day!
> — Slogan

**Vif Sparkle Tonic** is a brand of drink on [[Scadrial]].

It was made by Doctors [[Fronks Vif|Fronks]] and [[Selvest Vif]], who claimed that it relieved drowsiness and irritability caused by ulcers, animalcules, and poor nutrition. By , other drinks claiming to have similar benefits were on the market, and Vif Sparkle Co. ran an ad in *[[The Two Seasons]]* broadsheet of [[Bilming]] touting their secret formula and exhorting customers always to buy the Vif name brand.
