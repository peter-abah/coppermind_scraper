**Basin Bill's Old Time Pub & Playhouse** is a business in [[New Seran]] on [[Scadrial]]. It is owned by [[Basin Bill]]. In addition to its presumed functions as a tavern and theater, it also appears to serve as the headquarters of a treasure hunting operation run by Bill.
