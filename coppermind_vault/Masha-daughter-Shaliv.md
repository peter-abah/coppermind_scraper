**Masha-daughter-Shaliv** is a scholar of the [[Recreance]].{{book ref|sa3|56}} She is married to [[Szeth-son-Neturo]].{{epigraph ref|sa5|147}}

[[Jasnah]]'s observations of [[Feverstone Keep]] in one of [[Dalinar's visions]] suggest that the [[Recreance]] is more recent than Masha stated in her history.{{book ref|sa3|56}}

During the time of the [[True Desolation]], Masha was living with her parents in [[Shinovar]] near the [[Bondsmith]] monastery. She was asleep when [[Kaladin]] and Szeth purchased a wagon from her parents, and spent time later that day picking fruit in the family orchard.{{epigraph ref|sa5|129}}{{epigraph ref|sa5|131}} After Kaladin's apparent death, she helped Szeth bury him.{{epigraph ref|sa5|147}}

Six years after the [[cleansing of Shinovar]], Masha authored *[[Knights of Wind and Truth]]*, an account of Szeth and Kaladin's journey to cleanse [[Shinovar]] and help the [[Herald]]s [[Nale]] and [[Ishar]]. The account is mostly accurate, though it states only that Kaladin died the day of Stormfall, excluding the fact that he became a Herald simultaneously. She wrote it based on the testimonies of Szeth and [[Nightblood]] and with the help and approval of Jasnah Kholin, whom she views very highly.{{epigraph ref|sa5|133}}{{epigraph ref|sa5|147}}

As of the writing of *Knights of Wind and Truth*, she was a member of an order headed by Jasnah.{{epigraph ref|sa5|133}}<!--Please don't add speculation about what order this is-->
