The **Elariel Yearly Spring Salon** is an event hosted by House Elariel on Scadrial. It is held in the spring, during [[Marewill]]. In  it opened on [[Brassday]]. That same year, [[Idkwyl Elariel]] brought [[Terris]]-inspired designs to the Elariel Yearly Spring Salon.
