**Crzmak** is a [[Thaylen]] explorer on [[Roshar]]. A few centuries before the [[Night of Sorrows]], a coalition, later known as the Crzmak Stability, rose among the squabbling families of Thaylenah. Charismatic Crzmak led this coalition. While it’s unknown what exactly happened to it, the coalition didn’t last and its fate became known as an example of how the best intentions can go wrong when resources grow tight.{{book ref|sa5|89}}
