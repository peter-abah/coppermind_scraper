**Mirris** is an [[Aviar]] on [[First of the Sun]] bonded with [[Vathi]].{{book ref|iote|6}}

==Appearance and Personality==
{{image|Vathi_by_LittleGreyDragon.png|side=left|height=250px|Mirris and Vathi}}
Like most Aviar, Mirris is about three fists tall. She has subdued white and green plumage and is a breed of Aviar called a "streamer" which is native to some islands of the [[Pantheon]], though not [[Patji (island)|Patji]].{{book ref|iote|6}}

Mirris becomes aggressive towards strangers when injured, hissing at both [[Sixth of the Dusk]] and [[Kokerlii]] even when the former is applying care.{{book ref|iote|6}} Vathi sometimes comforts her by making clicking sounds.{{book ref|iote|8}}

When not in danger, Mirris is affectionate and nurturing toward both [[Rokke]] and Dusk.{{book ref|iote|19}}{{book ref|iote|21}}{{book ref|iote|13}}

==Attributes and Abilities==
{{for|Aviar}}
Mirris has a [[Nahel bond]] with Vathi and through it bestows on her the "talent" of concealing mental activity in her immediate vicinity;{{wob ref|11628}}{{book ref|iote|32}}{{book ref|iote|6}} streamers being one of five breeds of Aviar to provide such an ability.{{book ref|iote|15}} It is likely that Mirris would also be able to conceal the use of some forms of [[Investiture]].{{wob ref|9437}}

==History==
{{image|Iote_roomy_for_a_shack.jpg|side=right|height=450px|Vathi and Dusk take an injured Mirris into the trapper's safecamp}}
While working as a clerk for the [[Northern Interests Trading Company]], Vathi brought Mirris on an expedition to Patji. The two joined a scout team searching for a site to place a fortress, but despite Mirris' presence, a [[deepwalker]] destroyed the rest of the team. Vathi subsequently decided that she must find a [[trapper]] to have any chance of survival and she and Mirris headed into the jungle.

Mirris was injured when she and Vathi fell after a "monster" attacked them and Vathi ended up caught in a [[jellywire]] net outside of Dusk's [[safecamp]]. Dusk later found them and was pleased to note that Vathi had applied some first aid to Mirris.{{book ref|iote|6}} However, Dusk believed that Mirris's wing was broken and brought the pair into his safecamp where he cleaned Mirris's wound, wrapped her wings to prevent flight, and placed her with his other Aviar to calm her down.{{book ref|iote|8}} Because of her injury, Mirris remained in the safecamp while Dusk and Vathi left to investigate the Company's activities.{{book ref|iote|12}}

Five years later, Mirris accompanied Vathi to a number of important meetings discussing the [[Ones Above]] and the future of the [[Eelakin]] people.{{book ref|iote|7}}{{book ref|iote|9}} She also traveled with Vathi back to Patji and through [[Patji's Eye]], an experience the Aviar disliked,{{book ref|iote|17}} to see off Dusk before his journey.{{book ref|iote|19}}{{book ref|iote|21}} Mirris was also present when Vathi returned to Patji when Dusk returned.{{book ref|iote|61}}
