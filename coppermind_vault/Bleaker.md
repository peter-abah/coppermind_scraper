**Bleaker** is a resident of [[Elendel]] on [[Scadrial]] to whom [[Allriandre Durkel]] owes money. Bleaker sent people to collect a payment from Allriandre each month on the week after she had received her monthly stipend from [[Wayne]]. When [[Call]] and [[Garisel Daring]] visited her to talk about Wayne's testament he had on file with [[Call and Son and Daughters Accounting and Estate]], Allriandre thought at first that they were Bleaker's new collection men.{{book ref|mb7|epilogue|3}}
