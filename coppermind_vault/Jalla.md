**Jalla** is a [[Svordish]] woman from [[Sel]]. She is a member of the [[Teo Royal Family]] through her marriage to [[Lukel]].

## Appearance and Personality
Jalla is pretty, with dark hair and black eyes. Lukel considers her to be beautiful. She favors unembellished clothing, unlike her fashion-forward husband.

She is polite and speaks [[Aonic]] with a thick Svordish accent. She seems quiet and unassuming, but she is intelligent, steadfast, and strong. 

She does not consider herself [[Fjordell]], despite her country's longstanding status as a vassal state of Fjorden.

## History
She met [[Lukel]] at [[Svordish University]] where they studied together. They were married a few months before the [[Invasion of Arelon]]. They worked together as merchants, as Jalla was a fierce bargainer. After [[Raoden]] was taken by the incomplete [[Shaod]], he wished he had been given more time to get to know Jalla.

Jalla and Lukel attended the eclipse-viewing ball at [[Roial]]'s mansion and danced together there.

### Invasion of Arelon
During the invasion, Jalla attempted to take shelter in [[Kiin]]'s mansion alongside other members of their family. They were soon discovered by [[Dilaf]] and forced to go to [[Elantris (city)|Elantris]] to be executed. Lukel suggested that Jalla try to plead for mercy based on her Svordish heritage, but she refused. Despite her fear, she stayed by his side.

When [[Shuden]] launched a counterattack, Lukel tried to help, but he was untrained and sustained a severe wound to his thigh. Jalla wrapped his wound to try and stop the bleeding and then attempted to move him out of harm's way. Lukel and Jalla were holding each other and preparing for death when Raoden fixed [[AonDor]]; they watched in amazement as Elantris and the people in it were restored to glory. 

## Relationships
### Lukel

> Lukel is a merchant. Profit in everything, even marriage.
> — Jalla, joking about Lukel's motivations

Jalla and Lukel seem to have a close, affectionate relationship. Lukel believes she is the best thing that ever happened to him. [[Sarene]] becomes envious when she sees them together due to her anger about her arranged marriage to Raoden.
