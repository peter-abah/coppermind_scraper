**Jalla** is a [[Svordish]] woman from [[Sel]]. She is a member of the [[Teo Royal Family]] through her marriage to [[Lukel]].{{book ref|elantris|5}}

## Appearance and Personality
Jalla is pretty, with dark hair and black eyes.{{book ref|elantris|5}}{{book ref|elantris|32}} Lukel considers her to be beautiful.{{book ref|elantris|8}} She favors unembellished clothing, unlike her fashion-forward husband.{{book ref|elantris|32}}

She is polite and speaks [[Aonic]] with a thick Svordish accent.{{book ref|elantris|5}} She seems quiet and unassuming, but she is intelligent, steadfast, and strong.{{book ref|elantris|60}}{{book ref|elantris|8}} 

She does not consider herself [[Fjordell]], despite her country's longstanding status as a vassal state of Fjorden.{{book ref|elantris|5}}

## History
She met [[Lukel]] at [[Svordish University]] where they studied together.{{book ref|elantris|5}} They were married a few months before the [[Invasion of Arelon]].{{book ref|Elantris|5}} They worked together as merchants, as Jalla was a fierce bargainer.{{book ref|Elantris|8}} After [[Raoden]] was taken by the incomplete [[Shaod]], he wished he had been given more time to get to know Jalla.{{book ref|elantris|4}}

Jalla and Lukel attended the eclipse-viewing ball at [[Roial]]'s mansion and danced together there.{{book ref|elantris|32}}

### Invasion of Arelon
During the invasion, Jalla attempted to take shelter in [[Kiin]]'s mansion alongside other members of their family.{{book ref|elantris|58}} They were soon discovered by [[Dilaf]] and forced to go to [[Elantris (city)|Elantris]] to be executed.{{book ref|elantris|60}} Lukel suggested that Jalla try to plead for mercy based on her Svordish heritage, but she refused. Despite her fear, she stayed by his side.{{book ref|elantris|60}}

When [[Shuden]] launched a counterattack, Lukel tried to help, but he was untrained and sustained a severe wound to his thigh. Jalla wrapped his wound to try and stop the bleeding and then attempted to move him out of harm's way. Lukel and Jalla were holding each other and preparing for death when Raoden fixed [[AonDor]]; they watched in amazement as Elantris and the people in it were restored to glory.{{book ref|Elantris|61}}
