**Eltharon** is a *[[Metacha-im]]* [[Fused]] on [[Roshar]]. He is a sadistic Fused; a self-styled “sculptor” whose favored materials are the body parts of [[Knight Radiant|Radiant]]s. He keeps these “sculptures” in a gallery inside a stormshelter in [[Kholinar]]. He adorns himself with trinkets from his various kills. Eltharon also has many rivals among the Fused.

## History
During the [[True Desolation]], Eltharon was ordered to dismantle the resistance network in occupied [[Kholinar]]. He managed to capture [[Zoyah]], a [[Order of Windrunners|Windrunner]] spy, with a fake spanreed message. He then tortured her until she revealed [[Melur]]’s membership in the resistance. He used Zoyah’s code phrase to send a fake extraction request to Melur. Eltharon planned to let his targets gather at a hospital so he could kill them all at once. In the four weeks after capturing Zoyah, he caught many other resistance members. After Zoyah was rescued, she, along with the party that had rescued her, rushed back to the hospital. There, they found and fought Eltharon. It is unclear what happened afterwards.
