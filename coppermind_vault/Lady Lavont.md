**Lady Lavont** is a woman living in [[New Seran]] on [[Scadrial]].

She held a party in her home to auction off buttons from [[Spook|The Lord Mistborn]]'s favorite smoking jacket. She was with [[Jak]] when [[Mustaches]] confronted him at which point she backed off in fright. During the altercation her buttons that were to be auctioned off were stolen and replaced with fakes.
