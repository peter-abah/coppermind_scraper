**Suisna** is a small city a day away from [[Luthadel]], in the [[Central Dominance]] of the [[Final Empire]].

It is pillaged by the [[koloss]] army during the [[Siege of Luthadel]]. After being attacked, the people fled towards Luthadel, and [[Straff Venture]] sent them towards the besieged city to stretch their already strained resources further.
