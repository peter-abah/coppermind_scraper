**Erik Island** is an island in the [[Emerald Sea]] on [[Lumar]], known for its many lakes. The inhabitants export salmon.

==Trivia==

* Erik Island (known for its many lakes) is likely a reference to Eric Lake, one of the admins of [[17thshard.com]].
