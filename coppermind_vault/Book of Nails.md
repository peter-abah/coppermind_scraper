***Book of Nails***{{wob ref|16217}} (formerly *Boatload of Mummies*) is the working title of a [[Mistborn (series)|Mistborn]] novel{{wob ref|16054}} starring [[Nicki Savage]]{{wob ref|15159}} and written by [[Isaac Stewart]].{{wob ref|13744}}{{wob ref|9653}} It will be organized similarly to *[[Allomancer Jak and the Pits of Eltania]]*, although her "sensationalized" version of the story will only be in the epigraphs, with the actual events being recounted in the book.{{wob ref|12985}}

It has been suggested in the past that the novel might include an actress playing the role of Vin during a performance{{wob ref|13872}} and possibly [[Nazh]] in an antagonistic role.{{wob ref|13744}} As of September 2022, the first draft of the novel is complete.{{wob ref|16049}} The first draft is 118,000 words.{{sots ref|2023}}
