**Vardinar** is a member of the [[Kholinar Wall Guard]] in [[Alethkar]] on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa3|79}}  

Vardinar came to know [[Kaladin]] as a fellow soldier when Kaladin was investigating [[Azure]]'s activities during the [[Siege of Kholinar]]. Vardinar is not mentioned as part of [[Noro]]'s squad, but he may have been in the same platoon, since Kaladin knows him by name.{{book ref|sa3|79}}

Kaladin tasked him with taking command of two squads from the Eighth Platoon to defend against the [[Fused]] during the Battle of [[Kholinar Palace]]. Vardinar led the squads to one of the palace's towers to try and use archery against the Fused. Kaladin soon noted that the Fused must have "swept" the tower using [[Lashing]]s, as injured or dead men were falling to the wall below.{{book ref|sa3|79}} It is not known if Vardinar was among the few survivors of the battle.
