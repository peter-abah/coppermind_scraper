**Lady Mi'chelle Yomen**, née Ostlin, is a wealthy [[Elendel]] noblewoman on [[Scadrial]], and member of [[House Yomen]].

## History
Mi'chelle wed Lord [[Joshin Yomen]], and [[Yomen-Ostlin wedding dinner|their reception]] was held at [[Yomen Manor]]. The event was attended by [[Waxillium Ladrian]], [[Wayne]], [[Jackstom Harms]], [[Steris Harms]], [[Marasi Colms]], and [[Hoid]]. It was attacked by [[Miles]] and the [[Vanishers]]. They stole a number of valuables from the reception, including Mi'chelle's bridal necklace.

## House Ladrian
Lady Yomen is the first Senator to vote on the bill to give Elendel more power over the outer cities, voting against it, aligning her decision with Wax's speech. She is also described as a good friend and a very trustworthy senator by Wax when a letter implicating many members of the Elendel Senate is brought to light by [[Maraga]]. Wax and his allies's heroic actions at the [[Yomen-Ostlin wedding dinner|Yomen wedding]] as well as his involvement in the investigation and capture of the [[Vanishers]] is likely what caused a high amount of trust between these two senators. 

## Connection with Hoid

[[Hoid]] came to the reception, dressed as a beggar wearing black, to offer his congratulations. The reasons Hoid is friends with Joshin and Mi'chelle are still unknown, perhaps they are [[worldhoppers]] themselves.
