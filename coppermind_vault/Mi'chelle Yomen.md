**Lady Mi'chelle Yomen**, née Ostlin, is a wealthy [[Elendel]] noblewoman on [[Scadrial]], and member of [[House Yomen]].{{book ref|mb4|2}}{{book ref|mb4|4}}

## History
Mi'chelle wed Lord [[Joshin Yomen]], and [[Yomen-Ostlin wedding dinner|their reception]] was held at [[Yomen Manor]]. The event was attended by [[Waxillium Ladrian]], [[Wayne]], [[Jackstom Harms]], [[Steris Harms]], [[Marasi Colms]], and [[Hoid]].{{book ref|mb4|2}}{{book ref|mb4|4}} It was attacked by [[Miles]] and the [[Vanishers]].{{book ref|mb4|4}} They stole a number of valuables from the reception, including Mi'chelle's bridal necklace.{{book ref|mb4|4}}{{book ref|mb4|5}}{{book ref|mb4|6}}

## House Ladrian
Lady Yomen is the first Senator to vote on the bill to give Elendel more power over the outer cities, voting against it, aligning her decision with Wax's speech.{{book ref|mb7|2}} She is also described as a good friend and a very trustworthy senator by Wax when a letter implicating many members of the Elendel Senate is brought to light by [[Maraga]].{{book ref|mb7|33}} Wax and his allies's heroic actions at the [[Yomen-Ostlin wedding dinner|Yomen wedding]]{{book ref|mb4|4}} as well as his involvement in the investigation and capture of the [[Vanishers]] is likely what caused a high amount of trust between these two senators. 

## Connection with Hoid
{{theory}}
[[Hoid]] came to the reception, dressed as a beggar wearing black,{{wob ref|4698}} to offer his congratulations.{{wob ref|3546}} The reasons Hoid is friends with Joshin and Mi'chelle are still unknown, perhaps they are [[worldhoppers]] themselves.
