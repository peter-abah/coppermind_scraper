The **Warfields of Karkun** are an area of the [[Blackened Lands]] near [[Malmahan's Folly]] and the [[Quiet River]] on [[Mirandus]].{{map ref|Mirandus}} The warfields are named after [[Karkun]], the first [[Dark One (title)|Dark One]].{{book ref|do1|3}}

When [[Paul Tanasin]] arrived in Mirandus and met with Princess [[Feotora]], they left the Warfields of Karkun to enter the [[Kingdoms of Light]], but just before they entered the Kingdoms of Light, they encountered a [[drull]] fortification and attacked the drull there. Paul used his Dark One abilities in the fight and one drull, [[Rastik]], recognized him as the next Dark One. Feotora then left Paul with the drull, feeling betrayed by Paul's lies.{{book ref|do1|2}}
