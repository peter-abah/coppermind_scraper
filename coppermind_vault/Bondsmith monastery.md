The **Bondsmith monastery** is a [[Monastery of Truth]] dedicated to the [[Herald]] [[Ishar|Ishu]] and associated with the [[Order of Bondsmiths]]. It is located in the north of [[Shinovar]], near [[Ayabiza]].{{file ref|Map_of_Shin_Monasteries.jpg}}

## Location
The Bondsmith monastery is located half a day's walk east of Ayabiza in the rocky highlands.{{file ref|Map_of_Shin_Monasteries.jpg}}{{Book ref|sa5|92}}

## Structure
The monastery is not man-made, though it is called a monastery regardless. It is simply a sheltered section of ground flanked by rock formations. There are nine stone divots in the ground in the area known as the Sheathing of Swords, used by the [[Shinovar|Shin]] for safekeeping of the [[Honorblade]]s. The divots are kept clean by the shamans. The tenth, nonexistent divot would be for [[Talenel|Talmut's]] Blade, which he kept when he went to [[Braize]]. A few dozen feet down a trail outside of the Sheathing of Swords is a cavern where each prospective [[Honorbearer]] must stand before the [[Ishar|Voice]] to be accepted. Within the cavern is a ten foot wide, flat walled room filled with various [[spren]] -- both lesser spren and true spren -- nailed to the walls with spikes.{{Book ref|sa5|94}}

## History
At the end of [[Szeth|Szeth's]] pilgrimage to become an Honorbearer, he visited the Bondsmith monastery, guided by the Voice. After lingering in the Sheathing of Swords, the Voice told him to head to the cavern outside, down a path a little. Szeth obeyed reluctantly, feeling uneasy. To him, the path seemed dark. Upon entering the cavern, he noticed Shardblade cuts and fingerprints -- possibly by [[Stoneward]]s or [[Willshapers]] -- on the tunnel walls. In the actual cavern he came upon the room with the tortured spren tacked to the walls, then recoiled in horror. Szeth guessed that the Voice was one of the [[Unmade]], and as the Voice implored him to come and worship, he turned and fled.{{Book ref|sa5|94}}
