**Janice Vault** is a low-level member of the [[Fabian Augments]] cartel. She did some things while in [[Ulric Stromfin]]'s employ on [[Earth (Frugal Wizard)|Earth]] that particularly repulsed [[Runian]], and later she came with Ulric to [[Sefawynn]]'s dimension as a member of his crew. Upon seeing Runian in [[Maelport]], Janice went to shoot him, but he fired first. He destroyed her gun and then shot her several more times in the chest to make sure she was dead, despite knowing she did not have full platings to protect herself.
