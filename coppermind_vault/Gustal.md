**Gustal** is the helmsman of the *[[Oot's Dream]]* on [[Lumar]].{{book ref|tress|11}}

When the *[[Crow's Song]]* attacked his ship, Gustal attempted to evade its cannonballs by weaving the *Oot's Dream* back and forth.{{book ref|tress|11}} However, he died along with the rest of the crew of the *Oot's Dream* when it sank into the [[Emerald Sea]] following the attack.{{book ref|tress|12}}{{book ref|tress|19}}
