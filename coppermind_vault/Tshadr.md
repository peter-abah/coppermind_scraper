**Tshadr** is a [[Thaylen]] [[Shardbearer]] and [[highguard (Roshar)|highguard]] on [[Roshar]] during the [[True Desolation]]. He is a wealthy man who owns a manor. He possesses a suit of [[Shardplate]], and as such is a member of one of the eight Thaylen houses to own Shards.

## History
Tshadr likely received his Shardplate from his father in his youth, as was the tradition among Thaylen Shardbearers. By the time of the [[True Desolation]], he held a position in the highguard, protecting the Thaylen throne. During the [[Battle of Thaylen Field]], Tshadr led a group of soldiers and held [[Court Square]] in the fight against [[Amaram]]'s corrupted soldiers and [[Odium]]'s forces. Queen [[Fen Rnamdi]], having not seen the man, assumed he was either in his manor or mustering troops in one of the upper tiers of the [[Thaylen City|city]]. [[Hrdalm]] was able to relay news of Tshadr's location to [[Adolin]] at a later point in time.

Sometime after the battle in Thaylen City, Tshadr moved to [[Urithiru]] and settled in rooms on the fourth floor. He was the only non-[[Knights Radiant|Radiant]] Shardbearer in the tower when the [[Fused]] conquered the tower. Someone, presumably [[Taravangian]], told the invaders which room he was in, and the [[shanay-im]] were able to kill him and take his Plate without anyone noticing.
