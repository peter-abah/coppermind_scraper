A **keeper of songs** is a [[listener]] profession on Roshar. Their job is to memorize and, when necessary, sing the various listener [[Listener#Songs|songs]], which contain their most important memories and histories. Due to this they are among the most important people in listener society, way to important to for example be risked in battle. Apprentices spend a long time memorizing the various songs. They are also tasked with composing new songs when they are necessary. 

In the period of time between the [[Battle of Narak|First]] and [[Second Battle of Narak|Second]] Battles of Narak, the listeners had lost all the keepers of songs, as [[Jaxlim]] suffered from an illness that made her lose her sense of self. However, shortly before the Second battle began, [[Venli]] returned to the listeners, and also managed to give her mother a [[Nahel bond]], restoring her. This raised the number to two keepers.

## Known Keepers of Songs
