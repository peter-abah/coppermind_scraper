**Khyan** is a builder in [[Lossand]] who works with [[Trell (Taldain)|Trell]] under [[Kelzi]] [[Karmat]].

Khyan has medium length black hair and wears the dull, loose-fitting clothes and leather boots of a laborer in Lossand.

[[Kenton]] saved Khyan's life by holding up the roof of a structure after the scaffolding collapsed, allowing Khyan and others time to escape.
