**Seor** was an ancient [[Elantrian]] scholar on [[Sel]]. His name is based on [[Aon#Seo|Aon Seo]].

He authored the book *Seor's Encyclopedia of Political Myths*, a treatise regarding the false rhetoric of [[Fjorden]] and the [[Derethi]] church. [[Sarene]] is fascinated by the book, and tells [[Raoden]] that it contains the original version of the poem *[[Wyrn the King]]*. The poem was later altered by Derethi priests to both falsely indicate that the original [[Wyrn (character)|Wyrn]] was Derethi and to elevate [[Jaddeth]] from a minor god to the primary deity of Shu-Dereth. Sarene believes that the original version of the poem was suppressed by Fjorden, and would be a major embarrassment to them if it were publicized.
