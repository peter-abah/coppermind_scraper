The **Na-Alind** are a family of [[Tai-na]] in the [[Reshi Sea]] area of [[Roshar]]. They took in the few [[larkin]] that survived the [[scouring of Aimia]], promising to protect their "cousins".

[[Relu-na]] may be a member of the family given that she gifted [[Chiri-Chiri]] to [[Rysn]].
