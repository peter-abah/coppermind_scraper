**Iiaria** is a nation in the northern end of [[Darkside]] on the planet [[Taldain]]. It is the home of [[Skathan]], Darkside's emperor, and serves as the seat of power of the [[Dynasty]].{{book ref|ws|prologue}}{{book ref|ws|11}}

Iiarians typically have dark brown skin, brown eyes, and dark hair.{{book ref|ws|11}} They have an identifiable accent when speaking the Dynastic language.{{book ref|ws|5}} [[Jon Acron]] considers Iiarians to be quite superstitious{{book ref|ws|prologue}}

Few specifics are known about Iiaria, but it is presumably the base of operations for the Dynasty's significant military strength, including a powerful navy and a [[Starcarved]] army.{{book ref|ws|prologue}}{{book ref|ws|part=ars}} Emperor Skathan also suppresses the spread of knowledge and technology within the Dynasty and strictly controls travel between nations, using an extensive network of assassins and spies to do so.{{book ref|ws|4}}{{book ref|ws|prologue}}{{book ref|ws|5}} Skathan himself has several magical abilities that are not well-understood.{{book ref|ws|7}}{{book ref|ws|11}}
