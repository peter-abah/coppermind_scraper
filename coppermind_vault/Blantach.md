**Blantach** is a captain of the [[Bilming]] constabulary on [[Scadrial]]. She was involved in a sting on the Set during {{date|348|PC}}.{{book ref|mb7|22}}

## Appearence and Personality 
Blantach is a tall woman with short blonde hair. She isn’t after glory, but after protecting her people and city. She has been a constable longer then most others, including [[Reddi]]. This experience has led her to gain the rank of captain.{{book ref|mb7|22}}

## History
In {{date|347|PC}}, Blantach met with [[Marasi Colms]] at an intercity training event. A year later Blantach organized a sting on a local gang. Although there was a resistance from higher-ups against this mission, a tad more then usual, it was eventually approved. As they surprised the gangsters, who initially surrendered, Blantach was approached by Marasi Colms, who revealed that Elendel constables were also planning a sting on the gang. Initially outraged by this, Blantach had a conversation with Colms, until Colms realized that the Sequence would attack. During his initial Push, Blantach was thrown away, although she fell only a minor distance. After being saved by Colms, Blantach agreed to put aside their differences and organize a resistance. After realizing that the gangsters were ready to fight a prolonged battle, the constables prepared for a retreat, only to turn the tide once [[Waxillium Ladrian|Waxillium]] arrived. They captured most of the gangsters, although the Sequence fled.{{book ref|mb7|22}} 

{{sidequote|Listen. This is my city. I don’t know what—or who—you’re afraid of, but I’m not part of it. I want to help, and this is the only thing I can think of.|Blantach to Wax, Marasi and Wayne{{book ref|mb7|29}}|side=right|size=300px}}

Afterwards, she escorted the Elendel constables to the Bilming constabulary headquarters. She was also tasked with cleaning up the site of the attack. Three hours after the attack, shortly after arriving at the constabulary headquarters, Blantach sent for the mayor.{{book ref|mb7|26}} Blantach and Gave were discussing something, likely the situation they were in, when [[Marsh|Death]] arrived. After Wax, Wayne and Marasi left the building, Blantach quickly caught up to them, telling them that she wanted to help, and could keep Gave Entrone at bay if she managed to send [[Moonlight|someone]] with them.{{book ref|mb7|28}} They accepted her offer, and she sent a woman named Kim with them.{{book ref|mb7|29}}
