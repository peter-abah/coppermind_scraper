**Teur** is a resident of a small village of the [[Eastern Dominance]]. 

He witnesses old [[Jed]] die, killed by the [[Deepness]]. He explains what he saw to [[Sazed]], and questions Sazed's choice of funeral rites. He is married, and says that his wife can testify that he's never been out in the mists. He has a reputation for tall tales.
