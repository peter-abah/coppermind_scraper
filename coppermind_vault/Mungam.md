**Mungam** is an [[ardent]] in [[Kharbranth]] on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa1|48}}

She ran the hospital in Kharbranth where [[Shallan]] was treated after her trip to [[Shadesmar]]. She initially prohibited [[Kabsal]] from visiting Shallan while she was recuperating. She eventually relented, but Kabsal—who referred to her as "Old Mungam"—still complained that she was a tyrant.{{book ref|sa1|48}}
