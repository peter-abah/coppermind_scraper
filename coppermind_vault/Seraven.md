**Seraven** is the capital of [[Svorden]] during the [[Late Era]] on [[Sel]]. It is located on a peninsula in the northeast of Svorden on the coast of the [[Sea of Fjorden]], and is the northernmost point in the [[Fjorden|Fjordell Empire]].

[[Sarene]] has traveled there as an ambassador of [[Teod]].
