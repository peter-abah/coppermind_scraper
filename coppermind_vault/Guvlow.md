**Guvlow** is a scholar  on [[Roshar]] during the [[Era of Solitude]].

Guvlow wrote a book called *Incarnate*. The book contained a quote from "[[The Poem of the Seventh Morning]]", an ancient poem that had otherwise been lost. During [[Jasnah Kholin]]'s research regarding the [[Voidbringers]], she studied *Incarnate*, which was generally thought to be reliable by scholars. The quote that was purportedly copied from a fragment of "The Poem of the Seventh Morning" was from a [[Knights Radiant|Radiant]] [[Stoneward]] named [[Talatin]] and addressed the difficulty in fighting [[Voidbringers]].
