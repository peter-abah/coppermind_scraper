**Chillfever** is a malady on [[Scadrial]] that results from exposure to extreme cold. The term chillfever is used by the [[Southern Scadrian]]s and primarily refers to the effects of cold on the mind, including confusion and feeling "hot" even while freezing to death.

Several crewmembers of the *[[Brunstell]]* are afflicted by chillfever after being captured by the [[Set]] and taken into the mountains of the [[Seran Range]]. [[Jordis]] observes several people removing their clothing despite the cold, and she believes that they will soon be dead. She worries that she is experiencing a chillfever-induced vision when [[Allik]] first arrives to save the crew.
