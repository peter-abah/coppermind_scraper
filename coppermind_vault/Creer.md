**Creer** and his brother are members of [[Bridge crew|Bridge Twenty]].

He is distinguishable from his twin brother as Creer wears glasses.{{book ref|sa3|109}}

==History==
Creer was duty sergeant for guards the day when he delivers a note from [[Teshav Khal]] to [[Navani]], containing a [[spanreed]] transcription from [[Fen Rnamdi|Queen Fen]] warning for the [[singer]] navy. [[Dalinar]] orders him to let [[Teshav]] write back to Fen and propose to plan their next meeting in [[Thaylen City]].{{book ref|sa3|109}}

==Trivia==
* Creer and his brother are likely cameos of [[Jeff Creer]]--a roommate of Brandon's who made art for his website and the original [[Elantris (book)|Elantris]] maps--and potentially Greg Creer, who was the first person who read Brandon's writing that was not himself.
