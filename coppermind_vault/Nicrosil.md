**Nicrosil** is the external enhancement pushing metal. An Allomancer burning nicrosil is able to enhance the effects of another Allomancer's powers. Feruchemists can use nicrosil to store Investiture, and when used as a Hemalurgic spike, nicrosil steals Investiture. It is the paired alloy of [[chromium]] in the Metallic Arts. While the exact composition of Allomantic or Feruchemical nicrosil is unknown, real-world nicrosil is an alloy primarily of nickel, with some chromium and a small amount of silicon.

## Allomantic Use
A nicrosil [[Misting]] is known as a **Nicroburst**. A Nicroburst burning nicrosil and touching another Allomancer will cause the latter's reserves of the metals that are currently burning to disappear and give a huge burst of power to the recipient.{{book ref|aol|part=ars}} This could be used either to enhance an ally Allomancer or to hinder an enemy Allomancer.{{wob ref|5265}}

### Allomantic Interactions
* [[Iron]]/[[Steel]]: While Pulling or Pushing with nicrosil, an Allomancer gains an explosion of power, but grants them a far greater base strength, allowing them to Pull or Push on objects far heavier or [[Investiture|Invested]] than normal.{{book ref|mb2|57}}{{book ref|mb2|11}}{{book ref|mb2|43}}{{wob ref|7222}} These Pulls/Pushes could become so strong it can feel like the Allomancer is being torn apart.{{book ref|mb2|11}} A Coinshot could also launch incredibly far with a single Push.{{book ref|mb7|25}}
* [[Tin]]: While burning tin, a Tineye risks being shocked by the sensory overload and becoming temporarily numb, blind, and deaf.{{book ref|mb2|8}}
* [[Pewter]]: While burning pewter, a Thug can withstand or deal damage far greater than normal.{{book ref|mb2|38}} A Thug could also briefly resist the effects of drugs, but this does not remove the effects of the drugs long term.{{book ref|mb3|50}}
* [[Zinc]]/[[Brass]]: Pulling or Pushing on emotions while burning nicrosil causes the targets to feel an intense Rioting or Soothing of their emotions.{{book ref|mb3|44}} Those Soothed feel a flattening of emotions, making them feel empty and devoid of all feelings.{{book ref|mb2|27}}{{book ref|mb3|43}} Those Rioted feel an intense flare of emotions, making them to be more likely to act.{{book ref|mb3|44}} Nicrosil also allows an Allomancer to Riot/Soothe the emotions of a Smoker through a [[copper|coppercloud]] momentarily.{{book ref|mb3|43}} When combined with emotional Allomancy, nicrosil also allows Allomancers to "break into" and gain control of [[Hemalurgy|Hemalurgic]] constructs such as [[kandra]] and [[koloss]], even if they would not normally have enough raw power to do so.{{book ref|mb2|40}}{{book ref|mb2|54}} 
* [[Bronze]]: While burning bronze, a Seeker feels Allomantic pulses much louder and more acutely than normal.{{book ref|mb2|20}} With nicrosil, Seekers can also feel Allomantic pulses through a coppercloud momentarily, although not as effective as being able to pierce the coppercloud naturally.{{wob ref|7760}} This combination also allows emotional Allomancy to work on smokers.{{wob ref|784}}
* [[Cadmium]]/[[Bendalloy]]: While creating a time bubble with nicrosil, an Allomancer can condense or expand the time that the bubble is affecting. For bendalloy, this makes the time inside the bubble compressed, and from inside, time outside the bubble slows to a complete halt.{{book ref|mb7|71}}{{wob ref|9457}} For cadmium, it is likely the opposite effect, expanding the time outside the bubble and being able to skip time at a much faster pace. Cadmium's effect specifically is important for FTL space travel.{{wob ref|6297}}
* [[Atium]]: While burning atium, an Allomancer is able to see farther into the future, by allowing them to peer into the [[Spiritual Realm]].{{book ref|mb3|81}}{{msh ref|6|9}}{{wob ref|3610}}

### Other Invested Arts Interactions
Burning nicrosil could potentially be used to gain a burst of power from other forms of Investiture, such as [[Surgebinding]] or [[Feruchemy]].{{wob ref|12135}}{{wob ref|15240}}

## Feruchemical Use
A nicrosil [[Ferring]] is known as a **Soulbearer**. Nicrosil is used to store [[Investiture]]. This ability is not understood (even by those who utilize it).{{book ref|aol|part=ars}} A Soulbearer storing Investiture stores their ability to use their Investiture. Therefore, a Soulbearer could store their ability to use Feruchemy and another person tapping it would be turned into a temporary Feruchemist who has the ability to fill a metalmind (assuming the nicrosilmind was [[Feruchemy#Unkeyed Metalminds|unkeyed]]).{{book ref|mb6|21}} Among other things, a nicrosilmind can be used to store the [[divine Breath]] of the [[Returned]] and the ability to use various [[surge]]s of the [[Knights Radiant]].{{wob ref|3236}} Filling a nicrosilmind as fast as possible hides a person from an [[Awakener]]'s life sense.{{wob ref|4771}}

### Compounding 
Next to nothing is known about the possible applications of [[Compounding]] Feruchemical nicrosil, however it is theoretically possible to create a metalmind as Invested as [[Nightblood]].{{wob ref|4067}}

## Hemalurgic Use
When used as a Hemalurgic spike, nicrosil steals [[Investiture]].{{file ref|Hemalurgy table.jpg|Hemalurgy Table}} This does not steal powers&mdash;the ability to use Invested Arts like Allomancy or [[sand mastery]]&mdash;but rather general Investiture, such as a piece of a person's soul or [[BioChromatic Breath]]s.{{book ref|mb7|47}}{{wob ref|14896}}

If a [[Scadrian]] is stabbed with a spike Invested with stolen Investiture from another Scadrian, they may temporarily gain Allomantic powers.{{book ref|mb7|47}}

## Trivia
* In the [[Mistborn Adventure Game]] nicrosil can be used to convert between forms of [[Investiture]]. This allows for a [[Feruchemist]] to convert between attributes stored in their [[metalmind]]s (i.e. converting the mental speed in a zincmind into the strength in a pewtermind).
