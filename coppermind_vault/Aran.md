**Aran** is an earnest-looking bridgeman on [[Roshar]]. At some point during the [[War of Reckoning]], Aran was caught stealing, made a slave and assigned to [[Bridge Nine]]. Later, while he was seventeen, he was part of an expedition to research a group of recently uncovered ruins. The expedition was surprised by a [[chasmfiend]] while crossing a bridge though, and Aran was thrown into the chasm when the bridge broke. The bridge fell on top of him, although he survived the fall.
