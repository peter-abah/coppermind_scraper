**Smta** is a ship's captain on [[Roshar]]. She is a tall [[Thaylen]] woman with curled eyebrows that match her hair.{{book ref|sa3.5|prologue}}

In late {{Rosharan date|1174}}, she was the captain of a military ship; her crew included [[Yalb]], [[Brekv]], and [[Dok]]. Yalb notes that he is loyal to her because she was good to take him in spite of the rumours about him and the *[[Wind's Pleasure]]*. After Yalb spotted the crewless *[[First Dreams]]*, Smta directed him to lead a boarding party due to his "special experience". Yalb knew that Smta would want to bring the ship home to help with the war effort.{{book ref|sa3.5|prologue}}
