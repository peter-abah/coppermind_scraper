The **First Song** is one of the songs detailing the oral history of the [[listener]]s on [[Roshar]]. Often sung to the Rhythm of Memories, it is more of a chant than a real song. It is the original recitation of the listeners leaving [[Odium]] and his [[Desolation|war]]. [[Venli]] prompted [[Jaxlim]] to sing it at one point to calm her agitation.{{book ref|Sa4|73}}
