The **First Song** is one of the songs detailing the oral history of the [[listener]]s on [[Roshar]]. Often sung to the Rhythm of Memories, it is more of a chant than a real song. It is the original recitation of the listeners leaving [[Odium]] and his [[Desolation|war]]. [[Venli]] prompted [[Jaxlim]] to sing it at one point to calm her agitation.

## Excerpt

> Days we sing<br><!--
-->Days we once knew<br><!--
-->Days of pain<br><!--
-->Days of loss<br><!--
-->Days of glory
> — The start of the First Song
