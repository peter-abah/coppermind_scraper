**Vivare** is a dynasty in the [[Rose Empire]].

Art during the Vivare dynasty had a certain distinctive style. [[Gaotona]] asked [[Shai]] if the desk she [[Forgery|Forged]] while imprisoned was from the Vivare dynasty, or an imitation of [[Chamrav]], but it was neither.
