The [[Blackened Lands]] are a major region on [[Mirandus]].{{map ref|Mirandus}} The region is known as [[drull]] lands by the people of the [[Kingdoms of Light]], for the drull that live here. The capital city of the Blackened Lands is [[Pitch]], and is ruled by the [[Dark One (title)|Dark One]]. It is said that the air itself tells lies, and the ground changes between stone and sand to confuse travellers in the region.{{book ref|do1|2}}

## Geography and Ecology

North of the [[Quiet River]], the Blackened Lands are a barren, rocky landscape with very little in the way of flora and fauna.{{map ref|Mirandus}}{{book ref|do1|2}} Leafless trees are dotted around the harsh landscape, breaking up the stone and sand. The trees shoot lightning at intruders in these lands. The terrain is primarily open plains, with mountains and hills with sharp cliffs.{{book ref|do1|2}}

Packs of [[Evermouth]]s roam the land, hunting anyone foolish enough to wander around by themselves. Large birds soar in the air high above the ground. Human faces are not common in the Blackened Lands, however rumors speak of faces being cut off of unwary humans and worn as masks to sow confusion.{{book ref|do1|2}}

The cities in the Blackened Lands are primarily made out of stone and bricks, most of which have fallen into disrepair over the years from all the battles that have occurred against the Kingdoms of Light.{{book ref|do1|3}}

## Politics

The Blackened Lands is ruled by the Dark One of the current verse in the [[Narrative]], with all drull obeying the word of the Dark One and giving their lives for them.{{book ref|do1|2}} The drull and the Dark One are always opposed against the Kingdoms of Light, battling the [[Destined One]] as the Narrative foretells.{{book ref|do1|2}}
