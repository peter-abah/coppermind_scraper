**Ardous Geffenry** was a [[noble]] of [[House Geffenry]] in the [[Final Empire]].

He and his brother [[Callins Geffenry|Callins]] were killed in retaliation to the murder of [[Charrs Entrone]], although they were both innocent.

He had danced with [[Valette Renoux]] at a ball previous to his death. When she brings this up during a conversation around the intrigue of their deaths, [[Milen Davenpleu]] asks if he was a good dancer, and then [[Tyden]] makes a dark pun.
