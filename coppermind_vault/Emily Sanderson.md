**Emily Sanderson** (née Bushman) is an employee of [[Dragonsteel Entertainment]]. She is [[Brandon]]'s wife and business manager and holds the informal title of "Queen".{{bws ref|brandon-sanderson-newsletter-for-january-2015|Brandon Sanderson Newsletter for January 2015|date=2015-1-7}}

Emily was an English major at BYU and formerly worked as an English teacher.{{wob ref|2240}} She also speaks fluent Spanish and has taught ESL.{{wob ref|13961}} [[Matisse]] is based on one of her students.{{wob ref|2240}} As the Dragonsteel business manager, she plans Brandon's tour and event schedule and is sometimes present at his signings or livestreams.{{wob ref|10580}}

She has participated in Brandon's writing groups and has served as an alpha reader and advisor on many of his published works. She had a particularly large influence on *[[Warbreaker]]* and the novel is dedicated to her.{{article ref|Acknowledgements#Warbreaker|*Warbreaker* acknowledgements}} She is mentioned in the Acknowledgements of many other books in Brandon's bibliography, and *[[The Way of Kings]]* is also dedicated to her.{{article ref|Acknowledgements#The Way of Kings|*The Way of Kings* acknowledgements}}  She continues to advise Brandon on his novels, and has been involved in choosing the chapter titles for the *[[Stormlight Archive]]* books starting with *[[Words of Radiance]]*.{{wob ref|13961}}

Emily is intentionally not the direct inspiration for any of Brandon's characters, although some elements of [[Navani]]'s personality come from her.{{wob ref|13569}} Her top result in the [[Knights Radiant]] quiz was the [[Order of Bondsmiths]].{{wob ref|13960}}

Emily and Brandon met on a blind date set up by [[Isaac Stewart]] in 2005; they married in 2006.{{bws ref|about-brandon|About Brandon|date=unknown}}{{wob ref|1792}} They have three sons.
