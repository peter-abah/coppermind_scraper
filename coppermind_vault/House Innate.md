**House Innate** is a noble family in [[Elendel]].

Notable members include [[Winsting Innate]] and former Governor of Elendel [[Replar Innate]]. 

House Innate was one of the most powerful noble houses in Elendel. How the Innates made their fortune is unknown, but both Winsting and Replar were know to give out political favors in exchange for money. Additionally, the House seems to have come to prominence after the [[Catacendre]] as no mention is made of their existence during the time of the [[Final Empire]]. Their corruption and the deaths of both primary members of the house in  greatly damaged the its reputation. As Winsting was an [[Allomancy|Allomancer]], the ability is present in the family bloodline. 

The main political rival of House Innate is [[House Lekal]].
