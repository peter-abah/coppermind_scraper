**Melhi** is a [[Liveborn]] hacker.{{book ref|ps|1}}

She is snubbed by [[Kairominas]], taking offence at Kai's epiphany of the [[Wode]]'s steering him into a "perfect battle", and particularly at Kai's refusal to fight her.{{book ref|ps|6}} After this Melhi repeatedly hacks Kai's world and sends robots to fight him.{{book ref|ps|1}} This culminates in the creation of Sophie, a perfect human replica who Kai takes a liking to on a date ordered by the Wode to fulfill [[XinWey's Doctrine]]. She helps him defeat a robot sent by Melhi and then shoots herself under the command of Melhi.{{book ref|ps|7}}{{book ref|ps|8}}
