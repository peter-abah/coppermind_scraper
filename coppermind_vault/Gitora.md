**Gitora** is an [[Alethi]] scribe training to be an engineer on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa5|39}}

## History 
### Battle of Azimir
[[Adolin]] asks [[Kaminah]] if the balcony in the Azish Dome can handle a [[Shardwielding|Shardbearer's]] weight, and she sends the scribe Gitora to check, due to her training to be an engineer. Not long after, Gitora approaches Adolin and tells him that it is possible because the Azish Shardbearer is already standing on it, but did the extra math to conclude it can hold both of them.{{book ref|sa5|39}}
