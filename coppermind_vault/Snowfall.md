**Snowfall** is an [[Epic]] and leader of a group of Epics in Sacramento.{{book ref|Steelheart|7}} It is likely that Snowfall has abilities related to snow.

[[Lightning]] is a member of Snowfall's band.{{book ref|Steelheart|7}} 

When [[David Charleston]] first meets the [[Reckoners]], he thought Lightning would be a good target on the Reckoner's list of Epics to kill.{{book ref|Steelheart|7}}
