**Runestones** are stones imbued with magical properties of protection. They are located in many settlements of [[Weswara]], and are part of their mythology. 

==Appearance==
The stones are usually black and jagged, but shapes and sizes vary. The stones can be quite large, and their size is relevant to the amount of area they can protect. The runes are carved into the surface, and emit a glow that increases in intensity based on their relative power level. 

==Origin and Powers==
According to legend, the runes are said to have been created by the goddess [[Friag]]. After she heroically sacrificed herself fighting the [[Black Bear]], Friag's husband [[Woden]] forbid all writing, perhaps to keep humanity weak. However, [[Logna]] stole the runes from Woden and allowed early humans to use them to create the stones. Most of the runestones appear to be in Weswara, but other peoples such as the [[Geats]] also have them. The runes give the stones the power to bind [[wight]]s to serve as protectors, driving out bogs and other threats. The magic does not compel the wights, but instead offers them a home and additional powers to defend it. Woden was subsequently able to prevent new stones from being created, and has demonstrated the ability to punish those who do not obey his edicts. 

[[Skop]]s are said to carry the forbidden knowledge of Friag's runes. They retain some ability to interact with the power of the stones, by intimidating or inspiring the wights with their boasts. 

==History==
The people of Weswara believed that the protective power of the runes weakened over time. Woden's writing ban meant that they could not be recharged or recreated, making extant runestones very valuable. [[Hordamen]] constantly sought to invade villages with a stone, trying to capture them. During these invasions, the Hordamen used their own skops to try and disrupt the protective powers of the runes. 

The arrival of [[Runian]] and other interdimensional travelers interrupted Woden's powers, and the skop [[Sefawynn]] was seen creating a new runestone.
