The **Dustbringer monastery** is a [[Monastery of Truth]] dedicated to the [[Herald]] [[Chana]] and associated with the [[Order of Dustbringers]]. It is located in the east of [[Shinovar]].

## Location
The Dustbringer monastery is located in the east of Shinovar, near the [[Misted Mountains]]. It is located on a hill, near a pond that freezes over. A town surrounds it. Pine trees are plentiful in the surrounding area.

## Structure
The monastery is built in the same style as the other monasteries: as both a fortress and a watchtower. Its facade is a large stone block with slit windows. It is illuminated by gemstones in the walls. Like the other monasteries, it contains a grand hall.

## History
[[Gearil-daughter-Gearil]] was the Dustbringer [[Honorbearer]] when Szeth undertook his pilgrimage.

Szeth trained at the Dustbringer monastery as part of his pilgrimage to become an Honorbearer.  He enjoyed his stay there. He excelled in using the [[Surges]] of [[Division]] and [[Abrasion]]. His proficiency with Division would go on to be useful when he became a [[Order of Skybreakers|Skybreaker]]. He would sometimes skate on the icy pond nearby to practice moving with low friction, as a simulation of Abrasion.

Some time after Szeth was declared [[Truthless]] and expelled from Shinovar, his sister [[Elid-daughter-Zeenid]] joined the shamans, was selected for a pilgrimage, trained with the Honorblades, and became the bearer of the Dustbringer Honorblade, replacing the elderly Gearil. She wanted to prove that she wasn't weak.

When Szeth returned to [[Cleansing of Shinovar|cleanse Shinovar]], he reminisced on his earlier stay in the monastery. As he entered the monastery, he told [[Kaladin]] and [[Sylphrena]] he did not want help meeting the challenge of the monastery. He saw the Honorbearer, wrapped in grey robes that covered her face, and decided not to fight. He expertly dodged strikes from the Dustbringer Honorblade using his [[Gravitation|lashings]] and remained calm despite the flashy uses of Division. He asked the Honorbearer what was happening in Shinovar, which she ignored. When Elid expressed frustration at his unwillingness to fight, he recognized her voice. He summoned his Blade briefly to give him an opening to look at her eyes to confirm. He then told Elid that she could kill him. She was unable to, despite claiming she hated him. Once she stopped trying to kill him, she told him that the Heralds were grooming him for a spot in the [[Oathpact]], to replace [[Jezrien]]. Elid then vanished, possibly recalled by [[Ishar]]. [[Nale]] confirmed their intent to make Szeth a Herald, while also lamenting Elid's weakness. Kaladin and Syl attempted to argue that remaking the Oathpact might not do anything, which Nale dismissed. Szeth was shaken by the information. Nale told Szeth that he was going to fight his father soon, and confirmed that the Honorbearers he had been fighting were dead. The group left for the [[Skybreaker monastery]].
