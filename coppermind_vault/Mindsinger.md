A **Mindsinger** is a creature native to the [[Pantheon]] on [[First of the Sun]]. Mindsingers are known to draw a creature’s attention and lure them by ‘playing’ music inside their heads. What happens next is not entirely clear. [[Trapper]]s usually have an Aviar with the ability to shield their minds with them, as such a bird protects anyone near it from being affected by a mindsinger.
