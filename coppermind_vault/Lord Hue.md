**Lord Hue** is a [[noble]] member of the [[Luthadel Assembly]].{{book ref|mb2|38}}

During the deposition of King [[Elend Venture]], he votes for King [[Ashweather Cett]] and then, after Lord [[Habren]] changed his vote to Lord [[Ferson Penrod]], Hue followed suit, ensuring Penrod's victory.
