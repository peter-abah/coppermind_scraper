**Waterlog** was an [[Epic]] with water-manipulation abilities.{{book ref|firefight|41}} Prior to becoming an Epic, he was a street thug from Orlando known as **Georgi**.{{book ref|r2|25}}

## History
[[Regalia]] claimed to have turned Georgi into an Epic,{{book ref|r2|25}} although his powers were actually granted by [[Calamity (Epic)|Calamity]].{{book ref|r3|48}}

At some point, Waterlog was killed, presumably by the [[Reckoners]].{{book ref|firefight|41}} His cells were turned into a [[motivator]] that [[Knighthawk Foundry]] used to create the [[spyril]].{{book ref|firefight|18}}{{book ref|r2|25}}{{book ref|firefight|41}}

## Legacy
The spyril became a key tool for the Reckoners, used first by [[Sam (Reckoner)|Sam]],{{book ref|firefight|26}} and later by [[David Charleston]].{{book ref|firefight|25}} When Regalia attempted to have Calamity turn David into an Epic, she suggested that he should be given the same powers as Waterlog.{{book ref|firefight|41}}
