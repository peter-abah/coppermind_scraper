**Lotari** is a resident of [[Diggen's Point]] on [[Lumar]].{{book ref|tress|3}}

## History
At some point in their life, Lotari attempted to sneak off of Diggen's Point by stowing away on a merchant ship.{{book ref|tress|3}} Lotari failed in this attempt and was punished for it.{{book ref|tress|3}} Lotari's punishment was the only time that [[Tress]] ever saw the [[duke]] smile.{{book ref|tress|3}}
