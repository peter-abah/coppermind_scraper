**Intent** is a Spiritual concept in the [[cosmere]] referring to a being's express will or purpose. Intent plays a vitally important role in the workings of most manifestations of [[Investiture]] in the cosmere.{{book ref|elantris|part=ars}}{{wob ref|850}} [[Shard]]s have an innate Intent to them, a driving motivation that is encompassed by the name of the Shard itself.{{book ref|mb7|19}} Individuals can also focus their will into a deliberate Intent, something necessary when working with many types of [[Investiture]].{{epigraph ref|sa4|72}} 

## Nature
The precise nature of Intention, and how it functions and is used, is currently unclear. When Intent is exercised, one's mind reaches out into and interacts with the [[Spiritual Realm]], allowing for one's intentions to have influence in the Spiritual Realm.{{wob ref|77}} This influence can range from the power involved in chaining a [[Shard]] to a simple [[Aon]]'s effectiveness.

Intention plays an important, esoteric role in the underlying framework of the cosmere's magic. Within the context of specific magic systems it is often involved in specific ways, though the extent and importance of these functions varies.

## Shards
While a Vessel holds a Shard, the Intent of that Shard can inhibit what they can do with its power or drive them toward a particular goal. Over long spans of time, the Shard's Intent can influence or override their personality. As the Shards were once unified in Adonalsium's power, they are incomplete in their current state and lack much of the context that made each individual Intent valuable. Instead, each Shard and its corresponding Vessel tends to push toward the single ideal described in its Intent, without nuance or temperance.

; Ruin
: [[Ruin]] desires to facilitate change, especially to break things down and collapse existence into entropy. [[Ati]] was described as kinder than any person [[Tanavast]] or [[Hoid]] had previously met. He volunteered to hold the volatile Shard of Ruin, but after thousands of years, he had become nihilistic, braggadocios, and reveled in destruction.

; Preservation
: [[Preservation]] seeks to protect those around it and to prevent change. If given the opportunity, it would drive civilizations and worlds into stagnancy. The [[Rashek|Lord Ruler]] attempted to align himself with this Intent by inhibiting technological progress during the [[Final Empire]].

; Harmony
: When [[Leras]] wished to create a world, he found he was unable to until he collaborated with Ati to do so. [[Harmony]] found that the combination of Preservation and Ruin could be used to benefit Scadrial, but they later grew into conflict and forced him into inactivity.

; Devotion
: [[Devotion]] represents divine love, bonds of attachment, and selfless service. The [[seon|seons]] are a manifestation of this Intent.

; Dominion
: [[Dominion]] seeks to incite conquest and to place some beings in power over others.

; Endowment
: The exact Intent of [[Endowment]] is unclear, but it relates to gift-giving, and is manifest in [[BioChromatic Breath]] and the [[Returned]].

; Autonomy
: [[Autonomy|Autonomy's]] followers continually compete against each other, striving to prove their worthiness before [[Trell_(Trellism)|Trell]] or [[Bavadin]].

; Honor
: The power of [[Honor]] demands that oaths should be kept. It does not understand a nuanced distinction between morality and integrity. This power can intentionally abandon Vessels that fail to live up to its standards. Honor is also implied to represent the natural laws of physics and math.

; Cultivation
: [[Cultivation]] seeks to inspire change, especially growth, development, and self-improvement.

; Odium
: Other powers describe [[Odium]] as the embodiment of divine wrath, with none of the mercy or virtues that gave it context. However, Odium describes himself as the god of emotions, and that all passions are a part of his domain. His Intent informed the faith of the [[Passions]].

; Virtuosity
: Not much is known about [[Virtuosity]], but it relates to artistic mastery. Its influence on [[Komashi]] led to the creation of the [[hijo]] and [[nightmare]]s, spirits that react especially to artistic demonstrations.

## Role
In general, while use of magic can often be learned without guidance, and even be performed subconsciously, many applications can be learned significantly faster and with greater ease when the magic user receives instruction and is able to perform the magic intentionally within the bounds of its nature.{{wob ref|850}}

Intent is involved to some degree in the following contexts:

; Selish Magic
: Intent plays a particularly notable role on [[Sel]], where the copious manifestations of Investiture are accessed in ways that are distinctly tied to human culture.{{au ref|sel}} The precise shapes and movements associated with these magic systems must be composed with the intention of accessing the [[Dor]] for a specific purpose.{{wob ref|77}} For example, experiments performed by [[Khriss]] suggest that an [[Elantrian]] must know the required shape of an [[Aon]] and draw it willingly in order for the magic to manifest.{{book ref|elantris|part=ars}} For the creation of [[soulstamp]]s, a [[Forger]] must not only have proper Intent themselves, but also understand the intent behind the thing being stamped.{{tes ref|12}}

; BioChromatic Breath
: While the exact wording of a [[Command]] in [[Awakening]] is important, one must also exercise Intent through careful visualization of what the Awakener intends to happen.{{book ref|wb|46}}{{wob ref|850}} Without explicitly willing the expressed ideas to take place, merely pronouncing the words of the Command will not allow for a successful Awakening.{{wob ref|3661}} Notably, the Command used for [[Breath]] transfer, *"My life to yours, my Breath become yours,"* cannot be forced; in order for one person to give their Breath to another they must willingly speak the Command phrase.{{book ref|wb|prologue}}

; Allomancy and Feruchemy
: With [[Allomancy]] and [[Feruchemy]], the role of Intent is less significant than in many other systems. The specific application of Investiture is shaped by the metal used rather than by the user's will. However, Intention does allow for faster learning and greater skill with the magic.{{wob ref|850}} This also affects the usability of metalminds: if the user doesn't realize they are metalminds, they will be unable to use them.{{book ref|mb6|11}}{{book ref|mb6|17}} More complex uses, such as [[Compounding]] or tapping specific Investiture from a metalmind, also require some measure of intentionality.{{wob ref|850}}{{wob ref|1902}}

: For example, when [[Waxillium Ladrian|Waxillium]] receives the [[Feruchemy#Unsealed_Metalminds|unsealed metalmind]] (disguised as a coin) from [[Hoid]] in [[New Seran]], he is unable to tap it, or even feel a Feruchemical store within.{{book ref|mb6|11}} It is only once he understands the true nature of the metalmind that he his able to tap it and reveal the secrets within.{{book ref|mb6|epilogue}} Or when [[Wayne]] first picks up the [[Bands of Mourning]]: since he thinks the spearhead is simply [[aluminum]], he is unable to sense the power within the Bands -- and Wayne is an experienced Feruchemist.{{book ref|mb6|23}}{{book ref|mb4|2}}

; Hemalurgy
: While [[Hemalurgy]] can be performed anywhere in the [[cosmere]] and by anyone, Intent is required to some degree during the placement of the Hemalurgic spike.{{wob ref|3214}} It is unclear whether this means the person must only know generally what they are doing, or if they must have specific knowledge as to what the spike will do, where it must be placed, or why.

; On Roshar
: [[Honor]]'s power could be used to release and expel [[Odium]] from [[Roshar]], if the wielder of the power did so with Intent.{{book ref|sa3|57}} Prior to the [[Recreance]] some believed that the [[Sibling]] had withdrawn from humans by intent, though there is some evidence that this was not the case.{{epigraph ref|sa3|68}} In order to access the powers granted by an [[Honorblade]], one must do so intentionally. It is possible to hold an Honorblade and not have access to its powers if they are unknown.{{wob ref|1875}}
The [[Rhythm|Rhythms]] of the singers are attuned to particular Intents, and [[Nergaoul]] seems to alter and amplify the emotions of people who act in accordance with its Intent for violence.

; Aethers
: Each variety of [[Aether]] manifests as its own type of spore. When spores are exposed to water they react uniquely depending on the related aether, and on the intention of the one who initiated the reaction. For example, [[Midnight Essence]] will act and communicate according to the intention of the host{{book ref|tress|62}}, and similarly the one who initiates a verdant spore reaction forms a sort of [[Connection]] to the verdant vines and can control their growth.{{book ref|tress|41}} The more practised the sprouter, the stronger their connection to the manifestation.{{book ref|tress|58}}

;Technology 
: Some types of locks that are [[Awakening|Awakened]] can read your intent to tell if you have obtained the passcode with the owner's permission in order to increase security.{{book ref|mb7|40}}
