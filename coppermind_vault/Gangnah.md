**Gangnah** is a [[listener]] elder and foremost member of the [[Listener#culture|First-Rhythm Family]]’s [[Listener#Politics|ruling council]] on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa2|prologue}}

## History
Gangnah was a member of the Five during [[Eshonai]]'s days of exploration and the beginning of [[Venli]]'s search for new [[Singer#Known_common_forms|forms]]. She and the other elders heard both Eshonai's plea to unify the listeners and Venli's petition to try to adopt warform from the [[painspren]] she had trapped in a [[topaz]]. Gangnah expressed appreciation for Eshonai's efforts in her expedition and wished that the listeners did have a more unified government. After receiving doubtful comments from some of the other elders, Eshonai asked for permission to show the listeners her maps, thinking that her people would surely want to unite in the face of such a big world; after telling her that they would consider the matter more, Gangnah and the other four dismissed Eshonai and proceeded to interview Venli more closely.{{book ref|sa4|62}}

Gangnah attended the feast celebrating the signing of the peace treaty between the [[Alethi]] and the [[Parshendi]]. Aware that it could be a potentially dangerous mission, she appointed someone to take over her place at the five if she didn’t return.{{book ref|sa5|70}} She, [[Varnali]], and [[Klade]] remained behind in the wake of [[Szeth]]'s [[Assassination of Gavilar|assassination]] of the Alethi king, [[Gavilar Kholin]]. She confessed her responsibility for Gavilar's death to Brightlady [[Jasnah Kholin]], but offered no explanation beyond that they were attempting to avoid the return of their gods. Gangnah, [[Klade]], and [[Varnali]] were hanged for their crime.{{book ref|sa2|prologue}}
