> Classic antique ulius, the style used in lighteyed challenges of honor during the Sunmaker's reign. Had a little nick in it from the Velinar/Gulastis duel.
> — [[Adolin Kholin]]

The **ulius** is a type of classic dueling sword on [[Roshar]]. It was commonly used by [[Alethi]] [[lighteyes]] in duels of honor during the reign of [[Sadees]].

[[Adolin Kholin]] bought an antique ulius with a nick in it from the [[Velinar]]/[[Gulastis]] duel as a present for [[Idani]], misinterpreting her innuendo regarding swords. She did not accept the gift, and he kept it for himself.
