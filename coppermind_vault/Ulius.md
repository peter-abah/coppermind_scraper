The **ulius** is a type of classic dueling sword on [[Roshar]]. It was commonly used by [[Alethi]] [[lighteyes]] in duels of honor during the reign of [[Sadees]].{{book ref|sa4|34}}

[[Adolin Kholin]] bought an antique ulius with a nick in it from the [[Velinar]]/[[Gulastis]] duel as a present for [[Idani]], misinterpreting her innuendo regarding swords. She did not accept the gift, and he kept it for himself.{{book ref|sa4|34}}
