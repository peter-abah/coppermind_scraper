**Alai** is a scholar who lives on [[Roshar]] during the [[Era of Solitude]] and studies [[spren]].

Alai claims that spren are fragments of the [[Shard|powers of creation]], which is a common opinion among scholars. She also studies spren that have gained sentience, such as the [[Nightwatcher]]. Alai claims that the Nightwatcher is one of these, and that she is definitely female, contrary to [[Alethi]] folktales.

[[Shallan Davar]] reads some of Alai's work while studying sentient spren.
