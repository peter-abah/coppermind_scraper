**Scooch** is a sand fox owned by [[Kenton]]'s family in [[Lossand]] on [[Taldain]]. Little is known about sand foxes, but they are presumably a relatively small species of domesticated [[sandling]].

Scooch served as a guard fox, but she was never called into action and spent most of her time napping and eating table scraps, eventually becoming fat and lazy. At some point in Kenton's youth, an a'keldar (sand wolf) wandered into the city and tore Scooch apart in an alley, where Kenton later found her corpse.

Kenton recalls Scooch's death when the [[Diem]] is attacked by [[Kerzta|Kerztians]], comparing the complacency of the [[sand mastery|sand masters]] to that of his former pet.
