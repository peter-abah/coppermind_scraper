**Tearim** is the [[lighteyes|lighteyed]] captain of [[Gavilar Kholin]]'s [[King's Guard]].

==Attributes and Abilities==
He is known as one of the world's greatest duelists. He is often loaned [[Shardplate]] and [[Shardblade]]s, and has trained extensively with both.

==History==
Around , Tearim was already the captain of Gavilar's guard. During the major battle with [[Kalanor]]'s army, he wore Dalinar's extra set of [[Shardplate]] that technically belonged to [[Evi]]; this was the Plate that [[Adolin]] later inherited.

Tearim was also present at the [[Parshendi]] feast that preceded [[assassination of Gavilar|Gavilar's assassination]]. He wore Gavilar's Shardplate during the feast, since Gavilar had stopped wearing it in favor of traditional robes. Tearim stayed close to Gavilar, and [[Jasnah]] referred to him as a "bodyguard". Tearim was present when Gavilar stepped out of the party to speak privately to [[Meridas Amaram]]. By the time [[Szeth]] attacked Gavilar's quarters, Gavilar had donned his Plate. It is likely that Tearim was part of the decoy group that was intended to distract Szeth.
