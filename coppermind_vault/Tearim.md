**Tearim** is the [[lighteyes|lighteyed]] captain of [[Gavilar Kholin]]'s [[King's Guard]].{{book ref|sa2|prologue}}

==Attributes and Abilities==
He is known as one of the world's greatest duelists.{{book ref|sa2|prologue}} He is often loaned [[Shardplate]] and [[Shardblade]]s, and has trained extensively with both.{{book ref|sa3|26}}

==History==
Around {{Rosharan date|1145}}, Tearim was already the captain of Gavilar's guard. During the major battle with [[Kalanor]]'s army, he wore Dalinar's extra set of [[Shardplate]] that technically belonged to [[Evi]];{{book ref|sa3|26}} this was the Plate that [[Adolin]] later inherited.{{book ref|sa1|12}}

Tearim was also present at the [[Parshendi]] feast that preceded [[assassination of Gavilar|Gavilar's assassination]]. He wore Gavilar's Shardplate during the feast, since Gavilar had stopped wearing it in favor of traditional robes. Tearim stayed close to Gavilar, and [[Jasnah]] referred to him as a "bodyguard". Tearim was present when Gavilar stepped out of the party to speak privately to [[Meridas Amaram]].{{book ref|sa3|26}} By the time [[Szeth]] attacked Gavilar's quarters, Gavilar had donned his Plate.{{book ref|sa1|prologue}} It is likely that Tearim was part of the decoy group that was intended to distract Szeth.
