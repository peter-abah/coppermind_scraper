The **terha** is a domesticated animal on the [[Dayside]] of [[Taldain]].{{book ref|ws|9|review=yes}}

==Appearance==
The terha is a large quadruped. The body size and shape is similar to a rhinoceros, with a thickly muscled body and thick legs.{{book ref|ws|9|review=yes}} It is covered in brown carapace,{{ref|text=The first appearance of terha is in the background of a panel with tonks in the foreground, and both animals are shown as light gray. However, this is likely a coloring error, as terha are brown in all subsequent panels.}} with a large, oval-shaped head that has two large horns or tusks in the front and rounded armor in the back that helps protect the neck.{{book ref|ws|9|review=yes}} Plates of carapace hang from its long tail, with a particularly large triangle-shaped plate at the end.{{book ref|ws|9|review=yes}} It has clawed feet that appear to have opposable thumbs.{{book ref|ws|9|review=yes}}

==Usage==
Terha are domesticated, and are used for transportation while hunting [[sandling]]s in the [[deep sand]].{{book ref|ws|9|review=yes}} Like [[tonk]]s, they are ridden using a saddle, bridle, and saddlebags.{{book ref|ws|9|review=yes}} While tonks are used for general transportation, terha appear to be much more rare and specialized. [[Kenton]] has never ridden a terha before joining Lord [[Raagent]] for a sandling hunt.{{book ref|ws|9|review=yes}} Terha that are raised to hunt have their carapace treated with a special substance called [[DoKall]] from infancy to prevent the carapace from dissolving when it gets wet.{{book ref|ws|9|review=yes}} Raagent calls them tough, and they are not as skittish as tonks.{{book ref|ws|9|review=yes}}

==Taxonomy==
Terha have superficial similarities to both tonks and some species of [[sandling]]s, with both having horns and water-soluble carapace.{{book ref|ws|9|review=yes}} It is unknown if there is a relation, natural or bred, between the creatures.
