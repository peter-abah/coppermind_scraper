**Milen Davenpleu** is a [[noble]] in the [[Final Empire]] on [[Scadrial]].

==History==
He was the young heir of a minor house, and escorted [[Valette Renoux]] to a ball at [[Keep Hasting]] after his original date fell ill. During the ball, he and [[Kliss]] discussed the deaths of [[Charrs Entrone]] and [[Ardous Geffenry|Ardous]] and [[Callins Geffenry|Callins]] [[House Geffenry|Geffenry]], and how they were linked to  the relationship between [[House Tekiel]] and House Geffenry. He seemed polite, but Vin had to mask her disdain for him since he shared certain traits and attitudes with all nobles.

He played [[shelldry]] with Lords [[Rene]] and [[Tyden]] and another noble. He had failed to show up to their last game, and used an [[obligator]] to witness his promise to attend their next outing. He made a hasty exit when [[Shan Elariel]] approached to talk to Valette.
