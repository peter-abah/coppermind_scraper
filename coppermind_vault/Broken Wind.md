Callsign **Broken Wind** is a retired pilot in the [[DDF]]. She is a [[First Citizen]], having participated in and survived the [[Battle of Alta]]. She has an impressive flight record; a hundred thirty kills and an average of twenty engagements each year of her service. She retired prior to [[Spensa]] joining [[Flight School]]. Spensa used her as an example of a pilot with an embarrassing callsign.
