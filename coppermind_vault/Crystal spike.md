The **crystal spikes** are [[Hemalurgy|Hemalurgic]] objects on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa5|i|7}}{{wob ref|17034}}

## Magic
This method of [[Hemalurgy]] makes use of crystalline spikes instead of metallic ones.{{wob ref|17034}}{{book ref|mb3|42}} Crystal spikes appear to possess the aspect of Hemalurgy that warps the biology of the staked individual, as to keep them alive despite the spikes passing through where internal organs should be.{{book ref|sa5|81}} Crystal spikes also share some similarity with [[Inquisitors]].{{wob ref|5552}}{{book ref|mb7|47}}

When pounded through a person's eye sockets, it allows them to see [[Investiture]].{{book ref|sa5|i|7}} This includes people, [[spren]], and [[Light]].{{book ref|sa5|i|6}}{{book ref|sa5|81}}{{book ref|sa5|118}} The spikes also allow a user to recognize when different Investiture has different [[rhythm]]s.{{book ref|sa5|i|7}} The procedure to give someone spikes is extremely painful and occurs while they are awake.{{book ref|sa5|i|7}} The spikes will give off a violet glow, similar to [[Voidlight]].{{book ref|sa5|67}}

Crystal spikes can also be used to nail spren to a wall in the [[Physical Realm]], though how similar this is to the process for allowing people to see Investiture is currently unknown.{{book ref|sa5|94}}

==History==
{{image|Vyre by Palomaloid.jpg|[[Vyre]] with his crystal spikes|side=right|width=250px}}
[[Odium]] approached [[Battar]] shortly after his ascension, as part of a plan to set up a competent, trustworthy command staff. Odium had previously worked with Battar under her alias of Dova within the [[Diagram (group)|Diagram]], and he wanted a [[Herald]] working under him as Odium. Battar quickly recognized that Odium was capable of replicating the crystal spikes with his own knowledge and power, but agreed to his job and payment.{{book ref|sa5|i|6}} Odium approached [[Moash]] and offered him a chance at redemption, and to right injustice like he'd previously attempted when trying to assassinate [[Elhokar]]. The crystal spikes were not explained to Moash before he was strapped down and the procedure performed.{{book ref|sa5|i|7}} 

Moash used the ability to see Investiture, along with [[anti-Investiture]], to kill spren on the [[Shattered Plains]]. He successfully killed [[Leyten]]'s spren [[Ethenia]] and nearly killed [[Vienta]].{{book ref|sa5|81}} 

[[Ishar]] also used crystal spikes to nail spren to a wall in the [[Physical Realm]].{{book ref|sa5|94}}
