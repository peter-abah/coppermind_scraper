**Flen** is a [[skaa]] from the [[Tresting plantation]].

When Lord [[Themos Tresting]] took [[Jess (Scadrial)|Jess]]'s daughter, and [[Kelsier]] killed the lord and destroyed the plantation, Jess's daughter pounded on the door to the skaa hovels. Flen had thought that the girl was a [[mistwraith]].
