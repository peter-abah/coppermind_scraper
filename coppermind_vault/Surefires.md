The **Surefires** are a gang of criminals in the [[Roughs]] of [[Scadrial]].

## History
During his time as a lawman in the Roughs, [[Waxillium Ladrian]] came into contact with the Surefires and stopped them. He considered them to be a special case.

Afterwards, reports of Wax's actions against the Surefires slowly trickled into [[Elendel]], and were read by Lady [[Marasi Colms]].
