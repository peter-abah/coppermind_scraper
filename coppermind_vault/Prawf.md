**Prawf** is a [[Thaylen]] person on [[Roshar]]. She is the guardian of a temple of the [[Passions]] near [[Klna]]. She is soft-spoken, observant and distrustful, aiming to prove the Passions true. She has wistful eyes and long, dark hair she braids her eyebrows into. She is an ardent supporter of the Passions, and reveres spren because they reinforce her beliefs. She is also able to use the [[Surgebinding|Surge of Illumination]],{{rpg ref|ss|12}} though how she is able to do this is unknown.

## History
Prawf dedicated her life to the Passions. However, after the arrival of the [[True Desolation]], she came to doubt their truth.{{rpg ref|ss|12}} In an attempt to prove her beliefs, she made a proclamation; she would give the temple’s perfect gemstone to any who could prove their passion to her. Any candidate needed to both pass the temple’s maze and tell Prawf of their deepest desire before she even considered giving the gemstone.{{rpg ref|ss|14}} It is unknown whether or not anyone ever received the gemstone.
