** Steel Kitchen ** is a business operating in [[Bilming]] on [[Scadrial]] during the year . The goal of the company is to quickly deliver food from any open establishment to people's doorsteps. The company employed numerous [[Feruchemy#Feruchemical Properties of Metals|Steelrunners]], who used their Feruchemical abilities combined with thorough knowledge of the city to avoid traffic and make timely deliveries. Steel Kitchen was partly run by [[Vema]], who was responsible for taking the orders. Orders submitted by noon one day were able to be fulfilled on time the following day. Steel Kitchen advertised their services in *[[The Two Seasons]]* broadsheet published on 19 Marewill.
