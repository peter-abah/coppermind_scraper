**Fialia** is a woman kidnapped by the [[Set]] on [[Scadrial]]. She is an [[iron]] [[Misting]] (aka a [[Lurcher]]). After her kidnapping, she was a part of the [[Community]] project.

## History
She was kidnapped by the [[Vanishers]], who were employed by the [[Set]], sometime during . After she woke up, Fialia was told that the world would soon end, with the [[Ashmounts]] erupting and [[Hemalurgic chimera|metal mutants]] rampaging. She was also told that she was part of a group chosen to carry on human civilization and that her 'rescuers' had to stage a kidnapping to prevent mass panic. She spent the first two years being moved around several cavern complexes, before arriving at [[Wayfarer]], where she would stay for the next five years. During these years, she became completely convinced that what the Set had told her had really happened. During , she, [[Armal]], and [[Drenya]] spotted an [[Marasi Colms|outsider]] walking through Wayfarer. They immediately dragged her inside, planning to hide her from the [[Gave Entrone|mayor]], and Armal sent Fialia to fetch multiple friends of theirs. After returning, Marasi told everyone that they'd been lied to and that the world hadn't ended. Marasi decided that she needed to find Gave Entrone, and the women, including Fialia, took her to Entrone's mansion. After saving Marasi from Gave Entrone, the Allomancers, under directions from Marasi, burned up all the [[Investiture]] in [[Autonomy]]'s nearby [[perpendicularity]]. Afterwards, Fialia and the other captives were taken to the surface by Marasi. She was possibly part of the group Marasi escorted to [[Blantach]]'s constabulary offices to give evidence against Entrone.
