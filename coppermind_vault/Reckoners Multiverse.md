The **Reckoners Multiverse** is an unnamed multiverse{{wob ref|2958}} where multiple stories including [[The Reckoners]], *[[Snapshot]]*,{{wob ref|9227}} and *[[The Apocalypse Guard]]* would take place.{{wob ref|5559}} It is not connected to the [[cosmere]].{{wob ref|6424}}

==Concept==
The vast majority of worlds in the multiverse are unstable shadows. However, the few stable worlds, referred to as **Core Possibilities**, are mostly undergoing catastrophes, including the world of the Reckoners.{{wob ref|1485}} In theory, even *[[Alcatraz]]* and *[[The Rithmatist]]* could exist as alternate Earths in such a multiverse,{{wob ref|14683}} and there could even be a connection to the [[Cytoverse]].{{wob ref|13608}}

As originally conceived, the Apocalypse Guard was a group that was founded in order to save the various worlds of the multiverse.{{wob ref|6424}}{{wob ref|1485}}

==Status==
Brandon ran into various issues writing *The Apocalypse Guard*, cancelling and reviving it multiple times, with major changes to the story along the way.{{wob ref|11137}}{{sots ref|2019}}{{wob ref|13257}} As of 2021, it is not complete but it remains likely to be published.{{wob ref|14683}} Although both the *Reckoners* and *Apocalypse Guard* stories may contain multiverses,{{wob ref|7019}}{{wob ref|13890}} connecting them is not a top priority if the story does not work.{{wob ref|13608}}

Due to issues around film rights, *Snapshot* was removed from the Reckoners Multiverse.{{wob ref|2959}} However, these rights later lapsed,{{wob ref|14573}} so it is unclear if a connection may still exist.
