Highprince **Evinor** is a highprince of [[Jah Keved]] on [[Roshar]] during the [[Era of Solitude]].

In 1173, Evinor was killed by [[Szeth]] on the orders of King [[Taravangian]], along with King [[Hanavanar]] and five other Veden highprinces.
