**Delph** is a scholar from the [[Zephyr Islands]] in the [[Sapphire Sea]] on [[Lumar]]. He is a well-known and respected scholar. He claims that [[Xisis]] healed him of late-stage cancerous tumors in the year 1104.{{book ref|tress|31}}
