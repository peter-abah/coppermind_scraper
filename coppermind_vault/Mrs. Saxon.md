**Mrs. Saxon** is [[Joel Saxon|Joel]]'s mother and [[Trent Saxon]]'s widow.{{book ref|Rithmatist|6}} 

She works as a cleaning lady at [[Armedius Academy]] where her husband was employed as a chalkmaker before his death. She stayed at the academy following the accident, sharing a small room with Joel located in the family dormitories near her husband's former laboratory.

Mrs. Saxon generally works the night shifts, cleaning the classrooms that were used during the day. Because she works nights, Joel uses their single bed at night, while she sleeps during the day.{{book ref|Rithmatist|6}}
