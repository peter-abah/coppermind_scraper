**Pielle Fromed** is the leader of the opposition in the [[Bilming]] Council on [[Scadrial]]. He dresses professionally and is mostly bald with a few patches of hair.

Besides being the opposition leader in Bilming, Pielle also filled the role of local advisor to the [[Elendel]] Senate before being kidnapped by the [[Set]] and kept in captivity along with certain other political dissidents. He was extremely grateful when [[Marasi Colms]], [[Moonlight]], and [[TwinSoul]] discovered the group in the caverns below Bilming, as the Set had been performing experiments with [[Hemalurgy]] on the captives.
