**Fia** is a member of the [[Diagram (group)|Diagram]] on [[Roshar]] during the [[Era of Solitude]].{{book ref|sa3|43}}

Fia accompanied [[Febrth]], [[Moash]], and [[Graves]] on their trip across the [[Frostlands]] to [[Kharbranth]].{{book ref|sa3|43}} She was killed on the evening when the [[Fused]] ambushed their party.

Fia is not a very good cook.{{book ref|sa3|43}}
