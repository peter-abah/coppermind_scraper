**Narbin** is a type of simple woven cloth on [[Roshar]].

It is presumably made from a plant that grows near the [[Unclaimed Hills]] and the [[Shattered Plains]], as the [[listener]]s wore narbin clothing before they were "discovered" by [[Gavilar Kholin]] circa {{rosharan date|1165}}. Narbin cloth may also be used in [[Alethkar]], as [[Matain]] was able to easily identify it.{{book ref|sa1|45}}
