**Ambition** is a [[Shard|Shard of Adonalsium]].{{au ref|threnodite}} The Vessel of Ambition was [[Uli Da]],{{wob ref|8398}} but she was killed shortly after the [[Shattering]]. She at one point visited the [[Threnody|Threnodite]] system.

It is uncertain whether or not Ambition once had an [[Invested Art]] associated with her.{{au ref|threnodite}}

## Associated Magics
### The Evil
{{for|Evil}}
The Evil are [[Splinter]]s of Ambition, and were created when [[Odium]] fought Ambition.{{book ref|iote|25}}{{book ref|iote|39}} They are beings made of almost pure [[Investiture#Anti-Investiture|anti-Investiture]] and act more like forces of nature.{{book ref|iote|25}} They tend to avoid populated areas in the [[Cognitive Realm]], as they don't want to be changed and trapped by human perception. The only exception to this rule are the entities near the Grand Knell.{{book ref|iote|25}}

## History
The Shard Ambition was created when [[Adonalsium]] was Shattered by a group of people on [[Yolen]] around ten thousand years before the overthrowing of the [[Cinder King]].{{msh ref|3|2}}{{book ref|tsm|11}} Uli Da took up Ambition and Ascended; she then made her way to the [[Threnodite]] system.{{au ref|threnodite}} 

Over seven thousand years before the True Desolation,{{book ref|sa4|14}}{{book ref|sa4|76}} the Shard [[Odium]] arrived in the system.{{au ref|threnodite}} Odium desired to be the sole remaining Shard in the [[cosmere]] and considered Ambition to be a dangerous rival; as a result, he had her as number one on his list of Shards to eliminate.{{wob ref|1550}} Ambition, Odium, and [[Mercy]] fought one another in the space between the planets, struggling in all three [[Realmatic Theory|Realms]].{{epigraph ref|row|27}}{{au ref|threnodite}} Odium gained the upper hand and mortally wounded Ambition by tearing off chunks of her power, which altered the people of Threnody as well as the planets in the system. Ambition managed to escape the system, but she was killed and [[Splinter|Splintered]] in another location despite fighting back.{{au ref|threnodite}}{{wob ref|1554}} Odium tore her apart and removed her Intent.{{book ref|iote|54}} Her death left a wound on the Spiritual Realm and affected Threnody in "disturbing" ways,{{epigraph ref|sa4|27}} as well as having far reaching effects elsewhere in the cosmere.{{wob ref|8881}}{{wob ref|1662}} One of these effects was the creation of the [[Grand Knell]] near Threnody. The Knell gives off pulses, creating a ‘Current’ that can be used for navigation in the Cognitive Realm.{{book ref|iote|25}}

## Influence
The Shard Ambition may have had a local manifestation of Investiture at one point in its past.{{au ref|threnodite}} [[Nazh]] gave [[Khriss]] records from the [[Homeland]], which indicate that there used to be some sort of Investiture present on the planet prior to Ambition and Odium's fight. On the other hand, this Investiture may have existed prior to the arrival of both Shards. It is unknown if this Investiture is still in existence or not after Ambition fled the system.{{cite}}

There are several other notable sources of Investiture on Threnody that may be associated wholly or in part with Ambition.{{au ref|threnodite}} There appears to be excess Investiture in general, which infuses the spirits of some humans. The spirit of these people imprints upon this power, and when the person dies, the power remains behind in the form of a [[shade]], a [[Cognitive Shadow]] with, in general, a low level of consciousness. In addition to the shades, the [[Evil]] is also present on Threnody. This force of darkness destroyed an entire continent, sending people fleeing from the Homeland and other regions of the [[Fallen World]] to the [[Forests of Hell]] for safety.{{cite}} Eidoliths also threaten the rest of the cosmere, wandering through large parts of the Cognitive Realm.{{book ref|iote|25}} Khriss indicates that she believes that this may be the product of a chunk of Ambition's power, twisting the people and the planet itself. There is no stable [[perpendicularity]] on Threnody, although there are some unpredictable and unstable perpendicularities that are of morbid but unknown origin.{{cite}}

It is unknown what, if any, impact Ambition's power may have had on the planets Monody and Elegy.{{au ref|threnodite}} Endowment believes that they are better off for Ambition being dead.{{epigraph ref|sa3|40}}
