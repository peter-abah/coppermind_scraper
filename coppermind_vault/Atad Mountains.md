The **Atad Mountains**, known to the [[Fjordell]] as the **Dathreki Mountains**,{{book ref|Elantris|3}} comprise a mountain range on the continent of [[Opelon]] on [[Sel]].{{map ref|Arelon}}

The mountains lie along the border of [[Arelon]] and Fjorden.{{map ref|Opelon}} For many years, the [[Derethi]] were unable to invade Arelon due to the natural barrier afforded by the mountains, contributing to their hatred of the Arelene.{{book ref|Elantris|7}}

The range is considered a major geographical feature of Arelon; the curvature of the mountains is mirrored in the right side of [[Aon#Aon|Aon Aon]], the basic symbol of [[AonDor]].{{book ref|Elantris|46}}

[[Devotion's Perpendicularity]] is located in the northern end of the mountains, near [[Elantris (city)|Elantris]] and [[Kae]].{{book ref|Elantris|60}}{{book ref|Elantris|part=postscript}}
