**Vatwha** is a [[singer]] and one of the [[Fused]], a member of the shanay-im.{{book ref|sa3|epilogue}} During the [[True Desolation]], she wears long clothing.

[[Hoid]] danced with Vatwha thousands of years before the [[True Desolation]], but later, she was trained&mdash;presumably by [[Odium]]&mdash;to watch for Hoid.{{book ref|sa3|epilogue}}

Hoid saw Vatwha in [[Kholinar]] after the [[Siege of Kholinar]], hovering in the air, acting as a sentry. Despite her training to spot Hoid, she gave him barely a glance when he passed underneath her in disguise. Hoid decided to not take it as an insult.{{book ref|sa3|epilogue}}
