**Shu-Keseg** is a religion practiced on the [[Sel|Selish]] continent known as [[Opelon]] ([[Aonic]] term) or [[Sycla]] ([[Fjorden|Fjordell]] term), as well as the nearby nation of [[Teod]].{{book ref|Elantris|36}}{{glossary ref|Elantris|O}}{{glossary ref|Elantris|S}} Two opposing sects, [[Shu-Korath]] and [[Shu-Dereth]], later developed from this parent religion. {{book ref|Elantris|5}}{{book ref|Elantris|14}}

==Beliefs==
Shu-Keseg's core ideals center around unity – specifically unity of mind – and acceptance.{{book ref|Elantris|14}} Its followers believe in a single, omnipotent God named Dashu.{{glossary ref|Elantris|K}}{{wob ref|7591}}

==Origins & History==
Shu-Keseg was founded by a [[JinDo]] man named [[Keseg]] who preached the power of unity.{{book ref|Elantris|14}}{{glossary ref|Elantris|K}} Keseg was considered a great teacher, and was given the honorific name of KeHwo.{{book ref|elantris|part=ars}} The religion’s teachings are held in a book called the [[Do-Kando]].{{book ref|Elantris|43}} 

In [[Arelon]], Shu-Keseg was tolerated, but the godlike power of the pre-[[Reod]] [[Elantrian]]s precluded most people from converting.{{book ref|elantris|4}}

### Sects
Keseg’s students, [[Korath]] and [[Dereth]], left JinDo to spread Shu-Keseg, but they disagreed about how to achieve the central tenet of unity. Korath preached that mankind should be united through love, whereas Dereth taught of achieving unity through obedience. This schism resulted in the two opposing sects of Shu-Korath and Shu-Dereth.{{book ref|Elantris|14}}

Korath's teachings became popular in [[Teod]] and it is considered the home of Shu-Korath.{{book ref|elantris|6}}{{book ref|elantris|part=ars}} After the fall of its First Empire around three hundred years ago, the leader of Fjorden converted to Shu-Dereth.{{book ref|elantris|part=ars}} The new religion was then used as an excuse to conquer nearby nations under the guise of divine unification, creating the modern Fjordell Empire.{{book ref|Elantris|9}}{{book ref|Elantris|53}}

Due to the dominance of the Empire, Shu-Dereth is the state religion of all of Opelon, except for Arelon. Shu-Keseg is still recognized as the predecessor to Shu-Dereth and Shu-Korath,{{book ref|elantris|5}}{{book ref|elantris|43}} but it is only practiced (unofficially) in JinDo.{{book ref|Elantris|14}} After the [[Reod]], the Fjordell Empire sought to conquer the last remaining bastion of Shu-Korath in Teod; it also planned to convert or destroy the remaining Korathi in Arelon along with any possibility of the Elantrians returning.{{book ref|elantris|3}}{{book ref|elantris|50}}{{book ref|elantris|60}}
