A **zatalef** is a "bat-like cephalopod" native to [[Akak]] on Roshar.

The [[Alethi]] [[Glyphs/Index#Zatalef|glyph]] for zatalef is relatively recent, as the animal was only discovered by the Alethi during the [[House Kholin|Kholin]] conquest of the [[Akak Reshi]].
