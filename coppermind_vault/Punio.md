**Punio** is a [[Herdaz]]ian member of [[Bridge Four]]. He is a [[Order of Windrunners|Windrunner]] and one of [[Lopen]]'s many cousins on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa2|22}}{{book ref|sa3.5|11}}

==Appearance and Personality==
He is bald and muscular, and has the appearance of a fighter. He has tan skin and is identifiably Herdazian.{{book ref|sa2|22}} 

Punio shares Lopen's laid-back attitude,{{book ref|sa2|22}} although he was envious that his cousin bonded a [[spren]] before he did.{{book ref|sa3.5|11}} Punio is kind and cares greatly for his family.{{book ref|sa3.5|7}} He enjoys going out drinking and dancing.{{book ref|sa3.5|11}}{{book ref|sa3.5|15}} 

Punio is close with his cousins and fellow Radiants, Lopen and [[Huio]]. He once bet three [[Spheres#Informal_Names|clearchips]] that Lopen couldn't eat 12 [[chouta]] wraps in under two hours.{{book ref|sa3.5|13}} However, Lopen sometimes makes jokes at Punio's expense, such as mocking his hairstyling routine, and Punio has privately complained about Lopen's jokes to Huio.{{book ref|sa3.5|15}}{{book ref|sa3.5|epilogue}}

==Attributes and Abilities==
Punio speaks broken [[Alethi]].{{book ref|sa3|35}} He is married.{{book ref|sa3|35}}

Punio is a [[Knight Radiant]], a [[Windrunner]] of at least the [[Immortal Words|First Ideal]].{{book ref|sa3.5|11}} As long as he has access to [[Stormlight]], he is able to heal himself. Through [[Surgebinding]], he has the power of flight and several other abilities known as [[Order of Windrunners#Abilities|Lashings]].{{book ref|sa3|35}}{{book ref|sa3|55}}

==History==
Punio was a member of [[Roion]]'s army at the [[Shattered Plains]] and lived in his warcamp. After [[Bridge Four]] gained prominence in the [[House Kholin|Kholin]] army, Punio heard [[Lopen's mother]] say that they needed more guards. He decided to join them after returning from his annual furlough to Herdaz. [[Kaladin]] encountered him in the Bridge Four barrack and was not pleased to hear that he deserted his assignment, but Lopen and Punio argued that Roion would not miss him since nobody could tell Herdazians apart anyway. Kaladin thought he looked like a competent soldier and grudgingly allowed him to stay, telling him to pretend that he was always a bridgeman.{{book ref|sa2|22}} Kaladin saw Punio again when more of Lopen's cousins, [[Rod (Roshar)|Rod]] and [[Huio]], showed up in the barrack.{{book ref|sa2|46}} Punio's wife sometimes brought food to the Kholin warcamp, although he initially told people she was his sister in order to fit in with the bachelors of Bridge Four.{{book ref|sa3|35}}

After Kaladin began acquiring [[Knights Radiant#Squires|Radiant squires]], Punio was cited as one of the few non-original members of [[Bridge Four]] that was initially able to breathe in [[Stormlight]].{{book ref|sa3|46}}

Punio bonded an unknown [[honorspren]] and became a Windrunner weeks after Lopen did, shortly after the [[Battle of Thaylen Field]].{{book ref|sa3|121}}{{book ref|sa3.5|11}} When he began earning a stipend as a Radiant, he sent most of it to his family to help out the poorer cousins, and a large portion went to [[Rod (Roshar)|Rod]]'s family.{{book ref|sa3.5|7}}
