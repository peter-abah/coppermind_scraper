**Kenton's mother** is a woman from the [[Darkside]] of [[Taldain]].{{book ref|ws|1|review=yes}}

She marries a [[Dayside]]r, a powerful [[sand mastery|sand master]] named [[Praxton]], and gives birth to a number of children, including [[Kenton]].{{book ref|ws|1|review=yes}} The marriage between a sand master and a Darksider broke with tradition.{{book ref|ws|9|review=yes}} The couple's children lacked natural talent for sand mastery, with only Kenton attempting to join the [[Diem]]; it was rumored that this was due to the Darksider ethnicity polluting the family tree.{{book ref|ws|1|review=yes}}{{book ref|ws|11|review=yes}}

Kenton learned to speak [[Dynastic]] from his mother, as it was her native tongue.{{book ref|ws|3|review=yes}} After the attack on the [[Diem]], Kenton notes that it has been two years since he has heard someone speak Dynastic, implying that his mother died or otherwise lost contact with him at that time.{{book ref|ws|3|review=yes}}
