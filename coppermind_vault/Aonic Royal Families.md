The **Aonic Royal Family** is formed by the **Teo Royal Family** that rules [[Teod]] and the **Arelene Royal Family** that rules [[Arelon]] after the [[Reod]] caused the fall of the former system of government. To show commitment to their political alliance against the [[Fjordell Empire]], the two lines were joined.{{book ref|Elantris|16}}
