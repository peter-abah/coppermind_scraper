> That day, Friag saved all of humankind - not only those of our land, but the [[Waelish]], the [[Hordamen]], the distant Ériuians, and the men of the far lands, though they know it not.
> — Sefawynn to John

The **Ériuians** are a group of people who live far away from [[Weswara]].

After [[John West]] enquired about the origins of [[runestone]]s, [[Sefawynn]] told him that [[Friag]] saved the Ériuians, along with the rest of the world's peoples, by binding both the [[Black Bear]] and [[Fenris]] with boasts.

==Trivia==
*The Ériuians are likely the medieval dimension's version of the Irish, as "Ériu" is the Old Irish name for "Ireland".
