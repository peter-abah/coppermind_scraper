**North Seran** is a body of water on [[Scadrial]].

During the [[Final Empire]], it is situated at the edge of the [[Eastern Dominance]]; the eastern shore of the lake lies just inside the border with the [[Crescent Dominance]]. There are mountains to the north of the lake and farmland to the west. It is connected to [[South Seran]] by the [[River Seran]], and is the smaller of the two Seran lakes. Two settlements or plantations are located on the southern end of the lake, near the headwaters of the river.

After the [[Catacendre]], the lake remains in the same location, but the area is not known to be inhabited.
