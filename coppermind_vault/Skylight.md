**Skylights** are massive lights that provide illumination and warmth to [[Detritus]].{{book ref|skyward|prologue}}

The skylights were presumably placed in low orbit around [[Detritus]] when the humans living there decided to build thousands of semiautonomous defensive [[platform]]s to create a "shell" around the planet, which had the side effect of blocking out the light from its sun.{{book ref|skyward|30}}{{book ref|starsight|2}}{{book ref|starsight|29}}

They are incredibly large and very bright, even when viewed from the planet's surface. {{book ref|skyward|46}}{{book ref|skyward|13}} The light is relatively focused, like a giant spotlight, and anything outside of the beams remains relatively dark.{{book ref|skyward|30}}{{book ref|starsight|3}} Their orbit is low and fast enough that their movement is visible to people on the surface.{{book ref|skyward|15}}

The skylights are programmed to provide an artificial day/night cycle.{{book ref|starsight|3}} The lights in a given area will dim and then eventually turn off to simulate night.{{book ref|skyward|34}}{{book ref|skyward|42}}{{book ref|skyward|47}}

==Trivia==
*The term "skylight" is also used for the real-life architectural feature of the same name.{{book ref|sf2|5}}{{book ref|sf2|21}}
