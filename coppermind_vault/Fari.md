**Fari** is a [[Skybreaker]] squire of the Second Ideal under the Skybreaker masters [[Ki]] and [[Warren]].

He competes against [[Szeth]] in a martial competence test over the [[Purelake]], to test their ability with their [[Surge]]s. The squires have to throw bags of colored powder at each other and avoid being hit. At one point Fari was hoarding all pouches of one color.
