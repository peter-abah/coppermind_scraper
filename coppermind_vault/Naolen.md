**Naolen** is a [[Teo]] minister on [[Sel]].{{book ref|Elantris|2}}

In a conversation with King [[Eventeo]] and [[Sarene]] shortly after Sarene's arrival in [[Arelon]], Eventeo jokes that Sarene should go easy on the Arelene, saying he did not want to receive a note from Naolen in a month that said King Iadon had run off to join a Korathi monostery and Sarene had been named queen instead.{{book ref|Elantris|2}}
