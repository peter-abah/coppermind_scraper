**Venan** is a resident of [[Urithiru]] on [[Roshar]]. During the initial efforts to set up a defense for the [[Sibling]]'s crystal pillar, they were with [[Navani Kholin]]'s group on the first level of the tower following the [[spren]]'s light towards a node. They had the key needed to open the store room to which the Sibling directed them.{{book ref|sa4|42}}
