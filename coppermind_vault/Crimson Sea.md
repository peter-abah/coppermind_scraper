The **Crimson Sea** is an ocean on [[Lumar]]. It is sometimes nicknamed the domain of the [[Xisisrefliel|dragon]].{{book ref|tress|35}}

## Geography

The Crimson Sea is notable for its unpredictable weather patterns, which makes it one of the most dangerous seas on Lumar.{{book ref|tress|35}} The reason for this danger is due to the fact that the navigation of the seas is based upon the predictability of weather on Lumar, and the Crimson Sea simplifies this down to pure luck.{{book ref|tress|35}} Therefore, ships avoid traveling through it, and the sea is mostly uninhabited.{{book ref|tress|5}}

It borders the [[Emerald Sea]] and [[Midnight Sea]], along with three other unnamed seas.{{book ref|tress|23}}
