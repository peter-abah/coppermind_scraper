The **Night of Sorrows** is the eternal night in all of [[Roshar]] except [[Azir]] and [[Urithiru]] caused by the birth of the true [[Everstorm]]. It is sometimes referred to as the **End of All Things**.

==History==
The term "Night of Sorrows" was repeated in a number of [[Dalinar's visions]], spoken by a voice that [[Dalinar]] was not initially able to identify; it was always mentioned alongside the True Desolation and Everstorm. The [[Almighty]] (Honor) later revealed that he was the voice speaking in the visions, but he could not respond to Dalinar's questions, and did not elaborate on his warnings. When Dalinar later revisited one of the visions with [[Yanagawn]], Honor repeated his previous speech in the vision, including his reference to the Night of Sorrows.. [[Gavilar]] received the same visions.

The Night of Sorrows was referenced in a [[Death Rattle]] collected by the [[Silent Gatherer]]s in . It was once again referred to in the same breath as the True Desolation and Everstorm. It was referenced in another Death Rattle by Deti.

After Taravangian became [[Retribution]], [[Ishar]] declared that the Night of Sorrows had come. The Everstorm grew to cover all of Roshar, except Urithiru. A month later, the Everstorm went away in Azir.
