The **Night of Sorrows** is the eternal night in all of [[Roshar]] except [[Azir]] and [[Urithiru]] caused by the birth of the true [[Everstorm]].{{book ref|sa5|146}}{{book ref|sa5|147}} It is sometimes referred to as the **End of All Things**.{{epigraph ref|sa1|56}}{{book ref|sa5|147}}

==History==
The term "Night of Sorrows" was repeated in a number of [[Dalinar's visions]], spoken by a voice that [[Dalinar]] was not initially able to identify; it was always mentioned alongside the True Desolation and Everstorm.{{book ref|sa1|18}}{{book ref|sa1|52}}{{book ref|sa1|61}} The [[Almighty]] (Honor) later revealed that he was the voice speaking in the visions, but he could not respond to Dalinar's questions, and did not elaborate on his warnings.{{book ref|sa1|75}} When Dalinar later revisited one of the visions with [[Yanagawn]], Honor repeated his previous speech in the vision, including his reference to the Night of Sorrows.{{book ref|sa3|56}}. [[Gavilar]] received the same visions.{{book ref|sa5|prologue}}

The Night of Sorrows was referenced in a [[Death Rattle]] collected by the [[Silent Gatherer]]s in {{Rosharan date|1172}}. It was once again referred to in the same breath as the True Desolation and Everstorm.{{epigraph ref|sa1|5}} It was referenced in another Death Rattle by Deti.{{book ref|sa5|74}}

After Taravangian became [[Retribution]], [[Ishar]] declared that the Night of Sorrows had come.{{book ref|sa5|144}} The Everstorm grew to cover all of Roshar, except Urithiru.{{book ref|sa5|146}} A month later, the Everstorm went away in Azir.{{book ref|sa5|147}}
