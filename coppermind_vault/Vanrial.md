The **Vanrial** were an order of artists on [[Roshar]] who lived on the slopes of the [[Silent Mount]] in [[Jah Keved]].{{book ref|sa1|60}} For centuries they sang a song in a language whose meaning had been lost. The Vanrial claimed that the song had been given to them by the [[Herald]]s. Some evidence suggested that the lyrics themselves were in the [[Dawnchant]].

There is also a theory that was known as the **Vanrial Hypothesis**; what it postulates is unknown.{{book ref|sa1|60}}

The Vanrial were also responsible for preserving the full text of [[The Way of Kings (in-world)|*The Way of Kings*]] from earlier times in Roshar's history when many other classical works were lost.{{book ref|twok|45}}
