**Duskwatch** is a presumed [[Epic]] due to their name.{{book ref|Steelheart|7}}

When [[David Charleston]] first tried to join the [[Reckoners]], [[Prof]] was the first to seriously consider his proposal to assassinate [[Steelheart (Epic)|Steelheart]]. [[Tia]], still skeptical, warned Prof to "remember what happened with Duskwatch". It is possible Duskwatch had tried and failed to do something similar to what David was suggesting, or that Duskwatch was an Epic whom the Reckoners had suffered adverse consequences for trying to kill.
