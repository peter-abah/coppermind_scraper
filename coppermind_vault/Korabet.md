Sergeant **Korabet** was one of [[Gare]]'s sergeants in [[Amaram]]'s army on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa1|47}}

He and [[Nalem]] often accompanied Gare around.{{book ref|sa1|47}} He was present when [[Kaladin]] bribed Gare to transfer [[Cenn]] to his squad.
