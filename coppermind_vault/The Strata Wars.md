***The Strata Wars*** is a planned trilogy of contemporary/urban fantasy novels written by [[Peter Orullian]] and [[Brandon Sanderson]].

==Books==
The first book in the series, *[[Songs of the Dead]]*, will be published in summer 2026. *Songs of the Dead* was written as a collaboration between the two authors, but Peter will write the other two novels in the trilogy.

==Plot and Setting==
*Songs of the Dead* introduces a musician named Jack Solomon who dies and wakes up in a new reality where music and light are considered magic. Brandon describes the character as a "heavy metal musician who becomes a necromancer".

==Development==

The origin of the series can be traced back to a draft that Brandon finished around 2010. He liked the ideas, but the story was never a top priority. It was intermittently worked on over the years, and various changes were made to the characters and setting in subsequent drafts. Once Brandon decided that the world would include elements of music culture, he brought in Peter as a collaborator since he is a heavy metal musician. Brandon created the outline and world guide and helped with revisions on the first novel, but ultimately handed the project over to Peter due to time constraints.
