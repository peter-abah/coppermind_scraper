**Treledees** is a [[Hallandren]] man and High Priest to the God King [[Susebron]].

## Appearance and Personality
Treledees has a long face. He's unusually tall for a [[Hallandren]],{{book ref|wb|16}} and is one the tallest people in the [[Court of the Gods]]. In fact, he is only slightly shorter than the [[Returned]] themselves, though, unlike the men of the Court of Gods, he is spindly.{{book ref|wb|11}} As high priest of the God King, he is always dressed grandly. He wears ornate robes in silver and blue with high peaked shoulders that make them reminiscent of armor. He also wears a matching, mitre-like headdress.{{book ref|wb|4}}

While highly respected in the court, [[Sisirinah]] considers Treledees to be obnoxious and disdainful,{{book ref|wb|11}} as well as self-assured and arrogant.{{book ref|wb|18}} He does not believe in bending rules or playing favorites.{{book ref|wb|11}}

## Attributes and Abilities
Treledees is clever and observant as well as covert. In addition to his mental skills, he has additional [[Breath]]s to get to the Third [[Heightening]], which grants him Aura Recognition, Perfect Pitch and Perfect Color Recognition.{{book ref|wb|40}}{{wob ref|7303}}

He has researched the first and second princesses of [[Idris]] in great detail (although admits to the courts lack of research on [[Sisirinah]]) and understands how to use the [[Royal Locks]] to read their emotions.{{book ref|wb|40}}

Treledees is a devoted priest and takes his role as such seriously, even to the point of respecting [[Siri]]’s role as queen, though he will only call her "Vessel."{{book ref|wb|11}}

## History
{{quote
|Quiet you fool! We're trying to help you!
|Treledees to Siri{{book ref|wb|53}}
}}

[[Siri]] initially observed Treledees as an outwardly hostile man for most of her time in the [[Court of the Gods]]. She suspected him of plotting to kill the [[God King]], even after [[Susebron]] told her that Treledees was a man that he trusted along with all his other priests.

[[Susebron]] was proven correct during the [[Pahn Kahl rebellion]], when Treledees took up a fallen soldier’s sword to defend them{{book ref|wb|54}} and eventually died protecting him and [[Siri]].{{book ref|wb|55}}

## Relationships
Treledees is a loyal High Preist of [[Susebron]] the God King of Hallandren.{{book ref|wb|55}} He dislikes [[Siri]] as queen of Hallandren and tries to keep her in line. Despite this, he is ready to do all in his power to preserve her during the [[Pahn Kahl rebellion]].

## Trivia
In earlier drafts of Warbreaker, Treledees was named "Tridees."{{wob ref|7302}}
