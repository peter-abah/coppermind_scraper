**Donny** is a [[koloss#After the Ascension|koloss-blooded]] on [[Scadrial]]. Constable [[Matieu]] of [[Ironstand]] describes him as "little and feisty."{{book ref|mb6|9}}

By {{date|342|PC}}, he was the leader of the [[Nightstreet Gang]], though he either did not participate in the gang's attack on [[Waxillium Ladrian]]'s train to [[New Seran]] or escaped without encountering Wax. After the attack, Constable Matieu was planning on chasing down him and the rest of the gang with a posse.{{book ref|mb6|9}}
