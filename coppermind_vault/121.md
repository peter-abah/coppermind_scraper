**121** is a [[highspren]] on [[Roshar]]. He is bonded to [[Nale]].

He does not approve of close relationships between spren and their [[Knights Radiant|Radiant]]; he thinks that these make the spren an "auxiliary" to human will. [[Auxiliary|12124]] likely derived his new name from that idea, as much of his treatment of [[Szeth]] was influenced by 121's such opinions.

121 and Nale have reached the Fifth Ideal of the Skybreakers.

When in [[shardblade]] form, he resembles Nale's "[[Glyphs/Index#Fundamental Glyphs|swordglyph]]". Any relation between the two is unknown.
