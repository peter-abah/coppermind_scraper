**Nyen** is a [[Forger]] in the [[Rose Empire]] on [[Sel]].

Nyen is employed by [[Frava]] to reverse-engineer and potentially complete the [[soulstamp]]s that [[Wan ShaiLu|Shai]] created for [[Ashravan|Emperor Ashravan]].{{tes ref|101}} Nyen is presumed to be a highly experienced Forger and cryptographer, though inferior to Shai.
