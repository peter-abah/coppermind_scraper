**Kelsier's seon** is an unnamed [[seon]] currently located on Scadrial. It is likely that they are bonded to [[Kelsier]].{{book ref|mb7|40}}

## Appearance and Personality
It can be assumed that Kelsier's seon has the same characteristics as other seons; being made up of a glowing sphere of light about the size of a child’s head. At the center of this sphere is its namesake [[Aon]]. They generally float, and have the unique ability to link to other seons to instantly communicate across any distance.{{book ref|mb7|40}}

## History
Kelsier‘s seon was originally created on the world of [[Sel]], and likely stayed there for a time. At some point after the [[Catacendre]], they bonded Kelsier and started traveling with him. They served as a way for Kelsier to instantly communicate with other members of his [[Ghostbloods|organisation]].

It appears that during the Rosharan year 1167, they linked with a Ghostblood seon on Roshar in order to let Kelsier speak to [[Gavilar Kholin]] directly.{{book ref|sa5|prologue}} They also linked with [[Ala]], another seon, after the arrival of the [[Night of Sorrows]] to allow for a conversation between Kelsier and [[Shallan Davar]].{{book ref|sa5|147}}

During one trip, they flew with Kelsier to the south of Scadrial. Upon their return, about twelve hours (by Scadrian Airship) from Bilming, they were contacted by [[Dao]]. Together, they facilitated a conversation between Kelsier and various Ghostblood members in Bilming.{{book ref|mb7|40}}
