**Nabetse Zitro** is one of the artists of the graphic novel adaptation of [[Brandon Sanderson]]'s *[[White Sand (prose)|White Sand]]*. He is credited as an artist in the *[[White Sand]]* Omnibus and [[The Sunlit Man]].
