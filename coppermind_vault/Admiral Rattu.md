**Admiral Rattu** is an [[Eelakin]] politician on [[First of the Sun]] and a participant in the negotiations with the [[Ones Above]]. He is quite influential, with other Eelakin officials deferring to him to take the lead in political discussions. He has a large pure green [[aviar]] with a yellow chest that likes to puff out its feathers.

## History
Though he was not part of the expedition's crew, Rattu took a leading role in the preparation of the failed first expedition of a ship attempting to sail the [[unsea]]. He was decidedly against sending a second expedition due to the high cost of resources and lives and the low chance of success.

While [[Vathi]] and [[Dusk]] were away on [[Patji (island)|Patji]], in a bid to install himself in office Rattu began the process of ousting Vathi from her presidential position, filing a motion to cut off funding to Vathi's projects and to convene the [[First Company]] vice presidents to assess her leadership. He was likely involved in the attempted votes of no confidence in her leadership over the following months.
