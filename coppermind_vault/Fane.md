**Fane** is a [[Natan]] thief on [[Roshar]]. He was hired by [[Serli]] to steal [[fabrial]]s from the first ever [[Urithiru Grand Showcase]], but he was interrupted when an escaped goat wandered in after him. They each fled with one fabrial.{{rpg ref|ss|15}} Fane climbed out of the showroom’s window and fled by using Serli’s fabrial, a pair of boots that let its user reduce their weight. After the theft, he was to meet his anonymous employer in an establishment called [[Talamin's Kitchen]].{{rpg ref|ss|16}} It is unknown if the plot succeeded.
