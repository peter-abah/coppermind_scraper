**Fane** is a [[Natan]] thief on [[Roshar]]. He was hired by [[Serli]] to steal [[fabrial]]s from the first ever [[Urithiru Grand Showcase]], but he was interrupted when an escaped goat wandered in after him. They each fled with one fabrial. Fane climbed out of the showroom’s window and fled by using Serli’s fabrial, a pair of boots that let its user reduce their weight. After the theft, he was to meet his anonymous employer in an establishment called [[Talamin's Kitchen]]. It is unknown if the plot succeeded.
