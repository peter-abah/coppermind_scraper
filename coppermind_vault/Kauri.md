**Kauri** is a [[kitsen]] who travels to [[Starsight (station)|Starsight]] under the leadership of [[Hesho]] and acts as his shieldbearer and navigator.{{book ref|sky2|24}} 

## Appearance and Personality
{{for|kitsen}}
Kauri wears a red and gold uniform and carries an ornamental shield.{{book ref|sky2|20}} This shield is a physical symbol of her duty as [[Hesho]]'s shieldbearer. The duties and responsibilities of such a post are not made clear but it seems that shieldbearers serve as personal assistants, providing information and fulfilling menial tasks.{{book ref|sky2|25}} She also is tasked with piloting a small hoverplatform that the kitsen of the *[[Gaualako-An]]* use as transport outside of their ship.{{book ref|sky2|30}}

Kauri is the navigator of the *Gaualako-An*.{{book ref|sky2|24}} Though her duties as a navigator are not clear, she is often tasked with explaining details about the universe in Hesho's stead. Consequently, she is knowledgeable, skilled at explaining, and generally helpful.{{book ref|sky2|25}}

## History
Before coming to Starsight, Kauri worked as Hesho's navigator on the *Gaualako-An*.{{cite}} After arrival and after being accepted as a trainee, she changed ships to work again as Hesho's navigator on the *[[Swims Against the Current in a Stream Reflecting the Sun]]*, also known as the *Swims Upstream*.{{book ref|sky2|24}} Kauri later takes control of the *Swims Upstream* after Hesho's death.{{book ref|sky2|42}}
