**[[Peter Ahlstrom]]** is a friend of [[Brandon Sanderson]]'s. He is a longtime employee of [[Dragonsteel Entertainment]] and has a number of cameos in Brandon's works.<br/>
**[[Karen Ahlstrom]]** is also a friend and employee of Brandon's, and some of the cameos may also refer to her. Peter and Karen are married.
