> Wolfhound, miss? 'Tis no pet for a girl like yourself. Mean brutes, those. Let me find you a nice bobbie. Nice dogs, those&mdash;smart, too.
> — A [[Luthadel]] dog merchant to [[Vin]]

**Bobbies** are a breed of dogs on [[Scadrial]].

When [[Vin]] goes looking for a wolfhound to use as a new body for [[OreSeur]], the dog merchant tries to sell her a bobbie instead. The merchant considers them nice and smart, and therefore much better suited for a girl than a wolfhound.
