**Bobbies** are a breed of dogs on [[Scadrial]].{{book ref|mb2|5}}

When [[Vin]] goes looking for a wolfhound to use as a new body for [[OreSeur]], the dog merchant tries to sell her a bobbie instead. The merchant considers them nice and smart, and therefore much better suited for a girl than a wolfhound.{{book ref|mb2|5}}
