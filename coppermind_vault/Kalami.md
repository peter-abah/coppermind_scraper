**Kalami** is a lighteyed historian of note on [[Roshar]] and a senior scribe of [[House Kholin]]. She has long dark hair and is married to [[Teleb]]. 

She planned to write a history of the [[War of Reckoning]] and had gained permission to record meetings such as the discussion between [[Dalinar]] and Teleb on whether or not they would run a plateau assault.

After losing her husband in the [[Battle of Narak]], she throws herself into her work as a historian and a scribe. She has done scholarly work on Vorin theory, so she could provide Dalinar with some information about the [[Desolation]]s. She is also present to scribe the communication between Dalinar and the other monarchs on his first attempt to unify them against the [[Voidbringer]]s.
