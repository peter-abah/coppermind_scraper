**House Urbain** is one of the ten Great Houses of [[Luthadel]] on [[Scadrial]] in 1022 in the [[Final Empire]]. It is the second least powerful of the ten Great Houses, only above [[House Buvidas]].

Both House Urbain and [[House Teniert|Teniert]] had been hit by thieving crews before [[Kelsier]]'s return to [[Luthadel]], but not for their [[atium]].
