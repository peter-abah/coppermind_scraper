**Imalei** is a femalen [[Regal#Envoyform|envoyform]] [[Regal]] on [[Roshar]]. She is one of the three singer monarchs in [[Iri]], and therefore holds great power over one of [[Odium]]’s, and later [[Retribution]]’s, dominions. She serves as a public speaker and go-between for the [[Fused]]. During her transition to a form of power, Imalei’s [[Voidspren]] recognized the truth of her [[gemheart]] and freed her of her malen body. Imalei now serves the Fused fervently. She also seems to believe in the [[Iriali]] religion of the [[One]].
