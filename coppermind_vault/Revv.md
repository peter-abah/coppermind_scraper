**Revv** was a historical [[wikipedia: toparches|toparchy]] on [[Roshar]] during the [[Heraldic Epochs]].

Revv is mentioned in *[[Words of Radiance (in-world)|Words of Radiance]]* as the location of some disturbances which were quelled at the time of [[Nalan]]'s acceptance of the [[Skybreakers]]' patronage.

> And thus were the disturbances in the Revv toparchy quieted, when, upon their ceasing to prosecute their civil dissensions, Nalan'Elin betook himself to finally accept the Skybreakers who had named him their master, when initially he had spurned their advances and, in his own interests, refused to countenance that which he deemed a pursuit of vanity and annoyance; this was the last of the [[Herald]]s to admit to such patronage.
> — *Words of Radiance* - [[Words of Radiance (in-world)#Chapter_5|chapter 5, page 17]]
