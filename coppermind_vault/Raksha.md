**Raksha** was a soldier in [[Amaram]]'s army in [[Alethkar]] on [[Roshar]]. He was a member of [[Kaladin]]'s squad.

An unknown [[Veden]] [[Shardbearer]] killed him, along with [[Toorim]], [[Hamel]], [[Acis]], [[Navar]] and eleven other members of [[Kaladin]]'s squad. It was later discovered by [[Shallan Davar]] by identification of his [[Shardblade]], in the current possession of [[Amaram]], that the Veden Shardbearer was [[Helaran Davar]].
