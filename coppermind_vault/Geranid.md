**Geranid** is an [[ardent]], scientist, and philosopher on [[Roshar]].

She lives with [[Ashir]] on a small [[Reshi]] island.

She spends her time studying [[spren]] and finds that the act of writing down the dimensions of a [[Spren#Flamespren|flamespren]] at any particular time causes the spren to stop altering in shape, a behavior inspired by [[Wikipedia:Quantum mechanics|quantum mechanics]].

Later on her findings are being read through by the ardent [[Rushu]], who thinks they are important and suggests them to [[Navani Kholin]].

## Trivia
* The phrase "Starlight" has so far only been used by Geranid.
