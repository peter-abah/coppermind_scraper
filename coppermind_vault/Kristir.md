**Kristir** is a scholar on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa4|37}}

She works under [[Navani Kholin]] at [[Urithiru]]. On the day Navani tries [[Tomor]]'s lift [[fabrial]] gauntlet, Kristir is the head scholar on duty; she wins seven [[clearmark]]s in the scholars' betting pool on whether Navani would try the device. After Navani tries the gauntlet, she tells Kristir to assign several of their best mathematicians to work on isolating vertical and lateral movement in conjoined fabrials.{{book ref|sa4|37}}
