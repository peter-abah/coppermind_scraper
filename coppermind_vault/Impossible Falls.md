The **Impossible Falls** was a waterfall located in [[Kholinar]] on [[Roshar]] during the [[Era of Solitude]].{{book ref|sa1|26}}

## Location and Structure
The Impossible Falls are located in the southwest corner of the city of Kholinar, seemingly adjoining one of the [[windblades]].{{map ref|Kholinar}} Water flows down the Impossible Falls from tier to tier.{{book ref|sa3|77}} Whether the Falls are a natural formation or manmade is uncertain, but it is said that when they are viewed from the correct angles the water seems to flow down the tiers and then back to the top.

## History
A few months after they met the [[Parshendi]] on the [[Shattered Plains]], King [[Gavilar Kholin]] and Highprince [[Dalinar Kholin]] visited the Impossible Falls once and briefly discussed how Gavilar saw the world differently now.{{book ref|sa1|26}} Three days later, [[Szeth]] assassinated Gavilar.

Adolin also visited the Impossible Falls at some point, and later described them to [[Shallan Davar]], telling her that he wanted to show them to her in person one day.{{book ref|sa3|77}}
