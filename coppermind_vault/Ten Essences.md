The **Ten Essences** is the traditional [[Vorin]] term referring to the ten classical elements of Vorin theology, which are used to describe the world.{{book ref|sa1|part=ars}} 

## Soulcasting
Each Essence is connected to a particular [[Polestone|gemstone]].{{book ref|sa1|part=ars}} In order to [[Soulcast]] (via [[fabrial]]) an object into a new material, a Soulcaster must use the gemstone that corresponds to the new material's Essence.

## Vorinism
Modern [[Vorin]] teachings consider the Ten Essences as the collection of the ten classical elements that make up and describe the world; they connect each Essence with a [[Herald]] and that Herald's divine attributes, along with many other ideas and substances.{{book ref|sa1|part=ars}} There is an association between various forces and Essences, reflected in the Double Eye of the Almighty.{{file ref|TWoK_Front_Endsheet.jpg|The Double Eye of the Almighty}}{{wob ref|953}}{{wob ref|8476}} The exact nature of these connections is currently unknown. 

There is some correlation between the Essences and the spren of the corresponding orders of the [[Knights Radiant|Radiant]], but the relationship is imperfect and it is not clear how strongly it actually reflects reality.{{wob ref|16195}} Each [[Stances|stance]] used in Vorin combat is associated with an Essence.{{book ref|sa1|19}}

### Body Focus
Vorin doctrine also associates each Essence with a "Body Focus" related to the human body. [[Khriss]] believes that the concept is attributable to Vorin philosophy rather than any actual aspect of Rosharan [[Investiture]].{{book ref|sa1|part=ars}} However, it may in fact have some significance.{{wob ref|14636}} Body Focuses do not directly correspond to [[Hemalurgy|Hemalurgic]] bindpoints, but there is a loose relationship.{{wob ref|1100}}
