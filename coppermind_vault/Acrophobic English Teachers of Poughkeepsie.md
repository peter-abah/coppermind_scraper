> It's no more dangerous than when I testified against the Acrophobic English Teachers of Poughkeepsie!
> — [[Alcatraz Smedry]]

The **Acrophobic English Teachers of Poughkeepsie** are characters in one of [[Rikers Dartmoor]]'s books. They are known to be dangerous to testify against. Their name suggests that they have [[wikipedia:Acrophobia|acrophobia]], or the fear of heights.

[[Alcatraz]] mentioned testifying against the group in a conversation with [[Folsom]] and [[Himalaya]]. Having spoken about the incident earlier with the prince, he seemed to have forgotten that it occurred in a book and not in his real life.
