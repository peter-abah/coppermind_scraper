***The Most Boring Book Ever*** is a children's picture book by [[Brandon Sanderson]] and [[wikipedia:Kazu Kibuishi|Kazu Kibuishi]]. The title refers to the book's "boring" text, which is humorously contrasted with exciting pictures to accompany it. It was published by Roaring Brook Press, a children's imprint of Macmillan Publishers, on September 24, 2024.

==Development==
During the COVID-19 pandemic, Brandon found himself reading more picture books with his kids, notably *[[wikipedia:Don't Let the Pigeon Drive the Bus!|Don't Let the Pigeon Drive the Bus!]]* and *The Book With No Pictures*. These books (and one of his favorite picture books as a kid, *[[wikipedia:The Monster at the End of This Book|The Monster at the End of This Book]]*) each have elements that directly interact with the reader. Brandon's general love of this genre inspired him to create *The Most Boring Book Ever*. Brandon and his family were fans of Kazu Kibuishi through his *[[wikipedia:Amulet_(comics)|Amulet]]* series of epic fantasy graphic novels, and they were excited when he agreed to join the project.<ref name=announcement/> Brandon believes that a wide age range of kids will enjoy the book's concept.<ref name=cover/>

==Future==
Brandon believes that the experience of publishing *The Most Boring Book Ever* will help him realize his ambitions of creating [[cosmere]] picture books based on in-world stories such as "[[The Girl Who Looked Up]]" and "The Dog and the Dragon". The cosmere stories would presumably involve [[Hoid]].<ref name=announcement/>
