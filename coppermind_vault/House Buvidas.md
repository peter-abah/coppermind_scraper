**House Buvidas** was one of the ten Great Houses of [[Luthadel]] on [[Scadrial]] during the [[Final Empire]].{{book ref|mb1|9}}

House Buvidas had a keep built in Luthadel and maintained a high-profile presence in the city,{{book ref|mb1|2}} but they were the least influential of the Great Houses.{{book ref|mb1|9}}
