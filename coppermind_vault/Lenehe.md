**Lenehe** is a poet on [[Sel]]. The name, derived from the [[Aon#Ehe|Aon Ehe]], had been one they chose upon reaching maturity as an artist, and to distance themselves from their older works.{{wob ref|7592}}

Lenehe said that Aon Ehe was "The most inspiring of all Aons, a symbol for those with a creative heart and an unhindered mind."{{wob ref|7592}}
