**Berqq** is a town on [[Roshar]] that is on the eastern edge of the part of the border of [[Azir]] and [[Yulay]] that is not mountainous. It is east of [[Benru]]. It is unclear if Berqq is under Azir's or Yulay's jurisdiction.{{map ref|Eastern Makabak}}
