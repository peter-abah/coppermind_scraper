**Lar'Cal** is a city built by [[Dragon (cosmere)|dragonkind]] on [[Yolen]]. It is situated high in the air, along Yolen's [[skyways]] that run above the planet's forests. Lar'Cal was built "before the rivalry between the [[fain]] and the unfain," back when the dragon [[Frost]] was young. The majestic city looks ivory and has many alabaster towers, which have stood through war, disaster, and even the death of [[Adonalsium]]. The image and memory of Lar'Cal evokes a feeling of homesickness in the exiled dragon [[Starling]].{{book ref|iote|40}}
