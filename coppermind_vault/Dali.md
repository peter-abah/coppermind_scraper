**Dali** is a scholar on [[Roshar]].

During the [[singer]] occupation of [[Urithiru]], [[Navani Kholin]] told [[Rushu]] to assemble a team of abstract thinkers to work on ways to identify [[Fused]] in disguise. Rushu commented that Dali would be perfect for the team.
