The **gtet** is a traditional sword used in [[Thaylenah]] on [[Roshar]].{{book ref|sa2|i|3}}

## Appearance
The gtet has a large, triangular-shaped blade that is very wedge-like. The blade extends down past the grip, which is held like a fist. The grip is directly at the bottom and part of the hilt rests around the wrist for support. It is more of a handle than a true hilt.{{book ref|sa4|21}} The gtet is carried in a sheath at one's side when it is not in use.{{book ref|sa2|i|3}} 

## Known Users
[[Kylrm]], one of [[Vstim]]'s guards, carries a gtet as his weapon, along with a bow.{{book ref|sa2|i|3}}

[[Adolin Kholin]] brings a gtet with him on his expedition to visit [[Lasting Integrity]]. He shows it to [[Godeke]] before they depart.{{book ref|sa4|21}}

## Trivia
* The description of the gtet has some similarities to both a [[Roshar]]an blade known as the [[kattari]] and a real-life dagger from India known as the [[Wikipedia:katar|katar]].
