**Reven** is the  king of [[Seevis]], one of the Rim Kingdoms of [[Taldain]].

After the death of the majority of the [[Diem]], [[Kenton]] sought to preserve the [[sand mastery|sand masters]]. Kenton sought Lord [[Raagent]] for assistance, but Raagent knew that one of the Rim Kingdoms had been dealing with a mastrell. Raagent suspected that King Reven was going to hire the sand masters, and tried to hire them himself instead.
