**AonDor** is a manifestation of [[Investiture]] located on the [[Cosmere#Planets|planet]] [[Sel]] that is used by the [[Elantrian]]s.{{book ref|Elantris|4}} It involves the use of variously shaped [[Aon]]s, which draw energy from the [[Dor]] and must be fine-tuned to the landscape.

## Prerequisites
Only an [[Elantrian]] can use the [[Aon]]s to draw power from the [[Dor]].{{book ref|Elantris|7}} This means that to use AonDor, a person must originally be of [[Aonic]] descent.{{wob ref|2173}} The person must then be chosen by the [[Shaod]]. Their skin will glow, their hair will turn white, and they gain the ability to draw Aons that channel the Dor.{{book ref|Elantris|4}}

It is also possible to form this Connection by being invited into specific groups (presumably Elantrian groups, such as the [[Ire]]), though the exact mechanics of this are not yet known.{{book ref|tress|64}} Someone can also [[Forgery|stamp]] themselves to become an Elantrian.{{book ref|mb7|54}}

## Drawing Aons
In order to draw a functional Aon, the Elantrian drawing it must intend to do so, or else the glowing lines used to draw the Aon won’t appear.{{book ref|Elantris|7}} Elantrians can use their fingers to draw Aons, with each finger giving the lines different widths. Other objects like a quill or stick can also be used to draw Aons.{{book ref|Elantris|49}} Aons can also be crafted out of other materials by Elantrians, like stone, wire, or [[Light]].{{book ref|sa5|i|11}} In this way they can make amulets and the like, that have an actual magical effect.{{book ref|Elantris|19}} It seems like some technology similar to this, Aonic in origin, has made its way into the Space Age of the cosmere.{{book ref|iote|39}} Leaving an Aon alone for a short time without completing it will cause it to disappear with no effects.{{book ref|Elantris|46}}

Each Aon has a unique effect, which is determined by its shape. The Aon "Aon" is the starting point for every other Aon.{{book ref|Elantris|25}} It appears that the energy can only get to the [[Physical Realm]] in certain pulse lengths and frequencies, so an Aon’s shape only allows for a certain frequency to escape, which has a specific effect.{{book ref|Elantris|28}} If an Aon is the wrong shape, it may do nothing, and the Aon will flash once and fade away.{{book ref|Elantris|46}} An Aon drawn incorrectly with an omission of basic lines would begin the process of the Aon, but stop in the middle of the process due to not having enough energy from the Dor.{{book ref|Elantris|25}} Sometimes if it is targeted at a person or group of people, it could cause them to appear like a Reod Elantrian.{{book ref|Elantris|60}} The size of an Aon can affect its power.{{wob ref|8187}} Aons can also be combined together.{{wob ref|663}} Aons can be given more control or strength when the width of the lines match the proper locations that correspond with [[Arelon]].{{book ref|Elantris|49}}

Aons can only be destroyed by an Elantrian. Unless destroyed by an Elantrian an Aon will continue to function even if it is scuffed or erased.{{book ref|Elantris|52}} It is possible for AonDor to negate effects granted by [[Dakhor]].{{wob ref|3014}}

## Aon Modifiers
While Aons are undoubtedly powerful, in order to specify certain traits, like how long a column of fire from an Aon Ehe will last, or where an Aon Tia will take you, additional lines must be drawn around the central Aon to modify its effects.{{book ref|Elantris|19}}{{book ref|Elantris|25}}

### Types of Modifiers
*Linking Aons - used to link subsections of Aon lists together, it controls the timing of when other Aons take effect.{{book ref|Elantris|25}}{{wob ref|7594}}
*Setting off different chains of Aons.{{book ref|Elantris|62}}
*Modifying power levels of Aon effects.{{book ref|Elantris|49}}
*Modifying the range of Aons.{{book ref|Elantris|34}}

## Aon Equations
{{image|BattleOfWits.jpg|[[Hoid]] and [[Riina]] drawing Aon Equations|side=right|height=250px}}
In extremely advanced uses of AonDor, multiple Aons can be used in conjunction to modify each other in Aon equations.{{wob ref|1938}} These are used to channel the Dor in the most specific ways allowed by AonDor. With it, Elantrians could fabricate any object out of nothing, although the sheer skill and practice required makes Elantrians who can do this extremely rare.{{wob ref|1938}} In addition to its regular effect, it appears that each Aon has a secondary effect that can be used in the Aon equations. Almost any effect in the cosmere can be replicated with the right equation,{{wob ref|15860}} though AonDor struggles to fully imitate the effects of persistent bonds or [[Anti-Investiture]].{{wob ref|17125}}

### Curses
It is possible through complex Aon Equations to lay complex curses upon people with specific effects and conditions, even to the point of transforming someone into a completely different species.{{book ref|tress|64}} They can also completely change one's personality,{{book ref|tress|13}} limit their intelligence,{{book ref|tress|24}} and forbid one from being able to speak of their curse or how to break it.{{book ref|tress|24}}{{book ref|tress|61}}

The only known way to break a curse is to fulfill the conditions of the curse.{{book ref|tress|64}} These curses are difficult to break in any other way, even by the one who created it. Though it is possible to do so through unknown methods or editing the parameters of the conditions.{{book ref|tress|epilogue}}{{book ref|tress|64}}

Cursed individuals have a ring of Aons surrounding them, which describes and makes up their curse.{{book ref|tress|64}} These appear to be difficult or impossible to see by those without access to AonDor.

## Relationship To Arelon
AonDor has a specific tie to the land of Arelon.{{wob ref|2173}} The base Aon Aon is shaped like the land of Arelon, with its lines and dot representing the coast, Atad Mountains, and Lake Alonoe.{{book ref|Elantris|46}} Every Aon reflects some trait of the land, such as the Dii, the Aon for wood, contain circles where the forests of Arelon fall.{{book ref|Elantris|49}} This is a very fluid relationship; as the land changes so do the Aons. When an earthquake created a chasm in the south of Arelon, a new line was required in every Aon to make it functional.{{book ref|Elantris|49}} AonDor's power will diminish the further an Elantrian travels from the city of [[Elantris (city)|Elantris]].{{book ref|Elantris|19}}{{wob ref|8617}}

It is possible to create another city similar to Elantris in other locations on Sel.{{wob ref|254}} The [[Ire]] created a base similar to it in the [[Cognitive Realm]] of [[Scadrial]], though in order for this to work, Investiture from Sel needs to be piped to it.{{msh ref|5|2}} There are, however, other ways to achieve the same effect.{{wob ref|13387}} 

AonDor will not normally work on planets other than [[Sel]],{{wob ref|1473}}{{wob ref|6164}} but it is possible to hack the magic system and get it to work on other planets.{{book ref|tress|64}}{{wob ref|5633}}{{book ref|mb7|54}} A Full [[Feruchemist]] could use [[nicrosil#Feruchemical Use|nicrosil]] to create an [[unsealed metalminds|unsealed]] [[duralumin#Feruchemical Use|duraluminmind]] [[Southern_Scadrian#Technology|medallion]] that allows an Elantrian to store their [[Connection]] to Elantris for magic to work at full potential far from Elantris.{{wob ref|12895}}

## History
### The First Elantrians
King [[Raoshem|Rhashm]] led his people from other lands to Arelon, discovering the city of Elantris completely abandoned. Despite superstition about Elantris being haunted, Rhashm made it his capital. After a few decades, Princess [[Elashe]], one of his descendants, was the first Aonic person to be taken by the Shaod to become an Elantrian.{{wob ref|7592}}

### The Reod
For centuries, AonDor functioned fine, Elantrians ruling Arelon from Elantris.{{book ref|Elantris|prologue}} However, when the earthquake caused the Chasm, the Elantrians became the target of the now malformed Rao that made up Elantris and the Outer Cities. This caused them to become cursed and foul looking, as well as robbing them of their access to the magic system.{{book ref|Elantris|49}} They didn’t know where the new line was, so they couldn’t use the magic. Most were slaughtered mercilessly by the terrified citizenry.{{book ref|Elantris|5}}

### Restoration of Elantris
[[Raoden]] managed to discover what was wrong with the AonDor during his imprisonment in the city, and he repaired the Rao and restored the city to its former glory.{{book ref|Elantris|61}} He, an Elantrian, took his place as king of Arelon, ruling from the city of Elantris.{{book ref|Elantris|62}}

### The Sorceress of Lumar
At some point in the future, [[Riina]] settled on [[Lumar]] and became feared as the Sorceress. One source of her infamy as her various curses, which she laid upon people using complex AonDor arrays.{{book ref|tress|13}}{{book ref|tress|64}}{{file ref|BattleOfWits.jpg|Battle of Wits}}
